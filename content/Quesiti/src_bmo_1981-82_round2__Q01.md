# British Mathematical Olympiad 1981-82 — Round 2 — Quesito 1

*Angle bisectors meet circumcircle; prove sum inequality*

> $ABC$ is a triangle. The internal bisector of the angle $A$ meets the circumcircle again at $P$. $Q$ and $R$ are similarly defined.
> 
> Prove that $AP + BQ + CR > AB + BC + CA$.
