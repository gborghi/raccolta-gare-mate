*Cyclic pentagon with diagonals parallel to opposite sides: must it be regular?*

> Let $ABCDE$ be a pentagon inscribed in a circle. Suppose that $AC$, $BD$, $CE$, $DA$, $EB$ are parallel to $AB$, $BC$, $CD$, $DE$, $EA$ respectively. Does it follow that the pentagon has to be regular? Justify your claim.
