*Fill 2021x2021 with 1,2,3 so every 2x2 sums to 8; count mod 100*

> Consider the ways to write one of the numbers $1$, $2$, $3$ in each cell of a $2021\times 2021$ grid such that, for every $2\times 2$ block of cells, the sum of the four numbers written in it equals $8$. Suppose there are $A$ such fillings in total. Find the remainder when $A$ is divided by $100$. Fillings that coincide under a rotation or reflection are counted as distinct.
