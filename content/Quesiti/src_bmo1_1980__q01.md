*Prove x^8+y^8+z^8=n^8 has no integer solution with 0<=x,y,z<n*

> Prove that the equation $x^8 + y^8 + z^8 = n^8$, where $n$ is an integer $> 1$, has no solution in integers $x, y, z$, with $0 \le y \le n$.
