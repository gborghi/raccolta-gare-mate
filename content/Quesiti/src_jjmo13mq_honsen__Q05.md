# Japan Junior Mathematical Olympiad 2015 — Honsen (Final) — Quesito 5

*2015 points, edges with pairwise angles over 90 degrees, max edges*

> In the plane, take $2015$ distinct points, and join some pairs of them by edges. Suppose that any two distinct edges that share a common endpoint form an angle greater than $90^\circ$. Find the maximum possible value of the number of edges.
