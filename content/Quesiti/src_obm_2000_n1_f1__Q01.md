*Extend multiplication pattern to find missing multiplier*

> Observe the following multiplications:
> $$12\,345\,679 \times 18 = 222\,222\,222$$
> $$12\,345\,679 \times 27 = 333\,333\,333$$
> $$12\,345\,679 \times 36 = 444\,444\,444$$
> To obtain $999\,999\,999$ we must multiply $12\,345\,679$ by:
> 
> (A) $29$ \quad (B) $99$ \quad (C) $72$ \quad (D) $41$ \quad (E) $81$
