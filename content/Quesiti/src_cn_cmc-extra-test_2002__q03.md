*World Cup scheduling: count substitution plans*

> Before the World Cup tournament, the football coach of $F$ country will let seven players $A_1$, $A_2$, $\ldots$, $A_7$ join three training classes (90 minutes each) in order to assess them. Suppose, at any moment during a match, one and only one of them enters the field; and the total time (which is measured in minutes) on the field for each of $A_1$, $A_2$, $A_3$ and $A_4$ is divisible by 7 and the total time for each of $A_5$, $A_6$ and $A_7$ is divisible by 13. If there is no restriction about the number of times of substitution of players during each match, then how many possible cases are there within the total time for every player on the field?
