# British Mathematical Olympiad 2015-16 — Round 2 — Quesito 3

*Cyclic quadrilateral: PQ perp AC implies PQ perp BD*

> Let $ABCD$ be a cyclic quadrilateral. The diagonals $AC$ and $BD$ meet at $Q$. The lines $AB$ and $CD$ produced meet at $P$. Prove that if $PQ$ is perpendicular to $AC$, then $PQ$ is perpendicular to $BD$.
