*Prove a product inequality bounding the product of |x-alpha^k|/(x+alpha^k) by the product of (1-alpha^k)/(1+alpha^k).*

> Prove that for any natural number $n$ and real numbers $\alpha$ and $x$ satisfying $\alpha^{n+1} \le x \le 1$ and $0 < \alpha < 1$ it holds that
> $$\prod_{k=1}^{n} \left| \frac{x - \alpha^k}{x + \alpha^k} \right| \le \prod_{k=1}^{n} \frac{1 - \alpha^k}{1 + \alpha^k}.$$
