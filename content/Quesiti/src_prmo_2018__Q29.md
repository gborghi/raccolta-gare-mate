*Point D on BC, incentres I1 I2 of ABD ACD, angle BI1E=60, find angle CI2F*

> Let $D$ be an interior point of the side $BC$ of a triangle $ABC$. Let $I_1$ and $I_2$ be the incentres of triangles $ABD$ and $ACD$ respectively. Let $AI_1$ and $AI_2$ meet $BC$ in $E$ and $F$ respectively. If $\angle BI_1 E = 60^\circ$, what is the measure of $\angle CI_2 F$ in degrees?
