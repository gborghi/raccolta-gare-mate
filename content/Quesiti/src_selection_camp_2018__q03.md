*Find all prime pairs (p,q) with p>q such that a given expression is an integer*

> Find all pairs of primes $(p, q)$ with $p > q$ such that
> $$\frac{(p+q)^{p+q}(p-q)^{p-q} - 1}{(p+q)^{p-q}(p-q)^{p+q} - 1}$$
> is an integer.
