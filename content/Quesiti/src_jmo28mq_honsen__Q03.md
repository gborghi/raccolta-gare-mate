*function on 1..999 with iteration conditions has a fixed point*

> Let $S = \{1, 2, \ldots, 999\}$. Let $f$ be a function defined on $S$ taking values in $S$, such that for every element $n \in S$,
> $$f^{\,n + f(n) + 1}(n) = f^{\,n f(n)}(n) = n$$
> holds. Show that there exists an element $a \in S$ with $f(a) = a$. (Here $f^{k}(n)$ denotes $\underbrace{f(f(\cdots f(n)\cdots))}_{k}$, the $k$-fold composition of $f$.)
