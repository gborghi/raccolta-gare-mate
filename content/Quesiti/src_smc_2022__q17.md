*Regular octagon of side 1 with four corner equilateral triangles (side 1) removed; find the area of the resulting shape.*

> The shape shown is made by removing four equilateral triangles with side-length 1 from a regular octagon with side length 1. What is the area of the shape?
>
> - **(A)** $2 - 2\sqrt{3}$
> - **(B)** $3 + 2\sqrt{2} - \sqrt{3}$
> - **(C)** $2 + 2\sqrt{2} - \sqrt{3}$
> - **(D)** $3 - 2\sqrt{2} - \sqrt{3}$
> - **(E)** $2\sqrt{2} - \sqrt{3}$
