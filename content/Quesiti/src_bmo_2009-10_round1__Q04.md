*Two internally tangent circles; common tangent and secant; prove BD is diameter*

> Two circles of different radii touch internally at $A$. A common tangent, not through $A$, touches the inner circle at $C$, and passes through a point $B$ on the outer circle. The line $CA$ is produced to meet the outer circle at $D$. Prove that $BD$ is a diameter of the outer circle.
