*Length of SQ in a triangle where M is midpoint of PQ, PS bisects angle RPQ, ST is parallel to PR, angle MTI = 120 degrees*

> In the diagram shown, $M$ is the mid-point of $PQ$. The line $PS$ bisects $\angle RPQ$ and intersects $RQ$ at $T$. The line $ST$ is parallel to $PR$ and intersects $PQ$ at $S$. The length of $PQ$ is 12 and the length of $MT$ is 1. The angle $\angle STQ = 120^\circ$. What is the length of $SQ$?
>
> - **(A)** $2$
> - **(B)** $3$
> - **(C)** $3.5$
> - **(D)** $4$
> - **(E)** $5$
