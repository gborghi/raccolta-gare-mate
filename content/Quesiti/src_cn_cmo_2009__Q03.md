*Count convex m-gons with exactly two acute angles in regular (2s+1)-gon*

> Let $m$, $s$ be integers with $4 < m < s$, and $A_0$, $A_1$, $A_2$, $\ldots$, $A_{2s}$ be the vertices of a regular $(2s+1)$-gon. In addition, let $P = \{A_1, A_2, \ldots, A_{2s}\}$. Find the number of convex $m$-gons with exactly two acute interior angles whose vertices are all in $P$. (Posed by Leng Gangsong)
