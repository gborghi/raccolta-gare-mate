# British Mathematical Olympiad 1991-92 — Round 2 — Quesito 1

*Odd prime p: unique positive integers x,y with given relation; find formula*

> Let $p$ be an odd prime number. Prove that there are unique positive integers $x$, $y$ such that $x^2 = p^y + p^2$, and give the formula for $x$ and $y$ in terms of $p$.
