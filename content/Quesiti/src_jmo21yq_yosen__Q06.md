# Japan Mathematical Olympiad 2011 — Yosen (Preliminary) — Quesito 6

*Find AR in right triangle with angle bisector and equal-length conditions*

> In triangle $ABC$ with $\angle ABC = 90^\circ$, points $P, Q, R$ lie on sides $BC$, $CA$, $AB$ respectively, with $AQ : QC = 2 : 1$, $AR = AQ$, $QP = QR$, and $\angle PQR = 90^\circ$. Given $CP = 1$, find $AR$. Here, $XY$ denotes the length of segment $XY$.
