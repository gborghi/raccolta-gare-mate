*Count ways to fill 2×2014 Bitonho table with 0s and 1s*

> Bitonho is a game of filling a $2 \times 2014$ table with zeros and ones in such a way that if two horizontally adjacent cells in the same row are equal, then the two cells of the other row in the same columns are not equal. For example, in the drawing below, cells $A$ and $B$ are not equal. In how many ways can a $2 \times 2014$ table be filled following the Bitonho rule?
> 
> (A) $3 \cdot 2^{2014}$ $\quad$ (B) $4 \cdot 2^{2013}$ $\quad$ (C) $4^{2014}$ $\quad$ (D) $2 \cdot 3^{2014}$ $\quad$ (E) $3 \cdot 4^{2014}$
