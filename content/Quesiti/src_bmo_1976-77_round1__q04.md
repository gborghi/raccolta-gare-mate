*Cubic with roots u,v,w; express roots of related cubic and exclude interval*

> The equation $x^3 + qx + r = 0$, where $r \ne 0$, has roots $u, v, w$. Express the roots of
> $$r^2 x^3 + q^3 x + q^3 = 0 \qquad \ldots\ldots(1)$$
> in terms of $u, v, w$, and show that if $u, v, w$ are real then $(1)$ has no root in the interval $-1 < x < 3$.
