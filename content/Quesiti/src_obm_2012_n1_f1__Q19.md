# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2012 — Nível 1 — Fase 1 — Quesito 19

*Different rectangular hollow desk arrangements seating 30 students*

> In a classroom, the teacher arranged desks to form one large rectangular desk with a rectangular hole in the middle (as shown). For example, using small desks of equal size, a large desk with 10 small desks on the perimeter can be formed. In how many different ways can the teacher arrange all 30 student desks in such a hollow rectangular formation, so that each of the 30 students can sit at a small desk on the perimeter?
> 
> (A) 3 \quad (B) 6 \quad (C) 7 \quad (D) 8 \quad (E) 15
