*Count integers from 1 to 2003 with an even number of positive divisors*

> Among the integers from $1$ to $2003$, how many have an even number of positive divisors? For example, $6$ has positive divisors $1, 2, 3, 6$ (four in total), so $6$ satisfies this condition.
