*Min cyclic quadrilaterals from 40 points on two intersecting circles*

> Two circles intersect at two points $A$ and $B$. Let $X$ be a point on segment $AB$. Ten lines pass through $X$, each cutting both circles, yielding forty intersection points in total (four per line).
> 
> What is the minimum number of cyclic quadrilaterals whose four vertices are among these forty points?
> 
> **Note:** A quadrilateral is cyclic if and only if there exists a circle passing through its four vertices.
