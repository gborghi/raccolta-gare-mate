*Prove min{|A|,|B|} bounded by log₂n when ab+1 always a perfect square*

> Given an integer $n \geq 3$. Let $A$, $B \subseteq \{1, 2, \ldots, n\}$. Suppose that $ab + 1$ is a perfect square number for any $a \in A$ and $b \in B$. Prove that $\min\{|A|, |B|\} \leq \log_2 n$.
> 
> (posed by Xiong Bin)
