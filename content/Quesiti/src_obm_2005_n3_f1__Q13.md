# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2005 — Nível 3 — Fase 1 — Quesito 13

*Length CD in triangle ABC with D on BC, AB=AD=2, BD=1, angle bisector condition*

> $D$ is a point belonging to side $BC$ of triangle $ABC$. Knowing that $AB = AD = 2$, $BD = 1$ and the angles $B\hat{A}D$ and $C\hat{A}D$ are congruent, then the measure of segment $CD$ is:
> (A) $\dfrac{3}{2}$ (B) $\dfrac{4}{3}$ (C) $\dfrac{5}{4}$ (D) $\dfrac{6}{5}$ (E) $\dfrac{7}{6}$
