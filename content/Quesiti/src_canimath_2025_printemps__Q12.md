# Coupe Animath Printemps 2025 — Quesito 12

*Find all n>=2 for which two cyclic-sum sets each cover n consecutive integers*

> Determine all strictly positive integers $n \geq 2$ for which there exist strictly positive integers $a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_n$ such that both sets
> $$\{a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_n\} \quad \text{and} \quad \{a_1+a_2,\, a_2+a_3,\, \ldots,\, a_{n-1}+a_n,\, a_n+a_1\}$$
> each consist of $n$ consecutive integers.
