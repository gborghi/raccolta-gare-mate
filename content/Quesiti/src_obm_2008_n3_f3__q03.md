*Minimum of symmetric rational expression under constraint x+y+z=xy+yz+zx*

> Let $x, y, z$ be real numbers satisfying $x + y + z = xy + yz + zx$. Find the minimum value of
> $$\frac{x}{x^2+1} + \frac{y}{y^2+1} + \frac{z}{z^2+1}.$$
