*Right triangle ABC: find points D with two distance conditions*

> The triangle $ABC$ is right-angled at $C$. Find all the points $D$ in the plane satisfying the conditions
> $$AD \cdot BC = AC \cdot BD = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}} AB \cdot CD.$$
