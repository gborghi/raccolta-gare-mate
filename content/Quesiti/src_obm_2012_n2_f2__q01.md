*Knight minimum moves to reach any square on 5x5 board*

> Jo\~ao likes to verify properties of the chess knight on a $5 \times 5$ board. In one of his experiments, Jo\~ao places a knight in the lower-left square of the $5 \times 5$ board. What is the minimum number of moves the knight needs to make so that it can reach any square of the $5 \times 5$ board?
> 
> *Note:* The knight moves in an $L$-shape: two squares in one direction and then one square in a perpendicular direction, as illustrated in the figure.
