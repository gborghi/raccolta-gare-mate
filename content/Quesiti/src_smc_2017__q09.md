*An n×(n+1) rectangle is tiled by k×(k+1) rectangles for k=1 to 8; find n.*

> The diagram shows an $n \times (n+1)$ rectangle tiled with $k \times (k+1)$ rectangles, where $n$ and $k$ are integers and $k$ takes each value from $1$ to $8$ inclusive.
> 
> What is the value of $n$?
>
> - **(A)** $16$
> - **(B)** $15$
> - **(C)** $14$
> - **(D)** $13$
> - **(E)** $12$
