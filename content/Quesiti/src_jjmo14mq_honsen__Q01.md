*Altitude feet, two circumcircles, midpoint of chord*

> Let $ABC$ be an acute triangle. Let $D$, $E$, $F$ be the feet of the altitudes from $A$, $B$, $C$ to sides $BC$, $CA$, $AB$, respectively. Let $G$ be the intersection of line $AD$ with line $EF$. Among the intersections of the circumcircle of triangle $DFG$ with side $AB$, let $P$ be the one that is not $F$. Among the intersections of the circumcircle of triangle $DEG$ with side $AC$, let $Q$ be the one that is not $E$. Prove that line $DG$ bisects segment $PQ$.
