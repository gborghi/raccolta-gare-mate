*Cevians from interior point; express sum a+b+c as product*

> Let $P$ be a point inside a triangle $ABC$. The rays $AP$, $BP$, $CP$ intersect $BC$, $CA$, $AB$ at $A'$, $B'$, $C'$ respectively. Set $a = AP/PA'$, $b = BP/PB'$, $c = CP/PC'$. Express the product of the sum $a + b + c$.
