*Prove an inequality for real numbers with equality condition*

> Prove that if $a_0 = 0$, $a_1$, $a_2$, $\ldots$, $a_n$ are real numbers, then
> $$\sum_{i=1}^{n} a_i(a_{i-1} - a_i) \le \tfrac{1}{4}\sum_{i=1}^{n}(a_i - a_{i-1})^2,$$
> equality holding if and only if $a_i = a_n$ $(0 \le i \le n).$
