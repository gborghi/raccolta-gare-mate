*Find all sizes of subsets of Z/99Z closed under midpoints*

> Let $S$ be a subset of $m$ elements of $\{0, 1, 2, \ldots, 99\}$, $m \ge 3$, such that for any $x, y \in S$, there exists $z \in S$ with $x + y \equiv 2z \pmod{99}$. Find all possible values of $m$.
