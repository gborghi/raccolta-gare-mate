*Ben, Cam and Dan each make one statement about who is telling the truth. Exactly one of the three is lying. Which of the three is lying?*

> Three friends make the following statements. Ben says, "Exactly one of Dan and Cam is telling the truth." Dan says, "Exactly one of Ben and Cam is telling the truth." Cam says, "Neither Ben nor Dan is telling the truth." Which of the three friends is lying?
>
> - **(A)** Just Ben
> - **(B)** Just Cam
> - **(C)** Just Dan
> - **(D)** Each of Ben and Cam
> - **(E)** Each of Ben and Dan
