*Prove inequality (1+x)(1+y)(1+z) ≤ 4+4xyz for x²+y²+z²-2xyz=1*

> Let $x, y, z$ be real numbers such that $x^2 + y^2 + z^2 - 2xyz = 1$. Prove that
> $$(1+x)(1+y)(1+z) \le 4 + 4xyz.$$

*Appears in regions: Mumbai, Region 5.*
