*Inequality for positive reals with abc=1*

> Let $a$, $b$, $c$ be positive real numbers such that $abc = 1$. Prove that
> $$\frac{1}{a^3(b+c)} + \frac{1}{b^3(c+a)} + \frac{1}{c^3(a+b)} \ge \frac{3}{2}.$$
