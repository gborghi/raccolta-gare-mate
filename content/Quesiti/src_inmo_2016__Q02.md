*Which conditions on positive reals imply a=b=c*

> For positive real numbers $a, b, c$, which of the following statements necessarily implies $a = b = c$:
> (I) $a(b^3 + c^3) = b(c^3 + a^3)$,
> (II) $a(b^3 + c^3) = c(a^3 + b^3)$,
> (III) $a^2(b + c) = b^2(c + a) = c^2(a + b)$?
> Justify your answer.
