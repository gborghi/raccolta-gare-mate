*Maximize xyz subject to a symmetric radical constraint with x,y,z in (0,1)*

> Given $x$, $y$, $z \in (0, 1)$ satisfying $\sqrt{\frac{1-x}{yz}} + \sqrt{\frac{1-y}{zx}} + \sqrt{\frac{1-z}{xy}} = 2$, find the maximum value of $xyz$. (Posed by Tang Lihua)
