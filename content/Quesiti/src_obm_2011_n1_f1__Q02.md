*Cheapest way to buy 1.2 kg of CHOCOBM*

> (B) 1 can of 400 g and 1 can of 800 g
> (C) 4 cans of 200 g and 1 can of 400 g
> (D) 2 cans of 200 g and 1 can of 800 g
