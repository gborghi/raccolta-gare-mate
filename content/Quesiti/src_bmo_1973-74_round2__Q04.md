# British Mathematical Olympiad 1973-74 — Round 2 — Quesito 4

*Cube roots of distinct primes not in AP; or find x²+y²+z² given sum and sum of cubes*

> EITHER
> 
> (a) Show that the cube roots of three distinct prime numbers cannot be three terms (not necessarily consecutive) of an arithmetic progression. (USA)
> 
> OR
> 
> (b) $x + y + z = 3$, $x^3 + y^3 + z^3 = 15$. Being given that $x^2 + y^2 + z^2$ is less than 10, find $x^2 + y^2 + z^2$. (CZR, modified)
