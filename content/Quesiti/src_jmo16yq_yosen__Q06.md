*Count ways to color 3×3 grid red/blue avoiding monochromatic 2×2 squares*

> A $3 \times 3$ grid has each cell colored either red or blue. In how many ways can this be done so that:
> \begin{itemize}
> \item No $2 \times 2$ subgrid is entirely red.
> \item No $2 \times 2$ subgrid is entirely blue.
> \end{itemize}
> Colorings that differ by rotation or reflection are counted as distinct.
