*Three overlapping semicircles each of area 24; centres X, Y, Z with ∠ZXY = 30°; find total shaded area*

>
> - **(A)** $12$
> - **(B)** $6\sqrt{3}$
> - **(C)** $15$
> - **(D)** $18$
> - **(E)** $8\sqrt{3}$
