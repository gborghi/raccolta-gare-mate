# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2008 — Nível Universitário — Fase 2 — Quesito 3

*Sequence recurrence: bounded subsequence from infinite arithmetic progression*

> Given $n,a_1,a_2,\ldots,a_n$ positive integers, define $q_0=1$, $q_1=a_1$, and $q_{i+1}=a_{i+1}q_i+q_{i-1}$ for $1\le i\le n-1$.
> 
> Prove that, for every $c>1$, there exists $K>0$ such that for every $M>K$ there exist $n$ a positive integer and $a_1,a_2,\ldots,a_n$ belonging to $\{1,2\}$ such that $M\le q_n < c\cdot M$.
