*Assign 1 to 2n to vertices of 2n-gon; adjacent difference bounds*

> Let $n \geq 2$ be an integer. Assign the integers $1, 2, \ldots, 2n$ to the vertices of a regular $2n$-gon, one integer per vertex, each integer used exactly once.
> 
> (1) Show that there exists an assignment such that the absolute difference of the integers assigned to any two adjacent vertices is at least $n - 1$.
> 
> (2) Show that there is no assignment such that the absolute difference of the integers assigned to any two adjacent vertices is at least $n$.
