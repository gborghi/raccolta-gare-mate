*Bound f(x)<3/2 from ODE with initial conditions*

> Given $f : \mathbb{R} \to [0, +\infty)$ twice differentiable with $f(0) = 0$, $f'(0) = 1$, and $1 + f(x) = \dfrac{1}{f'(x)}$, show that for all $x \in [0, 1]$,
> $$f(x) < \frac{3}{2}.$$
