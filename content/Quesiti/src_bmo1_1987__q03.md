*Find limiting value of ratio of sums involving binomial coefficients*

> Find, with proof, the value of the limit as $n \to \infty$ of
> $$\frac{\displaystyle\sum_{r=0}^{n} \binom{2n}{r}^{-1} \cdot 2^r}{\displaystyle\sum_{r=0}^{n} \binom{2n}{r+1}^{-1} \cdot 2^r}.$$
> Here $\binom{2n}{r}$ denotes a binomial coefficient.
