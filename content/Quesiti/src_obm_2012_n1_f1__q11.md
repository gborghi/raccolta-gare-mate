*Weight of Juquinha's purchase given three system-of-equations clues*

> In a hardware store, various products are sold by weight. One nail, five bolts, and two nuts weigh 24 g. Two nails, five bolts, and four nuts weigh 44 g. Five nails, 32 bolts, and five nuts weigh 200 g. Juquinha bought 12 nails, 32 bolts, and five nuts. How much did his purchase weigh?
> 
> (A) 200 g \quad (B) 208 g \quad (C) 256 g \quad (D) 272 g \quad (E) 280 g

Cinque chiodi, 32 bulloni e cinque noci pesano 200 g. Juquinha ha comprato 12 chiodi, 32 bulloni e cinque noci. Quanto pesava l'acquisto?

> (A) 200 g \quad (B) 208 g \quad (C) 256 g \quad (D) 272 g \quad (E) 280 g
