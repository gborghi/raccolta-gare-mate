*Number of times the string '21' appears when the integers 1 to 999 are concatenated.*

> Esmeralda wrote (correctly!) all the numbers from 1 to 999, one after the other:
> $$1234567891011213\dots 997998999.$$
> How many times does the grouping "21" appear, in this order?
> 
> (A) 11 (B) 21 (C) 31 (D) 41 (E) 51
