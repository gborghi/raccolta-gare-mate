*Integers with prime factors at most 5 with x^2-y^2=2^k*

> Let $x$ and $y$ be positive integers with no prime factors larger than 5. Find all such $x$ and $y$ which satisfy
> $$x^2 - y^2 = 2^k$$
> for some non-negative integer $k$.
