*Prove a0 and a1 consecutive given a2023 and a2024 consecutive*

> The sequence $a_0, a_1, \ldots$ has the property that for each $i \ge 2$, $a_i$ is either $2a_{i-1} - a_{i-2}$ or $2a_{i-2} - a_{i-1}$.
> 
> Given that $a_{2023}$ and $a_{2024}$ are consecutive integers, prove that $a_0$ and $a_1$ are consecutive integers.
> 
> (Note that 6 and 7 are consecutive integers, as are 7 and 6.)
