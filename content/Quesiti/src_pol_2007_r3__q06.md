*Recursively defined real sequence via a weighted sum equation starting at a0=-1; prove all later terms positive.*

> The sequence of real numbers $a_0, a_1, a_2, \ldots$ is defined by $a_0 = -1$ and
> $$a_n + \frac{a_{n-1}}{2} + \frac{a_{n-2}}{3} + \cdots + \frac{a_1}{n} + \frac{a_0}{n+1} = 0 \quad \text{for } n \ge 1.$$
> Show that $a_n > 0$ for $n \ge 1$.
