*Find radius of circle C3 tangent to two parallel lines and two circles C1,C2*

> The figure below shows two parallel lines $r$ and $s$. The line $r$ is tangent to circles $C_1$ and $C_3$; the line $s$ is tangent to circles $C_2$ and $C_3$; and the circles also touch each other as shown in the figure.
> 
> The circles $C_1$ and $C_2$ have radii $a$ and $b$, respectively. What is the radius of circle $C_3$?
> 
> A) $2\sqrt{a^2 + b^2}$ \quad B) $a + b$ \quad C) $2\sqrt{ab}$ \quad D) $\dfrac{4ab}{a+b}$ \quad E) $2b - a$
