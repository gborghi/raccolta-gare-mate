*Nine ordered numbers: middle is the mean of all; find total sum*

> Nine numbers are written in increasing order. The middle number is the arithmetic mean of the nine numbers. The arithmetic mean of the $5$ largest is $68$ and the arithmetic mean of the $5$ smallest is $44$. The sum of all the numbers is:
> 
> (A) $500$ (B) $504$ (C) $112$ (D) $56$ (E) $70$
