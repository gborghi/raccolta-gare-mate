*Triangle with midpoint D and projections P, Q onto AD; prove BE=AE+AC iff AD=PQ*

> In a triangle $ABC$ with $AB > AC$, $D$ is the midpoint of $BC$ and $E$ is an arbitrary point on side $AC$. Points $P$ and $Q$ are the orthogonal projections of $B$ and $E$ onto $AD$, respectively. Show that $BE = AE + AC$ if and only if $AD = PQ$.
