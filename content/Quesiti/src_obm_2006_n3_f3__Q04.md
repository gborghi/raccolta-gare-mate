*Find smallest positive integer that is 'thrown' (arranjado)*

> A positive integer is called \emph{thrown} (arrojado) when it has 8 positive divisors whose sum is 3240. For example, 2006 is thrown because its 8 positive divisors are $1, 2, 17, 34, 59, 118, 1003, 2006$, and their sum is 3240. Find the smallest thrown positive integer.
