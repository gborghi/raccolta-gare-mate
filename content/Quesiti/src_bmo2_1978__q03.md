*Find all roots of a given sum with sign-alternating binomial-like terms*

> Determine with proof all the roots of the equation
> $$\sum_{r=0}^{n} (-1)^{n-r} \frac{x(x-1)(x-2)\cdots(x-r+1)}{1 \cdot 2 \cdot 3 \cdots r} = \frac{1}{2}$$
> where $n$ is a given positive integer.
