*Find points in plane satisfying distance conditions from right-angled triangle*

> The triangle $ABC$ is right-angled at $C$. Find all the points $D$ in the plane satisfying the conditions
> $$AD \cdot BC = BD \cdot AC = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}} AB \cdot CD .$$
