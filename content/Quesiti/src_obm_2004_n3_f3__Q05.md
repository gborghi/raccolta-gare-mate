*Sequence with a_n·a_{n+4}=a_{n+1}a_{n+3}+a_{n+2}^2 and initial 1s; show all terms are integers*

> Consider the sequence $(a_n)_{n \ge 0}$ with $a_0 = a_1 = a_2 = a_3 = 1$ and
> $$a_n a_{n+4} = a_{n+1} a_{n+3} + a_{n+2}^2.$$
> Show that all terms of this sequence are integers.
