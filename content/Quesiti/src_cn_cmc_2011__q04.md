*Max ratio |MN|/|AB| for chord AB of parabola with fixed angle AFB = pi/3*

> Let the focus and directrix of parabola $y^2 = 2px$ ($p > 0$) be $F$ and $l$, respectively. $A$ and $B$ are moving points on the parabola satisfying $\angle AFB = \frac{\pi}{3}$. Let the projection of $M$ the midpoint of segment $AB$ on $l$ be $N$. Then the maximum value of $\frac{|MN|}{|AB|}$ is __________.
