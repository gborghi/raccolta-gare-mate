*Find all sets of digits appearing an odd number of times in 1 to 2016*

> The integers $1, 2, 3, \ldots, 2016$ are written down in base $10$, appearing one after another. Each of the digits from $0$ to $9$ appears many times. A digit $d$ is called odd if the number of times $d$ appears in the list $1, 2, 3, \ldots, 2016$ is odd. Find all possibilities for the set of odd digits.
