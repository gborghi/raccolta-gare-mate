*Find minimum of sum of absolute deviations from distinct real numbers*

> Given distinct real numbers $a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_n$, find the minimum value of the function
> $$y = |x - a_1| + |x - a_2| + \cdots + |x - a_n|, \quad x \in \mathbb{R}.$$
