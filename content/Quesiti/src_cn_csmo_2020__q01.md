*Permutation of 1..17 with cyclic product equal to 2^t, find max t*

> Let $a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_{17}$ be a permutation of $1, 2, \ldots, 17$, satisfying that $(a_1 - a_2)(a_2 - a_3) \cdots (a_{16} - a_{17})(a_{17} - a_1) = 2^t$. Find the maximum value of the integer $t$.
