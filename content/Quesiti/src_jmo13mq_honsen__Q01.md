*Find angle BRC in triangle ABC with equal-length conditions*

> Let $P$ be a point inside triangle $ABC$. Let $Q$ be the intersection of line $BP$ with side $AC$, and let $R$ be the intersection of line $CP$ with side $AB$. Suppose that
> $$AR = RB = CP \quad \text{and} \quad CQ = PQ.$$
> Find the measure of $\angle BRC$.
> 
> (Here, for two points $X$ and $Y$, the length of segment $XY$ is denoted by $XY$.)
