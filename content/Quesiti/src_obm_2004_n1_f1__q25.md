*Height of a pile of 100 new notes after salary conversion*

> Between 1986 and 1989, Brazil experienced massive inflation and the currency was changed (Cruzado, Crz\$). The conversion between the old cruzeiro and the real is: $1\,\text{real} = 2{,}750{,}000{,}000\,\text{cruzeiros}$. Imagine that João earns a salary of $264$ km per month in new notes and that he now receives his salary entirely in new notes of $1$ cruzeiro. If a pile of $100$ new notes has a height of $1.5$ cm, what is the height, in kilometers, of João's salary pile?
> 
> (A) $26.4$ km \quad (B) $264$ km \quad (C) $26{,}400$ km \quad (D) $264{,}000$ km \quad (E) $2{,}640{,}000$ km
