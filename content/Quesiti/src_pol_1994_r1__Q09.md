*2n participants each acquainted with >=n others; pair into n double rooms by acquaintance*

> There are $2n$ participants in a conference. Each of the persons is acquainted with at least $n$ other persons. Prove that it is possible to accomodate the participants in $n$ double rooms so that each of them would share a room with his/her acquaintance.
