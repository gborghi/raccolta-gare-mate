# Japan Mathematical Olympiad 2013 — Yosen (Preliminary) — Quesito 12

*Sequence with fixed sum of consecutive ratios; minimal constant c*

> Let $a_1, a_2, \ldots$ be an infinite sequence of distinct nonzero reals such that $\dfrac{a_{i+1}}{a_i} + \dfrac{a_{i+1}}{a_{i+2}}$ takes a single fixed value, greater than $0$ and less than $2$, for all positive integers $i$. Find, in terms of $a_1, a_2, a_3$, the minimum value of a real number $c$ for which the following condition holds. Condition: for all positive integers $x, y$ with $x < y$,
> $$\frac{a_x a_{x+1} + a_{x+1} a_{x+2} + \cdots + a_{y-1} a_y}{a_x a_y} \le c$$
> holds.
