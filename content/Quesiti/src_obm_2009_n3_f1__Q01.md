# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2009 — Nível 3 — Fase 1 — Quesito 1

*Count 3-digit numbers that 'detonate' 314 (every digit strictly greater).*

> A three-digit natural number $A$ \textit{detonates} a three-digit natural number $B$ if each digit of $A$ is greater than the corresponding digit of $B$. For example, $876$ detonates $345$; however, $651$ does not detonate $542$ because $1 < 2$. How many three-digit numbers detonate $314$? (A) $120$ (B) $240$ (C) $360$ (D) $480$ (E) $600$
