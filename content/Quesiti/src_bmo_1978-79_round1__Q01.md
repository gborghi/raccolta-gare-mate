# British Mathematical Olympiad 1978-79 — Round 1 — Quesito 1

*Find triangles ABC with AB+AC=2 and altitude AD=sqrt(AB·AC)*

> Find all triangles $ABC$ for which
> $$AB + AC = 2 \text{ cm} \quad \text{and} \quad AD = \sqrt{AB \cdot AC} \text{ cm},$$
> where $AD$ is the altitude from $A$, meeting $BC$ at right angles in $D$.
