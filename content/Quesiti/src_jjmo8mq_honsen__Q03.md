# Japan Junior Mathematical Olympiad 2010 — Honsen (Final) — Quesito 3

*Five segments: 9 acute triples force the 10th to be a triangle*

> There are $5$ line segments. There are $10$ ways to choose $3$ of them, and in $9$ of these ways the $3$ chosen segments can be used as the sides of an acute triangle. Show that, for the remaining $1$ way, the $3$ chosen segments can also be used as the sides of a triangle.
