*Minimum of sum of squares under three product equations*

> Real numbers $a, b, c, d$ satisfy
> $$\begin{cases}(a+b)(c+d) = 2,\\ (a+c)(b+d) = 3,\\ (a+d)(b+c) = 4.\end{cases}$$
> Find the minimum possible value of $a^2 + b^2 + c^2 + d^2$.
