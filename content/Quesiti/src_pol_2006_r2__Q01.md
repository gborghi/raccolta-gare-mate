*Given positive integers a,b,c and reals x,y,z with |x-a|<=1, |y-b|<=1 and a^2+b^2=c^2, x^2+y^2=z^2, prove the sets {a,b} and {x,y} coincide.*

> Positive integers $a,b,c$ and $x,y,z$ satisfy $|x-a|\le 1$, $|y-b|\le 1$, and
> $$a^2+b^2=c^2, \quad x^2+y^2=z^2.$$
> Prove that the sets $\{a,b\}$ and $\{x,y\}$ coincide.
