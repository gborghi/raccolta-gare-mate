*Show the polynomial sum has no integer solutions in a, b*

> Show that $$a^2 + (a+1)^2 + (a+2)^2 + (a+3)^2 + (a+4)^2 + (a+5)^2 + (a+6)^2 = b^2 + (b+1)^2$$ has no solutions in integers $a$, $b$.
