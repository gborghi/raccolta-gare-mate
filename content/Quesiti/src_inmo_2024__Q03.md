# Indian National Mathematical Olympiad 2024 — Quesito 3

*Odd prime p divides sum of powers of integers a,b,c*

> Let $p$ be an odd prime number and $a, b, c$ be integers so that the integers
> $$a^{2021} + b^{2023},\quad a^{2023} + c^{2021},\quad a^{2025} + c^{2025}$$
> are all divisible by $p$. Prove that $p$ divides each of $a, b, c$.
