*Length AX in nested isosceles right triangles*

> Triangle $ABC$ is a right isosceles triangle with $\angle A = 90^\circ$. Inside it, three points $X, Y, Z$ are chosen so that triangle $XYZ$ is a right isosceles triangle with $\angle X = 90^\circ$, and moreover the points $A, Y, X$ are collinear in this order, $B, Z, Y$ are collinear in this order, and $C, X, Z$ are collinear in this order. Given $AB = 1$ and $XY = \frac{1}{4}$, find the length of segment $AX$. Here $ST$ denotes the length of segment $ST$.
