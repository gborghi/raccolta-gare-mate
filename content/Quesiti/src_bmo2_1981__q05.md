*A sequence of real numbers is defined recursively for n >= 0; prove that every term is an integer.*

> The sequence $\{u_n\}$ of real numbers is defined for $n \ge 0$ by
> $$u_0 = 2, \quad u_1 = 5$$
> and
> $$u_{n+1} u_{n-1} - u_n^2 = 6^{n-1} \quad \text{when } n \ge 1.$$
> Prove that each $u_n$ is an integer.
