*Find positive integers n and k satisfying a radical equation*

> Find positive integers $n$ and $k$ such that $\left(\sqrt{6} + \sqrt{5} - 1\right)^{49} + \left(\sqrt{6} + \sqrt{5} + 1\right)^{49} = 49 + 2\sqrt{6k}$.
