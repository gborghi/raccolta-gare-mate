*Square divided into 4 congruent trapezoids rearranged into square with central hole; fraction of area removed*

>
> - **(A)** $\dfrac{1}{9}$
> - **(B)** $\dfrac{2}{9}$
> - **(C)** $\dfrac{1}{4}$
> - **(D)** $\dfrac{1}{3}$
> - **(E)** $\dfrac{3}{8}$
