# Japan Mathematical Olympiad 2014 — Honsen (Final) — Quesito 4

*Prove a circle through four points is tangent to BC*

> Let $\Gamma$ be the circumcircle of triangle $ABC$, and let $\ell$ be the tangent line to $\Gamma$ at point $A$. Let $D$ and $E$ be points on sides $AB$ and $AC$ respectively, distinct from the endpoints, satisfying $BD : DA = AE : EC$. Let $F$ and $G$ be the two intersection points of line $DE$ with $\Gamma$. Let $H$ be the intersection of $\ell$ with the line through $D$ parallel to $AC$, and let $I$ be the intersection of $\ell$ with the line through $E$ parallel to $AB$. Prove that the four points $F, G, H, I$ lie on a common circle, and that this circle is tangent to line $BC$. Here, $XY$ denotes the length of segment $XY$.
