*In triangle ABC with AC+BC=3AB, the incircle (center I) touches BC at D and AC at E; K, L are reflections of D, E in I. Prove A, B, K, L are concyclic.*

> In a triangle $ABC$ with $AC+BC=3AB$, the incircle is centered at $I$ and touches $BC$ at $D$ and $AC$ at $E$. Let $K$ and $L$ be the points symmetric to $D$ and $E$ with respect to $I$. Prove that the points $A,B,K,L$ lie on a circle.
