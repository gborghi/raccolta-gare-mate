*Three consecutive naturals a<b<c; simplify c^2 - ab*

> The numbers $a$, $b$, $c$ are consecutive natural numbers in increasing order (so $b = a+1$, $c = a+2$). Then the value of $c^2 - ab$ is equal to:
> 
> (A) 0 \quad (B) 1 \quad (C) $2a + b$ \quad (D) $2a + c$ \quad (E) $2b + c$
