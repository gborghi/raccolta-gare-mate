*Count team formations for eight people into four unequal teams*

> The seven dwarfs decide to form four teams to compete in the Millennium Quiz. Of course, the sizes of the teams will be unequal. For instance, one team of Dopey alone, one of Sleepy, Happy and Grumpy, and one of Bashful and Sneezy, and one of Doc (the order of the teams does not matter, but each dwarf must be in exactly one team).
> 
> Suppose Snow White agreed to take part as well. In how many ways could the four teams then be formed?
