*Coin-flip game with three players; who has smallest win probability*

> Beatriz, Isabele, and Nicole are playing a game taking turns flipping a fair coin. Beatriz wins if, in two consecutive flips, both are heads. Nicole wins if, in two consecutive flips, both are tails. Isabele wins if in two consecutive flips one is heads and one is tails. They flip the coin repeatedly in order (Beatriz, then Isabele, then Nicole, then Beatriz, ...) until someone wins. Which player(s) has (have) the smallest probability of winning?
> 
> (A) Beatriz \quad (B) Isabele \quad (C) Nicole \quad (D) Beatriz and Nicole \quad (E) All three have the same probability.
