*Logic puzzle: who paid museum entry*

> Four friends want to visit a museum and one of them decides to pay the entry for all. A tax officer who wants to know who paid for entry questions them:
> 
> - "It wasn't me," says Benjamim. — "It was Carlos," says Mário.
> - "It was Pedro," says Carlos. — "Mário is wrong, it was Pedro," says Pedro.
> 
> Only one of them is lying. Who did NOT pay the entry for the museum?
> 
> (A) Mário \quad (B) Pedro \quad (C) Benjamim \quad (D) Carlos \quad (E) insufficient data
