*Polynomial with coefficients in {-1,0,1}, max number of positive integer roots*

> Let $f(x) = x^{2020} + \sum_{k=0}^{2019} c_k x^k$, $c_k \in \{-1, 0, 1\}$ be a polynomial, and $N$ be the number of positive integer roots of $f(x)$ (counted with their multiplicities). Given that $f(x)$ has no negative integer roots, find the maximum value of $N$.
