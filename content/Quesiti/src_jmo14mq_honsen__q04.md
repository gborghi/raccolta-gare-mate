*Prove sum inequality for positive reals with a+b+c=1*

> For positive real numbers $a$, $b$, $c$ satisfying $a + b + c = 1$, prove that
> $$\frac{1+a}{1-a} + \frac{1+b}{1-b} + \frac{1+c}{1-c} \le 2\left(\frac{b}{a} + \frac{c}{b} + \frac{a}{c}\right).$$
> (You do not need to state the conditions for equality.)
