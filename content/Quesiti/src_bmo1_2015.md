- [[src_bmo1_2015__Q01|Quesito 1]] — Order five power-tower numbers by size and justify.
- [[src_bmo1_2015__Q02|Quesito 2]] — Given p^2+a^2=b^2 with p prime greater than 3, prove a is a multiple of 12 and 2(p+a+1) is a perfect square.
- [[src_bmo1_2015__Q03|Quesito 3]] — Count ways a team can book seven rooms along a corridor with ten rooms per side so no two reserved rooms on the same side are adjacent.
- [[src_bmo1_2015__Q04|Quesito 4]] — With t=x+x^{-1} an integer greater than 2 and t_n=x^n+x^{-n}, prove t_n is always an integer and determine n for which t divides t_n.
- [[src_bmo1_2015__Q05|Quesito 5]] — In a cyclic quadrilateral with F the midpoint of arc AB not containing C or D, lines DF,AC meet at P and CF,BD meet at Q; prove PQ is parallel to AB.
- [[src_bmo1_2015__Q06|Quesito 6]] — Determine all functions f from the positive integers to the positive integers such that 1/a+1/b=1/c implies 1/f(a)+1/f(b)=1/f(c).
