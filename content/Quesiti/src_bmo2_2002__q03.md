*Sequence defined by recurrence consists only of integers*

> Prove that the sequence defined by
> $$b_0 = 1, \quad b_1 = 1, \quad b_{n+1} = b_n^2 - b_{n-1} \quad (n \ge 1)$$
> consists only of integers.
