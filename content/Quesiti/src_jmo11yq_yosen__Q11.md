*Count 3-digit n with S(6n) >= 12 S(n)*

> For a positive integer $n$, let $S(n)$ denote the sum of the positive divisors of $n$. How many three-digit positive integers $n$ satisfy $S(6n) \ge 12\,S(n)$?
