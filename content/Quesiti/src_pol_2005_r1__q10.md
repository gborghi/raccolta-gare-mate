*Three random subsets of an n-set; find the most probable size of their intersection.*

> Three subsets $A, B, C$ of a given $n$-element set $X$ have been chosen at random. It is assumed that each of the $2^n$ subsets of $X$ is equiprobable. Find the most probable number of elements of the set $A \cap B \cap C$.
