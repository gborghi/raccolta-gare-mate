*Digits a,b,c with prime two-digit numbers summing to aaa*

> The digits $a$, $b$ and $c$ are such that the two-digit numbers $\overline{aa}$, $\overline{bc}$ and $\overline{cb}$ are prime numbers, and $\overline{aa}+\overline{bc}+\overline{cb}=\overline{aaa}$. If $c<a$, then $\overline{bc}$ is equal to:
> (A) $19$ \quad (B) $17$ \quad (C) $37$ \quad (D) $29$ \quad (E) $59$
