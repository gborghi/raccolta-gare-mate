*Circle through A..E, diameter AB perpendicular to chord CD, angle BCE=35; find angle DAE*

> 
> (A) $35^\circ$ \quad (B) $10^\circ$ \quad (C) $20^\circ$ \quad (D) $30^\circ$ \quad (E) $55^\circ$
