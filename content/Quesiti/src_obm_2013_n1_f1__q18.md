*Sum of perimeters of six integer-sided squares tiling a 144 cm^2 square*

> A square of area $144$ cm$^2$ can be decomposed into six squares with integer sides, not all equal. What is the sum of the perimeters of all six squares?
> 
> (A) $36$ cm \quad (B) $84$ cm \quad (C) $96$ cm \quad (D) $112$ cm \quad (E) $164$ cm
