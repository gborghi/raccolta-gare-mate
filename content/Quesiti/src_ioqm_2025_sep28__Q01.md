# Indian Olympiad Qualifier in Mathematics (IOQM) 2025 — Sep 28 — Quesito 1

*Isosceles trapezoid ABCD in xy-plane; find a^b*

> Let $ABCD$ be a quadrilateral in the $xy$-plane with $AB$ parallel to $CD$ and $AD = BC$. Suppose $A = (0, 0)$, $B = (10, 0)$, $C = (8, 5)$ and $D = (a, b)$. Determine the value of $a^b$.
