# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2021 — Nível 3 — Fase x — Quesito 1

*Four circumcenters of subtriangles of convex quadrilateral are concyclic*

> Let $ABCD$ be a convex quadrilateral in the plane, and let $O_1, O_2, O_3, O_4$ be the circumcenters of triangles $BCD$, $CDA$, $DAB$, and $ABC$, respectively. Suppose that these four circumcenters are pairwise distinct. Prove that these four points lie on a common circle.
