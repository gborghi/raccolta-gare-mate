*Count pairs {A,B} of subsets of {1,...,11} with A != B and a given intersection*

> Let $X = \{1, 2, 3, \ldots, 11\}$. Find the number of pairs $\{A, B\}$ such that $A \subseteq X$, $B \subseteq X$, $A \neq B$ and $A \cap B = \{4, 5, 7, 8, 9, 10\}$.
