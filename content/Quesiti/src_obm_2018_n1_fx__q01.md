*Square from four congruent rectangles; find side lengths*

> A square is formed by four congruent rectangles and a smaller square in its interior, as shown in the figure. The rectangles, in gray, each have a perimeter of 24 cm.
> 
> a) How many centimeters is the side of the larger square?
> 
> b) Consider the cases in which the side of the inner square is a whole number of centimeters. How many such measurements are there?
