*Minimum of e^{x/e}-x; compare e^pi and pi^e*

> (a) Find the minimum value of the function $f:\mathbb{R}\to\mathbb{R}$ given by $f(x)=e^{(x/e)}-x$ (here $e=2{,}71828\ldots$ is the base of the natural logarithm).
> 
> (b) Which of these numbers is larger: $e^\pi$ or $\pi^e$?
