*Sequence a_1=k+1, a_{n+1}=a_n²-4a_n+1; show distinct terms are coprime*

> A positive integer $k$ is given. The sequence $(a_n)$ is defined by
> $$a_1 = k + 1, \quad a_{n+1} = a_n^2 - 4a_n + 1 \quad \text{for } n \ge 1.$$
> Show that for $m \ne n$ the numbers $a_m, a_n$ are relatively prime.
