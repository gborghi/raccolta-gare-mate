*Find limiting value of product of two binomial-coefficient sums*

> Find, with proof, the value of
> $$\lim_{n \to \infty} \left(\sum_{r=0}^{n} \binom{2r}{r} \frac{1}{4^r}\right)^{-1} \sum_{r=0}^{n-1} \binom{2r}{r} \frac{1}{4^r}$$
> as $n \to \infty$.
> 
> Here $\dbinom{2r}{r}$ denotes a binomial coefficient.
