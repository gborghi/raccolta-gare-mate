*Length of AB given two tangent circles through A and B with PB=QB=1000*

> Two circles $C_1$ and $C_2$ intersect at points $A$ and $B$. The tangent to $C_1$ at $A$ meets $C_2$ again at $P$; the tangent to $C_2$ at $A$ meets $C_1$ again at $Q$. Given that $PB = QB = 1000$, determine the length of segment $AB$.
