*Acute triangle with semicircles on each side: prove area sum identity*

> In an acute-angled triangle $ABC$, a semicircle is constructed outwards on each side. Points $B'$ and $C'$ are defined analogously. Prove that
> $$S_{BCA'} + S_{CAB'} + S_{ABC'} = S_{ABC},$$
> where $S_{XYZ}$ denotes the area of triangle $XYZ$.
