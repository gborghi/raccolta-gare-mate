*Angle $\angle PYX$ for internally tangent circles with diameter tangent to inner circle*

> The circle $\omega$ touches the circle $\Omega$ internally at $P$. The centre of $\Omega$ is $O$. Let $XY$ be a diameter of $\Omega$ which is also tangent to $\omega$. Assume $PY > PX$. Let $PY$ intersect $\omega$ at $Z$. If $YZ = 2PZ$, what is the value of $\angle PYX$ in degrees?
