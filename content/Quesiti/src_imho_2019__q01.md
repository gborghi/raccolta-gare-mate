*Find all functions f:Z->Z satisfying f(2a)+2f(b)=f(f(a+b))*

> Let $\mathbb{Z}$ be the set of integers. Determine all functions $f : \mathbb{Z} \to \mathbb{Z}$ such that, for all integers $a$ and $b$,
> $$f(2a) + 2f(b) = f(f(a+b)).$$
