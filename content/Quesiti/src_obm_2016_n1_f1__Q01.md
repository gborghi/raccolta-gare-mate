*Evaluate (2016^2-1)/2015.*

> Compute the value of $\dfrac{2016^2-1}{2015}$.
> 
> [Source is the official answer key (GABARITO); the original multiple-choice statement and options are not reproduced. From the solution: $\dfrac{2016^2-1}{2015}=\dfrac{(2016+1)(2016-1)}{2015}=\dfrac{2017\times 2015}{2015}=2017$. Alternatively, $\dfrac{2016^2-1}{2015}=\dfrac{4064256-1}{2015}=\dfrac{4064255}{2015}=2017$.]
