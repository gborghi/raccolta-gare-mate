*Min interior points of 4x4 square hit by every inscribed unit disk*

> Let $ABCD$ be a square of side $4$. The set $S$ of points in the interior of $ABCD$ has the following property: every circle of radius $1$ that is entirely contained in $ABCD$ (including on its boundary) contains at least one point of $S$. What is the minimum number of points of $S$?
