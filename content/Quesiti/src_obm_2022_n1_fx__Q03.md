*Square midpoints inner square area; octagon cut into trapezoid angles*

> (a) Pedro built a square of side $2$ using four unit-side small squares. Connecting the midpoints of the sides of the larger square, Pedro built a highlighted square in the figure. Determine the area and the perimeter of the highlighted square.
> 
> (b) Pedro took a regular octagon $ABCDEFGH$ of side $1$. He cut it into $4$ parts and rearranged them to form the second figure. Note that the triangles $A_1BE_1$ and $A_2HE_2$ correspond to triangles $ABE$ and $AHE$, respectively.
> 
> Calculate the interior angles of trapezoid $BCDE$.
> 
> *You may use the fact that each interior angle of a regular polygon with $n$ sides has measure $\frac{(n-2) \cdot 180^\circ}{n}$.*
> 
> (c) Explain why the points $B$, $A_1$, $A_2$ and $H$ lie on the same straight line in the second figure.
> 
> (d) Determine the length of segment $E_1 E_2$.
