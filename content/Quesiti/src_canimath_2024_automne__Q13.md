# Coupe Animath Automne 2024 — Quesito 13

*Infinite integer sequence: k+l | a_k+a_l; show k−l | a_k−a_l for k≠l*

> Let $a_1, a_2, \ldots$ be an infinite sequence of integers. Suppose that for all integers $k \ge 1$ and $l \ge 1$, $k + l$ divides $a_k + a_l$. Show that for all integers $k \ge 1$ and $l \ge 1$ with $k \ne l$, $k - l$ divides $a_k - a_l$.
