*Basketball passed among four players, count ways ball returns to A after seven passes*

> There are four basket-ball players $A$, $B$, $C$, $D$. Initially, the ball is with $A$. The ball is always passed from one person to a different person. In how many ways can the ball come back to $A$ after seven passes? (For example $A \to C \to B \to D \to A \to B \to C \to A$ and $A \to D \to A \to D \to A \to B \to C \to A$ are two ways in which the ball can come back to $A$ after seven passes.)
