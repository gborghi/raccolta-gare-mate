*Cone of height 5cm filled with sand to 3cm, then inverted; find sand level height*

> Cicero's favorite toy is a straight glass cone with $5$ cm of height. Cicero filled the cone with sand up to a height of $3$ cm, as shown in figure 1. Then, Cicero closed the base of the cone and turned it upside down, as indicated in figure 2. At what height from the base of the cone, in cm, does the mark of sand stand?
> 
> (A) $1$ (B) $2$ (C) $5 - \sqrt[3]{98}$ (D) $\sqrt[3]{98}$ (E) $1 - \dfrac{\sqrt[3]{98}}{5}$
