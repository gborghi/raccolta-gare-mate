*Triangle ABC: prove x+y+z+xyz=0 for given tangent expressions*

> Given a triangle $ABC$, denote
> $$x = \tan\frac{B-C}{2}\tan\frac{A}{2}, \quad y = \tan\frac{C-A}{2}\tan\frac{B}{2}, \quad z = \tan\frac{A-B}{2}\tan\frac{C}{2}.$$
> Prove that $x + y + z + xyz = 0$.
