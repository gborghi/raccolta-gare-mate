# British Mathematical Olympiad 2015-16 — Round 2 — Quesito 4

*Prime p with p^2 = a^3 + b^2: prove divisibility or factorisation result*

> Suppose that $p$ is a prime number and that there are positive integers $a$ and $b$ such that $p^2 = a^3 + b^2$. Prove that there exist positive integers $u$ and $v$ such that $p = u^2 + v^3$ and $a = u^2$, $b = v^3$.
