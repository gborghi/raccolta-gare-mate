*Which expression is a perfect square for every positive integer n?*

> Which one of the following expressions is a square number for each positive integer $n$?
>
> - **(A)** $n + 1$
> - **(B)** $n(n + 2) + 1$
> - **(C)** $n(n + 2(n + 3) + 1)$
> - **(D)** $n(n+1)(n+2)(n+3) + 1$
> - **(E)** $n(n+1)(2n+3(n+4)+1)$
