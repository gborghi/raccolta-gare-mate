*Find min and max of f(23)+f(2011) for function satisfying a functional inequality*

> Consider functions $f$ defined on the positive integers taking positive integer values such that for any positive integers $x, y$,
> $$(x + y) f(x) \le x^2 + f(xy) + 110$$
> holds. Find the minimum and maximum possible values of $f(23) + f(2011)$.
