*Non-negative p,q,r satisfying p+q+r=1 imply inequality*

> Non-negative reals $p$, $q$ and $r$ satisfy $p + q + r = 1$. Prove that
> $$7(pq + qr + rp) \le 2 + 9pqr.$$
