*Grid paths from (0,0) to (7,12) avoiding points with both coordinates odd*

> In the plane let the positive end of the $x$-axis be directed towards East and the positive end of the $y$-axis be directed towards North. Suppose you are at $(0, 0)$ and you want to go to $(7, 12)$. At every move you are allowed to move unit length towards East or unit length towards North from your current position but you are not allowed to visit any point $(h, k)$ where both $h, k$ are odd. Find the number of such paths $n$.
