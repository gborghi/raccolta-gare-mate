*Maximum area of convex pentagon with all sides 1 and two perpendicular diagonals*

> A convex pentagon has all sides of length $1$, and there exist two diagonals of the pentagon that are perpendicular to each other (forming a $90^\circ$ angle). Find the maximum possible area of such a pentagon. (A convex pentagon is one in which all interior angles are less than $180^\circ$.)
