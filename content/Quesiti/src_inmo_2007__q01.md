*Right triangle, median bisects angle; prove ratio bound*

> In a triangle $ABC$ right-angled at $C$, the median through $B$ bisects the angle between $BA$ and the bisector of $\angle B$. Prove that
> $$\frac{5}{2} < \frac{AB}{BC} < 3.$$
