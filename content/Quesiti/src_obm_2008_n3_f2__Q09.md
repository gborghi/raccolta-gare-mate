# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2008 — Nível 3 — Fase 2 — Quesito 9

*Minimize product of 2008 entries of 2008×2008 matrix (i+j), one per row and column*

> In a $2008 \times 2008$ matrix, the element in row $i$ and column $j$ is the number $i + j$ (rows and columns are numbered from $1$ to $2008$). We choose $2008$ elements from this matrix such that no two chosen elements lie in the same row or the same column. The chosen elements are then multiplied together. What is the smallest possible product that can be obtained in this way?
