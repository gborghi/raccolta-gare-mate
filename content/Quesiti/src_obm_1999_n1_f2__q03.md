*Equilateral triangles ABC and EFG sharing vertices; find shaded area perimeter and fraction*

> In the figure, triangles $ABC$ and $EFG$ are equilateral. The perimeter of triangle $ABC$ is $132\,\text{cm}$ and, additionally,
> $$AE = EC,\quad BD = DC,\quad EF = FC,\quad DG = GE.$$
> 
> a) What is the perimeter of the shaded region?
> 
> b) What fraction of the area of triangle $ABC$ does the shaded area represent?
