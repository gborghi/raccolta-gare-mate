*Polynomial (x-a)^k Q(x) with a nonzero has at least k+1 nonzero coefficients*

> Consider the polynomial $W(x) = (x - a)^k Q(x)$, where $a \ne 0$, $Q(x)$ is a nonzero polynomial, and $k$ is a natural number. Prove that $W$ has at least $k + 1$ nonzero coefficients.
