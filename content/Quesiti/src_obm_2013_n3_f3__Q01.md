# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2013 — Nível 3 — Fase 3 — Quesito 1

*Tangent lines from exterior point, find CE in terms of a,b*

> Let $\Gamma$ be a circle and $A$ a point exterior to $\Gamma$. The tangent lines to $\Gamma$ passing through $A$ touch $\Gamma$ at $B$ and $C$. Let $M$ be the midpoint of $AB$. The segment $MC$ meets $\Gamma$ again at $D$, and the line $AD$ meets $\Gamma$ again at $E$. Given that $AB = a$ and $BC = b$, find $CE$ in terms of $a$ and $b$.
