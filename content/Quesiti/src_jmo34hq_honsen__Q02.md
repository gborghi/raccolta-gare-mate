# Japan Mathematical Olympiad 2024 — Honsen (Final) — Quesito 2

*Find all functions on positive integers satisfying an lcm functional equation*

> Find all functions $f$ defined on the positive integers taking positive integer values such that, for all positive integers $m$ and $n$,
> $$\mathrm{lcm}(f(m + f(n)),\, f(m)) = \mathrm{lcm}(f(m),\, f(m + n)).$$
> Here $\mathrm{lcm}(x, y)$ denotes the least common multiple of $x$ and $y$.
