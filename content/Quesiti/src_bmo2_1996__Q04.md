*Find positive reals satisfying two symmetric equations in four variables*

> Let $a$, $b$, $c$, $d$ be positive real numbers such that
> $$a + b + c + d = 12$$
> and
> $$abcd = 27 + ab + ac + ad + bc + bd + cd.$$
> Find all possible values of $a$, $b$, $c$, $d$ satisfying these equations.
