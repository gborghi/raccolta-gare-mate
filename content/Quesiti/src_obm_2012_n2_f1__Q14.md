*Distance traveled by a golf/soccer ball following a semicircular path*

> The great artilleryman Tornado is about to make the most beautiful shot of his career. He is in front of the goal, and plans that the ball, on leaving, will travel $2$ metres in a straight line up to a point, and then describe a semicircle, arriving $3$ metres of altitude over the solid ground at the goal. What is the distance traveled until the ball enters the goal?
> 
> A) 3 \quad B) 3.5 \quad C) 4 \quad D) 4.5 \quad E) 5
