# Japan Mathematical Olympiad 2024 — Yosen (Preliminary) — Quesito 2

*Find all 3-digit n where n+2024 and n-34 are both prime-digit numbers*

> Call a positive integer a **prime-digit number** (素敵な数) if every digit that appears in it is a prime digit. Find all 3-digit positive integers $n$ such that both $n + 2024$ and $n - 34$ are prime-digit numbers. (There are exactly two such $n$; find all of them.)
