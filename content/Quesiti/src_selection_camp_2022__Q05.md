*Bead-moving game on a line with ratio r; find r allowing red bead to reach B in at most 2021 moves*

> Let $r > 1$ be a rational number, and let $B$ and $R$ be two distinct points on a line. Initially, one blue bead is placed at $B$ and one red bead is placed at $R$. Taro repeatedly performs the following operation:
> 
> > Choose an integer $k$ and a point $X$ at which one of the beads is placed. Let $Y$ be the point where the other bead is placed. On the ray $YX$, choose the point $X'$ such that $YX' = r^k \cdot YX$. Then move the bead at $X$ to $X'$.
> 
> Find all values of $r$ such that Taro can move the red bead to $B$ in at most $2021$ operations. Here $PQ$ denotes the length of segment $PQ$.
