*Find natural numbers m and n with digit-sum conditions in base 10*

> For any natural number $n$, written in base 10, let $s(n)$ denote the sum of all its digits. Find all natural numbers $m$ and $n$ such that $m < n$ and
> $$(s(n))^2 = m \quad \text{and} \quad (s(m))^2 = n.$$
