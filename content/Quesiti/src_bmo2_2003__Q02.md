*Point D with 4AD=AB, half-line at angle ACB meets circumcircle at P, prove PB=2PD*

> Let $ABC$ be a triangle and let $D$ be a point on $AB$ such that $4AD = AB$. The half-line $\ell$ is drawn on the same side of $AB$ as $C$, starting from $D$, making an angle $\theta$ with $DA$ where $\theta = \angle ACB$. If the circumcircle of $ABC$ meets the half-line $\ell$ at $P$, show that $PB = 2PD$.
