*Logical deduction: which box holds the coin from ordering clues*

> On a table there are three boxes and three objects, each one in a different box: a coin, a staple, and an eraser. It is known that:
> - The green box is to the left of the blue box;
> - The coin is to the left of the eraser;
> - The red box is to the right of the staple;
> - The eraser is to the right of the red box.
> 
> In which box is the coin?
> 
> **A)** In the red box. $\quad$ **B)** In the green box. $\quad$ **C)** In the blue box. $\quad$ **D)** The information provided is insufficient to give an answer. $\quad$ **E)** The information provided is contradictory.
