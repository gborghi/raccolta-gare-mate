*Minimum Q+R+S where pairwise HCFs of Q, R, S are three different primes*

> The highest common factors of all the pairs chosen from the positive integers $Q$, $R$ and $S$ are three different primes.
> 
> What is the smallest possible value of $Q + R + S$?
>
> - **(A)** 41
> - **(B)** 31
> - **(C)** 30
> - **(D)** 21
> - **(E)** 10
