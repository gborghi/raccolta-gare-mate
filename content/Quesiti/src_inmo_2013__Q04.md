*Good nonempty subsets of {1,2,...,n}; prove t_n and n are both odd or both even*

> Let $n$ be a positive integer. Call a nonempty subset $S$ of $\{1, 2, \ldots, n\}$ good if the arithmetic mean of the elements of $S$ is also an integer. Let $t_n$ denote the number of good subsets of $\{1, 2, \ldots, n\}$. Prove that $t_n$ and $n$ are both odd or both even.
