# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2005 — Nível 2 — Fase 2 — Quesito 3

*Staircase grid of numbered squares; count tape strips used*

> Juliana was writing positive integers in paper squares glued side by side in a staircase pattern, where each column (from left to right) has one more square than the previous column. A strip of tape is placed along each interior edge shared by two adjacent squares. She wrote up to the number $105$ and stopped. How many strips of tape did she use?
> 
> (The figure shows the staircase arrangement: column $1$ has $1$ square, column $2$ has $2$ squares, column $3$ has $3$ squares, and so on, with the squares numbered $1, 2, 3, \ldots$ from top to bottom within each column and columns ordered left to right.)
