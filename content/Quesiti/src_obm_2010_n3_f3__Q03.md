*Largest shadow (orthogonal projection area) of a unit-edge solid cube*

> What is the largest shadow that a solid cube of edge length $1$ can cast in direct overhead sunlight?
> 
> *Remark:* The "largest shadow of a figure in direct overhead sunlight" is understood as the largest possible area of the orthogonal projection of the figure onto a plane.
