*For positive reals a,b,c,d prove the square root of the average of pairwise products is at least the cube root of the average of triple products.*

> Let $a$, $b$, $c$, $d$ be positive numbers. Prove the inequality
> $$\sqrt{\frac{ab + ac + ad + bc + bd + cd}{6}} \ge \sqrt[3]{\frac{abc + abd + acd + bcd}{4}}.$$
