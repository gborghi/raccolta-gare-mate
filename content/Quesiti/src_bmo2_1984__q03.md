*Find maximum and minimum of cos α + cos β + cos γ with angle sum constraint*

> Find, with proof, the maximum and minimum values of
> $$\cos\alpha + \cos\beta + \cos\gamma,$$
> where $\alpha \ge 0$, $\beta \ge 0$, $\gamma \ge 0$ and
> $$\alpha + \beta + \gamma = \frac{4\pi}{3}.$$
