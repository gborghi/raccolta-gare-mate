*Martian domino: pieces have 3 numbers each from 0..6, one piece per unordered triple; find total sum of all numbers*

> In the Martian domino, the pieces have 3 numbers each (different from the earthly domino, where each piece has only 2 numbers). The numbers in the Martian domino also vary from 0 to 6, and each piece has a choice of 3 numbers (not necessarily distinct), there existing one and only one piece that contains those 3 numbers. What is the sum of the numbers of all the pieces of the Martian domino?
> (A) 756 (B) 1512 (C) 84 (D) 315 (E) 900
