*A standard die (opposite faces sum to 7) rolls across a 3x3 board from A to H; determine the top face and the two visible side faces*

> On a die, the sum of the numbers on two opposite faces is always $7$. Figure 1 shows a board with $9$ square cells, one empty and the others marked with the letters of the alphabet from $A$ to $H$. Figure 2 shows a common die occupying the empty cell of that board.
> 
> The die rolls over an edge, without sliding, to occupy cell $A$. Then it rolls over another edge to occupy cell $B$, and so on, rolling and following the alphabet until it occupies the central cell $H$. There the path ends.
> 
> a) What is the number on the top face of the die?
> 
> b) Considering the die when it reaches the central cell $H$, that is, from the same viewpoint as Figure 2, what are the numbers on the other two visible faces? Justify your answer.
