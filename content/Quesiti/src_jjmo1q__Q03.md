# Japan Junior Mathematical Olympiad 2003 — Quesito 3

*Find ratio DS/SR using two cevians in a parallelogram*

> In parallelogram $ABCD$, two points $P$ and $Q$ are placed on side $BC$ such that
> $$BP : PQ : QC = 3 : 2 : \sqrt{7}.$$
> Let $R$ be the intersection of line $AP$ with line $CD$, and let $S$ be the intersection of line $AQ$ with line $CD$. Find the value of $\dfrac{DS}{SR}$.
