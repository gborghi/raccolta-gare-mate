*T-shirt discounts: 3 at 10% vs 2 at 5%, difference 12 reais; find discounted price*

> Júlia bought $3$ equal T-shirts and paid with a $10\%$ discount, while her brother bought $2$ T-shirts of the same type and paid with a $5\%$ discount. Júlia's brother paid $12$ reais more than Júlia. What is the price, in reais, of each T-shirt at Júlia's discounted price?
