*Show triangle side quantity lies strictly between 1/2 and 2*

> Let $a, b, c$ be the sides of a triangle. Show that the quantity
> $$\frac{a}{b+c} + \frac{b}{c+a} + \frac{c}{a+b}$$
> must lie between $\frac{1}{2}$ and $2$. Can equality hold at either limit?
