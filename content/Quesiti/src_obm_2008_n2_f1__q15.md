*Largest possible a given chained inequalities and d<40*

> The numbers $a$, $b$, $c$, $d$ are integers such that $a<2b$, $b<3c$, $c<4d$. If $d<40$, the largest possible value of $a$ will be:
> (A) $960$ \quad (B) $959$ \quad (C) $951$ \quad (D) $934$ \quad (E) $927$
