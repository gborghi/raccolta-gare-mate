# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2007 — Nível 3 — Fase 3 — Quesito 1

*Prove the n-fold iterate of a fixed quadratic equals zero has a real solution for every n*

> Let $f(x) = x^2 + 2007x + 1$. Prove that, for every positive integer $n$, the equation $\underbrace{f(f(\ldots(f(x))\ldots))}_{n \text{ times}} = 0$ has at least one real solution.
