*Minimum of $f(x)=x^x$ on positive reals; prove $x^y + y^x > 1$*

> 1. Let $f$ be the function defined for every strictly positive real $x$ by
> $$f(x) = x^x.$$
> Determine the minimum value attained by this function as $x$ ranges over all strictly positive reals.
> 
> 2. Let $x$ and $y$ be two strictly positive reals. Show that $x^y + y^x > 1$.
