*Convex pentagon with symmetry conditions; prove right angle at I*

> Let $ABCDE$ be a convex pentagon in which $AB = AE$, $CB = CE$, and $\angle CBD = 90^\circ$. Let the internal bisectors of $\angle EAB$ and $\angle DCB$ intersect at $I$, and let $M$ be the midpoint of $AC$. Prove that $\angle MIC = 90^\circ$.
