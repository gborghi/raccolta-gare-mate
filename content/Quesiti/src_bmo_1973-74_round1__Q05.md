*Probability exactly one player has a complete suit in a bridge deal*

> A bridge deal is defined as the distribution of 52 ordinary playing cards among four players so that each player has 13 cards. In a bridge deal what is the probability that just one player has a complete suit? (Leave your answer in factorials.)
