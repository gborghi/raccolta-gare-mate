*Find all 3-digit odd perfect squares whose digit sum is a perfect square*

> Find all three-digit natural numbers $n$ satisfying all of the following properties:
> \begin{itemize}
> \item $n$ is odd;
> \item $n$ is a perfect square;
> \item the sum of the digits of $n$ is a perfect square.
> \end{itemize}
