*Triangle sides red; 3 blue lines cut into 4 congruent triangles and 3 pentagons; find area fraction*

> A given triangle has its three sides coloured red. Three blue straight lines cut the triangle into seven pieces, four of which are triangles and three are pentagons. Two of the sides of each triangle are blue and one of them has its third side blue also.
> 
> Given that all four triangles are congruent, express the area of each as a fraction of the area of the given triangle.
