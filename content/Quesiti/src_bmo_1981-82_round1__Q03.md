# British Mathematical Olympiad 1981-82 — Round 1 — Quesito 3

*Prove inequality n(n+1)^{1/n} < a < b for m >= 2*

> If $\frac{1}{n(n+1)} < a < \frac{1}{n}$ and $m \geq 2$, prove that
> $$n(m+1)^{1/n} < a < b_n$$
> and $m \geq 2$.
