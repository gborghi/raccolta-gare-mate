> Arnaldo has several squares of various colors: blue $2 \times 2$ squares, yellow $2 \times 2$ squares and green $3 \times 3$ squares. He wants to assemble a larger square so that at least three of each kind appear. What is the smallest number of squares he will be able to use?
> 
> (A) 3 \quad (B) 6 \quad (C) 7 \quad (D) 8 \quad (E) 9
