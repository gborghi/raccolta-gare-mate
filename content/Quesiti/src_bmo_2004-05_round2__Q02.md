# British Mathematical Olympiad 2004-05 — Round 2 — Quesito 2

*Angle bisectors in 120° triangle; circle on diameter EF passes through D*

> In triangle $ABC$, $\angle BAC = 120^\circ$. Let the angle bisectors of angles $A$, $B$ and $C$ meet the opposite sides in $D$, $E$ and $F$ respectively. Prove that the circle on diameter $EF$ passes through $D$.
