*Polynomial with integer coefficients approximating p/q on interval of length 1/q*

> Let $p$ and $q$ be positive integers. Show that there exists an interval $I$ of length $\dfrac{1}{q}$ and a polynomial $P$ with integer coefficients such that, for all $x$ in $I$,
> $$\left| P(x) - \frac{p}{q} \right| \le \frac{1}{q^2} .$$
