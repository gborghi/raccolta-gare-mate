# IMO 1989 (30th IMO, Braunschweig) — Quesito 1

*Partition 1..1989 into 117 equal-sum 17-element subsets*

> Prove that the set {1, 2, . . . , 1989} can be expressed as the disjoint union of
> subsets Ai (i = 1, 2, . . . , 117) such that:
> (i)
> Each Ai contains 17 elements;
> (ii)
> The sum of all the elements in each Ai is the same.
