*Find all positive integers n,x,y,z and prime p satisfying a product equation*

> Find all positive integers $n$, $x$, $y$, $z$ and primes $p$ such that
> $$(x^2 + y^2)(y^2 + z^2)(z^2 + x^2) = p^n.$$
