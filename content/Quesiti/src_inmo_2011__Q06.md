*Find all f:R→R satisfying a given functional equation*

> Find all functions $f : \mathbb{R} \to \mathbb{R}$, where $\mathbb{R}$ denotes the set of all real numbers, such that
> $$f(x+y)f(x-y) = (f(x)+f(y))^2 - 4x^2 f(y)$$
> for all $x, y \in \mathbb{R}$.
