*Composite integers n>1 with divisibility condition on divisors*

> Determine all composite integers $n > 1$ that satisfy the following property: if $d_1, d_2, \ldots, d_k$ are all the positive divisors of $n$ with $1 = d_1 < d_2 < \cdots < d_k = n$, then $d_i$ divides $d_{i+1} + d_{i+2}$ for every $1 \le i \le k - 2$.
