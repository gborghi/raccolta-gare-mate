*Minimum class size given winner had 10 votes and others distinct*

> Teacher Marli proposed an election for representative of the sixth-year class. Five students presented themselves as candidates. All the students voted and the winner was Pedrinho, with $10$ votes. The other four candidates received different numbers of votes, each one. At least how many students are there in this class?
> 
> (A) $16$ \quad (B) $30$ \quad (C) $34$ \quad (D) $36$ \quad (E) $40$
