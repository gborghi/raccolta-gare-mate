*Minimum total toll paid travelling on a one-way road map from A to B*

> The figure beside represents a road map. The numbers shown indicate how much a traveler must pay (the pedal/toll) on each stretch of road that arrives at that point. All the roads are one-way, as indicated by the arrows. We want to find the minimum amount of pedal a traveler pays when leaving city $A$ and arriving at city $B$. What is this minimum amount?
> 
> (A) 11 (B) 9 (C) 12 (D) 10 (E) 15
