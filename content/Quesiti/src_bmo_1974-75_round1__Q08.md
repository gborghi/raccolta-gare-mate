# British Mathematical Olympiad 1974-75 — Round 1 — Quesito 8

*Wine glass cone half-full; water level bisects furthest generator from P*

> The interior of a wine glass is a right circular cone. The glass is half filled with water and then slowly tilted so that the water starts and continues to spill from a point $P$ on the rim. Show that the fraction of the whole conical interior occupied by water when the horizontal plane of the water level bisects the generator of the cone furthest from $P$ is $0$.
