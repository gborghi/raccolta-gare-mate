*Intersection point of two circles is circumcenter of ABC*

> Let $A$ be one of the intersection points of two circles with centers $X$ and $Y$. The tangents to the circles at $A$ intersect the circles again at $B$ and $C$, respectively. Let $P$ be a point in the plane such that $PXAY$ is a parallelogram. Prove that $P$ is the circumcenter of triangle $ABC$.
