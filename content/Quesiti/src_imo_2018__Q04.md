*Massimo K pietre rosse di Amy (gioco)*

> A site is any point $(x, y)$ in the plane such that $x$ and $y$ are both positive integers less than or equal to $20$. Initially, each of the $400$ sites is unoccupied. Amy and Ben take turns placing stones with Amy going first. On each move, Amy places a new red stone on an unoccupied site such that the distance between any two sites occupied by red stones is not equal to $\sqrt{5}$. On each move, Ben places a new blue stone on any unoccupied site. They stop as soon as someone cannot move. Find the greatest $K$ such that Amy can always place at least $K$ red stones, no matter how Ben plays.
