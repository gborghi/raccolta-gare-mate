*Prove sum inequality for reals in [0,1]*

> Let $n$ be an integer, $n \ge 2$, and $x_1, x_2, \ldots, x_n \in [0, 1]$. Prove that
> $$3\sum_{1 \le k < r \le n} k x_k x_r \le (n-1)\sum_{k=1}^{n} k x_k.$$
> (posed by Guangjian)
