# Regional Mathematical Olympiad 2013 — Region 1 — Quesito 6

*Prove n divisible by 6 given both quadratics have integer roots*

> Suppose $m$ and $n$ are integers such that both the quadratic equations $x^2 + mx - n = 0$ and $x^2 - mx + n = 0$ have integer roots. Prove that $n$ is divisible by $6$.
