*Prove inequalities comparing n with sum of its roots for n>=9*

> Prove that:
> (a) $5 < \sqrt[3]{5} + \sqrt[4]{5} + \sqrt[5]{5}$;
> (b) $8 > \sqrt{8} + \sqrt[3]{8} + \sqrt[4]{8}$;
> (c) $n > \sqrt{n} + \sqrt[3]{n} + \sqrt[4]{n}$ for all integers $n \ge 9$.
