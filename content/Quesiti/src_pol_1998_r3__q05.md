*Points D,E on side AB with ratio AC²/CB²: prove equal angles ACD and BCE*

> Points $D$ and $E$ lie on the side $AB$ of a triangle $ABC$ and satisfy
> $$\frac{AD}{DB} = \frac{AE}{EB} = \frac{AC^2}{CB^2}.$$
> Prove that $\angle ACD = \angle BCE$.
