*Prove harmonic series fractional parts are dense in any subinterval of [0,1]*

> (50 marks) Let $S_n = 1 + \frac{1}{2} + \cdots + \frac{1}{n}$, where $n$ is a positive integer. Prove that for any real numbers $a$, $b$ with $0 \leq a < b \leq 1$, there are infinitely many terms in the sequence $\{S_n - \lfloor S_n \rfloor\}$ that are within $(a, b)$. (Here $\lfloor x \rfloor$ denotes the largest integer not greater than real number $x$.)
