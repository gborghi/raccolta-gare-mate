*Distinct vertices marked by three regular polygons (8,12,18 sides) inscribed in a circle sharing a vertex*

> Three regular polygons, of $8$, $12$ and $18$ sides respectively, are inscribed in the same circumference and have a vertex in common. The vertices of the three polygons are marked on the circumference. How many distinct vertices are marked?
> 
> (A) 20 (B) 24 (C) 26 (D) 28 (E) 30
