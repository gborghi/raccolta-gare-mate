*Iterated function application 2004 times from a table.*

> The function $f$ is given by the following table.
> 
> | $x$ | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 | 5 |
> |---|---|---|---|---|---|
> | $f(x)$ | 4 | 1 | 3 | 5 | 2 |
> 
> For example, $f(2)=1$. How much is $\underbrace{f(f(\dots(f(f(4))\dots))}_{2004\text{ times}}$?
> 
> (A) 1 (B) 2 (C) 3 (D) 4 (E) 5
