*Column containing 1000 when filling a grid column by column*

> Joana completely fills a rectangular grid writing the numbers from $1$ to $2013$, one number for each square. She begins at the upper-left corner and fills the first column, then fills the second column, from top to bottom, and continues in the same way, filling the third column, the fourth, etc., until reaching the last column and finishing at the lower-right corner. If the number $50$ is in the second column, in which column will the number $1000$ be written?
> 
> (A) $23$ \quad (B) $31$ \quad (C) $33$ \quad (D) $39$ \quad (E) $61$
