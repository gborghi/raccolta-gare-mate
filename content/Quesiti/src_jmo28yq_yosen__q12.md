*Largest N with a_{N+1}-a_N not equal to 1*

> An integer-valued sequence $a_1, a_2, \ldots$ satisfies, for all integers $m, n$: if $m, n \ge 30$ and $|m - n| \ge 2018$, then $a_{m+n}$ equals $a_m + n$ or $a_n + m$. Find the largest possible value of a positive integer $N$ for which $a_{N+1} - a_N \ne 1$.
