*Three mutually tangent circles and common tangent, prove perimeter inequality*

> Circles of radii $r_1$, $r_2$ and $r_3$ touch each other externally, and they touch a common tangent line at points $A$, $B$ and $C$ respectively, where $B$ lies between $A$ and $C$. Prove that $16(r_1 + r_2 + r_3) \ge 9(AB + BC + CA)$.
