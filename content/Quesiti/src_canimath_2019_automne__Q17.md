*Existence of sequence of strictly positive integers satisfying a_{n+2} = a_{n+1} + sqrt(a_n + a_{n+1})*

> Does there exist a sequence of strictly positive integers $a_0, a_1, \ldots$ such that
> $$a_{n+2} = a_{n+1} + \sqrt{a_n + a_{n+1}}$$
> for all integers $n \ge 0$?
