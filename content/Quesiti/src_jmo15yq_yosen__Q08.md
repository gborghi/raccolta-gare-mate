# Japan Mathematical Olympiad 2005 — Yosen (Preliminary) — Quesito 8

*Count valid seatings of 7 people at circular table where no one sits next to another's assigned chair*

> $7$ people are seated one per chair at a circular table with $7$ chairs. Each person has their own assigned chair. However, while there are still unoccupied chairs, no person may sit in a chair that is adjacent to any other person's chair. (People who have not yet been seated may sit only if no adjacent chair is occupied.) Find the number of possible orders in which all $7$ people can be seated.
