*Find possible GCDs after 999 iterated (lcm+gcd, lcm-gcd) operations on two coprime integers*

> Two coprime positive integers, each between $1$ and $2026$, are written on a blackboard. The following operation is performed exactly $999$ times:
> 
> Let the two numbers on the blackboard be $x$ and $y$. Set $l = \operatorname{lcm}(x,y)$ and $g = \gcd(x,y)$. Erase $x$ and $y$ and write $l + g$ and $l - g$.
> 
> What are the possible values of $\gcd$ of the two numbers remaining on the blackboard after all $999$ operations?
