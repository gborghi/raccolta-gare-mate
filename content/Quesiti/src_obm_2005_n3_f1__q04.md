*Find O+B+M given OBM=240, OB+M=46, O+BM=64*

> The letters $O$, $B$ and $M$ represent integer numbers. If $O \times B \times M = 240$, $O \times B + M = 46$ and $O + B \times M = 64$, then $O + B + M$ is equal to:
> (A) $19$ (B) $20$ (C) $21$ (D) $24$ (E) $36$
