*Find all positive real t with infinite set X and arithmetic progression inequality*

> Find all positive real numbers $t$ with the property that there exists an infinite set $X$ of real numbers such that the inequality
> $$\max\{\,|x - (a - d)|,\ |y - a|,\ |z - (a + d)|\,\} > td$$
> holds for all (not necessarily distinct) $x, y, z \in X$, all real numbers $a$ and all positive real numbers $d$.
