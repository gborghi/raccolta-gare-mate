# British Mathematical Olympiad 1996-97 — Round 1 — Quesito 2

*Sequence with a_{n+1} defined by digit sum; find a_{1997}*

> For positive integers $n \geq 1$, define the sequence $a_1, a_2, a_3, \ldots$ by
> $$a_1 = 1, \quad a_{n+1} = a_n + \frac{n+1}{\text{largest prime factor of }(n+1)}, \quad n \geq 1.$$
> Determine the value of $a_{1997}$.
