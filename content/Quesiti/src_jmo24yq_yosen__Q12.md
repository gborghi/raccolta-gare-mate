# Japan Mathematical Olympiad 2014 — Yosen (Preliminary) — Quesito 12

*Find largest m so that 2m indices guarantee product-sum bound 1/2.014*

> Find the largest positive integer $m$ satisfying the following condition: there exist $2m$ integers $i_1, \ldots, i_m$ and $j_1, \ldots, j_m$ (not necessarily distinct), each between $1$ and $1000$ inclusive, such that for every sequence of non-negative real numbers $a_1, \ldots, a_{1000}$ with $a_1 + a_2 + \cdots + a_{1000} = 1$,
> $$a_{i_1}a_{j_1} + a_{i_2}a_{j_2} + \cdots + a_{i_m}a_{j_m} \le \frac{1}{2.014}.$$
