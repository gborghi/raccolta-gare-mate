*Minimise sum of squared distances from a point on a line*

> Two points $A$, $B$ and a line $\ell$ are given in a plane. Locate, with proof, the point $F$ on $\ell$ such that $FA^2 + FB^2$ is a minimum, where $F$ is the foot of the perpendicular from $F$ to $\ell$.
> 
> Give a presentation without proof for three points $A$, $B$, $C$ and $FA^2 + FB^2 + FC^2$ a minimum.
