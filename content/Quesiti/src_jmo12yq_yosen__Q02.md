# Japan Mathematical Olympiad 2002 — Yosen (Preliminary) — Quesito 2

*Pairs from 1..14 with absolute difference at most 3*

> From the integers between $1$ and $14$ inclusive, choose two distinct numbers. How many pairs of two numbers are there such that the absolute value of their difference is at most $3$? Here, choosing the two numbers in either order is regarded as the same pair.
