*Find largest lambda(n) for sum-squared vs sum-of-squares inequality*

> Given integer $n \geq 2$, find the largest $\lambda(n)$ with the following property: if a sequence of real numbers $a_0, a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_n$ satisfies
> $$0 = a_0 \leq a_1 \leq a_2 \leq \cdots \leq a_n,$$
> $$a_i \geq \frac{1}{2}(a_{i+1} + a_{i-1}), \quad i = 1, 2, \ldots, n-1,$$
> then
> $$\left(\sum_{i=1}^{n} a_i\right)^2 \geq \lambda(n) \sum_{i=1}^{n} a_i^2.$$
