*In which range is x times the root of y*

> Given two real x and y such that 0 < x < y < 1, in what range is x√y?
>
> - **(A)** Between 0 and x
> - **(B)** between x and y
> - **(C)** between y and 1
> - **(D)** after 1
> - **(E)** depends on the values of x and y. | 0 | x | y | 1

**Answer:** A
