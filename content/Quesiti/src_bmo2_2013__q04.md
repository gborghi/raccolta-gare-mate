*Square circumcircle point: can PA,PB,PC,PD,AB all be integers?*

> Suppose that $ABCD$ is a square and that $P$ is a point which is on the circumcircle. Determine whether or not it is possible that $PA$, $PB$, $PC$, $PD$ and $AB$ are all integers.
