*Line intercepts ellipse; inscribed circle center locus and area*

> Straight line $l$ with slope $\dfrac{1}{3}$ intercepts ellipse $C\colon \dfrac{x^2}{36} + \dfrac{y^2}{4} = 1$ at points $A$, $B$, and point $P(3\sqrt{2},\, \sqrt{2})$ is in the top-left of $l$ (as shown in Fig. 11.1).
> (1) Prove that the center of the inscribed circle of $\triangle PAB$ is on a given line.
> (2) When $\angle APB = 60^\circ$, find the area of $\triangle PAB$.
