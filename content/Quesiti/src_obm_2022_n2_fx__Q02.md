*System of cyclic equations in a, b, c; determine abc*

> The real numbers $a$, $b$, $c$ are all different from zero and satisfy the following system of equations:
> $$\begin{cases} a + ab = c \\ b + bc = a \\ c + ca = b \end{cases}$$
> Determine all possible values of $abc$.
