*With incentre I and an excentre J of triangle ABC, prove AI.AJ=AB.AC and AI.BJ.CJ=AJ.BI.CI.*

> $I$ is the centre of the circle inscribed to triangle $ABC$; $J$ is the centre of the escribed circle which touches $AB$ and $AC$ produced beyond $B$ and $C$ respectively. Prove that $$AI \cdot AJ = AB \cdot AC$$ and that $$AI \cdot BJ \cdot CJ = AJ \cdot BI \cdot CI.$$
