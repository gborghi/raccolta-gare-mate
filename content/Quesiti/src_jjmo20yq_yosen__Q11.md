# Japan Junior Mathematical Olympiad 2022 — Yosen (Preliminary) — Quesito 11

*Isosceles triangle AB=AC with interior Brocard-like point P; find BC*

> Let $ABC$ be an isosceles triangle with $AB = AC$, and let $P$ be an interior point satisfying $\angle PAB = \angle PBC = \angle PCA$. If the areas of triangles $PAB$ and $PCA$ are $4$ and $1$ respectively, find the length $BC$. (Here $XY$ denotes the length of segment $XY$.)

**Topic:** [[topic_geometria_piana|Geometria piana]]

**Abilita:** [[skill_ragionamento_geometrico|Ragionamento geometrico]], [[skill_manipolazione_algebrica|Manipolazione algebrica]]
**Area:** [[Geometria]]
