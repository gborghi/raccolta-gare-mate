*Given two simultaneous equations in 3^x and 3^y, find the value of 3^x + 3^y.*

> The numbers $x$ and $y$ are such that $3^x + 3^{y+1} = 5\sqrt{3}$ and $3^{x+1} + 3^y = 3\sqrt{3}$. What is the value of $3^x + 3^y$?
>
> - **(A)** $\sqrt{3}$
> - **(B)** $2\sqrt{3}$
> - **(C)** $3\sqrt{3}$
> - **(D)** $4\sqrt{3}$
> - **(E)** $5\sqrt{3}$
