# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2012 — Nível Universitário — Fase 1 — Quesito 3

*Polynomial divisibility condition via g(x)^m*

> Let $f(x)$ and $g(x) \in \mathbb{R}[x]$ be non-constant polynomials, and $m$, $n$ positive integers. Show that $y^n - f(x)$ divides $y^n - g(x)$ in $\mathbb{R}[x, y]$ if and only if $m$ divides $n$ and $g(x) = f(x)^{n/m}$.
