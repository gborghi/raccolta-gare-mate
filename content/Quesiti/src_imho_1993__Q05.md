*Find all functions f: N→N with two given conditions*

> Does there exist a function $f : \mathbf{N} \to \mathbf{N}$ such that $f(1) = 2$, $f(f(n)) = f(n) + n$ for all $n \in \mathbf{N}$, and $f(n) < f(n+1)$ for all $n \in \mathbf{N}$?
