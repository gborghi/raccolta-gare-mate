*Beautiful integer sequences containing 2025 with convexity-like condition; among max length N find min a_N*

> For an integer $n \ge 3$, a sequence of integers $a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_n$ is called beautiful if all of the following conditions hold:
> - $0 = a_1 < a_2 < \cdots < a_n$;
> - there exists an integer $i$ with $1 \le i \le n$ such that $a_i = 2025$;
> - for all integers $i, j, k$ with $i < j < k$, the inequality $\dfrac{a_i + a_k}{2} \le a_j$ holds.
> Let $N$ be the largest possible length of a beautiful sequence. Among beautiful sequences $a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_N$ of length $N$, find the smallest possible value of $a_N$. Here, the length of a sequence $x_1, x_2, \ldots, x_l$ is $l$.
