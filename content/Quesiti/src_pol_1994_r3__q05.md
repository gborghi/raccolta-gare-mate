*Parallelepiped center, weighted sum of squared distances inequality*

> Let $O$ be the center of a parallelopiped $A_1 A_2 \cdots A_8$. Prove that
> $$4 \sum_{i=1}^{8} OA_i^2 \le \left( \sum_{i=1}^{8} OA_i \right)^2.$$
