*4-digit number: product of top-2 and bottom-2 digit parts divides n*

> Let $n$ be a 4-digit positive integer whose tens digit is not $0$. Write $n$ in decimal; let $a$ be the 2-digit integer formed by the hundreds digit and thousands digit of $n$ (the top two digits), and let $b$ be the 2-digit integer formed by the tens digit and units digit of $n$ (the bottom two digits). If the product $a \times b$ is a divisor of $n$, find all such $n$.
