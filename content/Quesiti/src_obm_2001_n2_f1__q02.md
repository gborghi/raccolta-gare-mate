*Angle alpha after rotating triangle ABC 90 degrees counterclockwise about C*

> Triangle $CDE$ can be obtained by the rotation of triangle $ABC$ by $90^\circ$ counterclockwise about $C$, as shown in the drawing below. We can state that $\alpha$ is equal to:
> 
> (A) $75^\circ$ \quad (B) $65^\circ$ \quad (C) $70^\circ$ \quad (D) $45^\circ$ \quad (E) $55^\circ$
