# British Mathematical Olympiad 1984-85 — Round 1 — Quesito 5

*Cylinder rolling inside hollow cylinder; find locus of centre of circular end*

> A cylindrical container has height 6 cm and radius 4 cm and the hoop is fixed in a horizontal plane parallel to the base with also radius 4 cm and with each of its circular rims touching the hoop at two points. The cylinder is now moved so that the rim still touches the hoop at two points. Find, with proof, the locus of the centre of one of the cylinder's circular ends.
