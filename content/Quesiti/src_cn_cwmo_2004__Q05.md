*Find a_{2004} for the recurrence a_{n+2}=(a_n+1)/a_{n+1} with a_1=a_2=1*

> The sequence $\{a_n\}$ satisfies $a_1 = a_2 = 1$ and $a_{n+2} = \dfrac{a_n + 1}{a_{n+1}}$, $n = 1, 2, \ldots$. Find $a_{2004}$.
