# Japan Mathematical Olympiad 2020 — Yosen (Preliminary) — Quesito 8

*Sequence of 100 positive integers satisfying two conditions, find minimum a_100*

> A sequence $a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_{100}$ of positive integers satisfies:
> \begin{itemize}
>   \item[(i)] For all $2 \le k \le 100$: $a_{k-1} < a_k$.
>   \item[(ii)] For all $6 \le k \le 100$: $a_k$ is equal to one of $2a_1, 2a_2, \ldots, 2a_{k-1}$.
> \end{itemize}
> Find the smallest possible value of $a_{100}$.
