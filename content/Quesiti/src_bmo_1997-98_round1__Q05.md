# British Mathematical Olympiad 1997-98 — Round 1 — Quesito 5

*Point D on arc BC; prove midpoint and angle sum conditions*

> $ABC$ is a triangle. $D$ is a point on the minor arc $BC$ of the circumcircle of triangle $ABC$. $F$ is the midpoint of $DA$. Prove that $M$ is the midpoint of $DF$, and that $\angle ADC = \angle DAB + \angle ABC + 12^\circ$.
