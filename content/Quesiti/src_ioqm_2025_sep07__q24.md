*P(x)=x^2025 divided by Q(x)=x^4+x^3+2x^2+x+1; find R(3)*

> Let $P(x) = x^{2025}$, $Q(x) = x^4 + x^3 + 2x^2 + x + 1$. Let $R(x)$ be the polynomial remainder when the polynomial $P(x)$ is divided by the polynomial $Q(x)$. Find $R(3)$.
