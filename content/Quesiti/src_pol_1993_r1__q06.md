*Given a sequence defined by a running-average recurrence, evaluate a weighted sum.*

> The sequence $(x_n)$ is defined by $x_0=1992$ and
> $$x_n=-\frac{1992}{n}\sum_{k=0}^{n-1}x_k$$
> for each $n\ge 1$. Calculate $\displaystyle\sum_{n=0}^{1992}2^n x_n$.
