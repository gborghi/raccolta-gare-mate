*Minimum number of 6th-grade students from a sports bar chart*

> The graph alongside refers to the sports practice of the students of the 6th year of a school. None of the boys who play soccer or volleyball plays basketball, and no girl who plays basketball or volleyball plays soccer. There are five boys and three girls who do not practice any of the three sports. At least how many students are there in the 6th year?
> 
> (A) $37$ \quad (B) $45$ \quad (C) $50$ \quad (D) $64$ \quad (E) $72$
