*Chords AP AQ AR AS with equal angles; prove product identity*

> $AP$, $AQ$, $AR$, $AS$ are chords of a given circle with the property that $\angle PAQ = \angle QAR = \angle RAS$.
> 
> Prove that
> $$AR(AP + AR) = AQ(AQ + AS).$$
