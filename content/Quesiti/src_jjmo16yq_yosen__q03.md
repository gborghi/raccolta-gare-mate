*Count ways Taro attends exactly 6 of 10 meetings with no-attend constraint after 3 absences*

> Taro has $10$ club meetings. He will attend exactly $6$ of them. However, if he is absent for $3$ consecutive meetings, he is not allowed to attend the meeting immediately following those $3$ consecutive absences (he must also miss that one). How many ways can he choose which meetings to attend?
