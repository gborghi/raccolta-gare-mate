- [[src_pol_2006_r1__Q01|Quesito 1]] — Find nonnegative integers n with 2^n+105 a perfect square.
- [[src_pol_2006_r1__Q02|Quesito 2]] — Solve sqrt[5]{x} = ceil(sqrt[5]{3x}) in nonnegative reals.
- [[src_pol_2006_r1__Q03|Quesito 3]] — Acute triangle inscribed in circle center O; D projection of C onto AB, E,F projections of D onto AC,BC; prove area EOFC equals half area ABC.
- [[src_pol_2006_r1__Q04|Quesito 4]] — Six problems each scored 6,5,2 or 0; for every two participants there are two problems on each of which they got different scores; find max participants.
- [[src_pol_2006_r1__Q05|Quesito 5]] — f(x)=ax+b|x|, g(x)=ax-b|x|; if f(f(x))=x for all x then prove g(g(x))=x for all x.
- [[src_pol_2006_r1__Q06|Quesito 6]] — Line through orthocenter H meets AC at D, BC at E; line through H perpendicular to DE meets AB at F; prove DH/HE = AF/FB.
- [[src_pol_2006_r1__Q07|Quesito 7]] — Prime p>3 and positive integers a,b,c with a+b+c=p+1 and p | a^3+b^3+c^3-1; show one of a,b,c equals 1.
- [[src_pol_2006_r1__Q08|Quesito 8]] — Tetrahedron ABCD circumscribed about unit sphere center S with SA>=SB>=SC; show SA>sqrt(5).
- [[src_pol_2006_r1__Q09|Quesito 9]] — k_1<...<k_m nonnegative integers, n=2^{k_1}+...+2^{k_m}; find number of odd coefficients of (x+1)^n.
- [[src_pol_2006_r1__Q10|Quesito 10]] — Positive a,b,c with ab+bc+ca=abc; prove a^3+b^3+c^3>=6abc.
- [[src_pol_2006_r1__Q11|Quesito 11]] — Concave quadrilateral ABCD with interior angle at A greater than 180 and AB*CD=AD*BC; P symmetric to A wrt BD; prove angle PCB=angle ACD.
- [[src_pol_2006_r1__Q12|Quesito 12]] — Sequence a_{i+1}=a_i/2 if even else 3a_i-1; if a_n=a_0 prove 2^n>a_0.
