*For all equilateral triangles circumscribed about an acute triangle, their centers lie on one circle.*

> An acute-angled triangle $ABC$ is given. Consider all equilateral triangles $XYZ$ in this plane such that the points $A,B,C$ lie on the segments $YZ,ZX,XY$, respectively. Prove that the centers of triangles $XYZ$ lie on a single circle.
