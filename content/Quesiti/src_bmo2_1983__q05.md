*Count arrangements of 1–8 satisfying seven order conditions*

> Find the number of arrangements
> $$a,\ b,\ c,\ d,\ e,\ f,\ g,\ h$$
> of the numbers $1,2,3,4,5,6,7,8$ which satisfy all seven conditions
> $$a < b,\ c < d,\ e < f,\ g < h \quad \text{and} \quad b > c,\ d > e,\ f > g.$$
