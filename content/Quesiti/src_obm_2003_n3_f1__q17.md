*Count guests at party given 77 plates of 4 types serving different numbers of people*

> At a typical party, each plate of rice served $2$ people, each plate of mayonnaise served $3$ people, each plate of meat served $4$ people, and each plate of sweets served $5$ people. A total of $77$ plates were used and every person at the party was served exactly once. How many people were at the party?
> 
> A) $20$ \quad B) $30$ \quad C) $45$ \quad D) $60$ \quad E) $75$
