# Japan IMO Selection Camp 2024 — Selection Camp — Quesito 7

*Longest sequence of integers in [1,2^2023] with no zero signed subsum*

> Find the largest positive integer $N$ such that there exists a sequence $a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_N$ satisfying both of the following:
> \begin{itemize}
> \item For every integer $i$ with $1 \le i \le N$, $a_i$ is an integer with $1 \le a_i \le 2^{2023}$.
> \item For all integers $i, j$ with $1 \le i \le j \le N$ and every choice of $s_i, s_{i+1}, \ldots, s_j$ each equal to $1$ or $-1$,
> $$s_i a_i + s_{i+1} a_{i+1} + \cdots + s_j a_j \neq 0.$$
> \end{itemize}
