*Diagonals of cyclic quadrilateral; circumcentre and orthocentre collinear*

> The diagonals $AC$ and $BD$ of a cyclic quadrilateral $ABCD$ intersect at point $P$. Let $O$ be the circumcentre of triangle $APB$ and $H$ be the orthocentre of triangle $CPD$. Show that the points $H$, $P$, and $O$ lie on a line.
