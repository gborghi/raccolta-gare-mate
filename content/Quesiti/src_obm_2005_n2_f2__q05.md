*Smallest positive integer satisfying four divisibility conditions*

> Let $a$ be a positive integer such that $a + 1$ is a multiple of $5$, $a + 2$ is a multiple of $7$, $a + 3$ is a multiple of $9$, and $a + 3$ is a multiple of $11$. Determine the smallest value that $a$ can assume.
