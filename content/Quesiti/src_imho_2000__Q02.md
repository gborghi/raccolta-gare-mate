*Inequality for positive reals with product 1*

> Let $a, b, c$ be positive reals with product 1. Prove that
> $$\left(a - 1 + \frac{1}{b}\right)\left(b - 1 + \frac{1}{c}\right)\left(c - 1 + \frac{1}{a}\right) \le 1.$$
