*Perpendicular bisectors in 60-degree triangle give equal segment*

> Let $ABC$ be a triangle such that $\widehat{BAC} = 60^\circ$. The perpendicular bisector of $[AC]$ meets line $(AB)$ at $P$, and the perpendicular bisector of $[AB]$ meets line $(AC)$ at $Q$. Show that $PQ = BC$.
