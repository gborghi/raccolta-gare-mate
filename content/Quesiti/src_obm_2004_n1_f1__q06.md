*Minimum buses needed so each bus carries equal students*

> Students from a school went on a trip for which two buses were hired. $57$ students arrived at the first bus stop and $31$ students at the second. How many students must move from the first bus to the second so that each bus carries the same number of students?
> 
> (A) $8$ \quad (B) $13$ \quad (C) $16$ \quad (D) $26$ \quad (E) $31$
