*Triangle ABC with vector inequality implies right angle*

> Let $\triangle ABC$ be a given triangle. If $|\overrightarrow{BA} - t\overrightarrow{BC}| \ge |\overrightarrow{AC}|$ for any $t \in \mathbf{R}$, then $\triangle ABC$ is (\ \ ).
> 
> (A) an acute triangle\quad (B) an obtuse triangle\quad (C) a right triangle\quad (D) not known
