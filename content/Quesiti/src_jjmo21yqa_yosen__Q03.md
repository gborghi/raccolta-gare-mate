# Japan Junior Mathematical Olympiad 2023 — Yosen (Preliminary) — Quesito 3

*Row of 7 cells with A,B,C, adjacent pairs of each*

> Consider ways to write one of the letters A, B, C in each of $7$ cells arranged in a single row. How many such writings are there in which there exist two adjacent cells both containing A, two adjacent cells both containing B, and two adjacent cells both containing C? (Writings that coincide under rotation or reflection are still counted as distinct.)
