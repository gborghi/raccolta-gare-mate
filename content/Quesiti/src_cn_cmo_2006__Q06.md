*Minimum n so union condition forces three subsets with nonempty intersection*

> Let $X$ be a set of 56 elements. Find the least positive integer $n$ such that for any 15 subsets of $X$, if the union of every 7 of these 15 subsets contains at least $n$ elements, then there exist 3 of the 15 subsets whose intersection is nonempty.
