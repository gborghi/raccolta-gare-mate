*Given S(x)=1-x and T(x)=x/2 on [0,1], does a composition f=g1 o ... o gn of these satisfy f(1/2)=1975/2^1975?*

> On the interval $[0,1]$ are given functions $S(x) = 1 - x$ and $T(x) = x/2$. Does there exist a function of the form $f = g_1 \circ g_2 \circ \cdots \circ g_n$, where $n \in \mathbb{N}$ and each $g_k$ is either $S(x)$ or $T(x)$, such that
> $$f\left(\frac{1}{2}\right) = \frac{1975}{2^{1975}}?$$
