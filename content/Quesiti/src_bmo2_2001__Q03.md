*Triangle with angle bisector and right angle; prove angle equality*

> A triangle $ABC$ has $\angle ACB > \angle ABC$. The internal bisector of $\angle BAC$ meets $BC$ at $D$. The point $E$ on $AB$ is such that $\angle EDB = 90^\circ$. The point $F$ on $AC$ is such that $\angle BDE = \angle CDF$.
> 
> Show that $\angle BAD = \angle FDC$.
