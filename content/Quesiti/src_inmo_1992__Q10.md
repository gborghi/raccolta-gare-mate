*Find all functions f: R\{0,1} → R satisfying a functional equation*

> Determine all functions $f : \mathbb{R} \setminus \{0, 1\} \to \mathbb{R}$ such that for all $x$,
> $$f(x) + f\!\left(\frac{1}{1-x}\right) = \frac{2(1-2x)}{x(1-x)}.$$
