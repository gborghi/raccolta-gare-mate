*Inequality for four positive reals summing to 1*

> If $a, b, c, d$ are positive numbers with $a + b + c + d = 1$, prove that
> $$ab + bc + cd \le \frac{1}{4}.$$
> Does the analogous inequality hold for $n$ variables?
