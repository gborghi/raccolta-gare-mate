*Show that abc divides (a+b+c)^7 given the divisibility conditions a|b^2, b|c^2, c|a^2*

> Let $a, b, c$ be positive integers such that $a$ divides $b^2$, $b$ divides $c^2$ and $c$ divides $a^2$. Prove that $abc$ divides $(a + b + c)^7$.
