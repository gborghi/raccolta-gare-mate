*Count positive integers n where floor(1000000/n) - floor(1000000/(n+1)) = 1*

> How many positive integers $n$ satisfy
> $$\left\lfloor \frac{1000000}{n} \right\rfloor - \left\lfloor \frac{1000000}{n+1} \right\rfloor = 1?$$
> (Here $\lfloor x \rfloor$ denotes the greatest integer not exceeding $x$.)
