*Prove sum-of-fractions inequality for reals summing to 3*

> Suppose $a$, $b$, $c$ are real numbers, with $a + b + c = 3$. Prove that
> $$\frac{1}{5a^2 - 4a + 11} + \frac{1}{5b^2 - 4b + 11} + \frac{1}{5c^2 - 4c + 11} \le \frac{1}{4}.$$
