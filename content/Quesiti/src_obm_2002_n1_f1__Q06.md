*Monthly production from sales split among three shops*

> The whole monthly production of cans of soft drink of a certain factory was sold to three shops. To shop A, $\dfrac{2}{5}$ of the production was sold; to shop B were sold $\dfrac{2}{5}$ of the production, and to shop C were sold $2500$ units. What was the monthly production of this factory?
> 
> (A) $4166$ cans (B) $10000$ cans (C) $20000$ cans (D) $25000$ cans (E) $30000$ cans
