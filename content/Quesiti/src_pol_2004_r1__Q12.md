*Find the number of real solutions of a cyclic cubic-quartic system in $x_1,\dots,x_n$ for $n\ge 5$.*

> Let $n\ge 5$ be an integer. Find the number of solutions in real numbers $x_1,\dots,x_n$ of the system
> $$x_{i-2}^3+x_{i-1}^3+x_i^3=x_i^4+x_{i+1}^3+x_{i+2}^2\quad\text{for }i=1,2,\dots,n,$$
> where $x_{-1}=x_{n-1}$, $x_0=x_n$, $x_1=x_{n+1}$, $x_2=x_{n+2}$.
