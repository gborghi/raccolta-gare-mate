# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2003 — Nível 3 — Fase 1 — Quesito 10

*Identify which sequence does NOT describe itself autologically*

> The sequence $``22"$ describes itself, since it is formed by exactly two $2$'s. Analogously, the sequence $``31\;12\;33\;15"$ describes itself since it contains exactly three $1$'s, one $2$, three $3$'s, one $4$ (=zero fours), and one $5$... Which of the following sequences does NOT describe itself?
> 
> A) $21\;32\;23\;16$ \quad B) $31\;12\;33\;18$ \quad C) $31\;22\;33\;17\;19$ \quad D) $31\;32\;33\;24\;15\;16\;18$ \quad E) $41\;32\;33\;24\;15\;16\;18$
