*Two circles through D tangent at A and B; collinearity of D, E, F*

> A point $D$ is taken on the side $AB$ of a triangle $ABC$. Two circles passing through $D$ and touching $AC$ and $BC$ at $A$ and $B$ respectively intersect again at $E$. Let $F$ be the point symmetric to $C$ with respect to the perpendicular bisector of $AB$. Prove that the points $D$, $E$, $F$ lie on a line.
