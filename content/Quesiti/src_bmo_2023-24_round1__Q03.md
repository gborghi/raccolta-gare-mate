*Prove BZ perpendicular to AC in circle geometry problem*

> Let $ABC$ be a triangle with $\angle ACB < 90^\circ$. Let $X$ and $Y$ be points on $AC$ and the circle $ABC$ respectively such that $XY \ne A$ and $BX = BY = BA$. Line $XY$ intersects the circle $ABC$ again at $Z$.
> 
> Prove that $BZ$ is perpendicular to $AC$.
