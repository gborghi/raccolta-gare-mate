# Pre-Regional Mathematical Olympiad (PRMO) 2017 — Quesito 13

*Find AB in rectangle ABCD with E midpoint of AB and two perpendicularity conditions*

> In a rectangle $ABCD$, $E$ is the midpoint of $AB$; $F$ is a point on $AC$ such that $BF$ is perpendicular to $AC$; and $FE$ is perpendicular to $BD$. Suppose $BC = 8\sqrt{3}$. Find $AB$.
