*Max n so 1..n can be 2-colored with no monochromatic square sum*

> Let $n$ be a positive integer. On a blackboard, each of the integers from $1$ to $n$ is written exactly once, each colored either red or white. The sum of any two distinct numbers written in the same color is not a perfect square. Find the largest possible value of $n$.
