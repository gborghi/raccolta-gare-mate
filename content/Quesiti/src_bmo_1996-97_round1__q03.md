*Count ways to pay 1997 Pippins with decimal coins*

> The Dwarfs in the Land-under-the-Mountain have just adopted a completely decimal currency system based on the Pippin. The coins available are worth 1 Pippin, 10 Pippins, 100 Pippins and 1000 Pippins.
> 
> In how many ways is it possible for a Dwarf to pay, in exact coinage, a bill of 1997 Pippins?
