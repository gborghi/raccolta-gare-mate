> Consider the equation
> $$x^2 + xy + y^2 + 3x + 3y + 6 = 0. \quad (*)$$
> (1) Find all pairs of integers $(x, y)$ satisfying $(*)$ with $x = 1$.
> 
> (2) Find all pairs of integers $(x, y)$ satisfying $(*)$.
