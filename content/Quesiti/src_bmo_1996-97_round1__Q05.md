*Positive reals x, y with x+y>=2; two inequality questions*

> Let $x$ and $y$ be positive real numbers.
> 
> (a) If $x + y \geq 2$, is it necessarily true that $\dfrac{1}{x} + \dfrac{1}{y} \geq 2$?
> 
> (b) If $x + y \geq 2$, is it necessarily true that $\dfrac{1}{x^2} + \dfrac{1}{y^2} \geq 2$?
