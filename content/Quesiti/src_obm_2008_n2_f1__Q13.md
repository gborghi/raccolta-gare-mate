# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2008 — Nível 2 — Fase 1 — Quesito 13

*Sum of digit-sums P(1)+...+P(1000)*

> Let $P(n)$ be the sum of the digits of the number $n$. For example, $P(1234)=1+2+3+4=10$. What is the value of $P(1)+P(2)+P(3)+\ldots+P(1000)$?
> (A) $200$ \quad (B) $500$ \quad (C) $400$ \quad (D) $900$ \quad (E) $2250$
