# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2004 — Nível 3 — Fase 1 — Quesito 2

*Length EF of two points on a semicircle whose feet on the diameter are at distances 1 and 8 from A.*

> Let $AB$ be a segment of length 26, and let $C$ and $D$ be points on the segment $AB$ such that $AC=1$ and $AD=8$. Let $E$ and $F$ be points on a semicircle of diameter $AB$, with $EC$ and $FD$ perpendicular to $AB$. How much is $EF$?
> 
> (A) 5 (B) $5\sqrt{2}$ (C) 7 (D) $7\sqrt{2}$ (E) 12
