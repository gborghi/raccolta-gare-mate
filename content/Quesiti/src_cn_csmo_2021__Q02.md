*Triangle with AB=BC; prove orthocenter is incentre of related triangle*

> In $\triangle ABC$, $AB = BC$. Let $O$ and $H$ be the circumcenter and orthocenter of $\triangle ABC$, respectively. Let $G$ be the midpoint of $AH$, and let $BE$ be the altitude from $B$ to $AC$. Prove that if $OE \parallel BC$, then $H$ is the incentre of $\triangle GBC$.
