*Find $a^2 - b^2 + c^2$ from two linear equations*

> Let $a$, $b$, $c$ be real numbers such that $a - 7b + 8c = 4$ and $8a + 4b - c = 7$. What is the value of $a^2 - b^2 + c^2$?
