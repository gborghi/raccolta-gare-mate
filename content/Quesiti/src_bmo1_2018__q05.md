*Minimum colours in grid to block all 100-combs*

> If we take a $200 \times 100$ (or $100 \times 2$) grid of unit squares, and remove alternate squares, the remaining $150$ squares form a $100$-comb. Henry takes a $200 \times 100$ grid of unit squares, removes $150$ squares, and chooses $k$ of these squares and colours them so that James is unable to choose $150$ multicoloured squares which form a $100$-comb. What is the smallest possible value of $k$?
