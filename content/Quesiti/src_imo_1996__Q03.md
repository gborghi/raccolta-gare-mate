*Find all f with f(m+f(n))=f(f(m))+f(n)*

> Let S denote the set of nonnegative integers. Find all functions f from
> S to itself such that
> f(m + f(n)) = f(f(m)) + f(n)
> ∀m, n ∈S.
> 37th International Mathematical Olympiad
> Mumbai, India
> Day II
> 9 a.m. - 1:30 p.m.
> July 11, 1996
