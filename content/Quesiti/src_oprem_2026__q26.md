*Exercise 3, Q7: full characterization, a x b (a,b>=4) pavable iff 3 | ab*

> Exercise 3 - (7) One supposes $a \ge 4$, $b \ge 4$. Show that one can tile a grid of size $a \times b$ if, and only if, $ab$ is divisible by $3$. Deduce the square grids that one can tile.
