*Positive integers satisfying 17(abcd+ab+ad+cd+1)=20(bcd+b+d); find a²+b²+c²+d²*

> If $a, b, c, d$ are positive integers such that
> $$17(abcd + ab + ad + cd + 1) = 20(bcd + b + d),$$
> find $a^2 + b^2 + c^2 + d^2$.
