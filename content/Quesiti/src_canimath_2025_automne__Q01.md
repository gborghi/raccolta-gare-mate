*Find central angle AOC from two given angles OBA and OBC on a circle*

> Let $A$, $B$ and $C$ be three points on a circle with center $O$, placed so that $A$, $O$ and $C$ are not aligned, so that $B$ lies on the minor arc $AC$, and respecting the following angle equalities: $\widehat{OBA} = 77^\circ$ and $\widehat{OBC} = 33^\circ$. What is the measure of the angle $\widehat{AOC}$?
> 
> *Only a numerical answer is expected here.*
