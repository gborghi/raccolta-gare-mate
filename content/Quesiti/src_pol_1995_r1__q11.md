*Expected difference of largest and smallest among m draws from {1,…,n}*

> Let $n > m > 1$ be rational numbers. We randomly draw $m$ distinct numbers from the set $\{1, 2, \ldots, n\}$. Find the expected value of the difference between the largest and the smallest of the drawn numbers.
