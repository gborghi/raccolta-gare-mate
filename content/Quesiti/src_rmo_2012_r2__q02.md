*Positive integers with divisibility, same set of prime divisors*

> Let $a, b, c$ be positive integers such that $a$ divides $b^5$, $b$ divides $c^4$ and $c$ divides $a^3$. Prove that $abc$ divides $(a + b + c)^{21}$.
