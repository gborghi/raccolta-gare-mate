*Nearest integer to square root of max trapezium area given two triangle-product conditions*

> The four triangles obtained by drawing the diagonals $AC$ and $BD$ of a trapezium $ABCD$. The product of the areas of these triangles, taken two at a time, are computed. If among the six products two are 1296 and 576, determine the square root of the maximum possible area of the trapezium to the nearest integer.
