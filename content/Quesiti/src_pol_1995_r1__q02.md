*Cyclic system of equations with absolute values, positive integer n≥2*

> For a positive integer $n \ge 2$, solve the following system of equations:
> $$x_1(n) = x_2(n) + |x_3(n) - 1| \cdot |x_n(n) - 1|,$$
> $$x_2(n) = x_3(n) + |x_4(n) - 1| \cdot |x_1(n) - 1|,$$
> $$\vdots$$
> $$x_n(n) = x_1(n) + |x_2(n) - 1| \cdot |x_{n-1}(n) - 1|.$$
