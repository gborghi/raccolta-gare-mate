*Most economical way to buy 1.2 kg of CHOCOBM powder from cans of various sizes*

> In a bakery, a $200$ g can of CHOCOBM powdered chocolate costs R\$3.00, a $400$ g can costs R\$5.00 and an $800$ g can costs R\$8.00. For a recipe one needs $1.2$ kg of CHOCOBM in that bakery. Among the options below, what is the most economical way to buy $1.2$ kg of CHOCOBM in that bakery?
>
> - **(A)** 6 cans of 200 g
> - **(B)** 1 can of 400 g and 1 can of 800 g
> - **(C)** 1 can of 400 g and 1 can of 800 g
> - **(D)** 2 cans of 200 g and 1 can of 800 g
> - **(E)** 2 cans of 200 g and 2 cans of 400 g
