*Cone cross-section ellipse; find condition for two surface routes to intersect*

> A right circular cone stands on a horizontal base, radius $r$. Its vertex V is at a distance 1 from every point on the perimeter of the base. A plane section of the cone is an ellipse whose lowest point is L and whose highest point is H. On the curved surface of the cone, on one side of the plane VLH, two routes from L to H are marked. $R_1$ is along the semi-perimeter of the ellipse and $R_2$ is the route of shortest length. Find the condition that $R_1$ and $R_2$ intersect between L and H.
