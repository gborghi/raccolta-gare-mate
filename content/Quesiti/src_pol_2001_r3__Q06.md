*Given 2000 increasing positive integers below 10^100, find two nonempty disjoint subsets with equal size, equal element sums, and equal sums of squares.*

> Let $n_1 < n_2 < \cdots < n_{2000} < 10^{100}$ be given positive integers. Show that there exist two nonempty and disjoint subsets $A$ and $B$ of $\{n_1, n_2, \ldots, n_{2000}\}$ such that:
> (i) $A$ and $B$ have the same number of elements;
> (ii) $A$ and $B$ have the same sums of elements;
> (iii) $A$ and $B$ have the same sums of the squares of elements.
