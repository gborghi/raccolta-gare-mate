# Indian Olympiad Qualifier in Mathematics (IOQM) 2025 — Sep 07 — Quesito 19

*Right triangle ABC, square DEFG with F on AC, G on circle centred at A; find area of DEFG*

> In triangle $ABC$, $\angle B = 90^\circ$, $AB = 1$ and $BC = 2$. On the side $BC$ there are two points $D$ and $E$ such that $E$ lies between $C$ and $D$ and $DEFG$ is a square, where $F$ lies on $AC$ and $G$ lies on the circle through $B$ with centre $A$. If the area of $DEFG$ is $\dfrac{m}{n}$ where $m$ and $n$ are positive integers with $\gcd(m, n) = 1$, what is the value of $m + n$?
