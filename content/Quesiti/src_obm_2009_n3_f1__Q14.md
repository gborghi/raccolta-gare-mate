# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2009 — Nível 3 — Fase 1 — Quesito 14

*Function f:Z->Z with f(0)=0, f(1)=1, f(2)=2 and f(x+12)=f(x+21)=f(x); find f(2009).*

> Let $f : \mathbb{Z} \to \mathbb{Z}$ be a function such that $f(0) = 0$, $f(1) = 1$, $f(2) = 2$ and $f(x+12) = f(x+21) = f(x)$ for every $x \in \mathbb{Z}$. Then $f(2009)$ is: (A) $0$ (B) $1$ (C) $2$ (D) $3$ (E) $2009$
