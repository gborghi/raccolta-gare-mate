*Find the units digit of a 4-digit sum on a blurry calculator*

> When adding five consecutive numbers on her calculator, Esmeralda found a $4$-digit number: $200\square$. The last digit is not clear because the calculator screen is scratched, but she knows it is not zero. This digit can only be:
> 
> (A) $5$ \quad (B) $4$ \quad (C) $5$ \quad (D) $7$ \quad (E) $9$
