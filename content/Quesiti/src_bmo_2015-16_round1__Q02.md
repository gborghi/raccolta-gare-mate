# British Mathematical Olympiad 2015-16 — Round 1 — Quesito 2

*Cyclic quadrilateral; tangent at D meets line CB; prove triangle DEF isosceles.*

> Let $ABCD$ be a cyclic quadrilateral and let the lines $CD$ and $BA$ meet at $E$. The line through $D$ which is tangent to the circumcircle of $ABCD$ at $D$ meets the line $CB$ at $F$. Prove that triangle $DEF$ is isosceles.
