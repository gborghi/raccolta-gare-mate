# Coupe Animath Automne 2022 — Quesito 11

*Integers 1–31 on circle; counting neighbors; number opposite 11*

> The integers $1, 2, \ldots, 31$ are arranged around a circle in some order. For each of the 31 integers $k$, Matthieu counts:
> - $a$: the number of integers among the 9 that follow $k$ clockwise and are less than $k$;
> - $b$: the number of integers among the 9 that follow $k$ counterclockwise and are less than $k$;
> and computes $A(k)$ and $B(k)$. Matthieu notes that $A(k) = B(k)$ for all $k$. What is the number diametrically opposite to 11 on the circle?
