*Maximum size of union of two disjoint subsets with mapping condition*

> Let $A$ and $B$ be two subsets of $\{1, 2, 3, \ldots, 100\}$, satisfying $|A| = |B|$ and $A \cap B = \emptyset$. If $a \in A$ always implies $2a + 2 \in B$, then the maximum of $|A \cup B|$ is $($\quad$)$.
> 
> (A) $62$ $\quad$ (B) $66$ $\quad$ (C) $68$ $\quad$ (D) $74$
