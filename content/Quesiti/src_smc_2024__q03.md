*Two standard dice stacked with 9 visible faces summing to 33; find sum on touching faces*

> Two standard dice are placed on a table, with one on top of the other, so that only nine of the faces of the dice may be seen. The touching faces have the same number on them. The sum of the numbers on the visible faces is 33. What is the sum of the numbers on the touching faces?
>
> - **(A)** $1$
> - **(B)** $2$
> - **(C)** $3$
> - **(D)** $4$
> - **(E)** $6$
