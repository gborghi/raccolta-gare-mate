# Japan Mathematical Olympiad 2010 — Yosen (Preliminary) — Quesito 12

*Max new routes opened after closing one bridge in a 2010-node tree network*

> There are $2010$ airports. Some pairs of airports are connected by direct two-way flights, satisfying the following two conditions:\n\n(1) For any two airports $A$ and $B$, one can travel from $A$ to $B$ using one or more direct flights.\n\n(2) Closing any single open direct flight would cause condition (1) to fail.\n\nOne day, one of the open routes is closed. New direct routes (each different from the closed one, and each is a two-way flight) are then opened one by one so that conditions (1) and (2) are satisfied again. Find the maximum possible number of new routes that need to be opened.\n\nNote: A direct flight from airport $X$ to airport $Y$ also serves as a direct flight from $Y$ to $X$.
