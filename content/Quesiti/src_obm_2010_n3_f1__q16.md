*Non-negative reals a, b with a^3 + a < b - b^3; deduce the ordering.*

> The numbers $a$ and $b$ are non-negative reals such that $a^3+a<b-b^3$. Then
>
> - **(A)** $b<a<1$
> - **(B)** $a=b=1$
> - **(C)** $a<1<b$
> - **(D)** $a<b<1$
> - **(E)** $1<a<b$
