# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2003 — Nível 2 — Fase 1 — Quesito 15

*Logic puzzle: KAB or BAK means yes in Lucianês; what can be deduced*

> You are in a foreign country, LUCIANÊS, and do not know the language. You know that the words "BAK" and "KAB" mean *yes* or *no* (one each), but you do not know which is which. You meet a person who understands both Portuguese and Lucianês and ask them: "Does 'KAB' mean *yes*?" The person gives a one-word answer. Based only on their answer, you can deduce:
> 
> (A) KAB means *yes*.
> (B) KAB means *no*.
> (C) The person who answered was lying.
> (D) The person who answered told the truth.
> (E) It is impossible to determine without a Lucianês–Portuguese dictionary.
