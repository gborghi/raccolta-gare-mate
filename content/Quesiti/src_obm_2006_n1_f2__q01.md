*Sum of digits of a 101-digit number with 0s and 1s*

> The number $N = 10100100100101\ldots$ contains only the digits $0$ and $1$, with the property that between two consecutive digits $1$ there is alternately one or two zeros. The number $N$ has exactly $101$ digits. What is the sum of all digits of $N$?
