*Prove an inequality for three real numbers.*

> Let $a,b,c$ be real numbers. Prove the inequality
> $$(a^2+b^2-c^2)(b^2+c^2-a^2)(c^2+a^2-b^2)\le(a+b-c)^2(b+c-a)^2(c+a-b)^2.$$
