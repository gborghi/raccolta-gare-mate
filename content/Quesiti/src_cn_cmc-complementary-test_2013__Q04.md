*2t integers not divisible by s; any 2-partition has a group with subset sum divisible by s*

> (50 marks) Let $s$, $t$ be integers greater than $1$ and satisfy $s < 2^t$. Prove that there are $2t$ integers not divisible by $s$, such that if we divide them into two groups, then there must exist a group in which the sum of some integers can be divided by $s$.
