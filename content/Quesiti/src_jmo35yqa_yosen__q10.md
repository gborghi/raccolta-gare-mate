*Count functions f:S->S on {0..8} with f(x)f(y)-f(f(z)) divisible by 9 whenever 9 | x+y-z*

> Let $S = \{0, 1, 2, \ldots, 8\}$. Consider functions $f$ defined on $S$ and taking values in $S$ such that, for all elements $x, y, z$ of $S$, if $x + y - z$ is a multiple of $9$ then $f(x)\,f(y) - f(f(z))$ is also a multiple of $9$. How many such functions $f$ are there?
