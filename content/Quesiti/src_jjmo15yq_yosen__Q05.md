*Ways to fill 3x3 grid with 1-9 with row increasing right and left-column decreasing down*

> Consider ways to write one each of the distinct integers from $1$ to $9$ into the cells of a $3\times 3$ grid so that the following conditions are satisfied. How many such ways are there?
> \begin{itemize}
> \item In each row, among the three integers written, the integer in the rightmost cell is the largest and the integer in the leftmost cell is the smallest.
> \item In the leftmost column, among the three integers written, the integer in the topmost cell is the largest and the integer in the bottommost cell is the smallest.
> \end{itemize}
