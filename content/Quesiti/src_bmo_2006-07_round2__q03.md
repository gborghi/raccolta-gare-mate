*Acute triangle, angle BAC=60 degrees, Euler line meets sides, prove PO=HQ*

> Let $ABC$ be an acute-angled triangle with $AB > AC$ and $\angle BAC = 60^\circ$. Denote the circumcentre by $O$ and the orthocentre by $H$ and let $OH$ meet $AB$ at $P$ and $AC$ at $Q$. Prove that $PO = HQ$.
> 
> Note: The circumcentre of triangle $ABC$ is the centre of the circle which passes through the vertices $A$, $B$ and $C$. The orthocentre is the point of intersection of the perpendiculars from each vertex to the opposite side.
