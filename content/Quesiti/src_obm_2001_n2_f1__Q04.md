*Litres of water evaporated reducing pear water content from 90% to 60%*

> A pear has about $90\%$ water and $10\%$ solid matter. A producer places $100$ kilograms of pears to dry until the total mass is $60\%$ water. How many litres of water will be evaporated? (Remember: $1$ litre of water has mass $1$ kilogram.)
> 
> (A) $15$ litres \quad (B) $45$ litres \quad (C) $75$ litres \quad (D) $80$ litres \quad (E) $30$ litres
