*Real square matrices satisfying (A+B)^k = A^k + B^k for all positive integers k*

> Let $A$ and $B$ be real square matrices of the same dimension such that, for every positive integer $k$,
> $$(A + B)^k = A^k + B^k.$$
> Prove that if $A$ is invertible then $B$ is the zero matrix.
