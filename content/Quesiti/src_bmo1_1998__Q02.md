*Recurrence mod 100; find remainder of sum of squares mod 8*

> Let $a_0 = 19$, $a_1 = 98$. For $n \ge 1$, define $a_{n+1}$ to be the remainder of $a_n + a_{n-1}$ when divided by 100. What is the remainder when
> $$a_0^2 + a_1^2 + a_2^2 + \cdots + a_{1998}^2$$
> is divided by 8?
