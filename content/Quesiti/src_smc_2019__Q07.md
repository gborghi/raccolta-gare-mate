*Minimise p/q + r/s where {p,q,r,s} = {1,2,3,4}*

> Lucy is asked to choose $p$, $q$, $r$ and $s$ to be the numbers $1$, $2$, $3$ and $4$, in some order, so as to make the value of $\frac{p}{q} + \frac{r}{s}$ as small as possible.
> 
> What is the smallest value Lucy can achieve in this way?
>
> - **(A)** $\frac{7}{12}$
> - **(B)** $\frac{2}{3}$
> - **(C)** $\frac{3}{4}$
> - **(D)** $\frac{5}{6}$
> - **(E)** $\frac{11}{12}$
