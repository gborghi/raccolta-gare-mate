# British Mathematical Olympiad 1999-00 — Round 1 — Quesito 2

*Prove a certain expression in n is divisible by 2000*

> Show that, for every positive integer $n$,
> $$121^n - 25^n + 1900^n - (-4)^n$$
> is divisible by 2000.
