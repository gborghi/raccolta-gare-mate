# Japan Junior Mathematical Olympiad 2013 — Yosen (Preliminary) — Quesito 5

*Ratio AE:EC in isosceles right triangle with 45-degree angle*

> There is an isosceles triangle $ABC$ in which $\angle BAC$ is a right angle. There is a point $D$ on side $BC$ and a point $E$ on side $CA$ with $\angle ADE=45^\circ$. Given $BD:DC=1:5$, find $AE:EC$. Here $\overline{XY}$ denotes the length of segment $XY$.
