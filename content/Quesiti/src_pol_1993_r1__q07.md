*Find the volume of the convex hull of points defined by vector equalities in space.*

> Consider the points $A_0(0,0,0)$, $A_1(1,0,0)$, $A_2(0,1,0)$ and $A_3(0,0,1)$ in space. Let the point $P_{ij}$ ($i,j=0,1,2,3$) be defined by $\overrightarrow{A_0 P_{ij}}=\overrightarrow{A_i A_j}$. Find the volume of the convex hull of points $P_{ij}$.
