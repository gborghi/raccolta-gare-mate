*Inequality involving square roots for real a, b, c*

> If $a, b, c$ are real numbers, prove that
> $$\sqrt{2(a^2+b^2)} + \sqrt{2(b^2+c^2)} + \sqrt{2(c^2+a^2)} \ge \sqrt{3(a+b)^2 + 5(b+c)^2 + 3(c+a)^2}.$$
