*Sequence with halving/rounding rules; does it hit every positive integer?*

> George defines a sequence of positive integers $t_1, t_2, t_3, \ldots$ as follows.
> 
> He first sets $t_1 = 1$. Then, for $n \ge 1$:
> \begin{itemize}
> \item If $t_n$ is even, then $t_{n+1} = t_n / 2$.
> \item If $t_n$ is odd and greater than 1, then $t_{n+1} = t_n / 3$ rounded to the nearest integer.
> \item If $t_n = 1$, then $t_{n+1} = 2025k$ where $k$ is the number of terms equal to $1$ amongst $t_1, t_2, \ldots, t_n$.
> \end{itemize}
> Does this sequence contain every positive integer?
