# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2003 — Nível 3 — Fase 1 — Quesito 8

*Find n in tennis club with n left-handed, 2n right-handed players, given win ratio 3:4*

> A tennis club has $n$ left-handed players and $2n$ right-handed players, with fewer than 20 players in total. In the last championship, each player played against every other club player exactly once. Each left-handed player beat exactly 20 right-handed players (in total, across all matches), and the ratio of total games won by left-handed players to total games won by right-handed players is $3:4$. What is the value of $n$?
> 
> A) $3$ \quad B) $4$ \quad C) $5$ \quad D) $6$ \quad E) More information is needed.
