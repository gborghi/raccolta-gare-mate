*Max ET-nerds among four nerds making truth/lie statements*

> On Planet Nerdia, there exist three species of nerds: ET-nerds, UFO-nerds and OVNI-nerds. The first species always tells the truth, the second always lies, and the third always tells the truth and then lies, or first lies and then tells the truth. A certain day, Bruberson, a nerd very friendly with everyone, meets four nerds. They say:
> 
> $X$: "Today is sunny."
> $Y$: "The nerd $Z$ is going to lie."
> $Z$: "Today is not sunny."
> $W$: "The first nerd lied to us; we are all ET-nerds."
> 
> With how many ET-nerds did Bruberson speak at most?
> 
> (A) $0$ \quad (B) $1$ \quad (C) $2$ \quad (D) $3$ \quad (E) $4$
