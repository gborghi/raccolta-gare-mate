*Prove a product relation for a centrally symmetric hexagon from intersection points of its side lines.*

> Let $ABCDEF$ be a centrally symmetric hexagon. The lines $AB$ and $EF$ meet at $A'$, the lines $BC$ and $AF$ meet at $B'$, and the lines $AB$ and $CD$ meet at $C'$. Prove that
> $$AB\cdot BC\cdot CD=AA'\cdot BB'\cdot CC'.$$
