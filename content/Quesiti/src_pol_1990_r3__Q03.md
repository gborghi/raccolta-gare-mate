*In a round-robin tournament of n players, prove one of two structural conclusions about the results.*

> On a tournament, any two of the $n$ players played exactly one match (no draws). Prove that it is possible either
> 
> (i) to partition the league in two groups $A$ and $B$ such that everybody in $A$ defeated everybody in $B$; or
> 
> (ii) to arrange all the players in a chain $x_1, x_2, \ldots, x_n, x_1$ in such a way that each player defeated his successor.
