*For a set X of n elements, prove the sum of |A∩B| over all pairs of subsets equals n·4^{n-1}.*

> Let $X$ be a set of $n$ elements. Prove that the sum of the numbers of elements of sets $A\cap B$, where $A$ and $B$ run over all subsets of $X$, is equal to $n\cdot 4^{n-1}$.
