*Non-negative reals S=a+2b+3c, T=a+b^2+c^3: find min of T-S and max of T given S=4*

> (Test Paper B1, Problem 1) Let $a$, $b$, $c$ be non-negative real numbers. Denote $S = a + 2b + 3c$, $T = a + b^2 + c^3$.
> (1) Find the minimum of $T - S$.
> (2) If $S = 4$, find the maximum of $T$.
