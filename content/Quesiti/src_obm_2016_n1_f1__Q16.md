*Booklet pagination: relate page numbers on the front/back of a sheet.*

> In a booklet, determine the requested page number(s) on a sheet.
> 
> [Source is the answer key; original statement/options not reproduced. From the solution: page $1$ has page $2$ on its back and pages $59$ and $60$ on its other part. So page $7$ (which is $1+6$) has on its back page $8$ (which is $2+6$) and pages $53$ ($=59-6$) and $54$ ($=60-6$) on its other part.]
