*Inequality for ordered reals with equality iff arithmetic progression*

> Given $n > 2$ and reals $x_1 \le x_2 \le \cdots \le x_n$, show that $$\left(\sum_{i,j} |x_i - x_j|\right)^2 \le \frac{2}{3}(n^2 - 1) \sum_{i,j} (x_i - x_j)^2.$$ Show that we have equality iff the sequence is an arithmetic progression.
