*Prove that a distance-non-increasing surjection of a cube onto itself is an isometry.*

> Let $\mathscr{C}$ be a cube and let $f:\mathscr{C}\to\mathscr{C}$ be a surjection such that $|PQ|\ge|f(P)f(Q)|$ for all $P,Q\in\mathscr{C}$. Prove that $f$ is an isometry.
