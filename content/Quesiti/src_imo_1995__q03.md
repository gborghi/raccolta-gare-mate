*Determine n with point areas equal to ri+rj+rk*

> Determine all integers n > 3 for which there exist n points A1, . . . , An
> in the plane, no three collinear, and real numbers r1, . . . , rn such that
> for 1 ≤i < j < k ≤n, the area of △AiAjAk is ri + rj + rk.
> 36th International Mathematical Olympiad
> Second Day - Toronto - July 20, 1995
> Time Limit: 41
> 2 hours
