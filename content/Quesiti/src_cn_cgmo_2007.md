- [[src_cn_cgmo_2007__Q04|Quesito 4]] — Set with m symmetry axes: prove m ≤ n
- [[src_cn_cgmo_2007__Q05|Quesito 5]] — Prove DE perpendicular to EF in triangle with given angles
