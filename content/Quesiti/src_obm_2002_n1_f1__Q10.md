*Fraction of road travelled when turning back for a magazine*

> Marcelo left exactly $20$ minutes before going from his house to school. On the way, he realized he had forgotten at home the magazine Eureka! that he was going to show a friend, and resolved to return; this way he would arrive $20$ minutes after the start. But, instead of returning, he continued to walk, reaching school $10$ minutes late. What fraction of the road already travelled would correspond to this point?
> 
> (A) $\dfrac{2}{5}$ (B) $\dfrac{9}{20}$ (C) $\dfrac{1}{2}$ (D) $\dfrac{2}{3}$ (E) $\dfrac{9}{10}$
