# Regional Mathematical Olympiad 2016 — Region 4 — Quesito 5

*Triangle with altitude and median; incentre of sub-triangle lies on median*

> Let $ABC$ be a triangle, $AD$ an altitude and $AE$ a median. Assume $B$, $D$, $E$, $C$ lie in that order on the line $BC$. Suppose the incentre of $\triangle ADC$ lies on $AE$. Find, with proof, the angles of $\triangle ABC$.
