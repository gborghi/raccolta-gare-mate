# Coupe Animath Printemps 2017 — Quesito 2

*Sum of 5-digit number and its digit-reversal has an even digit*

> Show that if $n$ is a five-digit integer and $m$ is the integer obtained by reversing the order of the digits of $n$ (for example, if $n = 34170$ then $m = 07143$), then the decimal representation of $n + m$ contains at least one even digit.
