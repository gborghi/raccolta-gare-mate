*Value of n given repeated tear-one-into-n yields 2011 pieces*

> Safira tore a sheet of paper into $n$ pieces and then took one of those pieces and tore it again into $n$ pieces. She continued this process, that is, on each step she took one of the pieces and tore it into $n$ pieces. Knowing that the final quantity of pieces of paper she obtained is $2011$, into how many pieces did Safira tear the sheet?
> 
> (A) $15$ \quad (B) $26$ \quad (C) $28$ \quad (D) $33$ \quad (E) $36$
