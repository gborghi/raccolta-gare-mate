*Max number after 2003 operations: replace digit by its square or double (choose max)*

> A written number is operated on as follows: if the number is greater than $1$, it is replaced by its square; if it equals $1$, it is doubled. Starting from any single digit and applying this operation exactly $2003$ times, what is the greatest number that can be obtained?
> 
> A) $2^{2003}$ \quad B) $4^{2003}$ \quad C) $2^{(2^{\cdots})}$ \quad D) $2^{(2^{2003})}$ \quad E) $2^{(2^{2004})}$
