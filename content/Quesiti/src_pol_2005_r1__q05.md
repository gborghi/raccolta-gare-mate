*Cyclic quadrilateral; two triangles have equal inradii, prove the other two do too.*

> A quadrilateral $ABCD$ is inscribed in a circle, and the incircles of the triangles $ABC$ and $BCD$ have equal radii. Prove that the incircles of the triangles $CDA$ and $DAB$ have equal radii as well.
