*Five congruent squares of side 2a placed edge to edge; two circles with same centre drawn through vertices; find area between the circles.*

> Five congruent squares, each of side $2a$, are placed edge to edge. Two circles with the same centre are drawn through the vertices as shown. What is the area of the region between the two circles?
>
> - **(A)** $2\pi a^2$
> - **(B)** $4\pi a^2$
> - **(C)** $6\pi a^2$
> - **(D)** $8\pi a^2$
> - **(E)** $10\pi a^2$
