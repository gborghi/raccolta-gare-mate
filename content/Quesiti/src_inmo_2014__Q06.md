# Indian National Mathematical Olympiad 2014 — Quesito 6

*Find all collections F of 2^{n-1} subsets of {1,...,n} closed under symmetric difference bound*

> Let $n$ be a natural number and $X = \{1, 2, \ldots, n\}$. For each subset $A$ of $X$ we define $\Delta A$ to be the set of all those elements of $X$ which belong to exactly one of $A$ and $B$. Let $\mathcal{F}$ be a collection of subsets of $X$ such that for any two distinct elements $A$ and $B$ in $\mathcal{F}$, the set $A \triangle B$ has at most $2^{n-1}$ elements. Find all such collections $\mathcal{F}$ with $2^{n-1}$ elements.
