*Minimise AP+BP+CP for point P on perimeter of right-angled triangle*

> Triangle $ABC$ has a right angle at $A$. Among all points $P$ on the perimeter of the triangle, find the position of $P$ such that
> $$AP + BP + CP$$
> is minimised.
