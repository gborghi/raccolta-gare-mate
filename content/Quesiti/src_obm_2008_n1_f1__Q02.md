*Expression for change when buying olive oil and juice*

> Esmeralda buys five cans of olive oil at four reais and seventy centavos each, five cartons of juice at three reais and twelve centavos each, and three boxes of juice at eighty centavos each. She pays with a fifty-real note and wants to know how much change she will receive. Which of the following arithmetic expressions represents the solution to this problem?
> 
> A) $50 - 5 \times (4{,}70 + 3{,}12) + 3 \times 6 \times 0{,}80$
> 
> B) $5 \times 4{,}70 + 5 \times 3{,}12 + 3 \times 6 \times 0{,}80 - 50$
> 
> C) $-\left[5 \times (4{,}70 + 3{,}12) + 3 \times 6 \times 0{,}80\right] + 50$
> 
> D) $50 - \left[5 \times (4{,}70 + 3{,}12) + 3 \times 6 \times 0{,}80\right]$
> 
> E) $50 - \left[5 \times (4{,}70 + 3{,}12) + 6{,}80\right]$
