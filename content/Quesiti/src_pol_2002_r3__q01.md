*Find positive integers a,b with a²+1, b²+1 prime satisfying equation*

> Determine all positive integers $a, b$ such that the numbers $a^2 + 1$ and $b^2 + 1$ are prime and the following equality
> $$(a^2 + 1)(b^2 + 1) = (a^2 - 1)(b^2 - 1) + r^2 + 1$$
> holds.
