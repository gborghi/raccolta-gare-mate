*Permutation of positive integers: infinite i with lcm(a_i, a_{i+1}) <= 3i/4*

> Let $a_1, a_2, \ldots$ be a permutation of all positive integers. Prove that there exist infinitely many positive integers $i$ such that $(a_i, a_{i+1}) \le \dfrac{3}{4}i$. (posed by Chen Yonggao)
