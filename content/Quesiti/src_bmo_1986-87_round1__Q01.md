*Integer solutions of two Diophantine equations*

> (a) Find, with proof, all integer solutions of
> $$a^3 + b^3 = 9.$$
> 
> (b) Find, with proof, all integer solutions of
> $$35a^4 + 46a^2b^2 + 13b^4 = 9.$$
