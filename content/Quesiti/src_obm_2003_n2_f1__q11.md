*Arithmetic minus harmonic mean of 4 and 6*

> Consider the following definitions:
> - The *arithmetic mean* of two positive real numbers is half their sum.
> - The *harmonic mean* of two positive real numbers is the reciprocal of the arithmetic mean of their reciprocals.
> 
> The difference between the arithmetic mean and the harmonic mean of the numbers 4 and 6 is:
> 
> (A) 0.1 \quad (B) 0.2 \quad (C) 0.3 \quad (D) 0.4 \quad (E) 0.5
