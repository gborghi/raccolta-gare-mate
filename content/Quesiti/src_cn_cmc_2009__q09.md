*Count integer-coefficient lines meeting ellipse and hyperbola with AC=BD*

> Suppose that the line $l$: $y = -kx + m$ (where $k$ and $m$ are integers) intercepts the ellipse $\dfrac{x^2}{4} + \dfrac{y^2}{12} = 1$ at two different points $A$, $B$, and intercepts the hyperbola $\dfrac{x^2}{4} - \dfrac{y^2}{12} = 1$ at two different points $C$, $D$. If $\overrightarrow{AC} = \overrightarrow{BD}$, how many different possibilities are there for the line $l$? Explain the reason.
