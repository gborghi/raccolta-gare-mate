# Japan Junior Mathematical Olympiad 2019 — Yosen (Preliminary) — Quesito 4

*Count of 1..999 divisible by 2 more times than by 5*

> Among the integers from $1$ to $999$ inclusive, how many are such that the number of times they are divisible by $2$ is greater than the number of times they are divisible by $5$? (For a prime $p$, the "number of times divisible by $p$" of an integer $n$ is the largest $k$ with $p^k \mid n$.)
