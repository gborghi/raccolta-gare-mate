*2×3 crossword-number grid using digits 1–9 once each; Across: multiple of 9, a square; Down: multiple of 11, multiple of 13 and 19; find the unused digit*

> A crossword is to be filled with the digits 1 to 9, with each used exactly once. The clues are: Across — 1. A multiple of 9; 3. A square. Down — 1. A multiple of 11; 2. A multiple of 13 and of 19. Which digit is not used?
>
> - **(A)** $9$
> - **(B)** $8$
> - **(C)** $5$
> - **(D)** $3$
> - **(E)** $2$
