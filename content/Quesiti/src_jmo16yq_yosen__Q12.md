# Japan Mathematical Olympiad 2006 — Yosen (Preliminary) — Quesito 12

*Count 20-tuples of positive integers satisfying product inequality conditions*

> Let $(p_1, p_2, \ldots, p_{10}, q_1, q_2, \ldots, q_{10})$ be a $20$-tuple of positive integers with $p_1 = q_{10} = 1$, and for each $i = 1, 2, \ldots, 9$, the condition $p_i q_i - p_{i+1} q_{i+1} \ge 1$ holds. How many such $20$-tuples are there?
