*Line through orthocenter H meets AC at D, BC at E; line through H perpendicular to DE meets AB at F; prove DH/HE = AF/FB.*

> A line passes through the orthocenter $H$ of an acute-angled triangle $ABC$ and meets the sides $AC$ and $BC$ at $D$ and $E$, respectively. The line through $H$ perpendicular to $DE$ intersects the line $AB$ at point $F$. Prove that $\frac{DH}{HE} = \frac{AF}{FB}$.
