*A1-11: ellipse x^2/2+y^2=1, foci F1,F2, P in first quadrant, extensions of PF1,PF2 meet ellipse at Q1,Q2; max of r1-r2 (incircle radii).*

> [Test Paper A1, Problem 11] (20 marks) In a plane rectangular coordinate system $xOy$, the left and right foci of the ellipse $\Gamma:\dfrac{x^2}{2}+y^2=1$ are $F_1,F_2$, respectively. Let $P$ be a point on $\Gamma$ in the first quadrant, and the extensions of $PF_1$, $PF_2$ intersect $\Gamma$ at points $Q_1,Q_2$, respectively. Let $r_1,r_2$ be the radii of the incircles of $\triangle PF_1Q_2$, $\triangle PF_2Q_1$, respectively. Find the maximum of $r_1-r_2$.
