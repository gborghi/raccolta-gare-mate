*Prove inequality involving sum of fractions for positive reals*

> If $a$, $b$, $c$ are three positive real numbers, prove that
> $$\frac{a^2+1}{b+c} + \frac{b^2+1}{c+a} + \frac{c^2+1}{a+b} \ge 3.$$
