*Count pairs {A,B} of subsets of {1,...,10} with fixed intersection {5,7,8}*

> Let $X = \{1, 2, 3, \ldots, 10\}$. Find the number of pairs $\{A, B\}$ such that $A \subseteq X$, $B \subseteq X$, $A \neq B$ and $A \cap B = \{5, 7, 8\}$.
