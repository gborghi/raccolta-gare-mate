*Find minimal f(n) so any m-element subset of n consecutive integers has 3 mutually prime elements*

> For integer $n \ge 4$, find the minimal integer $f(n)$, such that for any subset with $f(n)$ elements of the set $\{m, m+1, \ldots, m+n-1\}$ ($m \in \mathbb{N}$), there are at least 3 mutually prime elements.
