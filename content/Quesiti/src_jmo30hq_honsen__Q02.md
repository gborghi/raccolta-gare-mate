# Japan Mathematical Olympiad 2020 — Honsen (Final) — Quesito 2

*Circumcircles and perpendicularity condition in triangle ABC*

> Let $ABC$ be a triangle with $BC < AB$ and $BC < AC$. On sides $AB$ and $AC$, take points $D$ and $E$ respectively such that $BD = CE = BC$. Let $P$ be the intersection of line $BE$ and line $CD$. Let $Q$ be one of the intersection points of the circumcircle of triangle $ABE$ and the circumcircle of triangle $ACD$ other than $A$. Prove that line $PQ$ and line $BC$ are perpendicular.
