# British Mathematical Olympiad 1979-80 — Round 2 — Quesito 2

*Find all primes in the sequence u_{n+1} = u_n^2 - u_n + 1*

> Determine, with proof, all the prime numbers in the sequence $\{u_n\}$ of integers defined by
> $$u_2 = 2, \quad u_{n+1} = u_n^2 - u_n + 1 \qquad (n > 0).$$
