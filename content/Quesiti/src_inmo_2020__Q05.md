*Characterise which regular polygon vertex counts are frameable on parallel lines*

> Infinitely many equidistant parallel lines are drawn in the plane. A positive integer $n \geq 3$ is called \emph{frameable} if it is possible to draw a regular polygon with $n$ sides all of whose vertices lie on these lines and no line contains more than one vertex of the polygon.
> 
> (a) Show that $3, 4, 6$ are frameable.
> 
> (b) Show that every $n \geq 7$ is not frameable.
> 
> (c) Determine whether $5$ is frameable.
