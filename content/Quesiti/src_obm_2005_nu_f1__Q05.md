*Find all alpha making a cosine matrix have zero determinant*

> Determine all values of $\alpha$ for which the matrix $A = (a_{ij})_{n \times n}$, defined by
> $$a_{ij} = \cos((i-1)\cdot j\alpha),$$
> for $1 \le i,\, j \le n$, has determinant equal to zero.
