*Area of quadrilateral IXJY inside rectangle ABCD of area 4 (lycée version)*

> Let $ABCD$ be a rectangle of area 4. Let $I$ be the midpoint of $[AD]$ and $J$ the midpoint of $[BC]$. Let $X$ be the intersection point of $(AJ)$ and $(BI)$, and let $Y$ be the intersection point of $(DJ)$ and $(CI)$. What is the area of the quadrilateral $IXJY$?
