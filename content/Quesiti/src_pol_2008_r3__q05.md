*If all sections of a parallelepiped through the midpoints of three pairwise disjoint non-parallel edges have equal area, prove it is a cuboid.*

> The areas of all sections of a parallelepiped $\mathscr{R}$ by planes, passing through the midpoints of three pairwise disjoint and non-parallel edges, are equal. Show that $\mathscr{R}$ is a cuboid.
