*Given irrational a in (0,1) and positive integer N, find positive integers p,q,r,s with p/q < a < r/s, r/s - p/q < 1/N, and rq - ps = 1.*

> Let be given an irrational number $a$ in the interval $(0,1)$ and a positive integer $N$. Prove that there exist positive integers $p,q,r,s$ such that
> $$\frac{p}{q} < a < \frac{r}{s}, \quad \frac{r}{s} - \frac{p}{q} < \frac{1}{N}, \quad \text{and} \quad rq - ps = 1.$$
