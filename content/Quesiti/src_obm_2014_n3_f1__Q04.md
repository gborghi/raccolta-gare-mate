*Area of quadrilateral formed by apexes of equilateral triangles on a unit square*

> Consider a square $ABCD$ of side $1$. Externally to the square, equilateral triangles $ABE$, $BCF$, $CDG$ and $DAH$ are formed. What is the area of the quadrilateral $EFGH$?
> (A) $2$ (B) $2\sqrt{3}$ (C) $2+\sqrt{3}$ (D) $3$ (E) $6$
