*Ratio AR:RC for interior point with given angles and ratios*

> Let $P$ be a point in the interior of triangle $ABC$ such that $\angle APB = \angle APC = 130^\circ$ and $PB : PC = 2 : 3$. On the sides $AB$ and $AC$, respectively, take points $Q$ and $R$ such that $\angle APQ = \angle APR = 80^\circ$. Given that $AQ : QB = 4 : 3$, find $AR : RC$.
