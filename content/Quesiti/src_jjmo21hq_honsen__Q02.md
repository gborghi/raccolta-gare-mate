*Find maximum k distinct values of xy/(x+y)^2 from 2023 reals*

> On a blackboard, 2023 positive real numbers are written. For every pair $x, y$ chosen from these numbers, compute $\dfrac{xy}{(x+y)^2}$. The number of distinct values obtained is exactly $k$. Find the maximum possible value of $k$.
