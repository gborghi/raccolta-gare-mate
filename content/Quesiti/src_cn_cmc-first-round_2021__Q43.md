*B-10: Γ graph of y=1/|x|; P in first quadrant, Q in second quadrant, PQ tangent to part of Γ in second quadrant at Q; min of |PQ|.*

> [Test Paper B, Problem 10] (20 marks) In a plane rectangular coordinate system $xOy$, the graph of function $y=\dfrac{1}{|x|}$ is $\Gamma$. Let points $P,Q$ on $\Gamma$ satisfy: $P$ is in the first quadrant, $Q$ is in the second quadrant, and line $PQ$ is tangent to the part of $\Gamma$ in the second quadrant at point $Q$. Find the minimum of $|PQ|$.
