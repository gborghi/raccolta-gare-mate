*Chord through triangle parallel to BC; find DE*

> There is a triangle $ABC$ with $AB = 8$ and $AC = 9$. A line parallel to line $BC$ meets the circumcircle of triangle $ABC$ at two distinct points $P, Q$, and meets sides $AB$, $AC$ at points $D$, $E$ respectively. The four points $P, D, E, Q$ lie in this order. When $PD = 2$ and $EQ = 3$, find the length of segment $DE$. (Here $XY$ denotes the length of segment $XY$.)
