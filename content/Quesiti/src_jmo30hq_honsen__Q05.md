*Find all positive integer sequences where gcd condition holds for all m,n*

> Find all sequences $a_1, a_2, \ldots$ of positive integers such that for any positive integers $m, n$,
> $$\gcd(a_{m+n}, a_m + a_n) > c(m+n)$$
> holds, where $c$ is some fixed positive real number.
> 
> Here, for positive integers $x, y$, $\gcd(x, y)$ denotes the greatest common divisor of $x$ and $y$.
