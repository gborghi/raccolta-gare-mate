# IMO 1993 (34th IMO) — Quesito 1

*Prove x^n+5x^(n-1)+3 is irreducible over integers*

> Let f(x) = xn + 5xn−1 + 3, where n > 1 is an integer. Prove that f(x)
> cannot be expressed as the product of two nonconstant polynomials
> with integer coefficients.
