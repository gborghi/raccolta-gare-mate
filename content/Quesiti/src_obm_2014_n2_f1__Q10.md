*Find minimum cost to visit all five cities given price table*

> The table below shows the price in reais of trips between two of the five cities $A$, $B$, $C$, $D$, and $E$. The rows indicate the city of departure and the columns the city of arrival. For example, going from $A$ to $B$ costs 1 real and from $B$ to $A$ costs 2 reais. Pablo wants to visit all five cities spending the minimum possible (note that the price of the return trip to the starting city is also included). How much does Pablo need to spend?
> 
> (A) 4 reais $\quad$ (B) 5 reais $\quad$ (C) 6 reais $\quad$ (D) 9 reais $\quad$ (E) 11 reais
