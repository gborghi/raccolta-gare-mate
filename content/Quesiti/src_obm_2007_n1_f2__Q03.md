*In a number array each ring sum equals the square of its central cell; evaluate a ratio for 2007.*

> By the observed pattern, the sums are equal to the square of the central parcel (the one whose number of parcels to its left equals the number of parcels to its right). Hence, putting $A=2007^2$, evaluate $\dfrac{A}{223^2}=\dfrac{2007^2}{223^2}=\left(\dfrac{2007}{223}\right)^2=9^2=81$. Determine this value (the answer is $81$).
