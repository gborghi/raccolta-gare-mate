# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2014 — Nível 3 — Fase 1 — Quesito 2

*Logic puzzle: four doors, exactly one of four messages lies*

> Roraima Jonas, an intrepid adventurer, upon leaving a cave comes across four doors, numbered $1$ to $4$, and four messages. The messages say:
> Message 1: "Doors 1 and 2 are safe."
> Message 2: "Exactly two of doors 1, 2 and 3 are safe."
> Message 3: "Door 1 is safe."
> Message 4: "All doors are safe."
> Roraima Jonas is studious and, for this reason, knows that exactly one of the messages lies and the others tell the truth (that is, only one is a lie). However, exactly one of the doors is not safe (it would activate a trap). Which door can Roraima Jonas guarantee to be safe?
> (A) 1 (B) 2 (C) 3 (D) 4 (E) There is no door that Roraima Jonas can guarantee to be safe.
