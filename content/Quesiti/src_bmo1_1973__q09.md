*Lines L_r (r=1,2,3) defined by a determinant are concurrent*

> $$L_r = \begin{vmatrix} x & y & 1 \\ a + r\cos\phi & b + r\sin\phi & 1 \\ 1 + r\cos\phi & a + r\sin\phi & 1 \end{vmatrix}$$
> 
> Show that the lines $L_r = 0$, $r = 1, 2, 3$ are concurrent and find the co-ordinates of their concurrence.
