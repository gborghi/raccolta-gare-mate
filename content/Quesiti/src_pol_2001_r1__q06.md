*From a + b*sqrt(2001) = (x + y*sqrt(2001))^2000 prove a >= 44b.*

> The integers $a, b, x, y$ satisfy the equality
> $$a + b\sqrt{2001} = \left(x + y\sqrt{2001}\right)^{2000}.$$
> Prove that $a \ge 44b$.
