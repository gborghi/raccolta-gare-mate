# Japan Mathematical Olympiad 2015 — Yosen (Preliminary) — Quesito 2

*Find BC given two tangents meet at P and AB parallel to CP*

> Three points $A$, $B$, $C$ lie on a circle. Let $P$ be the intersection of the tangents to the circle at $B$ and at $C$. Suppose that lines $AB$ and $CP$ are parallel, $AB = 3$, and $BP = 4$. Find the length $BC$.
> 
> (Here $XY$ denotes the length of segment $XY$.)
