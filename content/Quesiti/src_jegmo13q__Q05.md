# Japan EGMO Selection / Girls MO 2024 — Quesito 5

*Prove two perpendiculars meet on circumcircle given concyclicity condition*

> Let $G$ be the centroid of triangle $ABC$, and let $M$, $N$ be the midpoints of sides $AB$, $AC$, respectively. Suppose that the four points $A$, $G$, $M$, $N$ are concyclic. Prove that the line through $A$ perpendicular to line $AG$ and the line through $G$ perpendicular to line $BC$ meet on the circumcircle of triangle $ABC$.
