*Maximum expected number of black-face-up coins after 2020 row/column flip operations*

> There is an $8 \times 8$ grid; each cell contains a coin. Each coin shows either white (face up) or black (face up). Initially all coins show black. One operation proceeds as follows: player $A$ selects $5$ squares in a single row; player $B$ then selects $1$ of those $5$ squares, and all $8$ coins in the row containing that square and all $8$ coins in the column containing that square are flipped (the coin at the intersection is flipped once). After $2020$ such operations, find the maximum possible expected number of coins showing black (face up), where player $A$ chooses their strategy to maximize this expected value.
