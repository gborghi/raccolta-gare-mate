*Find all positive integer triples satisfying a product equal to 2*

> Find all triples of positive integers $(a, b, c)$ such that
> $$\left(1 + \frac{1}{a}\right)\left(1 + \frac{1}{b}\right)\left(1 + \frac{1}{c}\right) = 2.$$
