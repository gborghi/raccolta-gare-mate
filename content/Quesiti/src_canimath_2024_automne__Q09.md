*Integers a≥2,b≥2 with (a−1)/(b−1)−a/b=1; show a/b and (a−1)/(b−1) are integers*

> Let $a \ge 2$ and $b \ge 2$ be two integers. Suppose that
> $$\frac{a-1}{b-1} - \frac{a}{b} = 1.$$
> Show that $\dfrac{a}{b}$ and $\dfrac{a-1}{b-1}$ are integers.
