# Japan Mathematical Olympiad 2024 — Yosen (Preliminary) — Quesito 9

*Find DF in cyclic quadrilateral with angle bisector and equal angle conditions*

> Cyclic quadrilateral $ABCD$ satisfies $AB = 7$ and $BC = 18$. The angle bisector of $\angle CDA$ meets side $BC$ at point $E$, and a point $F$ on segment $DE$ satisfies $\angle AED = \angle FCD$. Given $BE = 5$ and $EF = 3$, find the length $DF$. (Here $XY$ denotes the length of segment $XY$.)
