*Count 3-good non-empty subsets of {0,1,...,9}*

> A finite non-empty set $S$ of integers is called $3$-good if the sum of the elements of $S$ is divisible by $3$. Find the number of $3$-good non-empty subsets of $\{0, 1, 2, \ldots, 9\}$.
