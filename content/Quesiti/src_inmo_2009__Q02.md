# Indian National Mathematical Olympiad 2009 — Quesito 2

*Sequence of 0/1 by parity of divisor count; rational or irrational?*

> Define a sequence $(a_n)_{n \ge 1}$ as follows:
> $$a_n = \begin{cases} 0, & \text{if the number of positive divisors of } n \text{ is odd,} \\ 1, & \text{if the number of positive divisors of } n \text{ is even.} \end{cases}$$
> (The positive divisors of $n$ include $1$ as well as $n$.) Let $x = 0.a_1 a_2 a_3 \ldots$ be the real number whose decimal expansion contains $a_n$ in the $n$-th place, $n \ge 1$. Determine, with proof, whether $x$ is rational or irrational.
