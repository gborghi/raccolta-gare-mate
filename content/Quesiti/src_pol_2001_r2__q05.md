*In triangle ABC with incenter I and D=AI∩BC, show AI+CD=AC iff angle B = 60 + (1/2) angle C.*

> In a triangle $ABC$, $I$ is the incenter and $D$ the intersection point of $AI$ and $BC$. Show that $AI+CD=AC$ if and only if $\angle B=60^\circ+\frac{1}{2}\angle C$.
