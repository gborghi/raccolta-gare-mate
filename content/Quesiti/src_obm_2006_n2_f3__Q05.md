*Find angle MNR in acute triangle given orthocenter and midpoints*

> Let $ABC$ be an acute triangle and $H$ its orthocenter. Let $M$, $N$, and $R$ be the midpoints of $AB$, $BC$, and $AH$, respectively. Find the measure of angle $M\hat{N}R$ if angle $A\hat{B}C = 70^\circ$.
