*Area and perimeter of figures made of unit-square diamonds in a sequence*

> Rubinho built a sequence of $10$ figures, each formed by small squares (rhombus-shaped tiles) of side $1$ cm, as shown. Figure 2, for example, has area $5 \text{ cm}^2$ and perimeter $12 \text{ cm}$.
> 
> a) What is the area of Figure 5?
> 
> b) What is the perimeter of Figure 10?
