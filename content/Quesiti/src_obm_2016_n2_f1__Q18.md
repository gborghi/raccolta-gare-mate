*Smallest square root of product of rectangle dimensions, perimeter 58*

> Let $x$ and $y$ be the dimensions of the rectangle and $n$ the side of the square. Since $2x+2y=58$, $x+y=29$. Assuming $x\le y$, the possible dimensions are $(x,y)\in\{(1,28),(2,27),(3,26),\dots,(14,15)\}$. Of these, only $(4,25)$ has product a perfect square, so $n=\sqrt{4\cdot 25}=10$. What is the value of $n$? (Note: original multiple-choice options are not reproduced in the source gabarito.)
