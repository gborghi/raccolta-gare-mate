*Set of integers split into two sequences; find f(240)*

> The set of all positive integers is the union of two disjoint subsets $\{f(1), f(2), \ldots, f(n), \ldots\}$, $\{g(1), g(2), \ldots, g(n), \ldots\}$, where
> $$f(1) < f(2) < \cdots < f(n) < \cdots,$$
> $$g(1) < g(2) < \cdots < g(n) < \cdots,$$
> and $g(n) = f(f(n)) + 1$ for all $n \ge 1$.
> Determine $f(240)$.
