*Set of ten distinct elements with sum divisible by 6; replace ten with seven*

> (a) Find a set $A$ of ten distinct elements such that six distinct elements of $A$ have a sum which is divisible by 6. (b) Is it possible to find such a set when 'ten' is replaced by 'seven'?
