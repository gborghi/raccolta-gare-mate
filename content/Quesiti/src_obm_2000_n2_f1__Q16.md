*Nim game: first player's winning move with 1000 sticks, draw 1-5 each turn*

> In a game for two players, the players alternately take $1, 2, 3, 4$, or $5$ sticks from a pile that initially has $1000$ sticks. The player who takes the last stick wins. How many sticks should the first player take to guarantee a win?
> 
> A) $1$ \quad B) $2$ \quad C) $3$ \quad D) $4$ \quad E) None; the first player cannot guarantee a win.
