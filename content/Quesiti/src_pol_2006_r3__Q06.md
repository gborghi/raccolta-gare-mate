*Find all integer pairs (a,b) for which (x^2+ax+b)P(x) can be a monic polynomial all of whose other coefficients are 1 or -1.*

> Find all pairs of integers $(a, b)$ for which there exists a polynomial $P(x)$ with integer coefficients such that the product $(x^2 + ax + b)P(x)$ is a polynomial of the form
> $$x^n + c_{n-1}x^{n-1} + \cdots + c_1 x + c_0,$$
> where each of $c_0, \ldots, c_{n-1}$ is equal to $1$ or $-1$.
