*Circle tangent to AC and AB, prove AD=DP implies BP=AC*

> Let points $A$, $B$ and $C$ lie on a circle $\Gamma$. Circle $A$ is tangent to $AC$ at $A$. It meets $\Gamma$ again at $D$ and the line $AB$ again at $P$. The point $P$ lies between $B$ and $D$. Prove that if $AD = DP$, then $BP = AC$.
