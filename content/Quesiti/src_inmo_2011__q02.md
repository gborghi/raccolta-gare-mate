*Characterise natural numbers expressible as a+b+c with a|b|c*

> Call a natural number $n$ **faithful** if there exist natural numbers $a < b < c$ such that $a$ divides $b$, $b$ divides $c$, and $n = a + b + c$.
> (i) Show that all but a finite number of natural numbers are faithful.
> (ii) Find the set of all natural numbers which are **not** faithful.
