*Concyclicity of two points defined via angle and parallel conditions in triangle*

> In triangle $ABC$, point $A_1$ lies on side $BC$ and point $B_1$ lies on side $AC$. Let $P$ and $Q$ be points on segments $AA_1$ and $BB_1$, respectively, such that $PQ$ is parallel to $AB$. Let $P_1$ be a point on line $PB_1$, such that $B_1$ lies strictly between $P$ and $P_1$, and $\angle PP_1C = \angle BAC$. Let $Q_1$ be a point on line $QA_1$, such that $A_1$ lies strictly between $Q$ and $Q_1$, and $\angle CQ_1Q = \angle CBA$.
> 
> Prove that points $P$, $Q$, $P_1$, and $Q_1$ are concyclic.
