# British Mathematical Olympiad 1978-79 — Round 1 — Quesito 3

*Distinct odd positive integers with distinct pairwise differences; prove sum lower bound*

> $S$ is a set of distinct positive odd integers $\{a_i\}$, $i = 1$ to $n$.
> No two differences $|a_i - a_j|$ are equal, $1 \le i < j \le n$.
> 
> Prove that
> $$\sum_{i=1}^{n} a_i \ge \tfrac{1}{3}n(n^2 + 2).$$
