*Square ABCD with two perpendicular transversals; find side length*

> In square $ABCD$, points $P$, $Q$, $R$, $S$ lie on sides $AB$, $BC$, $CD$, $DA$ respectively. Line $PR$ is parallel to $BC$, and line $SQ$ is parallel to $AB$. Let $Z$ be the intersection of $PR$ and $SQ$. Given $BP = 7$, $BQ = 6$, $DZ = 5$, find the side length of square $ABCD$.
> 
> (Here, $XY$ denotes the length of segment $XY$.)
