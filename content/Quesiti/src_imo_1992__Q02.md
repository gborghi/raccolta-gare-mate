# IMO 1992 (33rd IMO, Moscow) — Quesito 2

*Find all f with f(x^2+f(y))=y+f(x)^2*

> Let R denote the set of all real numbers. Find all functions f : R →R
> such that
> f
> ³
> x2 + f(y)
> ´
> = y + (f(x))2
> for all x, y ∈R.
