# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2011 — Nível 2 — Fase 2 — Quesito 6

*Computer screen: starting from 5, reach 19 or 2011 via a−b operations*

> Initially, the number $5$ is displayed on a computer screen. At any moment, the number on the screen can be replaced by any number of the form $a - b$, where $a$ and $b$ are positive integers such that $a + b = n$, with $n$ being the current number on the screen.
> 
> (a) Show how to obtain the number $19$ by performing such operations.
> 
> (b) Is it possible to obtain the number $2011$? Do not forget to justify your answer.
