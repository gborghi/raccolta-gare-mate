*Square PQRS edge length 1; four quarter-circle arcs (TRU centred S, VPW centred Q); find perimeter of shaded region.*

> The diagram shows a square $PQRS$ with edges of length 1, and four arcs, each of which is a quarter of a circle. Arc $TRU$ has centre $S$; arc $VPW$ has centre $Q$.
> 
> What is the length of the perimeter of the shaded region?
>
> - **(A)** $6$
> - **(B)** $(2\sqrt{2} - 1)\pi$
> - **(C)** $(3\sqrt{2} - 2)\pi$
> - **(D)** $(\sqrt{2} - \tfrac{1}{2})\pi$
> - **(E)** $2\pi$
