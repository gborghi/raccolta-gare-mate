*Proper divisors of a multiple of 2020: sum of odd divisors below sum of even divisors*

> Aline chooses an integer $n$ divisible by $2020$ at the board. She then writes on the board all the integers $d$ that divide $n$ and such that $1 \le d < n$. Prove that the sum of the odd numbers written on the board is less than the sum of the even integers written on the board.
