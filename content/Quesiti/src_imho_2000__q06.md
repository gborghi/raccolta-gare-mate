*Altitude feet reflected in angle bisectors lie on incircle*

> Let $A_1A_2A_3$ be an acute-angled triangle. The foot of the altitude from $A_i$ is $K_i$, and the incircle touches the side opposite $A_i$ at $L_i$. The line $K_1K_2$ is reflected in the line $L_1L_2$. Similarly, the line $K_2K_3$ is reflected in the line $L_2L_3$, and the line $K_3K_1$ is reflected in $L_3L_1$. Show that the three new lines form a triangle with vertices on the incircle.
