*Fibonacci sequence divisibility by m for all n*

> The Fibonacci sequence $\{f_n\}$ is defined by
> $$f_1 = 1, \quad f_2 = 1, \quad f_n = f_{n-1} + f_{n-2} \quad (n \ge 2).$$
> Prove that there are unique integers $a$, $b$, $m$ such that $0 < a < m$, $0 < b < m$ and $f_n - ab^n$ is divisible by $m$ for all positive integers $n$.
