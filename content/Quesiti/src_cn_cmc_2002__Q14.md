*Koch snowflake-type fractal: area sequence general term and limit*

> As shown in the diagram, there is a sequence of curves $P_0, P_1, P_2, \ldots$ $P_0$ is an equilateral triangle. It is known that the region enclosed by $P_0$ has area 1. We build $P_{k+1}$ from $P_k$ by operating as follows: trisect every side of $P_k$, construct an equilateral triangle outwardly on each side of $P_k$ sitting on the middle segment of that side, and finally remove the middle segment $(k = 0, 1, 2, \cdots)$. Write $S_k$ as the area of the region enclosed by $P_k$.
> 
> (1) Find a formula for the general term of the sequence $\{S_k\}$.
> (2) Find $\lim_{n \to \infty} S_n$.
