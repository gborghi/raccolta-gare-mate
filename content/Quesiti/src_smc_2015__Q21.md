*Length of AP in a triangle XYZ with lines through a point parallel to the sides*

> The diagram shows a triangle $XYZ$. The sides $XY$, $YZ$ and $XZ$ have lengths $2$, $3$ and $4$ respectively. The lines $AMB$, $PMQ$ and $SMT$ are drawn parallel to the sides $YZ$, $XZ$ and $XY$ respectively, where $A$, $P$, $S$ are on the side $XY$ and $B$, $Q$, $T$ are on the side $XZ$. Also $M$ is the same point on each of these lines. What is the length of $AP$?
>
> - **(A)** $\dfrac{10}{11}$
> - **(B)** $\dfrac{11}{12}$
> - **(C)** $\dfrac{12}{13}$
> - **(D)** $\dfrac{13}{14}$
> - **(E)** $\dfrac{14}{15}$
