*Find all n where every player is not out-performed in round-robin tournament*

> There are $n$ ($n \ge 3$) players in a table tennis tournament, in which any two players have a match. Player $A$ is called not out-performed by player $B$, if at least one of player $A$'s losers is not a $B$'s loser. Determine, with proof, all values of $n$ such that the following case could happen: after finishing all the matches, every player is not out-performed by any other player.
