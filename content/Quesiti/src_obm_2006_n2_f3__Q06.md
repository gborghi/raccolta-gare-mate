# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2006 — Nível 2 — Fase 3 — Quesito 6

*Prove tournament with no k-cycle has a winner and a loser*

> In a table tennis tournament (in which no game ends in a draw), each of the $n$ participants played exactly once against each of the others. It is known that for all $k > 2$, there do not exist $k$ players $J_1, J_2, \ldots, J_k$ such that $J_1$ beat $J_2$, $J_2$ beat $J_3$, $J_3$ beat $J_4$, $\ldots$, $J_{k-1}$ beat $J_k$, and $J_k$ beat $J_1$.
> 
> Prove that there exists a player who beat all the others and there exists a player who lost to all the others.
