# Coupe Animath Automne 2014 — Quesito 8

*Triangle, M,N on BC with angle BAM = angle NAC; O1, O2, A are collinear*

> Let $ABC$ be a triangle. Let $M$ and $N$ be two points on $[BC]$ such that the angles $\widehat{BAM}$ and $\widehat{NAC}$ are equal. Let $O_1$ be the center of the circle circumscribed about triangle $ABC$ and $O_2$ the center of the circle circumscribed about triangle $AMN$. Show that the points $O_1$, $O_2$, and $A$ are collinear.
