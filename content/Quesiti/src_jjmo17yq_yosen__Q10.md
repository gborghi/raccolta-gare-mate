*Max direct routes added to 100-port network under Hamiltonian-path condition*

> A certain country has $100$ ports, and JJMO Shipping operates two-way direct routes between some pairs of distinct ports. JJMO Shipping decides to add a new two-way direct route for any pair of distinct ports $A, B$ that satisfies the following condition: one can leave port $A$, pass through every port exactly once, and arrive at port $B$, but there is no direct route between ports $A$ and $B$. Direct routes are added one after another until no two distinct ports satisfying the condition remain. Find the largest possible number of direct routes that can be created (added).
