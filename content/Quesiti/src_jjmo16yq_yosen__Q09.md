# Japan Junior Mathematical Olympiad 2018 — Yosen (Preliminary) — Quesito 9

*Count colorings of 8 unit cubes (red/yellow faces) satisfying two 2x2x2 assembly conditions*

> There are $8$ unit cubes. Each face of each cube is painted either red or yellow. Count the number of ways to paint all faces (i.e., assign a color to each face of each of the $8$ cubes) so that both of the following conditions can be satisfied simultaneously:\n\n(i) The $8$ cubes can be assembled into a $2 \times 2 \times 2$ cube such that the resulting large cube has exactly $5$ red faces and $1$ yellow face on its surface.\n\n(ii) The $8$ cubes can be assembled (possibly in a different arrangement) into a $2 \times 2 \times 2$ cube such that the resulting large cube has exactly $1$ red face and $5$ yellow faces on its surface.\n\nTwo paintings are considered the same if one can be obtained from the other by rotating one of the unit cubes. Two assembly arrangements that differ only by rotation or reflection of the whole $2 \times 2 \times 2$ cube are considered the same.
