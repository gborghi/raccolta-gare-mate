*Positive integer N with 2025 digits, first digit 3; every two consecutive digits form a multiple of 17 or 23; find units digit.*

> The positive integer $N$ has 2025 digits. The first digit is 3. Every two consecutive digits of $N$ form a two-digit number that is divisible by either 17 or 23. The units digit of $N$ could either be $p$ or $q$. What is $p + q$?
>
> - **(A)** 3
> - **(B)** 6
> - **(C)** 7
> - **(D)** 9
> - **(E)** 10
