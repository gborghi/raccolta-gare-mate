*power sums divisible by 2017 force every term divisible*

> Let $x_1, x_2, \ldots, x_{1000}$ be integers such that for every positive integer $k$ with $k \le 672$, the sum $\displaystyle\sum_{i=1}^{1000} x_i^k$ is a multiple of $2017$. Show that $x_1, x_2, \ldots, x_{1000}$ are all multiples of $2017$. (Note: $2017$ is prime.)
