*Isosceles triangle with altitude foot; find angle BCD*

> There is an isosceles triangle $ABC$ with $AB = AC$. Let $H$ be the foot of the perpendicular dropped from $B$ onto the line $AC$. When a point $D$ on segment $BH$ satisfies $AB = 2BD$ and $BC = 2CD$, find the measure of $\angle BCD$.
> 
> Here $XY$ denotes the length of the segment $XY$.
