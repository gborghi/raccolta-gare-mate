*Polynomial of least degree with integer coefficients satisfying P(sqrt(7)+sqrt(5))=2(sqrt(7)-sqrt(5)); find P(2)*

> Suppose $P$ is the polynomial of least degree with integer coefficients such that
> $$P(\sqrt{7} + \sqrt{5}) = 2(\sqrt{7} - \sqrt{5})$$
> Find $P(2)$.
