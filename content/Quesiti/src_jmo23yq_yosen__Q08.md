# Japan Mathematical Olympiad 2013 — Yosen (Preliminary) — Quesito 8

*Convex quadrilateral, perpendicular diagonals, four tangent circles; find DX*

> There is a convex quadrilateral $ABCD$ whose diagonals $AC$ and $BD$ meet perpendicularly at a point $X$, with $AX = 5$, $BX = 6$, $CX = 20$. Consider the circle $C_1$ centered at $A$ with radius $AX$, the circle $C_2$ centered at $B$ with radius $BX$, the circle $C_3$ centered at $C$ with radius $CX$, and the circle $C_4$ centered at $D$ with radius $DX$. When there exists a circle tangent to all four circles $C_1, C_2, C_3, C_4$, find $DX$. Here, $YZ$ denotes the length of segment $YZ$.
