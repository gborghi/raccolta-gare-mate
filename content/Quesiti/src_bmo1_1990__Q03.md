*Angles of convex quadrilateral with cos sum zero implies cyclic*

> The angles $A$, $B$, $C$, $D$ of a convex quadrilateral satisfy the relation
> $$\cos A + \cos B + \cos C + \cos D = 0.$$
> Prove that $ABCD$ is a cyclic quadrilateral.
