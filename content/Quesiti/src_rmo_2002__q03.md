*Show abc divides expansion of (a+b+c)^n*

> Let $a, b, c$ be positive integers such that $a$ divides $b^5$, $b$ divides $c^5$, and $c$ divides $a^5$. Prove that $abc$ divides $(a + b + c)^{31}$.
