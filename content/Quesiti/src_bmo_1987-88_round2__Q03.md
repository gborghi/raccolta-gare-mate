*System of equations in a,b,c,x,y,z; express z and a combined expression*

> The numbers $a$, $b$, $c$, $x$, $y$, $z$ satisfy the equations
> $$x^2 - y^2 - z^2 = 2ayz$$
> $$-x^2 + y^2 - z^2 = 2bxz$$
> $$-x^2 - y^2 + z^2 = 2cxy$$
> and also $xyz \neq 0$.
> 
> By using the first two equations express $z$ in terms of $a$, $b$, $x$, $y$. Prove that
> $$x^2(1-b^2) = y^2(1-a^2) = xy(ab-c)$$
> and hence find the value of $a^4 + b^4 + c^4 - 2a^2b^2 - 2b^2c^2 - 2a^2c^2$ (independently of $x$, $y$, $z$).
