*Papa-Leguas (Road Runner) 100-lap race; first lap at 200 km/h; bound the average speed*

> Papa-Leguas (Road Runner) took part in a race (together with Ligeirinho and Flash), which consisted of doing 100 laps around a circuit. As always, Coyote wanted to catch Papa-Leguas and placed a pile of birdseed in the middle of the track. Of course Coyote did not manage to catch Papa-Leguas, but he made Papa-Leguas's average speed on the first lap be only 200 km/h. Knowing this, the average speed of Papa-Leguas in the race:
> 
> **A)** Does not exceed 200 km/h. $\quad$ **B)** Does not exceed 250 km/h, but can exceed 200 km/h. $\quad$ **C)** Does not exceed 2000 km/h, but can exceed 250 km/h. $\quad$ **D)** Does not exceed 20000 km/h, but can exceed 2000 km/h. $\quad$ **E)** Can exceed 20000 km/h.
