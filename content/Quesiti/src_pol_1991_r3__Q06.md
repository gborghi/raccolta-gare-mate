*Prove inequality for reals with sum of squares equal to 2*

> If $x, y, z$ are real numbers satisfying $x^2 + y^2 + z^2 = 2$, prove the inequality
> $$x + y + z \le 2 + xyz,$$
> and find the conditions for equality.
