*For quadrilateral with inscribed circle, four associated quantities are equal*

> $ABCD$ is a quadrilateral which has an inscribed circle. With the side $AB$ is associated
> $$u_{AB} = p_1 \sin\widehat{DAB} + p_2 \sin\widehat{ABC}$$
> where $p_1$, $p_2$ are the perpendiculars from $A$, $B$ respectively to the opposite side $CD$. Define $u_{BC}$, $u_{CD}$, $u_{DA}$ likewise, using in each case perpendiculars to the opposite side. Show that
> $$u_{AB} = u_{BC} = u_{CD} = u_{DA}.$$
