# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2013 — Nível 3 — Fase 3 — Quesito 4

*Largest n with sequence of nonzero digits satisfying divisibility condition*

> Find the largest value of $n$ for which there exists a sequence $(a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_n)$ of nonzero digits (i.e., $a_i \in \{1,2,3,4,5,6,7,8,9\}$) such that for every $k$ with $1 \le k \le n$, the $k$-digit number $(a_1 a_2 \cdots a_k)$ formed by the first $k$ digits divides the $(k+1)$-digit number $(a_1 a_2 \cdots a_k a_{k+1})$. (Here $(a_1 a_2 \cdots a_m)$ denotes the number $a_1 \cdot 10^{m-1} + a_2 \cdot 10^{m-2} + \cdots + a_m$.)
