*Angle bisectors of two angles meet on side AB*

> Triangle $ABC$ satisfies $AB = AC \neq BC$. Let $D$ be a point inside triangle $ABC$ such that $\angle ABD = \angle ACD = 30^\circ$. Show that the angle bisectors of $\angle ACB$ and $\angle ADB$ intersect at a point on side $AB$. (Here $XY$ denotes the length of segment $XY$.)
