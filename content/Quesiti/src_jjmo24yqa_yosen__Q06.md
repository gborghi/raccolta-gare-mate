*Triangle, BD=DE=EC on BC, parallel cevians, equal angles; find AC*

> On side $BC$ of triangle $ABC$ (excluding the endpoints), there are two distinct points $D$ and $E$ satisfying $BD = DE = EC$. Points $P$ on side $AB$ and $Q$ on side $AC$ (both excluding endpoints) are chosen so that lines $DP$ and $EQ$ are both parallel. Furthermore, $\angle BPD = \angle EQC$. Given that $AP = 5$ and $PB = 14$, find the length of $AC$. (Here $XY$ denotes the length of segment $XY$.)
