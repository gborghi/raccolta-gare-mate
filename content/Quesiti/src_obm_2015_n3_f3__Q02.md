*Largest k so every 4n-element subset contains k pairs {a,b} with b multiple of a*

> Let $S = \{1, 2, 3, \ldots, 6n\}$, $n > 1$. Find the largest value of $k$ for which the following statement is true: every subset $A$ of $S$ with $4n$ elements has at least $k$ two-element subsets $\{a, b\}$ with $a < b$ and $b$ a multiple of $a$.
