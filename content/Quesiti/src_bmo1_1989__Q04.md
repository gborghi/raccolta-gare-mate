*Triangle of numbers: every row from row 3 contains an even number*

> Consider the following triangle of numbers:
> $$\begin{array}{ccccccccc}
> & & & & 1 & & & & \\
> & & & 1 & & 1 & & & \\
> & & 1 & & 2 & & 1 & & \\
> & 1 & & 3 & & 3 & & 1 & \\
> 1 & & 3 & & 6 & & 3 & & 1 \\
> & & \cdots & & & & \cdots & &
> \end{array}$$
> Each number is the sum of three numbers in the previous row: the number above it and the numbers immediately to the left and right of that number. If there is no number in one or more of these positions, $0$ is used.
> 
> Prove that, from the third row on, every row contains at least one even number.
