*Triangle with side AB trisected and two perpendicularity conditions; find ratio DB/DC*

> $E$ and $F$ are points on side $AB$ of triangle $ABC$, such that $AE = EF = FB$. $D$ is a point on line $BC$ such that $BC$ is perpendicular to $ED$, and $AD$ is perpendicular to $CF$. The angles $\angle BDF$ and $\angle CFA$ measure $x$ and $3x$, respectively. Calculate the ratio $\frac{DB}{DC}$.
