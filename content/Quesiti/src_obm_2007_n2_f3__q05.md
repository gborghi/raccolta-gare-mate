*Acute triangle with AB/√2 = BH = OB: find all angles*

> Let $ABC$ be an acute triangle and let $O$, $H$ be its circumcenter and orthocenter, respectively. Given that
> $$\frac{AB}{\sqrt{2}} = BH = OB,$$
> find the angles of triangle $ABC$.
