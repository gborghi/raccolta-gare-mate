*Angle from two angle bisectors in a triangle*

> In triangle $ABC$, $\angle ABC = 70^\circ$ and $\angle ACB = 50^\circ$. Let the bisectors of $\angle ABC$ and $\angle ACB$ meet sides $AC$ and $AB$ at the points $D$ and $E$, respectively. Find the measure of $\angle AED$.
