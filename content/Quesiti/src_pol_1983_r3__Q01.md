*Convex n-gon with interior point Q not on any diagonal; prove the number of triangles P_iP_jP_k containing Q is even when n is even.*

> On the plane are given a convex $n$-gon $P_1P_2\ldots P_n$ and a point $Q$ inside it, not lying on any of its diagonals. Prove that if $n$ is even, then the number of triangles $P_iP_jP_k$ containing the point $Q$ is even.
