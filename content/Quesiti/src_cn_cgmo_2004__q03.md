*Cover obtuse triangle inscribed in unit circle by isosceles right triangle*

> Let $ABC$ be an obtuse triangle inscribed in a circle of radius 1. Prove that triangle $ABC$ can be covered by an isosceles right-angled triangle with hypotenuse $\sqrt{2}+1$.
