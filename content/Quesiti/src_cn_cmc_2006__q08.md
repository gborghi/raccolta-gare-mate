*Find range of real a so |z|<=2 for all theta, where z=(a+cos theta)+(2a-sin theta)i*

**Area:** [[Algebra e Analisi]]
**Risposta:** $\left[-\dfrac{\sqrt{5}}{5},\, \dfrac{\sqrt{5}}{5}\right]$
