*Two concentric circles divided by radial lines into 14 equal pieces; radius of inner circle is 1; find outer radius minus 1*

> The diagram shows two concentric circles divided by radial lines into 14 pieces of equal area. The radius of the smaller circle is 1. What is the length, $s$, of an outer radial line?
>
> - **(A)** $\sqrt{14} - 1$
> - **(B)** $\sqrt{14} - 2$
> - **(C)** $\frac{\sqrt{14}}{2} - 1$
> - **(D)** $\frac{\sqrt{14}}{2} - 2$
> - **(E)** $\frac{\sqrt{14} - 1}{2}$
