*Positive reals with harmonic mean 1; minimize sum of reciprocals expression*

> Let $x_1, x_2, \ldots, x_n$ be positive numbers with the harmonic mean equal to $1$. Find the smallest possible value of
> $$x_1 + \frac{x_2^2}{2} + \frac{x_3^3}{3} + \cdots + \frac{x_n^n}{n}.$$
