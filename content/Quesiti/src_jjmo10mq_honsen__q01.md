*Pentagon with square base; prove triangle inscribed in circle*

> Let $ABCDE$ be a pentagon such that quadrilateral $ABCD$ is a square. Suppose $\angle AEC + \angle BED = 180^\circ$. Prove that triangle $\triangle AEC$ is an acute triangle. (A convex polygon is one in which all interior angles are less than $180^\circ$.)
