*Find all primes p dividing n^(n+1)+(n+1)^n for infinitely many n*

> Find all the prime numbers $p$, for which there are infinitely many positive integers $n$, such that $p \mid n^{n+1} + (n+1)^n$. (posed by Chen Yonggao)
