*Sum over ordered triples of subsets whose union is S; find in terms of n*

> Let $S = \{1, 2, \ldots, n\}$ and let $T$ be the set of all ordered triples of subsets of $S$, say $(A_1, A_2, A_3)$, such that $A_1 \cup A_2 \cup A_3 = S$. Determine, in terms of $n$,
> $$\sum_{(A_1,A_2,A_3)\in T} |A_1 \cap A_2 \cap A_3|$$
> where $|X|$ denotes the number of elements in the set $X$.
