*Count unordered pairs {A,B} subsets of {1,...,10} with A∩B={2,3,5,7}*

> Let $X = \{1, 2, 3, \ldots, 10\}$. Find the number of pairs $\{A, B\}$ such that $A \subseteq X$, $B \subseteq X$, $A \neq B$ and $A \cap B = \{2, 3, 5, 7\}$.
