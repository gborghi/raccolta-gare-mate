# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2015 — Nível 3 — Fase 2 — Quesito 6

*System 1/(ab)=b+2c, 1/(bc)=2c+3a, 1/(ca)=3a+b; (a+b+c)^3=p/q, find p+q*

> (Part A) The real numbers $a$, $b$ and $c$ satisfy the equations $\frac{1}{ab} = b + 2c$, $\frac{1}{bc} = 2c + 3a$, $\frac{1}{ca} = 3a + b$. We have $(a + b + c)^3 = \frac{p}{q}$, with $p$ and $q$ coprime integers and $q > 0$. Calculate $p + q$.
