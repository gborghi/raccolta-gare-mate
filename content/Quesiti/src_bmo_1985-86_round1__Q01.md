# British Mathematical Olympiad 1985-86 — Round 1 — Quesito 1

*Two-part: reduce large fraction to lowest terms; find circle radius given tangency*

> (A) Reduce the fraction $\dfrac{B}{D}$ to its lowest terms when
> $$B = 2^{24}851891801861611637, \qquad D = 8111881188118811800.$$
> (B) A circle $S$ of radius $R$ has two parallel tangents $t_1$, $t_2$. A circle $S_1$ of radius $r_1$ touches $S$ and $t_1$; a circle $S_2$ of radius $r_2$ touches $S$ and $t_2$. The circles are external. Calculate $R$ in terms of $r_1$ and $r_2$.
