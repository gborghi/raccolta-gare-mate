*A, midpoint of XY, midpoint of BC are collinear*

> Let $ABC$ be an acute triangle with circumcenter $O$. Let $D$, $E$, $F$ be the feet of the perpendiculars dropped from $A$, $B$, $C$ to the opposite sides, respectively, and let $M$ be the midpoint of side $BC$. Let $X$ be the intersection of line $AD$ and line $EF$, and let $Y$ be the intersection of line $AO$ and line $BC$. Let $Z$ be the midpoint of segment $XY$. Show that the three points $A$, $Z$, $M$ are collinear.
