*Six points with pairwise distances summing to i+j*

> Let $A_1$, $A_2$, $A_3$, $B_1$, $B_2$, $B_3$ be six points in the plane such that for all integers $i$ and $j$ in $\{1, 2, 3\}$:
> $$A_i B_j = i + j.$$
> What can be said about these six points?
