*Positive integer sequence with term exceeding average of next 2016 terms: bounded above*

> Let $a_1, a_2, \ldots$ be a sequence of positive integers satisfying, for every positive integer $n$,
> $$a_n > \frac{a_{n+1} + a_{n+2} + \cdots + a_{2n}}{n + 2016}.$$
> Prove that there exists a positive real constant $C$ such that $a_n < C$ for all positive integers $n$.
