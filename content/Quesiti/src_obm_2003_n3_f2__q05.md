*Probability that a 3x3 coloring contains a monochromatic 2x2 square*

> A square of side $3$ is divided into $9$ unit squares forming a $3 \times 3$ grid. Each unit square is painted blue or red, each color chosen with probability $\frac{1}{2}$, independently of the others. What is the probability that, after coloring all unit squares, at least one $2 \times 2$ square (aligned with the grid) is entirely one color?
