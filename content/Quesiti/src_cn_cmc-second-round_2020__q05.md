*Five points on a circle with three equal arcs; prove angle PAQ equals angle PEQ*

> (40 marks) As shown in Fig. 1.1, $A$, $B$, $C$, $D$, $E$ are five points in order on circle $\Omega$, satisfying $\overset{\frown}{ABC} = \overset{\frown}{BCD} = \overset{\frown}{CDE}$. Points $P$, $Q$ are on segments $AD$, $BE$, respectively, and $P$ lies on segment $CQ$. Prove that $\angle PAQ = \angle PEQ$.
