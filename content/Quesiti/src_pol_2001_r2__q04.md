*Find all n>=3 such that any arithmetic progression of n terms with rational weighted sum contains a rational term.*

> Find all integers $n\ge 3$ for which the following statement is true:
> Any arithmetic progression $a_1,\ldots,a_n$ with $n$ terms for which $a_1+2a_2+\cdots+na_n$ is rational contains at least one rational term.
