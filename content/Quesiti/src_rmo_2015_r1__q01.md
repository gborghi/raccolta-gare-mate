*Cyclic quadrilateral, diagonals meet, circumcircles, incenter angle*

> In a cyclic quadrilateral $ABCD$, the diagonals $AC$ and $BD$ intersect at $X$. Let the circumcircles of triangles $AXD$ and $BXC$ intersect again at $Y$. If $X$ is the incentre of triangle $ABY$, show that $\angle CAD = 90^\circ$.
