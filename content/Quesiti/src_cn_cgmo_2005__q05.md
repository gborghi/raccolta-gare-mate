*Prove upper bound x^2+4y^2 ≤ 2/9 given positive reals satisfying constraint*

> Let $x, y$ be positive real numbers with $x^2 + 4y^2 = x - y$. Prove that $x^2 + 4y^2 \le \dfrac{2}{9}$.
