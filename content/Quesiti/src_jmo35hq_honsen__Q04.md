# Japan Mathematical Olympiad 2025 — Honsen (Final) — Quesito 4

*Find all integer-valued functions with f(n)>0 and f(n) divides n^{f(n)}-1*

> Find all functions $f$ defined on integers $n \ge 2$ taking positive integer values such that both of the following conditions hold for all integers $n \ge 2$:
> \begin{itemize}
> \item $f(n) > 0$,
> \item $f(n) \mid n^{f(n)} - 1$.
> \end{itemize}
