*Band of width sqrt(2) covering four given points in the plane*

> In the plane, call the set of all points whose distance from a given line is at most $\frac{d}{2}$ a "band of width $d$". Let $A$, $B$, $C$, $D$ be four points in the plane. Suppose that for any choice of three of these four points, there exists a band of width $1$ containing all three. Prove that there exists a band of width $\sqrt{2}$ that contains all four points.
