*Equal-angle chords in a circle: prove AR(AP+AR)=AQ(AQ+AS)*

> $AP$, $AQ$, $AR$, $AS$ are chords of a given circle with the property that
> $$\angle PAQ = \angle QAR = \angle RAS.$$
> Prove that
> $$AR(AP + AR) = AQ(AQ + AS).$$
