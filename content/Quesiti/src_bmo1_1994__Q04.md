*Points on circle with tangent and angle condition*

> The points $Q$, $R$ lie on the circle $\gamma$, and $P$ is a point such that $PQ$, $PR$ are tangents to $\gamma$. $A$ is the midpoint of $PQ$ and $C$ is the circumcentre of triangle $PQR$. The circle $\gamma$ cuts $AQ$ again at the point $B$. Prove that $\angle PAR = \angle ABC$.
