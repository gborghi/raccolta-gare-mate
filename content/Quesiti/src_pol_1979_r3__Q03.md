*Probability of exactly k successes in n independent tests*

> An experiment consists of performing $n$ independent tests. The $i$-th test is successful with the probability equal to $p_i$. Let $p_k$ be the probability that exactly $k$ tests succeed. Prove that
> $$\sum_{k=0}^{n} k p_k = \sum_{i=1}^{n} p_i.$$
