*Sequence with a_{n+2}=2a_{n+1}-a_n+2; prove a_{4m+2} is a term*

> The sequence $(a_n)_{n \ge 1}$ is defined by $a_1 = 1$, $a_2 = 2$, and
> $$a_{n+2} = 2a_{n+1} - a_n + 2 \quad \text{for } n \ge 1.$$
> Prove that for any $m$, $a_{4m+2}$ is also a term of the sequence.
