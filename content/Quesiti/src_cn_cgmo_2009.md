- [[src_cn_cgmo_2009__Q01|Quesito 1]] — Finitely many positive integer triples satisfying abc=2009(a+b+c).
- [[src_cn_cgmo_2009__Q02|Quesito 2]] — Right triangle inscribed in circle, point E on arc, point F on ray EC, BF meets circle at D, circumcenter O of DEF; prove A,C,O collinear.
- [[src_cn_cgmo_2009__Q03|Quesito 3]] — Given n points on the coordinate axes with integer coordinates bounded by n, minimize the cyclic sum of squared distances between consecutive points.
- [[src_cn_cgmo_2009__Q04|Quesito 4]] — Chess pieces on n points connected by segments; moves to neighbors without swaps; find minimum number of segments in a harmonic set.
- [[src_cn_cgmo_2009__Q05|Quesito 5]] — Prove a triple-product inequality for real numbers x,y,z >= 1.
- [[src_cn_cgmo_2009__Q06|Quesito 6]] — Circle internally tangent to another, chord tangent at C, midpoint M of arc, foot N of perpendicular; prove AC*CB = 2r*MN.
- [[src_cn_cgmo_2009__Q07|Quesito 7]] — Region of 4n squares on a 10x10 board tileable by n 1x4 pieces and by n 2x2 pieces; minimize n for tiling by given combination.
- [[src_cn_cgmo_2009__Q08|Quesito 8]] — Sequence a_n = n*sqrt5 - floor(n*sqrt5); compute max and min over a_1..a_2009.
