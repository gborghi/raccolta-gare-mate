*Find a^2013 + b^2013 + c^2013 given polynomial root conditions*

> Let $f(x) = x^3 + ax^2 + bx + c$ and $g(x) = x^3 + bx^2 + cx + a$, where $a, b, c$ are integers with $c \neq 0$. Suppose the following conditions hold:
> (a) $f(1) = 0$;
> (b) the roots of $g(x)$ are squares of the roots of $f(x)$.
> Find the value of $a^{2013} + b^{2013} + c^{2013}$.
