*If P(P(x)) and P(P(P(x))) are integer-coeff then P is too*

> Let $P(x)$ be a polynomial with rational coefficients, and suppose that $P(P(x))$ and $P(P(P(x)))$ are polynomials with integer coefficients. Prove that $P(x)$ also has integer coefficients.
