*Acute triangle ABC; circumcenter O, orthocenter H, centroid G; find angle C*

> In an acute triangle $ABC$, $O$ is the circumcenter, $H$ is the orthocenter and $G$ is the centroid. Let $OD$ be perpendicular to $BC$ and $HE$ be perpendicular to $CA$, with $D$ on $BC$ and $E$ on $CA$. Let $F$ be the midpoint of $AB$. Suppose the areas of triangles $ODC$, $HEA$ and $GFB$ are equal. Find all the possible values of $\hat{C}$.
