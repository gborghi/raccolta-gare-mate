*For each n≤1000, count m≤1000 with floor(m/n) odd; find maximum count over all n*

> For positive integers $m$ and $n$, let $\left\lfloor \dfrac{m}{n} \right\rfloor$ denote the floor of $\dfrac{m}{n}$. For a fixed positive integer $n \le 1000$, let $f(n)$ be the number of positive integers $m \le 1000$ for which $\left\lfloor \dfrac{m}{n} \right\rfloor$ is odd. Find the maximum value of $f(n)$ over all positive integers $n \le 1000$.
