*Count lattice paths between two points on x-axis*

> Let $X$ be the set of all lattice points in the plane (points $(x, y)$ with $x, y \in \mathbb{Z}$). A path $\{p_1, p_2, \ldots, p_k\}$ of points of $X$ is a chain $(p_1, p_2, \ldots, p_k)$ such that $|p_{i+1} - p_i| = 1$ for $i = 1, \ldots, k-1$. Let $F(n)$ be the number of distinct paths of $n$ steps beginning at a point $P_0$ on line $y = 0$ and ending at any point $P_n$ on line $y = 0$. Prove that $F(n) = \binom{2n}{n}$.
