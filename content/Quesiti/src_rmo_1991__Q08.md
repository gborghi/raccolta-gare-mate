*8x8 chessboard integers averaged by neighbours, all equal*

> The 64 squares of an $8 \times 8$ chessboard are filled with positive integers in such a way that each integer is the average of the integers on the neighbouring squares. (Two squares are neighbours if they share a common edge or a common vertex. A square can have $8$, $5$ or $3$ neighbours depending on its position.) Show that all the 64 integer entries are in fact equal.
