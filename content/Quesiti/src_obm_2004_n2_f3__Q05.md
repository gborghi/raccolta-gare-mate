*Midpoint of hypotenuse; circumcenters of sub-triangles; tangency*

> Let $D$ be the midpoint of the hypotenuse $AB$ of a right triangle $ABC$ (with the right angle at $C$). Let $O_1$ and $O_2$ be the circumcenters of triangles $ADC$ and $DBC$, respectively.
> 
> a) Show that $\angle O_1 D O_2 = 90^\circ$.
> 
> b) Show that $AB$ is tangent to the circle with diameter $O_1 O_2$.
