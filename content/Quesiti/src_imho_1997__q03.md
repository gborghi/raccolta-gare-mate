*Real numbers with bounded partial sums; find permutation with bounded partial sums*

> Let $x_1, x_2, \ldots, x_n$ be real numbers satisfying the conditions
> $$|x_1 + x_2 + \cdots + x_n| = 1$$
> and
> $$|x_i| \le \frac{n+1}{2}, \quad i = 1, 2, \ldots, n.$$
> Show that there exists a permutation $y_1, y_2, \ldots, y_n$ of $x_1, x_2, \ldots, x_n$ such that
> $$|y_1 + 2y_2 + \cdots + ny_n| \le \frac{n+1}{2}.$$
