*Parallelogram with four unit circles covering it*

> Let $ABCD$ be a parallelogram with side lengths $AB = a$, $AD = 1$, and $\angle BAD = \alpha$. If $\triangle ABD$ is acute, prove that the four circles of radius 1 with centers $A$, $B$, $C$, $D$ cover the parallelogram if and only if
> $$a \le \cos\alpha + \sqrt{3}\sin\alpha.$$
