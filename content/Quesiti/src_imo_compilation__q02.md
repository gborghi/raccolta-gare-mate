*IMO 1997 problema 2*

> A triangle A1A2A3 and a point P0 are given in the plane. We define As = As−3
> for all s ≥4. We construct a set of points P1, P2, P3, . . . , such that Pk+1 is
> the image of Pk under a rotation with center Ak+1 through angle 120°clockwise
> (for k = 0, 1, 2, . . . ). Prove that if P1986 = P0, then the triangle A1A2A3 is
> equilateral.
