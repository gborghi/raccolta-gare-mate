*Max presses of broken sqrt (sqrt or /100) to first get below 2*

> Esmeralda's calculator is broken: when one presses the button $\sqrt{\phantom{x}}$, the calculator does, by chance, one of these two operations: it takes the square root (as it should) or it divides the number by $100$ (as it should not). Esmeralda entered the number $201120112011$ on the calculator and started pressing the button $\sqrt{\phantom{x}}$ repeatedly. How many times, at most, would Esmeralda press the button until appearing, for the first time, a number smaller than $2$?
> 
> (A) $2$ \quad (B) $4$ \quad (C) $5$ \quad (D) $8$ \quad (E) $9$
