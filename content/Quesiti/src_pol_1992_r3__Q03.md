*Prove double sum of a_m a_n/(m+n) is nonneg*

> If $a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_r$ are arbitrary real numbers, prove the inequality
> $$\sum_{n=1}^{r} \sum_{m=1}^{r} \frac{a_m a_n}{m+n} \ge 0.$$
