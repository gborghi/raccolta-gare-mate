*Bounded sequence satisfying sum condition; prove aₙ < 1/n*

> Suppose a bounded number sequence $\{a_n\}$ satisfies $$a_n \leq \sum_{k=n}^{2n+2006} \frac{a_k}{k+1}, \quad n = 1, 2, 3, \cdots.$$ Prove that $a_n < \frac{1}{n}$, $n = 1, 2, 3, \cdots$.
