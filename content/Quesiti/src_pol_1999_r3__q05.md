*For integers a_i,b_i prove sum over i<j of (|a_i-a_j|+|b_i-b_j|) <= sum over all i,j of |a_i-b_j|.*

> If $a_i, b_i$ ($i = 1, 2, \ldots, n$) are integers, prove that
> $$\sum_{1 \le i < j \le n} \left( |a_i - a_j| + |b_i - b_j| \right) \le \sum_{1 \le i, j \le n} |a_i - b_j|.$$
