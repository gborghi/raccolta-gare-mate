*In an acute triangle, with a foot of altitude and a further projection, prove two lines perpendicular.*

> In an acute-angled $ABC$ point $D$ is the projection of $C$ onto $AB$, and $E$ is the projection of $D$ onto $BC$. Point $F$ is taken on the segment $DE$ so that $EF : FD = AD : DB$. Prove that the lines $CF$ and $AE$ are perpendicular.
