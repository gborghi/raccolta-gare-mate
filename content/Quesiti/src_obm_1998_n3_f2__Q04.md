# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 1998 — Nível 3 — Fase 2 — Quesito 4

*Semicircles on legs of acute triangle; altitude lines meet them at equal segments*

> On the sides $AB$ and $AC$ of an acute triangle $ABC$, semicircles are constructed externally, with those sides as diameters. The lines containing the altitudes relative to sides $AB$ and $AC$ cut these semicircles at points $P$ and $Q$ respectively. Prove that $AP = AQ$.
