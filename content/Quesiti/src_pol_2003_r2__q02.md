*Cyclic quadrilateral ABCD; angle bisectors meet at P and Q; prove line PQ is perpendicular to MN where M,N are arc midpoints.*

> A quadrilateral $ABCD$ is inscribed in a circle $o$. The bisectors of the angles $DAB$ and $ABC$ meet at $P$, and the bisectors of the angles $BCD$ and $CDA$ meet at $Q$. Let $M$ be the midpoint of the arc $BC$ of $o$ not containing $D$ and $A$, and $N$ be the midpoint of the arc $DA$ of $o$ not containing $B$ and $C$. Prove that the line through $P$ and $Q$ is perpendicular to $MN$.
