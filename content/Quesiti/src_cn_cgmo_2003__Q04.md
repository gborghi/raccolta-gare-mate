*Sequence with a_{n+1}=a_n^2-a_n+1: prove sum of reciprocal products < 1*

> Let $\{a_n\}_{n=1}^{\infty}$ be a sequence of real numbers such that $a_1 = 2$, $a_{n+1} = a_n^2 - a_n + 1$ for $n = 1, 2, \ldots$. Prove that
> $$\frac{1}{a_1} + \frac{1}{a_1 a_2} + \cdots + \frac{1}{a_1 a_2 \cdots a_{2003}} < 1.$$
> (posed by Li Shenghong)
