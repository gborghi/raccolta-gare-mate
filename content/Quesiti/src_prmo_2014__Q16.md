# Pre-Regional Mathematical Olympiad (PRMO) 2014 — Mumbai — Quesito 16

*Value of angle QPR in triangle ABC with incenter I and angle BAC=40*

> In a triangle $ABC$, let $I$ denote the incenter. Let the lines $AI$, $BI$, and $CI$ intersect the incircle at $P$, $Q$, and $R$, respectively. If $\angle BAC = 40^\circ$, what is the value of $\angle QPR$ in degrees?
