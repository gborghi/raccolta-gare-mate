*Count non-empty subsets C of A∪B with equal intersection sizes*

> Let $A$ and $B$ be disjoint sets with $n(A) = 5$ and $n(B) = 7$, where $n(X)$ denotes the number of elements of $X$. How many non-empty subsets $C$ of $A \cup B$ are there such that $n(A \cap C) = n(B \cap C)$?
> 
> (A) 790 \quad (B) 791 \quad (C) 792 \quad (D) 793 \quad (E) 794
