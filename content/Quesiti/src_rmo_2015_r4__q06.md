*Count integers m in [1,5000] with floor(sqrt(m))=floor(sqrt(m+125))*

> How many integers $m$ satisfy both the following properties: (i) $1 \le m \le 5000$; (ii) $[\sqrt{m}] = [\sqrt{m+125}]$?
> 
> (Here $[x]$ denotes the largest integer not exceeding $x$, for any real number $x$.)
