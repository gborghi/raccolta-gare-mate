# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 1999 — Nível 1 — Fase 3 — Quesito 3

*Citizen exiled between cities; can he return to city A?*

> A kingdom is made up of ten cities. A citizen who misbehaves is exiled from city $A$ to city $B$, where $B$ is the city in the kingdom farthest from $A$. After some time, he is expelled from city $B$ to city $C$, the city in the kingdom farthest from $B$. It is known that city $C$ is not the same as city $A$.
> 
> **Note:** Distances between all cities are different.
> 
> If he continues to be exiled in this way, is it possible for him to return to city $A$?
