*Find all integer n>=2 and strictly increasing positive integer sequences with divisibility condition*

> Find all integers $n \ge 2$ and all $n$-tuples of positive integers $(a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_n)$ satisfying the following three conditions:
> \begin{itemize}
> \item $a_1 < a_2 < \cdots < a_n$.
> \item $a_n$ is a prime number.
> \item For every integer $k$ with $1 \le k \le n$, $a_k$ divides $a_1 + a_2 + \cdots + a_n$.
> \end{itemize}
