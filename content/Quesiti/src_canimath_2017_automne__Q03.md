# Coupe Animath Automne 2017 — Quesito 3

*Three-player goalkeeper game: count total phases and find who scored the 6th goal*

> Football with three people is played in successive phases: one player is the goalkeeper while the other two, called "field players", try to score a goal. As soon as a player scores, the phase ends and that player becomes the goalkeeper for the next phase. Amandine, Bobby and Charles play this game. When the game is over, they recall that Amandine was a field player $12$ times, Bobby $21$ times, and Charles $8$ times.
> a) How many phases were there in total?
> b) Who scored the sixth goal?
