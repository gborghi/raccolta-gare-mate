*Ratio of radii of mixtilinear circle to incircle equals tan^2((B-C)/4)*

> Let $\Gamma$ be the circle lying inside a triangle $ABC$ and touching the sides $AB$ and $AC$ and the incircle $\Gamma'$ of the triangle externally. Show that the ratio of the radii of $\Gamma$ and $\Gamma'$ equals $\tan^2\!\dfrac{B-C}{4}$.
