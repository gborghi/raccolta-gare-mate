*Find positive number s such that a fractional-part sum equals 1990; are there rational solutions?*

> A positive number $\lfloor k \rfloor$ is the greatest integer which is not greater than $k$. The fractional part $\{x\}$ is defined by $\{x\} = x - \lfloor x \rfloor$.
> 
> Find a positive number $s$ such that
> $$\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \left\{ \frac{s}{n} \right\} = 1990.$$
> 
> Are there any rational solutions?
