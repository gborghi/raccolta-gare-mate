*Find all positive integers x1,...,xs,y satisfying a given system of conditions*

> Find all positive integers $x_1, x_2, \ldots, x_s, y$ satisfying the following conditions
> $$\begin{cases} \lfloor x_i + x_j \rfloor = 0, & 1 \le i < j \le s, \\ \lfloor x \rfloor + 1 = x_k y^s. \end{cases}$$
