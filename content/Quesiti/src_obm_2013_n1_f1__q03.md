*Liters of juice extractable from two 5 kg bags of oranges*

> A market sells oranges only in bags of $5$ kg each. Of each kilogram of orange, $55\%$ is juice. Moreover, $1$ kg of juice corresponds to $900$ ml of juice. Thus, how many liters of juice can we extract from two bags of oranges?
> 
> (A) $4.5$ \quad (B) $4.8$ \quad (C) $4.95$ \quad (D) $5$ \quad (E) $5.1$
