*Four sequences satisfy coupled recurrences; given periodicity for some k, prove the second terms vanish.*

> Four sequences of real numbers $(a_n)$, $(b_n)$, $(c_n)$, $(d_n)$ satisfy for all $n$,
> $$a_{n+1}=a_n+b_n,\quad b_{n+1}=b_n+c_n,$$
> $$c_{n+1}=c_n+d_n,\quad d_{n+1}=d_n+a_n.$$
> Prove that if $a_{k+m}=a_m$, $b_{k+m}=b_m$, $c_{k+m}=c_m$, $d_{k+m}=d_m$ for some $k\ge 1$, $n\ge 1$, then $a_2=b_2=c_2=d_2=0$.
