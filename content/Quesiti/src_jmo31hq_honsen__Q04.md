# Japan Mathematical Olympiad 2021 — Honsen (Final) — Quesito 4

*Integer sequence with a_{n+2}>a_{n+1}+a_n in [1,2016]; minimize max minus min*

> Consider a sequence of integers $a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_{2021}$ where each term satisfies $1 \le a_i \le 2016$, and for all valid indices $n$ the inequality
> $$a_{n+2} > a_{n+1} + a_n$$
> holds. Find the minimum possible value of
> $$\max(a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_{2021}) - \min(a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_{2021}).$$
