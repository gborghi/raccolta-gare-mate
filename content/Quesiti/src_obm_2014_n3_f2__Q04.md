*Count subsets of {1,...,2014} with median 2012, find remainder mod 2014*

> The median of a set $\{a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_n\}$ with $a_1 < a_2 < \cdots < a_n$ is defined as the mean of the two central terms $\dfrac{a_{n/2} + a_{n/2+1}}{2}$ when $n$ is even, and as $a_{(n+1)/2}$ when $n$ is odd. Let $M$ be the number of subsets of $\{1, 2, \ldots, 2014\}$ with median equal to $2012$. Find the remainder of the division of $M$ by $2014$.
