*Count handshake pairings of 12 people at circular table, no adjacent pairs*

> Twelve people are seated around a circular table. In how many ways can six pairs of people engage in handshakes so that no two people who shake hands are sitting next to each other? (Nobody is allowed to shake hands with more than one person at once.)
