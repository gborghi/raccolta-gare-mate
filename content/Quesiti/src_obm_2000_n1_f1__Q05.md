# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2000 — Nível 1 — Fase 1 — Quesito 5

*Balls in a box: colour deduction by elimination*

> A box contains several balls: $5$ blue, $4$ yellow, $3$ red, $2$ white, and $1$ black. Renato draws $3$ balls from the box. Knowing that none of the three balls is black, not yellow, and not red, Renato wants to make the following assertion about these $3$ balls:
> 
> (A) They are all the same colour. \quad (B) They are red. \quad (C) One is red and the other two are white. \quad (D) One is white and the other two are red. \quad (E) At least one is red.
