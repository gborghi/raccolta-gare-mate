*Inequality for positive reals satisfying a ratio condition*

> Let $a, b, c, d$ be positive reals satisfying $\dfrac{ab}{cd} = \dfrac{a+b}{c+d}$. Prove that
> $$( a+b)(c+d) \ge (a+c)(b+d).$$
