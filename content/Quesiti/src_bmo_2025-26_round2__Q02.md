# British Mathematical Olympiad 2025-26 — Round 2 — Quesito 2

*Convex quadrilateral ABCD: prove AC=BD iff XY bisects angle DXC*

> The convex quadrilateral $ABCD$ has sides $AD$ and $BC$ not parallel. The diagonals $AC$ and $BD$ intersect at $X$. The perpendicular bisectors of sides $AD$ and $BC$ meet at $Y$. Suppose that $Y$ lies strictly inside triangle $XCD$.
> 
> Prove that $AC = BD$ if and only if $XY$ bisects $\angle DXC$.
