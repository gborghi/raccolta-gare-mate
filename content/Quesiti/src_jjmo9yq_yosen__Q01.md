*Max value of xy with z two-digit, y one-digit, all digits distinct*

> Let $z$ be a 2-digit positive integer, $y$ a 1-digit positive integer, and $x$ a 1-digit positive integer. The tens digit of $z$, the units digit of $z$, and $y$ are all different from each other. Find the maximum value of $xy$.
