*Count 8-tuples in {1,-1}^8 with weighted sum divisible by 3*

> Determine the number of 8-tuples $(\epsilon_1, \epsilon_2, \cdots, \epsilon_8)$ such that $\epsilon_1, \epsilon_2, \cdots \epsilon_8 \in \{1, -1\}$ and
> $$\epsilon_1 + 2\epsilon_2 + 3\epsilon_3 + \cdots + 8\epsilon_8$$
> is a multiple of 3.
