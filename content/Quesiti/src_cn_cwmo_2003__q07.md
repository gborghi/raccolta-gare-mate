*Inequality for nonneg reals summing to 1 via weighted fractions*

> Let $r_1, r_2, \ldots, r_n$ be nonnegative real numbers with $\sum_{i=1}^{n} \frac{r_i}{i + r_i} = 1$. Prove that $\sum_{i=1}^{n} \frac{r_i}{4i - 1} \le 1$.
