*Fibonacci-type sequence: prove recurrence and find divisibility pairs*

> Assume that $\alpha$, $\beta$ are two roots of the equation $x^2 - x - 1 = 0$. Let $a_n = \dfrac{\alpha^n - \beta^n}{\alpha - \beta}$, $n = 1, 2, \ldots$
> (1) Prove that for any positive integer $n$, we have $a_{n+2} = a_{n+1} + a_n$.
> (2) Find all positive integers $a$ and $b$, $a < b$, satisfying that $b$ divides $a_n - 2a \cdot n^b$ for any positive integer $n$. (posed by Li Shenghong)
