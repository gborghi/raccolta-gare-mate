*Sphere, plane, three non-collinear points with tangent lines; pyramid circumsphere tangent*

> A sphere $s$ and a plane $\pi$ are given. On the plane $\pi$ three non-collinear points $A, B, C$ are given. Through each of these points a tangent line to $s$ is constructed. The contact points of these lines with $s$ are denoted by $K, L, M$, respectively. A point $P$ lies on $s$ and satisfies
> $$\frac{AK}{AP} = \frac{BL}{BP} = \frac{CM}{CP}.$$
> Prove that the circumsphere of the pyramid $ABCP$ is tangent to $s$.
