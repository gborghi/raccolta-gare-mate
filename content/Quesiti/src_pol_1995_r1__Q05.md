*Equivalence of two conditions on positive reals a, b*

> Prove that the following two conditions on positive numbers $a, b$ are equivalent:
> (i) $\sqrt{a} + 1 > \sqrt{b}$,
> (ii) for every $x > \frac{1}{x-1}$, we have $ax > b$.
