*Max length of a 0/1 sequence balanced in every 200 but unbalanced in every 202*

> Anna writes a sequence of $0$s and $1$s on the board. Anna notices that in every block of $200$ consecutive digits written on the board, there are as many digits $0$ as digits $1$. She also notices that in every block of $202$ consecutive digits written on the board, the number of $0$s and the number of $1$s are not equal. What is the maximum number of digits that Anna could have written on the board?
