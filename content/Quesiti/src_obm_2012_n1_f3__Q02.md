*Two different hexagons with all interior angles 120 degrees; find the perimeter of ABCDEF and the value of ST+PU from given side lengths.*

> Ana drew two different hexagons with interior angles of $120^\circ$ each. One of them is the hexagon $ABCDEF$ and the other is the hexagon $PQRSTU$.
> 
> a) If $AB = CD = 5$, $BC = 8$ and $EF = 3$, what is the perimeter of the hexagon $ABCDEF$?
> 
> b) If $PQ = 3$, $QR = 4$, $RS = 5$ and $TU = 1$, what is the value of $ST + PU$?
