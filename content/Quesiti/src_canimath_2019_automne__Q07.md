*Does strict ordering of fractions imply ordering of their mediants?*

> Is it true that, if $a_1$, $a_2$, $b_1$, $b_2$, $c_1$, $d_1$, $c_2$, $d_2$ are strictly positive integers such that
> $$\frac{a_1}{b_1} < \frac{c_1}{d_1} \quad \text{and} \quad \frac{a_2}{b_2} < \frac{c_2}{d_2},$$
> one always has
> $$\frac{a_1 + c_1}{b_1 + d_1} < \frac{a_2 + c_2}{b_2 + d_2}\,?$$
