*Diagonal sums in a 10×10 grid; sum mod 100 for diagonal 2007*

> Consider a $10 \times 10$ grid in which each cell in row $i$ (from the top, $i = 0, 1, \ldots, 9$) and column $j$ (from the left, $j = 0, 1, \ldots, 9$) contains the value $(i + j) \bmod 10$. The diagonals are numbered $1, 2, 3, \ldots$ as indicated in the figure.
> 
> a) What is the sum of the elements of diagonal $9$?
> 
> b) What is the remainder when the sum of the elements of diagonal $2007$ is divided by $100$?
