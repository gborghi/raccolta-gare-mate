*Minimize a rational expression in a,b given a cubic with only real roots.*

> Let $a$, $b$ be real numbers such that the equation $x^3 - ax^2 + bx - a = 0$ has only real roots. Find the minimum of $\dfrac{2a^3 - 3ab + 3a}{b + 1}$.
