*Find point P inside triangle maximising product of distances to sides*

> Determine the point $P$ inside a triangle $ABC$ for which the product $PL \cdot PM \cdot PN$ is a maximum, where $L$, $M$, $N$ are the feet of the perpendiculars from $P$ to $BC$, $CA$, $AB$ respectively.
