*If m>1, n>1 not perfect square, m divides n^2+n+1, prove |m-n| > sqrt(3n)-2*

> (Test Paper B1, Problem 3) Let $m$, $n$ be integers greater than $1$, and $n$ is not a perfect square. If $n^2 + n + 1$ is divisible by $m$, prove that
> $$|m - n| > \sqrt{3n} - 2.$$
