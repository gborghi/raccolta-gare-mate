*Find largest positive integer m so that abcd divides all (a,b,c,d) in S*

> Let $\mathbb{N}$ be the set of all positive integers and $S = \{(a, b, c, d) \in \mathbb{N}^4 : a^2 + b^2 + c^2 = d^2\}$. Find the largest positive integer $m$ so that $m$ divides $abcd$ for all $(a, b, c, d) \in S$.
