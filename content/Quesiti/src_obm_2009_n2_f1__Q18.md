# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2009 — Nível 2 — Fase 1 — Quesito 18

*18 students, 6 questions; each gets 4 right; each question correct same times; find m*

> Professor Piraldo gave a test of $6$ questions worth $0$ or $1$ point each, with no partial credit, to $18$ students. After the test, Piraldo made a table where each row represents a student and each column represents a question. The table (showing 3 of the 18 students) is:
> 
> | Student | Q1 | Q2 | Q3 | Q4 | Q5 | Q6 |
> |---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
> | Arnaldo | 0 | 1 | 1 | 1 | 0 | 0 |
> | Bernaldo | 1 | 1 | 1 | 0 | 1 | 0 |
> | Cernaldo | 0 | 1 | 1 | 1 | 0 | 0 |
> 
> Piraldo noticed that each student got exactly $4$ questions right and each question was answered correctly the same number of times. What is the value of $m$ (the number of times each question was answered correctly)?
> 
> **(A)** $8$ $\quad$ **(B)** $9$ $\quad$ **(C)** $10$ $\quad$ **(D)** $12$ $\quad$ **(E)** $14$
