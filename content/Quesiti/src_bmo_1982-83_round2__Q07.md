*In a colony of mn+1 mice prove Dilworth-type antichain or chain exists*

> In a colony of $(mn + 1)$ mice, prove that at least one of the following statements is true:
> 
> (a) There is a set $A$ of $(m + 1)$ mice none of which is a parent of any other in the set.
> 
> (b) There is an ordered set $B$ of $(n + 1)$ mice $a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_n, a_{n+1}$ such that $a_{i+1}$ is a parent of $a_i$ for each $i = 1, 2, \ldots, n$.
