*Reducing 1 to 100 by iterated operations; find last remaining number*

> Zoroastro wrote the numbers $1, 2, \ldots, 100$ in a black box. He will perform operations to reduce the quantity of numbers until only one remains. The first operation consists of choosing any two numbers $a$ and $b$ from the box and replacing them by $a + b - 1$. The second operation consists of choosing any two numbers $a$ and $b$ and replacing them by $a + b - 2$. In general, after $k$ operations the new operation is: choose any two numbers $a$ and $b$ and replace them by $a + b - (k+1)$. Determine which number will remain at the end.
