# Regional Mathematical Olympiad 2015 — Region 5 — Quesito 6

*Sum over ordered triples of subsets, express in terms of n*

> Let $S = \{1, 2, \ldots, n\}$ and let $T$ be the set of all ordered triples of subsets of $S$, say $(A_1, A_2, A_3)$, such that $A_1 \cup A_2 \cup A_3 = S$. Determine, in terms of $n$,
> $$\sum_{(A_1,\, A_2,\, A_3)\, \in\, T} |A_1 \cap A_2 \cap A_3|,$$
> where $|X|$ denotes the number of elements in the set $X$. (For example, if $S = \{1, 2, 3\}$ then one of the elements of $T$ is $A_1 = \{1, 2\}$, $A_2 = \{2, 3\}$, $A_3 = \{3\}$.)
