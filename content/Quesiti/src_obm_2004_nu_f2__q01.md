*Find f(3) for differentiable function satisfying tangent midpoint condition*

> The differentiable function $f:\mathbb{R}\to\mathbb{R}$ has the following properties:
> 
> a) $f(0)=0$ and $f(2)=2$.
> 
> b) For every $a\in\mathbb{R}\setminus\{0\}$, the tangent line to the graph of $f$ at the point $P=(a,f(a))$ cuts the $x$-axis at a point $A$ and the $y$-axis at a point $B$ such that $A$ is the midpoint of segment $BP$.
> 
> Compute $f(3)$.
