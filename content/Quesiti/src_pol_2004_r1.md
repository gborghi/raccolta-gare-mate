- [[src_pol_2004_r1__Q01|Quesito 1]] — A light ray reflecting inside a rectilinear polygon, starting along an angle bisector, eventually reaches a vertex.
- [[src_pol_2004_r1__Q02|Quesito 2]] — Existence of a prime $p$ and nonnegative integers $x,y,z$ with $(12x+5)(12y+7)=p^z$.
- [[src_pol_2004_r1__Q03|Quesito 3]] — Find all functions $f:\mathbb{Q}\to\mathbb{Q}$ with $f(x^2+y)=xf(x)+f(y)$ for all rationals.
- [[src_pol_2004_r1__Q04|Quesito 4]] — For all equilateral triangles circumscribed about an acute triangle, their centers lie on one circle.
- [[src_pol_2004_r1__Q05|Quesito 5]] — Show $N(m,n+1)=N(n,m+1)$, where $N(m,n)$ counts non-decreasing length-$m$ sequences from $\{1,\dots,n\}$.
- [[src_pol_2004_r1__Q06|Quesito 6]] — For real $c$ giving $P$ five real roots, find the sum of absolute values of the coefficients of $Q$.
- [[src_pol_2004_r1__Q07|Quesito 7]] — Find all positive integer solutions of $a^2+b^2=c^2$ with $a,c$ prime and $b$ a product of at most three primes.
- [[src_pol_2004_r1__Q08|Quesito 8]] — For a point $P$ inside tetrahedron $ABCD$, prove $\angle APB+\angle BPC+\angle CPD+\angle DPA>360^\circ$.
- [[src_pol_2004_r1__Q09|Quesito 9]] — For nonconstant integer polynomials $W_1,\dots,W_n$, some integer $a$ makes all $W_k(a)$ composite.
- [[src_pol_2004_r1__Q10|Quesito 10]] — A convex polygon with an even number of sides, side lengths 2 or 3 (each count even), has two vertices bisecting its perimeter.
- [[src_pol_2004_r1__Q11|Quesito 11]] — In an isosceles trapezoid with an inscribed rhombus $KLMN$, the circumcenter $O$ lies on line $KM$.
- [[src_pol_2004_r1__Q12|Quesito 12]] — Find the number of real solutions of a cyclic cubic-quartic system in $x_1,\dots,x_n$ for $n\ge 5$.
