*Angle bisectors and opposite sides in triangle with circumscribed circle*

> In triangle $ABC$, $\angle BAC = 120^\circ$. Let the angle bisectors of angles $A$, $B$, $C$ meet the opposite sides at $D$, $E$, $F$ respectively. Prove that the circle with diameter $EF$ passes through $D$.
