# Pre-Regional Mathematical Olympiad (PRMO) 2014 — Mumbai — Quesito 19

*Value of sum of x_i^2/(1-x_i) given x_1+...+x_2014=1 and cyclic sum condition*

> Let $x_1, x_2, \ldots, x_{2014}$ be real numbers different from 1, such that $x_1 + x_2 + \cdots + x_{2014} = 1$ and
> $$\frac{x_1}{1-x_1} + \frac{x_2}{1-x_2} + \cdots + \frac{x_{2014}}{1-x_{2014}} = 1.$$
> What is the value of
> $$\frac{x_1^2}{1-x_1} + \frac{x_2^2}{1-x_2} + \cdots + \frac{x_{2014}^2}{1-x_{2014}}\,?$$
