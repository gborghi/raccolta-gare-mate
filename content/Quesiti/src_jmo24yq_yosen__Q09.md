*Square ABCD diagonal midpoint O; points P,Q,R,S on sides; OP=3, OQ=OR=4; find XY*

> Square $ABCD$ has diagonals meeting at $O$. Points $P, Q, R, S$ lie on sides $AB$, $BC$, $CD$, $DA$ respectively. It is given that $OP = 3$ and $OQ = OR = 4$. The line through $C$ and $D$ meets the line through $Q$ and $R$ at a point; the line through $B$ and $C$ meets the line through $R$ and $S$ at another point; these two intersection points are $X$ and $Y$ respectively. Find the length of segment $XY$. (Here $\overline{XY}$ denotes the length of segment $XY$.)
