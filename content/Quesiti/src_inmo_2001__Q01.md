*Reflections of point P in triangle sides; incentre, circumcentre, orthocentre*

> Let $ABC$ be a triangle in which no angle is $90^\circ$. For any point $P$ in the plane of the triangle, let $A_1, B_1, C_1$ denote the reflections of $P$ in the sides $BC, CA, AB$ respectively. Prove the following statements:
> (a) If $P$ is the incentre or an excentre of $ABC$, then $P$ is the circumcentre of $A_1B_1C_1$;
> (b) If $P$ is the circumcentre of $ABC$, then $P$ is the orthocentre of $A_1B_1C_1$;
> (c) If $P$ is the orthocentre of $ABC$, then $P$ is either the incentre or an excentre of $A_1B_1C_1$.
