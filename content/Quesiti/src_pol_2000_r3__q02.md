*Angle sum is 180 degrees in an isosceles triangle with an interior point*

> Point $P$ is taken in the interior of a triangle $ABC$ with $AC = BC$ such that $\angle PAB = \angle PBC$. Point $M$ is the midpoint of $AB$. Prove that
> $$\angle APM + \angle BPC = 180^\circ.$$
