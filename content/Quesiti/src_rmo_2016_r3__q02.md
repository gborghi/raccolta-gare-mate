*Prove inequality with three distinct positive reals abc*

> Let $a, b, c$ be three distinct positive real numbers such that $abc = 1$. Prove that
> $$\frac{a^3}{(a-b)(a-c)} + \frac{b^3}{(b-c)(b-a)} + \frac{c^3}{(c-a)(c-b)} \ge 3.$$
