*Acute triangle, constructed circle intersections, show Z on perpendicular bisector of BC*

> Let $ABC$ be a triangle with all angles acute and such that $AB > BC$ and $AC > BC$. Let $X$ be the point on the half-line $[BC)$ such that $BX = BA$. Let $Y$ be the point on the half-line $[BA)$ such that $BY = BC$. Let $P$ be the point distinct from $A$ on the half-line $[AX)$ such that $CP = CA$. Let $Q$ be the point distinct from $A$ on the half-line $[AY)$ such that $BQ = BA$. Suppose that the lines $(BQ)$ and $(CP)$ meet at a point $Z$. Show that $Z$ lies on the perpendicular bisector of $[BC]$.
