*Quadrilateral with trisection points on two sides; prove equal third-areas*

> In the quadrilateral $ABCD$, points $M$ and $N$ are on side $AB$ such that $AM = MN = NB$, and points $P$ and $Q$ are on side $CD$ such that $CP = PQ = QD$. Prove that
> $$\text{Area of } AMCP = \text{Area of } MNQP = \tfrac{1}{3} \text{ Area of } ABCD.$$
