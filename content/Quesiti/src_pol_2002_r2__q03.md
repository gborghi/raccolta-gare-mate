*In an association of n members with 6 commissions each of at least n/4 members, prove two commissions share at least n/30 members.*

> A positive integer $n$ is given. In an association consisting of $n$ members work 6 commissions. Each commission contains at least $n/4$ persons. Prove that there exists two commissions containing at least $n/30$ persons in common.

In un'associazione di n membri con 6 commissioni ciascuna di almeno n/4 membri, dimostrare che due commissioni condividono almeno n/30 membri.*
