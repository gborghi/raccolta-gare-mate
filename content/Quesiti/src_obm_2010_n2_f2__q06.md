*Count legal 2×2010 two-colorings avoiding monochromatic L-tromino*

> Joãozinho wants to color a $2 \times 2010$ board with two colors $A$ and $B$. A coloring is called \emph{legal} if it is not possible to find an L-tromino (L-shaped tromino), as in the figure below, with all its squares the same color. Determine the number of legal colorings.
> 
> (An L-tromino consists of three unit squares forming an L-shape, as illustrated.)
