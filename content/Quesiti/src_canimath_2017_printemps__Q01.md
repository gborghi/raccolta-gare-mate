# Coupe Animath Printemps 2017 — Quesito 1

*Find three positive integers satisfying two linear conditions*

> *N.B. In this exercise, and only here, only an answer is required without justification.*
> 
> Let $m > n > p$ be three positive integers such that $m + n + p = 74$ and $m - n - p = 44$. Determine $m$, $n$, and $p$.
> 
> (A prime number is a strictly positive integer greater than $1$ whose only divisors are $1$ and itself.)
