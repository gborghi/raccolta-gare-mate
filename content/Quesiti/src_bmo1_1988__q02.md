*Find all real-valued functions on naturals satisfying f(x+y)=f(x)f(y)*

> Find all the real-valued functions $f$ defined on the set $D$ of natural numbers $x \ge 10$ and satisfying the functional equation
> $$f(x + y) = f(x)\, f(y)$$
> for all $x,\, y \in D$.
