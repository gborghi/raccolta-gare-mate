*Drinks carton (pyramid from four congruent triangles, QP = RS = 4 cm, PR = 10 cm); find the volume in cm^3.*

> A drinks carton is formed by arranging four congruent triangles as shown. $QP = RS = 4\text{ cm}$ and $PR = 10\text{ cm}$. What is the volume, in cm$^3$, of the carton?
>
> - **(A)** $\frac{10}{3}\sqrt{23}$
> - **(B)** $\frac{11}{3}\sqrt{10}$
> - **(C)** $\frac{11}{3}\sqrt{6}$
> - **(D)** $\frac{11}{3}\sqrt{5}$
> - **(E)** $\frac{1}{3}\sqrt{6}$
