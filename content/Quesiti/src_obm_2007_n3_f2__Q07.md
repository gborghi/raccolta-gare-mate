*Find all six-digit numbers of the form AABBB that are one less than a perfect square*

> Determine all six-digit numbers $n$ of the form $\overline{AABBB}$ (the first two digits both equal to $A$ and the last three digits both equal to $B$, with $A \neq B$) such that $n + 1$ is a perfect square; that is, find all $n = \overline{AABBB}$ for which there exists a positive integer $k$ with $k^2 = n + 1$.
