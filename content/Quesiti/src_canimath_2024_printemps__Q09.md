*Two linear equations in a,b,c imply a^2+b^2=2c^2*

> Let $a$, $b$ and $c$ be three reals satisfying both equations $4a + 3b = c$ and $3a - 4b = 7c$. Show that $a^2 + b^2 = 2c^2$.
