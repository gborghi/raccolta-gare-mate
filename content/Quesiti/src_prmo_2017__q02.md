*Find floor(a+b) given two radical equations in positive reals a,b*

> Suppose $a, b$ are positive real numbers such that $a\sqrt{a} + b\sqrt{b} = 183$, $a\sqrt{b} + b\sqrt{a} = 182$. Find $\lfloor(a + b)\rfloor$.
