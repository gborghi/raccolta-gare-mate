*Acute triangle, circumcenter O, foot D from A; angle AOD=90, OD=4sqrt7, AP=11; find EF*

> There is an acute triangle $ABC$ with circumcenter $O$. Let $D$ be the foot of the perpendicular from $A$ to side $BC$. It holds that $\angle AOD = 90^\circ$ and $OD = 4\sqrt{7}$. Let $E$ and $F$ be the feet of the perpendiculars from $D$ to sides $AB$ and $AC$ respectively. The segment $AO$ and the segment $EF$ meet at a point $P$, and $AP = 11$. Find the length $EF$. Here, $XY$ denotes the length of segment $XY$.
