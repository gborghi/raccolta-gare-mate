*Find triangle with altitude meeting BC at right angles*

> Find all triangles $ABC$ for which $AB + AC = 2$ cm, $AD + BC = \sqrt{5}$ cm, where $AD$ is the altitude through $A$, meeting $BC$ at right angles in $D$.
