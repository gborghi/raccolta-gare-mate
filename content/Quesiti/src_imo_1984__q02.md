*Find positive integers with divisibility conditions modulo 7 powers*

> Find one pair of positive integers $a$ and $b$ such that:
> 
> (i) $ab(a + b)$ is not divisible by $7$;
> 
> (ii) $(a + b)^7 - a^7 - b^7$ is divisible by $7^7$.
> 
> Justify your answer.
