*Positive sequence with nonlinear recurrence; find terms and bound partial sums*

> Let $\{a_n\}$ be a sequence of positive real numbers such that $a_1 = \frac{1}{2}$, and for $n \ge 2$,
> $$a_n^2(a_{n-1}+1) + a_{n-1}^2(a_n+1) - 2a_{n-1}a_n(a_{n-1}+a_n+1) = 0.$$
> (1) Find $a_n$ for $n \ge 1$.
> (2) Let $S_n = \sum_{i=1}^n a_i$. Show that $\ln\!\left(\frac{n}{2}+1\right) < S_n < \ln(n+1)$.
