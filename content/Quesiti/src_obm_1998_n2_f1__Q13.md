# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 1998 — Nível 2 — Fase 1 — Quesito 13

*Find ticket price per student given total and absences*

> To attend the film \textit{Central do Brasil}, each of $x$ students from a class should pay $y$ reais for the bus fare. Since 3 students were absent, each of the students present had to pay 2 reais more to cover the total cost. What was the price per person?
> 
> A) $(x+3)(y-2)$
> B) $(x-3)y + 2$
> C) $x(y-2) - 3$
> D) $xy - 6$
> E) $(x-3)(y+2)$
