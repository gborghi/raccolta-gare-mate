*Find all real triples satisfying three cyclic quadratic equations*

> Determine all triples of real numbers $(a, b, c)$ satisfying the three equations
> $$a^2 + b = c^2, \quad b^2 + c = a^2, \quad c^2 + a = b^2.$$
