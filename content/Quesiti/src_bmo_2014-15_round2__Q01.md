*Find 2015th term of recursively defined sequence starting at 2014.*

> The first term $x_1$ of a sequence is $2014$. Each subsequent term of the sequence is defined in terms of the previous term. The iterative formula is
> $$x_{n+1} = \frac{(\sqrt{2}+1)x_n - 1}{(\sqrt{2}+1) + x_n}.$$
> Find the $2015$th term $x_{2015}$.
