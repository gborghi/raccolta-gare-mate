*For positive a,b,c,d with reciprocals summing to 4, prove a sum of cube-root terms is at most 2(a+b+c+d)-4.*

> Positive numbers $a,b,c,d$ satisfy $\frac{1}{a}+\frac{1}{b}+\frac{1}{c}+\frac{1}{d}=4$. Prove that $$\sqrt[3]{\frac{a^3+b^3}{2}}+\sqrt[3]{\frac{b^3+c^3}{2}}+\sqrt[3]{\frac{c^3+d^3}{2}}+\sqrt[3]{\frac{d^3+a^3}{2}}\le 2(a+b+c+d)-4.$$
