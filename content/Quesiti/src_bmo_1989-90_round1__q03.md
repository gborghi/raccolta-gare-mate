*Convex quadrilateral with cos identity; prove trapezium or cyclic*

> The angles $A$, $B$, $C$, $D$ of a convex quadrilateral satisfy the relation
> $$\cos A + \cos B + \cos C + \cos D = 0.$$
> Prove that $ABCD$ is a trapezium or is cyclic.
