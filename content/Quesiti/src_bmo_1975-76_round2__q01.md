*Prove a ratio sum is constant for point in triangle*

> Through a point $P$ in the interior of a fixed triangle $ABC$ lines $PL$, $PM$, $PN$ are drawn parallel to the medians through $A$, $B$, $C$ respectively to meet $BC$, $CA$, $AB$ at $L$, $M$, $N$ respectively. Prove that
> $$\frac{BL}{BC} + \frac{CM}{CA} + \frac{AN}{AB}$$
> is constant (independent of $P$).
