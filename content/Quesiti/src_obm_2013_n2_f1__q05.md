*Ant on pentagon perimeter, position after 2013 seconds*

> In pentagon $ABCDE$, side lengths are $AB = BC = CD = 2$ metres and $DE = EA = 3$ metres. An ant starts at vertex $A$ and walks at constant speed of one metre per second along the sides, always in the same direction. Where will the ant be after $2013$ seconds?
> 
> (A) $A$ \quad (B) $B$ \quad (C) $C$ \quad (D) $D$ \quad (E) $E$
