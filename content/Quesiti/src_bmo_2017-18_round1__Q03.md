# British Mathematical Olympiad 2017-18 — Round 1 — Quesito 3

*Triangle ABC with point N; line through N divides area and perimeter equally*

> The triangle $ABC$ has $AB = BC$. The point $N$ is on the side $AC$ and $BN = AN$. The perpendicular to $BD$ which passes through $N$ meets the side $AB$ at $M$. Prove that $MN$ divides both the area and the perimeter of triangle $ABC$ into equal parts.
