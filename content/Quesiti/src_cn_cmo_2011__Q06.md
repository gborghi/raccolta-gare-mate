*Prove infinitely many coprime pairs a,b with a+b dividing am^a+bn^b*

> Prove that for any given positive integers $m$, $n$, there exist infinitely many pairs of coprime positive integers $a$, $b$, such that $a + b \mid am^a + bn^b$.
