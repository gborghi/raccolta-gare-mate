*Quadrilateral with diagonal point and angle conditions, find angle BCD*

> In quadrilateral $ABCD$, let the segments $AC$ and $BD$ intersect at point $P$. Moreover,
> $$AB = BP = AD, \qquad \angle ABC = \angle BDC, \qquad \angle BCD = \angle CAD$$
> hold. Find $\angle BCD$.
