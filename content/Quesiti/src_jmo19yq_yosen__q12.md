*Subsets of 10 points cut off by a half-space*

> A plane in space divides space into two parts; one of these parts (not including the plane itself) is called a half-space. Let $S$ be a set of $10$ points in space such that no four of them are coplanar. Find the number of subsets of $S$ that arise as the intersection of $S$ with some half-space.
