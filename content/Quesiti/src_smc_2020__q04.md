*Find p+q+r+s given a chain of products equal to 2, 20, 202, 2020*

> The numbers $p$, $q$, $r$ and $s$ satisfy the equations $p = 2$, $p \times q = 20$, $p \times q \times r = 202$ and $p \times q \times r \times s = 2020$. What is the value of $p + q + r + s$?
>
> - **(A)** $32$
> - **(B)** $32.1$
> - **(C)** $33$
> - **(D)** $33.1$
> - **(E)** $34$
