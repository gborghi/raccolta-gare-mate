# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2001 — Nível 2 — Fase 2 — Quesito 3

*Find all super-Olympic years for OBM divisibility condition*

> If the $n$-th OBM is held in a year that is divisible by $n$, we say that year is a **super-Olympic** year. For example, the year $2001$, in which the $23^{\text{rd}}$ OBM is being held, is super-Olympic because $2001 = 87 \times 23$ is divisible by $23$. Find all super-Olympic years, given that the OBM has been held every year since its first edition in $1979$, and is supposed to continue being held every year.
