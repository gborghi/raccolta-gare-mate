*Multiples of 3 but not 9 whose digit sum raises it to a multiple of 9*

> Let $A$ be a positive integer that is a multiple of $3$ but not a multiple of $9$. When each digit of $A$ is added to $A$, the result is a multiple of $9$. Find the minimum possible value of $A$.
