# Japan Mathematical Olympiad 2019 — Honsen (Final) — Quesito 3

*Functional equation over positive reals*

> Find all functions $f$ defined on the positive real numbers and taking positive real values such that, for all positive real numbers $x,y$,
> $$f\left(\frac{f(y)}{f(x)}+1\right)=f\left(x+\frac{y}{x}+1\right)-f(x).$$
