*Minimum area of a square whose vertices are roots of a quartic with integer coefficients*

> Consider a square on the complex plane. The complex numbers corresponding to its four vertices are the four roots of some equation of the fourth degree with one unknown and integer coefficients $x^4 + px^3 + qx^2 + rx + s = 0$. Find the minimum value of the area of such square. (posed by Xiong Jin)
