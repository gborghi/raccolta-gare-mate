*Place 1 to 9 in a propeller so blade sums are equal and maximal*

> Write the whole numbers from $1$ to $9$ in the nine little squares, in such a way that the sums of each one of the blades of the "propeller" are equal and the largest possible value. This value is:
> 
> (A) $23$ (B) $22$ (C) $21$ (D) $20$ (E) $19$
