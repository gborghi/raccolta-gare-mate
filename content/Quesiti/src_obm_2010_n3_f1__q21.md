*With n=2010^2010 and log n defined, order the three quantities n!, n^{log n}, (log n)^n.*

> Let $n=2010^{2010}$ and let $\log n$ be the number $m$ such that $10^m=n$. Then
>
> - **(A)** $n!<n^{\log n}<(\log n)^n$
> - **(B)** $n^{\log n}<n!<(\log n)^n$
> - **(C)** $(\log n)^n<n^{\log n}<n!$
> - **(D)** $(\log n)^n<n!<n^{\log n}$
> - **(E)** $n^{\log n}<(\log n)^n<n!$
