# Japan Junior Mathematical Olympiad 2015 — Yosen (Preliminary) — Quesito 6

*Color 2015 boxes red/blue/white; odd boxes not white, adjacent boxes differ*

> There are $2015$ boxes arranged in a row. Into each box we place exactly one ball, which is red, blue, or white. Moreover, into the boxes at odd positions counting from the left we do not place a white ball, and adjacent boxes must not contain balls of the same color. How many ways are there to place the balls?
