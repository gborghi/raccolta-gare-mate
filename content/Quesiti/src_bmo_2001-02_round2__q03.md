*Recurrence with nested square root yields only integers*

> Prove that the sequence defined by
> $$b_1 = 1, \quad b_{n+1} = \tfrac{1}{2}\left(3b_n + \sqrt{5b_n^2 - 4}\right), \quad (n \ge 1)$$
> consists only of integers.
