*Seventh iterate of 2^x; show g(3)-g(0) is divisible by g(2)-g(0).*

> Consider the functions $f(x) = 2^x$ and $g(x) = f(f(f(f(f(f(f(x)))))))$ (the seventh iteration of $f$). Show that the number $g(3) - g(0)$ is divisible by $g(2) - g(0)$.
