*In a row of six cells, each interior cell equals the mean of its two neighbours. The first cell is 10 and the last is 25. Which number goes in the square marked X (the third cell)?*

> In the grid below each of the blank squares and the square marked $X$ are to be filled by the mean of the two numbers in its adjacent squares. $\lvert\, 10 \,\rvert\, \ldots \,\rvert\, X \,\rvert\, \ldots \,\rvert\, \ldots \,\rvert\, 25 \,\rvert$ Which number should go in the square marked $X$?
>
> - **(A)** 15
> - **(B)** 16
> - **(C)** 17
> - **(D)** 18
> - **(E)** 19
