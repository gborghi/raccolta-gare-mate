*Find all functions f:R->R with f(xf(y)+f(x))+f(y^2)=f(x)+yf(x+y)*

> Determine all functions $f : \mathbb{R} \to \mathbb{R}$ such that for all $x, y \in \mathbb{R}$:
> $$f(x f(y) + f(x)) + f(y^2) = f(x) + y f(x + y).$$
