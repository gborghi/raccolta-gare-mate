*Place n² cards on n×n grid so adjacent cards have coprime values*

> Let $n \ge 2$ be an integer. There are $n^2$ cards labeled $1, 2, \ldots, n^2$. For each integer $i$ with $1 \le i \le n^2$, card $i$ has the number $\left\lfloor \dfrac{n^2}{i} \right\rfloor$ written on it. We wish to place one card in each cell of an $n \times n$ grid (one card per cell) such that the following condition holds:
> 
> For any two cells that share a side, the two numbers written on the cards placed in those cells are coprime.
> 
> Find all values of $n$ for which such an arrangement exists.
