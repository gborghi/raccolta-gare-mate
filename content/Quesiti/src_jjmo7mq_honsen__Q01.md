# Japan Junior Mathematical Olympiad 2009 — Honsen (Final) — Quesito 1

*Sum of integers coprime to n; find when sum is prime*

> For a positive integer $n$, let $S(n)$ denote the sum of all integers from $1$ to $n$ whose greatest common divisor with $n$ is $1$.
> 
> (1) Find $S(30)$. (Proof is not required.)
> 
> (2) Find all positive integers $n$ such that $S(n)$ is prime. Here, a prime is an integer greater than or equal to $2$ that has no positive divisors other than $1$ and itself, and is not equal to $1$.
