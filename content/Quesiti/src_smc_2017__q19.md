*Circle radius 1 touches three sides of 2×4 rectangle; diagonal intersects circle at P and Q. Find length of chord PQ.*

> The diagram shows a circle of radius $1$ touching three sides of a $2 \times 4$ rectangle. A diagonal of the rectangle intersects the circle at the points $P$ and $Q$, as shown.
> 
> What is the length of the chord $PQ$?
>
> - **(A)** $\sqrt{5}$
> - **(B)** $\dfrac{2}{\sqrt{5}}$
> - **(C)** $\dfrac{5\sqrt{6}}{6}$
> - **(D)** $\dfrac{5\sqrt{6}}{5}$
> - **(E)** $2$
