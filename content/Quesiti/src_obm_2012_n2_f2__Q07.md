# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2012 — Nível 2 — Fase 2 — Quesito 7

*Area of triangle AEC given rectangle ABCD and external equilateral triangle CED*

> Let $ABCD$ be a rectangle such that $AD = 6$ and $DC = 8$. Construct an equilateral triangle $CED$ such that $C$, $A$, and $B$ lie in the same half-plane determined by line $CD$. Determine the area of triangle $AEC$.
