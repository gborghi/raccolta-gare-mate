*If b|a^3, c|b^3, a|c^3 then abc divides (a+b+c)^13*

> Let $a, b, c$ be positive integers such that $b \mid a^3$, $c \mid b^3$ and $a \mid c^3$. Prove that $abc \mid (a + b + c)^{13}$.
