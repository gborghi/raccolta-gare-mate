*Find range of c for positive reals satisfying a cyclic inequality*

> Let $x$, $y$, $z$ be positive real numbers satisfying
> $$\frac{1}{3} \le \frac{x}{x+y+z} \le \frac{1}{2}.$$
> Determine the range of values for (i) $\frac{y}{x+y+z}$, and (ii) $\frac{x+y}{x+y+z}$.
