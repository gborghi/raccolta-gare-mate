*Taurus numbers: determine if 2021 qualifies; find count f(n) among 1..n*

> For a positive integer $k$, if it is possible to remove a number from $M_k = \{1, 2, \ldots, k\}$ such that the sum of the remaining $k-1$ numbers is a perfect square, then $k$ is called a "Taurus number". For instance, $7$ is a Taurus number, since removing $3$ from $\{1,2,3,4,5,6,7\}$ results in the sum $1+2+4+5+6+7 = 5^2$.
> (1) Determine with reasoning whether $2021$ is a Taurus number.
> (2) Find $f(n)$ (in terms of $n$), the number of Taurus numbers among $1, 2, \ldots, n$.
