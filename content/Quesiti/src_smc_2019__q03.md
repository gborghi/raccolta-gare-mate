*Convert one mil (one thousandth of an inch, 2.5 cm per inch) to mm*

> Used in measuring the width of a wire, one mil is equal to one thousandth of an inch. An inch is about $2.5\,\text{cm}$.
> 
> Which of these is approximately equal to one mil?
>
> - **(A)** $\frac{1}{40}\,\text{mm}$
> - **(B)** $\frac{1}{25}\,\text{mm}$
> - **(C)** $\frac{1}{4}\,\text{mm}$
> - **(D)** $25\,\text{mm}$
> - **(E)** $40\,\text{mm}$
