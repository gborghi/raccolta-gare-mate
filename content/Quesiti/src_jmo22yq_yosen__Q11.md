*2n×2n grid; color exactly 2n^2 cells so no two colored cells share a side*

> There is a $2n \times 2n$ grid. Colour exactly $2n^2$ cells according to the following condition:
> 
> Condition: If a coloured cell shares a vertex with another coloured cell, they must NOT share a side (i.e., no two coloured cells are side-adjacent).
> 
> Find the number of such colourings. (Colourings related by rotation or reflection are counted as different.)
