*Find all positive k and irrational a>1 such that floor(a^n) is perfect square minus k for all large n*

> Find all positive integers $k$ for which there exists an irrational number $a > 1$ and a positive integer $N$ such that $\lfloor a^n \rfloor$ is a perfect square minus $k$, for every integer $n$ with $n > N$.
> 
> *Observation:* $\lfloor x \rfloor$ denotes the greatest integer less than or equal to $x$.
