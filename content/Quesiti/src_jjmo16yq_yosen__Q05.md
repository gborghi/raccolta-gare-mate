*Area of rectangle PQRS inscribed in rectangle ABCD with FQ:QR=2:3*

> On the sides $AB$, $BC$, $CD$, $DA$ of rectangle $ABCD$ there are points $P$, $Q$, $R$, $S$ respectively such that the quadrilateral $PQRS$ is a rectangle. It is given that $AB = 22$, $BC = 23$, and $BQ : QC = 2 : 3$ (where $XY$ denotes the length of segment $XY$). Find the area of rectangle $PQRS$.
