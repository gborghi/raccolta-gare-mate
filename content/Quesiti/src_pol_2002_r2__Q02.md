*Convex quadrilateral ABCD with angle ADB=2 angle ACB and angle BDC=2 angle BAC; prove AD=CD.*

> In a convex quadrilateral $ABCD$ the following equalities
> $$\angle ADB = 2\angle ACB \quad \text{and} \quad \angle BDC = 2\angle BAC$$
> hold. Prove that $AD=CD$.
