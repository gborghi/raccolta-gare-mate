# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2013 — Nível 3 — Fase 1 — Quesito 8

*Triangle of odd integers in rows of increasing length; which row contains 2013*

> The arithmetic triangle of Fibonacci is formed by the odd positive integers, arranged in rows of increasing length and jumping to the next row, starting from $1$. Row $n$ has exactly $n$ numbers. Look at the first four rows:
> 
> Row 1: $1$
> Row 2: $3\ 5$
> Row 3: $7\ 9\ 11$
> Row 4: $13\ 15\ 17\ 19$
> 
> In which row will $2013$ appear?
> 
> (A) $45$ \quad (B) $46$ \quad (C) $62$ \quad (D) $63$ \quad (E) $64$
