*Four-digit numbers, distinct digits summing to 12, two even and two odd*

> Carlinhos makes a list of all the four-digit numbers with distinct digits satisfying the following conditions: the sum of the digits is $12$, and among the digits two are even and two are odd. The number $2703$, for example, is in his list. Remember that the number cannot have a leading zero (zero on the left).
> 
> a) What is the number closest to $2016$ that is in Carlinhos's list?
> 
> b) Determine the sum of all the numbers in Carlinhos's list that are smaller than $2016$.
