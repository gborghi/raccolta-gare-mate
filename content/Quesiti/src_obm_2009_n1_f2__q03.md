*From a 5-day attendance table where each student missed exactly 2 days, find what percent of total absences fell on the lowest-attendance day*

> In a course lasting five days, the attendance of the students was recorded in the table below:
> 
> | Day of class | 1st day | 2nd day | 3rd day | 4th day | 5th day |
> |---|---|---|---|---|---|
> | Number of students present | $271$ | $296$ | $325$ | $380$ | $168$ |
> 
> Each student was absent exactly two days. On the day of lowest attendance, what percent of the total of absences [occurred on that day]?
