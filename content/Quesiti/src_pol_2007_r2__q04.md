*If positive integers a,b,c,d satisfy ad=b^2+bc+c^2, then a^2+b^2+c^2+d^2 is composite.*

> Prove that if $a,b,c,d$ are positive integers satisfying $ad=b^2+bc+c^2$, then the number $a^2+b^2+c^2+d^2$ is composite.
