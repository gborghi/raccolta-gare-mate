*Find all odd integer pairs (a,b)>1 satisfying Euler totient equation*

> Find all odd integer pairs $(a, b)$ with $a, b > 1$, such that
> $$7\varphi^2(a) - \varphi(ab) + 11\varphi^2(b) = b^2,$$
> where $\varphi(n)$ represents the number of positive integers less than $n$ and relatively prime to $n$.
