*Count pairs {A,B} with A,B subsets of {1,...,12} satisfying conditions*

> Let $X = \{1, 2, 3, \ldots, 12\}$. Find the number of pairs $\{A, B\}$ such that $A \subseteq X$, $B \subseteq X$, $A \neq B$ and $A \cap B = \{2, 3, 5, 7, 8\}$.
