*Internal point in triangle, three parallel lines, prove product inequality*

> Let $P$ be an internal point of triangle $ABC$. The line through $P$ parallel to $AB$ meets $BC$ at $L$, the line through $P$ parallel to $BC$ meets $CA$ at $M$, and the line through $P$ parallel to $CA$ meets $AB$ at $N$. Prove that
> $$\frac{BL}{BC} \cdot \frac{CM}{CA} \cdot \frac{AN}{AB} \le \frac{1}{8},$$
> and locate the position of $P$ in triangle $ABC$ when equality holds.
