# Regional Mathematical Olympiad 1990 — Quesito 7

*Censuswoman deduces individual ages from two sums of pairs*

> A censuswoman on duty visited a house which the lady inmates declined to reveal their individual ages, but said — "we do not mind giving you the sum of the ages of any two ladies you may choose". Thereupon the censuswoman said — "In that case please give me the sum of the ages of every possible pair of you". She gave the sums as follows: 30, 33, 41, 58, 66, 69. The censuswoman took these figures and happily went away. How did she calculate the individual ages of the ladies from these figures?
