*Bound total time over three road segments given average-speed ranges*

> A pilot covers three segments of a road, of lengths $240$ km, $300$ km and $400$ km, respectively. The average speeds on these three segments are between $40$ km/h, $75$ km/h and $80$ km/h. We can guarantee that the total time, in hours, spent by the pilot on the three segments is:
> (A) at least $13$ hours (B) at least $13$ hours and at most $18$ hours (C) at least $14$ hours and at most $18$ hours (D) at least $15$ hours and at most $18$ hours (E) at least $18$ hours

Possiamo garantire che il tempo totale, in ore, trascorso dal pilota sui tre segmenti sia: (A) almeno $13$ ore (B) almeno $13$ ore e al massimo $18$ ore (C) almeno $14$ ore e al massimo $18$ ore (D) almeno $15$ ore e al massimo $18$ ore (E) almeno $18$ ore
