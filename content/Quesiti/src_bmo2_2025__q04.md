*Count sequences of positive integers with u_1=1 and given recurrence*

> How many different sequences of positive integers satisfy $u_1 = 1$ and
> $$u_{n+1} = \frac{(u_n^2 + u_n + 1)^{2025}}{u_{n-1}}$$
> for all $n \ge 2$?
