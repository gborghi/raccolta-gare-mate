*Prove a telescoping inequality for non-negative reals*

> Let $a_1$, $a_2$, $\ldots$, $a_n$ be $n$ non-negative real numbers. Prove that
> $$\frac{1}{1+a_1} + \frac{a_1}{(1+a_1)(1+a_2)} + \cdots + \frac{a_1 a_2 \cdots a_{n-1}}{(1+a_1)(1+a_2)\cdots(1+a_n)} \le 1.$$
> (posed by Ai Yinghua)
