*Find all roots of a partial alternating binomial sum equal to 1/2*

> Determine with proof all the roots of the equation
> $$\sum_{r=1}^{m} (-1)^{r-1} \frac{n(n-1)\cdots(n-r+1)}{r!} = \frac{1}{2}$$
> where $m$ is a given positive integer.
