*Number of ways to give 37 cents change in 1,5,10,25-cent coins*

> Luca bought a magazine for R\$9.63 and gave a R\$10.00 note to pay. In how many ways can he receive the change of 37 cents in coins, if the coins available in the cash box are of $1, 5, 10$ and $25$ centavos? Suppose there are many coins of each type.
> 
> (A) 10 \quad (B) 12 \quad (C) 15 \quad (D) 24 \quad (E) 30
