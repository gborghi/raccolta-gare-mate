# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2008 — Nível 1 — Fase 1 — Quesito 20

*Three cars at constant speeds; given relative positions when A overtakes B; find A–C distance*

> Three cars travel at constant speeds along the same road, all passing through the same point at the same moment. When car A is 100 km ahead of car B, car A is at Brasília; at that same moment, car A is 20 km ahead of car B, and car B is 50 km ahead of car C. When A is 100 km ahead of B, how many kilometers ahead of C is A?
> 
> A) 20 $\quad$ B) 25{,}5 $\quad$ C) 30 $\quad$ D) 35 $\quad$ E) 37{,}5
