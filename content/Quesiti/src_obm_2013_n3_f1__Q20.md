*Count positive integers k<2013 with integers a,b,c satisfying a^2+b+c=b^2+c+a=c^2+a+b=k*

> For how many positive integers $k$ less than $2013$ do there exist integers $a$, $b$ and $c$, not necessarily distinct, satisfying
> $$a^2 + b + c = b^2 + c + a = c^2 + a + b = k\,?$$
> 
> (A) $43$ \quad (B) $44$ \quad (C) $87$ \quad (D) $88$ \quad (E) $89$
