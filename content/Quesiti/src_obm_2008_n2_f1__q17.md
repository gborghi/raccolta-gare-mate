*Smallest x+y making a sum of powers a perfect square*

> Observe that:
> $$3^{2}+4^{2}=5^{2},$$
> $$3^{3}+4^{3}+12^{2}=13^{2},$$
> $$3^{4}+4^{4}+12^{2}+84^{2}=85^{2}.$$
> What is the smallest possible value of the sum $x+y$ with $x$, $y$ positive integers such that $3^{5}+4^{5}+12^{2}+84^{2}+x^{2}=y^{2}$?
> (A) $289$ \quad (B) $250$ \quad (C) $425$ \quad (D) $795$ \quad (E) $103$
