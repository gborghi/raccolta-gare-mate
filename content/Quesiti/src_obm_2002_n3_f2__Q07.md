# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2002 — Nível 3 — Fase 2 — Quesito 7

*Non-congruent triangles in ABC with altitude AD and given ratio*

> In triangle $ABC$, let $AD$ be the altitude relative to $BC$. How many non-congruent triangles satisfy
> $$\frac{1}{AB^2} + \frac{1}{AC^2} = \frac{1}{AD^2}$$
> with $AD = 2012$ and $BD$ and $CD$ both integers? Note that $AB$ and $AC$ need not be integers.
