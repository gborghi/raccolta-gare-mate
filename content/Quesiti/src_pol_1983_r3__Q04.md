*Prove that for natural numbers a,b,c,d with ab=cd, gcd(a,c)gcd(a,d)/gcd(a,b,c,d) = a.*

> Prove that if natural numbers $a,b,c,d$ satisfy the equality $ab = cd$, then
> $$\frac{\gcd(a,c)\,\gcd(a,d)}{\gcd(a,b,c,d)} = a.$$
