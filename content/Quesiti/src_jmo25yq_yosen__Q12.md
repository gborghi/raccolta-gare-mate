# Japan Mathematical Olympiad 2015 — Yosen (Preliminary) — Quesito 12

*Find maximum constant a such that any black-white colored grid always admits a·n² non-overlapping L-tromino placements*

> For any positive integer $n$, consider an $(n+1) \times (n+1)$ grid of unit cells, each colored black or white. We wish to place non-overlapping L-shaped trominos (each covering $3$ cells forming a $2 \times 2$ square with one corner removed, i.e.\ an L-tromino) on the grid, subject to the condition that within the $2 \times 2$ bounding square of each placed tromino, the $2 \times 2$ region must contain both a black cell and a white cell (i.e.\ must not be monochromatic). Find the maximum real number $a$ such that, for every positive integer $n$ and every black-white coloring of the $(n+1) \times (n+1)$ grid, one can always place at least $\lfloor a n^2 \rfloor$ such non-overlapping trominos.
> 
> (Here $[r]$ denotes the greatest integer not exceeding the real number $r$.)
