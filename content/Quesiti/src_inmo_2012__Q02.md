# Indian National Mathematical Olympiad 2012 — Quesito 2

*Two prime sets with equal span 8; prove 30 divides difference of smallest elements*

> Let $\{p_1, p_2, p_3, p_4\}$ be a subset of $\{p_1, p_1+2, p_1+4, p_1+6, p_1+8\}$ and $\{q_1, q_2, q_3, q_4\}$ be a subset of $\{q_1, q_1+2, q_1+4, q_1+6, q_1+8\}$ be two sets of prime numbers such that $p_4 - p_1 = 8$ and $q_4 - q_1 = 8$. Suppose $p_1 > 5$ and $q_1 > 5$. Prove that $30$ divides $p_1 - q_1$.
