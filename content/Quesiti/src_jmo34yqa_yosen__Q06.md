*Find BC in isosceles triangle with circle tangent conditions and CF=10*

> In isosceles triangle $ABC$ with $AB = AC = 5$, let $D$ be the point on side $AB$ with $AD = 3$, and let $E$ be a point on side $BC$ (excluding endpoints). Let $\omega$ be the circle passing through $E$ and tangent to line $AB$ at point $B$; suppose $\omega$ is also tangent to the circumcircle of triangle $ADE$. Let $F$ be the intersection of $\omega$ and line $AE$ other than $E$. If $CF = 10$, find the length $BC$. (Here $XY$ denotes the length of segment $XY$.)
