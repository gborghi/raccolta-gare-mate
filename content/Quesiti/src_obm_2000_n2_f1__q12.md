*Minimum cards to remove to guarantee three equal smallest digit-sums*

> A box contains $900$ cards numbered from $100$ to $999$. Cards are drawn at random (without replacement) and the sum of their digits is recorded. What is the minimum number of cards that must be removed from the box so that it is guaranteed that the three cards drawn have equal digit-sums?
> 
> A) $51$ \quad B) $52$ \quad C) $53$ \quad D) $54$ \quad E) $55$

Qual è il numero minimo di carte che deve essere rimosso dalla scatola per garantire che le tre carte sorte abbiano le stesse somme digitali?
