*top and bottom edges all same color*

> Note. A path of n jumps is a sequence of vertices (P0, ..., Pn) such that
> (i) P0 = A, Pn = E;
> (ii) for every i, 0 ≤i ≤n −1, Pi is distinct from E;
> (iii) for every i, 0 ≤i ≤n −1, Pi and Pi+1 are adjacent.
