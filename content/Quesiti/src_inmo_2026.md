- [[src_inmo_2026__Q01|Quesito 1]] — Determine which positive integers m occur as a perfect-square term m^2 in a floor-of-square-root recurrence sequence.
- [[src_inmo_2026__Q02|Quesito 2]] — For f:N->N where f(k) counts the maximum multiplicity in f(1),...,f(k-1) for k>2026, prove f(n)=f(n+1) for infinitely many n.
- [[src_inmo_2026__Q03|Quesito 3]] — In an acute scalene triangle, with P,Q on the sides satisfying BP=BN, CQ=CN and K on line AN with MK=MN, prove angle PKQ is a right angle.
- [[src_inmo_2026__Q04|Quesito 4]] — Determine all f:N0->N0 with f(0)=0 such that f(n)+m and f(n)+f(m) are companions (share exactly the same prime divisors) for all m,n.
- [[src_inmo_2026__Q05|Quesito 5]] — For an interior point P of an acute triangle T with successive reflections in its sides, prove the six triple-reflection images are concyclic iff P is the orthocentre.
- [[src_inmo_2026__Q06|Quesito 6]] — Two decks of 40 cards play repeated deterministic duels with win/draw/no-interaction rules; prove the maximum time a terminating game can take is 356 hours.
