*Integer as sum of two different powers of 2 and two different squares*

> Given that an integer $n$ is the sum of two different powers of $2$ and also the sum of two different Mersenne primes, prove that $n$ is the sum of two different square numbers.
> 
> (A Mersenne prime is a prime number which is one less than a power of two.)
