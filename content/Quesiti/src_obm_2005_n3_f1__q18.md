*How many of 13 nonzero reals are negative given 22 negative pairwise products and more positives*

> Among thirteen nonzero real numbers there are more positive numbers than negative ones. Among the $\dfrac{13 \times 12}{2} = 78$ products of pairs of the thirteen numbers, $22$ are negative. How many of the thirteen given numbers are negative?
> (A) $2$ (B) $7$ (C) $8$ (D) $9$ (E) $10$
