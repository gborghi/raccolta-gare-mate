# Indian Olympiad Qualifier in Mathematics (IOQM) 2025 — Sep 07 — Quesito 23

*Rectangle ABCD, points M on AB and N on BC with MC=CD, MD=MN, C,D,M,N concyclic; find m+n*

> Let $ABCD$ be a rectangle and let $M$, $N$ be points lying on sides $AB$ and $BC$, respectively. Assume that $MC = CD$ and $MD = MN$, and that points $C$, $D$, $M$, $N$ lie on a circle. If $(AB/BC)^2 = m/n$ where $m$ and $n$ are positive integers with $\gcd(m, n) = 1$, what is the value of $m + n$?
