*Area of paper coloured black when a square ink pad is rotated 180 degrees about its corner*

> A square ink pad has sides of length 1 cm. It is covered in black ink and carefully placed in the middle of a piece of white paper. The square pad is then rotated $180^\circ$ about one of its corners. The pad is then removed from the paper. What area of paper, in $\text{cm}^2$, is coloured black?
>
> - **(A)** $\pi + 2$
> - **(B)** $2\pi - 1$
> - **(C)** $4$
> - **(D)** $2\pi - 2$
> - **(E)** $\pi + 1$
