# British Mathematical Olympiad 1983-84 — Round 2 — Quesito 2

*Tetrahedron with equal edges; minimise area of cross-section quadrilateral*

> $ABCD$ is a tetrahedron with $DA = DB = DC = d$ and $AB = BC = CA = e$. $M$ and $N$ are the midpoints of $AB$ and $CD$ respectively. A plane $p$ passes through $MN$ and cuts $AD$ at $P$ and $BC$ at $Q$ respectively.
> 
> (i) Prove that $AP/AD = BQ/BC$ (= $t$, say).
> 
> (ii) Determine with proof the value of $t$, expressed in terms of $d$ and $e$, which minimises the area of the quadrilateral $MQNP$.
