*State the cosine rule; prove sqrt(a)+sqrt(b)>sqrt(c) for triangle sides a,b,c*

> State the cosine rule for a triangle.
> 
> Prove that for positive real numbers $a$, $b$, $c$ which are the sides of a triangle,
> $$\sqrt{a} + \sqrt{b} > \sqrt{c}.$$
