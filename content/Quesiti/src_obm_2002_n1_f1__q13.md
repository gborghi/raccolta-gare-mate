*Minimum number of children to prefer the bus company option*

> A school is going to organize an excursion to the zoo. There are two options of transport. The first option is to rent "vans": each van can carry $8$ children and its rental costs R\$$60{,}00$. The second option is to contract a company that does the service: the company hires buses with capacity for $48$ children and charges R\$$237{,}00$, plus R\$$120{,}00$ per bus used. The school is going to prefer the bus company if there are at least $N$ children. The value of $N$ is:
> 
> (A) $28$ (B) $31$ (C) $32$ (D) $33$ (E) $36$

*Minimum di bambini che preferiscono l'opzione della compagnia di autobus*

> Una scuola organizzerà un'escursione allo zoo. Ci sono due opzioni di trasporto. The second option is to contract a company that does the service: the company hires buses with capacity for $48$ children and charges R\$$237{,}00$, plus R\$$120{,}00$ per autobus utilizzato. La scuola preferirà la compagnia degli autobus se ci sono almeno < MSK4/> bambini. Il valore di $N$ è:
> 
> (A) $28$ (B) $31$ (C) $32$ (D) $33$ (E) $36$
