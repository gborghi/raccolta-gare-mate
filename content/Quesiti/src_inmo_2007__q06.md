*Prove cyclic product inequality for positive reals*

> If $x, y, z$ are positive real numbers, prove that
> $$(x + y + z)^2(yz + zx + xy)^2 \le 3(y^2 + yz + z^2)(z^2 + zx + x^2)(x^2 + xy + y^2).$$
