*Integer-coefficient quadratic with positive real root less than 1/2*

> The equation $ax^2 + bx + c = 0$, with $a$, $b$, $c$ non-zero integers, has a positive real solution less than $\dfrac{1}{2}$.
> 
> (a) Give an example of an equation satisfying the statement in which two of the numbers $a$, $b$, $c$ are equal to $2025$ and the other has absolute value less than or equal to $2025$. (Recall that $a \neq 0$, $b \neq 0$, $c \neq 0$.)
> 
> (b) Prove that at least one of the numbers $a$, $b$, or $c$ has absolute value greater than or equal to $2025$.
