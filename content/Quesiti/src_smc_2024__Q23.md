*Perimeter of the Einstein 'hat tile' made of congruent kite pieces with area 8√3*

> The plane can be tiled using the 'hat tile' shown here. This tile can be subdivided into eight congruent kites. The area of the tile is $8\sqrt{3}$. What is the perimeter of the tile?
>
> - **(A)** $8 + 12\sqrt{3}$
> - **(B)** $16 + 6\sqrt{3}$
> - **(C)** $8\sqrt{3}$
> - **(D)** $6 + 8\sqrt{3}$
> - **(E)** $8 + 6\sqrt{3}$
