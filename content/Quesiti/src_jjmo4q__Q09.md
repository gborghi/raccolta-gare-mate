*Count coprime pairs (x,y,z) where tens digits of x·z and y·z equal tens digits of x and y*

> Let $x$, $y$, $z$ be mutually coprime positive integers of two digits each. The tens digit of $x \cdot z$ equals the tens digit of $x$, and the tens digit of $y \cdot z$ equals the tens digit of $y$, and the units digit of $x \cdot z$ equals the units digit of $y \cdot z$. How many such triples $(x, y, z)$ exist?
