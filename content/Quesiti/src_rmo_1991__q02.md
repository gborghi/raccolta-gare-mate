*Inequality for four positive reals summing condition*

> If $a$, $b$, $c$ and $d$ are any 4 positive real numbers, then prove that
> $$\frac{a}{b} + \frac{b}{c} + \frac{c}{d} + \frac{d}{a} \ge 4.$$
