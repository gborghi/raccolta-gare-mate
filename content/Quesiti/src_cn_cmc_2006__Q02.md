*Range of x satisfying logarithmic inequality*

> Suppose $\log_x(2x^2 + x - 1) > \log_x 2 - 1$. Then the range of $x$ is (\ \ ).
> 
> (A) $\frac{1}{2} < x < 1$\quad (B) $x > \frac{1}{2}$ and $x \ne 1$\quad (C) $x > 1$\quad (D) $0 < x < 1$
