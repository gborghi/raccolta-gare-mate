*Two triangles with related sides; prove right-angle and find angles*

> The sides $a$, $b$, $c$ and $u$, $v$, $w$ of two triangles $ABC$ and $UVW$ are related by the equations
> $$v(u + v - w) = a^2, \quad u(u + v - w) = b^2, \quad w(u + v - w) = c^2.$$
> Prove that triangle $ABC$ is right-angled and express the angles $U$, $V$, $W$ in terms of $A$, $B$, $C$.
