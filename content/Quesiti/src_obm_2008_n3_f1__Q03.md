*Positive integer a and 1/a on a number line; find their sum*

> The positive integer $a$ and the number $\dfrac{1}{a}$ are located on the line in the following manner. (On the number line there are marks at $0$, at $\dfrac{1}{a}$, and at $a$; the distance from $\dfrac{1}{a}$ to $a$ is $\dfrac{80}{9}$.)
> 
> What is the sum of these two numbers?
> 
> (A) $\dfrac{9}{81}$ (B) $\dfrac{9}{80}$ (C) $\dfrac{81}{9}$ (D) $\dfrac{82}{9}$ (E) $9$
