*Find XY in a parallelogram given midpoint and intersection conditions with CE=DF=3*

> In parallelogram $ABCD$, let $E$ be a point on side $CD$ (excluding endpoints). Let $M$ be the midpoint of segment $AE$, and let $G$ be the intersection of line $BM$ and line $AD$. Given that $CE = DF = 3$ (where $F$ is a specific point defined by the configuration), $MB = 5$, $MC = 4$, find the length $XY$, where $XY$ denotes the length of segment $AB$.
