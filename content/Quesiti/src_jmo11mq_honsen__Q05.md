# Japan Mathematical Olympiad 2001 — Honsen (Final) — Quesito 5

*Triangles ABC, PQR: midpoint and angle-bisector conditions; prove AB+AC=PQ+PR*

> In a plane, triangles $ABC$ and $PQR$ satisfy both of the following conditions:\n(1) Point $A$ is the midpoint of $QR$, and point $P$ is the midpoint of $BC$.\n(2) Line segment $QR$ is the angle bisector of $\angle BAC$, and line segment $BC$ is the angle bisector of $\angle QPR$.\nProve that $AB + AC = PQ + PR$. (Here $XY$ denotes the length of segment $XY$.)
