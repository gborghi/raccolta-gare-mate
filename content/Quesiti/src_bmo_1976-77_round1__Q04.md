# British Mathematical Olympiad 1976-77 — Round 1 — Quesito 4

*Equation with roots; express result in terms of coefficients*

> The equation $a^4 qx + r = 0$, where $r \ne 0$, has roots $u_1, u_2$. Express the roots of the equation in terms of $u_1, u_2$, and show that if $u_1, u_2$ are real then the equation
> $$x^4 + qx + r = 0$$
> has no root in the interval $-1 < x < 3$.
