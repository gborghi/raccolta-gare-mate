*Count row arrangements of 2010 white and 2010 black stones with an odd number of adjacent white-then-black pairs*

> In how many ways can $2010$ white stones and $2010$ black stones be arranged in a single row such that the following condition holds?\n\nCondition: The number of adjacent pairs (consecutive positions) in which the white stone is immediately to the left of the black stone is odd.
