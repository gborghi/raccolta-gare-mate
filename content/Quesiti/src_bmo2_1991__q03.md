*Triangle with perimeter 2: prove $a^2+b^2+c^2+2abc<2$*

> Prove that if the perimeter of a triangle with sides $a$, $b$, $c$ is $2$ then
> $$a^2 + b^2 + c^2 + 2abc < 2.$$
