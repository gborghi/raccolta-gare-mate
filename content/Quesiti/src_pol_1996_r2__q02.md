*Circle tangent to quadrilateral sides; collinearity of KL and MN intersections*

> A circle with center $O$ is tangent to the sides $AB$, $BC$, $CD$, $DA$ of a convex quadrilateral $ABCD$ at $K$, $L$, $M$, $N$. The lines $KL$ and $MN$ intersect at point $S$. Prove that $BD \perp OS$.
