*5 points in unit rectangle; minimum triangles of area at most 1/4*

> There are 5 points in a rectangle $ABCD$ (including its boundary) with unit area such that any three of them are not collinear. Find the minimum number of triangles with areas no more than $\dfrac{1}{4}$ and vertices chosen from these 5 points. (Posed by Leng Gangsong)
