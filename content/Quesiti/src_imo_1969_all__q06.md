*Prove quadratic-form inequality with equality conditions*

> Prove that for all real numbers $x_1, x_2, y_1, y_2, z_1, z_2$ with $x_1 > 0$, $x_2 > 0$, $x_1 y_1 - z_1^2 > 0$, $x_2 y_2 - z_2^2 > 0$, the following inequality is satisfied:
> $$\frac{8}{(x_1 + x_2)(y_1 + y_2) - (z_1 + z_2)^2} \leq \frac{1}{x_1 y_1 - z_1^2} + \frac{1}{x_2 y_2 - z_2^2}.$$
