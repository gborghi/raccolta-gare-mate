*Find all functions f:R->R satisfying a given functional equation.*

> Find all functions $f : \mathbb{R} \to \mathbb{R}$ that satisfy
> $$(x - y)f(x + y) - (x + y)f(x - y) = 4xy(x^2 - y^2).$$
