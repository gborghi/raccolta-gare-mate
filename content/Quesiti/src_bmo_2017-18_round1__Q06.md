# British Mathematical Olympiad 2017-18 — Round 1 — Quesito 6

*Find all positive integers k such that the k-th Fibonacci number is a perfect square*

> The Fibonacci sequence is defined by $F_1 = F_2 = 1$ and $F_{n+2} = F_{n+1} + F_n$ for each positive integer $n$. Find all positive integers $k$ such that $F_k$ is a perfect square.
