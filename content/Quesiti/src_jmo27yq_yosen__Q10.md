*Length of perpendicular from Q to BC in right triangle*

> Triangle $ABC$ satisfies $\angle B=90^\circ$, $AB=8$, $BC=3$. A point $P$ lies on side $BC$, a point $Q$ on side $CA$, and a point $R$ on side $AB$, satisfying $\angle CRP=\angle CRQ$ and $\angle BPR=\angle CPQ$. Moreover, the perimeter of triangle $PQR$ is $12$. Find the length of the perpendicular dropped from $Q$ to side $BC$. (If the answer contains a radical $\sqrt{XY}$, give the value $XY$ under the radical.)
