*For positive reals a,b,c,d with A=a^3+b^3+c^3+d^3 and B=bcd+cda+dab+abc, prove (a+b+c+d)^3 <= 4A+24B.*

> For positive real numbers $a$, $b$, $c$, $d$, denote $A=a^3+b^3+c^3+d^3$ and $B=bcd+cda+dab+abc$. Prove that
> $$(a+b+c+d)^3\le 4A+24B.$$
