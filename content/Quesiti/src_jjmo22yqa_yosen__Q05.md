*Area of quadrilateral ABCD with BC=1, angle C=90 deg, AC=AD, triangles ABD and BCD similar*

> There is a quadrilateral $ABCD$ satisfying $BC = 1$, $\angle BCD = 90^\circ$, and $AC = AD$. Given that triangles $ABD$ and $BCD$ are similar, find the area of quadrilateral $ABCD$. (Here $XY$ denotes the length of segment $XY$.)

**Topic:** [[topic_geometria_piana|Geometria piana]]

**Abilita:** [[skill_ragionamento_geometrico|Ragionamento geometrico]], [[skill_manipolazione_algebrica|Manipolazione algebrica]]
**Area:** [[Geometria]]
**Risposta:** $\dfrac{5\sqrt{2}}{4}$
