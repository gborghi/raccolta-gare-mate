*Find positive integers n with floor(sqrt(n))=2013; prove perfect square condition*

> a) Find all positive integers $n$ such that $\lfloor \sqrt{n} \rfloor = 2013$.
> 
> b) Let $a$ and $b$ be positive integers such that $\lfloor \sqrt{ab} \rfloor = \lfloor \sqrt{a} \rfloor \cdot \lfloor \sqrt{b} \rfloor$. Prove that at least one of the two integers must be a perfect square.
> 
> (For any real number $x$, the number $\lfloor x \rfloor$ is defined as the greatest integer less than or equal to $x$.)
