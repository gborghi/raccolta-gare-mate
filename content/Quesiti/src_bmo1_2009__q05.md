*Find all sequences satisfying a recurrence with a periodic rational term*

> Determine the sequences $a_1, a_2, a_3, \ldots$ which satisfy all of the following conditions:
> a) $a_{n+1} = 2a_n^2 - 1$ for every integer $n \geq 0$;
> b) $a_0$ is a rational number;
> c) $a_i = a_j$ for some $i \neq j$.
