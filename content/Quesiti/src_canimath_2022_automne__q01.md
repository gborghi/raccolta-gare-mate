*Compute an arithmetic fraction with triple products*

> Compute:
> $$\frac{1 \times 2 \times 4 + 2 \times 4 \times 8 + 3 \times 6 \times 12 + 4 \times 8 \times 16}{1 \times 3 \times 9 + 2 \times 6 \times 18 + 3 \times 9 \times 27 + 4 \times 12 \times 36}$$
> Only a numerical answer is expected. The answer must be given as an irreducible fraction, i.e. as $\frac{a}{b}$ where $a$ and $b$ are positive integers with no common divisor.
