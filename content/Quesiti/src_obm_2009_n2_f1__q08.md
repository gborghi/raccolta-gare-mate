*Chocolate bar divided 2/5, 1/4, remainder 70g; find total weight*

> A chocolate bar is divided among Nelly, Penha, and Sônia. Knowing that Nelly receives $\dfrac{2}{5}$ of the bar, Penha receives $\dfrac{1}{4}$ of the bar, and Sônia receives $70$ grams, the weight of the bar, in grams, is:
> 
> **(A)** $160$ $\quad$ **(B)** $200$ $\quad$ **(C)** $240$ $\quad$ **(D)** $280$ $\quad$ **(E)** $400$
