*Distribute 240 reais equally; reducing by 1 real per nephew allows exactly 2014 divisors*

> Rosa decided to distribute 240 reais to her nephews, giving each the same whole amount. She first thought of reducing it by 1 real per nephew, then she realized that then she could distribute it exactly with 2014 reais as the total. In the end she distributed exactly 240 reais in total. How much did each nephew receive?
> 
> (A) 5 reais $\quad$ (B) 10 reais $\quad$ (C) 12 reais $\quad$ (D) 15 reais $\quad$ (E) 20 reais
