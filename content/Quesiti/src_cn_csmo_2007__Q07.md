*Find all prime triples (a,b,c) with 0<a<b<c<100 and a+1,b+1,c+1 in geometric progression*

> Find the array $(a, b, c)$ satisfying conditions as follows:
> 
> (1) $0 < a < b < c < 100$, where $a$, $b$, $c$ are all prime numbers.
> 
> (2) $a + 1$, $b + 1$, $c + 1$ constitute the geometric progression.
