# Coupe Animath Automne 2019 — Quesito 14

*Ball-splitting game: prove Paul wins for N=2019 and loses for N=2020*

> Paul plays a game with $N$ balls and a bag; the balls form a single pile. At each turn, Paul chooses a pile that has at least 2 balls, removes one ball and places it in his bag, and then splits the remaining balls of that pile into two non-empty piles. The game stops when all piles consist of exactly 3 balls, and Paul loses if at any point he can no longer make a legal move. Paul wins if he reaches a state where all piles consist of exactly 3 balls.
> 
> a) Show that if $N = 2019$, Paul can win.
> 
> b) Show that if $N = 2020$, Paul cannot win.
