# Indian National Mathematical Olympiad 2007 — Quesito 5

*Isosceles triangle with ratio conditions; prove polynomial and equilateral iff*

> Let $ABC$ be a triangle in which $AB = AC$. Let $D$ be the mid-point of $BC$ and $P$ be a point on $AD$. Suppose $E$ is the foot of perpendicular from $P$ on $AC$. If
> $$\frac{AP}{PD} = \frac{BP}{PE} = \lambda, \quad \frac{BD}{AD} = m,$$
> and $z = m^2(1 + \lambda)$, prove that
> $$z^2 - (\lambda^3 - \lambda^2 - 2)z + 1 = 0.$$
> Hence show that $\lambda \ge 2$ and $\lambda = 2$ if and only if $ABC$ is equilateral.
