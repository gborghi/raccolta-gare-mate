*Prove an inequality between two rational functions of x and y*

> Prove that if $n$ is an integer greater than $1$ and $x > y > 1$, then
> $$\frac{x^{n+1}-1}{x(x^{n-1}-1)} > \frac{y^{n+1}-1}{y(y^{n-1}-1)}.$$
