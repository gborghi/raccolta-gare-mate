# Indian National Mathematical Olympiad 1990 — Quesito 6

*In scalene triangle with obtuse A, find locus of D on line BC with AD=sqrt(BD·CD)*

> In a scalene triangle $ABC$ the angle at $A$ is obtuse. Determine the set of points $D$ lying on the extended line $BC$ for which $AD = \sqrt{BD \cdot CD}$.
