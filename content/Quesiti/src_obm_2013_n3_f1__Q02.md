*Buying a computer today vs after a 5% price rise and a 5% discount*

> If Joana buys a computer today whose price is $2000$ reais, she will get a discount of $5\%$. If she waits until tomorrow she will get the same discount of $5\%$, but the price of the computer will have increased by $5\%$. So, if she waits, what will happen?
> 
> (A) Nothing, since she will pay the same amount. \quad (B) She will lose $100$ reais. \quad (C) She will gain $105$ reais. \quad (D) She will lose $95$ reais. \quad (E) She will lose $105$ reais.
