*Count pairs (a,b) for intersection of natural number sets*

> Suppose $A = \{x \mid 5x - a \le 0\}$, $B = \{x \mid 6x - b > 0\}$, $a, b \in \mathbf{N}$, and $A \cap B \cap \mathbf{N} = \{2, 3, 4\}$. The number of such pairs $(a, b)$ is (\ \ ).
> 
> (A) 20\quad (B) 25\quad (C) 30\quad (D) 42
