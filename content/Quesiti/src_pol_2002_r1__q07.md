*In a group of n>=3 people where each has an even number of acquaintances, prove three members have the same number of acquaintances.*

> In a group of $n \ge 3$ people each member of the group has an even number (possibly zero) of acquaintances in the group. Prove that there exist three members of the group which has the same number of acquaintances in this group.
> 
> \textit{Remark.} Assume that nobody includes himself into the set of his acquaintances and that $A$ knows $B$ if and only if $B$ knows $A$.
