*Sum inequality for set of distinct positive odd integers with distinct differences*

> $S$ is a set of distinct positive odd integers $\{a_i\}$, $i = 1$ to $n$. No two differences $|a_i - a_j|$ are equal, $1 \le i < j \le n$. Prove that $$\sum_{i=1}^{n} a_i \ge \frac{1}{3}n(n^2+2).$$
