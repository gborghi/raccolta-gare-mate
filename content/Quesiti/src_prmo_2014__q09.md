*Sum of all possible values of q given conditions on roots of two quadratics*

> Natural numbers $p, q, r$ are such that $a$ and $b$ are roots of $x^2 - px + 1 = 0$ then $a + \frac{1}{b}$ and $b + \frac{1}{a}$ are roots of $x^2 - rx + q = 0$. What is the sum of all possible values of $q$?
