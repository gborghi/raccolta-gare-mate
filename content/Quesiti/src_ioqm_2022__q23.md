*Triangle ABC, median AD divides angle BAC in ratio 1:2, EB perp AB, BE=3, BA=4, find nearest integer to BC*

> In a triangle $ABC$, the median $AD$ divides $\angle BAC$ in the ratio $1 : 2$. Extend $AD$ to $E$ such that $EB$ is perpendicular to $AB$. Given that $BE = 3$, $BA = 4$, find the integer nearest to $BC$.
