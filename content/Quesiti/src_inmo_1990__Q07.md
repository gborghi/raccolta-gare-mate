*Locus of P such that triangle DEF (feet of perps) is isosceles; when equilateral?*

> For any point $P$ lying within a given acute-angled triangle $ABC$, let $D, E, F$ denote the feet of the perpendiculars from $P$ onto $AB$, $BC$, $CA$ respectively. Find the set of all positions of $P$ for which the triangle $DEF$ is isosceles. For which position of $P$ is the triangle $DEF$ equilateral?
