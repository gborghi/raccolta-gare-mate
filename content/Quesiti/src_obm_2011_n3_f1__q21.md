*Area of triangle AMN in a triangle ABC of area 60 with cevians and midpoint*

> Let $ABC$ be a triangle. Point $D$ belongs to side $AC$ and is such that $BD = CD$. Let $E$ be the point where $BC$ meets ... and let $M$ be the midpoint of $AB$ and $N$ the intersection of $AM$ and $BD$. If the area of $AM$... is the area of $ABC$ ... what, in degrees, is the angle $\widehat{BCA}$?
> 
> (A) 15 (B) 22,5 (C) 30 (D) 37,5 (E) 45
