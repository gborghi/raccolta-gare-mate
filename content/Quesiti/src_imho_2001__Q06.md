*Integers a>b>c>d>0 with a product condition; ab+cd is not prime*

> Let $a$, $b$, $c$, $d$ be integers with $a > b > c > d > 0$. Suppose that
> $$ac + bd = (b + d + a - c)(b + d - a + c).$$
> Prove that $ab + cd$ is not prime.
