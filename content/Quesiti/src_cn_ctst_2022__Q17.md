*Subset of {1,…,n} with no two elements differing by a perfect square: prove size ≥ Cn^α, α>1/2*

> Prove that there exist constants $C > 0$ and $\alpha > \dfrac{1}{2}$ such that for any positive integer $n$, there exists a subset $A \subseteq \{1, 2, \ldots, n\}$ with $|A| \ge C n^{\alpha}$, such that the difference of any two distinct elements of $A$ is not a perfect square.
