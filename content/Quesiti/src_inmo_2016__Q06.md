# Indian National Mathematical Olympiad 2016 — Quesito 6

*Non-constant AP with certain powers also in AP implies all terms are integers*

> Consider a non-constant arithmetic progression $a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_n, \ldots$. Suppose there exist relatively prime positive integers $p > 1$ and $q > 1$ such that $a_1^p$, $a_{q+1}^p$ and $a_{q^2+1}^p$ are also the terms of the same arithmetic progression. Prove that the terms of the arithmetic progression are all integers.
