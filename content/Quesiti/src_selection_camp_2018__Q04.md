# Japan IMO Selection Camp 2018 — Selection Camp — Quesito 4

*Minimum total separation number over all lines through n points in general position*

> Let $n \ge 3$ be an integer. Consider a set $S$ of $n$ points in the plane such that no three points are collinear. Let $L$ be the set of all lines passing through two distinct points of $S$. For a line $\ell \in L$, define its separation number as the product of the number of points of $S$ in each of the two open half-planes determined by $\ell$. Find the minimum possible value of the sum of the separation numbers over all lines in $L$.
