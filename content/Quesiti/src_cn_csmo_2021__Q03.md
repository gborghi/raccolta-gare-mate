*Integer sequence with prime-weighted binomial sums; prove all terms zero*

> Given an odd prime $p$ and a sequence of integers $\{a_n\}$, $n \ge 0$. Define the sequence
> $$v_n = \sum_{i=0}^n \binom{n}{i} p^i a_i, \quad n \ge 1.$$
> Prove: if there are infinitely many values of $n$ such that $v_n = 0$, then $v_n = 0$ for all $n \ge 0$.
