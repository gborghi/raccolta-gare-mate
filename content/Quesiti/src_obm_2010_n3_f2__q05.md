*Compute telescoping product of (n^4+n^2+1) for even over odd indices*

> Compute
> $$\frac{(2^4+2^2+1)(4^4+4^2+1)(6^4+6^2+1)\cdots(32^4+32^2+1)}{(1^4+1^2+1)(3^4+3^2+1)(5^4+5^2+1)\cdots(31^4+31^2+1)}.$$
