# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2003 — Nível 1 — Fase 1 — Quesito 14

*Maximum congruent squares removed from 5×5 grid via row, column, and diagonal*

> A $5 \times 5$ square is cut into five rows of 5 unit squares each. All the unit squares are congruent. From each diagonal (of any unit square) and from one row and one column, the marked unit squares are removed. The area covered by the remaining unit squares is at most:
> 
> (A) $\dfrac{2}{5}$ \quad (B) $\dfrac{11}{25}$ \quad (C) $\dfrac{12}{25}$ \quad (D) $\dfrac{13}{25}$ \quad (E) $\dfrac{3}{5}$
