*Incircle tangent construction: triangle AB'C' similar to ABC*

> The incircle $l$ of a triangle $ABC$ is centered at $I$ and touches the side $BC$ at $T$. The line through $T$ parallel to $IA$ meets the incircle again at $S$ and the tangent to the incircle at $S$ meets $AB$, $AC$ at points $C'$, $B'$, respectively. Prove that the triangle $AB'C'$ is similar to the triangle $ABC$.
