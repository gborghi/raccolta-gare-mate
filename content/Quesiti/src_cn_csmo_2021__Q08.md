*Find all integer pairs (a,b)>1 satisfying equation involving Euler's totient*

> Find all positive integer pairs $(a, b)$ with $a, b > 1$, such that
> $$14\varphi^2(a) - \varphi(ab) + 22\varphi^2(b) = a^2 + b^2,$$
> where $\varphi(n)$ represents the number of positive integers less than $n$ and relatively prime to $n$.
