*Integer r,s; quadratic with integer roots; count possible values of |r−s|*

> Let $r$ and $s$ be integers. It is known that the quadratic equation
> $$x^2 - (r + s)x + rs + 2010 = 0$$
> has two integer solutions. How many are the possible values of $|r - s|$?
