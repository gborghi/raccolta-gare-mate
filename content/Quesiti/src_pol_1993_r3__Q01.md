*Find all rational solutions to a symmetric system in five unknowns.*

> Find all rational solutions $x, y, z, t, w$ to the following system:
> $$2xy = t^2 - w^2 + z^2,$$
> $$2xz = t^2 - y^2 + w^2,$$
> $$2yz = t^2 - w^2 + x^2.$$
