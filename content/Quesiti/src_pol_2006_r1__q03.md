*Acute triangle inscribed in circle center O; D projection of C onto AB, E,F projections of D onto AC,BC; prove area EOFC equals half area ABC.*

> An acute-angled triangle $ABC$ is inscribed in a circle with center $O$. Point $D$ is the projection of $C$ onto $AB$, and points $E$ and $F$ are the projections of the point $D$ onto $AC$ and $BC$, respectively. Prove that the area of quadrilateral $EOFC$ equals half the area of triangle $ABC$.
