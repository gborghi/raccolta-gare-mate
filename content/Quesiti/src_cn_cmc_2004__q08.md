*Find f(x) from functional equation f(x)-f(y)=f(x-y)+2*

> Let $f: \mathbf{R} \to \mathbf{R}$ be a function such that $f(0) = 1$ and for any $x$, $y \in \mathbf{R}$,
> $$f(x) - f(y) = f(x - y) + 2$$
> holds. Then $f(x) = $ ___.
