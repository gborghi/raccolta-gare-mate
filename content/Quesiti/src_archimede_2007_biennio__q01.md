*Time to earn 1000 Euro with annual salary*

>
> - **(A)** less than half an hour,
> - **(B)** between half an hour and one hour,
> - **(C)** between one and two hours,
> - **(D)** between two and four hours,
> - **(E)** greater than four hours.

**Answer:** C
