*Copeiro numbers: sum of squares of digits equals a power of 4*

> A positive integer is called *copeiro* if the sum of the squares of its digits in decimal representation is equal to a power of $4$. For example, $32111$ is a copeiro number of 5 digits, since $3^2 + 2^2 + 1^2 + 1^2 + 1^2 = 16 = 2^4$.
> 
> (a) Is there a 3-digit number such that the sum of the squares of its digits equals $4^4$? Do not forget that you must justify your answer.
> 
> (b) What are the two largest 3-digit copeiro numbers?
