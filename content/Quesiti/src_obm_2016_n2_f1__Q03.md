# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2016 — Nível 2 — Fase 1 — Quesito 3

*Households not receiving a newspaper, two-digit odd-digit codes*

> There are $5$ odd digits: $1$, $3$, $5$, $7$, $9$. Therefore there are $5\cdot 5=25$ two-digit numbers using only these digits. Consequently at most $25-18=7$ households did not receive the newspaper. How many households did not receive the newspaper?
