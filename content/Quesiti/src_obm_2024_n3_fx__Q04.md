*Three distinct reals satisfying cyclic system; prove all lie in (-1,1)*

> Let $x, y, z$ be three distinct real numbers such that
> $$\begin{cases} x^2 - x = yz \\ y^2 - y = zx \\ z^2 - z = xy \end{cases}$$
> Prove that $-1 < x, y, z < 1$.
