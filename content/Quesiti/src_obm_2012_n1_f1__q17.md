*Rows of strawberries and jabuticabas eaten alternately (≤5 each)*

> Rosinha picked several strawberries and jabuticabas, at most 5 of each kind. She wants to eat 5 of each kind, eating them in alternating runs, starting with strawberries and ending with jabuticabas (for example: 3 strawberries, then 2 jabuticabas, then 2 strawberries, then 3 jabuticabas). She forms a row with the fruits before eating them in order. How many different such rows can she form?
> 
> (A) 13 \quad (B) 14 \quad (C) 15 \quad (D) 16 \quad (E) 20
