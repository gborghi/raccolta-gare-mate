# Japan Junior Mathematical Olympiad 2003 — Quesito 6

*Find area of triangle DBC given incircle-related point and distances on cevian*

> Let triangle $ABC$ satisfy $AB = AC = 5$ and $BC = 6$. Inside the triangle, take a point $D$ on the incircle of triangle $ABC$. Let segment $AD$ meet side $AB$ at $E$ and side $AC$ at $F$ (so $E$ lies on $AB$ and $F$ lies on $AC$, with $D$ between $E$ and $F$). Given $DE = 1$ and $DF = 2$, find the area of triangle $DBC$.
