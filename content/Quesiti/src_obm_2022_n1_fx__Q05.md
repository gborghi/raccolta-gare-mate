# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2022 — Nível 1 — Fase x — Quesito 5

*2022-partido numbers: split into halves whose product equals original*

> A positive integer $N$ is called *2022-partido* if it has the following properties:
> 
> - It has $2k$ digits for some positive integer $k$.
> - Letting $X$ be the number formed by the first $k$ digits of $N$ and $Y$ the number formed by the last $k$ digits of $N$ in the order they appear in $N$, we have $N = 20 \cdot X + 22 \cdot Y$.
> 
> For example, $2022$ and $101045$ are not 2022-partido numbers, since $20 \cdot 20 + 22 \cdot 22 = 884 \neq 2022$ and $20 \cdot 101 + 22 \cdot 45 = 3010 \neq 101045$.
> 
> (a) Determine all 2022-partido numbers of 2 digits.
> 
> (b) Determine all 2022-partido numbers of 4 digits.
> 
> (c) Determine all 2022-partido numbers of 6 or more digits.
