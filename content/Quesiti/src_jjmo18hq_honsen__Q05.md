# Japan Junior Mathematical Olympiad 2020 — Honsen (Final) — Quesito 5

*Regular 2020-gon with distinct labels; show some n satisfies inequality*

> There is a regular $2020$-gon, and the vertices $1, 2, \ldots, 2020$ are arranged clockwise. Moreover, separately from the vertex numbers, a distinct positive integer is assigned to each vertex (so that no two vertices receive the same integer). Show that there exists an integer $n$ with $1 \le n \le 2020$ satisfying the following condition:
> 
> If the integers assigned to vertices $n$, $n+1$, $n+2$ are $a$, $b$, $c$ respectively, then $a^2 + b^2 \ge c^2 + n^2 + 3$ holds.
> 
> Here, vertex $2021$ and vertex $2022$ refer to vertex $1$ and vertex $2$ respectively.
