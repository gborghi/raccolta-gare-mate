*Count card multisets with sum 2007 allowing unique n-subset with sum divisible by n*

> There are some cards, each with a positive integer written on it, and the sum of all the written numbers is $2007$. For every integer $n$ with $1 \le n \le 2006$, one can choose $n$ cards such that the sum of the numbers on the chosen cards is divisible by $n$, and moreover this choice is unique (where cards with the same number written on them are treated as indistinguishable). How many such collections of cards are possible?
