*Determine rational periodic sequences satisfying a recurrence*

> Determine the sequences $a_0, a_1, a_2, \ldots$ which satisfy all of the following conditions:
> a) $a_{n+1} = 2a_n^2 - 1$ for every integer $n \ge 0$,
> b) $a_0$ is a rational number and
> c) $a_i = a_j$ for some $i, j$ with $i \neq j$.
