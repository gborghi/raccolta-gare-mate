*Ant walking around a pentagon; position at the 2013th second*

> In the pentagon $ABCDE$ alongside, $AB = BC = CD = 2$ meters and $DE = EA = 3$ meters. An ant starts from vertex $A$ and walks at constant velocity, one meter per second, along its sides, always in the same direction. At which point will it be at the $2013$th second?
> 
> (A) $A$ \quad (B) $B$ \quad (C) $C$ \quad (D) $D$ \quad (E) $E$
