# British Mathematical Olympiad 1983-84 — Round 1 — Quesito 4

*Count solutions of floor-equation in interval [1, N]*

> Let $N$ be a positive integer. Determine with proof the number of solutions of the equation
> $$x^2 - [x^2] = (x - [x])^2$$
> lying in the interval $1 \le x \le N$.
> 
> (For a real number $x$ the "integer part" $[x]$ is the largest integer which is $\le x$.)
