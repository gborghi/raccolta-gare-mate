# British Mathematical Olympiad 1979-80 — Round 1 — Quesito 5

*Party of 10: any 3 contain 2 strangers; prove 4 mutual strangers exist*

> In a party of ten persons, among any three persons there are at least two who do not know each other. Prove that at the party there are four persons none of whom knows another of the four.
