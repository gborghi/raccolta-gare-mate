# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2016 — Nível 2 — Fase 3 — Quesito 2

*Sequence of squares, each new square built on the diagonal of the previous larger one*

> Janaina draws a sequence of figures as shown below. Each figure has one square more than the previous figure, and each added square has side equal to the diagonal of the larger square of the previous figure. Moreover, all the squares of each figure share a common vertex. The square of figure $1$ has area $2\ \mathrm{cm}^2$.
> 
> a) What is the area of the larger square of figure $2$?
> 
> b) What is the total area of figure $2$?
> 
> c) What is the total area of figure $6$?
