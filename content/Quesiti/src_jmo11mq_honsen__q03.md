*For non-negative reals with a≥b≥c≥0, prove (a+b+c)(a³+b³+c³)≤4(a⁶+b⁶+c⁶); find equality cases*

> Let $a, b, c$ be non-negative real numbers with $a \ge b \ge c \ge 0$. Prove that $$(a + b + c)(a^3 + b^3 + c^3) \le 4(a^6 + b^6 + c^6),$$ and find all conditions under which equality holds.
