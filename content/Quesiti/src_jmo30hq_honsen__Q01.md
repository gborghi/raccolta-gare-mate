# Japan Mathematical Olympiad 2020 — Honsen (Final) — Quesito 1

*Find all positive integer pairs making two expressions integers*

> Find all pairs of positive integers $(m, n)$ such that both
> $$\frac{n^2+1}{2m}$$
> and
> $$\sqrt{2^{n-1}+m+4}$$
> are integers.
