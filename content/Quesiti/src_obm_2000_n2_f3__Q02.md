# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2000 — Nível 2 — Fase 3 — Quesito 2

*Two decks of 50 double-sided cards each showing 1-100; show all 100 cards can be placed so the up faces show every number from 1 to 100.*

> Isabel has two decks, each with $50$ cards. On each of the decks are written the numbers from $1$ to $100$ (on each card two numbers are written, one on each face of the card). Due to a manufacturing defect, the distribution of the numbers on the cards is not the same in the two decks (for example, in one of the decks the $1$ appears on the same card as the $2$; in the other, the $1$ appears with the $76$). Show how Isabel should proceed so that, when placing the $100$ cards on a table, the faces turned upward show all the numbers from $1$ to $100$.
