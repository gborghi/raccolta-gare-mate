*Find angle BAC from a perpendicular bisector condition*

> Let $O$ be the circumcenter of triangle $ABC$. Let $\ell$ be the line passing through the midpoint of side $BC$ and perpendicular to the bisector of $\angle BAC$. When $\ell$ passes through the midpoint of segment $AO$, find the measure of $\angle BAC$.
