*Functional equation roots condition*

> Let $f(x) = x^2 + ax + b$. If for all nonzero real $x$
> $$f\!\left(x + \frac{1}{x}\right) = f(x) + f\!\left(\frac{1}{x}\right)$$
> and the roots of $f(x) = 0$ are integers, what is the value of $a^2 + b^2$?
