*Find range of b and value of a given a minimum of a rational function equals 3*

> If $\displaystyle\min_{x\in\mathbf{R}} \frac{ax^2+b}{\sqrt{x^2+1}} = 3$, find
> 
> (1) the range of $b$;
> 
> (2) the value of $a$ for given $b$. (posed by Lu Xingjiang)
