*Minimum number of banknotes exchanged so three girls end with equal amounts of money*

> Esmeralda has $11$ banknotes of two reais, Rosa has $7$ banknotes of five reais and Nelly has $3$ banknotes of ten reais. What is the smallest possible number of banknotes that the three girls must exchange among themselves so that each one ends with the same amount of money?
> 
> (A) 5 (B) 6 (C) 7 (D) 8 (E) 9
