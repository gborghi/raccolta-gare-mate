*All positive integer triples (l,m,n) satisfying two simultaneous equations involving 2020*

> Find all triples of positive integers $(l, m, n)$ with $1 \le m \le n$ satisfying
> $$l^2 + mn = m^2 + ln \quad \text{and} \quad n^2 + lm = 2020.$$
