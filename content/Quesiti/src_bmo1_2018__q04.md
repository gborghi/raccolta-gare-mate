*Positive real sequence with recurrence; find possible values of a_2017*

> Consider sequences $a_1, a_2, a_3, \ldots$ of positive real numbers such that
> $$a_{n+1} + a_n = (a_{n+1} - a_n)^2$$
> for each positive integer $n$. What are the possible values that $a_{2017}$ can take?
