*Parabola y=x^2 reflected in line y=x+2; find equation of reflected parabola.*

> The parabola with equation $y = x^2$ is reflected in the line with equation $y = x + 2$.
> 
> Which of the following is the equation of the reflected parabola?
>
> - **(A)** $x = y^2 + 4y + 2$
> - **(B)** $x = y^2 + 4y - 2$
> - **(C)** $x = y^2 - 4y + 2$
> - **(D)** $x = y^2 - 4y - 2$
> - **(E)** $x = y^2 + 2$

**Risposta:** (C)
