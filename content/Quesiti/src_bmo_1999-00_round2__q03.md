*Find positive integers p,q satisfying a given floor/radical equation*

> Find positive integers $p$ and $q$ such that
> $$p + q = \lfloor \sqrt{p} \rfloor + \lfloor \sqrt{q} \rfloor + \lfloor \sqrt{p+q} \rfloor.$$
