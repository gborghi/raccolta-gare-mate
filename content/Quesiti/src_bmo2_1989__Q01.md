*Find smallest positive integer a for quadratic with two roots in (0,1)*

> Find the smallest positive integer $a$ with the property:
> 
> There exist integers $b, c$ such that the equation
> $$ax^2 - bx + c = 0$$
> has two distinct roots in the interval $0 < x < 1$.
