# Japan IMO Selection Camp 2017 — Selection Camp — Quesito 4

*Isosceles triangle, incenter, reflection: lies on circumcircle of BDE*

> Let triangle $ABC$ satisfy $AB = AC \neq BC$, and let $I$ be its incenter. Line $BI$ meets side $AC$ at $D$. The line through $D$ perpendicular to $AC$ meets line $AI$ at $E$. Prove that the reflection of $I$ over line $AC$ lies on the circumcircle of triangle $BDE$.
> 
> (Here $XY$ denotes the length of segment $XY$.)
