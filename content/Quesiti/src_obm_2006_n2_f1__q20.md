*Spreadsheet computes running mean; find last note Piralão entered*

> Professor Piralão applied a test to his five students and, after correcting them, entered the grades into an electronic spreadsheet that automatically calculates the running average after each grade is entered. The grades of the five students are, in increasing order: $71, 76, 80, 82, 91$. It was noted that after each entry the running average was always an integer. The last grade that Piralão entered was:
> 
> **A)** 71 \quad **B)** 76 \quad **C)** 80 \quad **D)** 82 \quad **E)** 91
