*Count binary sequences with exactly m occurrences of 01*

> Prove that the number of sequences $a_1 a_2 \ldots a_n$ with each of their $n$ terms $a_i = 0$ or $1$ and containing exactly $m$ occurrences of $01$ is $\dbinom{n+1}{2m+1}$.
