# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2011 — Nível 2 — Fase 1 — Quesito 17

*Angle BAC given D,E on BC with BD=BA, CE=CA and angle DAE=40*

> In the triangle $ABC$, the points $D$ and $E$ belong to side $BC$ and are such that $BD = BA$ and $CE = CA$. Given that $m(D\hat{A}E) = 40^\circ$, how much, in degrees, does angle $B\hat{A}C$ measure?
> 
> (A) $80$ \quad (B) $90$ \quad (C) $100$ \quad (D) $110$ \quad (E) $120$
