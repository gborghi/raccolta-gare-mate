*Find all positive integers n such that for all reals x_i,y_i the product inequality with sums of squares under square roots holds.*

> Find all positive integers $n$ such that for all real numbers $x_1,x_2,\ldots,x_n,y_1,y_2,\ldots,y_n$ the following inequality
> $$x_1x_2\ldots x_n + y_1y_2\ldots y_n \le \sqrt{x_1^2+y_1^2}\cdot\sqrt{x_2^2+y_2^2}\cdot\ldots\cdot\sqrt{x_n^2+y_n^2}$$
> holds.
