*Find minimum total size of n sets with symmetric-difference condition*

> Given an integer $n \geq 2$, suppose $A_1, A_2, \ldots, A_n$ are $n$ nonempty finite sets satisfying $|A_i \Delta A_j| = |i - j|$ for all $i, j \in \{1, 2, \ldots, n\}$.
> 
> Find the minimum value of $|A_1| + |A_2| + \cdots + |A_n|$.
> 
> (Here $|X|$ denotes the number of elements of a finite set $X$ and $X \Delta Y = \{a \mid a \in X,\, a \notin Y\} \cup \{a \mid a \in Y,\, a \notin X\}$ for any sets $X$ and $Y$.)
