*Common real roots of two polynomial equations*

> Find all real numbers $x$ that satisfy both of the following equations simultaneously:
> $$x^5 + 2x^4 - x^3 - 5x^2 - 10x + 5 = 0$$
> $$x^6 + 4x^5 + 3x^4 - 6x^3 - 20x^2 - 15x + 5 = 0$$
