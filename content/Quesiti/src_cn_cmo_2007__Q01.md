*Prove max of two complex moduli is at least mn/sqrt(m^2+n^2)*

> Given complex numbers $a$, $b$, $c$, let $|a + b| = m$, $|a - b| = n$, and suppose $mn \neq 0$. Prove that
> $$\max\{|ac + b|,\ |a + bc|\} \ge \frac{mn}{\sqrt{m^2 + n^2}}.$$
