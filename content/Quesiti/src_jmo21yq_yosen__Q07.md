*Count 3×3 grids with integers 1–9 placed once each satisfying a marking condition*

> A $3 \times 3$ grid is filled with the integers 1 through 9, each appearing exactly once (one per cell). For each column, the second-largest of the 3 numbers written in that column is marked. It turned out that the second-largest of the three marked numbers was 5. In this case, how many arrangements of the 9 integers are possible?
