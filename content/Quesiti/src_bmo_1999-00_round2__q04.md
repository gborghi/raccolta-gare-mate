*Find a 10-element set where no 6 elements sum divisible by 6; what about 11?*

> (a) Find a set $A$ of ten positive integers such that no six members of $A$ have a sum which is divisible by $6$.
> (b) Is it possible to find such a set if "ten" is replaced by "eleven"?
