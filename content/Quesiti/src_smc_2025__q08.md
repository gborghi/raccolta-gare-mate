*Area of shape formed by three arcs of circles of radius 1; P is centre of arc BQ and Q is centre of arc PR.*

> The diagram shows three arcs of circles of radius 1. $P$ is the centre of the circle of which $BQ$ is an arc, and $Q$ is the centre of the circle of which $PR$ is an arc. What is the area of this shape?
>
> - **(A)** \frac{\sqrt{3}\pi}{10}
> - **(B)** \frac{\pi}{6}
> - **(C)** \frac{\sqrt{3}\pi}{8}
> - **(D)** \frac{\sqrt{3}}{4}
> - **(E)** \frac{\sqrt{2}\pi}{9}
