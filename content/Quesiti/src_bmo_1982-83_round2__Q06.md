# British Mathematical Olympiad 1982-83 — Round 2 — Quesito 6

*Find all positive integer pairs (n,k) with (n+1)^k = n! + 1*

> $n$ and $k$ are positive integers. Find all pairs $(n, k)$ satisfying
> $$(n+1)^k = n! + 1,$$
> proving that you have the complete set of solutions.
