*Regular hexagon PQRSTU inscribed in circle of radius 5; X on circumference; find XP^2 + XQ^2 + XR^2 + XS^2 + XT^2 + XU^2.*

> A regular hexagon $PQRSTU$ is inscribed in a circle of radius 5. A point $X$ is on the circumference of the circle and is connected to the vertices of the hexagon to form six chords $XP$, $XQ$, $XR$, $XS$, $XT$ and $XU$. What is the value of $XP^2 + XQ^2 + XR^2 + XS^2 + XT^2 + XU^2$?
>
> - **(A)** 150
> - **(B)** 216
> - **(C)** 256
> - **(D)** 300
> - **(E)** 360
