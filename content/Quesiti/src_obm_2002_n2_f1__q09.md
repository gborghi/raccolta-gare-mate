*Patrícia drives São Paulo–Rio; computes savings per person vs plane ticket*

> Patrícia lives in São Paulo and wants to visit Rio de Janeiro during a long holiday weekend. A round-trip plane ticket costs R$\$580.00$, but Patrícia prefers to travel by car, which gets on average $12$ km per litre of fuel. The fuel price is R$\$1.60$ per litre, and Patrícia estimates R$\$48.00$ in road tolls for the round trip (São Paulo–Rio is approximately $450$ km one way). She intends to share all expenses equally with two friends. By taking her car instead of the plane, each person will:
>
> - **(A)** save R$\$20.00$.
> - **(B)** spend R$\$2.00$ more.
> - **(C)** save R$\$24.00$.
> - **(D)** spend the same as the bus option.
> - **(E)** save R$\$14.00$.
