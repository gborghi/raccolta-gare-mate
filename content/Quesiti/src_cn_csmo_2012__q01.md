*Find triple (l,m,n) with partial sums forming geometric sequence*

> Find a triple $(l, m, n)$ $(1 < l < m < n)$ of positive integers such that $\sum_{k=1}^{l} k$, $\sum_{k=l+1}^{m} k$, $\sum_{k=m+1}^{n} k$ form a geometric sequence in order. (posed by Tao Pingsheng)
