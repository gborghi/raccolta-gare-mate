# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2009 — Nível 2 — Fase 3 — Quesito 1

*Count positive 4-digit integers that are 'abestado'*

> We say that a positive integer $n$ is *abestado* ("beasted") if reading its digits from right to left gives a number greater than $n$. For example, $2009$ is abestado because $9002 > 2009$; on the other hand, $2010$ is not abestado because reading $2010$ from right to left gives $0102$, which equals $102$, which is less than $2010$; and $3443$ is not abestado because reading it from right to left gives $3443$, which is exactly equal to the original. How many positive integers with exactly four digits are abestado?
