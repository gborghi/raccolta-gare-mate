*Number of equal candies divisible among groups of 2, 3 or 4*

> Since the candies are equal and can be divided into groups of $2$, $3$ or $4$ people, the number of candies must be a multiple of the least common multiple of $2$, $3$ and $4$, namely $12$. Among the options only one is a multiple of $12$ (with $30$ pieces such a division is verified to be possible). How many candies are there?
