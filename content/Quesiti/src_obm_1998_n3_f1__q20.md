*Place two A's, two B's and two C's in a 2x3 table, one per cell, so that no column contains two equal letters; count the arrangements.*

> \begin{tikzpicture}\draw (0,0) grid (3,2);\end{tikzpicture}
>

> 
> B) $24$
> 
> C) $36$
> 
> D) $48$
