*Maximum k for valid coloring of 100 circle points paired as same-color non-crossing segments*

> (Test Paper A1, Problem 4) Given 100 different points on a circle, try to determine the maximum positive integer $k$ satisfying: colour any $k$ points of the 100 points arbitrarily red or blue, and the remaining points can be appropriately coloured red or blue, so that the 100 points can be used as endpoints to make 50 segments, any two segments have no common point, and the endpoints of each segment are of the same colour.
