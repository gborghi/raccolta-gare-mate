*Area of rectangle divided into 10 squares with smallest integer sides*

> The rectangle in the figure below is divided into 10 squares. The measures of the sides of all the squares are positive integers and are the smallest possible values. The area of this rectangle is:
> 
> (A) 180 \quad (B) 240 \quad (C) 300 \quad (D) 360 \quad (E) 450
