*If x+y, x^2+y^2, x^3+y^3, x^4+y^4 are integers, prove x^n+y^n is an integer for all n.*

> Suppose that $x, y$ are real numbers such that $x + y$, $x^2 + y^2$, $x^3 + y^3$, and $x^4 + y^4$ are integers. Prove that $x^n + y^n$ is an integer for all $n \in \mathbb{N}$.
