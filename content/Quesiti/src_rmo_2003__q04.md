*Count ordered triples of nonneg integers with x≤y≤z, x+y+z≤100*

> Find the number of ordered triples $(x, y, z)$ of nonnegative integers satisfying the conditions:
> (i) $x \le y \le z$;
> (ii) $x + y + z \le 100$.
