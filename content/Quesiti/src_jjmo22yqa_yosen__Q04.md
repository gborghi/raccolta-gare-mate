# Japan Junior Mathematical Olympiad 2024 — Yosen (Preliminary) — Quesito 4

*Find the smallest positive rational with finite decimal expansion whose integer and fractional parts have product 42*

> A positive rational number $x$ can be expressed as a terminating decimal, and the product of its integer part and its fractional part equals $42$. Find the smallest such $x$.
> 
> (A **terminating decimal** is a decimal with finitely many non-zero digits after the decimal point, such as $2.024$ or $5$. For a positive real number $r$, the **integer part** of $r$ is the largest integer not exceeding $r$, and the **fractional part** of $r$ is $r$ minus its integer part. For example, the integer part of $2.024$ is $2$, the fractional part is $0.024$; the integer part of $5$ is $5$, the fractional part is $0$.)
