*Find all real functions satisfying f(yf(x)-x)=f(x)f(y)+2x*

> Find all functions $f : \mathbb{R} \to \mathbb{R}$ defined on all real numbers such that for all real numbers $x, y$,
> $$f(yf(x) - x) = f(x)f(y) + 2x$$
> holds.
