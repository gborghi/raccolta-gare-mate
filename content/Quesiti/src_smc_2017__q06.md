*Circles in a diagram connected by line segments; find minimum number of colours needed so no two adjacent circles share a colour.*

> The circles in the diagram are to be coloured so that any two circles connected by a line segment have different colours.
> 
> What is the smallest number of colours required?
>
> - **(A)** $2$
> - **(B)** $3$
> - **(C)** $4$
> - **(D)** $5$
> - **(E)** $6$
