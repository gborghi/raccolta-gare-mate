*Count painted squares in grid pattern; find grid with 199 painted squares*

> Some squares of a grid with the same number of rows (horizontal lines) and columns (vertical lines) are painted black, following the pattern shown in the figure on page 1. The pattern is illustrated for a grid with 14 columns and 14 rows.
> 
> a) How many squares are painted in a $14 \times 14$ grid (with 14 rows and 14 columns)?
> 
> b) How many rows does a grid have if it contains 199 painted squares?
> 
> (The pattern consists of a staircase-shaped border along the top and right side of the grid, as shown in the accompanying figure.)
