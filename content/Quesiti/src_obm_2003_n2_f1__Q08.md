*Find x: multiply by 2, add 1, multiply by 3, subtract 5 gives 220*

> 
> (A) an odd number.
> (B) an even number.
> (C) a number between 40 and 50.
> (D) a multiple of 5.
> (E) a number whose digit sum is 9.
