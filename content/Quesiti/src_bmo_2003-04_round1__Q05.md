# British Mathematical Olympiad 2003-04 — Round 1 — Quesito 5

*Primes p,q,r with q|p-1, r|p-1; find all values of pq*

> Let $p$, $q$ and $r$ be prime numbers. It is given that $q$ divides $p - 1$, and $r$ divides $p - 1$. Determine all possible values of $pq$.
