# Japan Mathematical Olympiad 2002 — Honsen (Final) — Quesito 4

*Cyclic sum inequality under two normalization constraints*

> Let $n$ be a natural number with $n \ge 3$. Suppose positive real numbers $a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_n, b_1, b_2, \ldots, b_n$ satisfy $$a_1 + a_2 + \cdots + a_n = 1$$ $$b_1^2 + b_2^2 + \cdots + b_n^2 = 1.$$ Prove that the inequality $$a_1(b_1 + b_2) + a_2(b_2 + b_3) + \cdots + a_n(b_n + b_1) < 1$$ holds.
