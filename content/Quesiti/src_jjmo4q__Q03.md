# Japan Junior Mathematical Olympiad 2006 — Quesito 3

*Fraction with integer numerator and denominator closest to 1, with sum 109*

> Among the fractions whose numerator and denominator are both integers and whose sum is $109$,
> $$\frac{108}{1},\ \frac{107}{2},\ \frac{106}{3},\ \ldots,\ \frac{3}{106},\ \frac{2}{107},\ \frac{1}{108}$$
> find the one that is closest to $1$. Here, "closest to $1$" means the one whose absolute difference from $1$ is smallest.
