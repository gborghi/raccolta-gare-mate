*Function on non-negative integers satisfying two conditions, count all such functions*

> Let $f$ be a function from the set of non-negative integers to itself.
> (i) $\bigl(f\bigl(\tfrac{1}{2}(f(n)+1)\bigr) - 1\bigr)^2 = \bigl(f(n)\bigr)^2$ for all $n \ge 0$, and $f(0) = 0$, $f(1) = 1$.
> (ii) How many such functions $f$ are there?
