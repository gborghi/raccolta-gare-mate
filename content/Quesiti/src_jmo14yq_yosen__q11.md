*Convex quadrilateral ABCD with AD=CD, BD=BC and given angles; find angle BAD.*

> In convex quadrilateral $ABCD$, we have $AD = CD$, $BD = BC$, $\angle ADC = 168^\circ$, and $\angle ABC = 66^\circ$. Find $\angle BAD$.
