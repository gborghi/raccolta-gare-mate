*Logic puzzle: six friends, two per transport, deduce who rides what*

> Six friends plan to travel and do so in pairs, each pair using a different mode of transport: plane, train, and car. Alexandre accompanies Bento. André travels by plane. Dário does not use the train. Tomás does not travel by plane or train. Which of the following statements is correct?
> 
> **A)** Bento travels by car and Carlos travels by plane.
> **B)** Dário and Carlos travel by car.
> **C)** Tomás travels by plane and André travels by plane.
> **D)** Alexandre travels by train and Tomás travels by car.
> **E)** André travels by train and Alexandre travels by car.
