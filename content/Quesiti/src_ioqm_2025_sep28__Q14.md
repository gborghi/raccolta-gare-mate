# Indian Olympiad Qualifier in Mathematics (IOQM) 2025 — Sep 28 — Quesito 14

*Square ABCD side 1 as chord of circle; tangent from C has length 2; find d²*

> The side $AB$ of a square $ABCD$ is 1 and it is also a cord of a circle $S$. The side $CD$ does not intersect $S$. The length of the tangent $CK$, drawn from $C$ to $S$ at the point $K$ is 2. If $d$ is the diameter of $S$, then calculate $d^2$.
