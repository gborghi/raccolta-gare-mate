*Isosceles triangle, circumcircle, circumcenter of HDF lies on Gamma*

> Let $ABC$ be an isosceles triangle with $AB = AC$. Let $O$ be a point strictly inside the triangle (not on any side). Let $\Gamma$ be the circumcircle of triangle $ABC$. Lines through $O$ meet segment $BC$ at $D$ and segment $AC$ (excluding endpoints) at $E$, and these two lines intersect each other. Let $F$ be a point on $\Gamma$ other than $A$, $B$, $C$ such that $B$, $D$, $F$ are collinear (or the line $BD$ meets $\Gamma$ again at $F$). Let $H$ be the second intersection of the circumcircle of triangle $AEO$ with $\Gamma$. Prove that the circumcenter of triangle $HDF$ lies on $\Gamma$. Here, $XY$ denotes the length of segment $XY$.
