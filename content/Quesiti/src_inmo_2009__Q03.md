# Indian National Mathematical Olympiad 2009 — Quesito 3

*Find all real x satisfying [x^2+2x]=[x]^2+2[x]*

> Find all real numbers $x$ such that
> $$[x^2 + 2x] = [x]^2 + 2[x].$$
> (Here $[x]$ denotes the largest integer not exceeding $x$.)
