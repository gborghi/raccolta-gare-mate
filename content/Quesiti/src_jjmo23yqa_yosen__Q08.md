*Smallest n so that average of any n-3 cards is integer given average of any n-4 is integer*

> Let $n$ be an integer greater than $1$. One integer is written on each of $n$ cards. Suppose the following condition holds: no matter which $n - 4$ cards are chosen (from the $n$ cards), the average of the integers on those cards is an integer. Find the smallest value of $n$ for which it is also necessarily true that: no matter which $n - 3$ cards are chosen, the average of the integers on those cards is also an integer.
