# Japan Mathematical Olympiad 2004 — Yosen (Preliminary) — Quesito 5

*Strictly increasing sequence of 2004 positive integers; no product of two terms equals another; minimize last term.*

> Let $a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_{2004}$ be positive integers satisfying the following conditions:
> \begin{itemize}
> \item $a_1 < a_2 < \cdots < a_{2004}$.
> \item For any pairwise distinct positive integers $i, j, k$ each at most $2004$, we always have $a_i \times a_j \neq a_k$.
> \end{itemize}
> Find the minimum possible value of $a_{2004}$.
