# Japan Junior Mathematical Olympiad 2017 — Yosen (Preliminary) — Quesito 10

*Ways to fill 2x7 grid with 1-14, rows increasing, column gaps non-decreasing*

> Write one each of the distinct integers from $1$ to $14$ into the cells of a $2\times 7$ grid as shown below. How many ways of writing satisfy the following conditions?
> \begin{itemize}
> \item In each row, the seven integers written are arranged in increasing order from left to right.
> \item For every integer $k$ from $1$ to $6$, the difference of the two numbers written in the $(k+1)$-th column from the left is greater than or equal to the difference of the two numbers written in the $k$-th column.
> \end{itemize}
