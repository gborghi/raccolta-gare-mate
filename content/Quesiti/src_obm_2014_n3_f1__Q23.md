# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2014 — Nível 3 — Fase 1 — Quesito 23

*Fraction of fuel tank used with no leak, given consumption proportional to load*

> A tanker truck was full of water but began to leak. Suppose that the truck's fuel consumption is directly proportional to the weight it carries and that the water flow rate and the truck's speed are constant. After traveling $200$ km, the truck had half the water capacity and used half a tank of fuel. If it were empty, the truck would use, traveling the same distance under the same conditions, one sixth of a tank. What fraction of the tank would it use if there were no leak? Disregard the influence of the tank's weight on gasoline consumption.
> (A) $\frac{11}{18}$ (B) $\frac{5}{9}$ (C) $\frac{2}{3}$ (D) $\frac{3}{4}$ (E) $\frac{4}{5}$
