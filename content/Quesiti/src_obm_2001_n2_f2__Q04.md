*Triangle ABC with external bisectors meeting; find angles*

> The measures of the angles of triangle $ABC$ are such that $\hat{A} < \hat{B} < 90^\circ < \hat{C}$. The external bisectors of angles $\hat{A}$ and $\hat{C}$ meet the extensions of sides $BC$ and $AB$ at points $P$ and $Q$, respectively. Given that $\overline{AP} = \overline{CQ} = \overline{AC}$, determine the angles of triangle $ABC$.
