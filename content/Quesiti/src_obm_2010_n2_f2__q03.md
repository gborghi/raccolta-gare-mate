*Real p,q satisfy two quadratics with pq≠1; find (pq+p+1)/q*

> Let $p, q$ be real numbers satisfying the relations $2p^2 - 3p - 1 = 0$, $q^2 + 3q - 2 = 0$, and $pq \neq 1$. Find the value of $\dfrac{pq + p + 1}{q}$.
