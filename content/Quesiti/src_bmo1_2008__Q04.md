*Subset of 756 elements from 1 to 2008, show two elements with divisibility*

> Let $S$ be a subset of the set $\{1, 2, 3, \ldots, 2008\}$ which consists of 756 distinct members. Show that there are two distinct elements $a$, $b$ in $S$ such that $a$ divides $b$.
