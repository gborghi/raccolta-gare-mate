*Fill 3x3 grid with 1-9 so edge-adjacent entries differ by at most 3*

> In each cell of a $3 \times 3$ grid we write one integer from $1$ to $9$, with no integer repeated (so each of $1,2,\ldots,9$ is used exactly once). In how many ways can this be done so that, for any two cells sharing an edge, the difference of the two integers written in them is at most $3$? Here, arrangements that coincide under rotation or reflection are counted as different.
