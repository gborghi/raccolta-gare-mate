# Coupe Animath Automne 2024 — Quesito 10

*Existence of 5 points with quadrilateral and equal-length conditions*

> Determine whether there exist five distinct points $A$, $B$, $C$, $D$, and $P$ in the plane, verifying the following conditions:
> \begin{itemize}
> \item the quadrilateral $ABCD$ is non-self-intersecting;
> \item the point $P$ lies strictly inside this quadrilateral;
> \item the segments $[AP]$, $[BP]$, $[CP]$, and $[DP]$ are all contained in the interior of quadrilateral $ABCD$;
> \item the equalities of lengths $AP = AB$, $BP = BC$, $CP = CD$, and $DP = DA$.
> \end{itemize}
