*Angle equality in triangle with interior point and parallelogram*

> The point $P$ lies inside triangle $ABC$ such that $\angle ABP = \angle PCA$. The point $Q$ is such that $PBQC$ is a parallelogram. Prove that $\angle QAB = \angle CAP$.
