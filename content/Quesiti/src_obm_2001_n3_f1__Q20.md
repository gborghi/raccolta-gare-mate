# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2001 — Nível 3 — Fase 1 — Quesito 20

*Right trapezoid ABCD with right angles A,B; M,N midpoints of AB,CD; compare angles ANB and CMD*

> Let $ABCD$ be a right trapezoid whose only right angles are $\hat{A}$ and $\hat{B}$. $M$ and $N$ are the midpoints of $AB$ and $CD$, respectively. With respect to the angles $\alpha = A\hat{N}B$ and $\beta = C\hat{M}D$, we can say that:
> 
> **A)** $\alpha < \beta$ $\quad$ **B)** $\alpha > \beta$ $\quad$ **C)** $\alpha = \beta$ $\quad$ **D)** any one of the situations in alternatives A), B) and C) can occur. $\quad$ **E)** the angle $\alpha$ is right.
