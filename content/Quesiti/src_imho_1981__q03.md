*Maximum value of m^n + n^m with integer constraint*

> Determine the maximum value of $m^n + n^m$, where $m$ and $n$ are integers satisfying $m, n \in \{1, 2, \ldots, 1981\}$ and $(n^2 - mn - m^2)^2 = 1$.
