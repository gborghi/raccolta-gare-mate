*Count positive integers x with floor(x/99) = floor(x/101)*

> Find the number of positive integers $x$ which satisfy $\left\lfloor \dfrac{x}{99} \right\rfloor = \left\lfloor \dfrac{x}{101} \right\rfloor$.
> 
> (Here $\lfloor z \rfloor$ denotes, for any real $z$, the largest integer not exceeding $z$; e.g.\ $\lfloor 7/6 \rfloor = 1$.)
