*Find all real-valued functions satisfying a given functional equation*

> Find all functions $f$ defined on the real numbers taking real values such that for any real numbers $x, y$,
> $$f(f(x) - f(y)) = f(f(x)) - 2x^2 f(y) + f(y^2)$$
> holds.
