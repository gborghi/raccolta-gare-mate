*Convex hexagon with opposite sides conditions implies cyclic*

> For a convex hexagon $ABCDEF$ in which each pair of opposite sides is unequal, consider the following six statements:
> $(a_1)$ $AB$ is parallel to $DE$; $(a_2)$ $AE = BD$;
> $(b_1)$ $BC$ is parallel to $EF$; $(b_2)$ $BF = CE$;
> $(c_1)$ $CD$ is parallel to $FA$; $(c_2)$ $CA = DF$.
> 
> (a) Show that if all six statements are true, then the hexagon is cyclic (i.e., it can be inscribed in a circle).
> 
> (b) Prove that, in fact, any five of these six statements also imply that the hexagon is cyclic.
