*Max countries outscoring country J's team*

> In a competition with $100$ countries including country J, $3$ players from each country (a total of $300$ players) take a test, and all $300$ obtain distinct non-negative integer scores. Country J's three players obtain the scores ranked $1$st, $10$th, and $100$th highest among all $300$ players. Find the maximum possible number of countries, among the remaining $99$, that satisfy the following condition: the sum of that country's three players' scores is greater than the sum of country J's three players' scores.
