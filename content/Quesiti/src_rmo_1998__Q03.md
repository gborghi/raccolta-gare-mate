# Regional Mathematical Olympiad 1998 — Quesito 3

*Inequality between odd and even reciprocal sums*

> Prove the following inequality for every natural number $n$:
> $$\frac{1}{n+1}\left(1 + \frac{1}{3} + \frac{1}{5} + \cdots + \frac{1}{2n-1}\right) > \frac{1}{n}\left(\frac{1}{2} + \frac{1}{4} + \frac{1}{6} + \cdots + \frac{1}{2n}\right).$$
