# Regional Mathematical Olympiad 1997 — Quesito 3

*Solve equation involving floor functions*

> Solve for real $x$:
> $$\frac{1}{[x]} + \frac{1}{[2x]} = \{x\} + \frac{1}{3},$$
> where $[x]$ is the greatest integer less than or equal to $x$ and $\{x\} = x - [x]$. [e.g. $[3.4] = 3$ and $\{3.4\} = 0.4$.]
