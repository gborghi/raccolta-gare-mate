*Starting from 1, press sin or cos 2001 times (radians); find the largest possible result*

> A calculator has the number $1$ on its display. We must perform $2001$ operations, each of which consists of pressing one of the two keys $\sin$ or $\cos$ exactly once. These operations compute, respectively, the sine or the cosine of their argument, in radians. What is the largest possible result obtainable after these $2001$ operations?
