# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2004 — Nível 3 — Fase 1 — Quesito 25

*Number of positive integers n that can be weighed in one weighing on a two-pan balance with 1, 3, 10 kg weights placed on either pan.*

> A market vendor sells potatoes and, to weigh, uses a two-pan balance, a 1 kg weight, a 3 kg weight and a 10 kg weight. Consider the following statement: "This vendor can weigh (in a single weighing) $n$ kilograms of potatoes". How many positive values of $n$ make this statement true, supposing he may place the weights on both pans?
> 
> (A) 7 (B) 10 (C) 12 (D) 13 (E) 14
