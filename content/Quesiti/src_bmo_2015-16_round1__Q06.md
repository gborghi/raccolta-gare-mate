# British Mathematical Olympiad 2015-16 — Round 1 — Quesito 6

*Charming integers defined recursively; prove every positive integer is a sum of few charming integers.*

> A positive integer $n$ is called charming if it is equal to $1$ or it can be written as the sum of two non-negative integers in $k$ different charming ways, where $k$ is a non-negative integer. Prove that every positive integer can be written as a sum of a small number of charming integers.
