*Find sqrt(AB) where 19AB is a perfect square, A,B digits*

> The number $19\overline{AB}$, where $A$ and $B$ are digits, is a perfect square. The value of $\sqrt{\overline{AB}}$ (the square root of the two-digit number $\overline{AB}$) is:
> 
> A) $5$ \quad B) $6$ \quad C) $7$ \quad D) $8$ \quad E) $5$
