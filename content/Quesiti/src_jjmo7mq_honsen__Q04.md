*Double inequality for cyclic expression in positive reals a,b,c*

> For positive real numbers $a, b, c$, prove that
> $$\frac{3}{2} < \frac{4a+b}{a+4b} + \frac{4b+c}{b+4c} + \frac{4c+a}{c+4a} < 9.$$
