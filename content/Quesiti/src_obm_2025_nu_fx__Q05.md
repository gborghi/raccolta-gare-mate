# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2025 — Nível Universitário — Fase x — Quesito 5

*Sequences of positive terms: divergence and convergence of series*

> (a) Let $(a_n)_{n \ge 1}$ be a decreasing sequence of positive terms such that the series $\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{a_n}{n}$ diverges. Prove that for any increasing sequence $(n_k)_{k \ge 1}$ of positive integers with $\frac{n_{k+1}}{n_k}$ bounded, the series $\sum_{k=1}^{\infty} a_{n_k}$ diverges.
> 
> (b) Let $(a_n)_{n \ge 1}$ be a sequence of positive terms such that the series $\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{a_n}{n}$ converges. Prove that there exists an increasing sequence $(n_k)_{k \ge 1}$ of positive integers with $\lim_{k \to \infty} \frac{n_{k+1}}{n_k} = 1$ such that the series $\sum_{k=1}^{\infty} a_{n_k}$ converges.
