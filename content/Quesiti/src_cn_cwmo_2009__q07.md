*Smallest number of students s>12 ensuring 3 share 4 identical correct answers*

> There are $s$ ($s > 12$) students participating in a mathematics contest. The examination paper consists of 15 fill-in-the-blank questions. For each question, the score of a correct answer is 1 point, and no point will be awarded if the answer is wrong or left blank. After analyzing all the possible cases of score distributions of these $s$ students, one finds out that if the sum of total scores of any 12 students is not less than 16 points, then there are at least 3 students among these $s$ students who answer at least 4 identical questions correctly. Determine the smallest possible value of $s$.
