*Plane divided into unit squares filled with a bijection from positive integers; decide if each number can divide the sum of its four neighbors.*

> A plane is divided into unit squares. A positive integer should be written in each unit square so that each positive integer occurs exactly once. Decide whether this can be done in such a way that the number in each square divides the sum of the numbers in the four neighboring squares.
