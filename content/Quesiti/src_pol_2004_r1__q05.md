*Show $N(m,n+1)=N(n,m+1)$, where $N(m,n)$ counts non-decreasing length-$m$ sequences from $\{1,\dots,n\}$.*

> For positive integers $m$ and $n$, let $N(m,n)$ denote the number of non-decreasing sequences of $m$ terms from $\{1,2,\dots,n\}$. Show that $N(m,n+1)=N(n,m+1)$.
