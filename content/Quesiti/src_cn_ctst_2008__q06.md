*Find maximum M satisfying three sequence conditions for all n*

> Find the maximum positive number $M$ such that for every $n \in \mathbb{N}^*$, there are positive numbers $a_1, a_2, \cdots, a_n$ and $b_1, b_2, \cdots, b_n$ satisfying
> (a) $\sum_{k=1}^{n} b_k = 1$, $2b_k \geq b_{k-1} + b_{k+1}$, $k = 2, 3, \cdots, n-1$,
> (b) $a_k^2 \leq 1 + \sum_{i=1}^{k} a_i b_i$, $k = 1, 2, \cdots, n$,
> (c) $a_n = M$.
