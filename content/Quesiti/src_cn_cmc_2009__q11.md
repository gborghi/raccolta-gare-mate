*Maximum and minimum of sqrt(x)+sqrt(x+27)+sqrt(13-x) on [0,13]*

> Find the maximum and minimum of the function
> $$y = \sqrt{x} + \sqrt{x + 27} + \sqrt{13 - x}$$
> for $x \in [0, 13]$.

**Area:** [[Algebra e Analisi]]
**Risposta:** $\max = 11,\ \min = 3\sqrt{3} + \sqrt{13}$
