*Sum of all b for which two quadratics share a common root*

> Let $f(x) = x^2 - 3x + b$ and $g(x) = x^2 + bx - 3$, where $b$ is a real number. What is the sum of all possible values of $b$ for which the equations $f(x) = 0$ and $g(x) = 0$ have a common root?
