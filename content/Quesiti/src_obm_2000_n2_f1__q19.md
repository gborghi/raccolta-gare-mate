*Travel time from Iracimimirm to Salvador with moving traffic jam*

> From Iracimimirm to Salvador along the Coconut Road it is $60\text{ km}$. At $11{:}00$, an accident causes a traffic jam that extends $15\text{ km}$ in the direction of Salvador and moves at $4\text{ km/h}$. We leave Iracimimirm at $11{:}00$, traveling at $60\text{ km/h}$; in the jam zone our speed drops to $6\text{ km/h}$. Approximately what time do we arrive in Salvador?
> 
> A) $10\text{h}43\text{min}$ \quad B) $10\text{h}17\text{min}$ \quad C) $10\text{h}48\text{min}$ \quad D) $10\text{h}53\text{min}$ \quad E) $11\text{h}01\text{min}$
