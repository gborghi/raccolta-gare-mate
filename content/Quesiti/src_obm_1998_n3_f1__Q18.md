*Four people make statements about a problem's answer; find the maximum number that can be simultaneously true.*

> Regarding the answer of a problem, Maurício, Paulo, Eduardo and Carlos made the following statements:
> 
> - Maurício: It is greater than $5$.
> - Paulo: It is greater than $10$.
> - Eduardo: It is a prime number.
> - Carlos: It is greater than $12$.
> 
> Among the statements above, how many, at most, can be true?
> 
> A) $0$
> 
> B) $1$
> 
> C) $2$
> 
> D) $3$
> 
> E) $4$
