*True discount versus apparent discount in a sale*

> Horacio decided to increase the sales of CDs in his shop and announced a liquidation sale with 30% off the label price. It so happened that on the day before the sale, Horacio had raised the price marked on the labels. As a result, the true discount was only 9%. By how much did Horacio increase the price?
> 
> A) 30%
> B) 39%
> C) 21%
> D) 40%
> E) 31%

> Horacio ha deciso di aumentare le vendite di CD nel suo negozio e ha annunciato una vendita di liquidazione con un 30% di sconto sul prezzo dell'etichetta.
