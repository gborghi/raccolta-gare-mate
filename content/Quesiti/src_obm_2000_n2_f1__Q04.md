*Logic puzzle: identify who paid museum admission*

> Four friends want to visit a museum and one of them decides to pay for all the entrance tickets. A clue appears:
> 
> $-$ It wasn't me, says Benjamim. $\quad$ $-$ It was Carlos, says Mário.
> $-$ It was Mário, says Carlos. $\quad$ $-$ Mário didn't pay, says Pedro.
> 
> Only one of them is lying. Who paid for the museum entrance?
> 
> A) Mário \quad B) Pedro \quad C) Benjamim \quad D) Carlos
