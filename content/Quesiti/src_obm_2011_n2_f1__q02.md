*Width of equal side strips covering 20% of a rectangular plot*

> (A) $6\,\text{m}$ \quad (B) $8\,\text{m}$ \quad (C) $10\,\text{m}$ \quad (D) $16\,\text{m}$ \quad (E) $24\,\text{m}$
