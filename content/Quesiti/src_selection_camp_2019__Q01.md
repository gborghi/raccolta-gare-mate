*Pairs (n,k) where some s gives sn, sk equal divisor counts*

> Find all pairs $(n, k)$ of positive integers with $n \neq k$ satisfying the following condition: there exists a positive integer $s$ such that the number of positive divisors of $sn$ equals the number of positive divisors of $sk$.
