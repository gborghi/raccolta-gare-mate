*Count palindromic integers not divisible by 10 between 1 and 10000*

> Among positive integers, a number is called a palindrome (回文数) if it reads the same forwards and backwards. Among palindromes between $1$ and $10000$ (inclusive), count those that are not multiples of $10$.
