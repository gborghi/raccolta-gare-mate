*Find all real triples (x,y,z) satisfying two symmetric polynomial equations.*

> Find all triples $(x, y, z)$ of real numbers satisfying
> $$3(x^2 + y^2 + z^2) = 1,$$
> $$x^2 y^2 + y^2 z^2 + z^2 x^2 = xyz(x + y + z)^3.$$
