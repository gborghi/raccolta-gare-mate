*Solve the symmetric system (x+y)(x+y+z)=18, (y+z)(x+y+z)=30, (z+x)(x+y+z)=24 in terms of A.*

> Solve the system
> $$\begin{cases} (x + y)(x + y + z) = 18 \\ (y + z)(x + y + z) = 30 \\ (z + x)(x + y + z) = 24 \end{cases}$$
> in terms of the parameter $A$.
