*Digits 2,3,4,5,6 placed one per cell in a 3×3 grid to form two three-digit square numbers. Which digit is in the centre?*

>
> - **(A)** 2
> - **(B)** 3
> - **(C)** 4
> - **(D)** 5
> - **(E)** 6
