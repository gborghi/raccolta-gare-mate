# Japan Mathematical Olympiad 2020 — Yosen (Preliminary) — Quesito 11

*Circle with 5 points, two tangent circles meet at K, find length CQ*

> Five points $A, B, C, D, P$ lie on a circle $\Omega$ in this order. The circle passing through $P$ and tangent to line $AB$ at $A$, and the circle passing through $P$ and tangent to line $CD$ at $D$, intersect at a point $K$ inside $\Omega$. Let $M$ and $N$ be the midpoints of chords $AB$ and $CD$ respectively. It is given that the three points $A$, $K$, $N$ are collinear, and the three points $D$, $K$, $M$ are collinear. Given $AK = 5$, $DK = 3$, $KM = 7$, let $Q$ be the intersection of line $PK$ with $\Omega$ other than $P$. Find the length $CQ$, where $XY$ denotes the length of segment $XY$.
