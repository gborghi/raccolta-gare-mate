*Maximise cyclic sum of x_i^2(x_{i+1}-x_{i-1}) for x_i in [-1,1]*

> For real numbers $x_1, x_2, \ldots, x_{60} \in [-1, 1]$, find the maximum of
> $$\sum_{i=1}^{60} x_i^2 (x_{i+1} - x_{i-1}),$$
> where $x_0 = x_{60}$ and $x_{61} = x_1$.
