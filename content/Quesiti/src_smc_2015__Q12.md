*Angle y in terms of alpha and beta for tangent lines from a point to a circle inscribed in a triangle*

> A circle touches the sides of triangle $PQR$ at the points $S$, $T$ and $U$ as shown. Also $\angle PQR = \alpha^\circ$ and $\angle TSU = y^\circ$. Which of the following gives $y$ in terms of $\alpha$ and $\beta$?
>
> - **(A)** $\tfrac{1}{2}(\alpha + \beta)$
> - **(B)** $180 - \tfrac{1}{2}(\alpha + \beta)$
> - **(C)** $180 - (\alpha + \beta)$
> - **(D)** $\alpha + \beta$
> - **(E)** $\tfrac{1}{4}(\alpha + \beta)$
