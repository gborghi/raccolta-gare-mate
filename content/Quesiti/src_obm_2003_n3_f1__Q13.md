# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2003 — Nível 3 — Fase 1 — Quesito 13

*Logic puzzle about truth-telling in foreign language with 'KAB'/'BAK' meaning yes/no*

> You are in a foreign country and do not know the local language, called LUCIANÊS, but you know that the words "BAK" and "KAB" mean "yes" or "no" (you do not know which is which). You meet a person who speaks Portuguese and ask: "KAB significa sim?" ("Does KAB mean yes?"). The person answers "KAB". You can deduce:
> 
> A) KAB means no.
> B) The person said yes but lied.
> C) The person who answered lied.
> D) The person who answered told the truth.
> E) It is not possible to determine whether the person told the truth without a LUCIANÊS--PORTUGUESE dictionary.
