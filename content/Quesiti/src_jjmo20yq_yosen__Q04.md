*Triangle AB=5,BC=7,CA=6 with cevian points; circumcircle of BDF tangent to EF*

> In triangle $ABC$ with $AB = 5$, $BC = 7$, $CA = 6$, let $D$, $E$, $F$ be points on sides $BC$, $CA$, $AB$ respectively (not coinciding with any vertex). The quadrilateral $ABDE$ has a circumscribed circle, and the quadrilateral $BCEF$ also has a circumscribed circle. The circumscribed circle of triangle $BDF$ is tangent to line segment $EF$. Find the length $EF$. (Here $XY$ denotes the length of segment $XY$.)
