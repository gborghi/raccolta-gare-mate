*Remove one expression from {4x, 8x, 12x, 16x, 20x} so mean of remaining four is 11x*

> In the following expressions, $x$ is non-zero. When one of these expressions is removed, the mean of the remaining four is $11x$. Which expression is removed?
>
> - **(A)** $4x$
> - **(B)** $8x$
> - **(C)** $12x$
> - **(D)** $16x$
> - **(E)** $20x$
