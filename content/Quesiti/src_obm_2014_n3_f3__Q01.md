*Convex quadrilateral with equal incircle radii implies rhombus*

> Let $ABCD$ be a convex quadrilateral and let $P$ be the intersection of the diagonals $AC$ and $BD$. The radii of the incircles of triangles $ABP$, $BCP$, $CDP$, and $DAP$ are equal. Prove that $ABCD$ is a rhombus.
