# British Mathematical Olympiad 2005-06 — Round 1 — Quesito 6

*2005 points no three collinear: triangles containing point P have even count*

> Let $T$ be a set of 2005 coplanar points with no three collinear. Show that, for any point $P$ in $T$, the number of triangles whose vertices are points in $T$ and which contain $P$ in their interior is even.
