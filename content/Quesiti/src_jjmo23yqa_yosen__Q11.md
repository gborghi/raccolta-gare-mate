*Minimum total score for student A under given dominance conditions over student B*

> Let $a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_7$ be a permutation of the integers $1, 2, \ldots, 7$. Students A and B each attempt 7 problems numbered $1$ through $7$; for problem $i$, a student earns $a_i$ points if they solve it and $0$ points otherwise. Suppose the following two conditions hold for every subset of problems that A and B might each solve:
> 
> - If A solves more problems than B, then A's total score is greater than B's total score.
> - If A and B solve the same number of problems, and among the problems solved by exactly one of them the one with the largest index is solved by A, then A's total score is greater than B's total score.
> 
> Find the minimum possible value of A's total score (i.e., $\sum_{i \in S} a_i$ where $S$ is the set of problems A solves).
