*Count 3-digit integers whose digits form an arithmetic progression left to right*

> How many integers between 100 and 999 (inclusive) are such that their digits form an arithmetic progression when read from left to right?\\
> We say that a sequence of three numbers $a$, $b$, $c$ forms an arithmetic progression if $a + c = 2b$.
