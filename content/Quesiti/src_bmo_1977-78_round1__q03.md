*Show sequence u_n has one and only one solution with u_1 < u_2*

> Show that there is one and only one sequence $\{u_n\}$ of integers such that $u_1 < u_2$ and
> $$u_n^2 + 1 = u_{n-1} \cdot u_{n+1} \quad \text{for all } n > 1.$$
