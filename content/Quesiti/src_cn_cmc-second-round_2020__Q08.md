*Find all ordered pairs (a,b) with a^n+b^{n+9} constant mod 13 for all n*

> (50 marks) Let $a, b$ be positive integers not greater than 12 satisfying that there exists constant $C$ such that $a^n + b^{n+9} \equiv C \pmod{13}$ holds for any positive integer $n$. Find all ordered pairs $(a, b)$ that satisfy the conditions.
