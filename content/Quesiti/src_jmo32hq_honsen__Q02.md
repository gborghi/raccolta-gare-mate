# Japan Mathematical Olympiad 2022 — Honsen (Final) — Quesito 2

*Find all strictly increasing f with f^100(m)+mn=f(m)f(n)*

> Let $f$ be a strictly increasing function from the positive integers to the positive integers. Find all such $f$ such that
> $$f^{100}(m) + mn = f(m)f(n)$$
> holds for every pair of positive integers $m, n$. Here $f^{100}(n)$ denotes $f(f(\cdots f(n)\cdots))$ with $f$ applied $100$ times.
