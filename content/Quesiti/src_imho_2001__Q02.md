*Inequality with three positive reals and square roots*

> Prove that
> $$\frac{a}{\sqrt{a^2+8bc}}+\frac{b}{\sqrt{b^2+8ca}}+\frac{c}{\sqrt{c^2+8ab}}\ge 1$$
> for all positive real numbers $a$, $b$ and $c$.
