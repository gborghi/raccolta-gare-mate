# Japan Junior Mathematical Olympiad 2016 — Yosen (Preliminary) — Quesito 5

*Find DF with cevians trisecting angle A into three 45-degree parts*

> On side $BC$ of triangle $ABC$ there are points $D,E$, with $B,D,E,C$ lying in this order, such that $\angle BAD = \angle DAE = \angle EAC = 45^\circ$. Let $F$ be the intersection point of the circumcircle of triangle $ABE$ with line $AC$, other than $A$. Given $AC = 3$ and $AD = 1$, find the length of segment $DF$. Here $XY$ denotes the length of segment $XY$.
