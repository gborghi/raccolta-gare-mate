*Count arrangements of 2024 black and white stones with a sign-flip majority condition*

> A total of $2024$ stones — white and black — are arranged in a row. It turned out that the number of black stones that have a white stone to their right is greater than the number of black stones that have a white stone to their left. Find the number of such arrangements.
