> $$\frac{1}{a+1} + \frac{1}{a+2} + \cdots + \frac{1}{2a+1} \le a - 2007\tfrac{1}{2}$$
> holds for every positive integer $a$. Then the least positive integer $a$ satisfying the inequality is \_\_\_\_.
