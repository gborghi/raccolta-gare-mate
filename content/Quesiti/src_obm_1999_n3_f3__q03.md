*Max pieces on 10x10 board with no axis-parallel rectangle*

> We have a $10 \times 10$ square board. We wish to place $n$ pieces on the cells of the board in such a way that no $4$ pieces form the vertices of a rectangle with sides parallel to the sides of the board. Determine the maximum value of $n$ for which this construction is possible.
