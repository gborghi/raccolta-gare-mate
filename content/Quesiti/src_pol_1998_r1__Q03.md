*Three sequences defined by recurrences; prove one is bounded*

> The sequences $(a_n)$, $(b_n)$, $(x_n)$ are given by $a_1 = 4$ and for $n \ge 1$,
> $$a_{n+1} = a_n(a_n - 1), \quad 2^{b_n} = a_n, \quad 2^{x_n - x_{n-1}} = b_n.$$
> Prove that the sequence $(x_n)$ is bounded.
