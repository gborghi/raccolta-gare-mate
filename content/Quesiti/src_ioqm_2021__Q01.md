# Indian Olympiad Qualifier in Mathematics (IOQM) 2021 — Quesito 1

*Square with vertices on three parallel lines distance 3 apart; find area*

> Three parallel lines $l_1, l_2, l_3$ are drawn in the plane such that the perpendicular distance between $l_1$ and $l_2$ is 3 and the perpendicular distance between $l_2$ and $l_3$ is also 3. A square $ABCD$ is constructed such that $A$ lies on $l_1$, $B$ lies on $l_2$, $C$ lies on $l_2$, and $D$ lies on $l_3$. Find the area of the square.
