*Prove inequality for nonneg reals on unit sphere with cyclic rational and product terms*

> Given $a, b, c \ge 0$ with $a^2 + b^2 + c^2 \le 1$. Prove:
> $$\frac{a}{a^2+bc+1}+\frac{b}{b^2+ca+1}+\frac{c}{c^2+ab+1}+3abc < \sqrt{3}.$$
