# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2001 — Nível 2 — Fase 1 — Quesito 24

*Number of wolves among five animals where dogs tell truth and wolves lie*

> Five animals $A$, $B$, $C$, $D$ and $E$ are dogs or wolves. Dogs always tell the truth and wolves always lie. $A$ says that $B$ is a dog. $B$ says that $C$ is a wolf. $C$ says that $D$ is a wolf. $D$ says that $B$ and $E$ are animals of different species. $E$ says that $A$ is a dog. How many wolves are there among the five animals?
> 
> (A) $1$ \quad (B) $2$ \quad (C) $3$ \quad (D) $4$ \quad (E) $5$
