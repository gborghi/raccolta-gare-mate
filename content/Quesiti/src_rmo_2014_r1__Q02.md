# Regional Mathematical Olympiad 2014 — Region 1 — Quesito 2

*Arithmetic progression: express common difference d in terms of x, y, z*

> Let $a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_{2n}$ be an arithmetic progression of positive real numbers with common difference $d$. Let
> (i) $a_1^2 + a_3^2 + \cdots + a_{2n-1}^2 = x$,
> (ii) $a_2^2 + a_4^2 + \cdots + a_{2n}^2 = y$, and
> (iii) $a_n + a_{n+1} = z$.
> Express $d$ in terms of $x$, $y$, $z$, $n$.
