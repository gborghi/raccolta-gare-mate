*Days remaining rations feed cattle after buying and selling cows*

> A farmer had $24$ cows and rations to feed them for $60$ days. However, $10$ days later, he bought $6$ more cows and $10$ days after that purchase he sold $20$ cows. For how many more days, after this last purchase, can he feed the herd with the remaining rations?
> 
> (A) $50$ \quad (B) $60$ \quad (C) $70$ \quad (D) $80$ \quad (E) $90$
