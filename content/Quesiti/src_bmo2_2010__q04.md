*Prove the cyclic inequality 4(x+y+z)^3 > 27(x^2 y + y^2 z + z^2 x) for all positive reals x, y, z.*

> Prove that, for all positive real numbers $x$, $y$ and $z$,
> $$4(x + y + z)^3 > 27(x^2 y + y^2 z + z^2 x).$$
