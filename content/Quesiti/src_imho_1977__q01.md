*Midpoints of segments in square form dodecagon vertices*

> Equilateral triangles $ABK$, $BCL$, $CDM$, $DAN$ are constructed inside the square $ABCD$. Prove that the midpoints of the four segments $KL$, $LM$, $MN$, $NK$ and the midpoints of the eight segments $AB$, $BK$, $BL$, $BC$, $CM$, $DM$, $DN$, $AN$ are the twelve vertices of a regular dodecagon.
