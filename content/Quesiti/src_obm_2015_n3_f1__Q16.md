*Smallest n such that n! ends in exactly 2016 trailing zeros*

> For a positive integer $n$, the factorial is $n! = 1 \cdot 2 \cdot 3 \cdots n$. There is no $n$ for which $n!$ ends in exactly 2015 trailing zeros. Find the smallest $n$ such that $n!$ ends in exactly 2016 trailing zeros.
> 
> (A) 8064 \quad (B) 8065 \quad (C) 8070 \quad (D) 8075 \quad (E) 8080
