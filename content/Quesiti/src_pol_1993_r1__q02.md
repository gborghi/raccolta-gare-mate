*Solve a cyclic system of n equations involving tangent and cotangent.*

> Let $n\ge 3$ be integer. Solve the system of equations:
> $$\begin{aligned}\tan x_1+3\cot x_1&=2\tan x_2,\\\tan x_2+3\cot x_2&=2\tan x_3,\\&\;\;\vdots\\\tan x_n+3\cot x_n&=2\tan x_1.\end{aligned}$$
