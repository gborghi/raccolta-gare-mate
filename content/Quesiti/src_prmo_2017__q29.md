*Largest h_n for n<100 where h_n = gcd(n!+1, (n+1)!)*

> For each positive integer $n$, consider the highest common factor $h_n$ of the two numbers $n! + 1$ and $(n+1)!$. For $n < 100$, find the largest value of $h_n$.
