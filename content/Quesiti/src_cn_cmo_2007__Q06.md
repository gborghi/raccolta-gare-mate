*Find smallest n≥9 such that any integers allow b∈{4,7} with 9|sum*

> Find the smallest positive integer $n \geq 9$ satisfying that for any group of integers $a_1, a_2, \cdots, a_n$, there always exist $b_1, b_2, \cdots, b_n \in \{4, 7\}$ such that $9 \mid (a_1 b_1 + a_2 b_2 + \cdots + a_n b_n)$.
