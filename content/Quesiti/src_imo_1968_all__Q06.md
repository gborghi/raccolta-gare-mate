# IMO 1968 (Tenth International Olympiad) — Quesito 6

*Evaluate infinite sum of floor((n+2^k)/2^(k+1))*

> For every natural number $n$, evaluate the sum
> $$\sum_{k=0}^{\infty} \left\lfloor \frac{n + 2^k}{2^{k+1}} \right\rfloor = \left\lfloor \frac{n+1}{2} \right\rfloor + \left\lfloor \frac{n+2}{4} \right\rfloor + \cdots + \left\lfloor \frac{n + 2^k}{2^{k+1}} \right\rfloor + \cdots$$
> (The symbol $\lfloor x \rfloor$ denotes the greatest integer not exceeding $x$.)
