*Angle bisectors and external bisector: prove angle APM = 30 degrees*

> The measure of angle $B$ of triangle $ABC$ is $120^\circ$. Let $M$ be a point on side $AC$ and $K$ a point on the extension of side $AB$ beyond $B$, such that $BM$ is the internal bisector of angle $\angle ABC$ and $CK$ is the external bisector corresponding to angle $\angle ACB$. The segment $MK$ intersects $BC$ at point $P$. Prove that $\angle APM = 30^\circ$.
