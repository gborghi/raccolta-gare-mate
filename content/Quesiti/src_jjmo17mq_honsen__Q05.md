# Japan Junior Mathematical Olympiad 2019 — Honsen (Final) — Quesito 5

*Angle equality in configuration with circumcircles and midpoints*

> Let $ABC$ be a triangle with $AB \neq AC$. Let $M$ be the midpoint of side $BC$, and let $N$ be the midpoint of the arc $BC$ (the arc containing $A$) of the circumcircle of triangle $ABC$. Let $H$ be the foot of the perpendicular from $N$ to line $AC$. Let $K$ be the intersection point, other than $C$, of the circumcircle of triangle $AMC$ and line $CN$. Prove that $\angle AKH = \angle CAM$. (Here $XY$ denotes the length of segment $XY$.)
