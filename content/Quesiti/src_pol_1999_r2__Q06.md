*Integers with vanishing weighted power sums for i=1..k-1; prove the k-th weighted sum is divisible by k!.*

> Suppose that $a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_n$ are integers such that
> $$a_1 + 2^i a_2 + 3^i a_3 + \cdots + n^i a_n = 0 \quad \text{for } i = 1, 2, \ldots, k-1,$$
> where $k \geq 2$ is a given integer. Prove that $a_1 + 2^k a_2 + 3^k a_3 + \cdots + n^k a_n$ is divisible by $k!$.
