# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2006 — Nível 2 — Fase 3 — Quesito 2

*Max collinear vertices in a 12-sided polygon*

> Among all polygons with 5 sides, the maximum number of collinear vertices (i.e., belonging to a single line) is three, as shown below.
> 
> What is the maximum number of collinear vertices that a polygon with 12 sides can have?
> 
> **Attention:** after drawing a 12-gon with the maximum number of collinear vertices, remember to show that no other 12-gon has more collinear vertices than yours.
