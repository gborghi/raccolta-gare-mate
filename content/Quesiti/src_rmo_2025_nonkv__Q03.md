*Two circles, transversal line, concyclicity and equal lengths*

> Let $\Omega$ and $\Gamma$ be circles centred at $O_1, O_2$ respectively. Suppose that they intersect in points $A, B$. Suppose $O_1$ is outside $\Gamma$ and $O_2$ is outside $\Omega$. Let $\ell$ be a line not passing through $A$ and $B$ that intersects $\Omega$ at $P, R$ and $\Gamma$ at $Q, S$ so that $P, Q, R, S$ lie on the line in this order. Furthermore, the points $O_1, B$ lie on one side of $\ell$ and the points $O_2, A$ lie on the other side of $\ell$. Given that the points $A, P, Q, O_1$ are concyclic and $B, R, S, O_2$ are concyclic, as well prove that $AQ = BR$.
