*Sequence P_n defined by linear recurrence; area of pentagon P0,P2,P4,P6,P8*

> Let $P_0 = (3, 1)$ and define $P_{n+1} = (x_{n+1}, y_{n+1})$ for $n \ge 0$ by
> $$x_{n+1} = \frac{3x_n - y_n}{2}, \quad y_{n+1} = \frac{x_n + y_n}{2}$$
> Find the area of the quadrilateral formed by the points $P_0, P_2, P_4, P_6, P_8$.
