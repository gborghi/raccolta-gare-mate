*Greatest value of $x^2y + y^2z + z^2x$ subject to two constraints*

> Find, with proof, the greatest value of $x^2 y + y^2 z + z^2 x$ where $x, y, z$ are real numbers satisfying the conditions
> $$x + y + z = 0, \quad x^2 + y^2 + z^2 = 1.$$
