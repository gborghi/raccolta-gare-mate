*Find p given that positive integers m, n, p satisfy 3m + 3/(n + 3/p) = 17*

> The positive integers $m$, $n$ and $p$ satisfy the equation $3m + \dfrac{3}{n + \dfrac{3}{p}} = 17$. What is the value of $p$?
>
> - **(A)** $2$
> - **(B)** $3$
> - **(C)** $4$
> - **(D)** $6$
> - **(E)** $9$
