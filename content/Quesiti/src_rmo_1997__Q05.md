# Regional Mathematical Olympiad 1997 — Quesito 5

*Determine if three real expressions can be triangle side lengths*

> Let $x$, $y$ and $z$ be three distinct real positive numbers. Determine with proof whether or not the three real numbers
> $$\left|\frac{x}{y} - \frac{y}{x}\right|, \quad \left|\frac{y}{z} - \frac{z}{y}\right|, \quad \left|\frac{z}{x} - \frac{x}{z}\right|$$
> can be the lengths of the sides of a triangle.
