*Determine all functions f from the positive integers to the positive integers such that 1/a+1/b=1/c implies 1/f(a)+1/f(b)=1/f(c).*

> Determine all functions $f(n)$ from the positive integers to the positive integers which satisfy the following condition: whenever $a$, $b$ and $c$ are positive integers such that $1/a + 1/b = 1/c$, then
> $$1/f(a) + 1/f(b) = 1/f(c).$$
