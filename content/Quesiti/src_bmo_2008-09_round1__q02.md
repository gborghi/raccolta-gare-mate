*Find all real x, y, z satisfying three cyclic product equations*

> Find all real values of $x$, $y$ and $z$ such that
> $$(x+1)yz = 12, \quad (y+1)zx = 4 \quad \text{and} \quad (z+1)xy = 4.$$
