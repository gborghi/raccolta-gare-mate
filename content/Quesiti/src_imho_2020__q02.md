*Inequality for reals summing to 1 with a≥b≥c≥d≥0*

> The real numbers $a, b, c, d$ are such that $a \ge b \ge c \ge d > 0$ and $a + b + c + d = 1$. Prove that
> $$(a + 2b + 3c + 4d) a^a b^b c^c d^d < 1.$$
