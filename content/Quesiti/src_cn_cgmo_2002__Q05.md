*Permutation sum inequality: prove lower bound (n-1)/(n+2)*

> Assume $P_1, P_2, \ldots, P_n$ ($n \ge 2$) is an arbitrary permutation of $1, 2, \ldots, n$. Prove that $$\frac{1}{P_1 + P_2} + \frac{1}{P_2 + P_3} + \cdots + \frac{1}{P_{n-2} + P_{n-1}} + \frac{1}{P_{n-1} + P_n} > \frac{n-1}{n+2}.$$ (posed by Qiu Zonghu)
