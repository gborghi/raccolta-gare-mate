*Prove a binomial-type sum is divisible by a large power of 2.*

> Prove that the number
> $$\sum_{n=0}^{10^{10}} \binom{2 \cdot 10^{10}}{2n} 5^n$$
> is divisible by $2^{10^{10}-1}$.
