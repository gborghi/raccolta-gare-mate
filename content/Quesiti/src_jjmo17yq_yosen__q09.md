*Find AB in right triangle with cyclic quadrilateral ADFE*

> Triangle $ABC$ satisfies $\angle B = 90^\circ$ and $BC = 8$. Point $D$ lies on side $AB$ and point $E$ lies on side $AC$; let $F$ be the intersection of line $BE$ and line $CD$, with $BF = 6$ and $CF = 7$. Furthermore, the quadrilateral $ADFE$ has an inscribed circle. Find the length of segment $AB$. (Here $XY$ denotes the length of segment $XY$.)
