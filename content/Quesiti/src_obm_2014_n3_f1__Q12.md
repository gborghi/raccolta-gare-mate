# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2014 — Nível 3 — Fase 1 — Quesito 12

*Find real a where roots of x^2-ax+b are squares of roots of x^2-bx+a*

> The roots of the equation $x^2 - ax + b = 0$ are different from zero and are the squares of the roots of the equation $x^2 - bx + a = 0$. The roots of the equations are not necessarily real, but $a$ and $b$ are real. Then the value of $a$ is:
> (A) $-\sqrt{2}$ (B) $\sqrt{2}$ (C) $\sqrt{3}$ (D) $\sqrt[3]{2}$ (E) $\sqrt[3]{3}$
