*a_n = last digit of digit-sum of 2005 written n times in a row; find n with a_n=0 and compute a sum*

> For every strictly positive integer $n$, define $a_n$ as the last digit of the sum of the digits of the number $2005\,2005\ldots 2005$ (one writes "$2005$" $n$ times in a row). For example, $a_1 = 7$ and $a_2 = 4$.
> a) Which strictly positive integers $n$ satisfy $a_n = 0$?
> b) Calculate $a_1 + a_2 + \cdots + a_{2005}$.
