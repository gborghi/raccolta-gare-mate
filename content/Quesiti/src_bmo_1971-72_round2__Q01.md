*Label regular n²-gon vertices with digits 1,2 satisfying four conditions*

> Show how to assign to the vertices of a regular polygon with $n^2$ vertices, numbers such that:
> (i) only the digits $1$ and $2$ are used;
> (ii) only $n$ ones are used;
> (iii) each vertex has a different number; and
> (iv) neighbouring vertices have numbers differing in one and only one digit place.
