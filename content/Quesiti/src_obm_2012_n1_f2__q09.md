*Diamantino's digit-sum reduction game: trace, max 3-digit, count under 2012*

> Diamantino plays with numbers of two or more digits, transforming them into single-digit numbers in the following way: he adds the two rightmost digits and replaces those two digits with the result, repeating the process until he gets what he wants. For example, starting from $367$ he writes $313$, then writes $34$, and finishes writing $7$.
> 
> a) Starting with the number $2012$, what number will Diamantino obtain?
> 
> b) What is the largest three-digit number that Diamantino can transform into $1$?
> 
> c) Diamantino wrote all numbers less than $2012$, with two or more digits, that can be transformed into $9$. How many numbers did he write?
