*Number of palm-heart cans fitting in a wooden crate*

> A factory packs cans of palm heart in cubic cardboard boxes with side $20\text{ cm}$, so that they can be transported in wooden crates $80\text{ cm}$ wide, $120\text{ cm}$ long, and $60\text{ cm}$ high. The number of cans that fit in each crate is:
> 
> A) $576$ \quad B) $4{,}608$ \quad C) $2{,}304$ \quad D) $720$ \quad E) $144$
