*Prove triangle ABC right-angled; prove identity with circumcentre and centroid*

> (i) In $\triangle ABC$, $\sin^2 A + \sin^2 B + \sin^2 C = 2$. Prove that $\triangle ABC$ is right-angled.
> 
> (ii) If $O$ is the circumcentre and $G$ the centroid of $\triangle ABC$, prove that $9OG^2 = R^2(1 - 8\cos A \cos B \cos C)$.
