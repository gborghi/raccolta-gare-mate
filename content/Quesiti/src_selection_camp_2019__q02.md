*Max of a_2018 - a_2017 for an averaging recurrence*

> Let $a_0, a_1, a_2, \ldots$ be a real sequence satisfying $a_0 = 0$ and $a_1 = 1$. Suppose that for every integer $n$ with $n \ge 2$ there exists an integer $k$ with $1 \le k \le n$ such that
> $$a_n = \frac{a_{n-1} + a_{n-2} + \cdots + a_{n-k}}{k}.$$
> Find the maximum possible value of $a_{2018} - a_{2017}$.
