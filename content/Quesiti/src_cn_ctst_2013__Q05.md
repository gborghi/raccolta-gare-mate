*Prove inequality involving means and products of nonneg integers*

> Let $n \ge 2$ and $a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_n, b_1, b_2, \ldots, b_n$ be non-negative integers. Prove that
> $$\left(\frac{n}{n-1}\right)^{n-1}\left(\frac{1}{n}\sum_{i=1}^{n} a_i^2\right) + \left(\frac{1}{n}\sum_{i=1}^{n} b_i\right)^2 \ge \prod_{i=1}^{n}(a_i^2 + b_i^2)^{\frac{1}{n}}.$$
> (posed by Leng Gangsong)
