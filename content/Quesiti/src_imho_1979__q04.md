*All real numbers in pi make distances equal to fixed point*

> Given a plane $\pi$, a point $P$ in this plane and a point $Q$ not in $\pi$. Find all points $R$ in $\pi$ such that $\frac{QP + PR}{QR}$ is a maximum.
