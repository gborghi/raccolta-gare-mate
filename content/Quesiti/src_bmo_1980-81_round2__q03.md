*Solve symmetric simultaneous equations x^2y^2+x^2z^2=axyz etc.*

> Solve for $x$, $y$, $z$ the simultaneous equations
> $$x^2y^2 + x^2z^2 = axyz, \quad y^2z^2 + y^2x^2 = bxyz, \quad z^2x^2 + z^2y^2 = cxyz,$$
> where $a$, $b$, $c$ are given numbers.
