# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2008 — Nível 3 — Fase 1 — Quesito 15

*Cards: a square design in 5 regions painted with 5 colors; count distinct under rotation*

> Soninha has many cards, all with the same drawing on one of the faces. She is going to use five different colors (green, yellow, blue, red and orange) to paint each one of the five parts of the drawing, each part with a different color, in such a way that no two cards are equal. As shown in the figure below, for example, two cards are considered equal, since one of them can be turned to obtain the other. How many different cards can Soninha produce?
> 
> (A) $16$ (B) $25$ (C) $30$ (D) $60$ (E) $120$
