*Sequence recurrence; prove inequality for n<=m*

> Let $\{a_n\}$ be a sequence such that $a_1 = \dfrac{21}{16}$ and
> $$2a_n - 3a_{n-1} = \frac{3}{2^{n+1}}, \quad n \geq 2.$$
> Let $m$ be a positive integer and $m \geq 2$. Prove that for $n \leq m$,
> $$\left(a_n + \frac{3}{2^{n+3}}\right)^{\frac{1}{m}}\left(m - \left(\frac{2}{3}\right)^{\frac{n(m-1)}{m}}\right) < \frac{m^2 - 1}{m - n + 1}.$$
> (Posed by Zhu Huawei)
