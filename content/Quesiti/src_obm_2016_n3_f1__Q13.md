# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2016 — Nível 3 — Fase 1 — Quesito 13

*Counting ways to form committees over n people with day-by-day restrictions*

> One chooses, on the first day, two of the $n$ people in $\binom{n}{2}$ ways; on subsequent days the remaining $n-2$ people can be chosen in $(n-2)$ ways each (subject to the imposed restrictions), and the final count of ways to form the committees is $(n-2)(n-3)$ (as stated in the solution). Determine the number of ways to choose the committees. [Answer key value corresponds to option A.]
