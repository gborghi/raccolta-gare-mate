*Maximize the sum of nine natural numbers subject to a bound on the sum of their fifth powers.*

> Given a positive integer $n$, determine the maximum possible value of the sum of natural numbers $k_1,k_2,\ldots,k_9$ satisfying
> $$k_1^5+k_2^5+\cdots+k_9^5\le 7n.$$
