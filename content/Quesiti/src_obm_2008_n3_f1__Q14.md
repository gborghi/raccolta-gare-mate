*Probability Bernaldo has no spades given Arnaldo has none*

> Arnaldo, Bernaldo, Cernaldo and Dernaldo shuffle the $52$ cards of a deck and distribute $13$ cards to each. Arnaldo, surprised, says: "How strange, I have no card of spades." What is the probability that Bernaldo also has no cards of spades?
> 
> (A) $\dfrac{39!}{26!\,52!}$ (B) $\dfrac{26!}{13!\,39!}$ (C) $\dfrac{39!\,39!}{26!\,52!}$ (D) $\dfrac{26!\,26!}{13!\,39!}$ (E) $\dfrac{39!\,13!}{52!}$
