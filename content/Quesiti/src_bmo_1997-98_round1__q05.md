*Triangle with midpoint and trisection point; find angle BAC*

> In triangle $ABC$, $D$ is the midpoint of $AB$ and $E$ is the point of trisection of $BC$ nearer to $C$. Given that $\angle ADC = \angle BAE$ find $\angle BAC$.
