# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2001 — Nível Universitário — Fase 2 — Quesito 1

*Perpendiculars from points on a line envelop a parabola*

> Let $O$ be a point and $r$ a line in the plane. For each point $P$ of $r$, let $r_P$ be the perpendicular to $OP$ passing through $P$. Prove that the set $\{r_P \mid P \in r\}$ is the set of all tangent lines to a parabola.
