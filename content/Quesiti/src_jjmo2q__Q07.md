# Japan Junior Mathematical Olympiad 2004 — Quesito 7

*Arrange 8 balls of 3 colors with odd number of red*

> Among the ways to arrange a total of $8$ balls of the three colors red, blue, and yellow in a row, how many are there in which the number of red balls used is odd? Here, assume there is a sufficient supply of balls of each color, and there may be colors that are not used at all.
