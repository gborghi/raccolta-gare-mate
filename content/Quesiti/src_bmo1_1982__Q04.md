*Sequence by recurrence relation; describe behaviour as n tends to infinity*

> A sequence of real numbers $u_1, u_2, u_3, \ldots$ is given by $u_1$ and the recurrence relation $u_n = u_{n-1} + \frac{15}{u_{n-1}},\quad n \ge 2$. By considering the curve $x^2 = y + \frac{15}{y}$, or otherwise, describe with proof the behaviour of $u_n$ as $n$ tends to infinity.
