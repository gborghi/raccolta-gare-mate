# British Mathematical Olympiad 2024-25 — Round 2 — Quesito 4

*Quante successioni con $u_1=1$ e $u_{n+1}=(u_n^2+u_n+1)^{2025}/u_{n-1}$*

> $$u_{n+1}=\frac{(u_n^2+u_n+1)^{2025}}{u_{n-1}}$$
> per ogni $n\ge2$?
