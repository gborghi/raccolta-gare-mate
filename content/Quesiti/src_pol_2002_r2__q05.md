*Triangle ABC with angle BAC=90 is the base of pyramid ABCD with AD=BD and AB=CD; prove angle ACD>=30.*

> Triangle $ABC$ with $\angle BAC = 90^\circ$ is the base of the pyramid $ABCD$. Moreover it holds
> $$AD=BD \quad \text{and} \quad AB=CD.$$
> Prove that $\angle ACD \ge 30^\circ$.
