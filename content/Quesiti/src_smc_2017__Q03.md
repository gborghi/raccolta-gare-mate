*Five integers 2,5,6,9,14 rearranged so sum of first three equals sum of last three; find the middle number.*

> The five integers $2, 5, 6, 9, 14$ are arranged in a different order. In the new arrangement, the sum of the first three integers is equal to the sum of the last three integers.
> 
> What is the middle number in the new arrangement?
>
> - **(A)** $2$
> - **(B)** $5$
> - **(C)** $6$
> - **(D)** $9$
> - **(E)** $14$
