# British Mathematical Olympiad 2004-05 — Round 1 — Quesito 3

*Least n so any 2-colouring of {1,...,n} has monochromatic x+y+z=w*

> Determine the least positive integer $n$ such that the following holds:
> 
> No matter how the elements of $\{1, 2, \ldots, n\}$ are coloured red or blue, there are integers $x$, $y$, $z$, $w$ in $\{1, 2, \ldots, n\}$ (not necessarily distinct) of the same colour such that $x + y + z = w$.
