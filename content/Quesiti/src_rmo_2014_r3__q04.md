*Find all positive reals x,y,z satisfying three cyclic equations*

> Find all positive real numbers $x, y, z$ such that
> $$2x - 2y + \frac{1}{z} = \frac{1}{2014}, \quad 2y - 2z + \frac{1}{x} = \frac{1}{2014}, \quad 2z - 2x + \frac{1}{y} = \frac{1}{2014}.$$
