*Compare a/b and (a+1)/(b+1) given 0 < a/b < 1*

> Let $a$ and $b$ be positive real numbers such that $\dfrac{a}{b} < 1$. Then:
> 
> A) $\dfrac{a+1}{b+1}$ equals $\dfrac{a}{b}$.
> B) $\dfrac{a+1}{b+1}$ equals $\dfrac{a}{b} + 1$.
> C) $\dfrac{a+1}{b+1}$ is less than $\dfrac{a}{b}$.
> D) $\dfrac{a+1}{b+1}$ is greater than $\dfrac{a}{b}$ but less than $1$.
> E) $\dfrac{a+1}{b+1}$ can be less than $\dfrac{a}{b}$.
