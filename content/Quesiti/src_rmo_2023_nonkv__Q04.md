*Two intersecting circles; perpendicular bisectors and collinearity of midpoints*

> Let $\Omega_1, \Omega_2$ be two intersecting circles with centres $O_1, O_2$ respectively. Let $l$ be a line that intersects $\Omega_1$ at points $A, C$ and $\Omega_2$ at points $B, D$ such that $A, B, C, D$ are collinear in that order. Let the perpendicular bisector of segment $AB$ intersect $\Omega_1$ at points $P, Q$, and the perpendicular bisector of segment $CD$ intersect $\Omega_2$ at points $R, S$ such that $P, R$ are on the same side of $l$. Prove that the midpoints of $PR$, $QS$ and $O_1 O_2$ are collinear.
