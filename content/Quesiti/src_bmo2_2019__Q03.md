*Count non-empty subsets of {1,2,...,p-1} with sum divisible by p*

> Let $p$ be an odd prime. How many non-empty subsets of
> $$\{1, 2, 3, \ldots, p-2, p-1\}$$
> have a sum which is divisible by $p$?
