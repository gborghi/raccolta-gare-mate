# Indian National Mathematical Olympiad 2009 — Quesito 6

*For positive reals with a³+b³=c³, prove a²+b²−c²≥6(c−a)(c−b)*

> Let $a$, $b$, $c$ be positive real numbers such that $a^3 + b^3 = c^3$. Prove that
> $$a^2 + b^2 - c^2 \ge 6(c - a)(c - b).$$
