*Max pieces of rectangular cake with 7 straight cuts parallel to sides*

> Suzana has a cake in the shape of a rectangle and wants to cut it into smaller pieces by making 7 straight cuts, each parallel to one of the sides of the rectangle. She only separates the pieces after all cuts are made. What is the maximum number of pieces she can obtain?

> (A) 9 \quad (B) 11 \quad (C) 13 \quad (D) 17 \quad (E) 19
