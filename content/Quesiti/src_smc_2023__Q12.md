*Highest power of 7 dividing 50 factorial*

> The greatest power of $7$ which is a factor of $50!$ is $7^k$ (where $n!=1\times2\times3\times4\times\ldots\times(n-1)\times n$). What is $k$?
>
> - **(A)** $4$
> - **(B)** $5$
> - **(C)** $6$
> - **(D)** $7$
> - **(E)** $8$
