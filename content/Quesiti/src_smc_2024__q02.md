*Number of twips (≈0.0000018 m) in a league (≈4800 m)*

> A *twip* is a very short unit of length, derived from imperial units, and is equal to approximately 0.0000018 metres. A *league* is a long unit of length which is equal to approximately 4800 metres. Roughly how many twips are there in a league?
>
> - **(A)** $270\,000\,000$
> - **(B)** $27\,000\,000$
> - **(C)** $2\,700\,000$
> - **(D)** $270\,000$
> - **(E)** $27\,000$
