# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2002 — Nível Universitário — Fase 1 — Quesito 2

*Find x,y making n×n matrix invertible and compute its inverse*

> Let $A$ be the $n\times n$ matrix
> $$A=\begin{pmatrix} x+y & x & \cdots & x \\ x & x+y & \cdots & x \\ \vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots \\ x & x & \cdots & x+y \end{pmatrix}.$$
> For which values of $x$ and $y$ is the matrix $A$ invertible? Compute $A^{-1}$.
