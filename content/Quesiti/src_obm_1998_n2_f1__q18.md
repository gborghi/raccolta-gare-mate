*Count ways to paint flag with 4 colours, two cannot be adjacent*

> You are going to paint the flag below using 4 colours: blue, green, yellow, and red, one colour per region.
> 
> If red and yellow cannot be adjacent, in how many ways can the flag be painted?
> 
> A) 12
> B) 4
> C) 18
> D) 20
> E) 16
