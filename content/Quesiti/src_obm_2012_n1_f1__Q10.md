*Find length FP after repositioning dividing line to equalize areas*

> João and Maria inherited a plot of land in the shape of polygon $ABCDEF$. A straight line originally separated the land into two unequal areas. They decided to reposition the dividing line, keeping it perpendicular to line $DE$, moving it to a new point $P$, so that the two areas become equal. Given that the angles at $B$, $D$, $E$, and $F$ are right angles, and using the measurements shown in the figure (in metres), find the length $FP$ in metres.
> 
> (A) 5 \quad (B) 10 \quad (C) 12 \quad (D) 20 \quad (E) 20
