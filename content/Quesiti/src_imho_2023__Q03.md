*Infinite sequences of positive integers admitting a polynomial recurrence*

> For each integer $k \ge 2$, determine all infinite sequences of positive integers $a_1, a_2, \ldots$ for which there exists a polynomial $P$ of the form $P(x) = x^k + c_{k-1}x^{k-1} + \cdots + c_1 x + c_0$, where $c_0, c_1, \ldots, c_{k-1}$ are non-negative integers, such that
> $$P(a_n) = a_{n+1} a_{n+2} \cdots a_{n+k}$$
> for every integer $n \ge 1$.
