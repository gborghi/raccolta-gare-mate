*Sum involving reciprocals is always an integer*

> Let $x_1, x_2, \ldots, x_{2023}$ be pairwise different positive real numbers such that
> $$a_n = \sqrt{(x_1 + x_2 + \cdots + x_n)\left(\frac{1}{x_1} + \frac{1}{x_2} + \cdots + \frac{1}{x_n}\right)}$$
> is an integer for every $n = 1, 2, \ldots, 2023$. Prove that $a_{2023} \ge 3034$.
