*Product of all integers in a list of 2019 consecutive integers that sum to 2019*

> Jeroen writes a list of $2019$ consecutive positive integers. The sum of his integers is $2019$.
> 
> What is the product of all the integers in Jeroen's list?
>
> - **(A)** $2019^0$
> - **(B)** $\frac{2019 \times 2020}{2}$
> - **(C)** $2^{2019}$
> - **(D)** $2019$
> - **(E)** $0$
