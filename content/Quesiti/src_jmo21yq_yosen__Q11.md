# Japan Mathematical Olympiad 2011 — Yosen (Preliminary) — Quesito 11

*OM:ON for a tangential quadrilateral with incenter O*

> Quadrilateral $ABCD$ is circumscribed about a circle centered at $O$ (the circle is tangent to all four sides), with $OA = 5$, $OB = 6$, $OC = 7$, $OD = 8$. Let $M$ be the midpoint of segment $AC$ and $N$ be the midpoint of segment $BD$. Find $OM : ON$. Here, $XY$ denotes the length of segment $XY$.
