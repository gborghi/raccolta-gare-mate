*Find point on triangle perimeter minimising sum of distances to vertices*

> Triangle $ABC$ has angles $60^\circ$, $75^\circ$, $45^\circ$ at $A$, $B$, $C$ respectively. A point $P$ on the perimeter of the triangle is such that
> $$AP + BP + CP$$
> is minimised. Find the position of $P$.
