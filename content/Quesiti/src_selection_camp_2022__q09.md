*Find all real functions with f(-1)=-1 satisfying a functional equation*

> Find all functions $f : \mathbb{R} \to \mathbb{R}$ such that for all real numbers $x, y$,
> $$f(x)f(y) - f(x+y) = yf(x) - f(x+f(y))$$
> and $f(-1) = -1$.
