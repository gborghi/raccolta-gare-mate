*If (AB)^k = A^k B^k for three consecutive integers then AB=BA*

> Let $A$ and $B$ be real $n \times n$ invertible matrices. Show that if the condition $(AB)^k = A^k B^k$ holds for three consecutive integer values of $k$, then $AB = BA$.
