*Two-digit numbers equal to their digit-reversal image sum being a perfect square*

> We call the \emph{image} of a two-digit natural number the number obtained by reversing the order of its digits. For example, the image of $34$ is $43$. Which two-digit numbers, when added to their image, give a perfect square?
