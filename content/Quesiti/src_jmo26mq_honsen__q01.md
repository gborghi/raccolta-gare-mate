*Sum of counts of divisors of kp+1 in [k,p) over k=1..p-1*

> Let $p$ be a prime number. For each integer $k$ with $1 \le k \le p-1$, let $a_k$ be the number of divisors of $kp+1$ that are greater than or equal to $k$ and less than $p$. Find the value of $a_1 + a_2 + \cdots + a_{p-1}$.
