*Point P on CD; shaded region 1/3 of rectangle; find PC*

> In the figure, $P$ is a point on side $CD$ of rectangle $ABCD$ and $Q$ is the intersection of the diagonals of rectangle $ABCP$. The shaded region is the part common to rectangle $ABCD$ and triangle $ADP$.
> 
> If $AB = 5\text{ cm}$, $AD = 8\text{ cm}$, and the shaded area equals $\dfrac{1}{3}$ of the area of the rectangle, what is the distance $PC$?
> 
> **(A)** $1$ cm $\quad$ **(B)** $2$ cm $\quad$ **(C)** $3$ cm $\quad$ **(D)** $4$ cm $\quad$ **(E)** $5$ cm

*Punto P sul CD; regione ombreggiata 1/3 del rettangolo; trovare PC*

> 
> Se $AB = 5\text{ cm}$, $AD = 8\text{ cm}$, e la superficie ombrata è uguale a $\dfrac{1}{3}$ della superficie del rettangolo, qual è la distanza $PC$?
> 
> **(A)** $1$ cm $\quad$ **(B)** $2$ cm $\quad$ **(C)** $3$ cm $\quad$ **(D)** $4$ cm $\quad$ **(E)** $5$ cm
