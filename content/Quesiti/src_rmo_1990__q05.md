*Point P inside triangle ABC with perimeter 2s: prove s < AP+BP+CP < 2s*

> $P$ is any point inside a triangle $ABC$. The perimeter of the triangle $AB + BC + CA = 2s$. Prove that
> $$s < AP + BP + CP < 2s.$$
