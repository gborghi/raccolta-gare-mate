*Ratio of red painted area to unpainted white area when a painted cube is cut into 27 cubes*

> A wooden cube, painted red, was sawn into $27$ equal smaller cubes, whose faces that were inside are still not painted (the inner faces stay white). What is the ratio between the total painted (red) area and the area of the unpainted (white) surface?
> 
> (A) 1:2 (B) 1:1 (C) 2:1 (D) 1:3 (E) 2:3
