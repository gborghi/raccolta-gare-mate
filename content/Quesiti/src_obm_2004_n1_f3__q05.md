*Determine if a 13-sided polygon can tile the plane by translation copies*

> Consider the $6$-sided polygon $\boldsymbol{P}$ shown in the figure.
> 
> With copies of $\boldsymbol{P}$ we can tile the entire plane without overlaps, as shown in the figure.
> 
> Is there a $13$-sided polygon with which it is possible to tile the entire plane using its copies without overlaps? If yes, exhibit such a polygon. If not, explain why not.
