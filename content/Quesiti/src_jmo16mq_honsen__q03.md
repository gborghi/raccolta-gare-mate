*Find all real functions satisfying a given functional equation*

> Find all functions $f : \mathbb{R} \to \mathbb{R}$ defined on the reals such that for all real numbers $x, y$,
> $$f(x)^2 + 2y\,f(x) + f(y) = f\bigl(y + f(x)\bigr).$$
