*Three distinct integers making x+n, x+m, x+l a geometric progression forces x rational*

> Hadriel has chosen a real number $x$. He found three pairwise distinct positive integers $n$, $m$ and $l$ such that the numbers $x+n$, $x+m$ and $x+l$ are in geometric progression. Show that $x$ is a rational number, that is to say that it is written in the form $\frac{a}{b}$ where $a$ and $b$ are integers with $b \neq 0$.
> 
> *We say that real numbers $a$, $b$, $c$ are in geometric progression if there exists a non-zero real number $k$ such that $c = kb = k^2 a$.*

> Diciamo che i numeri reali $a$, $b$, $c$ sono in progressione geometrica se esiste un numero reale non zero $k$ tale che $c = kb = k^2 a$.*
