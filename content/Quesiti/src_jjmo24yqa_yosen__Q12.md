# Japan Junior Mathematical Olympiad 2026 — Yosen (Preliminary) — Quesito 12

*Square ABCD in circle; parallel lines through B and C intersect; find EQ*

> Square $ABCD$ with side length $AB = 4$ is inscribed in a circle. The line through $B$ parallel to diagonal $AD$ meets segment $AC$ at point $E$, with $BE = 5$. The line through $C$ parallel to diagonal $AD$ meets lines $AB$ and $BC$ at points $P$ and $Q$ respectively, with $BP = BQ = 6$. Find the length of segment $EQ$. (Here $XY$ denotes the length of segment $XY$.)
