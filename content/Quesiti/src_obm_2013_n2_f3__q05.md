*Scalene triangle, median AM, circle of diameter AM meets AB and AC; find angle BAC*

> Let $ABC$ be a scalene triangle and $AM$ the median relative to side $BC$. The circle with diameter $AM$ intersects the sides $AB$ and $AC$ again at points $P$ and $Q$, respectively, both different from $A$. Suppose that $PQ$ is parallel to $BC$. Determine the measure of angle $\angle BAC$.
