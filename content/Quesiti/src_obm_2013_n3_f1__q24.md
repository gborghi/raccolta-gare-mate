*Max real solutions of p(x)=k for an even polynomial with non-negative coefficients*

> A polynomial $p(x)$ is even when $p(-x) = p(x)$ for all real $x$. What is the maximum number of real solutions of the equation $p(x) = k$, where $p$ is a non-constant even polynomial with non-negative coefficients and $k$ is a fixed real?
> 
> (A) $0$ \quad (B) $1$ \quad (C) $2$ \quad (D) $4$ \quad (E) $n$, where $n$ is the degree of $p(x)$
