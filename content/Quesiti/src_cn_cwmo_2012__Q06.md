*Find integer k where sequence crosses 1*

> Sequence $\{a_n\}$ is defined by $a_0 = \dfrac{1}{2}$, $a_{n+1} = a_n + \dfrac{1}{2012}a_n^2$, $n = 0, 1, 2, \ldots$. Find integer $k$ such that $a_k < 1 < a_{k+1}$. (posed by Bian Hongping)
