# British Mathematical Olympiad 2013-14 — Round 1 — Quesito 6

*Prove weighted angle average lies between 60 and largest angle*

> The angles $A$, $B$ and $C$ of a triangle are measured in degrees, and $A$, $B$ and $C$ are positive integers with $A + B + C = 180$. The lengths of the opposite sides are $a$, $b$ and $c$ respectively. Prove that
> $$60 \leq \frac{aA + bB + cC}{a + b + c} \leq C$$
> where $C$ is the largest angle and $c$ is the corresponding longest side.
