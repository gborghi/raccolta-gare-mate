*Point on angle bisector; given area of one sub-triangle, find area of other*

> In triangle $ABC$, $AB = 5$ and $AC = 6$. Let $P$ be a point on the bisector of angle $\widehat{BAC}$. The area of triangle $APB$ is $\frac{5}{2}$. What is the area of triangle $APC$?

> (A) $\frac{5}{4}$ $\quad$ (B) $\frac{9}{4}$ $\quad$ (C) $\frac{23}{4}$ $\quad$ (D) $\frac{25}{4}$ $\quad$ (E) $\frac{4}{5}$
