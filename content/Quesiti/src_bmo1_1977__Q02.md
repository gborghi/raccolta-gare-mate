*Midpoint of BC collinear with midpoints of BX and CY for incircle contact*

> The sides $BC$, $CA$, $AB$ of a triangle touch a circle at $X$, $Y$, $Z$ respectively. Prove that the midpoint of $BC$ lies on the straight line through the midpoints of $BX$ and $CY$.
