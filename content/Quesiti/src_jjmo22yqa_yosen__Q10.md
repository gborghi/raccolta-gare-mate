*Find AB given triangle ABC with BC=16, D,E on BC, DF parallel AC, EF parallel AB, F on circumcircle, circumcircle of DEF tangent to AB*

> In triangle $ABC$ with $BC = 16$, points $D$ and $E$ lie on side $BC$ with $BD = 1$ and $EC = 7$. Let $F$ be the intersection of the line through $D$ parallel to $AC$ and the line through $E$ parallel to $AB$. It is given that $F$ lies on the circumcircle of triangle $ABC$. When the circumcircle of triangle $DEF$ is tangent to side $AB$, find the length $AB$. (Here $XY$ denotes the length of segment $XY$.)
