*Volume of solid defined by floor sum condition*

> Let $N$ be a positive integer. Compute, as a function of $N$, the volume of the solid defined by
> $$x,\, y,\, z \in [0, +\infty), \quad \lfloor x \rfloor + \lfloor y \rfloor + \lfloor z \rfloor \le N.$$
