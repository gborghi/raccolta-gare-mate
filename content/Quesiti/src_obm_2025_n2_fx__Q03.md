# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2025 — Nível 2 — Fase x — Quesito 3

*Prove quadrilateral KDMR is cyclic in acute triangle with orthocenter*

> Let $ABC$ be an acute triangle with $AB < AC$. Let $D$, $E$, $F$ be the feet of the altitudes from $A$, $B$, $C$ respectively, and let $M$ be the midpoint of $BC$. Let $H$ be the orthocenter of $ABC$. Let $P$ be the intersection of lines $EF$ and $BC$. The line $PH$ meets the circle with diameter $AH$ again at point $J$. Let $R$ be the reflection of $A$ over $BC$. The lines $JD$ and $PR$ meet at $K$. Prove that the quadrilateral $KDMR$ is cyclic.
