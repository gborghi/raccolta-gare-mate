# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2015 — Nível 3 — Fase 1 — Quesito 14

*Max subset of dodecahedron edges that are pairwise skew*

> Two line segments in space are called \emph{skew} (reversas) when there is no plane containing both. A regular dodecahedron is a polyhedron with twelve regular pentagonal faces.
> 
> What is the largest number of elements in a subset $S$ of edges of a regular dodecahedron such that any two elements of $S$ are skew?
> 
> (A) 3 \quad (B) 6 \quad (C) 9 \quad (D) 12 \quad (E) 15
