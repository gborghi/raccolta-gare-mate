*For n>1 let k(n) be the max perfect-power exponent; find limit of Cesaro mean*

> For each integer $n > 1$, let $k(n)$ be the largest positive integer $k$ such that $n = m^k$ for some positive integer $m$. Determine
> $$\lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{1}{n} \sum_{j=2}^{n} k(j).$$
