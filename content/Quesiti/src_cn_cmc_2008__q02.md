*Range of parameter a so that B is subset of A*

> Let $A = [-2, 4)$, $B = \{x \mid x^2 - ax - 4 < 0\}$. If $B \subseteq A$, then the range of $a$ is (  ).
> 
> (A) $[-1, 2)$ \quad (B) $[1, 2]$ \quad (C) $[0, 3]$ \quad (D) $[0, 3)$
