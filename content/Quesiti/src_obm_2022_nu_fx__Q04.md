*Convergence of series involving iterated exponential recurrence*

> Given $c, a > 0$, consider the sequence $(x_n)_{n \ge 1}$ defined by $x_1 = c$ and
> $$x_{n+1} = x_n e^{-x_n^a}, \quad n \ge 1.$$
> For which real values of $\beta$ is the series $\displaystyle\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} x_n^{\beta}$ convergent?
