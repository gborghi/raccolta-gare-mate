*Find q-p where sum of 1/i(i+1)(i+2) from 1 to 10 equals p/q in lowest terms*

> If the sum $\sum_{i=1}^{10} \frac{1}{i(i+1)(i+2)}$ is written in its lowest terms as $\frac{p}{q}$, find the value of $q - p$.
