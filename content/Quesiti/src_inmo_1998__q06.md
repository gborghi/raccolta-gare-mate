*Choose n integers from 0..2n so average is integer, find minimum n*

> We want to choose $n$ of the $2n$ integers $0, 1, 2, \ldots, 2n-1$ such that the average of the $n$ chosen integers is an integer and as small as possible. Show that this can be done for each positive integer $n$ and find this smallest value.
