*Positive reals a,b,c; prove cyclic inequality*

> For positive real numbers $a$, $b$, $c$, prove that
> $$(a^2 + b^2)^2(b + c)(c + a) \ge (a + b)^2(a^2 b + b^2 c + c^2 a - a^2 c).$$
