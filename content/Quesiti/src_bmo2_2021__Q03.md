*AI and KD meet on circumcircle via right-angle point K*

> Let $ABC$ be a triangle with $AB > AC$. Its circumcircle is $\Gamma$ and its incentre is $I$. Let $D$ be the contact point of the incircle of $ABC$ with $BC$.
> 
> Let $K$ be the point on $\Gamma$ such that $\angle AKI$ is a right angle.
> 
> Prove that $AI$ and $KD$ meet on $\Gamma$.
