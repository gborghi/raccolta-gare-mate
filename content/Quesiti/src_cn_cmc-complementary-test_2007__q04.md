*Acute triangle with foot of altitude; circumcenters concyclic with feet iff P is orthocenter*

> As shown in the figure, $\triangle ABC$ is an acute triangle with $AB < AC$. $AD$ is the perpendicular height on $BC$ with point $P$ along $AD$. Through $P$ draw $PE \perp AC$ with $E$ as foot drop, draw $PF \perp AB$ with $F$ as foot drop. $O_1$, $O_2$ are circumcenters of $\triangle BDF$, $\triangle CDE$ respectively. Prove that $O_1$, $O_2$, $E$, $F$ are concyclic if and only if $P$ is the orthocenter of $\triangle ABC$.
