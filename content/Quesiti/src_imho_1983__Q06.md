*Prove inequality involving sides of a triangle*

> Let $a$, $b$ and $c$ be the lengths of the sides of a triangle. Prove that
> $$a^2 b(a - b) + b^2 c(b - c) + c^2 a(c - a) \ge 0.$$
> Determine when equality occurs.
