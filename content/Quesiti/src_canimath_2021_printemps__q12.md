*Count 3-colorings (blue/green/red) of 1..2021 with no two consecutive same color*

> In how many ways can one color the integers from $1$ to $2021$ so that each integer is colored either blue, green, or red, and so that two consecutive integers are never of the same color?

**Topic:** [[topic_combinatoria|Combinatoria]]
**Metodo:** [[method_ricorsione|Ricorsione]], [[method_conteggio|Conteggio]]
**Abilita:** [[skill_conteggio_sistematico|Conteggio sistematico]], [[skill_riconoscimento_pattern|Riconoscimento di pattern]]
**Area:** [[Combinatoria, Logica e Probabilita]]
**Risposta:** $3\times 2^{2020}$
**Fonte:** [apri PDF](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1HT9T8KwtrtEAIUZX2Qh9AX2VGVFmNAVg/view)
