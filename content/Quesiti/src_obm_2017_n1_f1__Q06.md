# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2017 — Nível 1 — Fase 1 — Quesito 6

*Determinant of Wronskian-type matrix of monomials*

> Let $k_1, k_2, \ldots, k_n$ be non-negative integers. Let $M$ be the $n \times n$ matrix with entries
> $$M_{i1} = t^{k_i}, \qquad M_{i,j+1} = \frac{d}{dt} M_{ij}.$$
> Prove that there exist constants $C$ and $r$ such that $\det(M) = C t^r$. Find simple formulas for $C$ and $r$.
