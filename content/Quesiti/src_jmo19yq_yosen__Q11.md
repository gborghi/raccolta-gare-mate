*Sum of solutions of a floor-function equation*

> Find the sum of all solutions of the following equation in the real variable $x$: $$[x]+[2x]+[3x]+[4x]+[5x]+[6x]+[7x]+[8x]+[9x]=44x.$$ Here $[r]$ denotes the greatest integer not exceeding the real number $r$.
