# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2016 — Nível 2 — Fase 2 — Quesito 9

*Real solutions of iterated quadratic p(p(x))=x^2 and p(p(x))=p(x)*

> Consider the degree-two trinomial $p(x) = x^2 - x + 1$.
> 
> a) Determine the number of distinct real solutions of the equation $p(p(x)) = x^2$, that is,
> $$(x^2 - x + 1)^2 - (x^2 - x + 1) + 1 = x^2.$$
> 
> b) Determine the number of distinct real solutions of the equation
> $$p(p(x)) = p(x).$$
