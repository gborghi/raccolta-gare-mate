*Jemima takes n tests; after (n-2) tests average was m; full marks in test n-1 raised average by 4; full marks in test n raised average by 3; find n.*

> Jemima takes a series of $n$ tests each with the same maximum mark. After $(n-2)$ tests, her average score was $m$. She scored full marks in the $(n-1)$th test, and so raised her average score by 4 marks. In the $n$th test she again scored full marks and increased her average score by 3 marks. How many tests did Jemima take?
>
> - **(A)** 6
> - **(B)** 7
> - **(C)** 8
> - **(D)** 9
> - **(E)** 10
