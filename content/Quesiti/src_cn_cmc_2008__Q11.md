*Find f(2008) given f(0)=2008 and two functional inequalities*

> Suppose that $f(x)$ is defined on $\mathbf{R}$, satisfying $f(0) = 2008$, and for any $x \in \mathbf{R}$:
> $$f(x+2) - f(x) \le 3 \times 2^x,$$
> $$f(x+6) - f(x) \ge 63 \times 2^x.$$
> Then $f(2008) = $ ________.
