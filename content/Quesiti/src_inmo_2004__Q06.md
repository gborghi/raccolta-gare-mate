# Indian National Mathematical Olympiad 2004 — Quesito 6

*Count 5-tuples satisfying reciprocal-sum equation; prove the count is odd*

> Prove that the number of $5$-tuples of positive integers $(a, b, c, d, e)$ satisfying the equation
> $$abcde = 5(bcde + acde + abde + abce + abcd)$$
> is an **odd** integer.
