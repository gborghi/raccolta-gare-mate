*Prime p>=5; compute residue of a weighted floor sum modulo p*

> Let $p \ge 5$ be a prime number, $M = \{1, 2, \ldots, p-1\}$. Define
> $$T = \{(n, x_n) : p \mid (nx_n - 1) \text{ with } n, x_n \in M\}.$$
> Find the nonnegative least residue of $\displaystyle\sum_{(n,x_n)\in T} n\left[\frac{nx_n}{p}\right]$ modulo $p$, where $[x]$ denotes the greatest integer less than or equal to $x$.
