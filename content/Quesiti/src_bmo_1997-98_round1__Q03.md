# British Mathematical Olympiad 1997-98 — Round 1 — Quesito 3

*Parallelogram ABCD; PC meets diagonal; show M is midpoint of DA*

> $ABCD$ is a parallelogram and $P$ is a point on side $BC$. The line $PC$ meets the line $AB$ at $M$. (You may assume that the unique line $AB$ does not pass through $D$ or $M$.) The parallelogram $ABCD$; $PC$ meets $AB$ at $M$; $M$ is the midpoint of $DA$.
> 
> Show that $M$ is the midpoint of $DA$.
