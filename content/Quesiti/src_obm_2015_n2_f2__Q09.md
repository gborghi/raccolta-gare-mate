# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2015 — Nível 2 — Fase 2 — Quesito 9

*Measure of angle EOF in right triangle ABC with altitudes and circumcenter*

> In a right triangle $ABC$, the angle at $A$ is $45^\circ$. Let $BE$ and $CF$ be the altitudes from $B$ and $C$ to the sides $AC$ and $AB$ respectively, $E$ on $AC$ and $F$ on $AB$, and let $O$ be the circumcenter of triangle $ABC$ (i.e., the center of the circle passing through $A$, $B$, and $C$). Calculate the measure of angle $EOF$.
