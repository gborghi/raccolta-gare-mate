*Find CD in cyclic quadrilateral with diagonal intersection and collinear points*

> Quadrilateral $ABCD$ is inscribed in a circle with $AB = 7$ and $BC = 6$. Let $E$ be the intersection of diagonals $AC$ and $BD$. Line $BE$ meets the circle again at point $F$. The three points $A$, $D$, $F$ are collinear, and $AF = 11$, $DF = 7$. Find the length of segment $CD$. (Here $XY$ denotes the length of segment $XY$.)
