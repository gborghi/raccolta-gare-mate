*Convex hexagon with AB=BC; reflections of A,B,C across line l; circumcircles meet at E; BB1 and DE meet on circumcircle*

> Let $ABCC_1B_1A_1$ be a convex hexagon satisfying $AB = BC$. Let $A_1$, $B_1$, $C_1$ be the reflections of $A$, $B$, $C$ respectively across a line $\ell$. Let $D$ be the intersection of segments $AC_1$ and $A_1C$, and let $\omega$ be the circumcircle of triangle $ABC$. Suppose $\omega$ and the circumcircle of triangle $A_1BC_1$ meet at a point $E$ other than $B$. Prove that line $BB_1$ and line $DE$ meet on $\omega$.
