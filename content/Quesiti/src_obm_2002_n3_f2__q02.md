*Find reals a,b,c from three symmetric product equations*

> Let $a$, $b$, $c$ be real numbers such that
> $$ab(a+b+c) = 1001, \quad bc(a+b+c) = 2002, \quad ca(a+b+c) = 3003.$$
> Find $a$, $b$, and $c$.
