*Committee of 4 with 4 positions; each member assigned 2 distinct positions; count assignments*

> A student committee has $4$ members and $4$ positions (roles). Each committee member is assigned exactly $2$ of the $4$ positions. Different members must be assigned different pairs of positions (no two members hold the same set of two positions). Each member is happy to be assigned any pair of positions. In how many ways can all $4$ positions be distributed among the $4$ members so that every member receives exactly $2$ positions and no two members receive the same pair?
