*Numbers 1 to N in a circle; find max and min of sum of neighbour differences*

> $N$ numbers $1, 2, 3, \ldots, N$ are arranged in a circle. For each pair of neighbouring dwarfs the positive difference between the numbers is calculated, and the sum of these $N$ differences is called $V$.
> 
> Find (with proof) the maximum and minimum possible values of $V$.
