*Area quadrilatero EFGH da triangoli isosceli esterni*

> Si consideri il quadrato ABCD di lato 24. Esterni al quadrato si costruiscano
> i triangoli isosceli AEB, CGD di lato 13 e basi AB e CD, e i triangoli isosceli
> BFC, DHA di lato 15 e basi BC, DA.
> Quanto vale l’area del quadrilatero
> EFGH?
>
> - **(A)** 357
> - **(B)** 714
> - **(C)** 912
> - **(D)** 952
> - **(E)** 1428.

*Quadrilateral area of EFGH from external isosceles triangles*

> Consider the ABCD square on side 24. External to the square are the isosceles triangles AEB, CGD on side 13 and bases AB and CD, and the isosceles triangles BFC, DHA on side 15 and bases BC, DA. How much is the EFGH quadrilateral area?
>
> - **(A)** 357
> - **(B)** 714
> - **(C)** 912
> - **(D)** 952
> - **(E)** 1428.
