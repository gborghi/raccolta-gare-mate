*Non-constant polynomial with positive coefficients: prove P(-2015)=0 impossible*

> Let $P(x)$ be a non-constant polynomial whose coefficients are positive integers. If $P(n)$ divides $P(P(n) - 2015)$ for every positive natural number $n$, prove that $P(-2015) = 0$.
