*Nonnegative reals a,b with a^3+a<b-b^3; order a,b*

> The numbers $a$ and $b$ are nonnegative reals such that $a^3 + a < b - b^3$. Then
>
> - **(A)** $b < a < 1$
> - **(B)** $a = b = 1$
> - **(C)** $a < 1 < b$
> - **(D)** $a < b < 1$
> - **(E)** $1 < a < b$
