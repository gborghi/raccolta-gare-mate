*Prove ratio and angle equalities for two externally tangent circles*

> As shown in the figure, circles $\Gamma_1$ and $\Gamma_2$ are tangent externally at point $T$. Points $A$ and $E$ are on circle $\Gamma_1$, lines $AB$ and $DE$ are tangent to circle $\Gamma_2$ at points $B$ and $D$, respectively. Lines $AE$ and $BD$ meet at point $P$. Prove that
> (1) $\dfrac{AB}{AT} = \dfrac{ED}{ET}$;
> (2) $\angle ATP + \angle ETP = 180^\circ$.
> (posed by Xiong Bin)
