# British Mathematical Olympiad 2013-14 — Round 2 — Quesito 2

*Prove no cuboid has volume, surface area and perimeter numerically equal*

> Prove that it is impossible to have a cuboid for which the volume, the surface area and the perimeter are numerically equal.
> 
> The perimeter of a cuboid is the sum of the lengths of all its twelve edges.
