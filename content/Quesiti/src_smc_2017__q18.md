*Arithmetic mean A and geometric mean G of positive x>y satisfy A:G=5:4. Find x:y.*

> The arithmetic mean, $A$, of any two positive numbers $x$ and $y$ is defined as $A = \tfrac{1}{2}(x + y)$ and their geometric mean, $G$, is defined as $G = \sqrt{xy}$.
> 
> For two particular values $x$ and $y$, with $x > y$, the ratio $A : G = 5 : 4$.
> 
> For these values of $x$ and $y$, what is the ratio $x : y$?
>
> - **(A)** $5 : 4$
> - **(B)** $2 : 1$
> - **(C)** $5 : 2$
> - **(D)** $7 : 2$
> - **(E)** $4 : 1$
