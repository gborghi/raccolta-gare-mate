*Functional equation f(x) + f(1/(1-x)) = 24x; find f(3)*

> A function $f$ satisfies the equation $f(x) + f\!\left(\dfrac{1}{1-x}\right) = 24x$ for all real values of $x$ except $x = 0$ and $x = 1$. What is the value of $f(3)$?
>
> - **(A)** $40$
> - **(B)** $42$
> - **(C)** $45$
> - **(D)** $48$
> - **(E)** $50$
