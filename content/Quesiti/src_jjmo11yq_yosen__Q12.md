# Japan Junior Mathematical Olympiad 2013 — Yosen (Preliminary) — Quesito 12

*Perimeter of union of 2013 unit circles*

> In the plane there is a circle of radius less than $1$. On the circumference of this circle, $2013$ mutually distinct points are taken, and a circle of radius $1$ centered at each of them is drawn. The boundary of the region consisting of all points contained in every one of these $2013$ unit circles had length $1$. Find the length of the boundary of the region consisting of all points contained in at least one of these $2013$ unit circles.
