*Find all primes p for which 2003 has a base-p digit sum of 15*

> Let $p$ be a prime and $m$ a positive integer. Suppose the $m$ digits $a_{m-1}, a_{m-2}, \ldots, a_0$ in base $p$ (each satisfying $0 \le a_i \le p - 1$) satisfy
> $$\begin{cases} a_{m-1}\,p^{m-1} + a_{m-2}\,p^{m-2} + \cdots + a_0 = 2003, \\ a_{m-1} + a_{m-2} + \cdots + a_0 = 15. \end{cases}$$
> Find all primes $p$ for which such digits exist.
