*Minimum angle BAC given AB=4 and median AM=1*

> In triangle $ABC$, let $M$ be the midpoint of side $BC$, with $AB=4$ and $AM=1$. Find the smallest possible value of the angle $\angle BAC$. Here $XY$ denotes the length of segment $XY$.
