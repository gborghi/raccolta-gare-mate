*Sequence in columns: under which letter does 2000 appear*

> The positive integers are arranged in columns as shown below, starting from letter $A$:
> 
> $$\begin{array}{ccccccccc}
> A & B & C & D & E & F & G & H & I \\
> 1 & & 2 & & 3 & & & & 5 \\
> & 9 & & & & 7 & & 6 & \\
> & & 11 & 12 & & & 13 & & 14 \\
> 19 & & & 17 & & 16 & & 15 & \\
> & 18 & & & & & & & \\
> \end{array}$$
> 
> Below which letter does the number $2000$ appear?
> 
> (A) $F$ \quad (B) $B$ \quad (C) $C$ \quad (D) $I$ \quad (E) $A$
