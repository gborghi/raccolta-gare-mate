*Find all positive pairs (x,y) satisfying x^2+y^2=a^2+b^2 and x^3+y^3=a^3+b^3*

> Find all pairs of positive numbers $(x, y)$ which satisfy the system of equations
> $$x^2 + y^2 = a^2 + b^2,$$
> $$x^3 + y^3 = a^3 + b^3,$$
> where $a$ and $b$ are given positive numbers.
