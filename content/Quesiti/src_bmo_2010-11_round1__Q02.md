# British Mathematical Olympiad 2010-11 — Round 1 — Quesito 2

*Cube side a with square hole; volume equals surface area numerically*

> Let $a$ be an integer greater than $1$. A solid cube of side $a$ has a square hole of side $s$ drilled directly through from one face to the opposite face (so the drill removes a cuboid). The volume of the remaining solid is numerically equal to the total surface area of the remaining solid. Determine all possible values of $a$.
