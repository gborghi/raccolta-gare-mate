# British Mathematical Olympiad 1997-98 — Round 2 — Quesito 4

*Simultaneous symmetric equations in positive reals; find and prove uniqueness of solution*

> Find a solution of the simultaneous equations
> $$xy + yz + zx = 12$$
> $$xyz = 2 + x + y + z$$
> in which all of $x, y, z$ are positive, and prove that it is the only such solution.
> 
> Show that a solution exists in which $x, y, z$ are real and positive.
