*Prove inequality (1+a)(1+b)(1+c) >= 8(1-a)(1-b)(1-c)*

> If $a$, $b$ and $c$ are positive real numbers such that $a + b + c = 1$, prove that
> $$(1+a)(1+b)(1+c) \ge 8(1-a)(1-b)(1-c).$$
