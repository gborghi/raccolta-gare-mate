# British Mathematical Olympiad 2007-08 — Round 1 — Quesito 4

*Set of 256 integers from 1 to 2007; show two elements sum to multiple of 2007*

> Show that there is a set $S$ of $256$ distinct integers chosen from $\{1, 2, 3, \ldots, 2007\}$ such that no two elements of $S$ differ by at most $1$. Also show that for any such set $S$, there exist two distinct elements $a$, $b$ in $S$ such that $a + b$ is divisible by $2007$.
> 
> Note: $2007 = 9 \times 223$.
