*Points D,E on the hypotenuse with angle DAE=45 deg; circumcircle of ADE meets the legs at P,Q; prove BP+CQ=PQ.*

> Points $D$ and $E$ lie on the hypotenuse $BC$ of an isosceles right triangle $ABC$ such that $\angle DAE = 45^\circ$. The circumcircle of triangle $ADE$ meets the sides $AB$ and $AC$ again at $P$ and $Q$ respectively. Prove that $BP + CQ = PQ$.
