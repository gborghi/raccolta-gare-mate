*Count n-digit wave numbers; find f(10) and f(2008) mod 13*

> Let $a$ be an $n$-digit integer $a_1 a_2 \cdots a_n$ (a natural number) that satisfies the following conditions:
> 
> (i) $a_i \in \{1, 2, 3, 4\}$, and $a_i \ne a_{i+1}$, $i = 1, 2, \ldots, n-1$.
> 
> (ii) When $n \ge 3$, the numbers $a_i - a_{i+1}$ and $a_{i+1} - a_{i+2}$ have opposite signs, $i = 1, 2, \ldots, n-2$.
> 
> Let $f(n)$ denote the number of such $n$-digit integers. Find
> 
> (1) the value of $f(10)$;
> 
> (2) the remainder of $f(2008)$ divided by $13$.
