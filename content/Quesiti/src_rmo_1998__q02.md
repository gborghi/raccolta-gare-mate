*6 divides sum of squares of primes greater than 5 implies 6 divides n*

> Let $n$ be a positive integer and $p_1, p_2, \ldots, p_n$ be $n$ prime numbers all larger than $5$ such that $6$ divides $p_1^2 + p_2^2 + \cdots + p_n^2$. Prove that $6$ divides $n$.
