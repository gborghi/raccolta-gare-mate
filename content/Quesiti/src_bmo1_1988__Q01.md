*Find all real solutions of nested square root equation*

> Find all the real solutions $x$ of the equation
> $$\sqrt{x + 1972098 - \frac{1986}{x + 9860491}} + \sqrt{x + 1974083 - \frac{1988}{x + 986049}} = 1,$$
> where $\sqrt{\phantom{x}}$ indicates the non-negative square root.
