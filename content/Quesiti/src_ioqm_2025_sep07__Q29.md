# Indian Olympiad Qualifier in Mathematics (IOQM) 2025 — Sep 07 — Quesito 29

*Regular n-gon colourful iff 6 colours allow every 5 consecutive vertices different; largest non-colourful n*

> A regular polygon with $n \ge 5$ vertices is said to be colourful if it is possible to colour the vertices using at most 6 colours such that each vertex is coloured with exactly one colour, and such that any 5 consecutive vertices have different colours. Find the largest number $n$ for which a regular polygon with $n$ vertices is \textbf{not} colourful.
