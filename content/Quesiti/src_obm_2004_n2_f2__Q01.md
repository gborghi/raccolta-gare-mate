*Find digit sum a+b+c from a long multiplication*

> $$\begin{array}{r} 1\;a\;b \\ \times\quad b\;3 \\ \hline * * * \\ * * * \\ \hline 1\;c\;c\;0\;1 \end{array}$$
> Find $a + b + c$.
