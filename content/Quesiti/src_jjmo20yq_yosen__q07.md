*Count 5-tuples of non-negative integers summing to 2022 with none divisible by 3*

> Find the number of 5-tuples $(a, b, c, d, e)$ of non-negative integers satisfying $a + b + c + d + e = 2022$ such that none of $a, b, c, d, e$ is divisible by $3$. (The integer $0$ is not considered divisible by $3$ for the purposes of this problem.)
