# Japan IMO Selection Camp 2021 — Selection Camp — Quesito 12

*Functional equation on the integers using iterates of f*

> Find all functions $f$ defined on the integers and taking integer values such that, for all integers $a$ and $b$,
> $$f^{\,a^2 + b^2}(a + b) = a f(a) + b f(b).$$
> Here $f^0(n) = n$, and for a positive integer $k$, $f^k(n) = f\bigl(f^{k-1}(n)\bigr)$.
