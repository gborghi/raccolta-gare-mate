*Find k such that 16! divided by 2^k is an odd integer*

> The number $16! \div 2^k$ is an odd integer. Note that $n! = 1 \times 2 \times 3 \times \cdots \times (n-1) \times n$. What is the value of $k$?
>
> - **(A)** $9$
> - **(B)** $11$
> - **(C)** $13$
> - **(D)** $15$
> - **(E)** $17$
