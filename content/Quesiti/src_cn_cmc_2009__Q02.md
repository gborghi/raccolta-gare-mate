*Range of x-coordinate of point on line forming 45-degree angle with circle*

> Given the line $L: x + y - 9 = 0$ and the circle $M: 2x^2 + 2y^2 - 8x - 8y - 1 = 0$, point $A$ is on $L$ and points $B$, $C$ are on the circle $M$. If $\angle BAC = 45^\circ$ and the lines $AB$, $AC$ are tangent to circle $M$ from point $A$, then the range of the $x$-coordinate of point $A$ is \_\_\_\_.
