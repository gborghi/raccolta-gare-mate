*Integer-coefficient cubic nearly vanishing at 2+sqrt(3); is it a root?*

> Let $a$, $b$, $c$ be integers each with absolute value less than or equal to $10$. The cubic polynomial
> $$f(x) = x^3 + ax^2 + bx + c$$
> satisfies the property
> $$|f(2 + \sqrt{3})| < 0.0001.$$
> Determine if $2 + \sqrt{3}$ is a root of $f$.
