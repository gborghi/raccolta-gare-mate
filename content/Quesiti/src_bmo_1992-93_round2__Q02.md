*Prove 2^(m-1) ≡ 1 mod m where m=(4^p−1)/3, p prime >3*

> Let $m = (4^p - 1)/3$, where $p$ is a prime number exceeding $3$. Prove that $2^{m-1}$ has remainder $1$ when divided by $m$.
