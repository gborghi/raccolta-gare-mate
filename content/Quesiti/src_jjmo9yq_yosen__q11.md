*Count colorings of 8×8 grid symmetric about both diagonals with at most one per row and column*

> Unit squares of side length $1$ are arranged in $8$ rows and $8$ columns to form an $8 \times 8$ square $ABCD$. Some of these unit squares are to be colored (painted) subject to the following three conditions:\n\n$\bullet$ The arrangement of colored squares is symmetric with respect to both diagonals $AC$ and $BD$ of the square.\n$\bullet$ In every row, at most one unit square is colored.\n$\bullet$ In every column, at most one unit square is colored.\n\nHow many such colorings are possible (including the coloring where no square is colored)?
