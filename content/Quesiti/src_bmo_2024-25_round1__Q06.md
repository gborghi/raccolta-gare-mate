*4x4x4 cube of 3-flavour sugar cubes; find 12 same-flavour in 9 equal-distance pairs*

> Bjork has $64$ sugar cubes, each of size $1 \times 1 \times 1$. Each sugar cube is either white or demerara or muscovado in flavour. She piles the sugar cubes into a $4 \times 4 \times 4$ cube. Prove that there must be $12$ sugar cubes of the same flavour amongst those cubes whose centres lie on a $1 \times 1 \times 1$ axis-aligned sublattice, which can be split into $9$ disjoint pairs so that the cubes of each pair are the same colour.
