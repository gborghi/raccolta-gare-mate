# British Mathematical Olympiad 2024-25 — Round 1 — Quesito 6

*64 sugar cubes 4×4×4, three flavours: find 12 same-flavour cubes with equal pairwise distances*

> Bjork has 64 sugar cubes, all of size $1 \times 1 \times 1$. Each sugar cube is either white or demerara or muscovado in a $4 \times 4 \times 4$ cube. Prove that there must be 12 sugar cubes of the same flavour which can be put into pairs so that the distance between the centres of the cubes in each pair is the same.
