*Number of real solutions of x|x|+y|y|=1 and floor(x)+floor(y)=1*

> What is the number of real solutions of the system
> $$x \cdot |x| + y \cdot |y| = 1 \quad \text{and} \quad \lfloor x \rfloor + \lfloor y \rfloor = 1,$$
> where $\lfloor x \rfloor$ represents the integer part of $x$?
> 
> (A) $0$ (B) $1$ (C) $2$ (D) $4$ (E) infinitely many
