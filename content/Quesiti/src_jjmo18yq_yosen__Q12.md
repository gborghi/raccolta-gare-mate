*All quadruples (a,b,c,d) from 1-9 where every non-empty subset sum avoids multiples of 24*

> Find all quadruples $(a, b, c, d)$ of positive integers satisfying $1 \le a < b < c < d \le 9$ such that none of the $\binom{4}{1} + \binom{4}{2} + \binom{4}{3} + \binom{4}{4} = 15$ subset sums formed by choosing $1$, $2$, $3$, or $4$ elements from $\{a, b, c, d\}$ is a multiple of $24$.
