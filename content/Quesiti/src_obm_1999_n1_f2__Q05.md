*Building with 1000 floors: find floors served by all four elevators, and by exactly four*

> A very tall building has $1000$ floors, not counting the ground floor. From the ground floor, five elevators depart:
> 
> - Elevator $A$ stops at every floor.
> - Elevator $B$ stops at floors that are multiples of $5$: $0, 5, 10, 15, \ldots$
> - Elevator $C$ stops at floors that are multiples of $7$: $0, 7, 14, 21, \ldots$
> - Elevator $D$ stops at floors that are multiples of $17$: $0, 17, 34, 51, \ldots$
> - Elevator $E$ stops at floors that are multiples of $23$: $0, 23, 46, 69, \ldots$
> 
> a) Show that, excluding the ground floor, there is no floor where all five elevators stop.
> 
> b) Determine the number of floors where exactly four elevators stop.
