*Positive integer triples solving two equations*

> Find all triples $(a,b,c)$ of positive integers satisfying $$\begin{cases} ab+c=13 \\ a+bc=23 \end{cases}$$ Here triples that differ only in the order of the three numbers are regarded as distinct.
