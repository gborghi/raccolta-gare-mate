*Number of employees speaking both of two languages*

> A large company has $84$ employees, all of whom speak at least one of two languages, Portuguese and English. Moreover, $20\%$ of those who speak Portuguese also speak English, and $80\%$ of those who speak English also speak Portuguese. How many employees speak both languages?
> (A) $12$ \quad (B) $14$ \quad (C) $15$ \quad (D) $16$ \quad (E) $18$
