*Count ways to schedule rest days with no three consecutive training days*

> A tennis player has 30 days to prepare for a tournament. If he trains 3 consecutive days, the following days he suffers muscle fatigue. He decides that during those 30 days he will train exactly 20 days, never train 3 consecutive days in a row, and rest the remaining 10 days. In how many different ways can he choose the 10 rest days?
