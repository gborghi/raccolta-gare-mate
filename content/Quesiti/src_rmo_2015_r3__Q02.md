# Regional Mathematical Olympiad 2015 — Region 3 — Quesito 2

*Quadratic polynomial squares in AP implies a,b integers*

> Let $P(x) = x^2 + ax + b$ be a quadratic polynomial where $a$ and $b$ are real numbers. Suppose $P(-1)^2$, $P(0)^2$, $P(1)^2$ is an arithmetic progression of integers. Prove that $a$ and $b$ are integers.
