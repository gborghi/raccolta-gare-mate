*Acute triangle with midpoint circle tangencies: prove MA·MD = MB·MC*

> Let $ABC$ be an acute-angled triangle with $AB > AC$. Let $M$ be the midpoint of $BC$. The circle passing through $M$ that is tangent to $AB$ at $B$ and the circle passing through $M$ that is tangent to $AC$ at $C$ intersect again at $D$.
> 
> Prove that $MA \times MD = MB \times MC$.
