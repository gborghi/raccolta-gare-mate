*Sequence with max recurrence, find l≤s with a_n=a_{n+l} for large n*

> Let $a_1, a_2, a_3, \ldots$ be a sequence of positive real numbers. Suppose that for some positive integer $s$, we have
> $$a_n = \max\{a_k + a_{n-k} \mid 1 \le k \le n-1\}$$
> for all $n > s$. Prove that there exist positive integers $\ell$ and $N$, with $\ell \le s$ and such that $a_n = a_{\ell} + a_{n-\ell}$ for all $n \ge N$.
