*Find f(21,12) from functional equations defining f on positive integer pairs*

> The function $f$ is defined for all ordered pairs $(x; y)$ of positive integers and has the following properties:
> $$f(x;\, x) = x, \quad f(x;\, y) = f(y;\, x), \quad (x + y)\,f(x;\, y) = (2x + y)\,f(x;\, y + x).$$
> What is the value of $f(21;\, 12)$?
> 
> A) $\dfrac{7}{4}$ \quad B) $\dfrac{7}{2}$ \quad C) $\dfrac{11}{6}$ \quad D) $\dfrac{6}{11}$ \quad E) $\dfrac{1}{2003}$
