*Price in watermelons for a dozen oranges plus a dozen bananas*

> $2$ watermelons cost the same as $9$ oranges plus $6$ bananas; moreover, half a dozen bananas cost half a watermelon. Therefore, the price paid for one dozen oranges and one dozen bananas is equal to the price of:
> 
> (A) $3$ watermelons \quad (B) $4$ watermelons \quad (C) $6$ watermelons \quad (D) $5$ watermelons \quad (E) $2$ watermelons
