*Find all possible values of |BP - DP| for cyclic quadrilateral with given diagonal segments*

> Quadrilateral $ABCD$ is inscribed in a circle of radius $1$, and the angle between the two diagonals is $60^\circ$. Let $P$ be the intersection point of the diagonals $AC$ and $BD$. Given $AP = \dfrac{1}{2}$ and $CP = \dfrac{3}{2}$, find all possible values of $|BP - DP|$. Here $XY$ denotes the length of segment $XY$.

*Trova tutti i valori possibili di BP - DP di un quadrilaterale ciclico con segmenti diagonali dati*
