# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2014 — Nível 3 — Fase 2 — Quesito 8

*Minimum points so every unit circle inside square 4x4 contains a point*

> Let $ABCD$ be a square with side length $4$. A set $S$ of points in the interior of $ABCD$ has the following property: every circle of radius $1$ contained entirely within $ABCD$ (i.e., contained in its interior or boundary) contains at least one point of $S$. What is the minimum number of points in $S$?
