*Locus of orthocenter foot as point ranges over triangle region*

> Consider $\triangle OAB$ with acute angle $AOB$. Through a point $M \ne O$ perpendiculars are drawn to $OA$ and $OB$, the feet of which are $P$ and $Q$ respectively. The point of intersection of the altitudes of $\triangle OPQ$ is $H$. What is the locus of $H$ if $M$ is permitted to range over (a) the side $AB$, (b) the interior of $\triangle OAB$?
