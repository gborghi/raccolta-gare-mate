*Count ordered pairs (m,n) ≤ 20000 with m²+n⁴ a power of 2*

> Find the numbers of ordered pairs $(m, n)$ where $m$ and $n$ are positive integers less than or equal to 20000 such that $m^2 + n^4$ is a power of 2.
