*Minimum cake pieces for equal sharing among either 4 or 6 people*

> Esmeralda is organizing her birthday party and, because of an error in the distribution of the invitations, she does not know whether $4$ or $6$ people will be present. So she plans to leave the cake cut into some pieces so that, whether $4$ or $6$ people come, each of them will receive the same quantity of whole pieces of cake, with no piece needing to be cut further. What is the smallest number of pieces to reach this objective?
> 
> A) 24 \quad B) 10 \quad C) 8 \quad D) 7 \quad E) 6
