*Broken calculator: max number of square-root presses until a number smaller than 2 appears*

> Esmeralda's calculator is broken: when one presses the button $\sqrt{\ }$, the calculator does, instead of the square root, the following operation: it takes the square root (as it should) and then divides by the number of digits before the comma. Esmeralda types a number with $100$ digits in the calculator and starts to press the button $\sqrt{\ }$ repeatedly. How many times, at most, does Esmeralda press the button until reaching, for the first time, a number smaller than 2?
> 
> (A) 2 (B) 4 (C) 5 (D) 8 (E) 9
