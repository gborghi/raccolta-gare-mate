*Find a minus b given real non-zero a,b satisfying two joint equalities*

> Given real non-zero numbers $a$ and $b$ such that
> $$\frac{1}{a} + \frac{1}{b} = \frac{a-4}{2012} = ab = 4024,$$
> what is the value of $a - b$?
