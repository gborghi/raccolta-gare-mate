*Repeated card draws; find smallest n so k-th sum is always even*

> There are infinitely many cards, each labeled with a non-negative integer, and for each non-negative integer there are infinitely many cards carrying that number. From these cards, $2012$ cards are drawn (with replacement each time). This drawing operation is repeated. Find the smallest positive integer $n$ satisfying the following condition:
> 
> **Condition:** For every positive integer $k$, the sum of the numbers written on the $2012$ cards drawn at the $k$-th draw is always even.
