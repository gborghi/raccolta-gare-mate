*Find positive integers p for which 3^(p-1) divides 2^p+1; 3^(n+2) does not divide 2^n+1+1*

> (a) Find all positive integers $p$ for which $3^{p-1}$ divides $2^p + 1$.
> 
> (b) Prove that $3^{n+2}$ does not divide $2^{n+1} + 1$ for any positive integer $n$.
