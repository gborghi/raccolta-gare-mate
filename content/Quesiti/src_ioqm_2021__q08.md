*Greatest integer N with floor(sqrt(floor(sqrt(N))))=4; find digit sum of N*

> For any real number $t$, let $[t]$ denote the greatest integer $\le t$. Suppose that $N$ is the greatest integer such that
> $$\left[\sqrt{\left[\sqrt{N}\right]}\right] = 4$$
> Find the sum of digits of $N$.
