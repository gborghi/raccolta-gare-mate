*Random chain of subsets starting from an n-set, each a uniformly random subset of the previous; show the expected cardinality of A_k is n/2^k.*

> Let be given positive integers $n$ and $k$. Consider a chain of sets $A_0, A_1, \ldots, A_k$ in which $A_0 = \{1, \ldots, n\}$, and for each $i$, $A_i$ is a randomly chosen subset of $A_{i-1}$ (all choices are equiprobable). Show that the expected cardinality of $A_k$ is $n/2^k$.
