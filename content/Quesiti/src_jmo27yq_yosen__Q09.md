# Japan Mathematical Olympiad 2017 — Yosen (Preliminary) — Quesito 9

*Sum of (number of fixed points)^4 over all permutations*

> For a permutation $\sigma=(\sigma(1),\sigma(2),\dots,\sigma(2017))$ of $1,2,\dots,2017$, let $F(\sigma)$ denote the number of indices $i$ with $1\le i\le 2017$ such that $\sigma(i)=i$. Find the sum of $F(\sigma)^4$ over all permutations $\sigma$.
