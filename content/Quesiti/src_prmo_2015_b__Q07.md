*Sum of even-digits function E(1)+E(2)+…+E(100)*

> Let $E(n)$ denote the sum of the even digits of $n$. For example, $E(1243) = 2 + 4 = 6$. What is the value of $E(1) + E(2) + \cdots + E(100)$?
