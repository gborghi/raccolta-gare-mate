*Determine the number of sequences of length n with terms in {0,...,9} such that among any three consecutive terms at least two are equal.*

> For every integer $n\ge 3$ determine the number of sequences $(c_1,c_2,\ldots,c_n)$ with terms in $S=\{0,1,2,\ldots,9\}$ which satisfy the following condition: For every three consecutive terms, at least two of them are equal.
