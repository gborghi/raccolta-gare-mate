*Max value of difference of two half-sums given sum of squared differences*

> Let $a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_{2n}$ be real numbers with $\sum_{k=1}^{2n-1}(a_{k+1} - a_k)^2 = 1$. Find the maximum value of $(a_{n+1} + a_{n+2} + \cdots + a_{2n}) - (a_1 + a_2 + \cdots + a_n)$.
