*Find least positive value of (x²+y²)/y subject to quadratic constraint*

> Show that the least positive value of
> $$\frac{x^2 + y^2}{y},$$
> where $x$, $y$ are real numbers such that
> $$7x^2 + 3xy + 3y^2 = 1,$$
> is $\dfrac{1}{2}$.
