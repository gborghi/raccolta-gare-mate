*Concurrent diagonals in convex hexagon with equal sides and angle sum*

> In a convex hexagon $ABCDEF$ all sides have equal length and
> $$\angle A + \angle C + \angle E = \angle B + \angle D + \angle F.$$
> Prove that the diagonals $AD$, $BE$, and $CF$ are concurrent.
