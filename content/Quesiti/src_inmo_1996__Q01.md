# Indian National Mathematical Olympiad 1996 — Quesito 1

*Existence and uniqueness of divisibility condition for s+j*

> (a) Show that, for any positive integer $n$, there exist distinct positive integers $x$ and $y$ such that $x + j$ divides $y + j$ for $j = 1, 2, \ldots, n$.
> 
> (b) If for some positive integers $x$ and $y$, $x + j$ divides $y + j$ for all positive integers $j$, show that $x = y$.
