*Over all Goldbach representations of 126 as a sum of two primes, find the largest difference between the two primes.*

> The famous Goldbach Conjecture says that every even integer greater than $2$ can be written as the sum of two prime numbers. For example, $18$ can be represented as $5 + 13$ or, also, as $7 + 11$. Considering all the possible representations of $126$ as a sum of two primes, what is the largest difference between the two primes that form it? (A) $112$ (B) $100$ (C) $92$ (D) $88$ (E) $80$
