*Count hands of value 2004 from a 32-card deck with jokers*

> A deck of 32 cards has 2 different jokers each of which is numbered 0. There are 10 red cards numbered 1 through 10 and similarly for blue and green cards. One chooses a number of cards from the deck. If a card is chosen numbered $k$, then the value of the card is $2^k$, and the value of the hand is the sum of the values of cards in hand. Determine the number of hands having the value 2004.
