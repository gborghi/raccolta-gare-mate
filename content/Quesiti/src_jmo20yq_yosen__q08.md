*Find area of triangle ABC with interior point P, given distances AP, BP, CP and angle BAC*

> There is a point $P$ inside triangle $ABC$. Given that $AP = \sqrt{3}$, $BP = 5$, $CP = 2$, $AB : AC = 2 : 1$, and $\angle BAC = 60^\circ$, find the area of triangle $ABC$. Here $XY$ denotes the length of segment $XY$.
