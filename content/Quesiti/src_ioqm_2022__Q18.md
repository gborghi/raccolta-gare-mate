*Natural numbers m,n with m+3n-5=2LCM(m,n)-11GCD(m,n), find maximum m+n*

> Let $m, n$ be natural numbers such that
> $$m + 3n - 5 = 2\,\text{lcm}(m, n) - 11\,\gcd(m, n).$$
> Find the maximum possible value of $m + n$.
