*Count completed grids placing two identical blue disks, two red and one yellow on a 3x2 grid with edge-sharing restrictions*

> Diane has five identical blue disks, two identical red disks and one yellow disk. She wants to place them on the grid opposite so that each cell contains exactly one disk. The two red disks are not to be placed in cells that share an edge. How many different-looking completed grids can she produce?
>
> - **(A)** $96$
> - **(B)** $108$
> - **(C)** $144$
> - **(D)** $180$
> - **(E)** $216$
