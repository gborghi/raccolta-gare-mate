*Displacement FP when a land-dividing line is moved on polygon ABCDEF*

> Jo\~{a}o and Maria inherited a piece of land, represented by the polygon $ABCDEF$. There was a fence separating the land into two parts, made of straight pieces. They divided it again so that the line passes through point $P$, keeping the two regions equal. The angles at $A$, $B$, $D$, $E$ and $F$ remained equal. Using the dimensions in the figure ($100\,\text{m}$ on top and $60\,\text{m}$ on the right side), what is the displacement $FP$?
> 
> A) 5 \quad B) 8 \quad C) 10 \quad D) 12 \quad E) 20
