*A wooden cube of edge 7 cm is sawn into four rectangular blocks by cuts parallel to its faces; given total surface areas 136, 46, 94 of three blocks, find the cube's surface area and the fourth block's surface area.*

> Beto sawed a wooden cube of edge $7$ cm into four rectangular blocks by means of cuts parallel to its faces. The numbers in the figure indicate, in cm$^2$, the total surface area of three of the blocks ($136$, $46$ and $94$).
> 
> a) What was the total surface area of the cube before it was sawn?
> 
> b) What is the total surface area of the fourth rectangular block?
