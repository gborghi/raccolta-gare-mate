*Count ways to choose max lattice points with no equal coordinate differences*

> Let $n$ be a positive integer. Suppose $n$ points are chosen from the $xy$-plane and satisfy the following conditions.
> \begin{itemize}
> \item Each point has both its $x$- and $y$-coordinates integers between $0$ and $2017$ inclusive.
> \item For any two distinct chosen points $(a,b)$ and $(c,d)$, regardless of how the two points are chosen, $|a-c| \neq |b-d|$ holds.
> \end{itemize}
> Let $N$ be the largest value of $n$ for which such a situation can occur. In how many ways can one choose $N$ points from the $xy$-plane satisfying the conditions?
