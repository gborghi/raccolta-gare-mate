*Value of f(500) for a multiplicative function with f(10)=14 and f(40)=20*

> A function $f$, defined on the set of positive integers, is such that $f(xy) = f(x) + f(y)$ for all $x$ and $y$. It is given that $f(10) = 14$ and $f(40) = 20$. What is the value of $f(500)$?
>
> - **(A)** $29$
> - **(B)** $30$
> - **(C)** $39$
> - **(D)** $49$
> - **(E)** $50$
