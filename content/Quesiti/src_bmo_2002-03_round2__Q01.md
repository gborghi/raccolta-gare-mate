# British Mathematical Olympiad 2002-03 — Round 2 — Quesito 1

*Largest prime factor triples in arithmetic progression*

> For each integer $n > 1$, let $p(n)$ denote the largest prime factor of $n$. Determine all triples $(x, y, z)$ of distinct positive integers satisfying:
> (i) $x, y, z$ are in arithmetic progression, and
> (ii) $p(x), p(y), p(z)$ are in arithmetic progression.
