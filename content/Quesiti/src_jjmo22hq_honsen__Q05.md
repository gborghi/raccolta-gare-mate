*Similarity of triangles B'C'D' and BCD in acute triangle*

> In acute triangle $ABC$, let $D$ be the point on side $BC$ satisfying $AB : AC = DB : BC$. Let $B'$ be the reflection of $B$ over line $AC$, let $C'$ be the reflection of $C$ over line $AB$, and let $D'$ be the reflection of $D$ over the perpendicular bisector of $BC$. Prove that triangle $B'C'D'$ is similar to triangle $BCD$. Here, $XY$ denotes the length of segment $XY$.
