*Circumcenters of sub-triangles with circumcenter; collinearity proof*

> Let $ABC$ be an acute triangle with circumcenter $O$. Let $O_1$ and $O_2$ be the circumcenters of triangles $ABO$ and $ACO$, respectively. The circumcircle of triangle $AO_1O_2$ meets line $BC$ at two distinct points $P$ and $Q$. Let $O_3$ be the circumcenter of triangle $OPQ$. Prove that the three points $A$, $O$, $O_3$ are collinear.
