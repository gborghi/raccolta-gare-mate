# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2012 — Nível 2 — Fase 1 — Quesito 1

*Number of gold medals given 1:2:3 ratio and 90% medalists*

> In the final phase of the OBM, $600$ students from all over Brazil participated. Following the tradition of international olympiads, the olympiad distributes gold, silver and bronze medals in the proportion $1{:}2{:}3$, respectively. Knowing that $90\%$ of the total number of students won some of the 3 medals, how many students won a gold medal?
> 
> A) 60 \quad B) 120 \quad C) 180 \quad D) 240 \quad E) 300
