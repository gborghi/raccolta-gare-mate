*Chess: 7 rounds, 4 games/round, 8 players; after round 3, can there be a tied first-place group with the rest tied second, and can all scores differ*

> A chess championship has $7$ rounds, with $4$ games per round, $8$ participants, whose scores per game are as usual: one point for a victory, half a point for a draw and no point for a defeat. Each pair of players faces each other exactly once.
> 
> a) At the end of the third round, is it possible for a group of players to be in first place and the remaining players to be in second place? Explain by means of an example.
> 
> b) At the end of the third round, is it possible for all players to have different scores? Explain.
