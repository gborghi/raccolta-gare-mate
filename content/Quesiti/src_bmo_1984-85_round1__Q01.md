# British Mathematical Olympiad 1984-85 — Round 1 — Quesito 1

*Two circles tangent to line at same point; prove angle bisector and area ratio*

> Two circles $S_1$ and $S_2$ each touch a straight line $p$ at the same point $P$. All points of $S_1$, except $P$, are inside $S_2$. A straight line $l$ is drawn through $P$ cutting $S_1$ at $A$ and $B$, and $S_2$ at $C$ and $D$.
> 
> (a) Prove that $BP$ bisects angle $MPN$.
> 
> (b) If $MP$ bisects angle $RPL$, find, with proof, the ratio of the areas of $S_1$ and $S_2$.
