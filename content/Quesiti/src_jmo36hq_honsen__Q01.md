*Acute triangle, circumcenter, points on ray DO; find angle BAC*

> Let $ABC$ be an acute triangle with $AB < AC$, let $O$ be its circumcenter, and let $D$ be the intersection point of the bisector of $\angle BAC$ with side $BC$. On the ray $DO$ take two distinct points $P, Q$, both lying on the opposite side of line $AC$ from $B$ and on the same side of line $AB$ as $C$, so that the three points $D, P, Q$ lie in this order. Moreover, $AB = BP$, $AC = CQ$, and $\angle ABP = \angle ACQ$ hold. Find the value of $\angle BAC$. Here, $XY$ denotes the length of segment $XY$.
