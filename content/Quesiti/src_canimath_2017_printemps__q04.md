*Identify the diagonal among five given lengths of a quadrilateral*

> Five numbers are given in increasing order; they are the lengths of the four sides of a (non-self-intersecting, but not necessarily convex, meaning a diagonal is not necessarily inside the polygon) quadrilateral and of one of its diagonals $D$. These five numbers are $3$, $5$, $7$, $13$, and $19$. Which of these numbers can be the length of the diagonal $D$?
