*Non-empty subsets A,B of {1,...,n}; a subset D of A+B with D+D in 2(A+B) and |D| at least |A||B|/(2n)*

> Let $n$ be a positive integer, and $A$, $B$ be non-empty subsets of $\{1,2,\ldots,n\}$. Prove that there exists a subset $D$ of $A+B$ such that
> $$D+D\subseteq 2(A+B), \quad \text{and} \quad |D|\ge\frac{|A|\cdot|B|}{2n},$$
> where $|X|$ denotes the number of elements of a finite set $X$.
