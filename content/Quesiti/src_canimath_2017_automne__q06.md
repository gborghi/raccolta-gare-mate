*Find all values of xyz given x+2y=z and x^2-4y^2+z^2=310*

> The strictly positive integers $x$, $y$ and $z$ satisfy the two following equations: $x + 2y = z$ and $x^2 - 4y^2 + z^2 = 310$. Find all the values that the product $xyz$ can take.
