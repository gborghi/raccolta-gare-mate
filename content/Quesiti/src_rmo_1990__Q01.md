*Pigeonhole on balls of same colour and size in two boxes*

> Two boxes contain between them 65 balls of several different sizes. Each ball is white, black, red or yellow. If you take any 5 balls of the same colour at least 2 of them will always be of the same size (radius). Prove that there are at least 3 balls which lie in the same box have the same colour and have the same size (radius).
