*Count ways to fill 2024x2024 grid with J,M,O under two conditions*

> Find the number of ways to fill each cell of a $2024 \times 2024$ grid with one of the letters J, M, O such that both of the following conditions hold:
> 
> - Every $2 \times 2$ subgrid contains exactly $2$ copies of J, $1$ copy of M, and $1$ copy of O.
> - Every cell in an odd-numbered row (counting from the top) and an odd-numbered column (counting from the left) contains the letter J.
> 
> (Arrangements that coincide after rotation or reflection are counted as distinct.)
