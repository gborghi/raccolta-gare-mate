*Functional equation f(mn+1)=f(m)f(n)-f(n)-m+2, f(0)=1; largest N with sum f(k)<100*

> A function $f$ is defined on the set of integers such that for any two integers $m$ and $n$,
> $$f(mn + 1) = f(m)f(n) - f(n) - m + 2$$
> holds and $f(0) = 1$. Determine the largest positive integer $N$ such that $\displaystyle\sum_{k=1}^{N} f(k) < 100$.
