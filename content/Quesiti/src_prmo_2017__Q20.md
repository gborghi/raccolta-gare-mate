*Count triples (a,b,c) of positive integers with a<b<c<10 and (b-a) divides c*

> What is the number of triples $(a, b, c)$ of positive integers such that (i) $a < b < c < 10$ and (ii) $a, b, c$ are in arithmetic progression, and $b - a$ divides $c$?
