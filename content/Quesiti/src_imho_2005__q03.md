*Inequality for x,y,z >= 1 involving fractional expressions*

> Let $x, y, z$ be real numbers each greater than $1$. Prove that
> $$\frac{x^5-x^2}{x^5+y^3+z^3}+\frac{y^5-y^2}{y^5+z^3+x^3}+\frac{z^5-z^2}{z^5+x^3+y^3}\ge 0.$$
