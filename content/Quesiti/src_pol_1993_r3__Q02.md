*Inscribed circle in a trapezoid; prove DE=FC iff AB=2CD.*

> A circle $k$ with center $O$ is inscribed in a nonisosceles trapezoid $ABCD$ with the longer base $AB$. Let $M$ be the midpoint of $AB$. The line $CD$ touches $k$ at point $E$ and intersects the line $OM$ at point $F$. Prove that $DE = FC$ if and only if $AB = 2CD$.
