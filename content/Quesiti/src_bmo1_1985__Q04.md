*Sequence defined by recurrence; prove eventual periodicity*

> The sequence $f_n$ is defined by $f_0 = 1$, $f_1 = a$, where $a$ is a positive integer, and for all $n > 1$,
> $$f_n = 2f_{n-1} - f_{n-2} + 2.$$
> Prove that for each $k \geq 0$ there exists $h$ such that $f_h f_{h+1} = f_k$.
