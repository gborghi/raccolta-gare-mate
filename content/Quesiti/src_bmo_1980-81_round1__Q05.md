*Sum of a_i^k / P_i over distinct nonzero integers is an integer*

> $a_1, a_2, a_3, \ldots, a_n$ are distinct non-zero integers. $P_k$ is the product of the $(n-1)$ factors
> $$(a_1 - a_k),\ (a_2 - a_k),\ \ldots,\ (a_{k-1} - a_k),\ (a_{k+1} - a_k),\ \ldots,\ (a_n - a_k),$$
> the zero factor $(a_k - a_k)$ being excluded.
> 
> Prove that if $k$ is a non-negative integer,
> $$\sum_{i=1}^{n} \frac{a_i^k}{P_i}$$
> is an integer.
