*System a^2-ab=1, b^2-bc=1, c^2-ca=1; find abc(a+b+c)*

> Let $a$, $b$, $c$ be numbers such that
> $$a^2-ab=1,\quad b^2-bc=1,\quad c^2-ca=1.$$
> The value of $abc(a+b+c)$ is equal to:
> 
> (A) $0$ \quad (B) $1$ \quad (C) $2$ \quad (D) $-1$ \quad (E) $-3$
