# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 1998 — Nível 1 — Fase 1 — Quesito 8

*Find last number in a circle chain where every three consecutive sum to 12*

> Write a number in each circle in the row below so that the sum of any three consecutive numbers is $12$. The first circle contains $3$ and the sixth circle contains $5$.
> 
> $$\bigcirc\!3\!\bigcirc \quad \bigcirc \quad \bigcirc \quad \bigcirc\!5\!\bigcirc \quad \bigcirc \quad \bigcirc$$
> 
> The number that must appear in the last (rightmost) circle is:
> 
> (A) $3$
> (B) $2$
> (C) $1$
> (D) $4$
> (E) $7$
