*Digits P,Q,R,S,T are 1–5 in some order; PRT and QRS are both 3-digit primes; find R*

> $P$, $Q$, $R$, $S$ and $T$ are the digits 1, 2, 3, 4 and 5 in some order. '$PRT$' and '$QRS$' are both three-digit primes. Which digit is $R$?
>
> - **(A)** $1$
> - **(B)** $2$
> - **(C)** $3$
> - **(D)** $4$
> - **(E)** $5$
