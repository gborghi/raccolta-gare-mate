*Interi n con successione ciclica ai·ai+1+1=ai+2*

> Find all integers $n \geq 3$ for which there exist real numbers $a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_{n+2}$ satisfying $a_{n+1} = a_1$, $a_{n+2} = a_2$ and
> $$a_i a_{i+1} + 1 = a_{i+2}$$
> for $i = 1, 2, \ldots, n$.
