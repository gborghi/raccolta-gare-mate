*Recursive function on positive integers, prove integer values, count solutions*

> The function $f$ is defined on the positive integers by $f(1) = 1$, $f(2n) = 2f(n)$, and $f(2n+1) = f(2n) + 1$ for all $n \ge 1$.
> (i) Prove that $f(n)$ is always an integer.
> (ii) For how many positive integers $n$ is $f(n) = 2007$? And for how many positive integers $n \le 2007$ is $f(n) = 2n$?
