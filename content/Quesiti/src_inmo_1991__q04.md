*Real numbers a,b,c in (0,1) with a+b+c=2: prove sum of fractions >=8*

> Let $a, b, c$ be real numbers in the interval $(0, 1)$ with $a + b + c = 2$. Prove that
> $$\frac{a}{1-a} + \frac{b}{1-b} + \frac{c}{1-c} \ge 8.$$
