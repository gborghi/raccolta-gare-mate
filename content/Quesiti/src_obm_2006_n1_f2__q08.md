*Maximum number of distinct total masses from six discs of 1–6 kg*

> Esmeralda bought six iron discs to use with a gymnastics apparatus. The discs have masses $1, 2, 3, 4, 5,$ and $6$ kilograms, respectively. Esmeralda can combine them to obtain other masses; for example:
> $$1\text{ disc of }2\,\text{kg} + 1\text{ disc of }6\,\text{kg} = 8\,\text{kg}.$$
> What is the greatest number of different masses she can obtain?

*Nombre massimo di masse totali distinte da sei dischi di 16 kg*
