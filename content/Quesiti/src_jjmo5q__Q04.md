*Three-variable degree-3 polynomial with three divisibility conditions*

> Find all polynomials $f(x, y, z)$ with real coefficients, of degree $3$ in three variables $x$, $y$, $z$, satisfying all of the following conditions:
> \begin{itemize}
> \item $f(x, y, z) + z$ is divisible by $x + y + z$,
> \item $f(x, y, z) + y$ is divisible by $x + y + z$,
> \item $f(x, y, z) + z$ is divisible by $x + y + z$.
> \end{itemize}
> Here, a polynomial $P(x,y,z)$ is said to be divisible by $Q(x,y,z)$ if there exists a polynomial $R(x,y,z)$ such that $P(x,y,z) = Q(x,y,z)\,R(x,y,z)$.
