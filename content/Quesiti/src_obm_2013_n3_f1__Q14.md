# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2013 — Nível 3 — Fase 1 — Quesito 14

*Least n so the n-fold composition of f equals identity on S={1..9}*

> Let $S = \{1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9\}$. Consider a function $f : S \to S$ defined by the table below.
> 
> $\begin{array}{|c|c|c|c|c|c|c|c|c|c|} \hline x & 1 & 2 & 3 & 4 & 5 & 6 & 7 & 8 & 9 \\ \hline f(x) & 8 & 3 & 5 & 7 & 2 & 9 & 6 & 1 & 4 \\ \hline \end{array}$
> 
> What is the smallest positive integer $n$ for which $\underbrace{f(f(\cdots(f(x))\cdots))}_{n\ \text{times}} = x$ for every $x \in S$?
> 
> (A) $4$ \quad (B) $5$ \quad (C) $6$ \quad (D) $12$ \quad (E) $24$
