*n making product of floors plus 2*4^[k/2] a perfect square*

> Let $n$ be a positive integer, and let $k$ be the nonnegative integer satisfying $2^k \le n < 2^{k+1}$. Find all $n$ such that
> $$\left[\frac{n}{2^0}\right]\left[\frac{n}{2^1}\right]\cdots\left[\frac{n}{2^k}\right] + 2\cdot 4^{\left[\frac{k}{2}\right]}$$
> is a perfect square. Here, for a real number $r$, $[r]$ denotes the greatest integer not exceeding $r$, and for a positive integer $a$ we set $a^0 = 1$.
