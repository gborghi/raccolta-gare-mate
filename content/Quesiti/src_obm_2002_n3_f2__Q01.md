*Find 4-digit number from exactly one true clue*

> Arnaldo thought of a four-digit number and challenged Bernardo to discover which number it was. To help, he gave Bernardo the following three clues, exactly one of which is true:
> 
> - Clue 1: The number is a perfect cube.
> - Clue 2: The number is the smallest four-digit number that has exactly four positive divisors.
> - Clue 3: The number is a multiple of $59$.
> 
> What is the number Arnaldo thought of?
