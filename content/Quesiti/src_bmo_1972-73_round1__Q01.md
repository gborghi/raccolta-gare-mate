*Variable circle touching two fixed circles; PQ through fixed point*

> (i) Two fixed circles are touched by a variable circle at $P$ and $Q$.
> 
> Prove that $PQ$ passes through one of two fixed points.
> 
> (ii) State a true theorem about ellipses or if you like about conics in general of which (i) is a particular case.
