*Cyclic quadrilateral: AC bisects angle DAB; show CE=CA iff DE parallel AB*

> In the cyclic quadrilateral $ABCD$, the diagonal $AC$ bisects the angle $DAB$. The side $AD$ is extended beyond $D$ to a point $E$. Show that $CE = CA$ if and only if $DE \parallel AB$.
