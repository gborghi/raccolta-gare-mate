*Area of trapezium cross-section of a cube*

> $G$ and $H$ are midpoints of two adjacent edges of a cube. A trapezium-shaped cross-section is formed cutting through $G$, $H$ and two further vertices, as shown. The edge-length of the cube is $2\sqrt{2}$. What is the area of the trapezium?
>
> - **(A)** $9$
> - **(B)** $8$
> - **(C)** $4\sqrt{5}$
> - **(D)** $4\sqrt{3}$
> - **(E)** $4\sqrt{2}$

*Area di sezione trasversale del trapezio di un cubo*

>
> - **(A)** $9$
> - **(B)** $8$
> - **(C)** $4\sqrt{5}$
> - **(D)** $4\sqrt{3}$
