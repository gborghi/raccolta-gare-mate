*Find BD/BC in parallelogram ABCD with angle bisector meeting BC*

> In parallelogram $ABCD$, let $BC = 7$, $CA = 8$, $AB = 5$. Let $E$ be the point where the angle bisector of $\angle BAC$ meets side $BC$. The condition $BE + BC = BD$ holds. Find the value of $\dfrac{BD}{BC}$. (For points $X, Y$, the notation $XY$ denotes the length of segment $XY$.)
