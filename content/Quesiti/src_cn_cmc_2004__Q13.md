*Probability of crossing first three obstacles in dice game*

> The rule of an "obstacle course" specifies that at the $n$th obstacle, a person has to toss a die until the number of points in those $n$ tosses is bigger than $2^n$; the person is said to have crossed the obstacle.
> 
> (1) How many obstacles can a person cross?
> 
> (2) What is the probability that a person crosses the first three obstacles?
