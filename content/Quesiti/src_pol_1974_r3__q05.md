*For naturals n, r with r+3 <= n, prove the binomial coefficients C(n,r), C(n,r+1), C(n,r+2), C(n,r+3) cannot be successive terms of an arithmetic progression.*

> Prove that for any natural numbers $n, r$ with $r + 3 \le n$ the binomial coefficients $\binom{n}{r}$, $\binom{n}{r+1}$, $\binom{n}{r+2}$, $\binom{n}{r+3}$ cannot be successive terms of an arithmetic progression.
