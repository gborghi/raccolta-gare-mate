*Range of DF length in right triangular prism with GD perp EF*

> Given a right triangular prism $A_1B_1C_1 - ABC$ with $\angle BAC = \frac{\pi}{2}$ and $AB = AC = AA_1 = 1$, let $G$, $E$ be the midpoints of $A_1B_1$, $CC_1$ respectively, and $D$, $F$ be variable points lying on segments $AC$, $AB$ (not including endpoints) respectively. If $GD \perp EF$, the range of the length of $DF$ is (\ \ ).
> 
> (A) $\left[\frac{1}{2}, 1\right)$\quad (B) $\left[\frac{1}{2}, 2\right)$\quad (C) $\left(1, \sqrt{2}\right)$\quad (D) $\left[\frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}, \sqrt{2}\right)$
