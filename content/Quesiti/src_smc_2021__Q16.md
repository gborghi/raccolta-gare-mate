*25 equally spaced dots in 5×5 grid; O is bottom-left corner; count lines through O and exactly one other dot.*

> An array of $25$ equally spaced dots is drawn in a square grid as shown. Point $O$ is in the bottom left corner. Linda wants to draw a straight line through the diagram which passes through $O$ and exactly one other point. How many such lines can Linda draw?
>
> - **(A)** 4
> - **(B)** 6
> - **(C)** 8
> - **(D)** 12
> - **(E)** 24
