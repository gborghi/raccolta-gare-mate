*Interior point of triangle, integer p,q,r making weighted vector sum small*

> Let $O$ be an interior point of the triangle $ABC$. Prove that there exist positive integers $p$, $q$ and $r$, such that
> $$\left| p \cdot \overrightarrow{OA} + q \cdot \overrightarrow{OB} + r \cdot \overrightarrow{OC} \right| < \frac{1}{2007}.$$
