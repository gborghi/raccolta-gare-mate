*Construct line segment CD using compass/ruler given angle and ratio*

> Let $AOB$ be a given angle less than $180^\circ$ and let $P$ be an interior point of the angular region determined by $\angle AOB$. Show, with proof, how to construct, using only ruler and compasses, a line segment $CD$ passing through $P$ such that $C$ lies on the ray $OA$ and $D$ lies on the ray $OB$, and $CP : PD = 1 : 2$.
