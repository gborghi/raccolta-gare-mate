*Isosceles trapezoid, diagonal 100 m, angle 30°, find S/sqrt(3)*

> An isosceles trapezoid $ABCD$, with parallel sides $AB$ and $CD$, is such that the diagonal $BD$ measures $100\,\text{m}$ and the angle $\angle BDC$ measures $30^\circ$. Let $S$ be the area of the trapezoid in $\text{m}^2$. Determine $S/\sqrt{3}$.
