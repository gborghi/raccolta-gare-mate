*Convex quadrilateral midpoints form isosceles triangle*

> Let $ABCD$ be a convex quadrilateral such that $AD = DC$, $AC = AB$, and $\angle ADC = \angle CAB$. Let $M$ and $N$ be the midpoints of $\overline{AD}$ and $\overline{AB}$, respectively. Prove that triangle $MNC$ is isosceles.
