# Japan Mathematical Olympiad 2004 — Honsen (Final) — Quesito 2

*Find all real functions with f(xf(x)+f(y))=f(x)^2+y*

> Let $f(x)$ be a real-valued function defined on the real numbers such that, for all real numbers $x$ and $y$,
> $$f\left(x f(x) + f(y)\right) = \left(f(x)\right)^2 + y$$
> holds. Find all possible functions $f(x)$.
