# Indian National Mathematical Olympiad 2000 — Quesito 6

*f(n) counts integer-sided triangles; prove f(1999)>f(1996) and f(2000)=f(1997)*

> For any natural number $n$ ($n \ge 3$), let $f(n)$ denote the number of non-congruent integer-sided triangles with perimeter $n$ (e.g., $f(3) = 1$, $f(4) = 0$, $f(7) = 2$). Show that
> (a) $f(1999) > f(1996)$;
> (b) $f(2000) = f(1997)$.
