*Find all fractions m/n with m,n ≤ 20 in a given interval*

> Find all rational numbers that can be written as $\frac{m}{n}$, where $m$ and $n$ are positive integers each not exceeding $20$, and which are greater than $\frac{5}{4}$ and less than $\frac{4}{3}$.
