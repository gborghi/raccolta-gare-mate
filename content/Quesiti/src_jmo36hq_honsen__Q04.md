*Find all real functions with f(x^2+f(y)^2)+2f(x)y=f(x+f(y))^2*

> Find all functions $f$ defined on the real numbers and taking real values such that, for all real numbers $x, y$,
> $$f\!\left(x^2 + f(y)^2\right) + 2f(x)\,y = f\!\left(x + f(y)\right)^2$$
> holds.
