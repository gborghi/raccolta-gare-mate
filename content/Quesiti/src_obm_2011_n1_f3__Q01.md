*Erase even numbers from 1 to 2011; count remaining and count those written only with digits 0 and 1.*

> Esmeralda wrote on the blackboard the sequence of all the integers from $1$ to $2011$. Then she erased all the even numbers in the list.
> 
> a) How many numbers remained?
> 
> b) Of the remaining numbers, how many were written using only the digits $0$ and $1$?
