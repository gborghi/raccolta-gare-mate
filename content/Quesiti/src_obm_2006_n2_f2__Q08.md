*Find a+b and ab given two real equations in a and b*

> Let $a$ and $b$ be distinct real numbers such that $a^2 = 6b + 5ab$ and $b^2 = 6a + 5ab$.
> a) Determine the value of $a + b$.
> b) Determine the value of $ab$.
