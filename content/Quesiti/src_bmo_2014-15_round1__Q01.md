*Order five tower-exponential numbers by size*

> Place the following numbers in increasing order of size, and justify your reasoning:
> $$3^{3^4},\quad 3^{4^3},\quad 3^{4^4},\quad 4^{3^3}\quad\text{and}\quad 4^{3^4}.$$
> Note that $a^{b^c}$ means $a^{(b^c)}$.
