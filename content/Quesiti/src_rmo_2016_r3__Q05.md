*Right-angled triangle, incentre construction, prove BA=BC*

> Let $ABC$ be a right-angled triangle with $\angle B = 90^\circ$. Let $I$ be the incentre of $ABC$. Extend $AI$ and $CI$; let them intersect $BC$ in $D$ and $AB$ in $E$ respectively. Draw a line perpendicular to $AI$ at $I$ to meet $AC$ in $J$; draw a line perpendicular to $CI$ at $I$ to meet $AC$ in $K$. Suppose $DJ = EK$. Prove that $BA = BC$.
