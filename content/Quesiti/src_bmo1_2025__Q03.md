*Determine winning strategy in blackboard replacement game*

> Rhian and Jack are playing a game in which initially the number $10^6$ is written on a blackboard. If the current number on the board is $n$, a move consists of choosing two different positive integers $a, b$ such that $n = ab$ and replacing $n$ with $|a - b|$. Rhian starts, then the players make moves alternately. A player loses if they are unable to move.
> 
> Determine, with proof, which player has a winning strategy.
