# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2021 — Nível Universitário — Fase x — Quesito 2

*Find all C² functions satisfying f(t)²=f(t√2) for all real t*

> Determine all functions $f : \mathbb{R} \to \mathbb{R}$ of class $C^2$ (that is, $f$ is twice differentiable with continuous second derivative) such that
> $$f(t)^2 = f(t\sqrt{2})$$
> for every real $t$.
