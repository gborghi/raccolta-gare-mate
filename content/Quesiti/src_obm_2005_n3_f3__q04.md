*Minimum trials to guarantee radio works with unknown batteries*

> We have four charged batteries, four uncharged batteries, and a radio that requires two charged batteries to work. Suppose we do not know which batteries are charged and which are uncharged. Find the minimum number of trials sufficient to guarantee that the radio functions, where a trial consists of placing two batteries in the radio and checking whether it works.
