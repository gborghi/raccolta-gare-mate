# British Mathematical Olympiad 1997-98 — Round 2 — Quesito 3

*Positive integers with reciprocal equation; prove hcf times sum is perfect square*

> Suppose $x, y, z$ are positive integers satisfying the equation
> $$\frac{1}{x} - \frac{1}{y} = \frac{1}{z}$$
> and let $h = \mathrm{hcf}(x, y, z)$.
> 
> Prove that $h(x + y)$ is a perfect square.
> 
> Also prove that $h(x - y)$ is a perfect square.
