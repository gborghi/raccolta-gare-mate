# British Mathematical Olympiad 1976-77 — Round 1 — Quesito 2

*Quadrilateral circumscribed about a circle; prove midpoint collinearity with centre*

> The sides $M_1 M_2$, $M_2 M_3$, $M_3 M_4$, $M_4 M_1$ of a quadrilateral touch a circle at $X$, $Y$, $Z$, $W$ respectively. Prove that the centre of the circle lies on the straight line through the midpoints of $M_1 M_3$ and $M_2 M_4$.
