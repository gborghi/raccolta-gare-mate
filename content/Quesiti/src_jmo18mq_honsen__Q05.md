*Existence of n representing every rational as integer plus n unit fractions*

> Does there exist a positive integer $n$ satisfying the following condition?
> 
> Condition: For every rational number $r$, there exist an integer $b$ and nonzero integers $a_1,\dots,a_n$ such that
> $$r=b+\frac{1}{a_1}+\cdots+\frac{1}{a_n}.$$
