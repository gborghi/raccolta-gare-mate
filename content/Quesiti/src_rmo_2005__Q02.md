# Regional Mathematical Olympiad 2005 — Quesito 2

*Integers x,y: 17 divides two expressions implies 17 divides third*

> If $x$, $y$ are integers and 17 divides both $x^2 - 3xy + 2y^2 + x - y$ and $x^2 - 3xy + 2y^2 - 5x + 7y$, then prove that 17 divides $xy - 12x + 15y$.
