*Prove cyclic sum of a^k/(a+b) ≥ 3/2 for abc=1 and integer k≥2*

> Given real numbers $a$, $b$, $c$ such that $abc = 1$, prove that for all integers $k \geqslant 2$,
> $$\frac{a^k}{a+b} + \frac{b^k}{b+c} + \frac{c^k}{c+a} \geqslant \frac{3}{2}.$$
