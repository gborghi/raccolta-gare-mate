*Prove weighted inequality for real numbers on unit sphere*

> Let $x_1, x_2, \ldots, x_n$ (where $n \ge 2$) be real numbers with $x_1^2 + x_2^2 + \cdots + x_n^2 = 1$. Prove that
> $$\sum_{k=1}^{n} \left(1 - \frac{k}{\sum_{i=1}^{n} i x_i^2}\right)^2 \cdot \frac{x_k^2}{k} \le \left(\frac{n-1}{n+1}\right)^2 \sum_{k=1}^{n} \frac{x_k^2}{k}.$$
> Determine when the equality holds.
