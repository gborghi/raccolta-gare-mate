*Count 10-tuples of integers with bounded entries and sum-of-products condition*

> Find the number of 10-tuples $(a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_{10})$ of integers such that $|a_i| \leq 1$ and
> $$a_1^2 + a_2^2 + a_3^2 + \cdots + a_{10}^2 - a_1 a_2 - a_2 a_3 - a_3 a_4 - \cdots - a_9 a_{10} - a_{10} a_1 = 2.$$
