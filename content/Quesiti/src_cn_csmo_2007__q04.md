*Semicircle with points C, D, tangent at B meets CD at P; line PO meets CA and AD; prove OE = OF*

> As shown in the figure, points $C$ and $D$ are on the semicircle with $O$ as its center and $AB$ as its diameter. The tangent line to the semicircle at point $B$ meets the line $CD$ at point $P$. Line $PO$ intersects $CA$ and $AD$ at point $E$ and $F$ respectively. Prove $OE = OF$.
