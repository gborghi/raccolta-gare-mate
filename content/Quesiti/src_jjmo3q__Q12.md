*Triples of distinct positives with product equal to 12 times the sum*

> How many sets of three distinct positive integers are there whose product is equal to $12$ times their sum? Here, sets that differ only in the order of the numbers, such as $\{3, 6, 18\}$ and $\{6, 3, 18\}$, are regarded as the same and counted as one.
