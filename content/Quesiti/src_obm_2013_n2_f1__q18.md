*Inscribed pentagon: find angle DAE given BCE=35 and AB perpendicular CD*

> In the figure, $O$ is the centre of a circle passing through points $A$, $B$, $C$, $D$, $E$ (in order on the circle). Chords $AB$ and $CD$ are perpendicular to each other, and $\angle BCE = 35^\circ$. Find the value in degrees of angle $\angle DAE$.
> 
> (A) $35^\circ$ \quad (B) $40^\circ$ \quad (C) $45^\circ$ \quad (D) $50^\circ$ \quad (E) $55^\circ$
