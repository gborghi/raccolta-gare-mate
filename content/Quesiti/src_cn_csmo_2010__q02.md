*Sum of products of all 99-element subsets of {1,...,2010} is divisible by 2011*

> For each 99-element subset $A = \{a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_{99}\}$ of $\{1, 2, \ldots, 2010\}$, denote $P(A) = a_1 \cdot a_2 \cdots a_{99}$. Let $A_1, A_2, \ldots, A_n$ be all 99-element subsets of $\{1, 2, \ldots, 2010\}$, $n = \binom{2010}{99}$. Prove that $2011 \mid \sum_{i=1}^{n} P(A_i)$.
