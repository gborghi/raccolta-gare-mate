*Find 1/a+1/b+1/c given two symmetric equations*

> The positive real numbers $a, b, c$ satisfy:
> $$\frac{a}{2b+1} + \frac{2b}{3c+1} + \frac{3c}{a+1} = 1$$
> $$\frac{1}{a+1} + \frac{1}{2b+1} + \frac{1}{3c+1} = 2$$
> What is the value of $\frac{1}{a} + \frac{1}{b} + \frac{1}{c}$?
