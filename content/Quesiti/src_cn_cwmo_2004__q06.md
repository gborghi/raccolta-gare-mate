*Determine parity of good couples S in a 2-colored m×n chessboard*

> All the grids of an $m \times n$ chessboard ($m \ge 3$, $n \ge 3$) are colored either red or blue. Two adjacent grids (with a common side) are called a good couple if they are of different colors. Suppose that there are $S$ good couples, explain how to determine whether $S$ is odd or even. Does $S$ depend on certain specific color grids?
