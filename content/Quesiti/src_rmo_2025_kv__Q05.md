*Acute triangle with angle 60°; reflections of altitude feet bisect BC*

> Let $ABC$ be an acute-angled triangle with $\angle BAC = 60^\circ$ and $AB < BC < AC$. Let $M$, $N$ be the feet of the altitudes from $B$, $C$ respectively. Suppose $BE$, $CF$ are the altitudes, with $E$ on $CA$ and $F$ on $AB$. Let $X$ be the image of $M$ under reflection in the midpoint of $BF$, and let $Y$ be the image of $N$ under reflection in the midpoint of $CE$. Prove that $XY$ bisects $BC$.
