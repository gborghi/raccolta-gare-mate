*Length CQ where circle through B,A,D of parallelogram meets CD*

> A circumference passing through the vertices $B$, $A$, $D$ of the parallelogram $ABCD$ meets segment $CD$ at $Q$. Knowing that $m(B\hat{A}D) = 60^\circ$ and $AD = 10$, what is the size of segment $CQ$?
> 
> (A) $10$ \quad (B) $20$ \quad (C) $\dfrac{5\sqrt{3}}{2}$ \quad (D) $10\sqrt{3}$ \quad (E) $15$
