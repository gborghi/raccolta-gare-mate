*Consecutive positive integers divisible by consecutive odd integers; find smallest m*

> Consecutive positive integers $n$, $n + 2$ and $n + 4$ are each exactly divisible by consecutive odd positive integers $m$, $m + 2$ and $m + 4$ respectively. Determine the smallest possible value of $m$.
