*Find the largest natural number that, for some rearrangement (a,b,c,d) of (3,6,9,12), equals the integer cube root of 3^a 6^b 9^c 12^d.*

> Determine the largest natural number for which there exists a rearrangement $(a, b, c, d)$ of $(3, 6, 9, 12)$ (that is, $\{a, b, c, d\} = \{3, 6, 9, 12\}$) such that the number $\sqrt[3]{3^a \cdot 6^b \cdot 9^c \cdot 12^d}$ is an integer. Justify your answer.
