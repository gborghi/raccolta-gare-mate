*Functional equation on integers with 2f(f(n))=5f(n)-2n*

> Find all functions $f$ from the integers to the integers such that for all integers $n$:
> $$2f(f(n)) = 5f(n) - 2n.$$

*Equazione funzionale su numeri interi con 2f(f(n))=5f(n)-2n*
