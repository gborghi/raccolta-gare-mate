*Telescoping sum of cosecants equals cotangent identity*

> Prove that for every natural number $n$, and for every real number $x \neq \frac{k\pi}{2^t}$ ($t = 0, 1, \ldots, n$; $k$ any integer)
> $$\frac{1}{\sin 2x} + \frac{1}{\sin 4x} + \cdots + \frac{1}{\sin 2^n x} = \cot x - \cot 2^n x.$$
