# British Mathematical Olympiad 2011-12 — Round 2 — Quesito 1

*Cyclic quadrilateral midpoints give four triangles with equal circumradii*

> The diagonals $AC$ and $BD$ of a cyclic quadrilateral meet at $E$. The midpoints of the sides $AB$, $BC$, $CD$ and $DA$ are $P$, $Q$, $R$ and $S$ respectively. Prove that the circumradii of triangles $EPQ$, $EQR$, $ERS$ and $ESP$ are all equal.
