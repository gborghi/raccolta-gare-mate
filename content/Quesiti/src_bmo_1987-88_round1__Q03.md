*Find unique integer pair r, n with 0<n<200 satisfying two fraction inequalities*

> Find a pair of integers $r$, $n$ such that $0 < n < 200$ and
> $$\frac{r}{n} < \frac{51}{80} < \frac{r+1}{n}, \qquad \frac{r}{n+1} < \frac{51}{80} < \frac{r+1}{n+1}.$$
> Also prove that there is exactly one such pair $r$, $n$.
