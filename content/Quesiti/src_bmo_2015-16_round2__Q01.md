# British Mathematical Olympiad 2015-16 — Round 2 — Quesito 1

*Three mutually tangent circles with common tangent: prove inequality*

> Circles of radii $r_1$, $r_2$ and $r_3$ touch each other externally, and they also have a common tangent at points $A$, $B$ and $C$ respectively, where $B$ lies between $A$ and $C$. Prove that $16r_1r_3(r_1+r_2+r_3) \ge 9r_2(r_1+r_3)^2$.
