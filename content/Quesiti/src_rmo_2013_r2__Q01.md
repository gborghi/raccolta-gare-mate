# Regional Mathematical Olympiad 2013 — Region 2 — Quesito 1

*No integer solutions to (x^5-1)/(x-1)=y^2+1*

> Prove that there do not exist natural numbers $x$ and $y$, with $x > 1$, such that
> $$\frac{x^5 - 1}{x - 1} = y^2 + 1.$$
