*Nine-digit tickets coloured by differing-in-all-places rule; find colour of 123123123*

> In a lottery, tickets are given nine-digit numbers using only the digits $1$, $2$, $3$. They are also coloured red, blue or green in such a way that two tickets whose numbers differ in all the nine places get different colours. Suppose the ticket bearing the number $122222222$ is red and that bearing the number $222222222$ is green. Determine, with proof, the colour of the ticket bearing the number $123123123$.
