*Locus of point P and lines through incentre with 3 common points*

> In a plane rectangular coordinate system $xOy$ there are three points $A\!\left(0,\, \dfrac{4}{3}\right)$, $B(-1,\, 0)$ and $C(1,\, 0)$. The distance from point $P$ to line $BC$ is the geometric mean of the distances from this point to lines $AB$ and $AC$.
> 
> (1) Find the locus of point $P$.
> 
> (2) If line $L$ passes through the incentre (say, $D$) of $\triangle ABC$ and has exactly 3 common points with the locus of point $P$, determine all values of the slope $k$ of line $L$.
