*Count non-congruent integer-sided triangles with given perimeter*

> How many different (i.e. pairwise non-congruent) triangles are there with integer sides and with integer perimeter $p$? (Express your answer in terms of $p$, considering cases according to the residue of $p$ modulo 12.)
