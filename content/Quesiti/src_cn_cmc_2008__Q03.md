*Expected number of games in ping-pong match*

> $A$ and $B$ are playing ping-pong, with the agreement that the winner will get 1 point and the loser 0 points; the match ends as soon as one of the players is ahead by 2 points or the number of games reaches six. Suppose the probabilities of $A$ and $B$ winning a game are $\frac{2}{3}$ and $\frac{1}{3}$, respectively, and each game is independent. Then the expectation $E\xi$ for the number of games $\xi$ is (  ).
> 
> (A) $\dfrac{241}{81}$ \quad (B) $\dfrac{266}{81}$ \quad (C) $\dfrac{374}{81}$ \quad (D) $\dfrac{670}{243}$
