*Inscribed-circle quadrilateral: four associated quantities are equal*

> $ABCD$ is a quadrilateral which has an inscribed circle. With the side $AB$ is associated
> $$u_{AB} = P_A \sin DAB + P_B \sin ABC$$
> where $P_A$, $P_B$ are the perpendiculars from $A$, $B$ respectively to the opposite side $CD$. Define $u_{BC}$, $u_{CD}$, $u_{DA}$ likewise, using in each case perpendiculars to the opposite side. Show that
> $$u_{AB} = u_{BC} = u_{CD} = u_{DA}.$$
