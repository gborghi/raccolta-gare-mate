*For m given positive integers, prove infinitely many n make a weighted power sum composite*

> Given an integer $m \geqslant 2$ and $m$ positive integers $a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_m$, prove that there exist infinitely many positive integers $n$ such that $a_1 \cdot 1^n + a_2 \cdot 2^n + \cdots + a_m \cdot m^n$ is composite. (Posed by Chen Yonggao)
