*Find $k$ so $x^{k+1}+x+1$ divisible by $x^2+x+1$, then by $x^4+x+1$*

> Find all positive integers $k$ such that the polynomial $x^{k+1} + x + 1$ is divisible by $x^2 + x + 1$.
> 
> For each such $k$, specify the integers $a$ such that $a^{k+1} + a + 1$ is divisible by $a^4 + a + 1$.
