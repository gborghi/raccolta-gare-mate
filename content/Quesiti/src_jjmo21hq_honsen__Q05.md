# Japan Junior Mathematical Olympiad 2023 — Honsen (Final) — Quesito 5

*Prove circumcircle of triangle PRN is tangent to BC*

> In triangle $ABC$, let $\Gamma$ be the circumcircle, and let $M$ be the midpoint of side $AC$. Let $P$ and $Q$ be two distinct points on segment $BC$ that are symmetric with respect to the midpoint of $BC$. Let $N$ be the midpoint of segment $PQ$ (so $N$ is the midpoint of $BC$). The line $AN$ meets $\Gamma$ again at a point $R$ (different from $A$). Prove that the circumcircle of triangle $PRN$ is tangent to line $BC$.
