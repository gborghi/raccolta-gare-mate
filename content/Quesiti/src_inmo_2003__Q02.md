*Find all primes p,q and even n>2 satisfying a sum equation*

> Find all prime $p$ and $q$, and even numbers $n > 2$, satisfying the equation
> $$p^n + p^{n-1} + \cdots + p + 1 = q^2 + q + 1.$$
