*Esmeralda has one mass each of 1..6; count how many distinct totals she can form.*

> Esmeralda has masses of $1,2,3,4,5$ and $6$ (one of each). Combining one or more of them, she can add $1,2,4,\dots$ obtaining all the weights from $1$ up to the total $1+2+3+4+5+6=21$. Determine how many different total weights (masses) Esmeralda can obtain (she can obtain $21$ different masses).
