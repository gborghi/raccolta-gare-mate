*Triangle with angle bisector and point on side; prove AD = DB + BC*

> In a triangle $ABC$, $\angle BAC = 100^\circ$ and $AB = AC$. A point $D$ is chosen on the side $AC$ so that $\angle ABD = \angle CBD$. Prove that $AD = DB + BC$.

*Triangolo con bisettore angolare e punto laterale; dimostrare AD = DB + BC*
