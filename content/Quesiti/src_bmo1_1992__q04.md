*Minimize and maximize a quadratic form subject to a linear constraint*

> Determine the smallest value of $x^2 + 5y^2 + 9z^2$, where $x$, $y$, $z$ are real numbers subject to the condition $ax + by + cz = 1$ (for given constants). Does the expression have a greatest value subject to the condition? Justify your claim.
