*Solve a cyclic system of five real equations relating squares to sums of two cubes.*

> Solve in real numbers $a, b, c, d, e$ the system of equations:
> $$a^2 = b^3 + c^3,$$
> $$b^2 = c^3 + d^3,$$
> $$c^2 = d^3 + e^3,$$
> $$d^2 = e^3 + a^3,$$
> $$e^2 = a^3 + b^3.$$
