# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2000 — Nível 1 — Fase 1 — Quesito 12

*Ages of father and son given past and future conditions*

> Hélio is now $18$ years old. Three times Hélio's age was precisely Hélio's father's age. Now the father is twice the age of his son. How many years old are Hélio and his son?
> 
> (A) $72$ years and $36$ years and $18$ years. \quad (B) $36$ years and $18$ years and $18$ years. \quad (C) $40$ years and $20$ years. \quad (D) $50$ years and $25$ years. \quad (E) $38$ years and $19$ years.
