*Sequence of integers with cubic recurrence: can more than half the terms be negative?*

> Let $x_1, x_2, \ldots, x_{2004}$ be a sequence of integers satisfying $x_{k+3} = x_{k+2} + x_{k+1} x_k$ for $1 \le k \le 2001$.
> 
> Is it possible for more than half of its terms to be negative?
