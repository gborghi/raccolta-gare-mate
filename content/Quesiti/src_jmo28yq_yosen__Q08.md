*Minimum of cyclic dot-product sum over three permutations*

> Let $a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_6$ and $b_1, b_2, \ldots, b_6$ and $c_1, c_2, \ldots, c_6$ each be a permutation of $1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6$. Find the minimum possible value of $$a_1 b_1 + a_2 b_2 + \cdots + a_6 b_6 + b_1 c_1 + b_2 c_2 + \cdots + b_6 c_6 + c_1 a_1 + c_2 a_2 + \cdots + c_6 a_6.$$
