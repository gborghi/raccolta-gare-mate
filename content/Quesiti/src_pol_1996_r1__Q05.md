*Triangle with obtuse angle CAB; PQ a segment with midpoint A; show BP + CQ >= (alpha/2) BC tangent relation*

> In a triangle $ABC$, the angle $\angle CAB$ is obtuse. Let $PQ$ be any segment whose midpoint is $A$. Show that $BP + CQ \ge \tan\frac{\angle CAB}{2}\cdot BC$.
