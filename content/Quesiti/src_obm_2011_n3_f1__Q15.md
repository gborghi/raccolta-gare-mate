# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2011 — Nível 3 — Fase 1 — Quesito 15

*Logic puzzle: maximum number of ET-nerds Bruberson spoke with among four speakers*

> On Planet Nerdia there exist three species of nerd: ET-nerds, UFO-nerds and OVNI-nerds. The first always tells the truth, the second always lies, and the third sometimes lies, sometimes tells the truth. Certainly Bruberson, a member of one of these species, meets four nerds and they say:
> 
> $X$: "Today is cloudy."
> $Y$: "The nerd who just finished speaking is lying."
> $Z$: "Today is not cloudy."
> $W$: "The first nerd to speak or I am an ET-nerd."
> 
> With how many ET-nerds did Bruberson speak, at most?
> 
> (A) 0 (B) 1 (C) 2 (D) 3 (E) 4
