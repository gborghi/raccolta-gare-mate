*Four 20 cm square sheets folded in sequence; find area of figure 4*

> Esmeralda has four equal square sheets of paper with side 20 cm. She folds one sheet over the other, aligning the four vertices of these sheets horizontally with the top vertex of the sheet below, as shown in figures 1 and 2. She continues doing this to attach all four sheets, as shown in figures 3 and 4. What is the area of figure 4?
