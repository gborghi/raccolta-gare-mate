*Monic degree-2 polynomial whose roots are Gauss periods of a primitive 7th root of unity; compute p(3)*

> Let $\zeta\in\mathbb{C}$ be a root of $x^7-1$ with $\zeta\neq 1$. There exists a monic polynomial $p$ of degree $2$ with integer coefficients whose roots are the numbers $z_1=\zeta+\zeta^2+\zeta^4$ and $z_2=\zeta^3+\zeta^5+\zeta^6$. Compute $p(3)$.
