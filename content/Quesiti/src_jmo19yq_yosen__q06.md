*Volume of tetrahedron from edges and angles at O*

> The tetrahedron $OABC$ satisfies $OA=3$, $OB=4$, $OC=5$ and $\angle AOB=\angle AOC=45^\circ$, $\angle BOC=60^\circ$. Find the volume of the tetrahedron $OABC$. Here $XY$ denotes the length of segment $XY$.
