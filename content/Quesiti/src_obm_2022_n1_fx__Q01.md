# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2022 — Nível 1 — Fase x — Quesito 1

*Pyramid of same-size cubes: count base, painted faces*

> Janaína built a figure in the shape of a pyramid, as shown in the drawing below. The figure was made by gluing cubes of the same size. There is 1 cube in the first (top) layer, 3 cubes in the second, and 6 cubes in the third.
> 
> (a) How many cubes did she use in the base (fourth layer)? She painted all the faces of the figure, including the lateral faces and the bottom, because they do not appear in the drawing.
> 
> (b) How many cubes will have exactly 3 faces painted?
> 
> (c) How many cubes will have exactly 1 face painted?
