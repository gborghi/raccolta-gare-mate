*Find max and min of cyclic expression over unit cube*

> (a) Find the maximum value of the expression $x^2 y + y^2 z + z^2 x$ when $0 \le x \le 1$, $0 \le y \le 1$, $0 \le z \le 1$.
> 
> (b) Find the minimum value of the expression $x^2 y + y^2 z + z^2 x$ when $0 \le x \le 1$, $0 \le y \le 1$, $0 \le z \le 1$.
