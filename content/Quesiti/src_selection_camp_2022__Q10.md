*Find all n such that divisors can be arranged with all partial sums perfect squares*

> Let $n$ be a positive integer and let $d(n)$ denote the number of positive divisors of $n$. Find all positive integers $n$ for which there exists a sequence $a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_{d(n)}$ consisting of all distinct positive divisors of $n$ such that for every positive integer $i \le d(n)$, $a_1 + a_2 + \cdots + a_i$ is a perfect square.
