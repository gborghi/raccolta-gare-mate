*Counting solutions of an equation involving the floor of x over [38,2053]*

> Rewrite the given equation as $\lfloor x\rfloor=\frac{\lfloor x\rfloor-38}{2016}$ (the source writes $\{x\}=\frac{\lfloor x\rfloor-38}{2016}$). Since $0\le\{x\}<1$, we need $0\le\frac{\lfloor x\rfloor-38}{2016}<1$, i.e. $38\le\lfloor x\rfloor\le 2054$, so $1<\lfloor x\rfloor\le\dots\le 2054$. As $\lfloor x\rfloor$ is an integer it ranges over $38,\dots,2053$, a total of $2016$ values, and for each value there is exactly one $x$; therefore there are $2016$ solutions. How many solutions does the equation have? [Answer key value: $2016$.]
