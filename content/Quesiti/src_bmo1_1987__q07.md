*Prove existence of x such that x(x+1)/2 - k is divisible by 2^n*

> Prove that if $n$ and $k$ are any positive integers then there exists a positive integer $x$ such that $\frac{1}{2}x(x+1) - k$ is divisible by $2^n$.
