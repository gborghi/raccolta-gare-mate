*Five positive integers with given product-sum conditions, find total*

> Five positive integers $a, b, c, d, e$ greater than one satisfy the following conditions:
> $$a(b+c+d+e)=128$$
> $$b(a+c+d+e)=155$$
> $$c(a+b+d+e)=203$$
> $$d(a+b+c+e)=243$$
> $$e(a+b+c+d)=275$$
> How much is $a+b+c+d+e$?
> (A) $9$ \quad (B) $16$ \quad (C) $25$ \quad (D) $36$ \quad (E) $49$
