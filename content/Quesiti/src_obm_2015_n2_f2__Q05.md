*Find integer closest to z/y given a system in x,y,z*

> The real numbers $x$, $y$, and $z$ satisfy the system
> $$\frac{x}{y} + \frac{y}{z} = 2015, \qquad \frac{y}{z} + \frac{z}{x} = 37.$$
> Determine the integer closest to $\dfrac{z}{y}$.
