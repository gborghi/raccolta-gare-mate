*Mappings on subsets respecting intersection-min, limit of n-th root*

> Given a fixed integer $c \ge 1$, let $a(n)$ be the number of mappings $w$ from the subsets of $\{1, 2, \ldots, n\}$ to the integers $1, 2, \ldots, c$ such that
> $$w(A \cap B) = \min\{w(A), w(B)\} \quad \text{for any two subsets } A, B \text{ of } \{1, \ldots, n\}.$$
> Compute $\lim_{n \to \infty} \sqrt[n]{a(n)}$.
