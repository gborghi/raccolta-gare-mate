*Min S for 5x9 grid summing to 1 with bounded 2x3 blocks*

> There is a $5 \times 9$ grid of cells. In each cell we write one nonnegative real number (a real number $\ge 0$), so that the following conditions are satisfied:
> (i) The total sum of the $45$ written real numbers is $1$.
> (ii) However a $2 \times 3$ rectangular frame is placed onto the grid (oriented either vertically or horizontally, in any aligned position), the sum of the $6$ numbers inside it is at most $S$.
> Find the minimum value of the real number $S$ for which such a way of writing the numbers exists.
