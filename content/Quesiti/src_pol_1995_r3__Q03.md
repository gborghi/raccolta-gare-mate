*Sequence mod p defined by recursion; find remainder of a_n divided by p*

> Let $p > 3$ be a prime and let $q = p^2$. The sequence $(a_n)$ is defined by
> $$a_n = \begin{cases} n & \text{for } n = 0, 1, 2, \ldots, p-1; \\ a_{n-1} + a_{n-p} & \text{for } n \ge p. \end{cases}$$
> Determine the remainder when $a_q$ is divided by $p$.
