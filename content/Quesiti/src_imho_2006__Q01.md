*Point in triangle interior with angle and equality condition*

> Let $ABC$ be a triangle with incentre $I$. A point $P$ in the interior of the triangle satisfies
> $$\angle PBA + \angle PCA = \angle PBC + \angle PCB.$$
> Show that $AP \ge AI$, and that equality holds if and only if $P = I$.
