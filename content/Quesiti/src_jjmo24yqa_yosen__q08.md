*Triangle cevians; circumcircles of corner triangles through incenter of inner triangle; find AB/AC*

> In triangle $ABC$, let $P$, $Q$, $R$ be points on sides $BC$, $CA$, $AB$ respectively (excluding endpoints), with
> $$BP : PC = 6 : 1, \quad CQ : QA = 5 : 2, \quad AR : RB = 4 : 3.$$
> Suppose that the circumcircle of each of triangles $ARQ$, $BPR$, and $CQP$ passes through the incenter of triangle $PQR$. Find the value of $\dfrac{AB}{AC}$.
