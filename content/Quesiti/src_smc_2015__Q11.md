*Number of distinct heights of towers built from blocks of height 4cm, 6cm, or 10cm*

> Rahid has a large number of cubic building blocks. Each block has sides of length 4 cm, 6 cm or 10 cm. Rahid makes little towers of the same height by stacking three of the blocks on top of each other. How many different heights of tower can he make?
>
> - **(A)** $6$
> - **(B)** $8$
> - **(C)** $9$
> - **(D)** $12$
> - **(E)** $27$
