*Positive reals x,y,z satisfy 1/3 ≤ xy+yz+zx ≤ 3; find range of xyz and x+y+z.*

> Let $x, y, z$ be positive real numbers satisfying
> $$\frac{1}{3} \le xy + yz + zx \le 3.$$
> Determine the range of values for (i) $xyz$, and (ii) $x + y + z$.
