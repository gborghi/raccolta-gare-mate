# British Mathematical Olympiad 2010-11 — Round 1 — Quesito 1

*Remove one integer from 1..n; average of remaining is 163/4*

> One number is removed from the set of integers from $1$ to $n$. The average of the remaining numbers is $\dfrac{163}{4}$. Determine all possible values of the removed number.
