*Express a telescoping product quotient in the form a+b√2*

> Express the following quantity in the form $a + b\sqrt{2}$, where $a$ and $b$ are rational numbers:
> $$\frac{(1 \times 4 + \sqrt{2})(2 \times 5 + \sqrt{2}) \cdots (10 \times 13 + \sqrt{2})}{(2 \times 2 - 2)(3 \times 3 - 2) \cdots (11 \times 11 - 2)}.$$
