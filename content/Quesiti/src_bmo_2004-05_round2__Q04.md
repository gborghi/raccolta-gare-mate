# British Mathematical Olympiad 2004-05 — Round 2 — Quesito 4

*3-element subsets of {1,...,36} pairwise intersecting but empty total intersection; show n≤100*

> Let $X = \{A_1, A_2, \ldots, A_n\}$ be a collection of 3-element subsets of $\{1, 2, \ldots, 36\}$ such that:
> (i) $A_i$ and $A_j$ have non-empty intersection for every $i, j$;
> (ii) The intersection of all sets in $X$ is empty.
> Show that $n \le 100$. How many such sets $X$ are there when $n = 100$?
