# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2006 — Nível Universitário — Fase 2 — Quesito 3

*Billiard on elliptic table: prove ball returns to B after A-B-C-A path*

> A billiard table has the shape of an ellipse and has no pockets. When a ball hits a point $P$ on the edge of the table, it follows a direction symmetric with respect to the normal to the ellipse at $P$. Prove that if a ball starts from a point $A$ on the ellipse and, after bouncing at points $B$ and $C$, returns to $A$, then it will bounce at $B$ again.
