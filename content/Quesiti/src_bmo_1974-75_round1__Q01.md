# British Mathematical Olympiad 1974-75 — Round 1 — Quesito 1

*Sum of floor expressions equals 600; find and prove k*

> Given that $k$ is a solution of
> $$\left[\frac{k}{1}\right] + \left[\frac{k}{2}\right] + \left[\frac{k}{3}\right] + \cdots + \left[\sqrt[3]{k}\right] = 600$$
> (where $[x]$ means the integral part of $x$) find and prove your solution is complete.
