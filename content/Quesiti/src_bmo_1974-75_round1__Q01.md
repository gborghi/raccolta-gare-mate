# British Mathematical Olympiad 1974-75 — Round 1 — Quesito 1

*Solve floor-of-square-roots sum equal to 400*

> Given that $x$ is a positive integer solve
> $$[\sqrt{1}] + [\sqrt{2}] + \ldots + [\sqrt{x^2-1}] = 400$$
> (where $[z]$ means the integral part of $z$) and prove your solution is complete.
