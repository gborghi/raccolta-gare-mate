*Prove angle equivalence in triangle using midpoints*

> Let $ABC$ be a triangle, and $D$, $E$, $F$ be the midpoints of $BC$, $CA$, $AB$ respectively. Prove that $\angle DAC = \angle ABE$ if and only if $\angle AFC = \angle ADB$.
