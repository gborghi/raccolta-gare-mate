*Sum of products of trinomial coefficients over triples (a,b,c) with a+b+c=5*

> For all triples $(a, b, c)$ of non-negative integers satisfying $a + b + c = 5$, compute
> $$\sum_{\substack{a,b,c \ge 0 \\ a+b+c=5}} \binom{5}{a}\binom{5}{b}\binom{5}{c}.$$
> The answer should be expressed as a numerical value without a calculator.
