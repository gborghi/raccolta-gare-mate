*Sum of all numbers given means of 5 largest and 5 smallest*

> Some numbers are written on the board. The arithmetic mean of the $5$ largest is $68$ and the arithmetic mean of the $5$ smallest is $44$. The sum of all the numbers is:
> (A) $560$ \quad (B) $504$ \quad (C) $112$ \quad (D) $56$ \quad (E) $70$
