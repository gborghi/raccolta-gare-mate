# Coupe Animath Automne 2024 — Quesito 15

*Find largest α such that for all x_1,...,x_2024 some index i gives sum of fractional parts ≤ α*

> Determine the largest real number $\alpha$ having the following property:
> 
> For all real numbers $x_1, \ldots, x_{2024}$, there always exists an index $i$ with $1 \le i \le 2024$ such that
> $$\{x_i - x_1\} + \{x_i - x_2\} + \{x_i - x_3\} + \cdots + \{x_i - x_{2024}\} \le \alpha.$$
> 
> We recall that $\{x\}$ denotes the fractional part of the real number $x$, that is, $\{x\} = x - \lfloor x \rfloor$ where $\lfloor x \rfloor$ is the integer part of $x$ (the largest integer less than or equal to $x$). For example, $\{1.2\} = 0.2$, $\{3\} = 0$, and $\{-2.4\} = 0.6$.
