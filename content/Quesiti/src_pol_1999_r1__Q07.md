*Find all degree-n polynomials with exactly n real roots not exceeding -1 satisfying a_0^2+a_1 a_n=a_n^2+a_0 a_{n-1}.*

> Let $n \ge 2$ be an integer. Find all polynomials $P(x) = a_0 + a_1 x + \cdots + a_n x^n$ having exactly $n$ real roots not exceeding $-1$ and satisfying $$a_0^2 + a_1 a_n = a_n^2 + a_0 a_{n-1}.$$
