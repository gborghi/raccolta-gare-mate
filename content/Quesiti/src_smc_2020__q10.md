*Count people standing between Tara and Uma in a line of six friends*

> Six friends Pat, Qasim, Roman, Sam, Tara and Uma stand in a line for a photograph. There are three people standing between Pat and Qasim, two between Qasim and Roman, and one between Roman and Sam. Sam is not at either end of the line. How many people are standing between Tara and Uma?
>
> - **(A)** $4$
> - **(B)** $3$
> - **(C)** $2$
> - **(D)** $1$
> - **(E)** $0$
