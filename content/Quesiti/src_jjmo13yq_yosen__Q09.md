*Find angle PAC in isosceles triangle with interior point P*

> Let $ABC$ be an isosceles triangle with $AB = AC$, and let $P$ be a point inside it. Given that $\angle BAC = 70^\circ$, $\angle PBC = 30^\circ$, and $\angle APC = 150^\circ$, find the measure of $\angle PAC$. Here $XY$ denotes the length of segment $XY$.

**Topic:** [[topic_geometria_piana|Geometria piana]], [[topic_trigonometria|Trigonometria]]
**Metodo:** [[method_trigonometria|Trigonometria]]
**Abilita:** [[skill_ragionamento_geometrico|Ragionamento geometrico]]
**Area:** [[Geometria]]
**Risposta:** $\frac{35}{2}^\circ$
