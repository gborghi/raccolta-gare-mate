# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2006 — Nível Universitário — Fase 1 — Quesito 6

*Probability that minimum pairwise distance exceeds m*

> Choose three points $x_1, x_2, x_3$ at random, independently and with uniform distribution on $[0, 1]$. Determine, as a function of the positive number $m$, the probability that
> $$\min\{\,|x_1 - x_2|,\; |x_1 - x_3|,\; |x_2 - x_3|\,\} > m.$$
