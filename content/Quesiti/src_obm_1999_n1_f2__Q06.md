*Find smallest square table tileable by an L-tromino-like piece*

> Find the smallest square table that can be tiled using pieces of the following shape:
> 
> $$\begin{array}{|c|c|}\hline\phantom{X} & \phantom{X} \\ \hline \phantom{X} & \\\cline{1-1}\end{array}$$
> 
> (An L-shaped tromino of 3 unit squares arranged in an L.)
> 
> \textit{Note:} Tiling means completely covering, without overlapping pieces, so that no piece extends outside the table.
