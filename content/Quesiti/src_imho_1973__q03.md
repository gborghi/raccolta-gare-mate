*Real a,b: quartic equation has at least one real root, find min value*

> Let $a$ and $b$ be real numbers for which the equation
> $$x^4 + ax^3 + bx^2 + ax + 1 = 0$$
> has at least one real solution. For all such pairs $(a, b)$, find the minimum value of $b$.
