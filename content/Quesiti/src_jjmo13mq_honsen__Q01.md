*Cyclic quadrilateral, perpendicular line passes through circumcenter*

> Let $ABCD$ be a quadrilateral inscribed in a circle. Let $P$ be a point, different from $A$ and $B$, such that $\angle PAC = \angle PBD = 90^\circ$. Prove that the line through $P$ perpendicular to $CD$ passes through the circumcenter of triangle $PAB$.
