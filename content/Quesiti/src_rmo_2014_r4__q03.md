*Find all pairs m>n of positive integers with gcd chain equal to gcd(m,2m-n)*

> Determine all pairs $m > n$ of positive integers such that
> $$1 = \gcd(n+1,\, m+1) = \gcd(n+2,\, m+2) = \cdots = \gcd(m,\, 2m-n).$$
