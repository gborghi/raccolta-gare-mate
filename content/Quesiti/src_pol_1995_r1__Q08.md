*Regular n-gon pyramid: prove sin²α − sin²β ≥ tan²(π/n)*

> In a regular pyramid with a regular $n$-gon as a base, a lateral face and the base form an angle $\alpha$, while a lateral edge and the base edge form an angle $\beta$. Prove that
> $$\sin^2 \alpha - \sin^2 \beta \ge \tan^2 \frac{\pi}{n}.$$
