*Find all real c making an inequality hold for all positive x, y*

> Let $a$ be a positive number. Determine all real numbers $c$ with the property that, for any positive numbers $x$, $y$, the following inequality holds:
> $$(c - 1)x^{c+1} \le (cy - x)y^a.$$
