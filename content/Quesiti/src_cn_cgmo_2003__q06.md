*At most half of proper divisors of n end in digit 5*

> Let $n \ge 2$ be a positive integer, and let $S_n$ be the set of all positive integer divisors of $n$ (not including $1$ and $n$ itself). Prove that at most half of the elements in $S_n$ have their last digits equal to $5$. (posed by Feng Zanting)
