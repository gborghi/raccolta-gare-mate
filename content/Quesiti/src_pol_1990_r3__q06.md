*Prove divisibility by 3 of an alternating sum of binomial coefficients for integers n>2.*

> Prove that for all integers $n > 2$, $\sum_{k=0}^{\lfloor n/3 \rfloor} (-1)^k \binom{n}{3k}$ is divisible by 3.
