# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2011 — Nível 3 — Fase 1 — Quesito 19

*Number of integers n>1 such that 2011 is a sum of n distinct positive integers*

> There exist 3 values of integers $n > 1$ such that $10$ can be written as a sum of $n$ distinct positive integers:
> 
> $n = 2: 10 = 3 + 7$
> $n = 3: 10 = 2 + 3 + 5$
> $n = 4: 10 = 1 + 2 + 3 + 4$
> 
> How many values of integers $n > 1$ exist for which it is possible to express 2011 as a sum of $n$ distinct positive integers?
> 
> (A) 59 (B) 60 (C) 61 (D) 62 (E) 63
