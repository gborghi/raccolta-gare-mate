*Ratio of area of triangle ABC to sub-triangle using midpoints*

> Let $O$ be an interior point of $\triangle ABC$ such that $\overrightarrow{OA} + 2\,\overrightarrow{OB} + 3\,\overrightarrow{OC} = \vec{0}$. Then the ratio of the area of $\triangle ABC$ to the area of $\triangle AOC$ is ( ).
> 
> (A) $2$ \quad (B) $\dfrac{5}{2}$ \quad (C) $3$ \quad (D) $\dfrac{7}{2}$
