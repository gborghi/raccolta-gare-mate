# IMO 1993 (34th IMO) — Quesito 2

*Compute ratio and prove tangents perpendicular for interior D*

> Let D be a point inside acute triangle ABC such that ̸ ADB =
> ̸ ACB + π/2 and AC · BD = AD · BC.
> (a) Calculate the ratio (AB · CD)/(AC · BD).
> (b) Prove that the tangents at C to the circumcircles of △ACD and
> △BCD are perpendicular.
