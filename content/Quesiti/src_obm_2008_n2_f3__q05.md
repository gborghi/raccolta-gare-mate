*Ant turning 60 degrees each step: return after 2008 or 2009 steps?*

> An ant walks in the plane in the following manner: initially, it walks $1\,\text{cm}$ in any direction. After each step, it changes the direction of its trajectory by $60^\circ$ to the left or to the right, and walks $1\,\text{cm}$ in that new direction. Is it possible for the ant to return to its starting point in
> (a) $2008$ steps?
> (b) $2009$ steps?
