*Prime p divides product of (k^3+1); prove p ≤ n+1*

> Let $n$ be a strictly positive integer. Let $p$ be a prime number such that
> $$p \mid (1^3 + 1)(2^3 + 1)(3^3 + 1)\cdots((n-1)^3 + 1)(n^3 + 1).$$
> Show that $p \le n + 1$.
