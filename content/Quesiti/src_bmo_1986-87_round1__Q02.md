# British Mathematical Olympiad 1986-87 — Round 1 — Quesito 2

*Point on side of triangle, prove angle bisector property*

> In a triangle $ABC$, $\angle BAC = 100^\circ$ and $AB = AC$. A point $D$ is chosen on the side $AC$ so that $\angle ABD = \angle CBD$. Prove that $AD + DB = BC$.
