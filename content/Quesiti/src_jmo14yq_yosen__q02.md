*Find CD in triangle ABC with point D on BC, given sides and equal angles.*

> In triangle $ABC$, let $D$ be a point on segment $BC$ such that $AB = AD = 2$, $BD = 1$, and $\angle BAD = \angle CAD$. Find the length $CD$.
