# British Mathematical Olympiad 1997-98 — Round 1 — Quesito 1

*5×5 Latin square; show sum of entries below main diagonal is 20*

> A $5 \times 5$ square is divided into $25$ unit squares. One of the numbers $1, 2, 3, 4, 5$ is inserted into each of the unit squares in such a way that each row, each column and each of the two diagonals contains each of the five numbers once and only once. The number at the top left is $1$. The squares below the diagonal from top left to bottom right form the diagonal strip.
> 
> Show that the sum of the numbers in the diagonal strip is $20$.
