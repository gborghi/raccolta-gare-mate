*Prove angle BPE = 90 degrees in acute triangle*

> Let $ABC$ be an acute triangle with $AB < AC$. The perpendicular bisector of segment $BC$ meets lines $AB$ and $AC$ at points $D$ and $E$ respectively, and let $M$ be the midpoint of segment $DE$. The circumcircle of triangle $ABC$ and line $AM$ meet at a point $P$ other than $A$, and the three points $M, A, P$ lie in this order. Prove that $\angle BPE = 90^\circ$. (Here $XY$ denotes the length of segment $XY$.)
