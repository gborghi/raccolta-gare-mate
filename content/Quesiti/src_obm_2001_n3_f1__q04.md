*Pear 90% water, dehydrate to 60% water; find litres evaporated from 100 kg*

> A pear is about 90% water and 10% solid matter. A producer places 100 kilograms of pears to dehydrate until the water represents 60% of the total mass. How many litres of water will be evaporated? (Recall: 1 litre of water has a mass of 1 kilogram.)
> 
> **A)** 15 litres $\quad$ **B)** 45 litres $\quad$ **C)** 75 litres $\quad$ **D)** 80 litres $\quad$ **E)** 30 litres
