# IMO 1984 (25th International Mathematical Olympiad) — Quesito 5

*Bound diagonal-sum to perimeter ratio of convex polygon*

> Let $d$ be the sum of the lengths of all the diagonals of a plane convex polygon with $n$ vertices ($n > 3$), and let $p$ be its perimeter. Prove that
> $$n - 3 < \frac{2d}{p} < \left[\frac{n}{2}\right]\left[\frac{n+1}{2}\right] - 2,$$
> where $[x]$ denotes the greatest integer not exceeding $x$.
