*Maximize d_n - c_n for n girls and n boys around a round table*

> Find a way of arranging $n$ girls and $n$ boys around a round table for which $d_n - c_n$ is maximum, where $d_n$ is the number of girls sitting between two boys and $c_n$ is the number of boys sitting between two girls.
