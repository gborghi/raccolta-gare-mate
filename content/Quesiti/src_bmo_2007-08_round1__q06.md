*Recurrence on positive integers; prove integer values and count solutions to f(n)=2n*

> The function $f$ is defined on the set of positive integers by $f(1) = 1$, $f(2) = 2$, and
> $$f(n+1) = (1 + (2n + 1)f(n))f(n-1)$$
> for $n \geq 2$.
> 
> (i) Prove that $f(n)$ is always an integer.
> (ii) For how many positive integers less than $2007$ is $f(n) = 2n$?
