*Square KLMN contains nested squares PQRS and TUVW, each obtained by dividing the previous side in ratio 1:2; find the shaded fraction.*

> The diagram shows a square $KLMN$. A second square $PQRS$ is drawn inside it, where $P$ divides the side $KL$ in the ratio $1:2$. Similarly, a third square $TUVW$ is drawn inside $PQRS$ with $T$ dividing $PQ$ in the ratio $1:2$. What fraction of the area of $KLMN$ is shaded?
>
> - **(A)** $\frac{25}{81}$
> - **(B)** $\frac{15}{81}$
> - **(C)** $\frac{20}{81}$
> - **(D)** $\frac{20}{81}$
> - **(E)** $\frac{1}{3}$
