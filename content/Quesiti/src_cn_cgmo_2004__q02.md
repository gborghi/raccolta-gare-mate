*Minimize a sum of three fractions in positive reals*

> Let $a$, $b$ and $c$ be positive real numbers. Determine the minimum value of
> $$\frac{a+3c}{a+2b+c} + \frac{4b}{a+b+2c} - \frac{8c}{a+b+3c}.$$
