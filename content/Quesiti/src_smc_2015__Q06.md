*Numbers 5-10 placed in circles; sum of touching circles is prime; which number is in the shaded circle?*

> The numbers $5, 6, 7, 8, 9, 10$ are to be placed, one in each of the circles in the diagram, so that the sum of the numbers in each pair of touching circles is a prime number. The number $5$ is placed in the top circle. Which number is placed in the shaded circle?
>
> - **(A)** $6$
> - **(B)** $7$
> - **(C)** $8$
> - **(D)** $9$
> - **(E)** $10$
