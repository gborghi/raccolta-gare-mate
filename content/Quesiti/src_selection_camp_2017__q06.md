*Find all real-valued functions satisfying a max functional equation*

> Find all functions $f$ defined on the reals taking real values such that $f(0) \neq 0$ and, for all real numbers $x, y$,
> $$f(x+y)^2 = 2f(x)f(y) + \max\{f(x^2) + f(y^2),\, f(x^2 + y^2)\}.$$
