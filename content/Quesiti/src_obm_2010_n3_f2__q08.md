*Solve integer system x+y+z=77, xy+yz+2x+xyz=946 with x≤y≤z non-negative*

> Solve the system
> $$\begin{cases} x + y + z = 77 \\ xy + yz + 2x + xyz = 946 \end{cases}$$
> where $x \le y \le z$ are non-negative integers.
