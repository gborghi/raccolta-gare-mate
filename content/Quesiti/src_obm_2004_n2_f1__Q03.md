# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2004 — Nível 2 — Fase 1 — Quesito 3

*Height of banknote pile for monthly salary converted to cruzados*

> Between 1986 and 1989, an era when most of you had not yet been born, Brazil's currency was the cruzado (Cz$). With a small monthly inflation, prices rose a little each month: prices were given in cruzados, then cruzeiros, then cruzeiros reais, and finally in reais. The conversion between cruzado and real is:
> $$1 \text{ real} = 2{.}750{.}000{.}000 \text{ cruzados}$$
> Imagine that the value of the real coin had never changed and that Jo\~ao, who today earns 640 reais per month, had received the same salary in notes of 1 cruzado. If 100 new notes are 1.5 cm thick, what would be the height of the pile of Jo\~ao's salary in notes of 1 cruzado?
> 
> **A)** $26.4$ km $\quad$ **B)** $264$ km $\quad$ **C)** $26400$ km $\quad$ **D)** $264000$ km $\quad$ **E)** $2640000$ km
