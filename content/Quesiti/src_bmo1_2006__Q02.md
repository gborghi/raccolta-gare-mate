*Count ways to split six twin-pairs into two equal teams with no twins together*

> Afrifan teaches a class of six pairs of twins. He wishes to set teams for a quiz, splitting the 12 students into two teams of six. Subject to this condition that no pair of twins is in the same team:
> 
> (i) In how many ways can he split them into two teams of six?
> 
> (ii) In how many ways can he split them into two teams of four?
