*Find positive integers m (even) and n (odd) with 1/m+1/n=4/2001*

> Find all positive integers $m$, $n$, where $n$ is odd, that satisfy
> $$\frac{1}{m} + \frac{1}{n} = \frac{4}{2001}.$$
