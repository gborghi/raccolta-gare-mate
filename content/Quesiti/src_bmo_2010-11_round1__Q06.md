*Integers a,b,c with a^2+b^2-c^2=ab-bc-ca; prove triangle with 60 degree angle*

> Let $a$ and $b$ be positive integers and let $c$ be a positive integer less than $a + b$. Suppose that $a^2 + b^2 - c^2 = ab - bc - ca$. Show that $a$, $b$, $c$ are the sides of a triangle that contains an angle of $60^\circ$.
