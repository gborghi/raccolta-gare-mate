*100 reals on circle, each ≤ sum of two neighbors; max number of strictly positive ones*

> One hundred real numbers are placed around a circle, so that each number is at most equal to the sum of its two neighbors (going clockwise). Determine the maximum number of strictly positive real numbers that can appear on this circle.
