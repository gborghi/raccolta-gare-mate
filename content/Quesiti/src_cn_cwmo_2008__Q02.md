*In an isosceles triangle with inscribed circle, prove a concyclicity and a ratio relation involving points built from the incircle*

> In $\triangle ABC$, $AB = AC$, the inscribed circle $I$ touches $BC$, $CA$ and $AB$ at points $D$, $E$ and $F$ respectively. $P$ is a point on arc $\widehat{EF}$ (not containing $D$). Line $BP$ intersects the circle $I$ at another point $Q$, and lines $EP$, $EQ$ meet line $BC$ at $M$, $N$ respectively. Prove that
> (1) $P$, $F$, $B$, $M$ are concyclic.
> (2) $\frac{EM}{EN} = \frac{BD}{BP}$.
> (Posed by Bian Hongping)
