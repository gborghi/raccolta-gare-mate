*Find all real roots of composition P(Q(x)) = 0*

> Let $P(x) = x^2 + \frac{1}{2}x + b$ and $Q(x) = x^2 + cx + d$ be two polynomials with real coefficients such that $P(x)Q(x) = Q(P(x))$ for all real $x$. Find all the real roots of $P(Q(x)) = 0$.
