*Assign digits 1–9 to variables; find minimum possible maximum triple product sum*

> $a, b, c, d, e, f, g, h, i$ are mutually distinct integers each chosen from $1$ to $9$. Let $N$ be the maximum value of $a \times b \times c + d \times e \times f + g \times h \times i$ over all such assignments. Find the minimum value of $N$.
