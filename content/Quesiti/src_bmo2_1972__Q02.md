*Prove lower bound 2 for cyclic sum S of four positive reals*

> $a, b, c, d$ are positive numbers and
> $$S = \frac{a+b}{a+b+c} + \frac{b+c}{b+c+d} + \frac{c+d}{c+d+a} + \frac{d+a}{d+a+b}.$$
> Prove that $S$ is not less than 2, and obtain necessary conditions for $S = 4$.
