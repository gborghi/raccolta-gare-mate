*Anna buys books and shelves; count dates where books is multiple of shelves.*

> On Thursday 1st January 2015, Anna buys one book and one shelf. For the next two years, she buys one book every day and one shelf on alternate Thursdays, so the next shelf is bought on 15th January 2015. On how many dates in the period Thursday 1st January 2015 until Saturday 31st December 2016 inclusive does Anna have a number of books which is an exact multiple of the number of shelves she has?
