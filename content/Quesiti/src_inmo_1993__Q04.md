# Indian National Mathematical Olympiad 1993 — Quesito 4

*Locus of points with equal circumradii for sub-triangles*

> Find the set of all points $P$ in the set of a triangle $ABC$ such that $P \neq A, B, C$ and the triangles $ABP$, $BCP$, and $CAP$ have the same circumradius.
