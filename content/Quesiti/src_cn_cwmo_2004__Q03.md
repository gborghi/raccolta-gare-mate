*Find all k such that a 4-variable quadratic inequality holds on [-1,+∞)*

> Find all real numbers $k$ such that the inequality $a^2 + b^2 + c^2 + d^2 + 1 \ge k(a + b + c + d)$ holds for any $a, b, c, d \in [-1, +\infty)$.
