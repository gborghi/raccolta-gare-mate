*Two players write two integers each; product/sum conditions; find all possible sums of B's numbers*

> Players A and B each write two integers on a blackboard. The product of A's two numbers is twice the sum of B's two numbers, and the sum of A's two numbers is at least as large as the product of B's two numbers. Find all possible values of the sum of B's two numbers. (The four numbers written are not necessarily distinct.)
