*Real numbers x,y,z with sum and sum of squares conditions; show x_i in [1/2,2]*

> If real numbers $x, y, z$ satisfy $x + y + z = 4$ and $x^2 + y^2 + z^2 = 6$, show that each of $x, y, z$ lies in the segment $\left[\tfrac{1}{2}, 2\right]$. Can $x$ attain either of the endpoints of the segment?
