*Complex numbers with three symmetric sum equations, find m+n for x/yz+y/zx+z/xy=m/n*

> Let $x, y, z$ be complex numbers such that
> $$\frac{x}{y+z} + \frac{y}{z+x} + \frac{z}{x+y} = 9,$$
> $$\frac{x^2}{y+z} + \frac{y^2}{z+x} + \frac{z^2}{x+y} = 64,$$
> $$\frac{x^3}{y+z} + \frac{y^3}{z+x} + \frac{z^3}{x+y} = 488.$$
> If $\frac{x}{yz} + \frac{y}{zx} + \frac{z}{xy} = \frac{m}{n}$ where $m, n$ are nonzero integers with $\gcd(m, n) = 1$, find $m + n$.
