*Quadrilateral with perpendicular diagonals; find fourth side*

> In quadrilateral $ABCD$, we have $AB = 5$, $BC = 7$, $CD = 6$, and the diagonals $AC$ and $BD$ intersect perpendicularly inside the quadrilateral. Find $DA$.
