*Find minimum beta for fraction strictly between two rationals*

> Let $\alpha$ and $\beta$ be positive integers such that
> $$\frac{43}{197} < \frac{\beta}{\alpha} < \frac{17}{77}.$$
> Find the minimum possible value of $\beta$.
