*Prove $x^2y^2(x^2+y^2)\le 2$ for positive reals with $x+y=2$*

> Let $x, y$ be positive reals such that $x + y = 2$. Prove that
> $$x^2 y^2 (x^2 + y^2) \le 2.$$
