# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2016 — Nível 1 — Fase 3 — Quesito 6

*Convex non-cyclic quadrilateral; circumcenters of bisector quadrilaterals and radical axis point are collinear*

> Let $ABCD$ be a convex quadrilateral, not cyclic, with no parallel sides. The lines $AB$ and $CD$ meet at $L$. Let $M \ne L$ be the second intersection point of the circumcircles of triangles $ADL$ and $BCL$. The internal bisectors of $ABCD$ determine a convex cyclic quadrilateral with circumcenter $I$, and the external bisectors of $ABCD$ determine a convex cyclic quadrilateral with circumcenter $J$. Prove that $I$, $J$, and $M$ are collinear.
