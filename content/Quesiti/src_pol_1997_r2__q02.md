*Prove ratio identity for point P inside triangle with equal angles*

> Let $P$ be the point inside a triangle $ABC$ such that $\angle PBA = \angle PCA$. Prove that
> $$\frac{AC}{AB + PC} = \frac{AB}{AC + PB}.$$
