# Japan Mathematical Olympiad 2012 — Yosen (Preliminary) — Quesito 8

*Count sequences starting at 2012, ending at 1, each term less than sqrt of previous*

> In how many ways can one write a sequence of positive integers satisfying the following condition?
> 
> Condition: The first number written is $2012$ and the last number written is $1$. After writing a number $n$, the next number written is a positive integer less than $\sqrt{n}$.
