*A1-4: hyperbola x^2/a^2 - y^2/b^2=1, line of inclination π/4 through a vertex and (2,3); eccentricity.*

> [Test Paper A1, Problem 4] In the plane rectangular coordinate system, given hyperbola $\Gamma:\dfrac{x^2}{a^2}-\dfrac{y^2}{b^2}=1\ (a,b>0)$, a line with inclination angle $\dfrac{\pi}{4}$ passes through a vertex of $\Gamma$ and another point $(2,3)$ on it. Then the eccentricity of $\Gamma$ is ______.
