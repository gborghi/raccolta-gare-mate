*Functional equation f(x+y)=x+f(y), find f(2000)*

> Let $f$ be a function with the following properties:
> (i) For all reals $x,y$, $f(x+y)=x+f(y)$;
> (ii) $f(0)=2$.
> What is $f(2000)$?
>
> - **(A)** 0
> - **(B)** 2
> - **(C)** 1998
> - **(D)** 2000
> - **(E)** 2002
