*Incircles of four triangles in convex quadrilateral share a point iff rhombus*

> Let $ABCD$ be a convex quadrilateral. Prove that the incircles of triangles $ABC$, $BCD$, $CDA$, and $DAB$ have a common point if and only if $ABCD$ is a rhombus.
