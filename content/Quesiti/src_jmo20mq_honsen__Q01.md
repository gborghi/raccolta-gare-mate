# Japan Mathematical Olympiad 2010 — Honsen (Final) — Quesito 1

*H is circumcenter of triangle APQ*

> Let $ABC$ be an acute triangle with $AB \ne AC$, and let $H$ be the foot of the perpendicular from $A$ to $BC$. Points $P$ and $Q$ are taken so that $A$, $B$, $P$ lie on a line in this order and $A$, $C$, $Q$ lie on a line in this order, in such a way that the four points $B$, $C$, $P$, $Q$ are concyclic and $HP = HQ$. Prove that $H$ is the circumcenter of triangle $APQ$.
> 
> (Here, $XY$ denotes the length of segment $XY$.)
