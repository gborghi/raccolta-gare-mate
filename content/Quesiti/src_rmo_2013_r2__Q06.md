*Weighing balance placements counting inequality T(100)>T(99)*

> Let $n \ge 3$ be a natural number, let $T(n)$ denote the number of ways we can place $n$ objects of weights $1, 2, \ldots, n$ on a balance such that the sum of the weights in each pan is equal. Prove that $T(100) > T(99)$.
