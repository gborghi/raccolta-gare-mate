*Pyramid with inscribed circles of adjacent faces touching; prove contact points on circle*

> A pyramid is formed by joining the vertices of a plane quadrilateral $ABCD$, the "base", to the point $T$ outside its plane. It is found that the inscribed circles of each pair of adjacent triangular faces touch each other. Prove that the points of contact of the inscribed circles with the base of the pyramid lie on a circle.
