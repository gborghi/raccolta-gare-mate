*Parallelogram ABCD (AB=8, BC=4); circle through A,C, midpoint M of BC, tangent to AD, meets CD at P; find MP*

> (Part B) Let $ABCD$ be a parallelogram with $AB = 8$ and $BC = 4$. The circle $\Gamma$ passes through $A$, $C$ and through the midpoint $M$ of $BC$, and cuts the side $CD$ at the point $P \ne C$. It is known that $AD$ is tangent to $\Gamma$. Calculate the measure of segment $MP$.
