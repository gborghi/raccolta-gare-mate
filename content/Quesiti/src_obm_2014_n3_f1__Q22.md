# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2014 — Nível 3 — Fase 1 — Quesito 22

*Product of denominators of the two fractions intimate with 2014/51 (Farey neighbors)*

> Two fractions $\frac{a}{b}$ and $\frac{c}{d}$, with $a,b,c,d$ positive integers, are intimate when $ad - bc = \pm 1$. For example, $\frac{1}{2}$ is intimate with $\frac{1}{3}$ and $\frac{2}{3}$ since $1 \cdot 3 - 2 \cdot 1 = 1$ and $1 \cdot 3 - 2 \cdot 2 = -1$. Two fractions intimate with $\frac{2014}{51}$ have denominator smaller than $51$. Being $\frac{x}{y}$ and $\frac{z}{w}$ these fractions, what is the value of $y \cdot w$?
> (A) $58$ (B) $68$ (C) $78$ (D) $88$ (E) $98$
