# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2004 — Nível Universitário — Fase 2 — Quesito 6

*Prove chord inequality for unit-speed simple closed curve*

> Let $\gamma:[0,2\pi]\to\mathbb{R}^2$ be a differentiable function with continuous derivative, $|\gamma'(t)|=1$ for all $t$, whose image is a simple closed curve; that is,
> $$\gamma(t_1)=\gamma(t_2),\quad t_1<t_2\implies t_1=0,\;t_2=2\pi.$$
> 
> Prove that there exist $0\le t_0<t_1<2\pi$ such that
> $$|\gamma(t_1)-\gamma(t_0)|\le\frac{1}{\pi}\min\{t_1-t_0,\,2\pi+t_0-t_1\}.$$
