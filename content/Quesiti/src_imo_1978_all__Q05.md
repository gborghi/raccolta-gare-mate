# IMO 1978 (Twentieth International Olympiad) — Quesito 5

*sum a_k/k^2 >= sum 1/k*

> Let $\{a_k\}$ ($k = 1, 2, 3, \ldots$) be a sequence of distinct positive integers. Prove that for all natural numbers $n$,
> $$\sum_{k=1}^{n} \frac{a_k}{k^2} \geq \sum_{k=1}^{n} \frac{1}{k}.$$
