*Find xyz where x, y, z are given by expressions involving nested square roots of 7 and 3*

> The numbers $x$, $y$ and $z$ are given by $x = \sqrt{12 - 3\sqrt{7}} - \sqrt{12 + 3\sqrt{7}}$, $y = \sqrt{7 + 4\sqrt{3}} - \sqrt{7 - 4\sqrt{3}}$, and $z = \sqrt{7} + \sqrt{3}$.
> 
> What is the value of $xyz$?
>
> - **(A)** 1
> - **(B)** $-6$
> - **(C)** $-8$
> - **(D)** 8
> - **(E)** 12
