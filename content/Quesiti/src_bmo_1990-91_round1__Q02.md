*Find positive integers k such that x^(2k+1)+x+1 is divisible by x^k+x+1*

> Find all positive integers $k$ such that the polynomial $x^{2k+1} + x + 1$ is divisible by the polynomial $x^k + x + 1$.
> 
> For each such $k$ specify the integers $n$ such that $x^n + x + 1$ is divisible by $x^k + x + 1$.
