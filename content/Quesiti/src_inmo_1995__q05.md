*Real numbers a_k>1 satisfying sum condition, prove double sum inequality*

> The real numbers $a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_n$ are all greater than $1$ and satisfy $|a_k - a_{k+1}| < 1$ for $1 \le k \le n-1$. Prove that
> $$\frac{a_1}{a_2} + \frac{a_2}{a_3} + \cdots + \frac{a_{n-1}}{a_n} + \frac{a_n}{a_1} < 2n-1.$$
