*Count ordered triples satisfying a symmetric real system*

> For any real number $a$ find the number of ordered triples $(x, y, z)$ of real numbers which satisfy
> $$x + y^2 + z^2 = a, \quad x^2 + y + z^2 = a, \quad x^2 + y^2 + z = a.$$
