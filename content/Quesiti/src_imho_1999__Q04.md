*Pairs of positive integers with divisibility condition involving prime*

> Determine all pairs $(n, p)$ of positive integers such that
> 
> $p$ is a prime,
> $n$ not exceeded $2p$, and
> $(p-1)^n + 1$ is divisible by $n^{p-1}$.
