*Pairwise non-divisible positive integers: prove sum is at least 1.4n²−2n*

> Let $a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_n$ be positive integers such that no one divides another, i.e., $a_i \nmid a_j$ for all $i \ne j$. Prove that
> $$a_1 + a_2 + \cdots + a_n \ge 1.4n^2 - 2n.$$
> (Note: partial credit may be given for proving the inequality for all sufficiently large $n$.)
