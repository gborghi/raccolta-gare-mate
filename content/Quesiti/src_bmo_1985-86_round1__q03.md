*Prove a radical equation in positive integers forces m to be a square*

> Prove that if $m$, $n$ are positive integers and
> $$1 + m + n\sqrt{3} = \left(2 + \sqrt{3}\right)^{2n-1}$$
> then $m$ is a perfect square.
