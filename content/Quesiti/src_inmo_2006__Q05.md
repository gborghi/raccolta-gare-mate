*Cyclic quadrilateral ABCD with angle 120 degrees: two inequalities*

> In a cyclic quadrilateral $ABCD$, $AB = a$, $BC = b$, $CD = c$, $\angle ABC = 120^\circ$, and $\angle ABD = 30^\circ$. Prove that
> 
> (i) $c \ge a + b$;
> 
> (ii) $|\sqrt{c + a} - \sqrt{c + b}| = \sqrt{c - a - b}$.
