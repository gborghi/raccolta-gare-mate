*Count circular arrangements of 3 red, 3 blue, 3 yellow pegs; rotations identified, reflections distinct*

> There are $9$ pegs: $3$ red, $3$ blue, and $3$ yellow. Count the number of ways to place these $9$ pegs at equally spaced positions around a circular table. Two arrangements are considered the same if one can be obtained from the other by rotation. However, an arrangement and its mirror image (i.e., the arrangement seen after reversing the rotational direction) are counted as two distinct arrangements.
