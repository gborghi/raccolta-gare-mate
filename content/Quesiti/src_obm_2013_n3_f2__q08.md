*Number of ordered real quadruples (x,y,z,w) satisfying cyclic cubic system*

> Determine the number of ordered quadruples $(x, y, z, w)$ of real numbers such that
> $$\begin{cases}
> -x^3 = y + z + w \\
> -y^3 = z + w + x \\
> -z^3 = w + x + y \\
> -w^3 = x + y + z
> \end{cases}$$
