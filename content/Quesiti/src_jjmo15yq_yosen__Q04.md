# Japan Junior Mathematical Olympiad 2017 — Yosen (Preliminary) — Quesito 4

*Line through P on AB bisecting both a square's and a triangle's area; find AP*

> Let $ABCD$ be a square of side length $2$, and let point $E$ be the point symmetric to $B$ with respect to $C$ (so that $C$ is the midpoint of segment $BE$). A line $l$ intersects side $AB$ at point $P$. When the line $l$ bisects the area of square $ABCD$ and also bisects the area of triangle $CDE$, find the length of segment $AP$.
