# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2002 — Nível 3 — Fase 1 — Quesito 5

*Choosing cheapest transport for a school trip: rented vans vs hired buses, find minimum N children*

> A school is going to organize an outing to the zoo. There are two transport options. The first option is to rent "vans": each van can carry up to 6 children and the rental of each van costs R\$ 60,00. The second option is to hire a company that provides buses with a capacity of 48 children and charges R\$ 237,00 per bus plus R\$ 120,00 for the trip; this option is used regardless of the number of children. For which minimum number N of children is it more advantageous to use the buses for the outing? The value of N is:
> (A) 28 (B) 31 (C) 32 (D) 33 (E) 36
