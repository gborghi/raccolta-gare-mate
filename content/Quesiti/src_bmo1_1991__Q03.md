*Cyclic quadrilateral: $4r^2 = AC^2 + BD^2$ implies $AC \perp BD$?*

> $ABCD$ is a quadrilateral inscribed in a circle of radius $r$. The diagonals $AC$, $BD$ meet at $X$.
> 
> Is it true that if $4r^2 = AC^2 + BD^2$ then $AC$ is perpendicular to $BD$? Give a reason for your answer.
