*Existence of distinct positive reals forming a 7-term arithmetic progression*

> Do there exist three distinct positive real numbers $a, b, c$ such that $a, b, c, b+c-a, c+a-b, a+b-c$ and $a+b+c$ form a 7-term arithmetic progression in some order?
