*Count 4x4 arrays from {0,1,2,3} with row and column sums divisible by 4*

> Find the number of $4 \times 4$ arrays whose entries are from the set $\{0, 1, 2, 3\}$ such that the sum of the numbers in each of the four rows and in each of the four columns is divisible by $4$.
