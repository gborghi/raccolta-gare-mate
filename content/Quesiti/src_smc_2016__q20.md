*A tile from Marrakech has line of symmetry XY of length 8 cm; VZ is parallel to the sides of the square tile. Find the area of the central grey octagon.*

> Michael was walking in Marrakech when he saw a tiling formed by tessellating the square tile shown. The tile has a line of symmetry $XY$ and the length of $XY$ is 8 cm. The point $Z$ is such that $VZ$ is a straight line and $VZ$ is parallel to the sides of the square. What is the area of the central grey octagon?
>
> - **(A)** $4\text{ cm}^2$
> - **(B)** $7\text{ cm}^2$
> - **(C)** $8\text{ cm}^2$
> - **(D)** $9\text{ cm}^2$
> - **(E)** $10\text{ cm}^2$
