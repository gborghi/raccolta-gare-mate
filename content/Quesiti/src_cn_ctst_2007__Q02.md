*Find all good rational numbers x=p/q>1*

> The rational number $x$ is called "good" if $x = \frac{p}{q} > 1$, where $p$, $q$ are coprime positive integers, and there are $a$ and $N$ such that for every integer $n \geq N$,
> $$|\{x^n\} - a| < \frac{1}{2(p+q)},$$
> where $\{a\} = a - [a]$, and $[a]$ is the greatest integer less than or equal to $a$. Find all "good" rational numbers.
