*Find f(13) for a continuous differentiable functional equation*

> The function $f:(-1,+\infty)\to\mathbb{R}$ is continuous and differentiable. It is known that $f(0)=0$, $f'(0)=a$, and $f(x+1)=e^{f(x)}$ for all $x>-1$. Compute $f'(13)$.
