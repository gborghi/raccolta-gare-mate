*Right-angled triangle from trig identity; circumcentre–centroid distance formula*

> (i) In $\triangle ABC$, $\sin^2 A + \sin^2 B + \sin^2 C = 2$. Prove that $\triangle ABC$ is right-angled. (Hungary, modified)
> 
> (ii) $O$ is the circumcentre and $G$ is the centroid of $\triangle ABC$. Prove that $9OG^2 = R^2(1 - 8\cos A \cos B \cos C)$. (Bulgaria, modified)
