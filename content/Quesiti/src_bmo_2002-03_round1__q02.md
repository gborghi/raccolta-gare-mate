*Incircle tangent point and perpendicular; prove BX is a diameter*

> The triangle $ABC$, where $AB < AC$, has an incircle which touches $BC$ at $S$. The perpendicular from $B$ to $AS$ meets the line $AS$ at the point $P$. Let $X$ be the point on $AC$ such that $PX \parallel BC$. Show that $BX$ is a diameter of the incircle.
