# Indian National Mathematical Olympiad 1992 — Quesito 5

*Two circles; line through P meets them; midpoint of AB, midpoint of XZ coincide*

> Two circles $C_1$ and $C_2$ in the plane intersect at two points $P$ and $Q$. A line through $P$ meets $C_1$ at $A$ and $C_2$ at $B$. Let $Y$ be the midpoint of $AB$ and let $QY$ meet the circles $C_1$ and $C_2$ again at $X$ and $Z$ respectively. Show that $Y$ is the midpoint of $XZ$.
