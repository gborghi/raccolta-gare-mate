*Units digit of the sum 1!+2!+...+2010!+2011!*

> We represent by $n!$ the product of all positive integers from $1$ to $n$. For example, $5! = 1 \times 2 \times 3 \times 4 \times 5$. Calculating the sum $1! + 2! + 3! + 4! + \cdots + 2010! + 2011!$, what is the units digit of the result obtained?
> 
> (A) 1 \quad (B) 3 \quad (C) 4 \quad (D) 7 \quad (E) 9
