# Coupe Animath Printemps 2021 — Quesito 17

*8 ants on the edges of a unit cube: two at geodesic distance at most 1 (lycee)*

> $8$ ants are placed on the edges of a cube whose edges have length $1$. For every positive real $d$, two ants are said to be at distance $d$ if the first ant must travel a distance of at least $d$ to reach the second ant, moving only along the edges of the cube. Prove that there exist two ants at distance less than or equal to $1$.
