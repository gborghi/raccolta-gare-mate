*Length XD from incircle chord with given ratios*

> Let $\omega$ be the incircle of triangle $ABC$. Let $D$ be the point where side $BC$ touches $\omega$, and let $X$ be the second intersection of line $AD$ with $\omega$ (other than $D$). Given that $AX : XD : BC = 1 : 3 : 16$ and that the radius of $\omega$ is $1$, find the length of segment $XD$.
