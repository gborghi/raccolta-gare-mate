*Angle of two lines in a cyclic hexagon*

> Let $ABCDEF$ be a hexagon inscribed in a circle. Let $P$ be the intersection of line $AB$ and line $DC$, $Q$ the intersection of line $BC$ and line $ED$, $R$ the intersection of line $CD$ and line $FE$, $S$ the intersection of line $DE$ and line $AF$, and $T$ the intersection of line $FE$ and line $CB$. It is given that $\angle BPC = 50^\circ$, $\angle CQD = 45^\circ$, $\angle DRE = 40^\circ$, and $\angle ESF = 35^\circ$. Find the size of the angle formed by the intersection of line $BE$ and line $CF$.
