# British Mathematical Olympiad 2018-19 — Round 1 — Quesito 4

*Prove angle equality involving tangent lines to a semicircle*

> Let $\Gamma$ be a semicircle with diameter $AB$. The point $C$ lies on the diameter $AB$ and points $D$ and $E$ lie on the arc $BA$, with $E$ between $B$ and $D$. Let the tangents to $\Gamma$ at $D$ and $E$ meet at $F$.
> 
> Prove that $\angle EFD = \angle ACD + \angle ECB$.
