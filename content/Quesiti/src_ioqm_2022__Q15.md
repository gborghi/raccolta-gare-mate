# Indian Olympiad Qualifier in Mathematics (IOQM) 2022 — Quesito 15

*Real x,y with xy=1, find largest and smallest of a symmetric expression, compute T+t*

> Let $x, y$ be real numbers such that $xy = 1$. Let $T$ and $t$ be the largest and the smallest values of the expression
> $$\frac{(x+y)^2 - (x-y)^2 - 2}{(x+y)^2 + (x-y)^2 - 2}.$$
> If $T + t$ can be expressed in the form $\frac{m}{n}$ where $m, n$ are nonzero integers with $\gcd(m, n) = 1$, find the value of $m + n$.
