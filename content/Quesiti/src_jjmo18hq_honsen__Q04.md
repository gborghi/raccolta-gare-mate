*Minimum pieces so every cell has 2+ pieces on its diagonals*

> There is a $2020 \times 2020$ grid, and at most one piece is placed on each cell. Find the smallest possible value of the number of pieces used when pieces are placed so that the following condition is satisfied:
> 
> For every cell, there exist at least $2$ pieces lying on the diagonal lines through that cell.
> 
> Here, a piece on the cell in the $a$-th row from the top and the $b$-th column from the left is said to lie on a diagonal line of the cell in the $c$-th row from the top and the $d$-th column from the left when $|a-c| = |b-d|$ holds.
