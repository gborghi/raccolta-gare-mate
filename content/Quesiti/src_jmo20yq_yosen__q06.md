*Count bridge configurations among 9 color-island birds satisfying two graph conditions*

> There are $3$ red birds, $3$ blue birds, and $3$ yellow birds, each on its own island (giving $9$ islands in total). Bridges between islands are to be built satisfying the following two conditions:\n\n$\bullet$ Between any two islands there is at most one bridge; for any bridge, the two birds at its endpoints are of different colors.\n\n$\bullet$ For any two birds of the same color, their islands are not connected by any path of bridges (i.e., they are in different connected components).\n\nHow many ways are there to build the bridges? (The configuration with no bridges counts as $1$.)
