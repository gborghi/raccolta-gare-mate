*Maximum area of triangles inscribed in T; prove AI^2 = AB·AC for incentre I*

> Let a set of triangles be inscribed in a triangle $T$ (i.e., the vertices lie on the boundary of $T$). Find the maximum value of $\text{Area}(\Delta)$ over all such inscribed triangles.
> 
> Let $I$ be the centre of the circle inscribed in triangle $ABC$, with $AB$ and $AC$ produced beyond $B$ and $C$ respectively.
> 
> Prove that
> $$AI^2 = AB \cdot AC.$$
