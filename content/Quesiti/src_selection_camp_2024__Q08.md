*Function on integer pairs making an expression a perfect square*

> Find all functions $f$ defined on ordered pairs of positive integers and taking positive integer values such that, for all positive integers $x, y, z$,
> $$f(x, y)^2 + f(y^4, z) + 2x y^4 z$$
> is a perfect square.
