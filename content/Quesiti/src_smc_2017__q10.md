*Rectangle divided into three congruent smaller rectangles each similar to the large one; find ratio of longer to shorter side.*

> A rectangle is divided into three smaller congruent rectangles as shown.
> 
> Each smaller rectangle is similar to the large rectangle.
> 
> In each of the four rectangles, what is the ratio of the length of a longer side to that of a shorter side?
>
> - **(A)** $2\sqrt{3} : 1$
> - **(B)** $3 : 1$
> - **(C)** $2 : 1$
> - **(D)** $\sqrt{3} : 1$
> - **(E)** $\sqrt{2} : 1$
