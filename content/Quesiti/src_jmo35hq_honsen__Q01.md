*Minimum of cyclic sum of squared differences under gap constraints*

> Let $n \ge 2$ be an integer. Real numbers $a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_{2n}$ satisfy
> $$|a_k - a_{n+k}| \ge 1$$
> for every integer $k$ with $1 \le k \le n$. Find the minimum value of
> $$(a_1 - a_2)^2 + (a_2 - a_3)^2 + \cdots + (a_{2n-1} - a_{2n})^2 + (a_{2n} - a_1)^2.$$
