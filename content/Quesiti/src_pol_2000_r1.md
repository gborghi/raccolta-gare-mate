- [[src_pol_2000_r1__Q01|Quesito 1]] — Prove n divides the sum of cubes of all naturals less than n and coprime to n.
- [[src_pol_2000_r1__Q02|Quesito 2]] — Acute triangle with angle ACB = 2 angle ABC, D on BC with 2 angle BAD = angle ABC; prove 1/BD = 1/AB + 1/AC.
- [[src_pol_2000_r1__Q03|Quesito 3]] — For positive a,b,c summing to 1, prove a^2+b^2+c^2+2 sqrt(3abc) <= 1.
- [[src_pol_2000_r1__Q04|Quesito 4]] — Circle points painted in three colors; prove three same-color points form an isosceles triangle.
- [[src_pol_2000_r1__Q05|Quesito 5]] — Find all positive integer pairs (a,b) with a^3+6ab+1 and b^3+6ab+1 both cubes.
- [[src_pol_2000_r1__Q06|Quesito 6]] — X inside right triangle (angle C = 90), P,Q,R projections onto BC,CA,AB; prove AR*RB = BP*PC + AQ*QC iff X on AB.
- [[src_pol_2000_r1__Q07|Quesito 7]] — For each positive integer n and t in (1/2,1) there exist a,b in (1999,2000) with (1/2)a^n+(1/2)b^n < (ta+(1-t)b)^n.
- [[src_pol_2000_r1__Q08|Quesito 8]] — Functions c(n,k) with c(n,0)=c(n,n)=1 and c(n+1,k)=2^k c(n,k)+c(n,k-1); prove c(n,k)=c(n,n-k).
- [[src_pol_2000_r1__Q09|Quesito 9]] — Positive integers m,n with mn dividing m^2+n^2+m; prove m is a perfect square.
- [[src_pol_2000_r1__Q10|Quesito 10]] — OA, OB, OC pairwise orthogonal unit vectors; line/plane through O with A',B',C' projections of A,B,C; find set of values of OA'^2+OB'^2+OC'^2.
- [[src_pol_2000_r1__Q11|Quesito 11]] — Set M of n^2+1 positive integers where among any n+1 two are comparable by divisibility; prove a divisibility chain a_1,...,a_{n+1} with a_{i+1} | a_i.
- [[src_pol_2000_r1__Q12|Quesito 12]] — D,E,F on sides BC,CA,AB of acute triangle; circumcircles of AEF, BFD, CDE meet at P; given three ratio conditions prove AD,BE,CF are altitudes.
