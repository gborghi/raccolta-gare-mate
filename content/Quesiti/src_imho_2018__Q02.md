*Find all integers n≥3 with a specific recurrence on real sequences*

> Find all integers $n \ge 3$ for which there exist real numbers $a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_{n+2}$, such that $a_{n+1} = a_1$ and $a_{n+2} = a_2$, and
> $$a_i a_{i+1} + 1 = a_{i+2}$$
> for $i = 1, 2, \ldots, n$.
