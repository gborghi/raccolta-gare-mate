*Maximize a ratio of sums for non-negative reals summing to 1*

> Let $n \ge 2$ be a positive integer. For non-negative reals $a_1, \ldots, a_n$ satisfying $a_1 + \cdots + a_n = 1$, find the maximum possible value of
> $$\left(\sum_{i=1}^{n} i a_i\right)\left(\sum_{i=1}^{n} \frac{a_i}{i}\right)^2.$$
