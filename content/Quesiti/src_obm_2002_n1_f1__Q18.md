*Number of bottles to empty a tank using a level-vs-bottles table*

> A milk producer bottles daily the whole production of milk of his farm. After bottling, he leaves a part remaining in the cylindrical tank, conforming as we see in the figure to the side. The table shows the quantity of bottles filled and the level of the milk inside the tank. After how many bottles will the tank be empty?
> 
> Quantity of bottles filled: $0,\ 200,\ 400,\ 600$
> Level of the tank (cm): $210,\ 170,\ 130,\ 90$
> 
> (A) $1000$ (B) $1050$ (C) $1100$ (D) $1150$ (E) $1200$
