*Logic puzzle: one truth-teller among four suspects*

> A crime is committed by one person, and there are four suspects: André, Eduardo, Rafael, and João. When questioned, they make the following statements:
> - André: Eduardo is guilty.
> - Eduardo: João is guilty.
> - Rafael: I am not guilty.
> - João: Eduardo is lying when he says I am guilty.
> 
> Knowing that exactly one of the four told the truth, who is guilty?
> 
> A) André.
> B) Eduardo.
> C) Rafael.
> D) João.
> E) It cannot be determined.
