# British Mathematical Olympiad 2017-18 — Round 1 — Quesito 1

*Compare sums of remainders of 365 by 1..365 and 366 by 1..366*

> Helen divides $365$ by each of $1, 2, 3, \ldots, 365$ in turn, writing down a list of the $365$ remainders. Then Phil divides $366$ by each of $1, 2, 3, \ldots, 366$ in turn, writing down a list of the $366$ remainders. Whose list of remainders has the greater sum and by how much?
