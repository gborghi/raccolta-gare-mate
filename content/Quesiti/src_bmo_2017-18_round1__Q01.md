# British Mathematical Olympiad 2017-18 — Round 1 — Quesito 1

*Compare remainder sums: Helen vs Phil each dividing 306 by 1 to 306*

> Helen divides $306$ by each of $1, 2, 3, \ldots, 306$ in turn, writing down a list of the $306$ remainders. Then Phil divides $306$ by each of $1, 2, 3, \ldots, 306$ in turn, writing down a list of the $306$ remainders. Whose list of remainders has the greatest sum, and by how much?
