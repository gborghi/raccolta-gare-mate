*Find all integers n≥2 whose small divisors satisfy d² | n*

> Find all integers $n \geq 2$ satisfying the following condition.
> 
> \textit{Condition:} For every positive divisor $d$ of $n$ with $d \leq \sqrt{n}$, $d^2$ divides $n$.
