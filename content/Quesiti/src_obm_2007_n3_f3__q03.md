*Prove the set of side and diagonal lengths of a convex n-gon has at least floor(n/2) distinct values*

> $n$ points are given in the plane, which are the vertices of a convex polygon. Prove that the set of measures of the sides and diagonals of the polygon has at least $\lfloor n/2 \rfloor$ distinct elements.
> 
> *Remark:* $\lfloor x \rfloor$ denotes the greatest integer that does not exceed $x$. For example, $\lfloor 2.5 \rfloor = 2$, $\lfloor 3 \rfloor = 3$ and $\lfloor -1.2 \rfloor = -2$.
