*Determine value of a1997 in a recursively defined sequence*

> For positive integers $n$, the sequence $a_1, a_2, a_3, \ldots$ is defined by
> $$a_1 = 1, \quad a_n = \left(\frac{n+1}{n-1}\right)(a_1 + a_2 + \cdots + a_{n-1}), \quad n > 1.$$
> Determine the value of $a_{1997}$.
