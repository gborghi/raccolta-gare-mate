*If 1+m+n/3 is a perfect power, m is a perfect square*

> Prove that if $m$, $n$, $r$ are positive integers and
> $$1 + m + n\sqrt{3} = (2 + \sqrt{3})^{2r-1}$$
> then $m$ is a perfect square.
