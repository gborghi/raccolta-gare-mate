*Three workers paint walls at different rates; find total time*

> Manuel, Antônio, and Joaquim start at the same moment painting three equal walls of 60 meters each, one wall per person. In the first 10 minutes of work, Manuel paints 2 meters, Antônio 3 meters, and Joaquim 5 meters. Whoever finishes first immediately goes to help the others, until all three finish the whole job together. How long does the work take?
> 
> (A) 3 hours $\quad$ (B) 4 hours $\quad$ (C) 5 hours $\quad$ (D) 6 hours $\quad$ (E) 7 hours
