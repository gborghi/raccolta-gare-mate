*Cyclic integer sequence with sign condition: prove a difference of sums is divisible by 4*

> Integers $x_0, x_1, \ldots, x_{n-1}, x_n = x_0, x_{n+1} = x_1$ satisfy the inequality $(-1)^k x_{k-1} x_{k+1} > 0$ for $k = 1, 2, \ldots, n$. Prove that the difference
> $$\sum_{k=0}^{n-1} x_k - \sum_{k=0}^{n-1} |x_k|$$
> is divisible by $4$.
