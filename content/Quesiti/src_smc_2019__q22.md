*Find f(2019) given the functional equation (n-2019)f(n) - f(2019-n) = 2019*

> A function $f$ satisfies the equation $(n - 2019)f(n) - f(2019 - n) = 2019$ for every integer $n$.
> 
> What is the value of $f(2019)$?
>
> - **(A)** $0$
> - **(B)** $1$
> - **(C)** $2018 \times 2019$
> - **(D)** $2019^2$
> - **(E)** $2019 \times 2020$
