# British Mathematical Olympiad 2004-05 — Round 1 — Quesito 2

*Prove BP bisects angle EBC using perpendicular feet and semicircle*

> Let $ABC$ be an acute-angled triangle, and let $D$, $E$ be the feet of the perpendiculars from $A$, $B$ to $BC$, $CA$ respectively. Let $P$ be the point where the line $AD$ meets the semicircle constructed outwardly on $BC$. Prove that $BP$ bisects angle $EBC$.
