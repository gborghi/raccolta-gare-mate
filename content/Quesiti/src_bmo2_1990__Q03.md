*State the cosine rule and prove a sum-of-square-roots inequality for positive reals a, b, c.*

> State the cosine rule for a triangle. Prove that for arbitrary positive real numbers $a$, $b$, $c$, $$\sqrt{a^2 + b^2 - ab} + \sqrt{b^2 + c^2 - bc} \ge \sqrt{a^2 + c^2 + ac}.$$
