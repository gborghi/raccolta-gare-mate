# Japan Mathematical Olympiad 2003 — Yosen (Preliminary) — Quesito 10

*Count bad-relation pairs among 8 people given friendship/enmity constraints*

> There are $8$ people. Among any $3$ of them, at least $2$ have a good relationship with each other. Among any $4$ of them, at least $2$ have a bad relationship with each other. Find all possible values for the number of pairs of people who have a bad relationship.
