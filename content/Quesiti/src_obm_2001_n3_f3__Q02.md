*Greedy coprime sequence: find a_0 for which every term is a prime or a prime power*

> Given an integer $a_0 > 1$, we define a sequence $(a_n)_{n \ge 0}$ in the following way: for each $k \ge 0$, $a_{k+1}$ is the smallest integer $a_{k+1} > a_k$ such that $\gcd(a_{k+1}, a_0 \cdot a_1 \cdots a_k) = 1$. Determine for which values of $a_0$ all the terms $a_k$ of the sequence are primes or powers of primes.
