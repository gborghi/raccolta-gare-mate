*Esmeralda's 5 books are re-shelved with exactly two in their original place and the rest displaced; count the ways.*

> Esmeralda has five books about heraldry on a shelf. On the weekend, she cleaned the shelf and, upon replacing the books, she put two of them in the place where they were before and the others in places different from where they were. In how many ways could she have done this? (A) $20$ (B) $25$ (C) $30$ (D) $34$ (E) $45$
