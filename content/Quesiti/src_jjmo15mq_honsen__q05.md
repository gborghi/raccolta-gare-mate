*Acute triangle with orthocenter; four circumcircle-altitude intersections are concyclic*

> Let $ABC$ be an acute triangle with orthocenter $H$. Let $D$, $E$, $F$ be the feet of the altitudes from vertices $A$, $B$, $C$ respectively. Let $P$ be the second intersection of the circumcircle of triangle $ACD$ with segment $CF$ (other than $C$), and let $Q$ be the second intersection of the circumcircle of triangle $ABD$ with segment $CF$ (other than the vertex on $CF$). Furthermore, let $S$ be the second intersection of the circumcircle of triangle $ABH$ with segment $DE$, and let $T$ be the second intersection of the circumcircle of triangle $ACH$ with segment $DE$. Prove that the four points $P$, $Q$, $S$, $T$ are concyclic.
