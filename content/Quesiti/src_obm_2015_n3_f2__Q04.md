# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2015 — Nível 3 — Fase 2 — Quesito 4

*Count 5-element subsets of {1,...,20} that in increasing order have consecutive differences greater than 1,2,3,4*

> (Part A) A subset of $5$ elements of the set $\{1, 2, 3, \ldots, 20\}$ is called $largo$ (large) if, when we write its elements in increasing order, they have the property that the difference between the second and the first element is greater than $1$, the difference between the third and the second is greater than $2$, the difference between the fourth and the third is greater than $3$, and the difference between the fifth and the fourth is greater than $4$. How many large subsets are there?
