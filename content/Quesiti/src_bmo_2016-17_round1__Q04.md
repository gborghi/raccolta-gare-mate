# British Mathematical Olympiad 2016-17 — Round 1 — Quesito 4

*Triangle with 60° angle; prove angle BDM equals 90°*

> Let $ABC$ be a triangle with $\angle BAC = 60^\circ$. The tangent to the circumcircle of $ABC$ at $A$ meets line $BC$ (extended if necessary) at the point $T$. Let $D$ be the point on $BC$ such that $AD$ bisects $\angle BAC$, and let $M$ be the midpoint of $AD$.
> 
> Prove that $\angle BDM = 90^\circ$.
