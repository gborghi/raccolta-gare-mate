*Circular hoop of radius 1 in corner of room; find locus of centre*

> A circular hoop of radius 1 is placed in the corner of the room. (The corner consists of a horizontal floor and two perpendicular vertical walls and the hoop touches all three planes.) Find the locus of the centre of the hoop.
