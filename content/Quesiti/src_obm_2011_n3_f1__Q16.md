# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2011 — Nível 3 — Fase 1 — Quesito 16

*Ratio AB:AF after two folds of an isosceles paper triangle*

> A piece of paper has the form of an isosceles triangle of sides $BC = 6$ cm and $AB = AC = 5$ cm and is partially filled (shaded gray). Folding the vertex $A$ over the side $BC$, vertex $A$ comes to coincide with a point of side $BC$; folding again, point $A$ comes to rest over point $F$ of side $AB$. What is the ratio $\dfrac{AB}{AF}$?
> 
> (A) $\dfrac{4}{3}$ (B) $\dfrac{3}{2}$ (C) $\dfrac{8}{5}$ (D) $\dfrac{18}{5}$ (E) $\dfrac{24}{5}$
