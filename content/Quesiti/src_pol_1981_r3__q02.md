*In triangle ABC the perpendicular bisectors of AB and AC meet BC at X and Y; prove BC=XY iff tan B tan C = 3 or = -1.*

> In a triangle $ABC$, the perpendicular bisectors of sides $AB$ and $AC$ intersect $BC$ at $X$ and $Y$. Prove that $BC = XY$ if and only if $\tan B \tan C = 3$ or $\tan B \tan C = -1$.

*Nel triangolo ABC i bisettori perpendicolari di AB e AC incontrano BC a X e Y; dimostrare BC=XY se B tan C = 3 o = -1.*
