*Line through vertex C of parallelogram meets extensions of sides*

> A line through the vertex $C$ of a parallelogram $ABCD$ meets the extensions of sides $AB$ and $AD$ at $E$ and $F$ respectively. Prove that
> $$AC^2 + CE \cdot CF = AB \cdot AE + AD \cdot AF.$$
