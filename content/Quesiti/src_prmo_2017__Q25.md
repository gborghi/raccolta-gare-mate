# Pre-Regional Mathematical Olympiad (PRMO) 2017 — Quesito 25

*Find area of triangle AEF given areas of triangles in rectangle ABCD*

> Let $ABCD$ be a rectangle and let $E$ and $F$ be points on $CD$ and $BC$ respectively such that $\text{area}(AEF) = 9$, $\text{area}(CEF) = 9$ and $\text{area}(ABF) = 25$. What is the area of triangle $AEF$?
