*Two circles with common tangent and perpendicular bisector condition*

> Two circles, of different radii, with centres at $B$ and $C$, touch externally at $A$. A common tangent, not through $A$, touches the first circle at $D$ and the second at $E$. The line through $A$ which is perpendicular to $DE$ meets $BC$ at $F$. Prove that $BC = 2AF$.
