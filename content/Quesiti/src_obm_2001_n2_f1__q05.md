*How many times the group 89 appears writing the integers 1 to 1000 in a row*

> The positive integers from $1$ to $1000$ are written side by side, in increasing order, forming the sequence $1234567891011121314\ldots\,9991000$. In this sequence, how many times does the group ``$89$'' appear?
> 
> (A) $98$ \quad (B) $32$ \quad (C) $22$ \quad (D) $89$ \quad (E) $21$
