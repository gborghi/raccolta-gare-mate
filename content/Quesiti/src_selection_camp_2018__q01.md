*Convex pentagon with equal sides and angles, perpendicular from E to BC meets AC and BD at one point*

> Let $ABCDE$ be a convex pentagon satisfying $AB = BC = CD$, $\angle EAB = \angle BCD$, and $\angle EDC = \angle CBA$. Prove that the perpendicular dropped from $E$ to line $BC$, and the lines $AC$ and $BD$, all meet at a single point.
