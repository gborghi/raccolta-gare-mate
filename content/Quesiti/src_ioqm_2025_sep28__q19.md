*Count ordered pairs (m,n) with 1≤m<n≤50 and mn a perfect square*

> Find the number of ordered pairs $(m, n)$ where $m$ and $n$ are positive integers such that $1 \le m < n \le 50$ and the product $mn$ is a perfect square.
