*Count permutations of 1..9 where all elements between i and i+1 are less than i*

> How many permutations of $1, 2, 3, \ldots, 9$ have the property that, for every $1 \le i < 9$, all numbers appearing between $i$ and $i+1$ in the permutation (where $i$ may appear either before or after $i+1$) are all less than $i$? For example, $976412358$ is a permutation with this property.
