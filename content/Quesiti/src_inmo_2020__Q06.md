# Indian National Mathematical Olympiad 2020 — Quesito 6

*5x5 board cannot be covered by 16 trominoes with each square covered once or twice*

> A \emph{stromino} is a $3 \times 1$ rectangle. Show that a $5 \times 5$ board divided into twenty-five $1 \times 1$ squares cannot be covered by $16$ strominos such that each stromino covers exactly three squares of the board and every unit square is covered by either one or two strominos. [A stromino can be placed either horizontally or vertically on the board.]
