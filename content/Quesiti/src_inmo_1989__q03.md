*Subset of {1,11,21,...,551} with no pair summing to 552 has at most 38 elements*

> Let $A$ be a subset of the set $\{1, 11, 21, 31, \ldots, 551\}$ whose no two elements add up to $552$. Show that $A$ has not more than $38$ elements.
