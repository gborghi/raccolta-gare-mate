*Cubic root with 1≤a≤b≤c≥0: |λ|≤1*

> Let $a, b, c$ be three real numbers such that $1 \le a \le b \le c \ge 0$. Prove that if $\lambda$ is a root of the cubic equation $x^3 + ax^2 + bx + c = 0$ (real or complex), then $|\lambda| \le 1$.
