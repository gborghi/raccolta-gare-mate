*Perpendicular from altitude foot to line is parallel to side*

> In the acute-angled triangle $ABC$, the foot of the perpendicular from $A$ to $BC$ is $E$. Let $G$ be the foot of the perpendicular from $E$ to $AB$. The foot of the perpendicular from $C$ to $AB$ is $F$. Prove that the perpendicular from $E$ to $CF$ is parallel to $AB$.
