*Number of ways to give 37 centavos change with 1,5,10,25 coins*

> Luca bought a gibi (comic book) for R\$4.63 and paid with a R\$5.00 note. In how many ways can he receive his change of R\$0.37 in coins of $1$, $5$, $10$ and $25$ centavos? Suppose he has as many coins of each type as needed.
> 
> (A) $10$ \quad (B) $12$ \quad (C) $15$ \quad (D) $24$ \quad (E) $25$
