*Find integer triples (x,y,z) in [1,17] where a single integer >1 divides all four expressions*

> Find all triples $(x, y, z)$ of integers with $1 \le x, y, z \le 17$ such that there exists an integer greater than $1$ that divides all four of the following quantities:
> $$xy + 4, \quad yz + 5, \quad zx + 6, \quad xyz + 7.$$
