# British Mathematical Olympiad 2011-12 — Round 1 — Quesito 6

*In acute triangle, foot of altitude D, foot from D to EF lies on median*

> Let $ABC$ be an acute-angled triangle. The foot of the altitude from $A$ to $BC$ is $D$. The foot of the altitude from $B$ to $AC$ is $E$. The foot of the altitude from $C$ to $AB$ is $F$. The point $P$ is the foot of the perpendicular from $D$ to $EF$. Show that $AP$ bisects $BC$.
