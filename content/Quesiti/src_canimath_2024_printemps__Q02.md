*Parallelogram with equal segments, angle bisector perpendicular to KL*

> Let $ABCD$ be a parallelogram. Let $K$ be a point on segment $[BC]$ and $L$ a point on segment $[CD]$ such that $CK = CL$. Show that the bisector of $\widehat{DAB}$ and the line $(KL)$ are perpendicular.
