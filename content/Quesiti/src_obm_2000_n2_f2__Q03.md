*Find angle EGB in rectangle given angle FAE = 20°*

> In rectangle $ABCD$, $E$ is the midpoint of side $BC$ and $F$ is the midpoint of side $CD$. $G$ is the intersection of diagonal $DE$ with segment $FB$.
> 
> The angle $\widehat{FAE}$ measures $20^\circ$. Find the angle $\widehat{EGB}$.
