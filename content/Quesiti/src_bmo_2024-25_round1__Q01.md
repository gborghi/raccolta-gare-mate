# British Mathematical Olympiad 2024-25 — Round 1 — Quesito 1

*Find which n in 3..12 admit a happy circle arrangement*

> We say that a number $n \ge 3$ is $happy$ if it is possible to arrange the numbers $1$ to $n$ in a circle such that the following two conditions hold:
> (a) If $x$ and $y$ are neighbours in the circle, then $x$ divides $y$ or $y$ divides $x$;
> (b) If different integers $u$ and $v$ are in the circle but are not neighbours, then neither divides the other.
> 
> Find all $n$ in the range $3 \le n \le 12$ which are happy.
