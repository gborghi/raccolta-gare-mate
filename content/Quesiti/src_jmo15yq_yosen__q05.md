*Count unordered triples of distinct positive integers with product equal to 12 times sum*

> How many unordered triples of mutually distinct positive integers $\{a, b, c\}$ satisfy $abc = 12(a + b + c)$? Here, triples that differ only in order (such as $\{3, 6, 18\}$ and $\{6, 3, 18\}$) are counted as the same triple.
