*Prove sequence defined by nested radical recurrence yields integers and perfect squares*

> Given a sequence $\{a_n\}$ satisfying $a_0 = 1$ and
> $$a_{n+1} = \frac{7a_n + \sqrt{45a_n^2 - 36}}{2}, \quad n \in \mathbb{N}.$$
> Prove that:
> 
> (1) For each $n \in \mathbb{N}$, $a_n$ is a positive integer;
> 
> (2) For each $n \in \mathbb{N}$, $a_n a_{n+1} - 1$ is a perfect square.
