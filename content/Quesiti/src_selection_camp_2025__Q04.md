# Japan IMO Selection Camp 2025 — Selection Camp — Quesito 4

*n students, n races, badge (a,b) for exactly a top-b finishes; maximize total score*

> Let $n$ be a positive integer. Each of $n$ students runs in $n$ races. In each race all $n$ students are assigned distinct ranks (no two students share a rank). For a pair of positive integers $(a, b)$, a student is said to hold the title $(a, b)$ if the student finishes among the top $b$ in exactly $a$ of the $n$ races. A student's score is defined as the maximum value of $a - b$ over all titles $(a, b)$ that the student holds. Determine the maximum possible value of the sum of the scores of all $n$ students.
