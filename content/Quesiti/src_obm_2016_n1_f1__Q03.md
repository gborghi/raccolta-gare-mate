*Five-digit numbers with distinct odd digits; bound on houses with no newspaper.*

> [Source is the answer key; original statement/options not reproduced. From the solution: there are $5$ odd digits: $1,3,5,7,9$. Hence there are $5\cdot 5 = 25$ numbers of exactly five digits all odd and with an odd sum of digits. Therefore at most $25-18 = 7$ houses did not receive the newspaper.]
