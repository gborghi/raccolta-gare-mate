*Triangle with squares built outward on two sides; find all values of MN:BC where M, N are midpoints of two segments.*

> $ABC$ is a given triangle. $ABDE$ and $ACFG$ are the squares drawn outside of the triangle. The points $M$ and $N$ are the midpoints of $DG$ and $EF$ respectively. Find all values of the ration $MN : BC$.
