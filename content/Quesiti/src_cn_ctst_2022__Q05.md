*240 complex numbers on unit circle satisfying arc constraints: find max modulus of sum*

> Let $C = \{z \in \mathbb{C} \mid |z| = 1\}$ be the unit circle in the complex plane. 240 complex numbers $z_1, z_2, \ldots, z_{240} \in C$ (repetitions allowed) satisfy the following conditions:
> (1) for any open arc $\widehat{\Gamma}$ of length $\dfrac{\pi}{5}$ on $C$, there are at most $200$ indices $j$ ($1 \le j \le 240$) such that $z_j \in \widehat{\Gamma}$;
> (2) for any open arc $\widehat{\gamma}$ of length $\dfrac{\pi}{240}$ on $C$, there are at most $120$ indices $j$ ($1 \le j \le 240$) such that $z_j \in \widehat{\gamma}$.
> Find the maximum of $|z_1 + z_2 + \cdots + z_{240}|$.
