*Find smallest safe combination satisfying three clues*

> Abel keeps his savings in a safe. To not forget the safe's combination, he resolves to store the following clues:
> 
> - It is a number greater than $3001$;
> - It has $6$ divisors;
> - It is a multiple of $5$.
> 
> Abel knows his combination is the smallest number satisfying all the clues. What is Abel's safe combination?

*Trova la combinazione più piccola e sicura che soddisfi tre indizi*
