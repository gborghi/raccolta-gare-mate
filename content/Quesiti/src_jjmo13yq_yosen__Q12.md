*Sliding-piece puzzle on 2x7 board; minimum moves to displace one coin*

> There is a $2 \times 7$ grid of cells. On the bottom-left cell a piece is placed, and on each of the other cells a coin distinguishable by its two faces is placed. The following operation is performed $K$ times, after which the coin originally on the bottom-right cell has changed position, while every other coin is in the same position as at the start. Find the smallest possible value of $K$.
> 
> Operation: Choose one of the cells adjacent (up, down, left, or right) to the cell containing the piece, and swap the piece with the coin in that cell (exchange their positions).
