*21-sided regular polygon inscribed in circle; count triangles containing center*

> Let $A_1 A_2 A_3 \ldots A_{21}$ be a $21$-sided regular polygon inscribed in a circle with center $O$. How many triangles $A_i A_j A_k$, $1 \le i < j < k \le 21$, contain the point $O$ in their interior?
