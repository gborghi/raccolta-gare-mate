# Regional Mathematical Olympiad 2015 — Mumbai — Quesito 5

*Right-angled triangle, incentres of sub-triangles and circumcircle: prove collinearity*

> Let $ABC$ be a right-angled triangle with $\angle B = 90^\circ$ and let $BD$ be the altitude from $B$ on to $AC$. Draw $DE \perp AB$ and $DF \perp BC$. Let $P$, $Q$, $R$ and $S$ be respectively the incentres of triangles $DFC$, $DBF$, $DEB$ and $DAE$. Prove that $P$, $Q$, $R$, $D$ lie on a circle.
