# British Mathematical Olympiad 2023-24 — Round 1 — Quesito 5

*Minimum faults for 1000 red-blue dots on a circle*

> An artist arranges 1000 dots evenly around a circle, with each dot being either red or blue. A critic looks at the 1000 dots and counts *faults*: each time two red dots adjacent is one fault, and each time two blue dots are exactly two apart (that is, they have exactly one dot in between them) is another.
> 
> What is the smallest number of faults the critic could find?
