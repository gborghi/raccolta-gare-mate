*Chord, midpoint, tangent circle, and distance relation*

> Let $AB$ be a chord of a circle $\mathscr{C}_1$ that is not a diameter, and $M$ be the midpoint of $AB$. Let $T$ be a point on the circle $\mathscr{C}_2$ with $OM$ as diameter. The tangent to $\mathscr{C}_2$ at $T$ meets $\mathscr{C}_1$ at $P$. Show that
> $$PA^2 + PB^2 = 4PT^2.$$
