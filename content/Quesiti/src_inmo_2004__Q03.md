*Real root of cubic; prove floor of a^3 equals 3*

> If $a$ is a real root of the equation $x^3 - x^2 + x - 2 = 0$, prove that $\lfloor a^3 \rfloor = 3$. (For any real number $a$, we denote by $\lfloor a \rfloor$ the greatest integer not exceeding $a$.)
