*Equation $a^2+b^2+c^2=3abc$ has natural number solutions greater than any $n$*

> Prove that for every $n$ there exists a solution of the equation
> $$a^2 + b^2 + c^2 = 3abc$$
> in natural numbers $a, b, c$ greater than $n$.
