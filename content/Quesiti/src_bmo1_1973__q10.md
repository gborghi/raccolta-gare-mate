*Flowchart for printing integers up to 100 of form a^2 - b^2 - c^2*

> Construct a detailed flow chart for a computer program to print out all positive integers up to 100 of the form $a^2 - b^2 - c^2$, where $a$, $b$, $c$ are positive integers and $a \ge b + c$.
> 
> There is no need to print in ascending order or to avoid repetitions.
