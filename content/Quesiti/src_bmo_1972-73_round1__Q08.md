*Companion matrix of a Mobius map; product and conditions for T^n = I*

> Call $M_p = \begin{pmatrix} a_p & b_p \\ c_p & d_p \end{pmatrix}$ the companion matrix for the mapping $T_p : a \longmapsto \frac{a_p a + b_p}{c_p a + d_p}$. $\det M_p \ne 0$.
> 
> (i) Prove that $M_1 M_2$ is the companion matrix for the mapping $T_1 T_2$.
> 
> (ii) Find conditions on $a, b, c, d$ so that $T^n = I$ but $T^2 \ne I$.
