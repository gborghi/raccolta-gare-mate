# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2015 — Nível 1 — Fase 2 — Quesito 3

*Two 1 cm-wide strips cut into rectangles forming 100 cm decorative strip; find original width*

> Two strips of width $1$ cm, one black and one white, were glued together to form smaller rectangles. The rectangles were placed side by side (with no overlaps and using both strips completely) to form the decorative strip shown below. If the decorative strip has a total length of $100$ cm, what was the width, in cm, of each strip before it was cut?
> 
> (The strip shows alternating black-and-white rectangular pieces arranged in a row of total length $100$ cm.)
