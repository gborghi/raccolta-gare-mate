*Acute triangle, orthocenter, two circumcircles meet altitude lines outside; prove AE = AF*

> Let $H$ be the orthocenter of an acute triangle $ABC$, and let $D$ be the intersection of lines $AH$ and $BC$. Let $E$ be the point (other than $A$ and $B$) at which the circumcircle of triangle $ABD$ meets line $CH$, where $E$ lies outside triangle $ABC$. Let $F$ be the point (other than $A$ and $C$) at which the circumcircle of triangle $ACD$ meets line $BH$, where $F$ lies outside triangle $ABC$. Prove that $AE = AF$.
