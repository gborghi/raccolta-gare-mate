*Earthworm Dave: 40 cm long, 26 g mass. Find mass per unit length in g/cm.*

> Last year, an earthworm named Dave was wiggled into the record books as the largest found in the UK. Dave was 40 cm long and had a mass of 26 g.
> 
> What was Dave's mass per unit length?
>
> - **(A)** $0.6$ g/cm
> - **(B)** $0.65$ g/cm
> - **(C)** $0.75$ g/cm
> - **(D)** $1.6$ g/cm
> - **(E)** $1.75$ g/cm
