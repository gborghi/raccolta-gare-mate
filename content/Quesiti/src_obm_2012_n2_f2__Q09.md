# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2012 — Nível 2 — Fase 2 — Quesito 9

*Count non-crossing Hamiltonian routes through 20 cities on a circle*

> There are 20 cities marked on a circle. A merchant wants to travel through all 20 cities, visiting each city exactly once, passing between cities through straight-line segments. Knowing that these line segments (traversed between consecutive cities on the route) must never cross each other, determine the number of ways the merchant can establish his route.
