*Acute triangle: prove AM·PM = BM² with centroid and midpoint*

> Let $H$ be the centroid of an acute triangle $ABC$, $M$ the midpoint of $BC$. Let $P$ be the foot of the perpendicular from $H$ to the line $AM$. Prove that $AM \cdot PM = BM^2$. (Here $XY$ denotes the length of segment $XY$.)
