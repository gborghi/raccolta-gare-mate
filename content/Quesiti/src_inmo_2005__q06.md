*Find all f: R to R satisfying f(x^2 + yf(z)) = xf(x) + zf(y)*

> Find all functions $f : \mathbf{R} \to \mathbf{R}$ such that
> $$f(x^2 + yf(z)) = xf(x) + zf(y) \tag{1}$$
> for all $x, y, z \in \mathbf{R}$. (Here $\mathbf{R}$ denotes the set of all real numbers.)
