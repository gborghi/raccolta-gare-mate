*n a perfect square; find the n smallest integers expressible as product of two factors each at most n*

> Let $n$ be a perfect square. Consider all integers that can be expressed as a product $a \times b$ where $a$ and $b$ are positive integers each at most $n$ (not necessarily distinct). Among these integers, list the $n$ smallest ones (in increasing order), expressing them in terms of $n$.
