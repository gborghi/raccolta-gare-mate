*Midpoints and altitude foot in triangle ABC; area of pentagon AERSF with area ABC=252*

> In the figure, $E$ is the midpoint of $AC$, $R$ is the midpoint of $AB$, and $S$ is the foot of the altitude from $B$ to $AC$. Also, $BR = RS = SC$. If the area of triangle $ABC$ is $252$, what is the area of pentagon $AERSF$?
> 
> **(A)** $168$ $\quad$ **(B)** $189$ $\quad$ **(C)** $200$ $\quad$ **(D)** $210$ $\quad$ **(E)** $220$
