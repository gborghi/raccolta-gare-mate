*Find minimum of product involving gcd over n coprime integers*

> Given any $n\ (>1)$ coprime positive integers $a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_n$, denote $A = a_1 + a_2 + \cdots + a_n$. Let $d_i = (A,\, a_i)$ (the greatest common divisor), $i = 1, 2, \ldots, n$. Let $D_i$ be the greatest common divisor of $\{a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_n\} \setminus \{a_i\}$, $i = 1, 2, \ldots, n$. Find the minimum of $\prod_{i=1}^{n} \dfrac{A - a_i}{d_i D_i}$.
> 
> (posed by Zhang Sihui)
