# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2012 — Nível 1 — Fase 1 — Quesito 20

*Smallest central circle value when four 3-circle segments have equal sums*

> In the figure, there are 4 line segments, each passing through 3 circles. The circles are labelled with the numbers 1 through 9 (each used exactly once), so that the sum of the three numbers on each segment is the same for all four segments. What is the smallest number that can be written in the central circle (the circle shared by all four segments)?
> 
> (A) 1 \quad (B) 2 \quad (C) 3 \quad (D) 4 \quad (E) 5
