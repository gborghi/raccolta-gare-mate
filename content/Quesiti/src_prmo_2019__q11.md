*Triangles similar to ABC with cos A cos B sin C = 1*

> How many distinct triangles $ABC$ are there, up to similarity, such that the magnitudes of angles $A$, $B$ and $C$ in degrees are positive integers and
> $$\cos A \cos B + \sin A \sin B \sin kC = 1$$
> for some positive integer $k$, where $kC$ does not exceed $360^\circ$?
