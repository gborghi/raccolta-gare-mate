*Prove cyclic sum inequality for positive reals r_1,...,r_n with n≥3*

> Prove that for all positive integers $n \ge 3$ and for all positive real numbers $r_1, r_2, \ldots, r_n$ the following chain of inequalities
> $$\sum_{k=1}^{n} \frac{1}{r_{k+1} + r_{k+2}} \ge \frac{n}{\displaystyle\sum_{k=1}^{n} r_k}$$
> holds (where $r_{n+1} = r_1,\ r_{n+2} = r_2,\ r_{n+3} = r_3,\ r_{n+4} = r_4$).
