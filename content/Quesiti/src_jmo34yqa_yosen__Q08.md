# Japan Mathematical Olympiad 2024 — Yosen (Preliminary) — Quesito 8

*Count tuples (f(0),...,f(2024)) for functional equation on non-negative integers*

> A function $f$ defined on the non-negative integers takes non-negative integer values and satisfies, for all non-negative integers $m, n$:
> $$f(m+n)^2 = f(m \cdot f(n)) + f(n^2).$$
> How many tuples $(f(0), f(1), \ldots, f(2024))$ are possible?
