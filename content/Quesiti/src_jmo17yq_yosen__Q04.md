*4-digit integers whose upper and lower 2-digit parts have product equal to divisor count*

> Let $n$ be a 4-digit positive integer whose tens digit is not $0$. Consider the integer $p$ formed by the hundreds and thousands digits of $n$ (the upper two digits) and the integer $q$ formed by the tens and units digits of $n$ (the lower two digits), where both $p$ and $q$ are 2-digit integers. The product $p \cdot q$ equals the number of positive divisors of $n$. Find all such $n$.
