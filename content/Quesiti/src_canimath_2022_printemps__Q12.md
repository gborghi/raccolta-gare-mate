*Isosceles triangle, two angle bisectors, prove an angle equality*

> Let $ABC$ be a triangle isosceles at $B$. Let $D$ be the point of intersection of the bisector of the angle $\widehat{BAC}$ with the segment $[BC]$. Let $E$ be the point of segment $[AC]$ distinct from $C$ such that $DE = DC$. Let $F$ be the point of intersection of the bisector of the angle $\widehat{AED}$ with the side $[AB]$. Show that $\widehat{AFE} = \widehat{EFD}$.
