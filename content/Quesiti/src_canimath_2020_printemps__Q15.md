# Coupe Animath Printemps 2020 — Quesito 15

*Square ABCD with exterior equilateral triangle BCS; N,H,P midpoints; compute angles BPN and NHC*

> Let $ABCD$ be a square and let $S$ be a point on the exterior of the square $ABCD$ such that the triangle $BCS$ is equilateral. Let $N$ be the midpoint of the segment $[AS]$ and $H$ the midpoint of the segment $[CD]$. Let $P$ be the midpoint of the segment $[BS]$.
> 1) Compute the angle $\widehat{BPN}$.
> 2) Compute the angle $\widehat{NHC}$.
