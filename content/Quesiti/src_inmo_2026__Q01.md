# Indian National Mathematical Olympiad 2026 — Quesito 1

*Determine which positive integers m occur as a perfect-square term m^2 in a floor-of-square-root recurrence sequence.*

> Let $x_1, x_2, x_3, \ldots$ be a sequence of positive integers defined as follows: $x_1 = 1$ and for each $n \ge 1$ we have
> $$x_{n+1} = x_n + \lfloor \sqrt{x_n} \rfloor.$$
> Determine all positive integers $m$ for which $x_n = m^2$ for some $n \ge 1$. (Here $\lfloor x \rfloor$ denotes the greatest integer less or equal to $x$ for every real number $x$.)
