# Japan EGMO Selection / Girls MO 2024 — Quesito 2

*Count circle arrangements guaranteeing B wins grid game*

> Let $n \ge 2$ be an integer. There is an $n \times n$ grid, and zero or more cells have a circle written in them. Players A and B play a game using this grid. The following operation is repeated $n - 1$ times:
> 
> Player A chooses a row that has not been chosen before, and colors all cells in that row black. Then Player B chooses a column that has not been chosen before, and colors all cells in that column black.
> 
> After all operations are complete, if there exists a cell that is not colored black and contains a circle, then B wins; otherwise A wins. Find the number of ways to place circles such that B can guarantee a win regardless of A's actions.
> 
> Note: arrangements that coincide by rotation or reflection are counted as distinct.
