# British Mathematical Olympiad 2006-07 — Round 1 — Quesito 4

*Two tangent circles with common tangent; diameter AP; tangent PQ; prove AP = PQ*

> Two touching circles $S$ and $T$ have a common tangent which meets $S$ at $A$ and $T$ at $B$. Let $AP$ be a diameter of $S$ and let the tangent from $P$ to $T$ touch it at $Q$. Prove that $AP = PQ$.
