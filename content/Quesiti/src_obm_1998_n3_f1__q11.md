*On a calculator, key A maps x to 1/x and key 2 doubles; starting from 2, the sequence ABABAB...AB (998 keystrokes) is typed. Find the resulting number.*

> On a calculator, key $A$ transforms the number $x$ on the display into $\dfrac{1}{x}$ and key $2$ multiplies by $2$ the number on the display. If the number $2$ is on the display and we type the sequence ABABABAB...AB (total of $998$ keystrokes), the number that will appear on the display is equal to:
> 
> A) $1$
> 
> B) $2^{498}$
> 
> C) $2^{500}$
> 
> D) $2^{499}$
> 
> E) $2^{501}$
