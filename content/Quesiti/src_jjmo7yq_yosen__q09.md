*Volume of tetrahedron O-ABC with given distances and equal angles from interior point X*

> In tetrahedron $O$-$ABC$, let $X$ be a point in the base face $ABC$ such that $OA = 2$, $OB = 3$, $OC = 4$, $\angle AOB = \angle BOC = \angle COA$, and $\angle AOX = \angle BOX = \angle COX = 30^\circ$. Find the volume of tetrahedron $O$-$ABC$. (Here $PQ$ denotes the length of segment $PQ$.)
