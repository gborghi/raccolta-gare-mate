*Find all continuous f satisfying f(x)+f(ax)=x*

> Given $0 < a < 1$, find all continuous functions $f : \mathbb{R} \to \mathbb{R}$ such that $f(x) + f(ax) = x$ for all $x \in \mathbb{R}$.
