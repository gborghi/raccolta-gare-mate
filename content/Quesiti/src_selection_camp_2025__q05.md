*Find subsets S of powers of 2 realizable as image of f(a+b)-f(a)-f(b)*

> Determine all subsets $\mathcal{S}$ of $\{2^0, 2^1, 2^2, \ldots\}$ with the following property: there exists a function $f$ from the positive integers to the positive integers such that the set of integers of the form $f(a+b) - f(a) - f(b)$, as $a$ and $b$ range over all positive integers, equals $\mathcal{S}$.
