*Find all n>=2 for which a chain-sum inequality holds for all positive reals.*

> Find all integers $n \ge 2$ such that the inequality
> $$x_1 x_2 + x_2 x_3 + \cdots + x_{n-1} x_n \le \frac{n-1}{n}\left(x_1^2 + x_2^2 + \cdots + x_n^2\right)$$
> is satisfied for all positive numbers $x_1, x_2, \ldots, x_n$.
