# British Mathematical Olympiad 2005-06 — Round 1 — Quesito 5

*Show convex quadrilateral has a point X through which every line bisects the area*

> Let $G$ be a convex quadrilateral. Show that there is a point $X$ in the plane of $G$ with the property that every straight line through $X$ divides $G$ into two regions of equal area.
