*Least natural number n such that f(n*pi+x)=f(x) for all real x*

> Let $f(x) = \sin\frac{x}{3} + \cos\frac{3x}{10}$ for all real $x$. Find the least natural number $n$ such that $f(n\pi + x) = f(x)$ for all real $x$.
