# Coupe Animath Automne 2024 — Quesito 6

*Square ABCD, equilateral triangles ABE and DAF exterior; midpoint H of EF, G = (BF)∩(CE); show GH=GC*

> Let $ABCD$ be a square, and $E$ and $F$ points exterior to $ABCD$ such that triangles $ABE$ and $DAF$ are equilateral. Let $H$ be the midpoint of $[EF]$ and $G$ the intersection point of lines $(BF)$ and $(CE)$. Show that $GH = GC$.
