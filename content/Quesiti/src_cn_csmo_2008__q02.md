*Find general term of recurrence a_{n+1}=2a_n+n(1+2^n)*

> It is given the sequence $\{a_n\}$: $a_1 = 1$,
> $$a_{n+1} = 2a_n + n \cdot (1 + 2^n), \quad n = 1, 2, 3, \cdots.$$
> Find the general term $a_n$.
