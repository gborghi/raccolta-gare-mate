*For a tetrahedron with altitudes h_i and distances d_i between opposite edge pairs, prove the reciprocal-square sums are equal.*

> Prove that if $h_1,h_2,h_3,h_4$ are the altitudes of a tetrahedron and $d_1,d_2,d_3$ are the distances between the pairs of opposite edges of the tetrahedron, then
> $$\frac{1}{h_1^2}+\frac{1}{h_2^2}+\frac{1}{h_3^2}+\frac{1}{h_4^2}=\frac{1}{d_1^2}+\frac{1}{d_2^2}+\frac{1}{d_3^2}.$$

*Per un tetraedro con altitudini h_i e distanze d_i tra coppie di bordi opposte, dimostrare che le somme squadrate reciproche sono uguali.*
