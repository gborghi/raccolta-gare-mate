*Minimum value of x²+y² with constraint xy(x²−y²)=x²+y²*

> Find the minimum possible value of $x^2 + y^2$ given that $x$ and $y$ are real numbers satisfying
> $$xy(x^2 - y^2) = x^2 + y^2$$
> and $x \neq 0$.
