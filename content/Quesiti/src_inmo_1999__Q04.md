*Equilateral triangles inscribed in concentric circles inequality for arbitrary points*

> The equilateral triangles $ABC$ and $A_1B_1C_1$ are inscribed in concentric circles $\Gamma$ and $\Gamma_1$ respectively. If $P$ and $P_1$ are arbitrary points on $\Gamma$ and $\Gamma_1$ respectively, prove that
> $$P_1A^2 + P_1B^2 + P_1C^2 = PA_1^2 + PB_1^2 + PC_1^2.$$
