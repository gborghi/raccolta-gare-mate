# IMO 1988 (29th IMO, Canberra) — Quesito 3

*Count n<=1988 with recursively-defined f(n)=n*

> A function f is defined on the positive integers by
> f(1)
> =
> 1,
> f(3) = 3,
> f(2n)
> =
> f(n),
> f(4n + 1)
> =
> 2f(2n + 1) −f(n),
> f(4n + 3)
> =
> 3f(2n + 1) −2f(n),
> for all positive integers n.
> Determine the number of positive integers n, less than or equal to 1988, for
> which f(n) = n.
> 
> 29th International Mathematical Olympiad
> Canberra, Australia
> Day II
