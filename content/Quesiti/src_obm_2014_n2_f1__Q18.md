*Roots of two quadratics related; find value of a*

> The roots of the equation $x^2 - ax + b = 0$ are different from zero and are also the roots of the equation $x^2 - bx + a = 0$. The values $a$ and $b$ are not necessarily real. What is the value of $a$?
> 
> (A) $-\sqrt{2}$ $\quad$ (B) $\sqrt{2}\,\sqrt{3}$ $\quad$ (C) $\sqrt{3}$ $\quad$ (D) $\sqrt{2}$ $\quad$ (E) $\sqrt{3}$
