# British Mathematical Olympiad 2001-02 — Round 1 — Quesito 2

*Cyclic quadrilateral, equal lengths CD=CP=DQ; prove angle CAD=40°*

> The quadrilateral $ABCD$ is inscribed in a circle. The diagonals $AC$, $BD$ meet at $P$. The sides $DA$, extended beyond $A$, and $CB$, extended beyond $B$, meet at $Q$.
> Given that $CD = CP = DQ$, prove that $\angle CAD = 40^\circ$.
