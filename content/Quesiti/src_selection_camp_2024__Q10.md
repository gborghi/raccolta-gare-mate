*Existence of increasing sequence with divisibility and prime-divisor density*

> Let $a_1, a_2, \ldots$ be positive integers. Determine whether there exists such a sequence satisfying both of the following conditions:
> \begin{itemize}
> \item For every positive integer $k$, $a_k < a_{k+1}$, and $a_{k+1}$ divides $2(a_1 + a_2 + \cdots + a_k)$.
> \item There exists a prime $p$ such that infinitely many of the terms $a_1, a_2, \ldots$ are divisible by $p$.
> \end{itemize}
> Determine whether such a sequence $a_1, a_2, \ldots$ exists.
