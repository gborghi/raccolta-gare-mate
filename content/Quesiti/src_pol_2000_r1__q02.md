*Acute triangle with angle ACB = 2 angle ABC, D on BC with 2 angle BAD = angle ABC; prove 1/BD = 1/AB + 1/AC.*

> In an acute-angled triangle $ABC$ with $\angle ACB = 2\angle ABC$, $D$ is the point on side $BC$ satisfying $2\angle BAD = \angle ABC$. Prove that
> $$\frac{1}{BD} = \frac{1}{AB} + \frac{1}{AC}.$$
