*Positive integers m,n with 15m=20n; mn is necessarily a multiple of which value.*

> The positive integers $m$ and $n$ satisfy $15m = 20n$. Then it is possible to affirm, with certainty, that $mn$ is a multiple of: (A) $5$ (B) $10$ (C) $12$ (D) $15$ (E) $20$
