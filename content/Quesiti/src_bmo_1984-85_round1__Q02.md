*For a,b,c in (0,1), not all of a(1-b),b(1-c),c(1-a) exceed 1/4*

> $a, b, c$, are each numbers between $0$ and $1$. Prove that not all of $a(1-b)$, $b(1-c)$, $c(1-a)$ can be greater than $\frac{1}{4}$.
