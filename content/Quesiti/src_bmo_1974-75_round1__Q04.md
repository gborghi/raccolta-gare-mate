# British Mathematical Olympiad 1974-75 — Round 1 — Quesito 4

*Three parallel lines through triangle vertices; ratio k given by intersection*

> Three parallel lines $AD$, $BE$, $CF$ are drawn through the vertices of triangle $ABC$ meeting the opposite sides in $D$, $E$, $F$ respectively.
> 
> The points $P_1, Q_1$ divide $AD$, $BE$, $CF$ respectively in the same ratio $k:1$ and $P_2, Q_2$ divide $AD$, $BE$, $CF$ respectively and $P_2, Q_2$ at the centre of $AD$. Find the value of $k$.
