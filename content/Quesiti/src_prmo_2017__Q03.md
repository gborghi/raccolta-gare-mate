*Days team B works alone to finish job after A starts, B joins, then A withdraws*

> A contractor has two teams of workers: team $A$ and team $B$. Team $A$ can complete a job in 12 days and team $B$ can do the same job in 36 days. Team $A$ starts working on the job and team $B$ joins team $A$ after four days. Team $A$ withdraws after two more days. For how many more days should team $B$ work to complete the job?
