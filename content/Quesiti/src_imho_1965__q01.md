*Find all x in [0,2π] satisfying a trigonometric inequality*

> Determine all values $x$ in the interval $0 \le x \le 2\pi$ which satisfy the inequality
> $$2\cos x \le \sqrt{1+\sin 2x} - \sqrt{1-\sin 2x} \le \sqrt{2}.$$
