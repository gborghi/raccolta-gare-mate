*Count white sticks in the 2002nd figure of a 3-by-n grid sequence*

> The figures below are constructed with black and white sticks. To construct the figures, the black sticks form the squares and the white sticks the interior. The figure of number $n$ corresponds to a rectangle $3$ by $n$. Continuing this procedure, how many white sticks will we have in figure $2002$?
> 
> (A) $2001$ (B) $4004$ (C) $12006$ (D) $10007$ (E) $10010$
