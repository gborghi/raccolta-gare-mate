# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2007 — Nível 2 — Fase 2 — Quesito 3

*Angle chasing through several isosceles triangles to a target angle (174 degrees).*

> In a configuration built from the isosceles triangles $PTA$, $ABD$, $BCE$ and $PQC$, with certain given angles, determine the requested angle. [Source is the solutions sheet; the full statement and figure are NOT present. The solution computes: $\angle STP=108^\circ$, $\angle TPA=\angle PAT=72^\circ$, then $\angle TPA=36^\circ$, $\angle BAD=\angle BDA=18^\circ$, $\angle ABD=144^\circ$, $\angle EBC=66^\circ$, $\angle QPC=126^\circ$, $\angle QCP=27^\circ$, $\angle ECB=57^\circ$, and a final answer of $174^\circ$.]
