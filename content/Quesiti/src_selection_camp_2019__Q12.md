# Japan IMO Selection Camp 2019 — Selection Camp — Quesito 12

*x^3+y, x^2+y^2, x+y^3 integers implies x,y integers*

> Let $x$ and $y$ be real numbers. Prove that if
> $$x^3 + y, \qquad x^2 + y^2, \qquad x + y^3$$
> are all integers, then $x$ and $y$ are also integers.
