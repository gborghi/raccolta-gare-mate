# Coupe Animath Printemps 2025 — Quesito 6

*Prove all collinear integer-distance points have even pairwise distances*

> Let $n \geq 1$ be an integer. Rémi chose $2n+1$ collinear, pairwise distinct points such that all pairwise distances between these points are integers. Elsa observes that, for any point she picks among the $2n+1$ points, when she computes the $2n$ distances from that point to each of the other $2n$ points, she obtains an even count of odd distances.
> 
> Show that all pairwise distances between these $2n+1$ points are even integers.
