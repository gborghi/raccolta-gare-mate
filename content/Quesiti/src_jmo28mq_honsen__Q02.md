*lines BX and CY meet on circumcircle of ADE*

> Let $ABC$ be a triangle with $AB < AC$. Points $D$ and $E$ lie on sides $AB$ and $AC$ respectively (not at the endpoints) and satisfy $CA = CD$ and $BA = BE$. Let $\omega$ be the circumcircle of triangle $ADE$, and let $P$ be the reflection of $A$ across line $BC$. Let $X$ be the intersection of line $PD$ with $\omega$ other than $D$, and let $Y$ be the intersection of line $PE$ with $\omega$ other than $E$. Show that lines $BX$ and $CY$ meet at a point on $\omega$. (Here $ST$ denotes the length of the segment $ST$.)
