*Find x so that factors of 100 fill a 3×3 grid with equal row, column, and diagonal products*

> Each of the factors of 100 is to be placed in a $3 \times 3$ grid, one per cell, in such a way that the products of the three numbers in each row, column and diagonal are all equal. The positions of the numbers $1$, $2$, $50$ and $x$ are shown in the diagram.
> 
> What is the value of $x$?
>
> - **(A)** 4
> - **(B)** 5
> - **(C)** 10
> - **(D)** 20
> - **(E)** 25
