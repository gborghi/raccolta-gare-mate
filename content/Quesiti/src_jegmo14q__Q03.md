*Find all positive integers n with n = phi(n) + 2 phi(phi(n))*

> Find all positive integers $n$ satisfying
> $$n = \phi(n) + 2\phi\big(\phi(n)\big).$$
> Here, $\phi(n)$ denotes the number of integers from $1$ to $n$ that are coprime to $n$.
