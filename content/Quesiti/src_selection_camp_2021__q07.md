*Functional equation over the reals with nested f*

> Find all functions $f$ defined on the real numbers and taking real values such that, for all real numbers $x$ and $y$,
> $$f\left(x^2 + xy + y^2\right) = 2x^2 f(y) + 2x f\bigl(f(y)\bigr) + f\left(-x^2 - xy^2\right) + f\left(y^2\right).$$

*Equazione funzionale sui reali con f * annidato
