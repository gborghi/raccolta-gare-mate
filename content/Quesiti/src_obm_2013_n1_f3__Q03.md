# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2013 — Nível 1 — Fase 3 — Quesito 3

*Square paper folded at each vertex forms a pinwheel; find gray/white area ratio and overlap area*

> Paulo has a square sheet of paper $ABCD$ with side $20$ cm. The front of the sheet is white and the back is gray. He marks the center $X$ of the square $ABCD$. To make the fold he cuts along segment $BE$, where $E$ is the midpoint of a side, and folds the triangular flap so that point $B$ meets point $E$ (i.e., vertex $B$ is folded to meet the center $X$ of the fold). He repeats the procedure for each of the other three vertices of the square, completing the pinwheel (*cata-vento*), as shown in the figure.
> 
> a) What is the ratio between the gray area and the white area in the figure?
> 
> b) Paulo then took another square sheet $XYZW$, identical to sheet $ABCD$, and assembled another pinwheel. He rotated the pinwheel $XYZW$ by $45^\circ$ and placed it on top of pinwheel $ABCD$ so that the figures were superimposed, as shown in the figure below.
> 
> What is the total white area of the combined figure?
