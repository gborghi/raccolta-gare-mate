*2015 people at round table, each says one neighbor honest and one dishonest; count honest*

> There are $2015$ people seated around a circular table. Each of them is either honest (always tells the truth) or dishonest (always lies). Each person makes the following statement: "One of my two neighbors (the one on the left or the one on the right) is honest, and the other neighbor is dishonest." What is the number of honest people in the group?
