# Japan Junior Mathematical Olympiad 2009 — Honsen (Final) — Quesito 2

*Angle in triangle with BP+CQ=PQ and circumcircle intersection*

> Let $P$ and $Q$ be points on sides $AB$ and $AC$ of triangle $ABC$, respectively, satisfying $BP + CQ = PQ$. Let $R$ be the intersection of the angle bisector of $\angle BAC$ with the circumcircle of triangle $ABC$, other than $A$. Set $\angle BAC = \alpha$.
> 
> Find $\angle PRQ$ in terms of $\alpha$.
> 
> Here, $XY$ denotes the length of segment $XY$.
