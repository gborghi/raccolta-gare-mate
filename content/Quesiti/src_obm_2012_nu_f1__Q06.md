*Values of a for which parabola-sequence region has infinite area*

> Consider the parabola formed by the points $(x, x^2)$, $x \in \mathbb{R}$, and the sequence $(x_n)_{n \ge 0}$ given by $x_n = n^\alpha$, where $\alpha > 0$ is a fixed real constant. Consider the region formed by the points that lie to the right of the vertical axis ($x = 0$), below the parabola, and above the tangent lines to the parabola at the points $(x_n, x_n^2)$, $n \ge 0$. For which values of $\alpha$ does this region have infinite area?
