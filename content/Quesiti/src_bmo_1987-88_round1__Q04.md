*Perpendicular feet from orthocentre to angle bisectors are collinear with midpoint of BC*

> The triangle $ABC$ has orthocentre $H$. The feet of the perpendiculars from $H$ to the internal and external bisectors of angle $BAC$ (which is not a right angle) are $P$ and $Q$. Prove that $PQ$ passes through the midpoint of $BC$.
