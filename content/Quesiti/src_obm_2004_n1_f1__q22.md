*Minimum path visiting all nodes of an interconnected map*

> A map viewed from above shows a residential neighborhood where all houses, numbered $1$ through $23$, are interconnected by $23$ lanes. The vendor Zé Ruda, who lives in house $8$, plans to pass through all other houses and return home, traveling the minimum number of lanes. How many lanes must he travel?
> 
> (A) $15$ \quad (B) $10$ \quad (C) $12$ \quad (D) $12$ \quad (E) $11$
