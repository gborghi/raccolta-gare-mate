*Smallest n with S(n²) = S(n) − 7, where S is the sum-of-divisors function*

> For a positive integer $k$, let $S(k)$ denote the sum of all positive divisors of $k$. Find the smallest positive integer $n$ such that $S(n^2) = S(n) - 7$.
