*Prove 4(a^3+b^3) >= (a+b)^3 and 9(a^3+b^3+c^3) >= (a+b+c)^3*

> Let $a$, $b$ and $c$ be positive real numbers.
> (i) Prove that $4(a^3 + b^3) \ge (a + b)^3$.
> (ii) Prove that $9(a^3 + b^3 + c^3) \ge (a + b + c)^3$.
