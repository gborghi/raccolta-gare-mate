*Square ABCD, equilateral triangles ABP and DAQ exterior; compute NM/NC*

> Let $ABCD$ be a square, and $P$ and $Q$ points exterior to $ABCD$ such that triangles $ABP$ and $DAQ$ are equilateral. Let $N$ be the midpoint of $[PQ]$ and $M$ the intersection point of lines $(BQ)$ and $(DP)$. Compute $\dfrac{NM}{NC}$.
