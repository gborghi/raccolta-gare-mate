*Cyclic quadrilateral with E,F on AB,CD and P on EF; ratio of areas of APD and BPC is independent of E,F.*

> Let $ABCD$ be a cyclic quadrilateral and let $E$ and $F$ be the points on the sides $AB$ and $CD$ respectively such that $AE : EB = CF : FD$. Point $P$ on the segment $EF$ satisfies $EP : PF = AB : CD$. Prove that the ratio of the areas of $\triangle APD$ and $\triangle BPC$ does not depend on the choice of $E$ and $F$.
