*Regular n-gon A_1A_2...A_n; identity for reciprocals of products of sides*

> Find $n$ such that in a regular $n$-gon $A_1 A_2 \ldots A_n$ we have
> $$\frac{1}{A_1 A_2} = \frac{1}{A_1 A_3} + \frac{1}{A_1 A_4}.$$
