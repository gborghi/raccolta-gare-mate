- [[src_cgen_2013__Q01|Quesito 1]] — Superbe sequences of positive integers where every term divides the sum of all terms; characterize lengths 2-4, distinctness, primes, arithmetic ones, infinite magnifique sequences, and extensions.
- [[src_cgen_2013__Q02|Quesito 2]] — Skew lines and transversals in space; a quadric surface S given by xy+yz+zx-(x+y+z)+1=0 containing three cube edges D1,D2,D3; lines meeting S in 0,1,2 points; transversals to four lines.
- [[src_cgen_2013__Q03|Quesito 3]] — Sisyphe's dice game on 106 cells: wins by reaching/passing cell 100, loses on a prime cell below 100. Probabilities p_n, the position variable X, an algorithm for P(X=k), and an estimate of the gain probability via conditional probabilities alpha_p over consecutive primes.
