*Runners on a regular n-gon track meet at vertices; find n*

> On a running track, whose shape is a regular polygon with $n$ vertices, numbered from $1$ to $n$ in the counterclockwise direction, there are three people: Nelly, Sônia and Penha, all initially at the same vertex. At a given moment they begin to walk along the sides of the polygon. Nelly walks in the counterclockwise direction, while Sônia and Penha walk in the opposite direction. Nelly meets Sônia for the first time at a vertex, and meets Penha two vertices ahead. The speed of Nelly is double the speed of Sônia, and the speed of Sônia is double the speed of Penha. How many vertices does the polygon have?
> 
> (A) $30$ (B) $60$ (C) $15$ (D) $10$ (E) $6$
