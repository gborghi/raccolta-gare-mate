*Partitions of 1-12 into pairs with total |i-j| equal to 30*

> Partition the integers from $1$ to $12$ into $6$ pairs, two integers per pair. When $i$ and $j$ form a pair, the value $|i - j|$ is the score of that pair. In how many ways can the partition be made so that the total of the scores of the $6$ pairs equals $30$?
