*Prove symmetric inequality for positive reals on unit sphere*

> Let $x$, $y$, $z$ be positive real numbers such that $x^2 + y^2 + z^2 = 1$. Prove that
> $$x^2 y^2 + y^2 z^2 + z^2 x^2 \le \frac{1}{3}.$$
