*Find all prime pairs m<n with 2m+n, m+2n, m+n−18 all prime*

> Find all prime numbers $m$ and $n$ such that $0 < m < n$ and the three numbers
> $$2m + n, \quad m + 2n, \quad m + n - 18$$
> are also prime.
