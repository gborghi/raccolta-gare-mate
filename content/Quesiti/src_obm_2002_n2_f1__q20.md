*Given xy=2 and x²+y²+x²y²=5, find x²/y²+2+y²/x²*

> If $xy = 2$ and $x^2 + y^2 + x^2 y^2 = 5$, then $\dfrac{x^2}{y^2} + 2 + \dfrac{y^2}{x^2}$ equals:
>
> - **(A)** $\dfrac{5}{2}$
> - **(B)** $\dfrac{25}{4}$
> - **(C)** $\dfrac{5}{4}$
> - **(D)** $\dfrac{1}{4}$
> - **(E)** $1$
