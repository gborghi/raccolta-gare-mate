# Regional Mathematical Olympiad 2012 — Mumbai — Quesito 2

*Divisibility by 169 of a quadratic in n*

> Let $n$ be a natural number. Prove that $169$ divides $21n^2 + 89n + 44$ if and only if $13$ divides $n^2 + 3n + 51$.
