*Find all positive integer triples (a,b,c) satisfying a product-of-fractions equation*

> Find all triples $(a, b, c)$ of positive integers such that
> $$\left(1 + \frac{1}{a}\right)\left(1 + \frac{1}{b}\right)\left(1 + \frac{1}{c}\right) = 3.$$
