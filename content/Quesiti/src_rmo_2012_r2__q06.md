*Show x^3y+y^3z+z^3x is constant given two linear conditions*

> Show that for all real numbers $x, y, z$ such that $x + y + z = -3$ and $xy + yz + zx = -3$, the expression $x^3 y + y^3 z + z^3 x$ is a constant.
