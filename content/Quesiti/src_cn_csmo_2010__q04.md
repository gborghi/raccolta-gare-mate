*Count good pairs (a,b) with 1<=a<=b<=10 and ab | a^k - b^k for some k*

> Let $a$ and $b$ be positive integers such that $1 \le a \le b \le 10$. If there exists a positive integer $k$ such that $ab \mid (a^k - b^k)$, then we say that the pair $(a, b)$ is good. Determine the number of good pairs.
