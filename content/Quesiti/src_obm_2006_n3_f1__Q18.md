*Starting from (2048,1024), operation (a,b)->((3a+b)/4,(a+3b)/4); among five listed pairs which are obtainable.*

> Starting from the pair $(2048, 1024)$, we may apply, as many times as we wish, the operation that transforms the pair $(a, b)$ into the pair $\left(\dfrac{3a+b}{4}, \dfrac{a+3b}{4}\right)$; then, among the following pairs:
>
> 1) $(1664, 1408)$
> 2) $(1540, 1532)$
> 3) $(1792, 1282)$
> 4) $(1537, 1535)$
> 5) $(1546, 1526)$
>
> - **(A)** Only pairs 1 and 2 can be obtained.
> - **(B)** Only pair 4 cannot be obtained.
> - **(C)** Only pair 3 cannot be obtained.
> - **(D)** Only pairs 4 and 5 can be obtained.
> - **(E)** There exist more than two pairs that cannot be obtained.
