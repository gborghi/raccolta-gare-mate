*Positive reals with cyclic triple-sum power equalities imply all equal*

> Positive numbers $a, b, c, d$ satisfy the equalities
> $$a^2 + b^2 + c^2 = a^4$$
> $$b^2 + c^2 + d^2 = b^4$$
> $$c^2 + d^2 + a^2 = c^4$$
> $$d^2 + a^2 + b^2 = d^4.$$
> Prove that $a = b = c = d$.
