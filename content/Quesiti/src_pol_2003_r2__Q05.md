*From exterior point A two tangents touch circle at B,C; another tangent meets AB,AC at E,F; lines OE,OF meet BC at P,Q; prove BP,PQ,QC are sides of a triangle similar to AEF.*

> From a point $A$ exterior to a circle $o$ with center $O$, two tangent lines touching $o$ at $B$ and $C$ are drawn. Another tangent line to $o$ intersects the segments $AB$ and $AC$ at points $E$ and $F$, respectively. The lines $OE$ and $OF$ respectively meet the segments $BC$ at $P$ and $Q$. Prove that segments $BP$, $PQ$, and $QC$ are sides of a triangle similar to $\triangle AEF$.
