*Round table: prove equal numbers of tall and short among 2013*

> Around the same table sit $2013$ people who all have different heights. We say that a person is *grande* (tall) if she is taller than her two neighbours, and *petite* (short) if she is shorter than her two neighbours. Prove that the number of tall people around the table is equal to the number of short people.
