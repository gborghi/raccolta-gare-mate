*Unique positive-integer sequence; show a term divisible by 87*

> Show that there is a unique sequence of positive integers $(a_n)$ satisfying the following conditions:
> $$a_1 = 1, \quad a_2 = 2, \quad a_{n+2} = a_n^2 + a_{n+1} \quad \text{for } n = 1, 2, 3, \ldots$$
> and that $a_{1998}$ is divisible by $87$.
