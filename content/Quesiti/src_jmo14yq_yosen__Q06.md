# Japan Mathematical Olympiad 2004 — Yosen (Preliminary) — Quesito 6

*Find f(x) satisfying f(x)+f(1/(1-x))=1/x for all x not 0 or 1.*

> Let $f(x)$ be a real-valued function defined for all real numbers $x$ other than $0$ and $1$, satisfying
> $$f(x) + f\!\left(\frac{1}{1-x}\right) = \frac{1}{x}$$
> for all real $x \neq 0, 1$. Find $f(x)$.
