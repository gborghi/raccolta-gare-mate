*Maximise two- and three-variable expressions over the unit interval/cube*

> (a) Find the maximum value of the expression $x^2 y - y^2 x$ when $0 \le x \le 1$ and $0 \le y \le 1$.
> (b) Find the maximum value of the expression
> $$x^2 y + y^2 z + z^2 x - x^2 z - y^2 x - z^2 y$$
> when $0 \le x \le 1$, $0 \le y \le 1$, $0 \le z \le 1$.
