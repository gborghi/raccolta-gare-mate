*Count cubic polynomials whose values mod 2013 are all distinct*

> Find the number of polynomials $f(x) = ax^3 + bx$ that satisfy the following conditions:
> 
> (1) $a, b \in \{1, 2, \ldots, 2013\}$;
> 
> (2) the difference of any two numbers among $f(1), f(2), \ldots, f(2013)$ is not a multiple of $2013$.
