*Midpoints and parallel lines in quadrilateral give four equal-area parts*

> Let $ABCD$ be a quadrilateral; $X$ and $Y$ the midpoints of $AC$ and $BD$ respectively and the lines through $X$ and $Y$ respectively parallel to $BD$, $AC$ meet in $O$. Let $P$, $Q$, $R$, $S$ be the midpoints of $AB$, $BC$, $CD$, $DA$ respectively. Prove that
> 
> (a) quadrilaterals $APOS$ and $BQOP$ have the same area;
> 
> (b) the areas of the quadrilaterals $APOS$, $BQOP$, $CROQ$, $DSOR$ are all equal.
