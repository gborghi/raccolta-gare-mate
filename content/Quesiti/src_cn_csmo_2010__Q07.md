*Double sum with min of sorted sequences is non-negative*

> Let $n$ be a positive integer. The real numbers $a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_n$ and $r_1, r_2, \ldots, r_n$ are such that $a_1 \le a_2 \le \cdots \le a_n$ and $0 \le r_1 \le r_2 \le \cdots \le r_n$. Prove that $$\sum_{i=1}^{n} \sum_{j=1}^{n} a_i a_j \min(r_i, r_j) \ge 0.$$
