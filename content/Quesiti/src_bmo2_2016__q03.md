*Cyclic quadrilateral, perpendicularity of PQ and AB implies PE perpendicular to BC*

> Let $ABCD$ be a cyclic quadrilateral. The diagonals $AC$ and $BD$ meet at $Q$, and $DA$ and $CB$ produced meet at $P$. The midpoint of $AB$ is $E$. If $AB$ is perpendicular to $PQ$, prove that $PE$ is perpendicular to $BC$.
