# British Mathematical Olympiad 1985-86 — Round 1 — Quesito 2

*Prove positive-integer equation implies m is a perfect square*

> Prove that if $m$, $n$, $a$ are positive integers and
> $$1 + a + a^2 + \cdots + a^m = (1 + a + a^{2n})^{2^{n-1}}$$
> then $m$ is a perfect square.
