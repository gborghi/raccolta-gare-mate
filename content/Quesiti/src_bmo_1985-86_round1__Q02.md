*Radius of a circle from two externally tangent circles between parallel tangents*

> A circle $S$ of radius $R$ has two parallel tangents $\tau_1$, $\tau_2$. A circle $S_1$ of radius $r_1$ touches $S$ and $\tau_1$; a circle $S_2$ of radius $r_2$ touches $S$ and $\tau_2$, and also the circle $S_1$. All the circle contacts are external. Calculate $R$ in terms of $r_1$ and $r_2$.
