# Coupe Animath Automne 2022 — Quesito 4

*Rectangle ABCD; midpoints M, N; show angle MAN equals angle BPN*

> Let $ABCD$ be a rectangle. Let $M$ be the midpoint of the segment $[BC]$ and $N$ the midpoint of the segment $[CD]$. Let $P$ be the intersection point of the lines $(BN)$ and $(DM)$. Show that $\widehat{MAN} = \widehat{BPN}$.
