*Triangle altitude foot; four perpendicular feet are concyclic.*

> Let $ABC$ be a triangle, and let $D$ be the foot of the altitude from $A$. Let $P$, $Q$, $R$ and $S$ be the feet of the perpendiculars from $D$ to $AB$, $DB$, $DC$ and $CA$ respectively. Show that $P$, $Q$, $R$, $S$ are concyclic.
