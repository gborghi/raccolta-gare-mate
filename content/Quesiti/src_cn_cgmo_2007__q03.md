*Minimize cyclic sum over nonneg reals summing to 2*

> Let $n$ be an integer greater than 3, and let $a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_n$ be nonnegative real numbers with $a_1 + a_2 + \cdots + a_n = 2$. Determine the minimum value of
> $$\frac{a_1}{a_2^2+1} + \frac{a_2}{a_3^2+1} + \cdots + \frac{a_n}{a_1^2+1}.$$
