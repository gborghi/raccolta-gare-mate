# British Mathematical Olympiad 1974-75 — Round 1 — Quesito 6

*Prove inequality for integer u > 1 with x > y > 1*

> Prove that if $u$ is an integer greater than $1$ and $x > y > 1$, then
> $$\frac{x^{u+1}-1}{x(x^u-1)} > \frac{y^{u+1}-1}{y(y^u-1)}.$$
