> The circumference of a circle with radius $r$ is divided into four equal arcs. Two of the arcs are indicated, shown shaded. What is the area of the shaded region?
>
> - **(A)** 1
> - **(B)** $\sqrt{2}$
> - **(C)** $\dfrac{\pi}{4}$
> - **(D)** $\sqrt{\pi}$
> - **(E)** 2
