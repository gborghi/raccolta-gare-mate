*Three squares (areas 10, 10, 40) with vertices on sides of triangle FIL; find the area of triangle FIL.*

> Three squares $GQOP$, $HJNO$ and $RKMN$ have vertices which sit on the sides of triangle $FIL$ as shown. The squares have areas of 10, 10 and 40 respectively. What is the area of triangle $FIL$?
>
> - **(A)** 220.5
> - **(B)** $\frac{11}{2}\sqrt{10}$
> - **(C)** 252
> - **(D)** $\frac{11}{2}\sqrt{40}$
> - **(E)** 441
