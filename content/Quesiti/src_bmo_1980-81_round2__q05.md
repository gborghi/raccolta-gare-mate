*Sequence u_0=2, u_1=5 with u_{n+1}u_{n-1}-u_n^2=6^{n-1}; prove u_n integer*

> The sequence $\{u_n\}$ of real numbers is defined for $n \geq 0$ by
> $$u_0 = 2, \quad u_1 = 5$$
> and
> $$u_{n+1}\,u_{n-1} - u_n^2 = 6^{n-1} \quad \text{when } n \geq 1.$$
> Prove that each $u_n$ is an integer.
