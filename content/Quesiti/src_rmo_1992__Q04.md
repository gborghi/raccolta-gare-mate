# Regional Mathematical Olympiad 1992 — Quesito 4

*Cyclic quadrilateral ABCD with diagonal AC meeting BD at E; prove EA²+EB²+EC²+ED²=4R².*

> $ABCD$ is a cyclic quadrilateral with $AC \perp BD$, $AC$ meets $BD$ at $E$. Prove that
> $$EA^2 + EB^2 + EC^2 + ED^2 = 4R^2,$$
> where $R$ is the radius of the circumscribing circle.
