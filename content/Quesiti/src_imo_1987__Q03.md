# IMO 1987 (28th IMO, Havana) — Quesito 3

*Bounded integer combination of unit-norm reals is small*

> Let x1, x2, . . . , xn be real numbers satisfying x2
> 1 + x2
> 2 + · · · + x2
> n = 1. Prove that
> for every integer k ≥2 there are integers a1, a2, . . . , an, not all 0, such that
> |ai| ≤k −1 for all i and
> |a1x1 + a1x2 + · · · + anxn| ≤(k −1)√n
> kn −1
> .
> 
> 28th International Mathematical Olympiad
> Havana, Cuba
> Day II
> July 11, 1987
