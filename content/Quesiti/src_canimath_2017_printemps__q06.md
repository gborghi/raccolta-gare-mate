*Find all integers n≥2 where d|n implies (d-1)|(n-1) for all d≥2*

> Determine all integers $n \ge 2$ such that for every integer $d \ge 2$, if $d$ is a divisor of $n$ then $d - 1$ is a divisor of $n - 1$.
