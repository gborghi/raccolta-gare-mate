*S(x)=digit sum; show there exist n_1<...<n_50 with all n_i+S(n_i) equal.*

> Let $S(x)$ denote the sum of the digits of $x$. Show that there exist positive integers $n_1 < n_2 < \cdots < n_{50}$ such that
> $$n_1 + S(n_1) = n_2 + S(n_2) = \cdots = n_{50} + S(n_{50}).$$
