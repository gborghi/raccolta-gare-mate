*Number of distinct values of weighted sum over all permutations of 1 to n*

> Set the permutation $X = (x_1, x_2, \ldots, x_n)$ of $1, 2, \ldots, n$ in $A$. For $\forall X \in A$, let $f(X) = \sum_{k=1}^{n} k x_k$. Let $M = \{f(X) \mid X \in A\}$. Find the value of $|M|$. (Posed by Xiong Bin)
