# Japan IMO Selection Camp 2024 — Selection Camp — Quesito 1

*Integer sequence with a recurrence for some fixed shift c*

> Let $a_1, a_2, \ldots$ be positive integers. Suppose there exists an integer $c \ge 2$ such that, for every positive integer $n$,
> $$a_{n+c} = 2a_{n+1} - a_n$$
> holds. Find all such sequences (determine all sequences for which a suitable $c$ exists).
