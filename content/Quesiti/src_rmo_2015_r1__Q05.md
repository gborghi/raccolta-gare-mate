# Regional Mathematical Olympiad 2015 — Region 1 — Quesito 5

*Right triangle, midpoints, incircle on circumcircle of midpoint triangle, find ratio*

> Let $ABC$ be a triangle with $\angle B = 90^\circ$. Let $E$ and $F$ be respectively the mid-points of $AB$ and $AC$. Suppose the incircle $I$ of triangle $ABC$ lies on the circumcircle of triangle $AEF$. Find the ratio $BC/AB$.
