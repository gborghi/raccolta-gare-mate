*Square with one diagonal and a circle of radius 2 touching the diagonal and two sides; find side length of square.*

> The diagram shows a square, one of its diagonals and a circle. The circle touches the diagonal and two sides of the square. The circle has radius 2. What is the length of the side of the square?
>
> - **(A)** $4 + 2\sqrt{2}$
> - **(B)** $8 - \sqrt{3}$
> - **(C)** $2 + 2\sqrt{2}$
> - **(D)** $8 - 2\sqrt{2}$
> - **(E)** $4 + 2\sqrt{3}$
