# Japan Junior Mathematical Olympiad 2020 — Yosen (Preliminary) — Quesito 2

*Pairs (a,b) of positive integers whose consecutive sum equals 2020, minimizing a*

> Let $a, b$ be positive integers. Among all pairs $(a, b)$ such that the sum of all integers from $a$ to $b$ (inclusive) equals $2020$, find all pairs $(a, b)$ for which $a$ is as small as possible.
