*Find all positive reals a,b,c,d satisfying two symmetric equations*

> Let $a, b, c, d$ be positive real numbers such that
> $$a + b + c + d = ab + bc + cd + da$$
> and
> $$abcd = 27.$$
> Find all possible values of $a$, $b$, $c$, $d$.
