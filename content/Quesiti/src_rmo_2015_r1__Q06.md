*Find all reals a in (3,4) such that a(3-2f) is an integer*

> Find all real numbers $a$ such that $3 < a < 4$ and $a(3 - 2f)$ is an integer, where $f = \{a\}$ denotes the fractional part of $a$. (For example $\{1.5\} = 0.5$; $\{-3.4\} = 0.6$.)
