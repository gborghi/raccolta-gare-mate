*A circle divides each side of a rhombus into three pieces; the 12 segments are colored in three colors; prove the red total equals the white total.*

> A circle divides all sides of a rhombus into three pieces. Starting from a vertex of the rhombus and going in a fixed direction along the boundary of the rhombus the 12 segments are colored red, green and white successively. Prove that the sum of the lengths of the red segments is equal to the sum of the lengths of the white segments.
