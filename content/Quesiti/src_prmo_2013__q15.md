*Area of ABCD given A2B2C2D2 (midpoints of midpoints) is a 4x6 rectangle*

> Let $A_1$, $B_1$, $C_1$, $D_1$ be the midpoints of the sides of a convex quadrilateral $ABCD$ and let $A_2$, $B_2$, $C_2$, $D_2$ be the midpoints of the sides of the quadrilateral $A_1 B_1 C_1 D_1$. If $A_2 B_2 C_2 D_2$ is a rectangle with sides 4 and 6, then what is the area of $ABCD$?
