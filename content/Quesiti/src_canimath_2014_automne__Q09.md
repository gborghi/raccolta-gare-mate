# Coupe Animath Automne 2014 — Quesito 9

*25 distinct positive numbers; pick two whose sum/difference equals no other*

> Given $25$ distinct strictly positive numbers, show that one can choose two of them such that none of the $23$ other numbers is equal to the sum or to the difference of the two chosen numbers.
