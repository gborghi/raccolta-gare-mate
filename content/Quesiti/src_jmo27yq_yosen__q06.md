*Term x_144 of an integer recurrence*

> An integer sequence $x_1,x_2,\dots$ is defined by
> $$x_1=1,\qquad x_2=x_3=\cdots=x_{13}=0,$$
> $$x_{n+13}=x_{n+5}+2x_n\qquad(n=1,2,\dots).$$
> Find the value of $x_{144}$.
