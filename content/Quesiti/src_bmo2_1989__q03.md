*Polynomial of degree n with f(k)=1/(k+1); find f(n+1)*

> Let $f(x)$ be a polynomial of degree $n$ such that
> $$f(k) = \frac{1}{k+1}, \quad k = 0, 1, 2, \ldots, n.$$
> Find $f(n+1)$, expressing your result as simply as possible.
