*Count of dates with equal day, month, last two digits of year*

> A nice date occurs when, written as $11/11/11$, the month, the day, and the last two digits of the year are all equal. Last year, this pattern occurred on $10/10/10$. How many days are there from $10/10/10$ to $11/11/11$, including both $10/10/10$ and $11/11/11$?
> 
> (A) 396 \quad (B) 398 \quad (C) 400 \quad (D) 402 \quad (E) 404
