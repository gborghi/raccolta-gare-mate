*Find BC in acute triangle given orthocenter, midpoint, and angle condition*

> In acute triangle $ABC$, $AB = 11$ and $AC = 10$. Let $H$ be the orthocenter and $M$ the midpoint of $BC$. A point $P$ inside triangle $ABC$ lies on the circumcircle of triangle $BHC$ and satisfies $\angle ABP = \angle CPM$ and $PM = 3$. Find the length of side $BC$.

**Topic:** [[topic_geometria_piana|Geometria piana]]
**Metodo:** [[method_trigonometria|Trigonometria]], [[method_coordinate|Coordinate]]
**Abilita:** [[skill_ragionamento_geometrico|Ragionamento geometrico]], [[skill_manipolazione_algebrica|Manipolazione algebrica]], [[skill_lettura_attenta|Lettura attenta]]
**Area:** [[Geometria]]
**Risposta:** $2\sqrt{21}$
