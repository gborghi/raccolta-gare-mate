# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2008 — Nível 2 — Fase 3 — Quesito 6

*Circumcenter O, secondary intersections, three lines concurrent*

> Let $ABC$ be a triangle and $O$ its circumcenter. Lines $AB$ and $AC$ meet the circumcircle of $OBC$ again at $B_1 \neq B$ and $C_1 \neq C$, respectively; lines $BA$ and $BC$ meet the circumcircle of $OAC$ at $A_2 \neq A$ and $C_2 \neq C$, respectively; and lines $CA$ and $CB$ meet the circumcircle of $OAB$ at $A_3 \neq A$ and $B_3 \neq B$, respectively. Prove that lines $A_2A_3$, $B_1B_3$, and $C_1C_2$ are concurrent.
