*Distribute 5-color balls to A,B,C so each holds 0 or >=2 of each color*

> A bag contains balls of $5$ colors: blue, yellow, black, green, red, with $4$ balls of each color, $20$ balls in total. These balls are distributed to three people A, B, C, giving $6$ balls to each, and $2$ balls remain in the bag. How many ways are there to distribute the balls so that all three people satisfy the following condition?
> 
> Condition: For every color of ball, each person holds either $0$ balls of that color or at least $2$ balls of that color.
