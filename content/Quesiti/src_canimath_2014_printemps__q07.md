*Integer sequences satisfying x_10 ≤ 2(x_1+…+x_9) with 10 terms*

> Determine all integer sequences $x_1, x_2, \ldots, x_{10}$ such that
> $$0 < x_1 < x_2 < \cdots < x_{10}$$
> and
> $$x_{10} \le 2(x_1 + x_2 + \cdots + x_9).$$
