# British Mathematical Olympiad 1977-78 — Round 1 — Quesito 6

*Show 2cosθ is polynomial in 2cos(θ/n); prove rationality or irrationality*

> Show that if $k$ is a non-zero integer, $2\cos k\theta$ is a polynomial in $2\cos\theta$ of degree $k$ in $2\cos\theta$.
> 
> Hence or otherwise prove that if $k$ is rational then $2\cos k\theta$ is either equal to one of the numbers $0, \pm 1, \pm 2$, or is irrational.
