*Number of children of Emir Abdel Aziz given twin/triple constraints*

> The Emir Abdel Aziz became famous for many reasons. He had $39$ children, including his descendants; the historian Ahmed Aab claims: all of his children were twins (born in pairs), except $39$; they were also triplets (born in threes), except $39$. The number of children of the Emir is:
> 
> A) $75$ \quad B) $48$ \quad C) $51$ \quad D) $1{,}050$ \quad E) $111$

*Numero di figli di Emir Abdel Aziz con vincoli doppi/tripli*
