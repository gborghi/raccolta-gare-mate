*Find all positive integer solutions of 1/x + 1/y - 1/z = 1*

> Find, with proof, all solutions of
> $$\frac{1}{x} + \frac{1}{y} - \frac{1}{z} = 1$$
> where $x$, $y$, $z$ are positive integers.
