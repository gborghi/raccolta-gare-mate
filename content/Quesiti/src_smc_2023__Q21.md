*Length QX with a semicircle inscribed in an equilateral triangle*

> Triangle $PQR$ is equilateral. A semicircle with centre $O$ is drawn with its diameter on $PR$ so that one end is at $P$ and the curved edge touches $QR$ at $X$. The radius of the semicircle is $\sqrt{3}$. What is the length of $QX$?
>
> - **(A)** $\sqrt{3}$
> - **(B)** $2-\sqrt{3}$
> - **(C)** $2\sqrt{3}-1$
> - **(D)** $1+\sqrt{3}$
> - **(E)** $2\sqrt{3}$
