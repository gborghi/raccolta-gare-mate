*Two circles touching a line at a point, angle bisector ratio*

> Two circles $S_1$ and $S_2$ each touch a straight line $p$ at the same point $P$. All points of $S_2$, except $P$, are in the interior of $S_1$. A straight line $q$ (i) is perpendicular to $p$; (ii) touches $S_2$ at $R$; (iii) cuts $p$ at $L$; and (iv) cuts $S_1$ at $N$ and $M$, where $N$ is between $L$ and $M$.
> 
> (a) Prove that $q$ bisects angle $RPM$.
> 
> (b) If $MP$ bisects angle $RPL$, find, with proof, the ratio of the areas of $S_1$ and $S_2$.
