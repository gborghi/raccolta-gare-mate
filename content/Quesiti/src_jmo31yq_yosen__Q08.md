# Japan Mathematical Olympiad 2021 — Yosen (Preliminary) — Quesito 8

*Count 17-tuples with two power towers both 1 mod 17*

> Find the number of tuples $(a_1,a_2,\dots,a_{17})$ of integers, each at least $2$ and at most $20$, such that
> $$a_1^{a_2^{\cdot^{\cdot^{a_{17}}}}}\equiv a_2^{a_3^{\cdot^{\cdot^{a_{17}}}}}\equiv 1\pmod{17}.$$
> Here each exponent is the power tower formed by the two (and further) numbers written to its upper right.
