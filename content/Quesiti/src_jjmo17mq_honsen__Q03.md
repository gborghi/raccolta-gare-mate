*GCD-min inequality and equality cases for positive integers*

> Let $a, b$ be positive integers. Here $\gcd(x, y)$ denotes the greatest common divisor of $x$ and $y$, and $\min(x, y)$ denotes the smaller of $x$ and $y$.
> 
> (1) Show that the inequality
> $$\min\bigl(\gcd(a,\, b+1),\; \gcd(a+1,\, b)\bigr) \le \frac{\sqrt{4a+5}-1}{2}$$
> holds.
> 
> (2) Find all pairs of positive integers $(a, b)$ for which equality holds in the above inequality.
