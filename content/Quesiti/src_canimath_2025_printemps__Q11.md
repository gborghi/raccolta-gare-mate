# Coupe Animath Printemps 2025 — Quesito 11

*Prove PM bisects angle NPB in isosceles triangle ABC*

> Let $ABC$ be a triangle isosceles at $A$. Let $M$ and $N$ be the midpoints of segments $[BC]$ and $[AC]$ respectively, and let $P$ be the foot of the altitude from $C$ in triangle $ABC$.
> 
> Show that $[PM]$ is the angle bisector of $\angle NPB$.
