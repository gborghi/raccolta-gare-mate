*Triangle is right-angled iff a²+b²+c²=8R²*

> A triangle has sides of length $a$, $b$, $c$ and its circumcircle has radius $R$. Prove that the triangle is right-angled if and only if $a^2 + b^2 + c^2 = 8R^2$.
