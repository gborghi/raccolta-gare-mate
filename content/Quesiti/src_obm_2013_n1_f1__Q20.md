*Number of ways to tile a 24-cell grid with six identical 4-cell pieces*

> Luisa has six equal pieces formed by $4$ unit squares of area $1$. She wants to fit all these pieces into the grid formed by $24$ unit squares of area $1$ and has already placed one of these pieces, highlighted in the figure alongside, and the pieces can be placed in any orientation. In how many different ways can she finish her task?
> 
> (A) $2$ \quad (B) $3$ \quad (C) $4$ \quad (D) $5$ \quad (E) $6$
