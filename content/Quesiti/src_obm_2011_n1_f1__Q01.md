*Cell phone bill split among calls, internet, messaging*

> In May, the total amount of Esmeralda's cell phone bill was R\$119.76, without taxes. This amount corresponds to the following items: calls, internet access, and message sending. She spent R\$29.90 on internet access and R\$15.50 on the message sending service. How much did she spend on calls?
> 
> (A) R\$74.36 \quad (B) R\$74.46 \quad (C) R\$84.36 \quad (D) R\$89.86 \quad (E) R\$104.26
