*Functional equation f(f(x)f(y)) = f(x+y) over the reals*

> Let $\mathbb{R}$ be the set of real numbers. Determine all functions $f : \mathbb{R} \to \mathbb{R}$ such that, for all real numbers $x$ and $y$,
> $$f(f(x)f(y)) = f(x + y).$$
