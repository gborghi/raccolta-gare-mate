*In a convex pentagon ABCDE with given equal side and angle conditions, prove that BCDE is a parallelogram.*

> A convex pentagon $ABCDE$ is such that $BC = DE$, $\angle ABE = \angle CAB = \angle AED - 90^\circ$ and $\angle ACB = \angle ADE$. Prove that $BCDE$ is a parallelogram.
