*Rectangle ABCD, E = foot of perp from B to AC, circle through A and E centred on (AD), F on [CD]; prove BF bisects angle AFC*

> Let $ABCD$ be a rectangle such that $AB > BC$. Let $E$ be the orthogonal projection of $B$ onto $(AC)$, and $\Gamma$ the circle passing through $A$ and $E$ whose centre lies on $(AD)$. Let $F$ be the point of intersection of $\Gamma$ and $[CD]$. Prove that $(BF)$ is the bisector of $\widehat{AFC}$.
