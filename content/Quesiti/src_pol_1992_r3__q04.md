*Solve f_n(x)=2x for recursive square-root sequence*

> The sequence of functions $f_n : \mathbb{R} \to \mathbb{R}$ is defined by $f_0(x) = 8$ and
> $$f_{n+1}(x) = \sqrt{x^2 + 6f_n(x)} \quad \text{for all } x.$$
> For every integer $n \ge 0$, solve the equation $f_n(x) = 2x$.
