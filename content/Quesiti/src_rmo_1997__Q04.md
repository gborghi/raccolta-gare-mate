# Regional Mathematical Olympiad 1997 — Quesito 4

*Prove two inequalities in parallelogram ABCD with perpendicular diagonals*

> In a quadrilateral $ABCD$, it is given that $AB$ is parallel to $CD$ and the diagonals $AC$ and $BD$ are perpendicular to each other. Show that
> 
> (a) $AD \cdot BC \ge AB \cdot CD$;
> 
> (b) $AD + BC \ge AB + CD$.
