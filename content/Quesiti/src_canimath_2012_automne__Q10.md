# Coupe Animath Automne 2012 — Quesito 10

*Find angle ABC given incircle center I, AI=BC and angle ICA=2 angle IAC*

> Let $ABC$ be a triangle and $I$ the centre of its inscribed circle. Suppose that $AI = BC$ and that $\widehat{ICA} = 2\,\widehat{IAC}$. What is the value of $\widehat{ABC}$?
