*A sphere inscribed in pyramid SABCD (convex quad base) touches the base at P; prove angle APB + angle CPD = 180 degrees.*

> The base of a pyramid $SABCD$ is a convex quadrilateral $ABCD$. A sphere is inscribed in the pyramid and touches the base $ABCD$ at point $P$. Prove that $\angle APB + \angle CPD = 180^\circ$.
