*Sequence defined by recurrence divisible by r for all n*

> The sequence $(a_n)_{n \ge 0}$ is defined by $a_1 = a_2 = 1$ and $a_n = a_{n-1} \cdot a_{n-2} + a_{n-1} + a_{n-2}$ for $n \ge 3$. Prove that for any positive integer $r$ there is a positive integer $s$ such that $a_s$ is divisible by $r$.
