# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2021 — Nível 1 — Fase x — Quesito 3

*Two adjacent lots: perimeters, lawn area, equal-area passage*

> Pedro has two adjacent lots — one rectangular and one square — both facing a street. Each lot has an area of $400\,\text{m}^2$. He built a wall along the front of both lots, 36 m long in total (the thicker line in the figure).
> 
> a) What are the perimeters (i.e., the sums of the side lengths) of each lot?
> 
> b) Pedro made a lawn 2 m wide, running alongside the wall and the side of the square lot, as shown in the figure. What is the total area of this lawn?
> 
> c) Pedro wants to give the lots to his two sons, José and João. Since José does not want the lawn, but João wants a passage of width $x$ through José's lot, as indicated in the figure, leaving the rest of the lawn to João. Pedro will build a fence, calculating $x$ in such a way that the areas of the two lots remain equal. What should the value of $x$ be?
