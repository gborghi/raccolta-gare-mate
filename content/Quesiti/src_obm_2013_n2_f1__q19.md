*Recursive sequence mod 4 with count of multiples of 4; find 2013th term*

> Each term of a sequence is defined as the remainder when dividing by 4 the sum of the previous term and the number of multiples of 4 that appear among the sequence terms up to that point. Given that the first term is 0, what is the $2013^{\text{th}}$ term of the sequence?
> 
> (A) 0 \quad (B) 1 \quad (C) 2 \quad (D) 3 \quad (E) Impossible to determine.

*Sequenza ricursiva mod 4 con conteggio di moltipli di 4; troverà il termine 2013*

> 
> (A) 0 \quad (B) 1 \quad (C) 2 \quad (D) 3 \quad (E) Impossibile da determinare.

**Risposta:** A
