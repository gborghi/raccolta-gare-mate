*Find volume of piece containing vertex A when unit cube is cut by three mid-planes through center*

> A cube $ABCD$-$EFGH$ of side length $1$ has center $O$ (the intersection of its four space diagonals). The cube is cut by the following three planes, each passing through $O$:
> \begin{itemize}
> \item The plane through edges $BC$ and $AD$ (two opposite edges of a face) passing through $O$.
> \item The plane through edges $CA$ and $BD$ passing through $O$.
> \item The plane through edges $AB$ and $CD$ passing through $O$.
> \end{itemize}
> The cube is thereby divided into several solids. Find the volume of the solid that contains vertex $A$.
