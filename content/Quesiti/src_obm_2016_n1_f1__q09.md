*Rectangle of integer sides with perimeter 58 and square area; find side of square.*

> A rectangle has integer dimensions $x$ and $y$ and its area equals that of a square of side $n$. Find $n$.
> 
> [Source is the answer key; original statement/options not reproduced. From the solution: $2x+2y = 58$ so $x+y = 29$; the possible pairs $(x,y)$ are $(1,28),(2,27),(3,26),\dots,(14,15)$. Of these, only $(4,25)$ gives a product that is a perfect square, $4\cdot 25 = 100$, so $n = \sqrt{4\cdot 25} = \sqrt{100} = 10$.]
