*Functional equation: multisets {f(xf(y)+1),f(yf(x)-1)} equal {xf(f(y))-1,yf(f(x))+1}*

> Find all functions $f : \mathbb{R} \to \mathbb{R}$ such that for any real numbers $x$ and $y$, the following multisets are identical:
> $$\{f(xf(y) + 1),\, f(yf(x) - 1)\} = \{xf(f(y)) - 1,\, yf(f(x)) + 1\}.$$
> (Note: two multisets $\{a, b\}$ and $\{c, d\}$ are identical if and only if $a = c,\, b = d$, or $a = d,\, b = c$.)
