*Prove $2^{m-1} \equiv 1 \pmod{m}$ for Mersenne-type $m$*

> Let $m = 2^p - 1$, where $p$ is a prime number exceeding $3$. Prove that $2^{m-1}$ has remainder $1$ when divided by $m$.
