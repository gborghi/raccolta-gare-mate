*Solve a symmetric system of three cubic equations in real numbers x,y,z.*

> Solve in real numbers $x, y, z$ the system of equations
> $$\begin{cases} x^3 = 5y^3 - 4z \\ y^3 = 5z^3 - 4x \\ z^3 = 5x^3 - 4y \end{cases}$$
