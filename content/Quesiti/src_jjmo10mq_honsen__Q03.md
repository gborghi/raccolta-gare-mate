# Japan Junior Mathematical Olympiad 2012 — Honsen (Final) — Quesito 3

*Grid stamping operation; find minimum a,b given N initial coins*

> Let $a, b$ be positive integers. Consider an $a \times b$ grid of cells, with $N$ cells initially containing a coin. The following operation may be performed any number of times:
> 
> **Operation:** Choose any row; if in that row exactly $1$ cell does not have a coin, place a coin in that cell.
> 
> Find, in terms of $N$, the minimum value of $a$ (with appropriate $b$) such that, for some initial placement of $N$ coins, one can eventually place a coin in every cell by repeatedly applying the operation.
