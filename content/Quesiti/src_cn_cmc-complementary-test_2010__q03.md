*Inequality for averages of bounded positive reals*

> (50 marks) Given integer $n > 2$, suppose positive real numbers $a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_n$ satisfy $a_k \le 1$, $k = 1, 2, \ldots, n$. Let $A_k = \dfrac{a_1 + a_2 + \cdots + a_k}{k}$, $k = 1, 2, \ldots, n$. Prove $\left| \displaystyle\sum_{k=1}^{n} a_k - \sum_{k=1}^{n} A_k \right| < \dfrac{n-1}{2}$.
