*Real functions satisfying (f(x)+f(y))(f(u)+f(v))=f(xu-yv)+f(xv+yu)*

> Find all real-valued functions on the reals such that $(f(x) + f(y))(f(u) + f(v)) = f(xu - yv) + f(xv + yu)$ for all $x, y, u, v$.
