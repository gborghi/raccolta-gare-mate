# Japan EGMO Selection / Girls MO 2025 — Quesito 5

*Functional equation involving the floor function*

> Find all functions $f$ defined on the reals and taking real values such that for all real numbers $x, y$,
> $$f(x)\,f\big(f(x) + y\big) = f(x)^2 + [x][y]$$
> holds. Here, for a real number $r$, $[r]$ denotes the greatest integer less than or equal to $r$; for example, $[3.14] = 3$, $[5] = 5$, $[-2.71] = -3$.
