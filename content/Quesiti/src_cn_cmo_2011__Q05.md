*Find maximum of ratio of weighted sums for non-negative reals*

> Given integer $n \ge 4$. Find the maximum of
> $$\frac{\sum_{i=1}^{n} a_i(a_i + b_i)}{\sum_{i=1}^{n} b_i(a_i + b_i)}$$
> for non-negative real numbers $a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_n, b_1, b_2, \ldots, b_n$ satisfying
> $$a_1 + a_2 + \cdots + a_n = b_1 + b_2 + \cdots + b_n > 0.$$
