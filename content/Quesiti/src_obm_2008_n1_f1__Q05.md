# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2008 — Nível 1 — Fase 1 — Quesito 5

*People numbered 1–125; multiples of 3 listed; some names appear twice*

> At a neighborhood community meeting, each of the 125 people present received a different number, from 1 to 125. At that moment, a list was made of all people whose number is a multiple of 3, who were supposed to participate in a project. Some people complained that their name appeared twice on the list. How many people appeared twice on the list?
> 
> A) 2 $\quad$ B) 6 $\quad$ C) 20 $\quad$ D) 41 $\quad$ E) 62
