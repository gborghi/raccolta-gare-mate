*Real numbers summing to integer with bounded partial sums*

> The real numbers $x_1, x_2, \ldots, x_n$ from the interval $(0,1)$ satisfy the equality $x_1 + x_2 + \cdots + x_n = m + r$, where $m$ is an integer and $r \in [0,1)$. Prove that
> $$x_1^2 + x_2^2 + \cdots + x_n^2 \le m + r^2.$$
