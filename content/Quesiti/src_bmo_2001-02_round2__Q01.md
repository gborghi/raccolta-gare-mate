*Altitude foot perpendiculars form equal segment EF for any vertex*

> The altitude from a vertex of an acute-angled triangle $ABC$ meets the opposite side at $D$. From $D$ perpendiculars $DE$ and $DF$ are drawn to the other two sides. Prove that the length of $EF$ is the same whichever vertex is chosen.
