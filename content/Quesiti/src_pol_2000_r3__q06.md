*An odd-degree polynomial with P(x^2-1)=P(x)^2-1 must equal x*

> Suppose that $P(x)$ is a polynomial of an odd degree satisfying
> $$P(x^2 - 1) = P(x)^2 - 1 \quad \text{for all } x.$$
> Prove that $P(x) = x$ for all $x$.
