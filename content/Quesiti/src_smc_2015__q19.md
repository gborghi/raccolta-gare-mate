*Side lengths of smallest square in a configuration of three squares with bases on a line*

> Three squares are arranged as shown so that their bases lie on a straight line. Also, the corners $Q$ and $R$ lie on a straight line. The middle square has sides that are 1 cm longer than the sides of the smallest square. The largest square has sides of 50 cm. Which of the following are the possible values for the length (in cm) of the sides of the smallest square?
>
> - **(A)** $2, 32$
> - **(B)** $4, 42$
> - **(C)** $4, 34$
> - **(D)** $32, 40$
> - **(E)** $34, 42$
