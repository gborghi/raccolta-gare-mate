# British Mathematical Olympiad 2019-20 — Round 1 — Quesito 3

*Two tangent circles with common tangent; prove AC perpendicular to BD*

> Two circles $S_1$ and $S_2$ are tangent at $P$. A common tangent, not through $P$, touches $S_1$ at $A$ and $S_2$ at $B$. Points $C$ and $D$, on $S_1$ and $S_2$ respectively, are outside the triangle $APB$ and are such that $P$ is on the line $CD$.
> 
> Prove that $AC$ is perpendicular to $BD$.
