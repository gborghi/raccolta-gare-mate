*Polynomial sequence from recurrence; prove negative value for all n, then count such integers*

> Consider the degree-$2$ polynomial $P_1(x) = 4x^2 + 12x - 3015$. Define the sequence of polynomials by $P_{n+1}(x) = \frac{P_n(x)^2}{2016}$ for every integer $n \ge 1$.
> 
> (a) Prove that there exists a real number $r$ such that $P_n(r) < 0$ for every positive integer $n$.
> 
> (b) Determine the number of integers $m$ such that $P_n(m) < 0$ for infinitely many positive integers $n$.
