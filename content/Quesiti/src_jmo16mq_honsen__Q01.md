# Japan Mathematical Olympiad 2006 — Honsen (Final) — Quesito 1

*Circle geometry: prove two arc-midpoint chords are equal*

> Let $O$ be a circle, and let five points $A, M, B, C, D$ lie on $O$ in this order. Suppose the arc $MA$ and the arc $MB$ are bisected (i.e., their midpoints are taken). Let $P$ and $Q$ be the intersections of line $AC$ with line $MD$ and of line $BD$ with line $MC$, respectively, and let $X$ and $Y$ be the two intersection points of line $PQ$ with circle $O$. Prove that $MX = MY$.
