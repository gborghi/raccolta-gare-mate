*Ellipse endpoints and foci dot-product condition, find ratio*

> (Paper A, Part I Q2) In ellipse $\Gamma$, $A$ is an endpoint of the major axis, $B$ is an endpoint of the minor axis, and $F_1$, $F_2$ are the foci. If $\overrightarrow{AF_1} \cdot \overrightarrow{AF_2} + \overrightarrow{BF_1} \cdot \overrightarrow{BF_2} = 0$, then the value of $\dfrac{|AB|}{|F_1F_2|}$ is $\underline{\qquad}$.
