*3-smooth greatest divisor; find all pairs (m,n) with n·f(m)=m·f(n)*

> For each positive integer $n$, let $f(n)$ be the greatest $a$ such that $a \mid n$ and $a$ has no prime factor greater than $3$. For example, $f(6) = 6$, $f(11) = 1$, $f(12) = 12$, $f(30) = 6$.
> 
> Determine all pairs $(m, n)$ of positive integers which satisfy the equation
> $$n \cdot f(m) = m \cdot f(n).$$
