*Max value of n-m for 3-digit number with digit sum condition*

> Let $n$ be a positive integer that is not a multiple of $10$ and whose hundreds digit is not $0$. When the hundreds digit and the units digit of $n$ are swapped, let the resulting integer be $m$. Find the maximum value of $n - m$.
