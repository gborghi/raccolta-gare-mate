# Coupe Animath Automne 2012 — Quesito 11

*Round table 2013 people: tall vs short, two sub-questions*

> Around the same table sit $2013$ people who all have different heights. We say that a person is *grande* (tall) if she is taller than her two neighbours, and *petite* (short) if she is shorter than her two neighbours.
> 
> a) Prove that, whatever the arrangement chosen around the table, there are as many tall people as short people.
> 
> b) Which are the integers $n$ for which one can find an arrangement of the people containing exactly $n$ tall people?
