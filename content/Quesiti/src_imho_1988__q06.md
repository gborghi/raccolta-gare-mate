*ab+1 divides a²+b², quotient is a perfect square*

> Let $a$ and $b$ be positive integers such that $ab + 1$ divides $a^2 + b^2$. Show that
> $$\frac{a^2 + b^2}{ab + 1}$$
> is the square of an integer.
