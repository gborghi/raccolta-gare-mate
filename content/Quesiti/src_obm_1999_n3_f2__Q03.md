*Largest n for rearrangement of (3,6,9,12) making an expression an integer*

> Determine the largest integer $n$ for which there exists a rearrangement $(a, b, c, d)$ of $(3, 6, 9, 12)$ such that
> $$\sqrt[4]{a\sqrt[3]{b\sqrt{c^{\,d}}}}$$
> is an integer. Justify your answer.
