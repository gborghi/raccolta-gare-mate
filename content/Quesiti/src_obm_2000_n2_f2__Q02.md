*Count distinct rectangular boxes built from 216 unit cubes*

> In how many different ways can we construct a rectangular box (parallelepiped) using exactly 216 cubic blocks of dimensions $1 \times 1 \times 1$?
> 
> **Note:** Boxes of dimensions $2 \times 3 \times 36$ and $2 \times 36 \times 3$ must be considered the same.
