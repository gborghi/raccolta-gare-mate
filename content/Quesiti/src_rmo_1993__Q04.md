*Inequality for inscribed circle radii in rectangle*

> Let $ABCD$ be a rectangle with $AB = a$ and $BC = b$. Suppose $r_1$ is the radius of the circle passing through $A$ and $B$ and touching $CD$; and similarly $r_2$ is the radius of the circle passing through $B$ and $C$ and touching $AD$. Show that
> $$r_1 + r_2 \ge \frac{5}{8}(a + b).$$
