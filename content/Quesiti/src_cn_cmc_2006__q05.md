*Condition on a+b>=0 for f(a)+f(b)>=0 with odd function*

> Suppose $f(x) = x + \log_x\!\left(\sqrt{x^2 + 1}\right)$. For any $a, b \in \mathbf{R}$, to satisfy $f(a) + f(b) \ge 0$, the condition $a + b \ge 0$ is (\ \ ).
> 
> (A) necessary and sufficient\quad (B) not necessary but sufficient\quad (C) necessary but not sufficient\quad (D) neither necessary nor sufficient
