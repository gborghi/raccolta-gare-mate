*Colombian configuration of 4027 points; find least k lines*

> A configuration of $4027$ points in the plane is called Colombian if it consists of $2013$ red points and $2014$ blue points, and no three of the points are collinear. By drawing some lines, the plane is divided into several regions. An arrangement of lines is good for a Colombian configuration if the following two conditions are satisfied:
> \begin{itemize}
> \item no line passes through any point of the configuration;
> \item no region contains points of both colours.
> \end{itemize}
> Find the least value of $k$ such that for any Colombian configuration of $4027$ points, there is a good arrangement of $k$ lines.
