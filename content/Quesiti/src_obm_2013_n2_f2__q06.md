*Pattern in two-column table: find 10th row and sum on row 21*

> In the table shown, starting from the second row, the number written in column $X$ is equal to the product of the numbers in the previous row, and the number written in column $Y$ is equal to the quotient of the $X$ value divided by the $Y$ value of the previous row.
> 
> $$\begin{array}{c|c|c}
> \text{Row} & X & Y \\
> \hline
> 1^\circ & & 1 \\
> 2^\circ & 2 & 2 \\
> 3^\circ & 4 & 1 \\
> \hline
> \cdots & \cdots & \cdots
> \end{array}$$
> 
> a) What number appears in column $X$ on the tenth row?
> 
> b) What is the sum of the numbers that appear on row $21$?
