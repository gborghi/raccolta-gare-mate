*Find max and min of a symmetric ratio expression in non-negative reals*

> Let $m, n \in \mathbb{N}^*$, $m, n > 1$, $a_{ij}$ ($i = 1, 2, \cdots, n$, $j = 1, 2, \cdots, m$) be non-negative real numbers (not all zero). Find the maximum and minimum values of
> $$f = \frac{n\sum_{i=1}^{n}\left(\sum_{j=1}^{m} a_{ij}\right)^2 + m\sum_{j=1}^{m}\left(\sum_{i=1}^{n} a_{ij}\right)^2}{\left(\sum_{i=1}^{n}\sum_{j=1}^{m} a_{ij}\right)^2 + mn\sum_{i=1}^{n}\sum_{j=1}^{m} a_{ij}^2}.$$
