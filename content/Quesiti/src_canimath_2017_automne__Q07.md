# Coupe Animath Automne 2017 — Quesito 7

*2012 numbers (±1) on a circle, no 10 consecutive sum to 0; find possible values of total sum S*

> On a circle, one writes $2012$ numbers. Each of them is equal to $1$ or $-1$. Let $S$ be their sum. Suppose that there do not exist $10$ consecutive numbers on the circle whose sum is $0$. What values can $S$ take under this condition?
