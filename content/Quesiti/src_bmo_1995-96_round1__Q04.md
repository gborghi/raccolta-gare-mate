# British Mathematical Olympiad 1995-96 — Round 1 — Quesito 4

*Find all positive integers n where g(n) ≤ g(n+1) for g(x)=1/(x(x+1))*

> Let $g$ be a function defined on the positive real numbers by
> $$g(x) = \frac{1}{x(x+1)}.$$
> Determine all positive integers for which $g(n) \le g(n+1)$.
