*Minimize a/b+b/c+c/d+d/a under (a+c)(b+d)=ac+bd*

> Let $a, b, c, d$ be real numbers satisfying $(a+c)(b+d) = ac + bd$. Find the minimum value of
> $$\frac{a}{b} + \frac{b}{c} + \frac{c}{d} + \frac{d}{a}.$$
