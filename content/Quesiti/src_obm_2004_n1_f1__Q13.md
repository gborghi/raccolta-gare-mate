*When must a craftsman's assistant stop to match output?*

> A craftsman starts working at $8$ h and produces $6$ bracelets every $20$ minutes; his assistant starts working $1$ hour later and produces $8$ bracelets of the same type every $1$ hour. The craftsman will work until $12$ h and has told his assistant how many hours he should work to produce the same number as the craftsman. At what time should the assistant stop?
> 
> (A) $12$ h \quad (B) $12{:}30$ min \quad (C) $13$ h \quad (D) $13{:}30$ min \quad (E) $14{:}30$ min
