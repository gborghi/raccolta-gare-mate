*Squared numbers: digit sum is a perfect square*

> We say that a positive integer is *quadradoso* (squarish) when the sum of its digits is a perfect square. For example, the numbers 211 and 10 are squarish, because $2+1+1=2^2$ and $1+0=1^2$.
> 
> a) What is the smallest squarish number greater than 2021?
> 
> b) What is the largest squarish number with no repeated digits?
