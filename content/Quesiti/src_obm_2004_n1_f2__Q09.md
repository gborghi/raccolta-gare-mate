*Digit-count toy: iterated row description gives sequence; find digits on line 10 and 2006*

> Esmeralda invented a toy. She typed some numbers in the first line of a sheet. Then, the description of the numbers typed appears on the second line: she presented the quantities of each number that appeared, in increasing order of the number. For example, after typing 21035662112, she typed $1\,0\;1\,1\;3\,2\;1\,5\;2\,6$, meaning: one 0, one 1, three 2s, one 5, two 6s.
> 
> (a) She started a new sheet with com 1. So, her description is typed on the second line. Then, she described the second line on the third line, and so on. What is the digit typed on the $10^{\text{th}}$ line of the first sheet?
> 
> (b) Esmeralda liked this and decided to repeat the process on several sheets, starting with 01 on the first line of the first sheet. Which are the first two digits from the left that she typed on the $2006^{\text{th}}$ line?
