# Regional Mathematical Olympiad 2013 — Region 2 — Quesito 5

*Polygon side-perimeter sum inequality*

> Let $n \ge 3$ be a natural number and let $P$ be a polygon with $n$ sides. Let $a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_n$ be the lengths of the sides of $P$ and let $p$ be its perimeter. Prove that
> $$\frac{a_1}{p - a_1} + \frac{a_2}{p - a_2} + \cdots + \frac{a_n}{p - a_n} < 2.$$
