*Count three-element subsets of {1,...,65} with a+b+c<95*

> Show that the number of three-element subsets $\{a,b,c\}$ of $\{1,2,\ldots,65\}$ with $a+b+c < 95$ is less than the number of those with $a+b+c > 95$.
