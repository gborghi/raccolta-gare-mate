*Equilateral triangle of side 2√3 covers at least 16 of 1000 points in unit square*

> Inside a square of side $16$ are placed $1000$ points. Show that it is possible to place an equilateral triangle of side $2\sqrt{3}$ in the plane so that it covers at least $16$ of these points.
