*Real area of a bedroom from a 1:45 scale drawing*

> The measurements indicated in the figure refer to the drawing that represents a rectangular bedroom, including a bathroom, of a house. If the scale of the drawing is $1{:}45$, what is the real area of this room? (The drawing measures $10$ cm by $6$ cm with a bathroom region cut out of one corner.)
> 
> (A) $12.15$ m$^2$ \quad (B) $15.5$ m$^2$ \quad (C) $27$ m$^2$ \quad (D) $32$ m$^2$ \quad (E) $60$ m$^2$

> (A) $12.15$ m$^2$ \quad (B) $15.5$ m$^2$ \quad (C) $27$ m$^2$ \quad (D) $32$ m$^2$ \quad (E) $60$ m$^2$
