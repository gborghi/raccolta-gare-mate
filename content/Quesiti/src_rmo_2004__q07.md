*Positive reals satisfying y³+y ≤ x−x³; prove y<x<1 and x²+y²<1*

> Let $x$ and $y$ be positive real numbers such that $y^3 + y \le x - x^3$. Prove that
> 
> (a) $y < x < 1$; and
> 
> (b) $x^2 + y^2 < 1$.
