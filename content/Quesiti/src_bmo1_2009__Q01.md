*Count zig-zag paths on a standard 8x8 chessboard*

> Consider a standard $8 \times 8$ chessboard consisting of 64 small squares coloured in the usual pattern, so 32 are black and 32 are white. A zig-zag path across the board is a collection of eight white squares, one in each row, which meet at their corners. How many zig-zag paths are there?
