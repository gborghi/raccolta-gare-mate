*Minimize L-trominoes tiling a grid with L and S tiles*

> On a $2020 \times 2021$ grid, several tiles of the two kinds $L$ and $S$ shown in the figure are placed along the cells so that they do not overlap and the whole grid is covered. Find the smallest possible value of the number of $L$ tiles used. The tiles may be rotated and reflected.
> 
> Here $L$ is an L-shaped tromino (three unit cells) and $S$ is an S-shaped tetromino (four unit cells).
