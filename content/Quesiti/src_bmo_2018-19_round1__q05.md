*Find all possible dimensions of two similar cylinders with height sum 1 and volume sum 8pi*

> Two solid cylinders are mathematically similar. The sum of their heights is $1$. The sum of their volumes is $8\pi$. Find all possibilities for the dimensions of each cylinder.
