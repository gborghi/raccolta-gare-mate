*Prove an inequality for positive reals x,y,u,v.*

> If $x,y,u,v$ are positive real numbers, prove the inequality
> $$\frac{xu+xv+yu+yv}{x+y+u+v} \ge \frac{xy}{x+y}+\frac{uv}{u+v}.$$
