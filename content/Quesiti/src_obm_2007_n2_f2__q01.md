*Find Ludmilson's age x given (x-55)(x+55) is a prime cube.*

> Let $x$ be Ludmilson's age. It is known that $(x-55)(x+55)=p^3$, where $p$ is a prime number. Determine Ludmilson's age. [Source is the official solutions sheet (gabarito); statement reconstructed from the solution. The solution factors into the cases $\{x-55=1,\ x+55=p^3\}$ and $\{x-55=p,\ x+55=p^2\}$, giving $p^2-p=110$, $p=11$, $x=66$.]
