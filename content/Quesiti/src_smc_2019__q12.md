*Area of a rhombus in which a unit circle touches all four sides and the smaller angles are 60°*

> A circle with radius $1$ touches the sides of a rhombus, as shown. Each of the smaller angles between the sides of the rhombus is $60^\circ$.
> 
> What is the area of the rhombus?
>
> - **(A)** $6$
> - **(B)** $4$
> - **(C)** $2\sqrt{3}$
> - **(D)** $3\sqrt{3}$
> - **(E)** $\frac{8\sqrt{3}}{3}$
