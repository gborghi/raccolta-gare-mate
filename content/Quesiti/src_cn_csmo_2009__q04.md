*Minimum triangles covering all chords among 12 red circle points*

> There are given 12 red points on a circle. Find the minimum of $n$, such that there exist $n$ triangles, whose vertices are red points, satisfying every chord with red endpoints being a side of one triangle. (Posed by Tao Pingsheng)
