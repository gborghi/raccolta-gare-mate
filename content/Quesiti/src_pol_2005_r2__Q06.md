*For real a,b,c in [0,1], prove a/(bc+1)+b/(ca+1)+c/(ab+1) <= 2.*

> Prove that if real numbers $a, b, c$ lie in the interval $[0,1]$, then
> $$\frac{a}{bc+1} + \frac{b}{ca+1} + \frac{c}{ab+1} \le 2.$$
