*Find all functions on rationals with two functional equations*

> Find all functions $f : \mathbb{Q}^+ \to \mathbb{Q}^+$ such that for all $x \in \mathbb{Q}^+$,
> $$f(x+1) = f(x) + 1 \quad \text{and} \quad f(x^3) = f(x)^3.$$
