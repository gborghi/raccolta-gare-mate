*Set of positive integers with 2002 elements, no element is a perfect power*

> Show that there exists a set $A$ formed by positive integers with the following properties:
> 
> a) $A$ has 2002 elements.
> 
> b) The sum of any quantity of distinct elements of $A$ (at least one) is never a perfect power.
> 
> **Obs:** A perfect power is a number of the form $a^b$, where $a$ and $b$ are positive integers and $b \ge 2$.
