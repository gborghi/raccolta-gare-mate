*Inequality for positive reals: sum of fractions ge 3/2*

> For all positive real numbers $a, b, c$ prove that
> $$\frac{a}{b+c} + \frac{b}{c+a} + \frac{c}{a+b} \ge \frac{3}{2}.$$
