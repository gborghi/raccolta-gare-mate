*Count pairs with sqrt(2)n < m, given count for m<sqrt(2)n<2m*

> It is known that the number of pairs $(m, n)$ of positive integers at most $100$ satisfying $m < \sqrt{2}\,n < 2m$ is exactly $2958$. How many pairs $(m, n)$ of positive integers at most $100$ satisfy $\sqrt{2}\,n < m$?
