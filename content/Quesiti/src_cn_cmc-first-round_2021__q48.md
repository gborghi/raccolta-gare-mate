*B1-4: parabola y^2=2px, line of inclination π/4 meets Γ at P(3,2) and Q; area of triangle OPQ.*

> [Test Paper B1, Problem 4] In a plane rectangular coordinate system $xOy$, given parabola $\Gamma:y^2=2px\ (p>0)$, a line with inclination angle $\dfrac{\pi}{4}$ intersects $\Gamma$ at point $P(3,2)$ and another point $Q$. Then the area of $\triangle OPQ$ is ______.

**Area:** [[Geometria]]
**Risposta:** $\dfrac{4}{3}$
