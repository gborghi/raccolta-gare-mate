*Right triangle median angle bound: sin theta ≤ 1/√5*

> In triangle $ABC$, $B$ is a right-angle and $\theta$ is the angle between $AC$ and the median from $C$ to $AB$.
> 
> Prove that $\sin\theta \le \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{5}}$.
