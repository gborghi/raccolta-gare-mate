*Find all real triples satisfying three symmetric equations*

> Find all triples $(x, y, z)$ of real numbers satisfying the equations
> $$x^2 + 2yz = 4, \quad y^2 + 2zx = 4, \quad z^2 + 2xy = 1.$$
