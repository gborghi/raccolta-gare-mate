*Find range of a so that f(x+a)>=2f(x) for all x in [a,a+2], f odd*

> Let $f$ be an odd function on $\mathbf{R}$, and $f(x) = x^2$ for $x > 0$. Suppose for any $x \in [a, a + 2]$, $f(x + a) \ge 2f(x)$. Then the range of the real number $a$ is __________.

*Ricerca l'intervallo di a in modo che f(x+a)>=2f(x) per tutte le x in [a,a+2], f odd*
