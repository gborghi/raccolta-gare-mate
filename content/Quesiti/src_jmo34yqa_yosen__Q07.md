# Japan Mathematical Olympiad 2024 — Yosen (Preliminary) — Quesito 7

*Count pairs (p,a) with p prime >= 3 and ap^4+2p^3+2p^2+1 a perfect square*

> Find the number of pairs $(p, a)$ where $p$ is a prime with $p \ge 3$ and $a$ is an integer with $1 \le a \le 2024$, satisfying $a < p^4$ and $ap^4 + 2p^3 + 2p^2 + 1$ is a perfect square.
