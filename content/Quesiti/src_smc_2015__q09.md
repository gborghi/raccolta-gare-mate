*Four straight lines drawn on paper; count of points where two or more lines intersect*

> Four different straight lines are drawn on a flat piece of paper. The number of points where two or more lines intersect is counted. Which of the following could NOT be the number of such points?
>
> - **(A)** $1$
> - **(B)** $2$
> - **(C)** $3$
> - **(D)** $4$
> - **(E)** $5$
