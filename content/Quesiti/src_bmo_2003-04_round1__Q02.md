# British Mathematical Olympiad 2003-04 — Round 1 — Quesito 2

*Rectangle with midpoint and perpendicular; prove triangle isosceles*

> $ABCD$ is a rectangle, $P$ is the midpoint of $AB$, and $Q$ is the point on $PD$ such that $CQ$ is perpendicular to $PD$.
> Prove that the triangle $BQC$ is isosceles.
