*Triangle inequality: a^2+b^2+c^2 > sqrt(3) max{|a^2-b^2|,|b^2-c^2|,|c^2-a^2|}*

> Show that for any triangle $ABC$, the following inequality is true:
> $$a^2 + b^2 + c^2 > \sqrt{3} \max\{|a^2 - b^2|,\, |b^2 - c^2|,\, |c^2 - a^2|\},$$
> where $a$, $b$, $c$ are, as usual, the sides of the triangle.
