*Ways to tile a 4x4 board with one 2x2 square and four L-trominoes*

> In how many ways can we cover a $4 \times 4$ board with one square of side $2$ and four identical pieces of $L$ format, each occupying three little squares of the board? The board cannot be rotated, that is, the possibilities shown below must be considered distinct.
> 
> A) 2 \quad B) 3 \quad C) 4 \quad D) 5 \quad E) 6
