*Existence of an integer n>2003 so that binomial coefficients a_k=C(n,k) have a divisibility property.*

> Show that there exists a positive integer $n > 2003$ for which the sequence $a_k = \binom{n}{k}$, $k = 0, 1, \ldots, 2003$, has the following property: $a_k$ divides $a_m$ whenever $0 \le k \le m \le 2003$.
