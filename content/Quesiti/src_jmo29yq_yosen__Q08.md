*Triangle AB>AC, points at 1:8 on sides, incenter forms unit equilateral; find AB*

> Let $ABC$ be a triangle with $AB > AC$, and let $I$ be its incenter. Let $D$ and $E$ be the points that divide the sides $AB$ and $AC$ respectively in the ratio $1:8$ (so $AD:DB = AE:EC = 1:8$). Given that triangle $DIE$ is an equilateral triangle of side length $1$, find the length of the segment $AB$. Here, $XY$ denotes the length of the segment $XY$.
