*Nonnegative a,b,c and p,q,r summing to 1 with p,q,r <= 1/2; prove 8abc <= pa+qb+rc*

> Let $a, b, c$ and $p, q, r$ be nonnegative numbers satisfying $a + b + c = p + q + r = 1$ and $p, q, r \le 1/2$. Prove that $$8abc \le pa + qb + rc$$ and find when equality occurs.
