*Count good positive integers k that partition {1,...,20} into subsets each summing to k*

> A positive integer $k$ is said to be \textit{good} if there exists a partition of $\{1, 2, 3, \ldots, 20\}$ in to disjoint proper subsets such that the sum of the numbers in each subset of the partition is $k$. How many \textit{good} numbers are there?
