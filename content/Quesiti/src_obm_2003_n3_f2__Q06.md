# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2003 — Nível 3 — Fase 2 — Quesito 6

*Closed form for a telescoping sum involving powers of 3*

> Compute the sum
> $$\sum_{i=0}^{n} \frac{2^{i+1}}{3^{2^i}+1} = \frac{2}{3^1+1} + \frac{2^2}{3^2+1} + \frac{2^3}{3^{2^2}+1} + \cdots + \frac{2^{n+1}}{3^{2^n}+1}.$$
