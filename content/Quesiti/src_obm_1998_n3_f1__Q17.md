*Two radios sold at equal prices, one with 25% profit and one with 25% loss; find the net result on invested capital.*

> I sold two radios at equal prices. On one of them I made a profit of $25\%$ over the purchase price, and on the other a loss of $25\%$. With respect to the invested capital:
> 
> A) I had neither profit nor loss
> 
> B) I made a profit of $6{,}25\%$
> 
> C) I made a profit of $16\%$
> 
> D) I had a loss of $6{,}25\%$
> 
> E) I had a loss of $16\%$
