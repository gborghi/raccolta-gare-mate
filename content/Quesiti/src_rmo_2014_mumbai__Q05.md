# Regional Mathematical Olympiad 2014 — Quesito 5

*Prove (1+a^2)(1+b^2)(1+c^2) >= 125 given reciprocal sum at most 1*

> Let $a, b, c$ be positive numbers such that
> $$\frac{1}{1+a} + \frac{1}{1+b} + \frac{1}{1+c} \le 1.$$
> Prove that $(1 + a^2)(1 + b^2)(1 + c^2) \ge 125$. When does the equality hold?
