*Side length of equilateral triangle with interior point and given foot distances*

> Let $P$ be a point inside an equilateral triangle. The lengths of the perpendiculars dropped from $P$ to each side are $1$, $2$, and $3$ respectively. Find the side length of this equilateral triangle.
