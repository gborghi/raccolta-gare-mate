*Triangle side lengths a,b,c with ab+bc+ca=1; prove (a+1)(b+1)(c+1)<4.*

> Let $a$, $b$ and $c$ be the lengths of the sides of a triangle. Suppose that $ab + bc + ca = 1$. Show that $(a+1)(b+1)(c+1) < 4$.
