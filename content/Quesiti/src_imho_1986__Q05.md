*Find all functions on nonneg reals satisfying functional equation*

> Find all functions $f$, defined on the non-negative real numbers and taking non-negative real values, such that:
> \begin{itemize}
> \item[(i)] $f(xf(y))f(y) = f(x+y)$ for all $x, y \ge 0$,
> \item[(ii)] $f(2) = 0$,
> \item[(iii)] $f(x) \ne 0$ for $0 \le x < 2$.
> \end{itemize}
