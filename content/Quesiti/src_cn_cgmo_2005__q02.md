*Find all real triples satisfying a system with coefficients 12 and 13*

> Find all triples $(x, y, z)$ of real numbers such that $$\left\{\begin{array}{l} \left(x + \tfrac{1}{2}\right)^2 = 12\left(y + \tfrac{1}{2}\right),\\[4pt] \left(y + \tfrac{1}{2}\right)^2 = 13\left(z + \tfrac{1}{2}\right),\\[4pt] xy + yz + zx = 1. \end{array}\right.$$
