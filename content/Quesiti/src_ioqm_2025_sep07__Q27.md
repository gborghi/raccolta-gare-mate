# Indian Olympiad Qualifier in Mathematics (IOQM) 2025 — Sep 07 — Quesito 27

*Count ordered triples (a,b,c) with 1≤a,b,c≤50 satisfying (lcm(a,c)+lcm(b,c))/(a+b)=26c/27*

> Find the number of ordered triples $(a, b, c)$ of positive integers such that $1 \le a, b, c \le 50$ which satisfy the relation
> $$\frac{\text{lcm}(a, c) + \text{lcm}(b, c)}{a + b} = \frac{26c}{27}.$$
> Here, by $\text{lcm}(x, y)$ we mean the LCM, that is, least common multiple of $x$ and $y$.
