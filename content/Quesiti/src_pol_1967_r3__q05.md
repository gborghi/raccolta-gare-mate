*Five points ABCDE in space with equal sides and angles; prove coplanar*

> Points $A, B, C, D, E$ in space have the property that
> $$AB = BC = CD = DE = EA,$$
> $$\angle ABC = \angle BCD = \angle CDE = \angle DEA = \angle EAB.$$
> Prove that the points $A, B, C, D, E$ lie on a plane.
