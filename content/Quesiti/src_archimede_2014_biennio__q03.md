> (E) dipende dall’ora a cui pranzano a Topolinia.

What time does he get back to Paperopoli if he leaves two hours later? (A) 12 noon; (B) 2 pm; (C) 3 pm; (D) 4 pm; (E) depends on the time of lunch in Topolinia.

**Answer:** B
