# Japan Junior Mathematical Olympiad 2024 — Honsen (Final) — Quesito 2

*Reflection of midpoint over line DN lies inside triangle, AM' perp BC*

> In triangle $ABC$ with $AB < AC$, let $M$ be the midpoint of side $BC$. Let $N$ be the midpoint of the arc $BC$ not containing $A$ on the circumcircle of triangle $ABC$. Let $D$ be the intersection of the angle bisector of $\angle BAC$ with side $BC$. Let $M'$ be the reflection of $M$ over line $DN$. Prove that $M'$ lies in the interior of triangle $ABC$ (not on its boundary), and that line $AM'$ is perpendicular to line $BC$. Here, $XY$ denotes the length of segment $XY$.
