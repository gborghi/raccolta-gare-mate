*Prove inequality for positive reals a, b, c*

> Let $a, b, c$ be positive real numbers. Prove that
> $$\left(\frac{a}{b} + \frac{b}{c} + \frac{c}{a}\right)^2 \ge (a + b + c)\left(\frac{1}{a} + \frac{1}{b} + \frac{1}{c}\right).$$
