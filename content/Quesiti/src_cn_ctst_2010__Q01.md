*Circumcenters in acute triangle; AO bisects O1O2*

> For acute triangle $ABC$ with $AB > AC$, let $M$ be the midpoint of side $BC$ and $P$ a point inside $\triangle AMC$ such that $\angle MAB = \angle PAC$. Let $O_1$, $O_2$, $O_3$ be the circumcenters of $\triangle ABC$, $\triangle ABP$, $\triangle ACP$ respectively. Prove that line $AO_1$ bisects segment $O_2O_3$.
