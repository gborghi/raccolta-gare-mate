# Regional Mathematical Olympiad 2014 — Region 1 — Quesito 6

*Positive reals summing to 1; find smallest K for sum inequality*

> Let $x_1, x_2, \ldots, x_{2014}$ be positive real numbers such that $\sum_{j=1}^{2014} x_j = 1$. Determine with proof the smallest constant $K$ such that
> $$K \sum_{j=1}^{2014} \frac{x_j^2}{1 - x_j} \ge 1.$$
