*Minimum sum of six pairwise-adjacent sums on a hexagon labelled 1-6*

> Rosa wrote the numbers from $1$ to $6$ at the vertices of the hexagon below, one at each, then added the numbers at each pair of adjacent vertices and wrote each result next to the corresponding side. The numbers written next to the sides were all different. What is the minimum value of the sum of these six obtained numbers?
> 
> (A) $69$ \quad (B) $58$ \quad (C) $59$ \quad (D) $61$ \quad (E) $57$
