*Units digit of a power tower 11^(12^13)*

> Find the units digit of $11^{12^{13}}$. Here $11^{12^{13}}$ denotes $11$ raised to the power $12^{13}$ (not $(11^{12}) \cdot 13$), and $11^{12}$ denotes $11$ raised to the power $12^{13}$, not the $13$th multiple of $11^{12}$.
