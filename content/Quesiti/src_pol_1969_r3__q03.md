*Natural numbers with qr-ps=1 and p/q<a/b<r/s imply b≥q+s*

> Show that if natural numbers $a, b, p, q, r, s$ satisfy the conditions
> $$qr - ps = 1 \quad \text{and} \quad \frac{p}{q} < \frac{a}{b} < \frac{r}{s},$$
> then $b \ge q + s$.
