*Prove two inequalities for positive reals a, b, c*

> $a$, $b$, $c$ are positive numbers. Prove that
> 
> (i) $a^3 + b^3 + c^3 \ge a^2b + b^2c + c^2a$,
> 
> (ii) $abc \ge (a+b-c)(b+c-a)(c+a-b)$.
