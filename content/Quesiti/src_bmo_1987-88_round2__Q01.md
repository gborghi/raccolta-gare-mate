# British Mathematical Olympiad 1987-88 — Round 2 — Quesito 1

*Equilateral triangle with inscribed circles; prove ratio AP/AC = 4/5 and find AQ/AC*

> $ABC$ is an equilateral triangle. The circle $\Gamma_1$ has centre $A$ and radius $AB$. $\Gamma_2$ is the circle on $AC$ as diameter. The circle with centre $P$ on $AC$ touches $\Gamma_1$ internally at $C$ and $\Gamma_2$ externally at $Q$.
> 
> Show that $AP/AC = 4/5$ and calculate the ratio $AQ/AC$.
