# Japan Junior Mathematical Olympiad 2026 — Yosen (Preliminary) — Quesito 9

*Count permutations of 1..30 where every index has at least 2 indices giving an inversion*

> How many permutations $a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_{30}$ of $1, 2, \ldots, 30$ satisfy the following condition?
> 
> For every integer $i$ with $1 \le i \le 30$, there exist at least $2$ integers $j$ with $1 \le j \le 30$ such that $(i - j)(a_i - a_j) < 0$.
