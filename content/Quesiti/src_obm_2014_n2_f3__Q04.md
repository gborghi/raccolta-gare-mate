# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2014 — Nível 2 — Fase 3 — Quesito 4

*Square, equal segments on sides, area product identity*

> Let $ABCD$ be a square with center $O$. Let $E$, $F$, $G$, $H$ be interior points on sides $AB$, $BC$, $CD$, $DA$, respectively, such that $AE = BF = CG = DH$. Suppose that line $OA$ meets $HE$ at point $X$, line $OB$ meets $EF$ at point $Y$, line $OC$ meets $FG$ at point $Z$, and line $OD$ meets $GH$ at point $W$. Given that $\text{Area}(EFGH) = 1$, find
> $$\left(\text{Area}\,ABCD\right) \times \left(\text{Area}\,XYZW\right).$$
