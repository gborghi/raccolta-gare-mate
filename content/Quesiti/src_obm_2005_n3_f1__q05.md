*Find digit n: a multiple of 7 flanked by 2004 and a number of 2005 digits*

> Esmeralda correctly typed a multiple of $7$ greater than one billion, with $4010$ digits. To its left she wrote the number $2004$ and to its right, $1$, obtaining new numbers $2004\ldots1$ with a digit $n$, and $2005$ digits. What is the value of $n$?
> (A) $3$ (B) $4$ (C) $5$ (D) $6$ (E) $7$
