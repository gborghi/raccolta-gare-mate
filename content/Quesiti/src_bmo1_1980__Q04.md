*Find initial values making the recurrence a_{n+1}=2^n-3a_n strictly increasing*

> Find the set of real numbers $a_n$ for which the infinite sequence $\{a_n\}$ of real numbers defined by
> $$a_{n+1} = 2^n - 3a_n \quad (n \ge 0)$$
> is strictly increasing, i.e.
> $$a_n < a_{n+1} \quad (n \ge 0).$$
