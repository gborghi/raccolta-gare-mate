# British Mathematical Olympiad 2010-11 — Round 2 — Quesito 1

*Show equal angles from interior point using cevian construction*

> Let $ABC$ be a triangle and $X$ be a point inside the triangle. The lines $AX$, $BX$ and $CX$ meet the opposite sides at $P$, $Q$ and $R$ respectively. Choose a point $U$ on $XP$ which is between $X$ and $P$. The line through $B$ and $U$ meets $XC$ at $V$ and the line through $C$ and $U$ meets $XB$ at $W$. Show that $\angle WXP = \angle VXP$.
