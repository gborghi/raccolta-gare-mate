*Two-player game: alternately pick integer greater than double previous; who reaches 2004?*

> In a game for two players, Arnaldo and Bernaldo alternately choose a positive integer. At each turn, the number chosen must be greater than double the last number chosen. The winner is the first player to choose the number $2004$.
> 
> In this game, Arnaldo goes first and begins with the number $2$. Which of the two players has a winning strategy — that is, can guarantee choosing the number $2004$ regardless of the opponent's moves?
