*Time the assistant stops so as to match the artisan's bracelet output*

The artisan stops working at 12 h but warns his assistant that he must continue working until he produces the same amount as the artisan. At what time will the assistant stop?
> 
> **A)** 12h $\quad$ **B)** 12h30min $\quad$ **C)** 13h $\quad$ **D)** 13h30min $\quad$ **E)** 14h30min
