*Properties of three-digit numbers with digit sum and product conditions*

> Starting with any three digit number $n$ (such as $n = 625$) we obtain a new number $f(n)$ which is the sum of the three digits of $n$, their three products in pairs, and the product of all three digits.
> 
> (i) Find the value of $n/f(n)$ when $n = 625$. (The answer is an integer!)
> 
> (ii) Find all three digit numbers $n$ such that $n/f(n) = 1$.
