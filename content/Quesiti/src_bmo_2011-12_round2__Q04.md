*Integers a,b,c,d: divisibility condition for all positive n*

> Show that if $a$, $b$, $c$, $d$ are integers and $a$ is a divisor of $b^n + c^n + d^n - b - c - d$ for all positive integers $n$, then $a$ divides $b + c + d$.
