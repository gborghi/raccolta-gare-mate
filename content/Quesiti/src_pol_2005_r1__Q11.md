*Inscribed circle in a convex quadrilateral; perpendicular through diagonals' intersection gives equal segments.*

> A circle with center $I$ is inscribed in a convex quadrilateral $ABCD$, where $I$ does not lie on $AC$. The diagonals $AC$ and $BD$ intersect at $E$. The line through $E$ perpendicular to $BD$ meets the lines $AI$ and $CI$ at $P$ and $Q$, respectively. Prove that $PE = EQ$.
