*Exercise 1, Q1: love-graph logic on who loves whom*

> Exercise 1 (all candidates) - The games of love. If Alice does not love Jordan, then Brenda loves Jordan. If Brenda loves Jordan, then Brenda loves Dan. If Alice loves Jordan, then Brenda does not love Dan. Brenda loves Jordan, so Brenda loves Dan. Does Dan love Alice?
