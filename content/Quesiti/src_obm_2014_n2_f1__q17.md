*Find angle x with isosceles triangles and parallel chords on a circle*

> Consider the figure on the right, where points $A$ through $I$ lie on a circle. It is known that triangles $ABC$, $CDE$, $EFG$, and $GHI$ are isosceles, and that segments $BC$, $DE$, $FG$, and $HI$ are parallel. What is the measure of angle $x = \angle APC$?
> 
> (A) $15^\circ$ $\quad$ (B) $20^\circ$ $\quad$ (C) $30^\circ$ $\quad$ (D) $40^\circ$ $\quad$ (E) $45^\circ$
