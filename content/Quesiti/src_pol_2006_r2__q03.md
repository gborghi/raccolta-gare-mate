*For positive numbers a,b,c with ab+bc+ca=abc, prove a cyclic sum of fractions involving fourth powers is at least 1.*

> Positive numbers $a,b,c$ satisfy the condition $ab+bc+ca=abc$. Prove that
> $$\frac{a^4+b^4}{ab(a^3+b^3)}+\frac{b^4+c^4}{bc(b^3+c^3)}+\frac{c^4+a^4}{ca(c^3+a^3)}\ge 1.$$
