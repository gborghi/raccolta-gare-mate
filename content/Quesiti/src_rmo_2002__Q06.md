*Prove double inequality involving harmonic-type sum*

> For any natural number $n > 1$, prove the inequality:
> $$\frac{1}{2} < \frac{1}{n^2+1} + \frac{2}{n^2+2} + \frac{3}{n^2+3} + \cdots + \frac{n}{n^2+n} < \frac{1}{2} + \frac{1}{2n}.$$
