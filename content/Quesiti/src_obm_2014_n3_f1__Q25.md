*Probability a 3x4 rectangular coin lands fully inside a 10x20 rectangular tile*

> To calculate the probability that a coin of radius $r$ lands totally inside a tile formed of square tiles of side $\ell$, we compute the probability that its center lands inside a smaller square of side $\ell - 2r$ (we remove a "border" of size $r$ from the sides of the square). That probability is equal to $\left(\frac{\ell - 2r}{\ell}\right)^2$. In Esmeralandia, the coins are rectangular. What is the probability that a coin with sides $3$ and $4$ lands totally inside a tile formed by rectangles with sides $10$ and $20$?
> (A) Less than $0.375$. (B) Exactly $0.375$. (C) More than $0.375$ and less than $0.595$. (D) Exactly $0.595$. (E) More than $0.595$.
