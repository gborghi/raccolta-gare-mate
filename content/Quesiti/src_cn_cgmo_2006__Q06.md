*Nonneg reals summing to 1: prove inequality with square roots*

> For nonnegative real numbers $a$, $b$, $c$ with $a + b + c = 1$, prove that
> $$\sqrt{a + \frac{(b-c)^2}{4}} + \sqrt{b} + \sqrt{c} \le \sqrt{3}.$$
