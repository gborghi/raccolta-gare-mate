*Count ways to fill 10 cards so all prefix sums are in {-1,0,1}*

> Ten cards are lined up in a row. On the odd-numbered cards (counting from the left) a positive integer is written, and on the even-numbered cards a negative integer is written. How many ways are there to fill in the cards so that the following condition holds: for every integer $i$ with $1 \le i \le 10$, the total of the numbers written on cards $1$ through $i$ is one of $-1$, $0$, $1$?
