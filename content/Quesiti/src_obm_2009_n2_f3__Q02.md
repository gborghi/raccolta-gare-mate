# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2009 — Nível 2 — Fase 3 — Quesito 2

*Circumcenter of triangle CEF lies on circumcircle of triangle ABD*

> Let $ABCD$ be a parallelogram and $\Gamma$ the circumscribed circle of triangle $ABD$. Let $E$ and $F$ be the intersections of $\Gamma$ with lines $BC$ and $CD$, respectively. Prove that the circumcenter of triangle $CEF$ lies on $\Gamma$.
