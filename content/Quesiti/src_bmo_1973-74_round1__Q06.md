# British Mathematical Olympiad 1973-74 — Round 1 — Quesito 6

*Feet of perpendiculars from P to sides of triangle; line through P perpendicular to AB meets XY at Z bisecting AB*

> $X$ and $Y$ are the feet of the perpendiculars from a point $P$ to $CA$ and $CB$ respectively, where $P$ is a point in the triangle $ABC$. The straight line through $P$ which is perpendicular to $AB$ cuts $XY$ at $Z$. Prove that $CZ$ bisects $AB$.
