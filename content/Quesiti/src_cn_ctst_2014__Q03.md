*Show no positive integer 2-tuples satisfy a given multiplicative equation.*

> Show that there are no $2$-tuples $(x, y)$ of positive integers satisfying the equation
> $$(x + 1)(y + 2)^{x-1}(y + 2014) = (y + 1)(x + 2)^{y-1}(x + 1023).$$
> (posed by Li Weigu)
