*Equal sums of odd/even lateral edge segments on regular 2n-gon pyramid*

> The base of a regular pyramid is a regular $2n$-gon $A_1 A_2 \ldots A_{2n}$. A sphere passing through the top vertex $S$ intersects the lateral edge $SA_i$ at $B_i$ for $i = 1, 2, \ldots, 2n$. Prove that $\sum_{i=1}^{n} SB_{2i-1} = \sum_{i=1}^{n} SB_{2i}$.
