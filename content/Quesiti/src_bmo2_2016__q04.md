*Prime p and integers a,b with p^2 mean of a^2 and b^2, prove 2p-a-b is square or double square*

> Suppose that $p$ is a prime and that $a$ and $b$ are positive integers such that $p^2$ is the mean of $a^2$ and $b^2$. Prove that $2p - a - b$ is a perfect square or twice a perfect square.
