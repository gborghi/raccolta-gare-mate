*Count negative integer values satisfying floor equation*

> How many negative integer values of $x$ satisfy the equation $\left[\dfrac{x}{3}\right] = \left[\dfrac{x}{5}\right]$? (Here $[z]$ denotes the greatest integer less than or equal to $z$. For example $[3.4] = 3$ and $[-2.3] = -3$.)
