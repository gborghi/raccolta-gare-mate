*Saba, Rayan, Derin complete task together in 5 min; Saba+Derin take 7 min; Rayan+Derin take 15 min; find time for Derin alone.*

> Saba, Rayan and Derin are cooperating to complete a task. They each work at a constant rate independent of whoever else is working on the task. When all three work together, it takes $5$ minutes to complete the task. When Saba is working with Derin, the task takes $7$ minutes to complete. When Rayan is working with Derin, the task takes $15$ minutes to complete. How many minutes does it take for Derin to complete the task on his own?
>
> - **(A)** 21
> - **(B)** 28
> - **(C)** 35
> - **(D)** 48
> - **(E)** 105
