*Cyclic product of differences of 2011 non-negative reals with given sum is bounded*

> Let $x_1, x_2, x_3, \ldots, x_{2011}$ be non-negative real numbers whose sum is $\dfrac{2011}{2}$. Prove that
> $$\left|\prod_{\text{cyc}}(x_i - x_{i+1})\right| = \left|(x_1-x_2)(x_2-x_3)(x_3-x_4)(x_4-x_5)\cdots(x_{2009}-x_{2010})(x_{2010}-x_{2011})(x_{2011}-x_1)\right| \le \frac{3\sqrt{3}}{16}.$$
