*Cyclic quadrilateral ABCD: angle bisector of angle ADB meets AC at N; prove CD parallel to AB iff AN=NC*

> Consider a cyclic quadrilateral $ABCD$. The diagonals $AC$ and $BD$ meet at $T$. The internal angle bisector of $\angle ADB$ meets $AC$ at $N$. Prove that $CD$ is parallel to $AB$ if and only if $AN = NC$.
