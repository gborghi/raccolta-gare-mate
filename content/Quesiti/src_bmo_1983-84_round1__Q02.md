# British Mathematical Olympiad 1983-84 — Round 1 — Quesito 2

*Binomial coefficients mod 3: remainders 1 exceed remainders 2*

> Let $a_n$ be the number of binomial coefficients $\binom{n}{r}$ ($0 \le r \le n$) which leave remainder $1$ on division by $3$, and let $b_n$ be the number which leave remainder $2$. Prove that $a_n > b_n$ for all positive integers $n$.
