# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2002 — Nível Universitário — Fase 1 — Quesito 6

*Tangent lines to a curve and periodicity of intersection points*

> Consider the curve $C=\{(x,y)\in\mathbb{R}^2 \mid y^2=x^3-43x+166\}$.
> 
> a) Let $Q=(a,b)$ be a point on $C$. Suppose that the tangent line to $C$ at $Q$ meets $C$ at a unique other point $Q'$. Determine the coordinates of $Q'$.
> 
> b) Let $P_0=(3,8)$. For each non-negative integer $n$, define $P_{n+1}=P_n'$, the point of intersection of $C$ with the tangent to $C$ at $P_n$. Determine $P_{2002}$.
