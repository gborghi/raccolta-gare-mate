*Largest c for quadratic inequality; nearest integer to 2009c*

> Let $c$ be the largest real constant such that
> $$x^2 + 3y^2 \ge c\,(x^2 + xy + 4y^2)$$
> for all real numbers $x$ and $y$. Determine the integer nearest to $2009c$.
