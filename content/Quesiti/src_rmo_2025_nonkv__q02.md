*Find value of expression given three nonzero reals satisfying a system*

> Let $a, b, c$ be distinct nonzero real numbers satisfying
> $$a + \frac{2}{b} = b + \frac{2}{c} = c + \frac{2}{a}.$$
> Determine the value of $|a^2 b + b^2 c + c^2 a|$.
