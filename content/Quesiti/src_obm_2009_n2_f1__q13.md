*Queue of 49 fans (25 Corinthians, 14 Flamengo, 10 Grêmio): no two same team consecutive*

> In a ticket queue for a Brazilian national team match there are 49 people: 25 Corinthians fans, 14 Flamengo fans, and 10 Grêmio fans. Knowing that each person appears only once in the queue and no two fans of the same team are in consecutive positions, we can conclude:
> 
> **(A)** Such a queue does not exist.
> **(B)** Some Grêmio fan is at an end of the queue.
> **(C)** Some Grêmio fan is a neighbor of a Flamengo fan.
> **(D)** Some Flamengo fan is a neighbor of a Grêmio fan.
> **(E)** Some Grêmio fan is a neighbor of two Corinthians fans.
