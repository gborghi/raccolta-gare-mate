*Find all values of cyclic sum x_i/(x_{i-1}+x_i+x_{i+1}) for positive reals, n>3 fixed*

> Let $n > 3$ be a fixed integer and let $x_1, x_2, \ldots, x_n$ be positive real numbers. Find, in terms of $n$, all possible real values of
> $$S = \frac{x_1}{x_n + x_1 + x_2} + \frac{x_2}{x_1 + x_2 + x_3} + \frac{x_3}{x_2 + x_3 + x_4} + \cdots + \frac{x_{n-1}}{x_{n-2} + x_{n-1} + x_n} + \frac{x_n}{x_{n-1} + x_n + x_1}.$$
