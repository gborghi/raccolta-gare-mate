*Cube cross-section area equals face area; prove coplanarity*

> $AB$, $AC$, $AD$ are three edges of a cube. $AC$ is produced to $E$ so that $AE = 2AC$ and $AD$ is produced to $F$ so that $AF = 3AD$. Prove that the area of the section of the cube by any plane parallel to $BEF$ is equal to the area of the section of the tetrahedron $ABEF$ by the same plane.
