*Line up children so each is beside at most two of their friends*

> There are $2010^{2010}$ children at a mathematics camp. Each has at most three friends at the camp, and $A$ is friends with $B$ if and only if $B$ is friends with $A$. The camp leader would like to line the children up so that every child is next to at most two children between any pair of friends. Is it always possible to do this?
