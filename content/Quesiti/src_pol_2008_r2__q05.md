*Triangle ABC with AC=BC, point D on AB with AD<DB, E reflection of A in CD; prove a ratio identity.*

> In a triangle $ABC$ with $AC = BC$, point $D$ on the side $AB$ is such that $AD < DB$ and $E$ is the reflection of $A$ in $CD$. Prove that
> $$\frac{AC}{CD} = \frac{BE}{BD - AD}.$$
