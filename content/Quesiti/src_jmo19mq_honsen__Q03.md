*Find all triples (n1,n2,n3) of positive integers with b1*b2=b3*

> Let $k \ge 2$ be an integer, let $n_1, n_2, n_3$ be positive integers, and let $a_1, a_2, a_3$ be integers each satisfying $1 \le a_i \le k-1$. Define
> $$b_i = a_i \sum_{j=0}^{n_i} k^j \quad (i = 1, 2, 3).$$
> Find all triples $(n_1, n_2, n_3)$ such that $b_1 b_2 = b_3$.
