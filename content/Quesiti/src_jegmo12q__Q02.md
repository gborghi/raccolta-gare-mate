*Midpoint of PQ lies on circumcircle of ABC; orthocenter collinearity*

> Let $ABC$ be an acute triangle with orthocenter $H$. Take a point $P$ (different from $H$) on the circumcircle of $\triangle AHB$, and a point $Q$ (different from $H$) on the circumcircle of $\triangle AHC$, such that $P$, $H$, $Q$ are collinear in this order. Show that the midpoint of segment $PQ$ lies on the circumcircle of $\triangle ABC$.
