*Seven coins in a circle, one showing its visible face; determine whether by flipping five consecutive coins each time (a) or four consecutive coins each time (b) all can be made to show face down*

> Seven coins are arranged in a circle, with one face visible (showing heads).
> 
> a) Is it possible, turning over five consecutive coins each time, to make all of them show face down (tails)?
> 
> b) Is it possible, turning over four consecutive coins each time, to make all of them show face down (tails)?
