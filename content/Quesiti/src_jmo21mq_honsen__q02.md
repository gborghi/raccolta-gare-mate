*Find all positive integer quadruples satisfying a^n-1 factored product*

> Find all quadruples $(a, n, p, q, r)$ of positive integers satisfying the equation
> $$a^n - 1 = (a^p - 1)(a^q - 1)(a^r - 1).$$
