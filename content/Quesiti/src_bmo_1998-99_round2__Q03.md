# British Mathematical Olympiad 1998-99 — Round 2 — Quesito 3

*Prove inequality for non-negative reals summing to 1*

> Non-negative real numbers $p$, $q$ and $r$ satisfy $p + q + r = 1$. Prove that
> $$7pq + r \le \frac{3}{4} + r.$$
