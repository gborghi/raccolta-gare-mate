*Find all real functions satisfying a symmetric cubic functional equation*

> Find all functions $f$ from the real numbers to the real numbers which satisfy
> $$f(x)^3 + f(y)^3 + f(z)^3 = f(x+y+z)\bigl(f(x)^2 + f(y)^2 + f(z)^2 - f(x)f(y) - f(y)f(z) - f(z)f(x)\bigr)$$
> for all real numbers $x, y, z$.
