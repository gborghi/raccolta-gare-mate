*Find k such that sqrt(2)+sqrt(8)+sqrt(18)=sqrt(k).*

> The positive integer $k$ satisfies the equation $\sqrt{2} + \sqrt{8} + \sqrt{18} = \sqrt{k}$.
> 
> What is the value of $k$?
>
> - **(A)** $28$
> - **(B)** $36$
> - **(C)** $72$
> - **(D)** $128$
> - **(E)** $288$
