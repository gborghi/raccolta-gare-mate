*Equazione funzionale f(x+f(x+y))+f(xy)=...*

> Let $\mathbb{R}$ denote the set of real numbers. Determine all functions $f : \mathbb{R} \to \mathbb{R}$ satisfying the equation
> $$f(x + f(x + y)) + f(xy) = x + f(x + y) + y f(x)$$
> for all real numbers $x$ and $y$.
