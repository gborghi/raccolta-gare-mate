*Quadratic with two roots; prove b<0*

> Consider the equation $ax^2 + bx + c = 0$, where $a$, $b$, $c$ are real and $a > 0$. Suppose that this equation has two real roots $r$ and $s$ such that $0 < r < 1$ and $0 < s < 1$. Prove that $b < 0$.
