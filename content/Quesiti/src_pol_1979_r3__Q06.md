*Polynomial of degree n>1 with n distinct roots: sum of reciprocal derivative values is zero*

> A polynomial $w$ of degree $n > 1$ has $n$ distinct roots $x_1, x_2, \ldots, x_n$. Prove that:
> $$\frac{1}{w'(x_1)} + \frac{1}{w'(x_2)} + \cdots + \frac{1}{w'(x_n)} = 0.$$
