*Random coins thrown on desk; probability all removed within 4 operations.*

> Some number of coins are thrown onto a desk simultaneously. Consider the following operation: viewed from above, remove all coins that are visible from the top (those not covered by any other coin). Thinking of a situation where coins are stacked, repeat this operation starting from some initial arrangement, until all coins have been removed. The operation is performed repeatedly. Find the probability that all coins are removed in at most 4 operations.
