*Binomial coefficients mod 3: remainder-1 count exceeds remainder-2 count*

> Let $a_n$ be the number of binomial coefficients $\binom{r}{n}$ ($0 \le r \le n$) which leave remainder $1$ on division by $3$ and let $b_n$ be the number which leave remainder $2$. Prove that $a_n > b_n$ for all positive integers $n$.
