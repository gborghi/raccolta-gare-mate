*Seven dwarfs queue by height rule; count valid queues with and without Snow White*

> The seven dwarfs decide to queue. They insist on queuing so that, in each group of consecutive dwarfs in the queue, the tallest dwarf in that group is at the front. How many such queues are possible?
> 
> What if Snow White always comes along too?
