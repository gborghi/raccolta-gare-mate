*Count 'power-hour' minutes between 00:00 and 02:59*

> A \textit{hora potência} (power hour) is an hour whose format represents a perfect power of a positive integer with exponent greater than $1$; that is, something in the format $a^b$ where $a$ and $b$ are positive integers and $b > 1$. For example, $03{:}43$ is a power hour because $343 = 7^3$, but $01{:}10$ is not a power hour because $110$ is not a perfect power. Also, $02{:}89$ is not a power hour because there is no time $02{:}89$. How many power-hour minutes are there from $00{:}00$ to $02{:}59$?
