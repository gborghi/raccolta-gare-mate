*In a friendship graph with no common friends and two common strangers, find n*

> There are $n$ persons present at a meeting. Every two persons are either friends of each other or strangers to each other. No two friends have a friend in common. Every two strangers have two and only two friends in common. Prove that each person has the same number of friends at the meeting. If this number is $b$, find $n$.
