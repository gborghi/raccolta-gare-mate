# Concours Général des Lycées 1991 — Matematica — Quesito 4

*Max subset of {1,...,2^p} with no element double another*

> Let $p$ be a given natural number and $n = 2^p$. We consider the subsets $A$ of $E = \{1, 2, \ldots, n\}$ possessing the following property:
> $$\text{if } x \in A, \text{ then } 2x \notin A.$$
> Determine the maximum number of elements of such a subset $A$.
