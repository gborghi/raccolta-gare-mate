*Digits 1–9 placed one per cell in a 3×3 grid; row products are 12, 112, 216. Find the product of the digits in the shaded cells (second column, first and third rows).*

> The digits from 1 to 9 are to be written one per cell in the nine cells of the $3 \times 3$ grid. The product of the three digits in the first row is 12. The product of the three digits in the second row is 112. The product of the three digits in the third row is 216. What is the product of the digits in the shaded cells?
>
> - **(A)** 24
> - **(B)** 30
> - **(C)** 36
> - **(D)** 48
> - **(E)** 140
