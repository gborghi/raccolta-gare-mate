*Right triangle, midpoint D of AC, BD=3DC, find hypotenuse*

> Let $ABC$ be a right triangle with right angle at $A$. Let $D$ be the midpoint of $AC$. Given that $BD = 3DC$ and $AC = 2$, find the length of the hypotenuse of triangle $ABC$.
> 
> (A) $\sqrt{7}$ \quad (B) $2\sqrt{2}$ \quad (C) $3$ \quad (D) $\sqrt{10}$ \quad (E) $2\sqrt{3}$
