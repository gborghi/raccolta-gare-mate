*Pairs (a,b) with eventually constant gcd of power expressions*

> Determine all pairs $(a, b)$ of positive integers for which there exist positive integers $y$ and $N$ such that
> $$\gcd(a^n + b,\, b^n + a) = y$$
> holds for all integers $n \geq N$. (Note that $\gcd(x, y)$ denotes the greatest common divisor of integers $x$ and $y$.)
