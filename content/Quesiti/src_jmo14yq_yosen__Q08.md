*Maximize the minimum of a sum-of-fractions expression over positive reals summing to 1.*

> Let $n$ be a positive integer. For positive reals $a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_n$ with $a_1 + a_2 + \cdots + a_n = 1$, define
> $$A = \frac{a_1}{1 + a_1} + \frac{a_2}{1 + a_1 + a_2} + \cdots + \frac{a_n}{1 + a_1 + a_2 + \cdots + a_n}.$$
> Let $A$ depend on the choice of $a_1, \ldots, a_n$. Find the maximum value of the minimum of $A$ over all such choices.
