*Concyclic D,B,C,E with interior point P and equal angles; find BP/CP*

> On sides $AB$ and $AC$ of triangle $ABC$ there are points $D$ and $E$ respectively, and the four points $D$, $B$, $C$, $E$ lie on one circle. Moreover, $P$ is a point inside quadrilateral $DBCE$ satisfying $\angle BDP=\angle BPC=\angle PEC$. Given $AB=9$, $AC=11$, $DP=1$, $EP=3$, find the value of $\dfrac{BP}{CP}$. Here $XY$ denotes the length of segment $XY$.
