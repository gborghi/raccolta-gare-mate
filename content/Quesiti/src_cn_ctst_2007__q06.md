*Bound on size of set with lcm condition*

> Let $n$ be a positive integer, set $A \subseteq \{1, 2, \cdots, n\}$, and for every $a, b \in A$, $\text{lcm}(a, b) \le n$. Prove that
> $$|A| \le 1.9\sqrt{n} + 5.$$
