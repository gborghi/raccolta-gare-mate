*Find a^b after correcting one digit in a wrong addition*

> The following addition is incorrect. However, if we replace only one digit $a$ wherever it appears with another digit $b$, the addition becomes correct. What is the value of $a^b$?
> $$\begin{array}{r} 742586 \\ +\;829430 \\ \hline 1212016 \end{array}$$
