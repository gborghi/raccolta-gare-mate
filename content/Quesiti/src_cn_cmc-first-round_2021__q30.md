*A2-8: two unit circles centred at (2,1) and (10,11); line through O cutting equal arcs; sum of slopes.*

> [Test Paper A2, Problem 8] In a plane rectangular coordinate system $xOy$, $\Gamma_1$ is a unit circle centred at $(2,1)$ and $\Gamma_2$ is a unit circle centred at $(10,11)$. Make a line $l$ through the origin $O$ such that $l$ has two intersections with each of $\Gamma_1$ and $\Gamma_2$, dividing $\Gamma_1$ and $\Gamma_2$ into four arcs, and two of these four arcs are of equal length. The sum of the slopes of all the lines $l$ satisfying the conditions is ______.
