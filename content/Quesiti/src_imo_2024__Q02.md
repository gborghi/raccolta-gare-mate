# IMO 2024 papers — Quesito 2

*Coppie (a,b) con gcd(a^n+b,b^n+a) costante*

> Determine all pairs $(a, b)$ of positive integers for which there exist positive integers $g$ and $N$ such that
> $$\gcd(a^n + b,\, b^n + a) = g$$
> holds for all integers $n \geq N$.
> 
> (Note that $\gcd(x, y)$ denotes the greatest common divisor of integers $x$ and $y$.)
