*Positive integers satisfy ratio conditions, prove d >= b + f*

> Let $a, b, c, d, e, f$ be positive integers such that
> $$\frac{a}{b} < \frac{c}{d} < \frac{e}{f}.$$
> Suppose $af - be = -1$. Show that $d \ge b + f$.
