*Triangle ABC with midpoint D on BC; find angle BAC given angle condition*

> In triangle $ABC$, $D$ is the midpoint of $BC$ and $E$ is the point on $AB$ nearest to $C$. Given that $\angle ADC = \angle BAC$, find $\angle BAC$.
