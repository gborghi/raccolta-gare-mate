*Find integer pair r,s with 0<s<200 between two given fractions*

> Find a pair of integers $r$, $s$ such that $0 < s < 200$ and
> $$\frac{45}{61} \le \frac{r}{s} \le \frac{89}{60}.$$
> Also prove that there is exactly one such pair $r$, $s$.
