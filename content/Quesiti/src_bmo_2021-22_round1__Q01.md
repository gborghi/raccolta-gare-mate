*Count sums of consecutive positive odd numbers below 400*

> Find three even numbers less than 400, each of which can be expressed as a sum of consecutive positive odd numbers in at least six different ways.
> 
> (Two expressions are considered to be different if they contain different numbers. The order of the numbers forming a sum is irrelevant.)
