*Existence of a rational 2019x2019 matrix satisfying a cubic / quartic polynomial equation*

> Let $I$ and $0$ be the identity and zero square matrices, both of size 2019. Does there exist a square matrix $A$ with rational entries and size 2019 such that:
> 
> (a) $A^3 + 6A^2 - 2I = 0$?
> 
> (b) $A^4 + 6A^3 - 2I = 0$?
