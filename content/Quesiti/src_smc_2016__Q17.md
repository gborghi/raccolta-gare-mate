*Aaron chooses a three-digit code from digits 1–9, all different, in increasing order. How many codes can he choose?*

> Aaron has to choose a three-digit code for a lock. The digits can be chosen from 1 to 9. To help him remember them, Aaron chooses three different digits in increasing order, for example 278. How many different codes can Aaron choose?
>
> - **(A)** 779
> - **(B)** 504
> - **(C)** 168
> - **(D)** 84
> - **(E)** 9
