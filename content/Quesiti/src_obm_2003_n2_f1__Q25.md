# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2003 — Nível 2 — Fase 1 — Quesito 25

*Right triangle ABC; BD=8, EC=9; find angle DAE*

> In triangle $ABC$, we have $AB = 20$, $AC = 21$, and $BC = 29$. Points $D$ and $E$ are on side $BC$ with $BD = 8$ and $EC = 9$. Find the measure of angle $\angle DAE$, in degrees.
> 
> (A) $30^\circ$ \quad (B) $40^\circ$ \quad (C) $45^\circ$ \quad (D) $60^\circ$ \quad (E) $75^\circ$
