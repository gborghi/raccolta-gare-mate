*Infinitely many m >= k with C(m,k) coprime to l*

> (50 marks) Suppose that $k$, $l$ are two positive integers. Prove that there are infinitely many positive integers $m \geq k$ such that $\binom{m}{k}$ and $l$ are relatively prime.
