*Convex quadrilateral with equal sum of squares of sides; find diagonal*

> Let $ABCD$ be a convex quadrilateral with $AB = a$, $BC = b$, $CD = c$ and $DA = d$. Suppose
> $$a^2 + b^2 + c^2 + d^2 = ab + bc + cd + da,$$
> and the area of $ABCD$ is 60 square units. If the length of one of the diagonals is 30 units, determine the length of the other diagonal.
