*Distinct reals a,b,c with cyclic sum condition imply abc+t=0*

> Suppose $a, b, c$ are distinct real numbers and $t$ a real number such that $a + \frac{1}{b} = b + \frac{1}{c} = c + \frac{1}{a} = t$. Show that $abc + t = 0$.
