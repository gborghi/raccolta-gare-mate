*Find all positive reals a for which a valid partition of the integers exists*

> Determine all positive real numbers $a$ such that there exists a positive integer $n$ and sets $A_1, A_2, \cdots, A_n$ satisfying the following conditions:
> (1) every set $A_i$ has finitely many elements;
> (2) every pair of distinct sets $A_i$ and $A_j$ do not share any common element;
> (3) the union of sets $A_1, A_2, \cdots, A_n$ is the set of all integers;
> (4) for every set $A_i$, the positive difference of any pair of elements in $A_i$ is at least $a^i$.
