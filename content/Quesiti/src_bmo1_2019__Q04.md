*Angle equality in semicircle with tangent lines meeting*

> Let $\Gamma$ be a semicircle with diameter $AB$. The point $C$ lies on the diameter $AB$ and points $E$ and $D$ lie on the arc $BA$, with $E$ between $B$ and $D$. Let the tangents to $\Gamma$ at $D$ and $E$ meet at $F$. Suppose that $\angle ACD = \angle ECB$.
> 
> Prove that $\angle EFD = \angle ACD + \angle ECB$.
