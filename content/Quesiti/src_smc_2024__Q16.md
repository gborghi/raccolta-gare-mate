*Painting 3 of 8 blue doors (2×4 grid) red: at least one per row and at least two corners red*

> A set of cupboards containing eight identical blue doors is arranged in a $2 \times 4$ grid as shown. A factory decorator wishes to paint three of the doors red such that at least one door in each row is painted red and at least two of the four corners are painted red. How many ways are there to do this?
>
> - **(A)** $12$
> - **(B)** $24$
> - **(C)** $36$
> - **(D)** $40$
> - **(E)** $56$
