*Count color arrangements of 12 marbles in a row with adjacency condition*

> Red, blue, and yellow marbles — 12 in total — are arranged in a horizontal row. Find the number of arrangements satisfying the following condition. Here, arrangements with 2 or fewer colors for the marbles are also considered.
> 
> **Condition:** For every marble of any given color, there exists a marble of the same color that is adjacent to it.
