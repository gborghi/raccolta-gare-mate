*Perimeter of triangle AMC at least perimeter of quadrilateral ABME*

> Let $ABCD$ be a convex quadrilateral in which the sides $(AD)$ and $(BC)$ are parallel, $AB = CD$ and $AD < BC$. Let $M$ be the midpoint of the segment $[BC]$ and let $E$ be the point of intersection of the lines $(MD)$ and $(AC)$. Prove that the perimeter of triangle $AMC$ is greater than or equal to the perimeter of quadrilateral $ABME$.
