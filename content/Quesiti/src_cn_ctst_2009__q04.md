*Find nonempty set S in [ab,(a+1)(b+1)] making product ratio a perfect square*

> Let $a$, $b$ be positive real numbers with $b - a \geq 2$. Prove that for any two distinct integers $m$, $n$ in the interval $(a, b)$, there is a nonempty set $S$ consisting of some integers in the interval $[ab, (a+1)(b+1)]$, and that $\frac{\prod_{s \in S} s}{mn(m+n+1)^2}$ is a square of a rational number. (Posed by Yu Hongbing)
