*Sequence of positive integers with x1=1 and bounded growth; show every positive integer k is a difference of two terms.*

> Let $(x_n)$ be the sequence of positive integers such that $x_1=1$ and $x_n < x_{n+1} \le 2n$ for each $n \in \mathbb{N}$. Show that for every positive integer $k$ there exist indices $r,s$ such that $x_r-x_s=k$.
