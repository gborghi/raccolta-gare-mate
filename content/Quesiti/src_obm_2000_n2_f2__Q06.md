# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2000 — Nível 2 — Fase 2 — Quesito 6

*Football championship: find minimum second-round points guaranteeing Vulcano FC a final*

> The Venusian football championship is played by 10 teams in two rounds. In each round, each team plays once against each of the other teams. Suppose that $Vulcano$ $FC$ wins all matches in the first round. If it does not win the second round, $Vulcano$ $FC$ will play a final against the second-round winner (a victory is worth 3 points, a draw 1 point, a defeat 0 points).
> 
> a) Find the smallest $n$ such that, if $Vulcano$ $FC$ scores **exactly** $n$ points in the second round (regardless of who the opponents are and who else scores $n$ points), it is guaranteed to reach the final (regardless of who scores $n$ points among the opponents).
> 
> b) Find the smallest $n$ such that, if $Vulcano$ $FC$ scores **at least** $n$ points in the second round (regardless of who the opponents are and who else scores at least $n$ points), it is guaranteed to reach the final (regardless of who scores at least $n$ points among the opponents).
