*Minimum value of x^2+y^2 for point on a circle*

> If real numbers $x$ and $y$ satisfy $(x + 5)^2 + (y - 12)^2 = 14^2$, then the minimum value of $x^2 + y^2$ is \quad (\quad).
> 
> (A) 2 \qquad (B) 1 \qquad (C) 0 \qquad (D) $\frac{1}{2}$
