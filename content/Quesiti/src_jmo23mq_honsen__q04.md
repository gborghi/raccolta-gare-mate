*Prove KD bisects angle BKC via radical axis foot*

> Let $ABC$ be an acute triangle with orthocenter $H$. The circle passing through the points $B$ and $C$ and the circle having segment $AH$ as a diameter intersect at two distinct points $X, Y$. Let $D$ be the foot of the perpendicular from $A$ to line $BC$, and let $K$ be the foot of the perpendicular from $D$ to line $XY$. Prove that $\angle BKD = \angle CKD$.
