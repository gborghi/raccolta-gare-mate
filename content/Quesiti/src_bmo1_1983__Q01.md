*Circumcentre, midpoint, centroid collinearity in triangle*

> In the triangle $ABC$ with circumcentre $O$, $AB = AC$, $D$ is the midpoint of $AB$ and $E$ is the centroid of triangle $ACD$. Prove that $OE$ is perpendicular to $CD$.
