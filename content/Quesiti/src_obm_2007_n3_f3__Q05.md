*Convex quadrilateral; if angle POQ is right then PO bisects angle AOD and QO bisects angle AOB*

> Let $ABCD$ be a convex quadrilateral, $P$ the intersection of lines $AB$ and $CD$, $Q$ the intersection of lines $AD$ and $BC$, and $O$ the intersection of the diagonals $AC$ and $BD$. Prove that if $\angle POQ$ is a right angle then $PO$ is the bisector of $\angle AOD$ and $QO$ is the bisector of $\angle AOB$.
