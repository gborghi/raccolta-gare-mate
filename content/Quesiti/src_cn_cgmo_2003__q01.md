*Triangle area inequalities with ratio x on segment DE*

> Let $ABC$ be a triangle. Points $D$ and $E$ are on sides $AB$ and $AC$ respectively, and point $F$ is on line segment $DE$. Let $\frac{AD}{AC} = \frac{DF}{DE} = x$. Prove that
> $$S_{\triangle CEF} = x(1-x)S_{\triangle ABC}$$
> and
> $$\sqrt{S_{\triangle ABE}} + \sqrt{S_{\triangle ADF}} \le \sqrt{S_{\triangle ABC}}.$$
