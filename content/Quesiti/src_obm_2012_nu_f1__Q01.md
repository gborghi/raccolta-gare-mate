# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2012 — Nível Universitário — Fase 1 — Quesito 1

*Max of |sin²x · sin 2x| and product inequality*

> **a)** Determine the maximum possible value of $|\sin^2(x) \cdot \sin(2x)|$ for $x \in \mathbb{R}$.
> 
> **b)** Prove that for every positive integer $k$, if $x = \dfrac{2\pi r}{2^k - 1}$ with $r \in \mathbb{Z}$, then
> $$\prod_{j=0}^{k-1} |\sin(2^j x)| = |\sin(x) \cdot \sin(2x) \cdots \sin(2^{k-1} x)| \le \left(\frac{\sqrt{3}}{2}\right)^k.$$
