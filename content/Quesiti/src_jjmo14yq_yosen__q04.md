*Max people getting at least one more apple than orange*

> There are $2016$ apples and $2016$ oranges, and these are distributed among $2016$ people under the following conditions:
> \begin{itemize}
> \item Every piece of fruit must be distributed.
> \item No person receives $0$ pieces of fruit.
> \item No person may receive more than $4$ pieces of fruit in total (counting both kinds).
> \end{itemize}
> Under these conditions, what is the maximum possible number of people who receive at least $1$ more apple than orange?
