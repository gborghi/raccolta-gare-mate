*Solve a system of equations in real x and y*

> Solve the system of equations for real $x$ and $y$:
> $$5x\!\left(1 + \frac{1}{x^2 + y^2}\right) = 12$$
> $$5y\!\left(1 - \frac{1}{x^2 + y^2}\right) = 4.$$
