# Japan Junior Mathematical Olympiad 2017 — Yosen (Preliminary) — Quesito 3

*Fill boxes with 1-6 to make fraction closest to 1*

> Place the integers $1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6$, one each, into the boxes $\square$ in the expression below and compute the resulting value. Among all values obtainable, give the one closest to $1$. Here $2^{\square}$ denotes $2$ raised to the power written in the box.
> $$\frac{2^{\square}+2^{\square}+2^{\square}}{2^{\square}+2^{\square}+2^{\square}}$$
