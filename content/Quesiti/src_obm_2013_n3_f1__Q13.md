*Billiards: minimum speed for Jade's ball to meet a ball hit at 60 km/h, 30 degrees, balls 50 cm apart*

> Super Esmeralda and Jade Maravilha are playing billiards. Super Esmeralda makes a shot on a ball with a velocity of $60\,\text{km/h}$, at an angle of $30^\circ$ with one of the cushions, as in the figure. Jade Maravilha must hit Super Esmeralda's ball with another ball. The two balls leave the table cushion simultaneously, and are at a distance of $50\,\text{cm}$. Jade Maravilha can choose any angle for her shot. What is the minimum velocity with which Jade Maravilha can make her shot?
> 
> (A) $15\,\text{km/h}$ \quad (B) $30\,\text{km/h}$ \quad (C) $30\sqrt{2}\,\text{km/h}$ \quad (D) $30\sqrt{3}\,\text{km/h}$ \quad (E) $60\,\text{km/h}$
