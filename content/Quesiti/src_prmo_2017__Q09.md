*Ways to start from city A and return after visiting some of 5 fully-connected cities*

> There are five cities $A, B, C, D, E$ on a certain island. Each city is connected to every other city by a road. In how many ways can a person starting from city $A$ come back to $A$ after visiting some cities without visiting a city more than once and without taking the same road more than once? (The order in which he visits the cities also matters: e.g., the routes $A \to B \to C \to A$ and $A \to C \to B \to A$ are different.)
