*Two circles meet at L and M; with P on S2, chords PL and PM meet S1 again at Q and R; prove K lies on a fixed circle as P varies.*

> Circles $S_1$ and $S_2$ meet at $L$ and $M$. Let $P$ be a point on $S_2$. Let $PL$ and $PM$ meet $S_1$ again at $Q$ and $R$ respectively. The lines $QM$ and $RL$ meet at $K$. Show that, as $P$ varies on $S_2$, $K$ lies on a fixed circle.
