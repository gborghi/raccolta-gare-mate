*Small cubes with both a red and a blue face from a cut cube*

> I have a wooden cube with three red faces and three blue faces. It is cut into $3\times 3\times 3=27$ smaller cubes. How many of these smaller cubes have at least one red face and at least one blue face?
> (A) $6$ \quad (B) $12$ \quad (C) $14$ \quad (D) $16$ \quad (E) It depends on the arrangement of the red faces and the blue faces.
