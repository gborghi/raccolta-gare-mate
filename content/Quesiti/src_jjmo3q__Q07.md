# Japan Junior Mathematical Olympiad 2005 — Quesito 7

*Fill 3x3 grid so every row and column product is 2005*

> Fill each cell of a $3 \times 3$ grid with one positive integer so that the following condition is satisfied: the product of the three numbers in each column equals $2005$, and the product of the three numbers in each row also equals $2005$. In how many ways can such a filling with positive integers be done? Note that fillings that coincide under rotation or reflection are still counted as distinct.
