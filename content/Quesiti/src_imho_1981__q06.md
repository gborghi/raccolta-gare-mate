*Functional equation determine f(4,1981)*

> The function $f(x, y)$ satisfies
> (1) $f(0, y) = y + 1$,
> (2) $f(x + 1, 0) = f(x, 1)$,
> (3) $f(x + 1, y + 1) = f(x, f(x + 1, y))$,
> for all non-negative integers $x$, $y$. Determine $f(4, 1981)$.
