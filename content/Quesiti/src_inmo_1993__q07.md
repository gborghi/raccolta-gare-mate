*53-element subset of {1,...,100} contains two elements with sum divisible by 9*

> Let $A$ be a 53-element subset of $A = \{1, 2, 3, \ldots, 100\}$. Prove that there are two distinct elements $x, y \in A$ whose sum is divisible by $9$.
