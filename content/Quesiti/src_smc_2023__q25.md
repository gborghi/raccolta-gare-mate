*Area of region defined by a polynomial inequality with constraints*

> What is the area of the part of the $xy$-plane within which $x^3y^2-x^2y^2-xy^4+xy^3\ge 0$ and $0\le x\le y$?
>
> - **(A)** $\dfrac{1}{4}$
> - **(B)** $\dfrac{1}{2}$
> - **(C)** $1$
> - **(D)** $2$
> - **(E)** $4$
