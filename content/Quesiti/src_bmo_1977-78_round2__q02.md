*Infinitely many n where last m decimal digits of 3^n alternate parity*

> Given any integer $m > 1$ prove that there exists an infinity of positive integers $n$ such that the last $m$ decimal digits of $3^n$ form a sequence in which each digit except the last is in opposite parity to its successor, i.e. if one is odd then the next is even and vice versa.
