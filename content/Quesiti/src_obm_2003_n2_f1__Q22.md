# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2003 — Nível 2 — Fase 1 — Quesito 22

*Partition {2,3,5,7,11,13,17} so products differ by 1; digit sum of larger*

> Divide the numbers $2, 3, 5, 7, 11, 13$, and $17$ into two non-empty groups such that the product $A$ of one group and the product $B$ of the other satisfy $A - B = 1$. What is the sum of the digits of $A$?
> 
> (A) 10 \quad (B) 11 \quad (C) 13 \quad (D) 14 \quad (E) 15
