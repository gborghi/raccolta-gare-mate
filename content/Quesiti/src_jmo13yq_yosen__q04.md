*Find x^3+y^3+z^3 given three symmetric power-sum equations*

> Three real numbers $x, y, z$ satisfy
> $$\begin{cases} x + y + z = 0, \\ x^2 + y^2 + z^2 = 3, \\ x^5 + y^5 + z^5 = 15. \end{cases}$$
> Find the value of $x^3 + y^3 + z^3$.
