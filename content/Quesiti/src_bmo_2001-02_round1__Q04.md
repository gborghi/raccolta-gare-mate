# British Mathematical Olympiad 2001-02 — Round 1 — Quesito 4

*Count simultaneous handshake arrangements for 12 people at circular table*

> Twelve people are seated around a circular table. In how many ways can six pairs of people each shake hands simultaneously, so that no two pairs of people who are shaking hands are sitting next to each other at the table?
> (Nobody is allowed to shake hands with more than one person at once.)
