*Limits of a sequence defined by partial sums*

> Consider the sequence $a_n = \sum_{k=1}^{n} \frac{k}{n^2}$ for $n \geq 1$.
> 
> a) Determine $\lim_{n \to \infty} a_n$.
> 
> b) Determine $\lim_{n \to \infty} \left(\frac{2^n(2 - a_n)}{n+1}\right)^{n+1}$.
