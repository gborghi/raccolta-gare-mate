*Sequences of 1 and -1 with multiplicative and density properties*

> Determine all sequences $a_1, a_2, a_3, \ldots$ of $1$ and $-1$ that satisfy the equality
> $$a_{mn} = a_m a_n$$
> for all $m, n$ and have the property: Among any three successive terms $a_n, a_{n+1}, a_{n+2}$, both $1$ and $-1$ occur.
