*Area of triangle in convex quadrilateral with two perpendicularity conditions*

> Let $ABCD$ be a convex quadrilateral, and let $P$ be an interior point such that $AP \perp AD$ and $BP \perp CD$. Given $AB = 7$, $AP = 3$, $BP = 6$, $AD = 5$, and $CD = 10$, find the area of triangle $ABC$.
