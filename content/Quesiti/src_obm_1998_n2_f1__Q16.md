*Fill grid with digits 1-5; find most repeated digit*

> Place the digits 1, 2, 3, 4, or 5 in each small cell of the grid shown, so that each digit appears at least once and the largest possible number formed is a multiple of 9.
> 
> (The grid has 9 cells in a row.)
> 
> Which digit appeared most often in the number you constructed?
> 
> A) 6 times
> B) 5 times
> C) 4 times
> D) 3 times
> E) 2 times
