*Smallest a with a<=b<=c positive integers and 1/a+1/b+1/c = 1/2011*

> If $a$, $b$ and $c$ are positive integers such that $a \le b \le c$ and $\dfrac{1}{a} + \dfrac{1}{b} + \dfrac{1}{c} = \dfrac{1}{2011}$, what is the smallest value possible of $a$?
> 
> (A) 2011 (B) 2012 (C) 2013 (D) 2014 (E) 2011-2012
