*A sequence built from greatest proper divisors is bounded*

> The sequence $(p_n)$ of natural numbers satisfies:
> 
> (i) $p_1$ and $p_2$ are primes;
> 
> (ii) For $n \geq 3$ the number $p_n$ is the greatest proper divisor of $p_{n-1} + p_{n-2} + 2000$.
> 
> Prove that the sequence $(p_n)$ is bounded.
