*Prove property of orthocentre on circumcircle of acute triangle*

> Let $ABC$ be an acute-angled triangle with circumcircle $\omega$. Let the centre of $\omega$ be $O$ and the orthocentre be $H$. Prove that the centre of the circumcircle of triangle $BOH$ (where $B$ is a vertex) lies on the circle $\omega$.
