*Write 840 = p!/q! with p, q positive integers less than 10; find p + q.*

> The number $840$ can be written as $\dfrac{p!}{q!}$, where $p$ and $q$ are positive integers less than $10$. What is the value of $p + q$? Note that $n! = 1 \times 2 \times 3 \times \cdots \times (n-1) \times n$.
>
> - **(A)** 8
> - **(B)** 9
> - **(C)** 10
> - **(D)** 12
> - **(E)** 15
