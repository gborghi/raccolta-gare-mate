# Japan Junior Mathematical Olympiad 2016 — Honsen (Final) — Quesito 2

*n×n Latin-row grid, maximize adjacent equal-number pairs*

> Let $n \ge 2$ be an integer. Consider an $n \times n$ grid of cells, where each cell contains an integer from $1$ to $n$. Each integer from $1$ to $n$ appears exactly once in every row. (The columns may contain repetitions.) Two cells are called a "neighboring pair" if they share a side (not merely a vertex). Find the maximum possible number of neighboring pairs of cells that contain the same integer.
> 
> (Note: two cells sharing only a vertex are not considered neighbors.)
