*Smallest 'this-year number' divisible by 17*

> A number obtained by writing two positive integers that differ by $1$ side by side in decreasing order is called a "this-year number". For example, writing $20$ and $19$ gives $2019$, which is a this-year number. Find the smallest this-year number that is divisible by $17$.
