*Circle tangent to BC at B through midpoint M meets AB at P; prove AB×BP=2BM²*

> Consider triangle $ABC$. The midpoint of $AC$ is $M$. The circle tangent to $BC$ at $B$ and passing through $M$ meets the line $AB$ again at $P$.
> 
> Prove that $AB \times BP = 2BM^2$.

*Circolo tangente a BC a B attraverso il punto medio M incontra AB a P; prova AB×BP=2BM2*

> Prove che $AB \times BP = 2BM^2$.
