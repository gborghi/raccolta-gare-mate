*Floor function sum of square roots equals 400*

> Given that $x$ is a positive integer solve
> $$\left[\sqrt{x}\right] + \left[\sqrt[3]{x}\right] + \cdots + \left[\sqrt[k]{x}\right] = 400$$
> (where $[z]$ means the integral part of $z$) and prove your solution is complete.
