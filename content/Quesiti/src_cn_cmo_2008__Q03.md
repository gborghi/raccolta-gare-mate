*Prove inequality involving floor function for two ordered real sequences*

> Given an integer $n > 0$ and real numbers $x_1 \le x_2 \le \cdots \le x_n$, $y_1 \ge y_2 \ge \cdots \ge y_n$, satisfying $\displaystyle\sum_{i=1}^n x_i = \sum_{i=1}^n y_i$. Prove that for any real number $a$,
> $$\sum_{i=1}^n x_i \left[ia\right] \ge \sum_{i=1}^n y_i \left[ia\right],$$
> where $[p]$ is defined as the greatest integer less than or equal to $p$.
