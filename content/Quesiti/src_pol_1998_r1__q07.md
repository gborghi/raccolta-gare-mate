*Count functions f: A→A with f(f(k))=f(k) taking exactly m values*

> Let $m, n$ be given positive integers and $A = \{1, 2, \ldots, n\}$. Determine the number of functions $f : A \to A$ taking exactly $m$ values such that
> $$f(f(k)) = f(k) \le f(l) \quad \text{for all } k, l \in A \text{ with } k \le l.$$
