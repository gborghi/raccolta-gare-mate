*Distance in metres between two cars each at 180 km/h that are 1 second apart*

> In 2018, a racing driver was allowed to use the Drag Reduction System provided that the car was within 1 second of the car ahead. Suppose that two cars were 1 second apart, each travelling at 180 km/h in the same direction. How many metres apart were the cars?
>
> - **(A)** $100$
> - **(B)** $50$
> - **(C)** $10$
> - **(D)** $5$
> - **(E)** $1$
