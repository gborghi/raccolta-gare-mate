*AP of positive integers: cubes appear infinitely or not at all*

> (i) Prove that if an infinite sequence of strictly increasing positive integers in arithmetic progression has one cube then it has infinitely many cubes.
> 
> (ii) Find, with justification, an infinite sequence of strictly increasing positive integers in arithmetic progression which does not have any cube.
