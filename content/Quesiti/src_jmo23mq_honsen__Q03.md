*Minimal m with increasing sequence whose adjacent square-sum halves are squares*

> Let $n$ be an integer with $n \ge 2$. Find the minimum value of the positive integer $m$ for which there exist positive integers $a_1, \ldots, a_n$ satisfying the following two conditions:
> 
> - $a_1 < \cdots < a_n = m$.
> - The $n-1$ numbers $\dfrac{a_1^2 + a_2^2}{2}, \ldots, \dfrac{a_{n-1}^2 + a_n^2}{2}$ are all perfect squares.
