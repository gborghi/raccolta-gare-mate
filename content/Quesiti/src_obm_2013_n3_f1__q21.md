*Trapezoid ABCD, AB||CD, angle BAD=82, ABC=74, P on CD with AD+DP=PC+CB=AB; find angle APB*

> In trapezoid $ABCD$, with $AB$ parallel to $CD$, the angle $\angle BAD$ measures $82^\circ$ and the angle $\angle ABC$ measures $74^\circ$. Suppose there exists a point $P$ on side $CD$ such that $AD + DP = PC + CB = AB$. How much does angle $\angle APB$ measure?
> 
> (A) $76^\circ$ \quad (B) $77^\circ$ \quad (C) $78^\circ$ \quad (D) $79^\circ$ \quad (E) $80^\circ$
