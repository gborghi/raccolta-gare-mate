# British Mathematical Olympiad 1997-98 — Round 1 — Quesito 4

*Show unique positive-integer sequence satisfies given recurrence*

> Show that there is a unique sequence of positive integers $(a_n)$ satisfying the following conditions:
> $$a_1 = 1, \quad a_2 = 2, \quad a_4 = 12,$$
> $$a_{n+1}a_{n-1} = a_n^2 \pm 1 \quad \text{for} \quad n = 2, 3, 4, \ldots.$$
