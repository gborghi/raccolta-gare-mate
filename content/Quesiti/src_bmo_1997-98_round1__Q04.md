# British Mathematical Olympiad 1997-98 — Round 1 — Quesito 4

*Show unique sequence of positive integers satisfying given recurrence and divisibility*

> Show that there is a unique sequence of positive integers $(a_n)$ satisfying the following conditions:
> $$a_1 = 1, \quad a_2 = 2, \quad a_{n+2} = a_{n+1} + a_n + 12 \quad \text{for } n = 1, 2, 3, \ldots$$
