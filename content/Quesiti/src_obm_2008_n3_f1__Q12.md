*Wooden cube with 3 red and 3 blue faces cut into 27; count cubes with red and blue face*

> I have a wooden cube, with three red faces and three blue faces, in such a way that opposite faces have different colors. The cube is cut into $3 \times 3 \times 3 = 27$ smaller cubes. How many of these smaller cubes have, at the same time, one red face and another blue one?
> 
> (A) $6$ (B) $12$ (C) $13$ (D) $14$ (E) $16$
