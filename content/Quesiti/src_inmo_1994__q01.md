*Triangle with obtuse angle at C; medians, centroid, circle, parallelogram*

> In a triangle $ABC$ with an obtuse angle at $C$, $AD$ and $CF$ are the medians and $G$ the centroid.
> 
> (a) If points $B, D, G, F$ lie on a circle, show that $\frac{BG}{GD} \ge \sqrt{2}$.
> 
> (b) Moreover, if $P$ is the fourth vertex of the parallelogram $AGCP$, prove that triangle $GAP$ is similar to $\triangle ABC$.
