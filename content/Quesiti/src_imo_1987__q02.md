*Quadrilateral AKNM and triangle ABC have equal areas*

> In an acute-angled triangle ABC the interior bisector of the angle A intersects
> BC at L and intersects the circumcircle of ABC again at N. From point L
> perpendiculars are drawn to AB and AC, the feet of these perpendiculars being
> K and M respectively. Prove that the quadrilateral AKNM and the triangle
> ABC have equal areas.
