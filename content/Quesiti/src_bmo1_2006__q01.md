*Show n^2(n^2+16) divisible by 720 when n-1 and n+1 both prime*

> Let $n$ be an integer such that $n - 1$ and $n + 1$ are both prime. Show that $n^2(n^2 + 16)$ is divisible by 720. Is the converse true?
