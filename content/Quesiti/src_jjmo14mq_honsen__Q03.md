*Minimize product of two quadratics with ab+bc+cd=1*

> Let $a, b, c, d$ be real numbers satisfying $ab + bc + cd = 1$. Find the minimum value of
> $$(a^2 + ac + c^2)(b^2 + bd + d^2).$$
