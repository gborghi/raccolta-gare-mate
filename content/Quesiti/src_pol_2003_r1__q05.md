*n_1 has 333 nonzero digits; n_{i+1} moves the last digit of n_i to the beginning. Prove 333 divides either four, or all of n_1,...,n_333.*

> A positive integer $n_1$ contains $333$ decimal digits, and all those digits are nonzero. For $i=1,2,\ldots,332$, set $n_{i+1}$ to be the number obtained from $n_i$ by moving the last digit of $n_i$ to the beginning. Prove that $333$ divides either four, or all of the numbers $n_1,n_2,\ldots,n_{333}$.
