# Japan Mathematical Olympiad 2021 — Honsen (Final) — Quesito 3

*Acute triangle, points on sides with BD=CE; prove angle equality with circumcircle point*

> Let $\triangle ABC$ be an acute triangle. Let $D$ and $E$ be points on sides $AB$ and $AC$ respectively, satisfying $BD = CE$. Let $P$ be a point on segment $DE$, and let $Q$ be a point on the arc $BC$ of the circumscribed circle of $\triangle ABC$ not containing $A$. Suppose that $BP \cdot PC = QD \cdot QE$ and that the points $A, B, C, D, E, P, Q$ are all distinct. Prove that
> $$\angle BPC = \angle BAC + \angle EQD.$$
