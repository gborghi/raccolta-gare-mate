*Ratio of two sums of nested square roots*

> Compute $$\frac{\sqrt{10+\sqrt{1}}+\sqrt{10+\sqrt{2}}+\cdots+\sqrt{10+\sqrt{99}}}{\sqrt{10-\sqrt{1}}+\sqrt{10-\sqrt{2}}+\cdots+\sqrt{10-\sqrt{99}}}.$$ Here the denominator is the sum of $\sqrt{10-\sqrt{n}}$ as $n$ ranges over the integers from $1$ to $99$, and the numerator is the sum of $\sqrt{10+\sqrt{n}}$ as $n$ ranges over the integers from $1$ to $99$.
