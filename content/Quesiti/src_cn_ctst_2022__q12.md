*Real sequences a_i, b_j: bound number of pairs with |a_i+b_j−ij|≤m*

> Let $m$ and $n$ be positive integers with $m \ge n \ge 2022$. Prove that for any real numbers $a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_n$, $b_1, b_2, \ldots, b_n$, the number of ordered pairs $(i, j)$ with $1 \le i, j \le n$ satisfying $|a_i + b_j - ij| \le m$ does not exceed $3n\sqrt{m \ln n}$.
