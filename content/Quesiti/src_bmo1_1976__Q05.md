*Binomial coefficient sum identity with real parameters*

> Prove that
> $$\sum_{t=0}^{r-1} \binom{n-1}{t} \binom{m}{t+1} \left[\binom{n+m-2t}{r-2t}\right] = \sum_{t=0}^{r-1} \binom{n-1}{t} \binom{m}{t+1} \left[\binom{n+m-2t}{r-2t-2}\right],$$
> where $a$ and $b$ are real numbers, $r$ and $n$ are positive integers with $r$ odd and $r \le n$.
> 
> $\left[\binom{s}{k}\right]$ denotes the coefficient of $x^k$ in the expansion of $(1+x)^s$.
