*Prove symmetric inequality given sum of squares equals one*

> Let $x, y, z$ be positive real numbers such that $x^2 + y^2 + z^2 = 1$. Prove that
> $$x^2 y^2 + y^2 z^2 + z^2 x^2 \leq \frac{1}{3}.$$
