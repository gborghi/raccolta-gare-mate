*Two non-intersecting curves in xy-plane; 5 points chosen; minimize maximum axis-aligned rectangle area*

> In the $xy$-plane, there are two curves $y = f(x)$ and $y = g(x)$ that do not intersect each other, and for any point on either curve the $y$-coordinate is at least $1$. Among any $5$ points selected on these two curves (with at least $2$ points on each curve), consider all axis-aligned rectangles whose sides are parallel to the coordinate axes and whose opposite corners are two of the chosen points lying on different curves; each such rectangle has area at least $1$. Let $M$ be the maximum area of such rectangles over all choices of the $5$ points. Find the minimum possible value of $M$.
