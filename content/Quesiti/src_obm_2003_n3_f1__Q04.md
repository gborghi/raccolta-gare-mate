# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2003 — Nível 3 — Fase 1 — Quesito 4

*Count arrangements of 35 people in a line with Arnaldo first among his 5 friends*

> Five friends — Arnaldo, Bernaldo, Cornaldo, Dernaldo, and Ernaldo — must form a line together with another 30 people (35 people in total). In how many ways can we form this line so that Arnaldo is ahead of each of the other four friends? (Note: The friends do not need to be in consecutive positions.)
> 
> A) $35!$ \quad B) $\dfrac{35!}{5!}$ \quad C) $\dfrac{35!}{5}$ \quad D) $\left(\dfrac{35}{5}\right)! \cdot 5!$ \quad E) $e^{\sqrt{10}}$
