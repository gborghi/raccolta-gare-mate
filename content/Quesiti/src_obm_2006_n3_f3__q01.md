*Angle bisector foot and incenter give isosceles triangle*

> Let $ABC$ be a triangle, $P$ the foot of the internal angle bisector from $B$ to side $AC$, and $I$ the incenter of $ABC$. If $AP + AB = CB$, prove that $API$ is an isosceles triangle.
