*Find all functions satisfying 2yf(f(y)+x)=f(x+1)f(2y)*

> Find all functions $f$ from the positive integers to the positive integers such that for all $x, y$ we have:
> $$2y\, f(f(y) + x) = f(x+1)\, f(2y).$$
