*Find all prime numbers in an integer sequence defined by recurrence*

> Determine, with proof, the prime numbers in the sequence $\{u_n\}$ of integers defined by
> $$u_0 = 2, \quad u_1 = 3,$$
> $$u_{n+2} = u_{n+1} \cdot u_n + u_{n+1} - u_n + 2 \quad (n \geq 0).$$
