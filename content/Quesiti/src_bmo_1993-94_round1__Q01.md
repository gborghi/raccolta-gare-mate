*Three-digit number ratio n/f(n) where f sums digits, pair products, full product*

> Starting with any three digit number $n$ (such as $n = 625$) we obtain a new number $f(n)$ which is equal to the sum of the three digits of $n$, their three products in pairs, and the product of all three digits.
> (i) Find the value of $n/f(n)$ when $n = 625$. (The answer is an integer!)
> (ii) Find all three digit numbers such that the ratio $n/f(n) = 1$.
