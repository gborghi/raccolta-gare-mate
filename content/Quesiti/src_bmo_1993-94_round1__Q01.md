# British Mathematical Olympiad 1993-94 — Round 1 — Quesito 1

*f(n)=digit-sum+digit-product; find f(625) and all n with f(n)=n/f(n)*

> Starting with any three digit number $n$ (such as $n = 625$) we obtain a new number $f(n)$ which is equal to the sum of the three digits of $n$, plus the product of its three digits.
> 
> (i) Find the value of $f(625)$. (The answer is $f(625) = 73$.)
> 
> (ii) Find all three digit numbers such that $f(n) = n / f(n)$.
