*Solve the symmetric system 1/(xy)=x/z+1, 1/(yz)=y/x+1, 1/(zx)=z/y+1 for positive reals*

> Solve the system of equations for positive real numbers:
> $$\frac{1}{xy} = \frac{x}{z} + 1, \quad \frac{1}{yz} = \frac{y}{x} + 1, \quad \frac{1}{zx} = \frac{z}{y} + 1.$$
