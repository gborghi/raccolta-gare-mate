*n>1 is prime iff n divides all intermediate binomial coefficients*

> Prove that an integer $n > 1$ is a prime number if and only if, for every integer $k$ with $1 \le k \le n-1$, the binomial coefficient $\binom{n}{k}$ is divisible by $n$.
