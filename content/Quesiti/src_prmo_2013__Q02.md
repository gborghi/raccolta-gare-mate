*Value of sum of 1/(S_n+S_{n-1}) for n=1 to 99*

> Let $S_n = \sum_{k=0}^{n} \frac{1}{\sqrt{k+1} + \sqrt{k}}$. What is the value of $\sum_{n=1}^{99} \frac{1}{S_n + S_{n-1}}$?
