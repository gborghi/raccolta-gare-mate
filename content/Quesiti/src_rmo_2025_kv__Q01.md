*System of cyclic product equations in nonneg integers, a_i≠1*

> Solve the following system of equations in nonneg integers $a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_8$ where $a_i \neq 1$ for $i = 1, \ldots, 8$:
> $$a_1 a_2 = a_3 + a_4,$$
> $$a_3 a_4 = a_5 + a_6,$$
> $$a_5 a_6 = a_7 + a_8,$$
> $$a_7 a_8 = a_1 + a_2.$$
