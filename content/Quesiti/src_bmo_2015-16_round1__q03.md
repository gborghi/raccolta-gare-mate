*Sequence defined by recurrence with constants A and B; find rational initial values.*

> Suppose that a sequence $a_1, a_2, a_3, \ldots$ is defined by a formula $a_n = a_{n-1} + A + B/a_{n-1}$ where $A$ and $B$ are rational constants with $A \neq 0$. Determine all values of $a_1$ which give the smallest possible number of terms in the sequence which are rational numbers.
