*Find positive integers a,b,c,x,y,z satisfying two symmetric equations*

> Find all positive integers $a, b, c, x, y, z$ with $a \ge 2$, $b \ge 2$, $c \ge 2$, $x \ge y \ge z$ which satisfy
> $$a + b + c = xyz, \quad x + y + z = abc.$$
