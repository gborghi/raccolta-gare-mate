*Convex quadrilateral with special sides and angle; prove angle difference 90°*

> In a convex quadrilateral $PQRS$, $PQ = RS = (\sqrt{3}+1)QR = SP$ and $\angle RSP - \angle SPQ = 30^\circ$. Prove that
> $$\angle PQR - \angle QRS = 90^\circ.$$
