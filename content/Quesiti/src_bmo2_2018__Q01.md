*Circle tangent to BC through M meets AB; prove AB = BP = 2BM*

> Consider triangle $ABC$. The midpoint of $AC$ is $M$. The circle tangent to $BC$ at $B$ and passing through $M$ meets the line $AB$ again at $P$. Prove that $AB = BP = 2BM$.
