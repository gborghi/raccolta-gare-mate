*Sequence defined by floor sums; determine a_1979*

> The sequence of positive integers $a_n$ is defined by
> $$a_0 = 1979, \quad a_{n+1} = \lfloor \tfrac{1}{2}(a_0 + a_1 + a_2 + \cdots + a_n) \rfloor \quad (n \ge 0),$$
> where $\lfloor x \rfloor$ denotes the greatest integer not greater than $x$. (For example $\lfloor 3 \rfloor = 3$ and $\lfloor 5 \rfloor = 5$.) Determine $a_{1979}$.
