# Japan Junior Mathematical Olympiad 2019 — Yosen (Preliminary) — Quesito 11

*Circumradius given incenter, orthocenter, AI=5, AH=6, angle AIH=90*

> Let $I$ be the incenter and $H$ the orthocenter of triangle $ABC$. Given that $AI = 5$, $AH = 6$ and $\angle AIH = 90^\circ$ hold, find the length of the radius of the circumscribed circle of triangle $ABC$. (Here $XY$ denotes the length of segment $XY$.)
