*Given product ratio equals 1/11, find cyclic sum of a/(a+b)*

> Given that $$\frac{(a-b)(b-c)(c-a)}{(a+b)(b+c)(c+a)} = \frac{1}{11},$$ what is the value of $$\frac{a}{a+b} + \frac{b}{b+c} + \frac{c}{c+a}\,?$$
