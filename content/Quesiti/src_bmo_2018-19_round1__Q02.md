*Determine how many n in [3,2018] allow an n-ring of positive integers with bounded neighbour products*

> For each positive integer $n \ge 3$, we define an $n$-ring to be a circular arrangement of $n$ (not necessarily different) positive integers such that the product of any three neighbouring integers is in the range $3 \le s \le 2018$ for the integers in the range. Determine the number of integers $n$ in the range $3 \le n \le 2018$ for which it is possible to form an $n$-ring.
