*Find all real functions satisfying f(f(x)+y)=f(x+y)+xf(y)*

> Find all functions $f$ defined on the real numbers and taking real values which satisfy the equation $f(f(x) + y) = f(x + y) + x f(y)$ for all real numbers $x$ and $y$.
