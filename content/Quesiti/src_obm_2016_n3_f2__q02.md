*Find a+b given a shared root of two polynomials*

> Let $r$ be a root of the equation $x^2 - 12x - 12 = 0$. We know that $r$ is also a root of the equation $x^4 - ax^2 - b = 0$, where $a$ and $b$ are real numbers. Find $a + b$.
