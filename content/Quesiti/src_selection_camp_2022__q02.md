*Find all real functions satisfying a given functional equation*

> Find all functions $f : \mathbb{R} \to \mathbb{R}$ such that for all real numbers $x, y$,
> $$f(xf(y) + f(f(y))) + yf(f(x)) = f\!\left((f(f(x))+1)f(y)\right) + xy.$$
