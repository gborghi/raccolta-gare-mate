*Sequence of positive numbers with recurrence; find monotonicity threshold a*

> Suppose that the sequence of positive numbers $x_1, x_2, \ldots, x_n, \ldots$ satisfies $(8x_2 - 7x_1)x_1^7 = 8$ and
> $$x_{k+1}x_{k-1} - x_k^2 = \frac{x_{k-1}^8 - x_k^8}{x_k^7 x_{k-1}^7}, \quad k \ge 2.$$
> Find positive real number $a$ such that when $x_1 > a$ one has $x_1 > x_2 > \cdots > x_n > \cdots$, and when $0 < x_1 < a$ one does not have such monotonicity. (Posed by Li Shenghong)
