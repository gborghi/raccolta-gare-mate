*Height of a stack of cruzado banknotes equal to a 640-real salary, given a conversion rate.*

> Between 1986 and 1989, the country's currency was the cruzado (Cz\$). With the immense inflation of that time, the currency had to change its values several times: there were the cruzado novo, the cruzeiro, the cruzeiro real and, finally, the real. The conversion between cruzado and real was:
> 
> $$1\text{ real}=2{.}750{.}000{.}000\text{ cruzados}$$
> 
> Imagine that the currency had not changed and that João, who earns 640 reais per month today, were to receive his salary entirely in 1-cruzado banknotes, each 1,5 cm tall. The salary of João in cruzados would form a stack of height:
> 
> (A) 26,4 km (B) 264 km (C) 2640 km (D) 26400 km (E) 264000 km
