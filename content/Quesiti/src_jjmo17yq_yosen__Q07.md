# Japan Junior Mathematical Olympiad 2019 — Yosen (Preliminary) — Quesito 7

*Smallest n with d(n^2)=d(n^2+7^2019)*

> For a positive integer $x$, let $d(x)$ denote the number of positive divisors of $x$. Find the smallest positive integer $n$ satisfying $d(n^2) = d(n^2 + 7^{2019})$.
