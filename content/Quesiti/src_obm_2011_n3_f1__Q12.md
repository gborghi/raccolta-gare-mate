*Measure of angle ADE in a triangle where angle B - angle C = 50 deg, with bisector and a right angle*

> In a triangle $ABC$, $m(\widehat{ABC}) - m(\widehat{ACB}) = 50^\circ$, and the bisector from $A$ intersects the side $BC$ at point $D$. If $E$ is the point of side $AC$ such that $m(\widehat{CDE}) = 90^\circ$, the measure of angle $\widehat{ADE}$ is:
> 
> (A) 25° (B) 30° (C) 40° (D) 45° (E) 50°
