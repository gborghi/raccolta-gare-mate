*Infinitely many integers a with no two divisors summing to given odd k*

> Given an odd integer $k \ge 3$. Prove that there exist infinitely many positive integers $a$ such that $a$ has no two positive divisors $d_1, d_2$ satisfying $d_1 + d_2 = k$.
> 
> (Proposed by Ya Hongbing)

*Infinitamente molti interi a senza due divisori che si sommano a un dato k*

>
