*Find all real functions satisfying f(x^2+yf(x))=xf(x+y)*

> Find all real functions $f$ from $\mathbb{R} \to \mathbb{R}$ satisfying the relation
> $$f(x^2 + yf(x)) = xf(x + y).$$
