# British Mathematical Olympiad 1989-90 — Round 1 — Quesito 6

*Rational x,y satisfy x^4+y^4=2(xy)^3; prove 1-xy is rational square*

> Prove that if $x$, $y$ are rational numbers satisfying the equation
> $$x^4 + y^4 = 2(xy)^3,$$
> then $1 - xy$ is the square of a rational number.
