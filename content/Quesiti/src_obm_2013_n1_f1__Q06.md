*Two cats on a sofa; fraction of sofa occupied by Tica*

> The cats Mate and Tica are sleeping on the sofa. Mate arrived first, and when Tica arrived, she occupied one quarter of the surface of the sofa that was left over. The two of them together occupy exactly half of the surface of the sofa. What fraction of the surface of the sofa is occupied by Tica?
> 
> (A) $\dfrac{1}{12}$ \quad (B) $\dfrac{1}{8}$ \quad (C) $\dfrac{1}{6}$ \quad (D) $\dfrac{1}{5}$ \quad (E) $\dfrac{1}{2}$
