*If two triangles have sides a1,b1,c1 and a2,b2,c2, prove geometric means form a triangle*

> Prove that if $a_1$, $b_1$, $c_1$ and $a_2$, $b_2$, $c_2$ are the lengths of the sides of two triangles (in some unit of measurement) then
> $$a = \sqrt{a_1 a_2}, \quad b = \sqrt{b_1 b_2}, \quad c = \sqrt{c_1 c_2}$$
> are also the lengths of the sides of some triangle.
