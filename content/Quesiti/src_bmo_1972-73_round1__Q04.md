# British Mathematical Olympiad 1972-73 — Round 1 — Quesito 4

*Congruent equilateral triangular discs forming a convex equilateral hexagon; determine all valid n*

> You have a large number of congruent triangular equilateral discs on a table and wish to fit $n$ discs together to make a convex equilateral hexagon (i.e., one whose interior angles are each $120^\circ$).
> 
> Obviously $n$ can be $6$; the next smallest is $10$ and the next $13$. Determine conditions for possible $n$.
