*For each positive integer n and t in (1/2,1) there exist a,b in (1999,2000) with (1/2)a^n+(1/2)b^n < (ta+(1-t)b)^n.*

> Show that for each positive integer $n$ and each number $t \in \left(\frac{1}{2}, 1\right)$ there exist numbers $a, b \in (1999, 2000)$ such that
> $$\frac{1}{2}a^n + \frac{1}{2}b^n < (ta + (1-t)b)^n.$$
