*Choose semicircle point to maximize sum of triangle areas*

> Diameter $AB$ divides a circle into two semicircles. Points $P_1, P_2, \ldots, P_n$ are given on one of the semicircles in this order. How should a point $C$ be chosen on the other semicircle in order to maximize the sum of the areas of triangles $CP_1P_2, CP_2P_3, \ldots, CP_{n-1}P_n$?
