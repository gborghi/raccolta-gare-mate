*Minimum boxes to store 2015 water bottles and 2015 orange juice cartons*

> A factory has several boxes, each with a capacity of $31$ litres. It has $2015$ water bottles, each holding $3$ litres, and $2015$ orange juice cartons, each holding $5$ litres. Each box can hold any combination of bottles and cartons, as long as their total volume does not exceed the box's capacity. No box may be overfilled. What is the minimum number of boxes the factory needs to store all the bottles and all the cartons it has produced?
