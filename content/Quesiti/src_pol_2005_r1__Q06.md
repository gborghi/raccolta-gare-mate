*Decide whether an infinite positive-integer sequence can satisfy a reciprocal recurrence.*

> Determine whether there exists an infinite sequence $a_0, a_1, a_2, \ldots$ of positive integers satisfying
> $$\frac{1}{a_n} = \frac{1}{a_{n+1}} + \frac{1}{a_{n+2}}$$
> for all $n \in \mathbb{N}$.
