*Find ratio BA:BC given midpoint of BD and equal angles*

> In triangle $ABC$ with $AC = 28$, let $M$ be the midpoint of segment $BD$, where $D$ is a point such that the figure is determined as drawn. It is given that $\angle AMB = \angle CMB$, $AM = 20$, and $CM = 16$. Find the ratio $BA : BC$. Here $XY$ denotes the length of segment $XY$.

**Abilita:** [[skill_ragionamento_geometrico|Ragionamento geometrico]], [[skill_manipolazione_algebrica|Manipolazione algebrica]]
**Area:** [[Geometria]]
**Risposta:** $\sqrt{5} : 2$
