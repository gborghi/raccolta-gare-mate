*Count matrices M_i with A^4 = M_i among three given matrices*

> Let $M_1 = \begin{pmatrix} 2 & 1 \\ 1 & 2 \end{pmatrix}$, $M_2 = \begin{pmatrix} 2 & 1 \\ 0 & 2 \end{pmatrix}$, $M_3 = \begin{pmatrix} 2 & 0 \\ 0 & 2 \end{pmatrix}$. For each of the matrices $M_i$ ($i = 1, 2, 3$), determine how many matrices $A$ exist such that $A^4 = M_i$.
