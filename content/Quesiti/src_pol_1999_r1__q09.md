*D,E,F on sides of triangle ABC; incircles of AEF, CA, AB triangles tangent to incircle of DEF; prove AD, BE, CF concurrent.*

> Suppose that $D, E, F$ are points on the sides $BC, CA, AB$ of a triangle $ABC$ respectively such that the incircles of the triangles $AEF$, $BFD$, $CDE$ are tangent to the incircle of the triangle $DEF$. Show that the lines $AD$, $BE$, $CF$ are concurrent.
