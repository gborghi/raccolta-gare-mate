*Sequence u_n defined by recurrence; prove floor equals 2^{...}*

> The sequence $\{u_n\}$ is defined by
> $$u_0 = 2,\quad u_1 = \tfrac{5}{2},\quad u_{n+1} = u_n(u_{n-1}^2 - 2) - u_1 \quad \text{for } n = 1, 2, \ldots$$
> Prove that for positive integers $n$,
> $$[u_n] = 2^{[2^n - (-1)^n]/3}$$
> where $[x]$ denotes the greatest integer $\le x$.
