*Find all prime triples (p,q,n) with divisibility condition*

> Find all triples $(p, q, n)$ such that $p^{n+1}q^{n+1} \mid p^{2n+1} + q^{2n+1} - 3^{2n+1}$, where $p$, $q$ are prime and $n$ is a positive integer.
