# Japan Junior Mathematical Olympiad 2009 — Honsen (Final) — Quesito 5

*Perimeter of convex hexagon ≥ sum of three main diagonals at 60°*

> In a convex hexagon $ABCDEF$, any two of the three main diagonals $AD$, $BE$, $CF$ form an angle of $60^\circ$ with each other. Prove that
> $$AB + BC + CD + DE + EF + FA \geq AD + BE + CF.$$
> A polygon is called convex if all its interior angles are less than $180^\circ$. Here, $XY$ denotes the length of segment $XY$.
