*Binary integers with exactly 2013 digits: sum plus count has more 1s*

> Consider the set of positive integers which, when written in binary, have exactly 2013 digits and more 0s than 1s. Let $s$ be the number of such integers and let $n$ be their sum. Prove that, when written in binary, $n + s$ has more 1s than 0s.
