# Japan Junior Mathematical Olympiad 2012 — Honsen (Final) — Quesito 2

*7-digit multiples of 7 stable under all digit rearrangements*

> Let $n$ be a 7-digit number that is a multiple of $7$. Suppose that no matter how the digits of $n$ are rearranged, the resulting number is always a multiple of $7$. Find all such $n$.
