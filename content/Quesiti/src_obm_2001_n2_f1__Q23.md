*White tiles needed when 80 black tiles are used in the mosaic sequence*

> With white and black square tiles, all of the same size, the following mosaics are built. The rule for building these mosaics is the following: initially we form a square with $1$ white tile surrounded by black tiles; next, another square, with $4$ white tiles, also surrounded by black tiles; and so on successively. With $80$ black tiles, how many white tiles will be needed to make a sequence of mosaics like this one?
> 
> (A) $55$ \quad (B) $65$ \quad (C) $75$ \quad (D) $85$ \quad (E) $100$
