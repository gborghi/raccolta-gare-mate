*Card game: count cards used and minimum draws to guarantee two blue*

> Jade wrote all 3-digit numbers on yellow cards, one per card, and all 4-digit numbers on blue cards, one per card. All cards are the same size.
> 
> (a) In total, how many cards were used? Note that, for example, 037 is a 3-digit number.
> 
> (b) All cards are placed face down in the same pile and shuffled. Jade draws the cards one by one, without looking at what she is drawing. How many cards must Jade draw in order to be certain that there are at least two blue cards among those drawn?
