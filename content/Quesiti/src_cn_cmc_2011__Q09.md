*Find a, b given conditions on f(x)=|lg(x+1)|*

> Suppose $f(x) = |\lg(x + 1)|$ and real numbers $a$, $b$ ($a < b$) satisfy $f(a) = f\!\left(-\dfrac{b+1}{b+2}\right)$, $f(10a + 6b + 21) = 4\lg 2$. Find the values of $a$, $b$.

**Risposta:** $a = -\dfrac{2}{5}$, $b = -\dfrac{1}{3}$
