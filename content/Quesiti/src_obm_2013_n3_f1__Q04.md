# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2013 — Nível 3 — Fase 1 — Quesito 4

*Among 1..n with given counts of multiples of 5 and 6, max multiples of 7*

> Among the natural numbers from $1$ to $n$, at least $11$ are divisible by $5$ and at most $9$ are divisible by $6$. At most, how many of these numbers are divisible by $7$?
> 
> (A) $4$ \quad (B) $5$ \quad (C) $6$ \quad (D) $7$ \quad (E) $8$
