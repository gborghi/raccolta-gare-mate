*If abc=1 with a,b,c positive reals, prove a^(b+c)·b^(c+a)·c^(a+b)≤1*

> If $a, b, c$ are positive real numbers such that $abc = 1$, prove that
> $$a^{b+c} \cdot b^{c+a} \cdot c^{a+b} \le 1.$$
