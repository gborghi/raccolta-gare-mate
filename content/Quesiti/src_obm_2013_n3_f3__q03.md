*Find all injective functions from nonzero reals satisfying a functional equation*

> Find all injective functions $f$ from the nonzero reals to the nonzero reals such that
> $$f(x+y) \cdot \bigl(f(x) + f(y)\bigr) = f(xy)$$
> for all $x, y$ real and nonzero with $x + y \neq 0$.
