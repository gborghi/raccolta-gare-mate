*Prove (1-p^m)^n + (1-q^n)^m >= 1 for nonneg p,q with p+q=1*

> Let $p, q$ be nonnegative real numbers with $p + q = 1$, and let $m, n$ be positive integers. Prove that
> $$(1 - p^m)^n + (1 - q^n)^m \ge 1.$$
