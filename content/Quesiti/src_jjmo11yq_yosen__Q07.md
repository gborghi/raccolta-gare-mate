*Two-coloring 100-gon vertices with perpendicular chords*

> Among the vertices of a regular $100$-gon, $2$ are colored red and $2$ are colored blue. How many ways are there to do this coloring satisfying the condition below? A vertex may not be colored with two colors at once, and colorings coinciding under rotation or reflection are also counted as distinct.
> 
> Condition: the line through the two red vertices and the line through the two blue vertices are perpendicular to each other.
