# Regional Mathematical Olympiad 2019 — Quesito 1

*Prove x is rational given x^5 and 20x+19/x are rational.*

> Suppose $x$ is a nonzero real number such that both $x^5$ and $20x + \frac{19}{x}$ are rational numbers. Prove that $x$ is a rational number.
