# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2014 — Nível 2 — Fase 1 — Quesito 9

*Minimum button presses on calculator to display 100*

> It is only possible to press one digit at a time and some simple operations: $+$, $-$, $\times$, or $\div$ between digits. It is also not possible to press two digits in a row. The calculator starts with 0 in the display and the first press must be an operation; the operation is carried out immediately after a digit is pressed. For example, pressing $[+][7][+][7]$ gives $0+7=7$ and then $7+7=14$; pressing $[-][6]$ gives $14-6=8$. Thus it is possible to get 29 with 6 presses. Pedro wants to display the number 100 by pressing operations and digits alternately, starting with an operation. What is the minimum number of presses Pedro needs?
> 
> (A) 2 $\quad$ (B) 4 $\quad$ (C) 6 $\quad$ (D) 8 $\quad$ (E) 10
