# British Mathematical Olympiad 1985-86 — Round 2 — Quesito 5

*Locus of midpoint of $P_1P_2$ for parallel chords of two circles*

> $C_1$ and $C_2$ are two circles; $A_1$, $A_2$ are their centres respectively. $A_1P_1$, $A_2P_2$ are parallel chords of $C_1$, $C_2$ respectively. Find the locus of the midpoint of $P_1 P_2$.
