*Car-washing earnings split in dimes among three people*

> Edmilson, Carlos and Eduardo earned a total of R\$150.00 washing cars. They earned the amounts in dimes (moedas de dez centavos). Since none of them had change to divide the equal amounts among the three, Carlos and Eduardo gave their dimes to Edmilson, who then had more money in dimes, and they divided the dimes among themselves. In the end, each one could divide his original amount of dimes; Eduardo received R\$10.00 in dimes and the others divided equally, so that each one ended up with an integer number of dimes. How much did Eduardo earn before the division?
> (A) R\$76.00 \quad (B) R\$51.00 \quad (C) R\$23.00 \quad (D) R\$50.00 \quad (E) R\$100.00
