*Replace 3 triangles by 2 squares and 1 pentagon on a hexagon*

> Luana glued, with adhesive tape, 6 equilateral triangles onto the sides of a hexagon, as shown in the figure, obtaining a polygon with 12 sides. If she replaces 3 of the triangles by 2 squares and 1 regular pentagon, all with sides of the same size as those of the hexagon, with how many sides will the polygon now obtained have?
> 
> (A) 14 \quad (B) 16 \quad (C) 17 \quad (D) 18 \quad (E) 25
