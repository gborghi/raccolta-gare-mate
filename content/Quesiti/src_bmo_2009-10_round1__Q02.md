*Angle ABC = 90° iff AD = CE; points on circle with perpendicularity conditions*

> Points $A$, $B$, $C$, $D$ and $E$ lie, in that order, on a circle and the lines $AB$ and $DC$ are produced to meet at $F$. The line through $A$ which is perpendicular to $AB$, the line through $D$ which is perpendicular to $DC$, and the perpendicular bisector of $BC$ are concurrent. Prove that $\angle ABC = 90^\circ$ if and only if $AD = CE$.
