*Find total percentage of correct answers on a three-subject exam*

> Gabriel solved a mathematics exam containing questions on algebra, geometry, and logic. After checking his result, Gabriel observed that he answered correctly $50\%$ of the algebra questions, $70\%$ of the geometry questions, and $80\%$ of the logic questions. Gabriel also noticed that he answered correctly exactly $62\%$ of the algebra and logic questions combined, and $74\%$ of the geometry and logic questions combined. What percentage of all questions on Gabriel's exam did he answer correctly?
> 
> (Note: this is Parte B, Problema 1, worth 10 points each.)
