# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2006 — Nível 3 — Fase 1 — Quesito 5

*Two distinct nonzero reals a,b with a-b=a/b; find a possible value of a/b+1/b-ab.*

> The two distinct real numbers $a$ and $b$ are nonzero and satisfy $a - b = \dfrac{a}{b}$. Indicate the alternative that shows one of the possible values of $\dfrac{a}{b} + \dfrac{1}{b} - ab$.
> 
> (A) $-2$ \quad (B) $-\dfrac{1}{2}$ \quad (C) $\dfrac{1}{3}$ \quad (D) $\dfrac{1}{2}$ \quad (E) $2$
