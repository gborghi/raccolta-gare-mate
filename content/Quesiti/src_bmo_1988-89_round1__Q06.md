# British Mathematical Olympiad 1988-89 — Round 1 — Quesito 6

*Prove all terms of integer sequence are odd*

> The sequence $\{a_n\}$ of integers is defined by
> $$a_1 = 2, \quad a_2 = 7$$
> and
> $$-\frac{1}{2} < a_{n+1} - \frac{a_n^2}{a_{n-1}} \le \frac{1}{2} \quad \text{for } n \ge 2.$$
> Prove that $a_n$ is odd for all $n > 1$.
