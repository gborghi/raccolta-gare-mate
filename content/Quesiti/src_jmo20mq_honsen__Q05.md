# Japan Mathematical Olympiad 2010 — Honsen (Final) — Quesito 5

*Max self-intersections of closed diagonal tour of convex 2010-gon*

> A convex $2010$-gon is given in which no three diagonals have a common point other than the vertices. Consider a closed broken line that consists of $2010$ diagonals (sides of the polygon are not allowed) and passes through every vertex exactly once. Find the maximum possible number of self-intersections of such a closed broken line.
> 
> (For a broken line $P_1 P_2 \cdots P_n P_{n+1}$ with $P_1 = P_{n+1}$, we call it a closed broken line.)
