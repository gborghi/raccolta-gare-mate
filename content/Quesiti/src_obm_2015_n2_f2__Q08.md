# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2015 — Nível 2 — Fase 2 — Quesito 8

*Count positive integers n<100 making (8n+5)/(5n+8) irreducible*

> Determine the number of positive integers $n$ less than $100$ such that the fraction $\dfrac{8n+5}{5n+8}$ is irreducible.
> 
> A fraction is called irreducible when the greatest common divisor (GCD) of its numerator and its denominator equals $1$.
