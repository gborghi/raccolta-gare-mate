*Find the largest real t_n such that a symmetric max inequality holds*

> Let $n \ge 3$ be a positive integer. Find the largest real number $t_n$, as a function of $n$, such that the inequality
> $$\max(|a_1 + a_2|, |a_2 + a_3|, \ldots, |a_{n-1} + a_n|, |a_n + a_1|) \ge t_n \cdot \max(|a_1|, |a_2|, \ldots, |a_n|)$$
> holds for all real numbers $a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_n$.
