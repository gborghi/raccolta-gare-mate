*Quadrilateral with inscribed circles on four sub-quadrilaterals: three lines concurrent or parallel*

> Let $ABCD$ be a convex quadrilateral, and let $P, Q, R, S$ be points on sides $AB, BC, CD, DA$ respectively. Let $O$ be the intersection of segments $PR$ and $QS$. Suppose that each of the quadrilaterals $APOS$, $BQOP$, $CROQ$, and $DSOR$ has an inscribed circle. Prove that the three lines $AC$, $PQ$, and $RS$ either all meet at a single point or are mutually parallel.
