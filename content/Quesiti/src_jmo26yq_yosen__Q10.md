*Minimum travel distance to visit 2016 points on a circle*

> On a circle there are $2016$ flags placed at equally spaced positions ($2016$ points, adjacent ones at arc-distance $1$). Person A moves along the circle and wants to take down all the flags. For the worst case over A's starting position, find the minimum total distance A must travel so as to be able to take down all the flags. A does not need to return to the starting point. (The answer has the form below.)
