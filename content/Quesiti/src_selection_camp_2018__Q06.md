*Integer sequence with positive integer parameters: one of a_{2017}, a_{2018} is at least 2017*

> Let $a_0, a_1, a_2, \ldots$ be a sequence of integers and $b_0, b_1, b_2, \ldots$ be a sequence of positive integers. Suppose $a_0 = 0$, $a_1 = 1$, and for $n = 1, 2, \ldots$,
> $$a_{n+1} = \begin{cases} a_n b_n + a_{n-1} & \text{if } b_{n-1} = 1, \\ a_n b_n - a_{n-1} & \text{if } b_{n-1} > 1. \end{cases}$$
> Prove that at least one of $a_{2017}$ and $a_{2018}$ is greater than or equal to $2017$.
