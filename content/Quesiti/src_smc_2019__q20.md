*Find angle ZXY in a heart formed from equilateral triangle ABC and two semicircles, with lines through centre O meeting AC*

> The "heart" shown in the diagram is formed from an equilateral triangle $ABC$ and two congruent semicircles on $AB$. The point $O$ is the centre of one of the semicircles, and lies on a point $X$. The lines $XO$ and $XP$ are extended to meet $AC$ at $Y$ and $Z$ respectively. The lines $XY$ and $XZ$ are of equal length.
> 
> What is $\angle ZXY$?
>
> - **(A)** $20^\circ$
> - **(B)** $25^\circ$
> - **(C)** $30^\circ$
> - **(D)** $40^\circ$
> - **(E)** $45^\circ$

*Cerca angolo ZXY in un cuore formato da triangolo equilaterale ABC e da due semicircoli, con linee attraverso il centro O che incontrano AC*

> Che cos' è $\angle ZXY$?

> - **(A)** $20^\circ$
> - **(B)** $25^\circ$
> - **(C)** $30^\circ$
