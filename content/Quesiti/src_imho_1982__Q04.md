*Prove x^2 - 3xy + y^2 = n has no integer solutions when n = 2^(2891)*

> Prove that if $n$ is a positive integer such that the equation
> $$x^2 - 3xy + y^2 = n$$
> has a solution in integers $(x, y)$, then it has at least three such solutions. Show that the equation has no solution in integers when $n = 2^{2891}$.
