*Find all integer pairs (a,b) satisfying equation with prime p > 3*

> Suppose $p$ is a prime greater than $3$. Find all pairs of integers $(a, b)$ satisfying the equation
> $$a^2 + 3ab + 2(a + b) + p^2 = 0.$$
