- [[src_pol_1994_r2__Q01|Quesito 1]] — Find all degree-5 real polynomials with divisibility conditions involving (x-1)^3 and (x+1)^3.
- [[src_pol_1994_r2__Q02|Quesito 2]] — Given positive reals whose sum equals their product, and positive b_i with a_i <= b_i, prove the sum of b_i is at most their product.
- [[src_pol_1994_r2__Q03|Quesito 3]] — A plane through the center of a cube intersects it in a cyclic hexagon; show the hexagon is regular.
- [[src_pol_1994_r2__Q04|Quesito 4]] — Each vertex of a cube is assigned 1 or -1, each face the product of its four vertices; find all possible values of the sum of all 14 numbers.
- [[src_pol_1994_r2__Q05|Quesito 5]] — Incircle of triangle ABC touches AB and BC at P and Q; the bisector from A meets PQ at S; prove angle ASC is 90 degrees.
