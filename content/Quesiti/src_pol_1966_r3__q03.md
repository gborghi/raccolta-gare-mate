*Nonneg reals summing to $\le\frac{1}{2}$: product $(1-x_i)\ge\frac{1}{2}$*

> If nonnegative real numbers $x_1, x_2, \ldots, x_n$ satisfy $x_1 + \cdots + x_n \le \frac{1}{2}$, prove that
> $$( 1 - x_1)(1 - x_2) \cdots (1 - x_n) \ge \frac{1}{2}.$$
