*In square PQRS, arc QS is a quarter circle with centre Q. Line TU is tangent to the arc, with T on QR and U on SR extended. Find TR : UR.*

> The diagram shows a square $PQRS$. The arc $QS$ is a quarter circle. The line $TU$ is a tangent to the arc $QS$. The point $T$ lies on $QR$ and the point $U$ lies on $SR$ extended. What is the length of $TR : UR$?
>
> - **(A)** $3:2$
> - **(B)** $4:3$
> - **(C)** $5:4$
> - **(D)** $7:6$
> - **(E)** $9:8$
