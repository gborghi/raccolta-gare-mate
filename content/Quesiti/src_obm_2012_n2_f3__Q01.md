*Amoeba transformations: tube with 201 blue and 112 red amoebas*

> When two red amoebas join together, they transform into a single blue amoeba; when a blue amoeba joins a red amoeba, they both transform into three red amoebas; when two blue amoebas join together, they transform into four red amoebas. A test tube initially contains $201$ blue amoebas and $112$ red amoebas.
> 
> a) Is it possible that after some transformations the tube contains $100$ blue amoebas and $314$ red amoebas?
> 
> b) Is it possible that after some transformations the tube contains $99$ blue amoebas and $314$ red amoebas?
