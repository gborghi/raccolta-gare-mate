*Find the largest possible value of an expression with two constrained sequences.*

> A natural number $n$ and positive real numbers $a$ and $b$ are given. Find the largest possible value of the expression
> $$x_1 y_1 + x_2 y_2 + \cdots + x_n y_n,$$
> where $x_i, y_i$ are numbers from the interval $[0, 1]$ such that $x_1 + x_2 + \cdots + x_n \le a$ and $y_1 + y_2 + \cdots + y_n \le b$.
