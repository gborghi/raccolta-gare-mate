*Triangle with altitude, circumcenter, and tangent collinearity*

> In a triangle $ABC$, $AD$ is the altitude from $A$ to $BC$ and $H$ is the orthocentre. Let $K$ be the centre of the circle passing through $D$ and tangent to $BH$ at $H$. Prove that $DK$ bisects $AC$.
