*Convolution recurrence with a_0=0, a_1=pi/3; compute the sum of a_k/2^k*

> Consider the sequence $a_0,a_1,a_2,\ldots$ defined by $a_0=0$, $a_1=\pi/3$ and, for $n\geq 1$,
> $$a_{n+1}=\frac{\pi\,(a_0 a_n+a_1 a_{n-1}+a_2 a_{n-2}+\cdots+a_n a_0)}{3(n+1)}.$$
> Compute
> $$\sum_{k=0}^{\infty}\frac{a_k}{2^k}=a_0+\frac{a_1}{2}+\frac{a_2}{4}+\frac{a_3}{8}+\cdots.$$
