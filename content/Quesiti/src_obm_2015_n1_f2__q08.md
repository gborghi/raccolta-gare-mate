*Regular hexagon with center O made of 6 equilateral triangles (side 6 cm); find areas of various sub-regions*

> The regular hexagon $ABCDEF$ with center $O$, shown to the side, is composed of $6$ equilateral triangles of side $6$ cm.
> 
> a) What is the area, in $\text{cm}^2$, of the triangle whose vertices are the points $B$, $F$, and $D$?
> 
> b) What is the area, in $\text{cm}^2$, of the quadrilateral $ACDF$?
> 
> c) The triangles $ABC$ and $BCD$ overlap partially. What is the area, in $\text{cm}^2$, of the region common to both triangles, indicated in gray in the figure below?
> 
> d) What is the area, in $\text{cm}^2$, of the triangle whose vertices are the midpoints of sides $AB$, $CD$, and $EF$?
