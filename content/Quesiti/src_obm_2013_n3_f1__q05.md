*Boustrophedon column filling of 1..2013; column 2 holds 50, column 4 holds 100, find column of 1000*

> Joana completely fills a rectangular grid by writing the numbers from $1$ to $2013$, one number in each cell. She starts in the first column and fills it from top to bottom; then she fills the second column from bottom to top, and continues this way, filling the third column from top to bottom, the fourth, etc., until reaching the last column and ending in the bottom-right corner. The number $50$ is in the second column and the number $100$ is in the fourth column. In which column is the number $1000$ written?
> 
> (A) $23$ \quad (B) $31$ \quad (C) $33$ \quad (D) $39$ \quad (E) $61$
