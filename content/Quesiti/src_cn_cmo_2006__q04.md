*Prove AE + AP = PD in right triangle with incircle*

> Let $\triangle ABC$ be a right-angled triangle with $\angle ACB = 90^\circ$. The inscribed circle $\odot$ of $\triangle ABC$ is tangent to $BC$, $CA$, $AB$ at $D$, $E$, $F$ respectively. $AD$ intersects $\odot$ at $P$. $\angle BPC = 90^\circ$. Prove that $AE + AP = PD$.

*Prove AE + AP = PD in triangolo rettangolo con incircolo*
