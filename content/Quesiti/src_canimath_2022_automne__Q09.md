*Isosceles triangle; angle PCQ in terms of x (lycée)*

> Let $ABC$ be an isosceles triangle at $A$ such that $AB < BC$. Let $P$ be the point of the line $(BC)$ situated outside the segment $[BC]$ such that $BP = BA$. Let $Q$ be the point of the line $(AP)$ different from $A$ such that $CQ = CA$. If $\widehat{QPC} = x$, determine the measure of the angle $\widehat{PCQ}$ as a function of $x$.
