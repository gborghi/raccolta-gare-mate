# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2013 — Nível 2 — Fase 1 — Quesito 15

*Hero and villain: find angle BHA from arc and arrow velocities*

> In an animated drawing, a villain is at position $V$ and fires an energy beam along the arc of a circle from point $H$ to hit the friend at $A$. The hero is at position $H$ and fires an arrow in a straight line toward $A$. Given that $VA = 60\,\text{m}$, the beam travels at $10\,\text{m/s}$, and the arrow travels at $15\,\text{m/s}$, and that the hero fires exactly when the beam is launched so both arrive at $A$ simultaneously, determine the value of angle $\angle BHA$.
> 
> (A) $10^\circ$ \quad (B) $15^\circ$ \quad (C) $30^\circ$ \quad (D) $45^\circ$ \quad (E) $60^\circ$
