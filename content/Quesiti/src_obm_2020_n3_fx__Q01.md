# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2020 — Nível 3 — Fase x — Quesito 1

*Existence of positive integers making a weighted reciprocal sum equal 1*

> Prove that there exist positive integers $a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_{2020}$ such that
> $$\frac{1}{a_1} + \frac{1}{2a_2} + \frac{1}{3a_3} + \cdots + \frac{1}{2020\,a_{2020}} = 1.$$
