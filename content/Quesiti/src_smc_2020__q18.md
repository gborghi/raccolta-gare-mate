*Sum of radii of two circles centred at (3,4) each touching a circle centred at origin with radius 2*

> Two circles $C_1$ and $C_2$ have their centres at the point $(3, 4)$ and touch a third circle $C_3$. The centre of $C_3$ is at the point $(0, 0)$ and its radius is 2. What is the sum of the radii of the two circles $C_1$ and $C_2$?
>
> - **(A)** $6$
> - **(B)** $7$
> - **(C)** $8$
> - **(D)** $9$
> - **(E)** $10$
