*Minimum N points in plane guaranteeing a 2004-point monotone-slope subsequence.*

> Find the minimum positive integer $N$ such that the following statement holds: for any placement of $N$ points in the $xy$-plane with no 3 collinear and no 2 sharing the same $x$-coordinate, one can always choose 2004 of these points $(x_1, y_1), (x_2, y_2), \ldots, (x_{2004}, y_{2004})$ with $x_1 < x_2 < \cdots < x_{2004}$ such that either
> $$\frac{y_2 - y_1}{x_2 - x_1} < \frac{y_3 - y_2}{x_3 - x_2} < \cdots < \frac{y_{2004} - y_{2003}}{x_{2004} - x_{2003}}$$
> or
> $$\frac{y_2 - y_1}{x_2 - x_1} > \frac{y_3 - y_2}{x_3 - x_2} > \cdots > \frac{y_{2004} - y_{2003}}{x_{2004} - x_{2003}}$$
> holds.
