*Find minimum constant k for a sum inequality over positive reals*

> Find the smallest real number $k$ such that for every integer $n \ge 2$ and every sequence of positive real numbers $a_0, a_1, \ldots, a_n$,
> $$\frac{1}{a_0+a_1} + \frac{1}{a_0+a_1+a_2} + \cdots + \frac{1}{a_0+a_1+\cdots+a_n} < k\left(\frac{1}{a_0} + \frac{1}{a_1} + \cdots + \frac{1}{a_n}\right).$$
