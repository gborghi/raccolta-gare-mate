# Japan Mathematical Olympiad 2002 — Yosen (Preliminary) — Quesito 7

*Numerator of factorial sum reduced fraction, mod 2002*

> Express the following expression as a single irreducible fraction (a fraction that cannot be reduced further). When its numerator is divided by $2002$, find the remainder.
> $$\frac{2}{3!}+\frac{3}{4!}+\cdots+\frac{11}{12!}$$
> Here, for a natural number $n$, $n!$ denotes $1\times 2\times\cdots\times(n-1)\times n$.
