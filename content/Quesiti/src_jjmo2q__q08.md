*Triangle with two cevians, equal angles, find BE*

> There is a triangle $ABC$ with $BC = 3$, $CA = 2$, $AB = 4$. Take two points $D, E$ on side $AB$ such that $AD = 1$ and $\angle ACD = \angle BCE$. Find the length of segment $BE$.
