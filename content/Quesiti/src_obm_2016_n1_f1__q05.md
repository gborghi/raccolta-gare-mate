*Boy/girl reading magazines per week; on which date they become equal.*

> [Source is the answer key; original statement/options not reproduced. From the solution: with $x$ the number of weeks, $29+3x = 12+4x \Leftrightarrow x = 17$ weeks. Counting $17\times 7 = 119$ days from the start of the second week of February, and since $31+30+31$ (March, April, May) plus the remaining February days total $21+31+30+31 = 113$ days, one concludes the boy equals the girl in number of magazines only at the end of the first week of June.]
