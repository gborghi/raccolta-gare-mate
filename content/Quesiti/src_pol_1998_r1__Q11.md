*Tournament: prove existence of a player beating every other directly or via one intermediate*

> In a tennis tournament $n$ players took part. Any two players played a match (no draws). Prove that there is a player $A$ such that for any other player $B$, either $A$ defeated $B$ or there is a player $C$ whom $A$ defeated and $B$ lost to $C$.
