*Find all real functions satisfying given functional equation*

> Determine all functions $f : \mathbf{R} \longrightarrow \mathbf{R}$ such that
> $$f(x - f(y)) = f(f(y)) + xf(y) + f(x) - 1$$
> for all real numbers $x, y$.
