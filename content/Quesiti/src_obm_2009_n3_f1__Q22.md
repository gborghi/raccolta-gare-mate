*N is a power tower of 2009 eights; repeatedly sum digits to a single digit (digital root); find it.*

> Let $N = 8^{8^{\cdot^{\cdot^{8}}}}$, in which there appear $2009$ number $8$'s (a tower of $2009$ eights). Agilulfo, under punishment, must write the sum of the digits of $N$, obtaining a number $M$; then he must calculate the sum of the digits of $M$; and he must repeat the procedure until obtaining a number with a single digit. Let us help Agilulfo: that digit is (A) $1$ (B) $2$ (C) $3$ (D) $7$ (E) $8$
