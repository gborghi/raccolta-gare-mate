*Find f(1998) for a function satisfying a given functional equation*

> Let $f: \mathbb{N} \to \mathbb{R}$ be a function such that $f(1) = 999$ and
> $$f(1) + f(2) + \cdots + f(n) = n^2 f(n)$$
> for every positive integer $n$. Determine the value of $f(1998)$.
