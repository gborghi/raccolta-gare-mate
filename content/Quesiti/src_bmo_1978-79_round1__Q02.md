# British Mathematical Olympiad 1978-79 — Round 1 — Quesito 2

*Three rays from O in 3-space; find points with equal-perimeter triangles and express OX*

> From a point $O$ in $3$-space, three rays $OA$, $OB$, $OC$ emerge, the angles $BOC$, $COA$, $AOB$ being $\alpha$, $\beta$, $\gamma$ respectively.
> 
> Prove that, for any $2n > 0$, there exist points $X$, $Y$, $Z$ on $OA$, $OB$, $OC$ respectively such that the triangles $YOZ$, $ZOX$ and $XOY$ all have the same perimeter $2n$, and express $OX$ in terms of $n$, $\alpha$, $\beta$ and $\gamma$ simply.
