*Area of triangle with vertices at midpoints of square sides*

> The quadrilateral $ABCD$ is a square of area $4\,\mathrm{m}^2$. Points $M$ and $N$ are the midpoints of sides $DC$ and $BC$, respectively. What is the area, in $\mathrm{m}^2$, of the shaded triangle (the triangle with vertices at $A$, $M$, and $N$)?
> 
> A) 2
> B) 1.5
> C) 2.5
> D) 3
> E) 3.5
