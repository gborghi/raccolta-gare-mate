# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2002 — Nível 3 — Fase 3 — Quesito 2

*Convex cyclic quadrilateral ABCD with point M on CD; triangles ADM and ABCM have equal area and perimeter*

> $ABCD$ is a convex quadrilateral inscribed in a circle, and $M$ is a point on side $CD$, such that triangle $ADM$ and quadrilateral $ABCM$ have the same area and the same perimeter. Prove that $ABCD$ has two equal sides.
