*Find the largest perfect square that is a factor of 10!*

> One of the following is the largest square that is a factor of $10!$. Which one?
> 
> Note that $n! = 1 \times 2 \times 3 \times \cdots \times (n-1) \times n$.
>
> - **(A)** $(4!)^2$
> - **(B)** $(5!)^2$
> - **(C)** $(6!)^2$
> - **(D)** $(7!)^2$
> - **(E)** $(8!)^2$
