# British Mathematical Olympiad 1981-82 — Round 1 — Quesito 5

*Cone ellipse cross-section: find condition for two surface routes to intersect*

> A right circular cone stands on a horizontal base, radius $r$. Its vertex $V$ is at a distance $l$ from every point on the perimeter of the base. A plane section of the cone is an ellipse whose lowest point is $L$ and whose highest point is $R$. On the curved surface of the cone in the same plane via $L$, two routes from $L$ to $R$ are marked. $R_1$ is along the semi-perimeter of the ellipse, and $R_2$ is the route of shortest length on the surface of the cone.
> 
> Find the condition that $R_1$ and $R_2$ intersect between $L$ and $R$.
