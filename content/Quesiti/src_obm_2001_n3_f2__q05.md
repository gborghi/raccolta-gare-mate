*Evaluate nested radical sum expression and express as rational p/q*

> The number
> $$S = \sum_{a=1}^{2000} \sqrt{1 + \frac{1}{a^2} + \frac{1}{(a+1)^2}}$$
> is rational; express it in the form $\dfrac{p}{q}$, where $p$ and $q$ are positive integers.
