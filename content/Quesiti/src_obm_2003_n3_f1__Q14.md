# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2003 — Nível 3 — Fase 1 — Quesito 14

*Find which player has fewer chances in coin-toss game with Beatriz,Isabele,Nicole*

> Beatriz, Isabele, and Nicole are playing a game of consecutive coin tosses. Isabele wins if tails appear in two consecutive tosses; Nicole wins if heads appear in two consecutive tosses. They toss until one of them wins. Beatriz wins if neither Isabele nor Nicole has won yet when the game ends. Which player(s) have fewer chances of winning the game?
> 
> A) Beatriz \quad B) Isabele \quad C) Nicole \quad D) Beatriz and Nicole \quad E) All three have the same chance.
