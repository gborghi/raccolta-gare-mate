- [[src_pol_2003_r2__Q01|Quesito 1]] — Existence of an integer n>2003 so that binomial coefficients a_k=C(n,k) have a divisibility property.
- [[src_pol_2003_r2__Q02|Quesito 2]] — Cyclic quadrilateral ABCD; angle bisectors meet at P and Q; prove line PQ is perpendicular to MN where M,N are arc midpoints.
- [[src_pol_2003_r2__Q03|Quesito 3]] — For W(x)=x^4-3x^3+5x^2-9x, find all pairs of distinct integers a,b with W(a)=W(b).
- [[src_pol_2003_r2__Q04|Quesito 4]] — For each prime p>3 show there exist integers x,y,k with 0<2k<p such that kp+3=x^2+y^2.
- [[src_pol_2003_r2__Q05|Quesito 5]] — From exterior point A two tangents touch circle at B,C; another tangent meets AB,AC at E,F; lines OE,OF meet BC at P,Q; prove BP,PQ,QC are sides of a triangle similar to AEF.
- [[src_pol_2003_r2__Q06|Quesito 6]] — A function f on pairs of nonnegative integers satisfies given recurrences; given f(a,b)=n, find the number of integral solutions x of f(a,x)+f(b,x)=n.
