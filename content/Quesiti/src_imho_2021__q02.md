*Double sum inequality with square roots of absolute values*

> Show that the inequality
> $$\sum_{i=1}^{n}\sum_{j=1}^{n}\sqrt{|x_i - x_j|} \leq \sum_{i=1}^{n}\sum_{j=1}^{n}\sqrt{|x_i + x_j|}$$
> holds for all real numbers $x_1, \ldots, x_n$.
