*Continuous f with some iterate equal to 1 for every x; show f(1)=1*

> Suppose that $f : \mathbb{R} \to \mathbb{R}$ is a continuous function such that for every real $x$ there exists $n \in \mathbb{N}$ such that
> $$\underbrace{f \circ f \circ \cdots \circ f}_{n}(x) = 1.$$
> Show that $f(1) = 1$.
