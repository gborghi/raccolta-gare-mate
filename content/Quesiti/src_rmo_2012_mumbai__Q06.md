*Weighted alternating sum over all subsets of a 10-element set*

> For a non-empty subset $A$ of $S = \{1, 2, \ldots, 10\}$, define $w(A)$ by arranging the elements of $A$ in decreasing order and then alternately adding and subtracting them, starting with addition (the largest element is added). Let $\mathrm{WSUM}$ be the sum of $w(A)$ over all non-empty subsets $A$ of $S$. Show that $\mathrm{WSUM} = 70656$.
