*Common tangent and equal areas of triangles from two intersecting circles*

> Two intersecting circles $C_1$ and $C_2$ have a common tangent which touches $C_1$ at $P$ and $C_2$ at $Q$. The two circles intersect at $M$ and $N$, where $N$ is nearer to $PQ$ than $M$. Prove that the triangles $MNP$ and $MNQ$ have equal areas.
