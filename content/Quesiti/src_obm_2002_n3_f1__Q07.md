*Marcelo's walk to school; fraction of path covered when he turns back, given timing*

> Marcelo takes exactly 20 minutes to go from his house to school. One day, while walking, he realized he had forgotten a comic book to show to a classmate; he went back home and went out again, arriving at school 8 minutes after the time he would normally have arrived, that is, 10 minutes late. What fraction of the path had he already walked when he turned back?
