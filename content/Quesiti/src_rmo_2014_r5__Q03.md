# Regional Mathematical Olympiad 2014 — Region 5 — Quesito 3

*Angle bisectors from perpendicular bisectors in acute triangle; prove BO perp to DE*

> Let $ABC$ be an acute-angled triangle in which $\angle ABC$ is the largest angle. Let $O$ be its circumcentre. The perpendicular bisectors of $BC$ and $AC$ meet $AB$ and $BC$ at $X$ and $Y$ respectively. The internal bisectors of $\angle AXB$ and $\angle BYC$ meet $AC$ at $D$ and $E$ respectively. Prove that $BO$ is perpendicular to $DE$.
