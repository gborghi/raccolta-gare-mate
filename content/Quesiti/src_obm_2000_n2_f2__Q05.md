# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2000 — Nível 2 — Fase 2 — Quesito 5

*Find n and m after erasing m from {1,...,n} leaves average 134/11*

> List the integers from $1$ to $n$. From this list we erase the integer $m$. The average of the remaining $n - 1$ numbers equals $\dfrac{134}{11}$. Find $n$ and $m$.
