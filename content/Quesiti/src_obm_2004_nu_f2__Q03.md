# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2004 — Nível Universitário — Fase 2 — Quesito 3

*Prove sub-multiplicative inequality for matrix norm and dilation factor*

> Let $A$ be a real invertible matrix of order $n$ and $A^t$ its transpose. Let $\lambda_1\ge\lambda_2\ge\cdots\ge\lambda_n>0$ be the eigenvalues of $A^tA$. Define the *norm* of $A$ by $\|A\|=\sqrt{\lambda_1}$ and the *dilation factor* of $A$ by $d(A)=\sqrt{\dfrac{\lambda_1}{\lambda_n}}$.
> 
> Prove that, for any two real invertible matrices $A$ and $B$,
> $$d(AB)\le\frac{\|AB\|}{\|A\|\cdot\|B\|}\,d(A)\cdot d(B).$$
