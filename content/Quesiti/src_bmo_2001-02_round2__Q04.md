*Product of tangent lengths from point to unit spheres bounded by distances to centres*

> $B_1, B_2, \ldots, B_n$ are spheres of unit radius arranged in space. Let $P$ be a point and for each $i$ ($1 \le i \le n$) the length of the tangent from $P$ to the sphere $B_i$ is denoted by $t_i$. Prove that the product of the $t_i$ is not more than the product of the distances $PC_i$, where $C_i$ is the centre of $B_i$.
