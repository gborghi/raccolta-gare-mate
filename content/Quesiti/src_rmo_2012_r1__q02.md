*Divisibility chain implies abc divides (a+b+c)^13*

> Let $a, b, c$ be positive integers such that $a$ divides $b^3$, $b$ divides $c^3$ and $c$ divides $a^3$. Show that $abc$ divides $(a + b + c)^{13}$.
