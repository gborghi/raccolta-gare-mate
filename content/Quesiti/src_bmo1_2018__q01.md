*Compare remainder sums: Helen divides 365, Henry divides 366*

> Helen divides $365$ by each of $1, 2, 3, \ldots, 365$ in turn, writing down a list of the $365$ remainders. Henry divides $366$ by each of $1, 2, 3, \ldots, 366$ in turn, writing down a list of the $366$ remainders. Which list of remainders has the greater sum and by how much?
