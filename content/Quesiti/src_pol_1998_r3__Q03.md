*Pentagon base of pyramid: diagonal intersections of five trapezoids are coplanar*

> A convex pentagon $ABCDE$ is the base of a pyramid $ABCDES$. A plane, not passing through any vertex of the pyramid, meets the edges $SA$, $SB$, $SC$, $SD$, $SE$ at points $A'$, $B'$, $C'$, $D'$, $E'$ respectively. Prove that the intersection points of the diagonals of the quadrilaterals $ABB'A'$, $BCC'B'$, $CDD'C'$, $DEE'D'$, $EAA'E'$ are coplanar.
