*Circle through four points with two parallel chords*

> Points $A$, $B$, $C$, $D$ and $E$ lie, in that order, on a circle and the lines $AB$ and $ED$ are parallel. The line through $D$ which is parallel to $AC$ meets $BE$ at $F$. Prove that $BC = DF$.
