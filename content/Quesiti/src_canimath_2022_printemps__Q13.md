# Coupe Animath Printemps 2022 — Quesito 13

*Three-colored plane with all colors present; prove a right triangle with three different-colored vertices (lycee)*

> Each point of the plane is colored with one of the three colors blue, green or red. For each color, suppose that there exists at least one point of the plane colored with that color. Show that there exists a right triangle whose three vertices are of pairwise different colors.
