*Prove inequality sum of x/(y+z) >= 3/2 for positive reals*

> Prove that if $x, y, z$ are positive real numbers then
> $$\frac{x}{y+z} + \frac{y}{z+x} + \frac{z}{x+y} \ge \frac{3}{2}.$$
