*Points P, Q, R on a circle with centre O have chord lengths PQ:QR:RP = 1:2:3; find ratio of sector areas POQ:QOR:ROP.*

> Three points $P$, $Q$ and $R$ are placed on the circumference of a circle with centre $O$. The lengths $PQ$, $QR$ and $RP$ are in the ratio $1:2:3$. What are the ratios of the areas of the sectors $POQ$, $QOR$ and $ROP$?
>
> - **(A)** $1:1:1$
> - **(B)** $1:2:3$
> - **(C)** $1:2:4$
> - **(D)** $1:4:9$
> - **(E)** $1:8:27$
