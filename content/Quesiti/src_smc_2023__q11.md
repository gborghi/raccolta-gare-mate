*Truth-tellers among Wendi, Xander, Yasser and Zoe*

> Wendi, Xander, Yasser and Zoe make the following statements. Wendi: "Xander is lying." Yasser: "Zoe is telling the truth." Xander: "Yasser is lying." Zoe: "Wendi is telling the truth." What are the possible numbers of people telling the truth?
>
> - **(A)** $1$ or $2$
> - **(B)** $1$ or $3$
> - **(C)** $2$
> - **(D)** $2$ or $3$
> - **(E)** $3$
