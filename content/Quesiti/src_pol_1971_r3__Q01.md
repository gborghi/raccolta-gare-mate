*Infinite sequence of distinct integers with no digit 0*

> Show that if $(a_n)$ is an infinite sequence of distinct positive integers, neither of which contains digit $0$ in the decimal expansion, then
> $$\sum_{i=1}^{\infty} \frac{1}{a_i} < 29.$$
