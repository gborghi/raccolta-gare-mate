*Count 3-digit integers whose digits form an isosceles triangle*

> Let $\sigma = \overline{abc}$ be a 3-digit number. If we can construct an isosceles triangle (including equilateral triangle) with $a$, $b$ and $c$ as the lengths of the sides, the number of such 3-digit integers is ( ).
> 
> (A) $45$ \quad (B) $81$ \quad (C) $165$ \quad (D) $216$
