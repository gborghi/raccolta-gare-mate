# Japan Mathematical Olympiad 2001 — Honsen (Final) — Quesito 1

*m×n grid coloring: each black cell has odd many black neighbours; show m,n odd*

> An $m \times n$ grid of unit squares is given. Each cell is coloured either black or white satisfying the following condition: for every black cell, the number of black cells sharing an edge with it is odd. Show that both $m$ and $n$ must be odd.
