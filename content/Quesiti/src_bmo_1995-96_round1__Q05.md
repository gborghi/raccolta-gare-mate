# British Mathematical Olympiad 1995-96 — Round 1 — Quesito 5

*Prove 4(a³+b³)≥(a+b)³ and 9(a³+b³+c³)≥(a+b+c)³*

> Let $a$, $b$, $c$ be positive real numbers.
> (i) Prove that $4(a^3 + b^3) \ge (a + b)^3$.
> (ii) Prove that $9(a^3 + b^3 + c^3) \ge (a + b + c)^3$.
