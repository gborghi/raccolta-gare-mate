*The inscribed sphere of a tetrahedron touches two faces at their centroids; prove the tetrahedron is regular.*

> The inscribed sphere of a tetrahedron $ABCD$ with $AB = CD$ touches the faces $ABC$ and $ABD$ at $K$ and $L$, respectively. Prove that if $K$ and $L$ are the centroids of the corresponding faces, then $ABCD$ is a regular tetrahedron.
