*Count the total number of digits of a number built from repeated blocks 10100.*

> A number $N$ is formed by equal blocks, each of the form "$10100$". Knowing that the number $N$ has $101$ digits, it is formed by $20$ complete blocks plus one extra leading digit, and the sum of the digits of each block equals $1+0+1+0+0=2$. Determine the value of the total number of digits of $N$ (the requested quantity equals $20\times 2+1=41$).
