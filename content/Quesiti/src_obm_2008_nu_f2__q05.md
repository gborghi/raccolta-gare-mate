*Find all functions Z to Z satisfying two functional conditions*

> Find all functions $f:\mathbb{Z}\to\mathbb{Z}$ satisfying:
> 
> (i) $f(f(n))=f(n)+1$ for all $n\in\mathbb{Z}$;
> 
> (ii) $f(2009n+2008)=2009f(n)$ for all $n\in\mathbb{Z}$.
