*Trapezoid ABCD, diagonals meet at E, line through E parallel to AB meets AD at F, show angle BFC=90*

> Let $ABCD$ be a trapezoid with $AB + CD = AD$. The diagonals $(AC)$ and $(BD)$ meet at a point $E$. The line through $E$ parallel to $(AB)$ meets $(AD)$ at point $F$. Show that $\widehat{BFC} = 90^\circ$.
