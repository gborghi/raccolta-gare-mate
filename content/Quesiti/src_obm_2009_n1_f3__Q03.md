> [Figure 1 shows a small staircase-like solid; Figure 2 shows a $3\times3\times3$ cube with numbers indicating the count of unit cubes along each direction.]

> 
> Find the values of $a$, $b$, $c$, $d$, $e$, $f$, $x$ and $m$.
