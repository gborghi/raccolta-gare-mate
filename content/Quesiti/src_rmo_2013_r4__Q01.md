*Circle through O intersects another circle; angle equality proof*

> Let $\Gamma$ be a circle with centre $O$. Let $A$ be another circle passing through $O$ and intersecting $\Gamma$ at points $A$ and $B$. A diameter $CD$ of $\Gamma$ intersects $A$ at a point $P$ different from $O$. Prove that
> $$\angle APC = \angle BPD.$$
