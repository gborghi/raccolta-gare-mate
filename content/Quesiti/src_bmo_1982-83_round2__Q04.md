*Prove inequality for six positive reals with harmonic-mean structure*

> Prove that if $a, b, c, d, e, f$ are positive real numbers then
> $$\frac{ab}{a+b} + \frac{cd}{c+d} + \frac{ef}{e+f} \le \frac{(a+c+e)(b+d+f)}{a+b+c+d+e+f}.$$
