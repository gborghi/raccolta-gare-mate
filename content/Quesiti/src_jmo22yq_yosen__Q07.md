# Japan Mathematical Olympiad 2012 — Yosen (Preliminary) — Quesito 7

*Triangle ABC with circumcenter O; midpoint of DE is O; find CE*

> Let $O$ be the circumcentre of triangle $ABC$. Let $D$ be a point on segment $AB$ and $E$ a point on segment $AC$ such that the midpoint of segment $DE$ is $O$. Given $AD = 8$, $BD = 3$, $AO = 7$, find $CE$.
> 
> (Here, $XY$ denotes the length of segment $XY$.)
