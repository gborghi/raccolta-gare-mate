*Triangle FGH with angles 60°, 30°, 90° overlaps right-angled isosceles triangle EGH; find ratio of areas IFG to IEH.*

> The diagram shows two overlapping triangles: triangle $FGH$ with interior angles $60^{\circ}$, $30^{\circ}$ and $90^{\circ}$, and triangle $EGH$ which is a right-angled isosceles triangle. What is the ratio of the area of triangle $IFG$ to the area of triangle $IEH$?
>
> - **(A)** $1:1$
> - **(B)** $1:\sqrt{2}$
> - **(C)** $1:\sqrt{3}$
> - **(D)** $1:2$
> - **(E)** $1:3$
