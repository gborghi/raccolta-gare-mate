*Count integers 1..2016 with small remainder mod 20*

> Among the integers from $1$ to $2016$, how many are there whose remainder upon division by $20$ is smaller than half of $16$ (that is, the remainder of the integer modulo $20$ is smaller than $\tfrac{16}{2} = 8$)?
