*Find all three-digit numbers (abc)_10 with (abc)_10 = (abc)_b * (acb)_b*

> Find all three-digit natural numbers of the form $(abc)_{10}$ such that $(abc)_{10} = (abc)_b \cdot (acb)_b$. (Here $(abc)_{10}$ is the number in base 10.)
