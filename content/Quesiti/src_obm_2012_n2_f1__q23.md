*Year in which the gcd of year and OBM edition is maximized*

> In $2012$ we are holding edition $34$ of the OBM, and $\mathrm{mdc}(2012, 34) = 2$. Supposing that the OBM is always held every year, in which year will the largest possible value for the $\mathrm{mdc}$ of the year and the edition of the OBM held in that year occur?
> 
> A) 12 \quad B) 28 \quad C) 38 \quad D) 1978 \quad E) 2012
