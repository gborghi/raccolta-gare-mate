*Find side ratio of triangle from incentre-circumcentre angles*

> Let triangle $ABC$ have incentre $I$ and circumcentre $O$. Suppose that $\angle AIO = 90^\circ$ and $\angle CIO = 45^\circ$. Find the ratio $AB : BC : CA$.
