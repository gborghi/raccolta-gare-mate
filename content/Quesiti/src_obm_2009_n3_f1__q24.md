*Rectangular notebook sheet with yellow borders of 24 cm and red borders of 36 cm; each point painted the color of its nearest side; find the yellow area.*

> A sheet of Carlos's notebook is a rectangle with two yellow sides (borders) of $24$ cm and two red sides (borders) of $36$ cm. Carlos paints each point of the rectangle in the same color as the side nearest to that point. What is the area of the region painted yellow? (A) $144$ cm$^2$ (B) $288$ cm$^2$ (C) $364$ cm$^2$ (D) $442$ cm$^2$ (E) $524$ cm$^2$
