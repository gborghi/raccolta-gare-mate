*Find least positive integer m with 105 | 9^(p^2) - 29^p + m for all primes p > 3*

> Find the least positive integer $m$, such that for every prime number $p > 3$,
> $$105 \mid 9^{p^2} - 29^p + m.$$
> (posed by Yang Hu)

**Topic:** [[topic_aritmetica|Aritmetica / Teoria dei Numeri]]
