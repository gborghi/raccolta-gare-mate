# Pre-Regional Mathematical Olympiad (PRMO) 2015 — Set B — Quesito 16

*Acute triangle midpoints D,E,F; angle conditions; find angle ADF*

> In acute-angled triangle $ABC$, let $D$ be the midpoint of $BC$. Let $E$ be the midpoint of $AC$. Let $F$ be the midpoint of $AB$. Suppose $\angle BAE = 40^\circ$. If $\angle DAE = \angle DFE$, what is the magnitude of $\angle ADF$ in degrees?
