*Convex hexagon ABCDEF with angle A+C+E=360 deg and (AB/BC)(CD/DE)(EF/FA)=1; prove (AB/BF)(FD/DE)(EC/CA)=1.*

> A convex hexagon $ABCDEF$ satisfies
> $$\angle A + \angle C + \angle E = 360^\circ \quad \text{and} \quad \frac{AB}{BC} \cdot \frac{CD}{DE} \cdot \frac{EF}{FA} = 1.$$
> Prove that
> $$\frac{AB}{BF} \cdot \frac{FD}{DE} \cdot \frac{EC}{CA} = 1.$$
