# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2001 — Nível 2 — Fase 1 — Quesito 19

*Minimum corridor width to push a rectangular table around a right-angle turn*

> A rectangular table, whose feet have wheels, must be pushed along a corridor of constant width that forms a right angle. If the dimensions of the table are $a$ and $b$ (with $2a < b$), what must be the minimum width of the corridor so that the table can be pushed through it?
> 
> (A) $a + b$ \quad (B) $(a+b)\dfrac{\sqrt{2}}{2}$ \quad (C) $(a+b)\dfrac{\sqrt{2}}{4}$ \quad (D) $(2a+b)\dfrac{\sqrt{2}}{4}$ \quad (E) $(a+2b)\dfrac{\sqrt{2}}{4}$
