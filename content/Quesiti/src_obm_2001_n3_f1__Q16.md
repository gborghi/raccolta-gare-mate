*Rectangular table (2a < b) pushed through a right-angle corridor; minimum corridor width*

> A rectangular table, whose feet have wheels, must be pushed through a corridor of constant width that forms a right angle. If the dimensions of the table are $a$ and $b$ (with $2a < b$), what must be the minimum width of the corridor so that the table can be pushed through it?
> 
> **A)** $a + b$ $\quad$ **B)** $(a + b)\,\dfrac{\sqrt{2}}{2}$ $\quad$ **C)** $(a + b)\,\dfrac{\sqrt{2}}{4}$ $\quad$ **D)** $(2a + b)\,\dfrac{\sqrt{2}}{4}$ $\quad$ **E)** $(a + 2b)\,\dfrac{\sqrt{2}}{4}$
