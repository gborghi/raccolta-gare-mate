*A non-decreasing sequence of positive integers has exactly k values equal to k; find all primes among its partial sums.*

> In a non-decreasing sequence $a_1, a_2, a_3, \ldots$ all values are positive integers and exactly $k$ values are equal to $k$. Find all primes of the form
> $$a_1 + a_2 + \cdots + a_n.$$
