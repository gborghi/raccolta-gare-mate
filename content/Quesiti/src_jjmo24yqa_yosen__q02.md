*Maximize n(a+b)(a+b+c)(a+b+c+d) equal to a perfect square*

> Let $a, b, c, d$ be pairwise distinct integers each from $1$ to $4$. Let $P = n(a+b)(a+b+c)(a+b+c+d)$ for a positive integer $n$. Given that $P$ is a perfect square, find the maximum possible value of $P$.
