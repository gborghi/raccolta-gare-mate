*Bag of 4 red/white balls; P(both drawn red) = 1/6; find P(both drawn white)*

> A bag contains four balls each of which is coloured either red or white. If one ball is drawn at random and not replaced, then another is drawn at random, the probability that both balls are red is $\frac{1}{6}$. What is the probability that both balls are white?
>
> - **(A)** $\frac{1}{2}$
> - **(B)** $\frac{1}{6}$
> - **(C)** $\frac{1}{4}$
> - **(D)** $\frac{1}{3}$
> - **(E)** $0$
