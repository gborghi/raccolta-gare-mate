# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2006 — Nível 3 — Fase 1 — Quesito 20

*Altino (1.5 m tall) against a wall at night, lamp atop 4 m post 10 m from wall; choose graph of wall shadow height f(x) versus distance x.*

> Altino is leaning against a high wall, during the night. The street where Altino is located is illuminated by a lamp on top of a post 4 meters high, located 10 meters from the wall. Altino, one and a half meters tall, is in the direction of the wall. Let $f(x)$ be the height, in meters, of the shadow of Altino produced by the lamp on the wall when Altino is at a distance of $x$ meters from the wall. Which alternative best represents the graph of $f(x)$?
