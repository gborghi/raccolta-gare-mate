# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2019 — Nível 2 — Fase x — Quesito 1

*Count and characterize 'robust' and 'super-robust' 8-digit numbers*

> An eight-digit number is called *robust* if it satisfies both of the following conditions:
> 
> (i) None of its digits is $0$.
> 
> (ii) The difference between any two consecutive digits is $4$ or $5$.
> 
> Answer the following questions:
> 
> (a) How many robust numbers are there?
> 
> (b) A robust number is called *super-robust* if all its digits are distinct. Compute the sum of all super-robust numbers.
