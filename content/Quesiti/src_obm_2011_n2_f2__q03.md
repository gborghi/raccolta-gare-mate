*Right triangle; angle bisectors meet BC; find AB given AD,AE*

> Triangle $ABC$ is right-angled at $B$. The internal and external bisectors of angle $\hat{BAC}$ meet line $BC$ at points $D$ and $E$, respectively. Given that $AD = 360$ and $AE = 480$, determine the length of side $AB$.

*Triangolo retto; bisettieri angolari incontrano BC; trovare AB con AD,AE*
