*Number of pairs (x,y) with z=gcd satisfying 2xy + y(z,y) = (x+y)^2*

> For natural numbers $x$ and $y$, let $(x, y)$ denote the greatest common divisor of $x$ and $y$. How many pairs of natural numbers $x$ and $y$ with $x \le y$ satisfy the equation $xy + y(x, y) = (x + y)^2$?
