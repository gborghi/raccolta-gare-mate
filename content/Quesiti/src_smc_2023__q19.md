*Sum of integer values of a rational expression*

> The expression $\dfrac{7n+12}{2n+3}$ takes integer values for certain integer values of $n$. What is the sum of all such integer values of the expression?
>
> - **(A)** $4$
> - **(B)** $8$
> - **(C)** $10$
> - **(D)** $12$
> - **(E)** $14$
