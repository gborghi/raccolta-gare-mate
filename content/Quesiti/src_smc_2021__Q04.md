*Find sum of digits of positive integer k satisfying (k ÷ 12) ÷ (15 ÷ k) = 20.*

> The positive integer $k$ is a solution of the equation $(k \div 12) \div (15 \div k) = 20$. What is the sum of the digits of $k$?
>
> - **(A)** 15
> - **(B)** 12
> - **(C)** 9
> - **(D)** 6
> - **(E)** 3
