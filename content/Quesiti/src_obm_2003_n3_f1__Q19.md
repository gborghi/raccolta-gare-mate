# Olimpíada Brasileira de Matemática 2003 — Nível 3 — Fase 1 — Quesito 19

*Find speed of train given Augusto and Eduardo barely escape from bridge*

> Two friends, Augusto and Eduardo, are crossing a bridge. Each has walked $\dfrac{2}{5}$ of the bridge when a train appears from behind Augusto (coming from Augusto's side). If Augusto runs back at $15\,\text{km/h}$, he barely escapes (exits just as the train arrives). Eduardo runs forward at $15\,\text{km/h}$ and also barely escapes. What is the speed of the train, in $\text{km/h}$?
> 
> A) $30$ \quad B) $45$ \quad C) $60$ \quad D) $75$ \quad E) $90$
