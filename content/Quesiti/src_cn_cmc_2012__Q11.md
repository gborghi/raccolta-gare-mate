*Rhombus in coordinate system; product OA·OC constant and trace of C*

> (20 marks) As seen in Fig. 11.1, in the rectangular coordinate system $XOY$, the side of the rhombus $ABCD$ is 4, and $|OB| = |OD| = 6$.
> (1) Prove that $|OA| \cdot |OC|$ is a constant.
> (2) When point $A$ is moving on the half circle $(x - 2)^2 + y^2 = 4$ ($2 \le x \le 4$), find the trace of $C$.
